**meadow** *n*
**1** a grassy [[field]] used for hay or for grazing animals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[field]], [[pasture]], [[grassland]], [[lea]] (*poetic*)
**2** a low-lying piece of [[grassland]], often near a river
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[field]], [[pasture]], [[grassland]], [[lea]] (*poetic*)
