**ready** *adj* **readier**, **readiest**
**1** [[prepared]] for use or action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prepared]], [[set]], [[primed]], [[organized]]
**2** [[prompt]] or [[eager]]: the ready use of corporal punishment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[willing]], [[happy]], [[glad]], [[disposed]], [[keen]], [[eager]], [[inclined]], [[prone]]
**3** [[quick]] or intelligent: a ready wit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prompt]], [[smart]], [[quick]], [[bright]], [[sharp]], [[keen]], [[alert]], [[clever]]
**4** **ready to** on the point of or liable to: ready to pounce
**5** easily [[available]]: his ready tears
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[available]], [[handy]], [[present]], [[near]], [[accessible]], convenient
 *n*
**6** **at the ready** poised for use: with pen at the ready
 *vb* **readies**, **readying**, **readied**
**7** to make ready; prepare
