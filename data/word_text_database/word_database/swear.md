**swear** *vb* **swearing**, **swore**, **sworn**
**1** to use words considered obscene or blasphemous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curse]], [[blaspheme]], [[be foul-mouthed]]
**2** to [[promise]] solemnly on oath; [[vow]]: Sally and Peter swore to love and cherish each other
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vow]], [[promise]], [[testify]], [[attest]]
**3** **swear by** to have complete confidence in (something)
**4** to state (something) earnestly: I swear he was all right
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[declare]], [[assert]], [[affirm]]
**5** to give evidence on oath in a law court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vow]], [[promise]], [[testify]], [[attest]]
