**volatile** (**voll**-a-tile) *adj*
**1** (of circumstances) liable to sudden change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[changeable]], [[shifting]], [[variable]], [[unsettled]], [[unstable]], [[explosive]], [[unreliable]], [[unsteady]]
**2** (of people) liable to sudden changes of mood and behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[temperamental]], [[erratic]], [[mercurial]], [[up and down]] (*informal*), [[fickle]], [[overemotional]]
**3** (of a substance) changing quickly from a solid or liquid form to a vapour
 **[[volatility]]** *n*
