**harsh** *adj*
**1** [[severe]] and difficult to cope with: harsh winters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[severe]], [[hard]], [[tough]], [[stark]], [[austere]], [[inhospitable]], [[bare-bones]]
**2** unkind and showing no understanding: the judge was very harsh on the demonstrators
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cruel]], [[savage]], [[ruthless]], [[barbarous]], [[pitiless]]
**3** excessively [[hard]], bright, or [[rough]]: harsh sunlight
**4** (of sounds) unpleasant and [[grating]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[raucous]], [[rough]], [[grating]], [[strident]], [[rasping]], [[discordant]], [[guttural]], [[dissonant]]
 **[[harshly]]** *adv* 
 **[[harshness]]** *n*
