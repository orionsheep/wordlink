**ensure** * or * (*esp* *US*) **insure** *vb* **-suring**, **-sured**
**1** to [[make certain]]: we must ensure that similar accidents do not happen again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make certain]], [[guarantee]], [[secure]], [[make sure]], [[confirm]], [[warrant]], [[certify]]
**2** to [[make safe]] or [[protect]]: to ensure against fraud, voters will have to bring a piece of identification
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]], [[defend]], [[secure]], [[safeguard]], [[guard]], [[make safe]]
