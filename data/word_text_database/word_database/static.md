**static** *adj*
**1** not active, changing, or moving; stationary
**2** (*physics*) (of a weight, force, or pressure) acting but causing no movement
**3** (*physics*) of forces that do not produce movement
 *n*
**4** hissing or crackling or a speckled picture caused by interference in the reception of radio or television transmissions
**5** electric sparks or crackling produced by friction
