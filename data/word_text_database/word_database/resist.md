**resist** *vb*
**1** to stand firm against or [[oppose]]: the party's old guard continue to resist economic reform
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oppose]], [[battle against]], [[combat]], [[defy]], [[stand up to]], [[hinder]]
**2** to [[refrain from]] in spite of temptation: I couldn't resist a huge portion of almond cake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refrain from]], [[avoid]], [[keep from]], [[forgo]], [[abstain from]], [[forbear]]
**3** to refuse to comply with: to resist arrest
**4** to [[be proof against]]: airport design should be strengthened to help resist explosion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[withstand]], [[be proof against]]
 **[[resistible]]** *adj*
