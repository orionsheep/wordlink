**constant** *adj*
**1** [[continuous]]: she has endured constant criticism, mockery, and humiliation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continuous]], [[sustained]], [[perpetual]], [[interminable]], [[unrelenting]], [[incessant]], [[ceaseless]], [[nonstop]]
**2** [[unchanging]]: the average speed of the winds remained constant over this period
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unchanging]], [[even]], [[fixed]], [[permanent]], [[stable]], [[steady]], [[uniform]], [[invariable]]
**3** [[faithful]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faithful]], [[true]], [[devoted]], [[loyal]], [[stalwart]], [[staunch]], [[trustworthy]], trusty
 *n*
**4** (*maths*, *physics*) a quantity or number which remains [[invariable]]: the velocity of light is a constant
**5** something that is [[unchanging]]
 **[[constancy]]** *n* 
 **[[constantly]]** *adv*
