**pointer** *n*
**1** something that is a helpful [[indicator]] of how a situation has arisen or may turn out: a significant pointer to the likely resumption of talks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hint]], [[tip]], [[suggestion]], [[recommendation]], [[caution]], [[piece of information]], [[piece of advice]]
**2** an [[indicator]] on a measuring instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indicator]], [[hand]], [[guide]], [[needle]], [[arrow]]
**3** a long stick used by teachers, to point out particular features on a map, chart, etc.
**4** a large smooth-coated gun dog
