**partial** *adj*
**1** relating to only a part; not complete: partial deafness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incomplete]], [[unfinished]], [[imperfect]], [[uncompleted]]
**2** [[biased]]: religious programmes can be as partial as they like
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[biased]], [[prejudiced]], [[discriminatory]], [[partisan]], [[unfair]], [[one-sided]], [[unjust]]
**3** **be partial to** to have a particular liking for
