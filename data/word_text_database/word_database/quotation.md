**quotation** *n*
**1** a written or spoken [[passage]] repeated exactly in a later work, speech, or conversation, usually with an acknowledgment of its source
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passage]], [[quote]] (*informal*), [[excerpt]], [[reference]], [[extract]], [[citation]]
**2** the act of quoting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passage]], [[quote]] (*informal*), [[excerpt]], [[reference]], [[extract]], [[citation]]
**3** an [[estimate]] of costs submitted by a contractor to a prospective client
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimate]], [[price]], [[tender]], [[rate]], [[cost]], [[charge]], [[figure]], [[quote]] (*informal*)
