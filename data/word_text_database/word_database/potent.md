**potent** *adj*
**1** having great power or influence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[powerful]], [[commanding]], [[dynamic]], [[dominant]], [[influential]], [[authoritative]]
**2** (of arguments) persuasive or [[forceful]]
**3** highly effective: a potent poison
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strong]], [[powerful]], [[mighty]], [[vigorous]], [[forceful]]
**4** (of a male) capable of having sexual intercourse
 **[[potency]]** *n*
