**dog** *n*
**1** a domesticated [[canine]] mammal occurring in many different breeds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hound]], [[canine]], [[pooch]] (*slang*), [[cur]], [[man's best friend]], kuri *or* goorie (*NZ*), [[brak]] (*S African*)
**2** any other member of the dog family, such as the dingo or coyote. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**[[canine]]**
**3** the male of animals of the dog family
**4** (*informal*) a person: you lucky dog!
**5** (*US** & **Canad* *informal*) something unsatisfactory or inferior
**6** **a dog's life** a wretched existence
**7** **dog eat dog** ruthless competition
**8** **like a dog's dinner** dressed smartly and ostentatiously
 *vb* **dogging**, **dogged**
**9** to [[follow]] (someone) closely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pursue]], [[follow]], [[track]], [[chase]], [[trail]], [[hound]], [[stalk]]
**10** to [[trouble]]: dogged by ill health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plague]], [[follow]], [[trouble]], [[haunt]], [[hound]], [[torment]]
