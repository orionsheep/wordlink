**boost** *n*
**1** [[encouragement]] or [[help]]: a boost to morale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[encouragement]], [[help]]
**2** an upward thrust or push
**3** an [[increase]] or [[rise]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rise]], [[increase]], [[jump]], [[addition]], [[improvement]], [[expansion]], [[upsurge]], upturn
 *vb*
**4** to encourage or improve: to boost morale
**5** to cause to [[rise]]; [[increase]]: we significantly boosted our market share
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[develop]], [[raise]], [[expand]], [[add to]], [[heighten]], [[enlarge]], [[amplify]]
**6** to advertise on a big scale
