**abrupt** *adj*
**1** [[sudden]] or [[unexpected]]: an abrupt departure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sudden]], [[unexpected]], [[rapid]], [[surprising]], [[quick]], [[rash]], [[precipitate]]
**2** rather [[rude]] in speech or manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curt]], [[brief]], [[short]], [[rude]], [[impatient]], [[terse]], [[gruff]], [[succinct]]
 **[[abruptly]]** *adv* 
 **[[abruptness]]** *n*
