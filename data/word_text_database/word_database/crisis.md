**crisis** *n*, *pl* **-ses**
**1** a crucial stage or [[turning point]] in the course of anything
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[critical point]], [[climax]], [[height]], [[crunch]] (*informal*), [[turning point]], [[culmination]], [[crux]], [[moment of truth]], [[tipping point]]
**2** a time of extreme [[trouble]] or danger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[emergency]], [[plight]], [[predicament]], [[trouble]], [[deep water]], [[meltdown]] (*informal*), [[dire straits]]
