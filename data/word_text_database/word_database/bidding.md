**bidding** *n*
**1** an [[order]] or [[command]]: she had done his bidding
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[order]], [[request]], [[command]], [[instruction]], [[summons]], [[beck and call]]
**2** an invitation; [[summons]]: he knew to knock and wait for bidding before he entered
**3** the bids in an auction, card game, etc.
