**stout** *adj*
**1** solidly built or [[fat]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fat]], [[big]], [[heavy]], [[overweight]], [[plump]], [[bulky]], [[burly]], [[fleshy]]
**2** [[strong]] and [[sturdy]]: stout footwear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strong]], [[strapping]], [[muscular]], [[robust]], [[sturdy]], [[stalwart]], [[brawny]], [[able-bodied]]
**3** [[brave]] or determined: we met unexpectedly stout resistance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brave]], [[bold]], [[courageous]], [[fearless]], [[resolute]], [[gallant]], [[intrepid]], valiant
 *n*
**4** a kind of [[strong]], black beer
 **[[stoutly]]** *adv*
