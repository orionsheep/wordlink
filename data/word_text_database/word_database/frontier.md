**frontier** *n*
**1** the region of a country bordering on another, or a line marking such a [[boundary]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[border]], [[limit]], [[edge]], [[boundary]], [[verge]] (*Brit*), [[perimeter]], [[borderline]], [[dividing line]]
**2** the [[edge]] of the settled area of a country
**3** **frontiers** the [[limit]] of knowledge in a particular field: at that time, laser spectroscopy was on the frontiers of chemical research
