**hump** *n*
**1** a rounded lump on the ground
**2** a rounded deformity of the back
**3** a rounded lump on the back of a camel or related animal
**4** **the hump** (*Brit* *informal*) a fit of sulking: you've got the hump today
 *vb*
**5** (*slang*) to carry or heave: who would be responsible if they were injured humping heavy gear around?
