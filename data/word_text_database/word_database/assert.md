**assert** *vb*
**1** to [[state]] or [[declare]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state]], [[argue]], [[maintain]], [[declare]], [[swear]], [[pronounce]], [[affirm]], [[profess]]
**2** to [[insist upon]] (one's rights, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insist upon]], [[stress]], [[defend]], [[uphold]], [[put forward]], [[press]], [[stand up for]]
**3** **assert oneself** to speak and act forcefully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[assert yourself]]**: be forceful, put your foot down (*informal*), put yourself forward, make your presence felt, exert your influence
