**acceptable** *adj*
**1** able to be endured; [[tolerable]]: in war killing is acceptable
**2** [[good enough]]; [[adequate]]: he found the article acceptable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satisfactory]], [[fair]], [[all right]], [[suitable]], [[sufficient]], [[good enough]], [[adequate]], [[tolerable]]
 **[[acceptability]]** *n* 
 **[[acceptably]]** *adv*
