**lap**^3 *vb* **lapping**, **lapped**
**1** (of small waves) to [[wash]] against (the shore or a boat) with light splashing sounds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ripple]], [[wash]], [[splash]], [[swish]], [[gurgle]], [[slosh]], [[purl]], [[plash]]
**2** (often foll. by *up*) (esp. of animals) to scoop (a liquid) into the mouth with the tongue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drink]], [[sip]], [[lick]], [[swallow]], [[gulp]], sup
 *n*
**3** the act or sound of lapping
 See also **lap up**
