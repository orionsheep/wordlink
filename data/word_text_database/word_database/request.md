**request** *vb*
**1** to [[ask]] for or politely demand: we requested a formal meeting with the committee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seek]], [[ask]] (for), solicit
 *n*
**2** the act or an instance of [[asking]] for something: a polite request
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[asking]], [[plea]]
**3** something asked for
**4** **on request** if asked for: most companies will send samples on request
