**fountain** *n*
**1** an ornamental feature in a pool or lake consisting of a [[jet]] of water forced into the air by a pump
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[font]], [[spring]], [[reservoir]], [[spout]], [[fount]], [[water feature]], [[well]]
**2** a [[jet]] or [[spray]] of water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jet]], [[stream]], [[spray]], [[gush]]
**3** a natural [[spring]] of water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[font]], [[spring]], [[reservoir]], [[spout]], [[fount]], [[water feature]], [[well]]
**4** a cascade of sparks, lava, etc.
