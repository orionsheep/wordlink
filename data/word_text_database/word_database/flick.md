**flick** *vb*
**1** to [[touch]] or move with the finger or hand in a quick jerky movement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strike]], [[tap]], [[remove quickly]], [[hit]], [[touch]], [[stroke]], [[flip]], [[whisk]]
**2** to move with a short sudden movement, often repeatedly: the windscreen wipers flicked back and forth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jerk]], [[pull]], [[tug]], [[lurch]], [[jolt]]
**3** **flick through** to look at (a book or magazine) quickly or idly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[flick through something]]**: browse, glance at, skim, leaf through, [[flip]] through, thumb through, skip through
 *n*
**4** a [[tap]] or quick [[stroke]]
**5** **give the flick** (*informal*, *chiefly* *Austral*) to dismiss or reject
