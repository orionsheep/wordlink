**dunk** *vb*
**1** to dip (a biscuit or piece of bread) in a drink or soup before eating it
**2** to put (something) in liquid: dunk the garment in the dye for 15 minutes
**3** (*basketball*) to drop (the ball) through the hoop after having leapt high enough to have the hands above the rim
 *n*
**4** (*basketball*) a scoring shot in which one player drops the ball through the hoop after having leapt high enough to have the hands above the rim
