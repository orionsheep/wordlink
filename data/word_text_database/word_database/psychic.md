**psychic** *adj*
**1** relating to or having powers (esp. [[mental]] powers) which cannot be explained by natural laws
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supernatural]], [[mystic]], [[occult]]
**2** relating to the mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[psychological]], [[emotional]], [[mental]], [[spiritual]], [[inner]], [[psychiatric]], cognitive
 *n*
**3** a person who has psychic powers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clairvoyant]], [[fortune teller]]
 **[[psychical]]** *adj*
