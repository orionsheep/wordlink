**doctrine** (**dock**-trin) *n*
**1** a body of teachings of a religious, political, or philosophical group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teaching]], [[principle]], [[belief]], [[opinion]], [[conviction]], [[creed]], [[dogma]], [[tenet]], [[kaupapa]] (*NZ*)
**2** a [[principle]] or body of principles that is taught or advocated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teaching]], [[principle]], [[belief]], [[opinion]], [[conviction]], [[creed]], [[dogma]], [[tenet]], [[kaupapa]] (*NZ*) 
 **[[doctrinal]]** *adj*
