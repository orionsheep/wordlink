**award** *vb*
**1** to [[give]] (something) for merit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[present with]], [[give]], [[grant]], [[hand out]], [[confer]], [[endow]], [[bestow]]
**2** (*law*) to declare to be entitled, such as by decision of a court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grant]], [[give]], [[confer]]
 *n*
**3** something awarded, such as a [[prize]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prize]], [[gift]], [[trophy]], [[decoration]], [[grant]], [[bonsela]] (*S African*), [[koha]] (*NZ*)
**4** (*law*) the decision of an arbitrator or court
