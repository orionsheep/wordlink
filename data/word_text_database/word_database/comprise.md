**comprise** *vb* **-prising**, **-prised**
**1** to be made up of: the group comprised six French diplomats, five Italians, and three Bulgarians
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be composed of]], [[include]], [[contain]], [[consist of]], [[take in]], [[embrace]], [[encompass]]
**2** to [[form]] or [[make up]]: women comprised 57 per cent of all employees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make up]], [[form]], [[constitute]], [[compose]]
