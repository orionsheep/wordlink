**hardly** *adv*
**1** [[scarcely]]; [[barely]]: he'd hardly sipped his whisky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[barely]], [[only just]], [[scarcely]], [[just]], [[with difficulty]], [[with effort]]
**2** (*humorous*) not at all: it was hardly in the Great Train Robbery league
**3** [[with difficulty]]: their own families would hardly recognize them
