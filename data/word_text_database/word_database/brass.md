**brass** *n*
**1** an alloy of copper and zinc
**2** an object, ornament, or utensil made of brass
**3**
**A** the large family of wind instruments including the trumpet, trombone, etc. made of brass
 **B** instruments of this family forming a section in an orchestra
**4** same as **top brass**
**5** (*N English* *dialect*) money
**6** (*Brit*) an engraved brass memorial tablet in a church
**7** (*informal*) bold self-confidence; nerve
