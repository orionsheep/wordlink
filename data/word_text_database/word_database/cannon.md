**cannon** *n*, *pl* **-nons*** or ***-non**
**1** a large [[gun]] consisting of a metal tube mounted on a carriage, formerly used in battles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gun]], [[big gun]], [[field gun]], [[mortar]]
**2** an automatic aircraft [[gun]]
**3** (*billiards*) a shot in which the cue ball strikes two balls successively
 *vb*
**4** **cannon into** to collide with
