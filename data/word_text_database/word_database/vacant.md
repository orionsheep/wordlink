**vacant** *adj*
**1** (of a toilet, room, etc.) [[unoccupied]] or not being used: I sat down in a vacant chair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empty]], [[free]], [[available]], [[abandoned]], [[deserted]], [[for sale]], [[on the market]], [[void]]
**2** (of a job or position) [[unfilled]] at the present time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unfilled]], [[unoccupied]]
**3** having or suggesting a lack of interest or understanding: he sat there staring at me with a vacant look
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blank]], [[vague]], [[dreamy]], [[empty]], [[abstracted]], [[idle]], [[vacuous]], [[inane]]
**4** (of a period of time) not set aside for any particular activity: two slots in his programme have been left vacant
 **[[vacantly]]** *adv*
