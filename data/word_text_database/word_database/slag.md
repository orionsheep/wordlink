**slag** *n*
**1** the waste material left after metal has been smelted
**2** (*Brit** & **NZ* *derogatory*, *slang*) a woman considered sexually immoral
 *vb* **[[slagging]]**, **slagged**
**3** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *slang*) (often foll. by *off*) to criticize in an unpleasant way: I don't think anyone can slag it off

 **[[slaggy]]** *adj*
