**bastard** *n*
**1** (*informal*, *derogatory*) an obnoxious or despicable person
**2** (*old-fashioned*, *or* *offensive*) a person born of parents not married to each other
**3** (*informal*) something extremely difficult or unpleasant
 *adj*
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *or* *offensive*) illegitimate by birth
**5** counterfeit; spurious
 **[[bastardy]]** *n*
