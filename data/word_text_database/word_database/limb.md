**limb** *n*
**1** an [[arm]], [[leg]], or [[wing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[member]], [[arm]], [[leg]], [[wing]], [[extremity]], [[appendage]]
**2** any of the main branches of a tree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[branch]], [[spur]], [[projection]], [[offshoot]], [[bough]]
**3** **out on a limb**
**A** in a precarious or questionable position
 **B** (*Brit** & **NZ*) isolated, esp. because of unpopular opinions
 **[[limbless]]** *adj*
