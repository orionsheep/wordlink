**custom** *n*
**1** a long-established activity, action, or festivity: the custom of serving port after dinner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tradition]], [[practice]], [[convention]], [[ritual]], [[policy]], [[rule]], [[usage]], [[kaupapa]] (*NZ*)
**2** the long-established habits or traditions of a society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tradition]], [[practice]], [[convention]], [[ritual]], [[policy]], [[rule]], [[usage]], [[kaupapa]] (*NZ*)
**3** a usual [[practice]] or [[habit]]: she held his hand more tightly than was her custom in public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[habit]], [[way]], [[practice]], [[procedure]], [[routine]], [[wont]]
**4** regular use of a shop or [[business]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[customers]], [[business]], [[trade]], patronage
 *adj*
**5** made to the specifications of an individual customer: a custom car; custom-tailored suits
