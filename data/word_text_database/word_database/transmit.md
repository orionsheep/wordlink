**transmit** *vb* **-mitting**, **-mitted**
**1** to pass (something, such as a message or disease) from one place or person to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pass on]], [[carry]], [[spread]], [[send]], [[bear]], [[transfer]], [[hand on]], [[convey]]
**2**
**A** to [[send out]] (signals) by means of [[radio]] waves
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[broadcast]], [[televise]], [[relay]], [[air]], [[radio]], [[send out]], [[disseminate]], [[beam out]], [[stream]], [[podcast]]
 **B** to [[broadcast]] (a [[radio]] or television programme)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[broadcast]], [[televise]], [[relay]], [[air]], [[radio]], [[send out]], [[disseminate]], [[beam out]], [[stream]], [[podcast]]
**3** to allow the passage of (particles, energy, etc.): water transmits sound better than [[air]]
**4** to [[transfer]] (a force, motion, etc.) from one part of a mechanical system to another: the chain of the bike transmits the motion of the pedals to the rear wheel
 **[[transmittable]]** *adj*
