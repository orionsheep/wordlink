**robot** *n*
**1** a [[machine]] programmed to perform specific tasks in a human manner, esp. one with a human shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[machine]], [[automaton]], [[android]], mechanical man *or* woman
**2** a person of [[machine]]-like efficiency
**3** (*S African*) a set of traffic lights
 **[[robotic]]** *adj*
