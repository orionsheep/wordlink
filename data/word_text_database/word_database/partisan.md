**partisan** *n*
**1** a person who supports a particular cause or party
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[devotee]], [[adherent]], [[upholder]]
**2** a member of an armed resistance group within occupied territory
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[underground fighter]], [[guerrilla]], [[freedom fighter]], resistance fighter
 *adj*
**3** [[prejudiced]] or [[one-sided]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prejudiced]], [[one-sided]], [[biased]], [[partial]], [[sectarian]]
 **[[partisanship]]** *n*
