**removal** *n*
**1** the act of removing or state of being removed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dismissal]], [[expulsion]], [[elimination]], [[ejection]]
**2** the process of moving one's possessions from a previous address to a new one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[move]], [[transfer]], [[departure]], [[relocation]], [[flitting]] (*Scot** & **N English* *dialect*)
