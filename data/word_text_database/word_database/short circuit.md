**short circuit** *n*
**1** a faulty or accidental connection in an electric circuit, which deflects current through a path of low resistance, usually causing the failure of the circuit
 *vb* **short-circuit**
**2** to develop a short circuit
**3** to bypass (a procedure): she wrote to them direct and short-circuited the job agency
**4** to hinder or frustrate (a plan)
