**future** *n*
**1** the time yet [[to come]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[time to come]], [[hereafter]], [[what lies ahead]]
**2** undetermined events that will occur in that time
**3** the condition of a person or thing at a [[later]] date
**4** prospects: he had faith in its future
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prospect]], [[expectation]], [[outlook]]
**5** (*grammar*) a tense of verbs used when the action specified has not yet taken place
**6** **in future** from now on
 *adj*
**7** that is yet [[to come]] or be
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forthcoming]], [[coming]], [[later]], [[approaching]], [[to come]], [[succeeding]], [[fated]], [[subsequent]]
**8** of or expressing time yet [[to come]]
**9** destined to become
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forthcoming]], [[coming]], [[later]], [[approaching]], [[to come]], [[succeeding]], [[fated]], [[subsequent]]
**10** (*grammar*) in or denoting the future as a tense of verbs
