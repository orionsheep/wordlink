**minimum** *n*, *pl* **-mums*** or ***-ma**
**1** the [[least possible]] amount, degree, or quantity: fry the burgers in the minimum of oil
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lowest]], [[least]], [[lowest level]], [[nadir]]
**2** the [[least]] amount recorded, allowed, or reached: soak the beans for a minimum of eight hours
 *adj*
**3** of, being, or showing a minimum or minimums: the minimum age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lowest]], [[smallest]], [[least]], [[slightest]], [[minimal]], [[least possible]]
