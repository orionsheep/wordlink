**salute** *vb* **-luting**, **-luted**
**1** to [[greet]] with friendly words or gestures of respect, such as bowing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[greet]], [[welcome]], [[acknowledge]], [[address]], [[hail]], [[mihi]] (*NZ*)
**2** to [[acknowledge]] with praise: the statement salutes the changes of the past year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[honour]], [[acknowledge]], [[recognize]], pay tribute *or* homage to
**3** (*military*) to pay formal respect to (someone) by raising the right hand to the forehead
 *n*
**4** the act of saluting as a formal military gesture of respect
**5** the act of firing guns as a military greeting of [[honour]]
