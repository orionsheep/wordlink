**duel** *n*
**1** a formal [[fight]] between two people using guns, swords, or other weapons to settle a quarrel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[single combat]], affair of honour
 *vb* **duelling**, **duelled** (*US*) **dueling**, **[[dueled]]**
**2** to [[fight]] in a duel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[struggle]], [[clash]], [[compete]], [[contest]], [[contend]], [[vie with]], [[lock horns]]
 **[[duellist]]** * or * (*US*) **[[duelist]]** *n*
