**sale** *n*
**1** the exchange of goods or property for an agreed sum of money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[selling]], [[marketing]], [[dealing]], [[transaction]], [[disposal]]
**2** the amount sold
**3** an event at which goods are sold at reduced prices
**4** an [[auction]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[auction]], [[fair]], [[mart]], [[bazaar]]
**5** **sales** the department [[dealing]] with [[selling]] its company's products
