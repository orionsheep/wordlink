**outrageous** *adj*
**1** unusual and [[shocking]]: his sense of humour made him say and do the most outrageous things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[atrocious]], [[shocking]], [[terrible]], [[offensive]], [[appalling]], [[cruel]], [[savage]], [[horrifying]]
**2** [[shocking]] and socially or morally unacceptable: I will fight these outrageous accusations of corruption in the courts if necessary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unreasonable]], [[unfair]], [[steep]] (*informal*), [[shocking]], [[extravagant]], [[scandalous]], [[preposterous]], [[unwarranted]]
 **[[outrageously]]** *adv*
