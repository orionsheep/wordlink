**scatter** *vb*
**1** to [[throw about]] in various directions: scatter some oatmeal on top of the cake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throw about]], [[spread]], [[sprinkle]], [[strew]], [[shower]], [[fling]], [[diffuse]], [[disseminate]]
**2** to separate and move in various directions; [[disperse]]: the infantry were scattering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disperse]], [[dispel]], [[disband]], dissipate
 *n*
**3** the act of scattering
**4** a number of objects scattered about
