**miniature** *n*
**1** a model or representation on a very [[small]] scale
**2** a very [[small]] painting, esp. a portrait
**3** a very [[small]] bottle of whisky or other spirits, which can hold 50 millilitres
**4** **in miniature** on a [[small]] scale
 *adj*
**5** much smaller than usual; [[small]]-scale
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[small]], [[little]], [[minute]], [[tiny]], [[toy]], [[scaled-down]], [[diminutive]], [[minuscule]]
 **[[miniaturist]]** *n*
