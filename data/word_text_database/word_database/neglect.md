**neglect** *vb*
**1** to fail to give due care or attention to: she had neglected her child
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disregard]], [[ignore]], [[fail to look after]]
**2** to fail (to do something) through carelessness: he neglected to greet his guests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shirk]], [[forget]], [[overlook]], [[omit]], [[evade]], [[pass over]], [[skimp]], be remiss in *or* about
**3** to [[disregard]]: he neglected his duty
 *n*
**4** lack of due care or attention: the city had a look of shabbiness and neglect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[negligence]], [[inattention]]
**5** the state of being neglected
