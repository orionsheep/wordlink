**renounce** *vb* **-nouncing**, **-nounced**
**1** to [[give up]] (a belief or habit) voluntarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disown]], [[quit]], [[forsake]], [[recant]], [[forswear]], [[abjure]]
**2** to [[give up]] formally (a claim or right): he would renounce his rights to the throne
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disclaim]], [[deny]], [[give up]], [[relinquish]], [[waive]], [[abjure]]
