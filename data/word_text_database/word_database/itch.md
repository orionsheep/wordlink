**itch** *n*
**1** a skin [[irritation]] causing a [[desire]] to scratch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[irritation]], [[tingling]], [[prickling]], [[itchiness]]
**2** a restless [[desire]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desire]], [[longing]], [[craving]], [[passion]], [[yen]] (*informal*), [[hunger]], [[lust]], [[yearning]]
**3** any skin disorder, such as scabies, characterized by intense itching
 *vb*
**4** to feel an irritating or tickling sensation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prickle]], [[tickle]], [[tingle]]
**5** to have a restless [[desire]] (to do something): they were itching to join the fight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[long]], [[ache]], [[crave]], [[pine]], [[hunger]], [[lust]], [[yearn]], [[hanker]]
