**rhetoric** (**ret**-a-rik) *n*
**1** the art of using speech or writing to persuade or influence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oratory]], [[eloquence]], [[public speaking]], [[speech-making]], [[elocution]], [[declamation]], [[grandiloquence]], [[whaikorero]] (*NZ*)
**2** artificial or exaggerated language: there's been no shortage of soaring rhetoric at this summit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hyperbole]], [[bombast]], [[wordiness]], [[verbosity]], [[grandiloquence]], [[magniloquence]]
 **[[rhetorical]]** (rit-**tor**-ik-kl) *adj*
