**entertainment** *n*
**1** [[enjoyment]] and interest: a match of top-quality entertainment and goals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enjoyment]], [[fun]], [[pleasure]], [[leisure]], [[relaxation]], [[recreation]], [[amusement]]
**2** an act or [[show]] that entertains, or such acts and shows collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pastime]], [[show]], [[sport]], [[performance]], [[treat]], [[presentation]], [[leisure activity]]
