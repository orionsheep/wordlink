**engagement** *n*
**1** a business or social [[appointment]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appointment]], [[meeting]], [[interview]], [[date]], [[commitment]], [[arrangement]], [[rendezvous]]
**2** the period when a couple has agreed to get married but the wedding has not yet taken place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[betrothal]] (*old-fashioned*), [[marriage contract]], [[troth]] (*archaic*), [[agreement to marry]]
**3** a limited period of employment, esp. in the performing arts
**4** a [[battle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[battle]], [[fight]], [[conflict]], [[action]], [[struggle]], [[clash]], [[encounter]], [[combat]]
