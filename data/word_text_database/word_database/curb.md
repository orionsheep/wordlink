**curb** *n*
**1** something that restrains or holds back
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restraint]], [[control]], [[check]], [[brake]], [[limitation]], [[rein]], [[deterrent]], [[bridle]]
**2** a horse's bit with an attached chain or strap, used to [[check]] the horse
**3** a raised edge that strengthens or encloses
 *vb*
**4** to [[control]] or [[restrain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[control]], [[check]], [[restrict]], [[suppress]], [[inhibit]], [[hinder]], retard
 See also **kerb**
