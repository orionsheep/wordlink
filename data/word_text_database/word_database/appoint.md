**appoint** *vb*
**1** to [[assign]] officially to a job or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assign]], [[name]], [[choose]], [[commission]], [[select]], [[elect]], [[delegate]], [[nominate]]
**2** to [[fix]] or [[decide]] (a time or place for an event)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decide]], [[set]], [[choose]], [[establish]], [[fix]], [[arrange]], [[assign]], [[designate]]
**3** to equip or furnish: it was a beautifully appointed room with rows and rows of books
 **[[appointee]]** *n*
