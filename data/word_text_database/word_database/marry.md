**marry** *vb* **-ries**, **-rying**, **-ried**
**1** to take (someone) as one's partner in marriage
**2** to [[join]] or give in marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tie the knot]] (*informal*), [[wed]], [[get hitched]] (*slang*)
**3** Also: **marry up** to fit together or [[unite]]; [[join]]: their playing marries Irish traditional music and rock
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unite]], [[join]], [[link]], [[bond]], [[ally]], [[merge]], [[knit]], [[unify]]
