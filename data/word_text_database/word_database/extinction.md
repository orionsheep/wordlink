**extinction** *n*
**1** the [[dying out]] of a plant or animal species
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dying out]], [[destruction]], [[abolition]], [[oblivion]], [[extermination]], [[annihilation]], [[eradication]], [[obliteration]]
**2** the end of a particular way of life or type of activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dying out]], [[destruction]], [[abolition]], [[oblivion]], [[extermination]], [[annihilation]], [[eradication]], [[obliteration]]
