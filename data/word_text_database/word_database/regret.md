**regret** *vb* **-gretting**, **-gretted**
**1** to feel sorry or upset about
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mourn]], [[miss]], grieve for *or* over
**2** to express apology or distress about: we regret any misunderstanding caused
 *n*
**3** a feeling of repentance, guilt, or [[sorrow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sorrow]]
**4** **regrets** a polite expression of refusal: she had sent her regrets
 **[[regretful]]** *adj* 
 **[[regretfully]]** *adv* 
 **[[regrettable]]** *adj* 
 **[[regrettably]]** *adv*
