**psychological** *adj*
**1** relating to the mind or [[mental]] activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mental]], [[emotional]], [[intellectual]], [[inner]], [[cognitive]], [[cerebral]]
**2** relating to psychology
**3** having its origin in the mind: his backaches are purely psychological
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imaginary]], [[psychosomatic]], [[irrational]], [[unreal]], [[all in the mind]]
 **[[psychologically]]** *adv*
