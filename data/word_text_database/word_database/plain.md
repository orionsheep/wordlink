**plain** *adj*
**1** flat or smooth
**2** easily understood: he made it plain what he wanted from me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clear]], [[obvious]], [[patent]], [[evident]], [[visible]], [[distinct]], [[understandable]], [[manifest]]
**3** [[honest]] or [[blunt]]: the plain fact is that my mother has no time for me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[straightforward]], [[open]], [[direct]], [[frank]], [[blunt]], [[outspoken]], [[honest]], [[downright]]
**4** without adornment: a plain brown envelope
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unadorned]], [[simple]], [[basic]], [[severe]], [[bare]], [[stark]], [[austere]], [[spartan]], [[bare-bones]]
**5** not good-looking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ugly]], [[unattractive]], [[homely]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[unlovely]], [[unprepossessing]], [[not beautiful]], [[no oil painting]] (*informal*), [[ill-favoured]]
**6** (of fabric) without pattern or of [[simple]] weave
**7** lowly, esp. in social rank or education: the plain people of the nation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ordinary]], [[common]], [[simple]], [[everyday]], [[commonplace]], [[unaffected]], [[unpretentious]]
**8** (*knitting*) of or done in plain stitch
 *n*
**9** a level stretch of country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flatland]], [[plateau]], [[prairie]], [[grassland]], [[steppe]], [[veld]]
**10** a [[simple]] stitch in knitting made by passing the wool round the front of the needle
 *adv*
**11** clearly or simply: that's just plain stupid!
 **[[plainly]]** *adv* 
 **[[plainness]]** *n*
