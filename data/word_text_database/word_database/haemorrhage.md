**haemorrhage** * or * (*US*) **hemorrhage** (**hem**-or-ij) *n*
**1** heavy bleeding from ruptured blood vessels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drain]], [[outpouring]], rapid loss
 *vb* **-rhaging**, **-rhaged**
**2** to [[bleed]] heavily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drain]], [[bleed]] (*informal*), [[flow rapidly]]
