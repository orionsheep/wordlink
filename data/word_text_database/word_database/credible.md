**credible** *adj*
**1** capable of being believed; convincing: there is no credible evidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believable]], [[possible]], [[likely]], [[reasonable]], [[probable]], [[plausible]], [[conceivable]], [[imaginable]]
**2** [[trustworthy]] or [[reliable]]: the latest claim is the only one to involve a credible witness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reliable]], [[honest]], [[dependable]], [[trustworthy]], [[sincere]], [[trusty]]
 **[[credibility]]** *n*
