**specimen** *n*
**1** an individual or part regarded as typical of its group or class
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sample]], [[example]], [[model]], [[type]], [[pattern]], [[instance]], [[representative]], [[exemplification]]
**2** (*med*) a [[sample]] of tissue, blood, or urine taken for analysis
**3** (*informal*) a person: I'm quite a healthy specimen
