**elicit** *vb*
**1** to [[bring about]] (a response or reaction): her remarks elicited a sharp retort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring about]], [[cause]], [[derive]], [[bring out]], [[evoke]], [[give rise to]], [[draw out]], [[bring forth]]
**2** to [[draw out]] (information) from someone: a phone call elicited the fact that she had just awakened
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obtain]], [[extract]], [[exact]], [[evoke]], [[wrest]], [[draw out]], [[extort]]
