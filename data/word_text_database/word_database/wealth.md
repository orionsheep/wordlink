**wealth** *n*
**1** the state of being rich
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[riches]], [[fortune]], [[prosperity]], [[affluence]], [[money]], [[opulence]]
**2** a large amount of [[money]] and valuable material possessions: redistribution of wealth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[property]], [[capital]], [[fortune]]
**3** a great amount or number: a wealth of detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abundance]], [[plenty]], [[richness]], [[profusion]], [[fullness]], [[cornucopia]], [[copiousness]]
