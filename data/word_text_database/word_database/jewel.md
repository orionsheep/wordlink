**jewel** *n*
**1** a precious or semiprecious stone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gemstone]], [[gem]], [[ornament]], [[sparkler]] (*informal*), [[rock]] (*slang*)
**2** a person or thing regarded as precious or special: a fantastic little car, a real little jewel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[treasure]], [[wonder]], [[darling]], [[pearl]], [[gem]], [[paragon]], [[pride and joy]], [[taonga]] (*NZ*)
**3** a [[gemstone]] used as part of the machinery of a watch
