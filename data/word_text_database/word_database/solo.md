**solo** *n*, *pl* **-los**
**1** a piece of music or section of a piece of music for one performer: a trumpet solo
**2** any performance by an individual without assistance
**3** Also: **solo whist** a card game in which each person plays on his or her own
 *adj*
**4** performed by an individual without assistance: a solo dance
 *adv*
**5** by oneself; alone: to fly solo across the Atlantic
 **[[soloist]]** *n*
