**artificial** *adj*
**1** [[man-made]]; not occurring naturally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[synthetic]], [[manufactured]], [[plastic]] (*slang*), [[man-made]], [[non-natural]]
**2** made in imitation of a natural product: artificial flavourings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[synthetic]], [[manufactured]], [[plastic]] (*slang*), [[man-made]], [[non-natural]]
**3** not sincere
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insincere]], [[forced]], [[affected]], phoney *or* phony (*informal*), [[false]], [[contrived]], [[unnatural]], [[feigned]]
 **[[artificiality]]** *n* 
 **[[artificially]]** *adv*
