**pool**^2 *n*
**1** a shared [[fund]] of resources or workers: a typing pool
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supply]], [[reserve]], [[fall-back]]
**2** a billiard game in which all the balls are potted with the cue ball
**3** the combined stakes of those betting in many gambling games
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kitty]], [[bank]], [[fund]], [[stock]], [[store]], [[pot]], [[jackpot]], [[stockpile]]
**4** (*commerce*) a group of producers who agree to maintain output levels and high prices
 *vb*
**5** to put into a common [[fund]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[combine]], [[share]], [[merge]], [[put together]], [[amalgamate]], [[lump together]], [[join forces on]]
