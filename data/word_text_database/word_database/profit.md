**profit** *n*
**1** (*often pl*) money gained in business or trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[earnings]], [[return]], [[revenue]], [[gain]], [[yield]], [[proceeds]], [[receipts]], [[takings]]
**2** a [[benefit]] or [[advantage]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[benefit]], [[good]], [[use]], [[value]], [[gain]], [[advantage]], advancement
 *vb* **-iting**, **-ited**
**3** to [[gain]] a profit or [[advantage]]: we do not want to profit from someone else's problems
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[benefit]], [[help]], [[serve]], [[gain]], [[promote]], [[be of advantage to]]
