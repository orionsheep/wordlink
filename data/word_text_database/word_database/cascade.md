**cascade** *n*
**1** a [[waterfall]] or series of waterfalls over rocks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waterfall]], [[falls]], [[torrent]], [[flood]], [[shower]], [[fountain]], [[avalanche]], [[deluge]]
**2** something flowing or falling like a [[waterfall]]: a cascade of luxuriant hair
 *vb* **-cading**, **-caded**
**3** to [[flow]] or [[fall]] in a cascade: rays of sunshine cascaded down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[fall]], [[flood]], [[pour]], [[plunge]], [[surge]], [[spill]], [[tumble]]
