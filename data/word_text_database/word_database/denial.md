**denial** *n*
**1** a statement that something is not true
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[negation]], [[contradiction]], [[dissent]], [[retraction]], [[repudiation]]
**2** a [[rejection]] of a request
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refusal]], [[veto]], [[rejection]], [[prohibition]], [[rebuff]], [[repulse]]
**3** (*psychol*) a process by which painful thoughts are not permitted into the consciousness
