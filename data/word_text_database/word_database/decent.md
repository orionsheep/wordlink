**decent** *adj*
**1** conforming to an acceptable standard or quality: a decent living wage; she's made a few decent films
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satisfactory]], [[fair]], [[all right]], [[reasonable]], [[sufficient]], [[good enough]], [[adequate]], [[ample]]
**2** polite or [[respectable]]: he's a decent man
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[respectable]], [[pure]], [[proper]], [[modest]], [[chaste]], [[decorous]]
**3** [[fitting]] or [[proper]]: that's the decent thing to do
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proper]], [[becoming]], [[seemly]], [[fitting]], [[appropriate]], [[suitable]], [[respectable]], [[befitting]]
**4** conforming to conventions of sexual behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[respectable]], [[pure]], [[proper]], [[modest]], [[chaste]], [[decorous]]
**5** (*informal*) [[kind]]; [[generous]]: she was pretty decent to me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good]], [[kind]], [[friendly]], [[neighbourly]], [[generous]], [[helpful]], [[obliging]], [[accommodating]]
 **[[decently]]** *adv*
