**picket** *n*
**1** a person or group standing outside a workplace to dissuade strikebreakers from entering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protester]], [[demonstrator]], [[picketer]]
**2** a small unit of troops posted to give early warning of attack
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lookout]], [[watch]], [[guard]], [[patrol]], [[sentry]], [[sentinel]]
**3** a pointed [[stake]] that is driven into the ground to support a fence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stake]], [[post]], [[pale]], [[paling]], [[upright]], stanchion
 *vb* **-eting**, **-eted**
**4** to act as pickets outside (a workplace)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blockade]], [[boycott]], [[demonstrate outside]]
