**comb** *n*
**1** a toothed instrument for disentangling or arranging hair
**2** a tool or machine that cleans and straightens wool or cotton
**3** a fleshy serrated crest on the head of a domestic fowl
**4** a honeycomb
 *vb*
**5** to use a comb on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untangle]], [[arrange]], [[groom]], [[dress]]
**6** to [[search]] with great care: police combed the streets for the missing girl
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[search]], [[hunt through]], [[rake]], [[sift]], [[scour]], [[rummage]], [[ransack]], [[forage]], [[fossick]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
