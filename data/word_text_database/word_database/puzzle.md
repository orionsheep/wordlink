**puzzle** *vb* **-zling**, **-zled**
**1** to [[baffle]] or [[bewilder]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perplex]], [[confuse]], [[baffle]], [[stump]], [[bewilder]], [[confound]], [[mystify]], [[faze]]
**2** **puzzle out** to solve (a [[problem]]) by mental effort
**3** **puzzle over** to think deeply about in an attempt to understand: he [[puzzled]] over the squiggles and curves on the paper
 *n*
**4** a [[problem]] that cannot be easily solved
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mystery]], [[problem]], [[paradox]], [[enigma]], [[conundrum]]
**5** a toy, game, or [[question]] presenting a [[problem]] that requires skill or ingenuity for its solution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[riddle]], [[question]], [[conundrum]], [[poser]]
 **[[puzzlement]]** *n* 
 **[[puzzled]]** *adj* 
 **[[puzzler]]** *n* 
 **[[puzzling]]** *adj*
