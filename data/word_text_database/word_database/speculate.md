**speculate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to form opinions about something, esp. its future consequences, based on the information available; [[conjecture]]: it is too early to speculate about Jackie getting married
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conjecture]], [[consider]], [[wonder]], [[guess]], [[surmise]], [[theorize]], [[hypothesize]]
**2** to buy securities or property in the hope of selling them at a profit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gamble]], [[risk]], [[venture]], [[hazard]]
 **[[speculation]]** *n* 
 **[[speculative]]** *adj* 
 **[[speculator]]** *n*
