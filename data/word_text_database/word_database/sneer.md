**sneer** *n*
**1** a facial expression showing distaste or contempt, typically with a curled upper lip
**2** a remark showing distaste or contempt
 *vb*
**3** to make a facial expression of scorn or contempt
**4** to say (something) in a scornful manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[say contemptuously]], [[snigger]]
 **[[sneering]]** *adj*, *n*
