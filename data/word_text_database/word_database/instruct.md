**instruct** *vb*
**1** to [[order]] to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[order]], [[tell]], [[direct]], [[charge]] (*formal*), [[bid]], [[command]], [[mandate]], [[enjoin]]
**2** to [[teach]] (someone) how to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teach]], [[school]], [[train]], [[coach]], [[educate]], [[drill]], [[tutor]]
**3** to brief (a solicitor or barrister)
