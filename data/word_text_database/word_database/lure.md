**lure** *vb* **luring**, **lured**
**1** (sometimes foll. by *away* or *into*) to [[tempt]] or [[attract]] by the promise of reward
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tempt]], [[draw]], [[attract]], [[invite]], [[trick]], [[seduce]], [[entice]], allure
 *n*
**2** a person or thing that lures
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[temptation]], [[attraction]], [[incentive]], [[bait]], [[carrot]] (*informal*), [[inducement]], [[enticement]], [[allurement]]
**3** (*angling*) a brightly coloured artificial spinning [[bait]]
**4** (*falconry*) a feathered decoy to which small pieces of meat can be attached
