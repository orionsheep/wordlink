**career** *n*
**1** the series of jobs in a profession or [[occupation]] that a person has through his or her life: a career in child psychology
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occupation]], [[calling]], [[employment]], [[pursuit]], [[vocation]], [[livelihood]], [[life's work]]
**2** the part of a person's life spent in a particular [[occupation]] or type of work: a school career punctuated with exams
 *vb*
**3** to [[rush]] in an uncontrolled way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rush]], [[race]], [[speed]], [[tear]], [[dash]], [[barrel]] (along) (*informal*), [[bolt]], hurtle
 *adj*
**4** having chosen to dedicate his or her life to a particular [[occupation]]: a career soldier
