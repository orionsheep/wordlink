**devotion** *n*
**1** strong [[attachment]] to or [[affection]] for someone or something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[love]], [[passion]], [[affection]], [[attachment]], [[fondness]]
**2** religious zeal; [[piety]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worship]], [[reverence]], [[spirituality]], [[holiness]], [[piety]], [[godliness]], [[devoutness]]
**3** **devotions** [[religious observance]] or [[prayers]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prayers]], [[religious observance]], [[church service]], [[divine office]]
 **[[devotional]]** *adj*
