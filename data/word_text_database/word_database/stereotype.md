**stereotype** *n*
**1** a set of characteristics or a fixed idea considered to represent a particular kind of person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[formula]], [[pattern]]
**2** an idea or convention that has grown stale through fixed usage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[formula]], [[pattern]]
 *vb* **-typing**, **-typed**
**3** to form a standard image or idea of (a type of person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[categorize]], [[typecast]], [[pigeonhole]], [[standardize]]
