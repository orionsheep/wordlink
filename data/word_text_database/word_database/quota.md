**quota** *n*
**1** the [[share]] that is due from, due to, or allocated to a person or group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share]], [[allowance]], [[ration]], [[part]], [[limit]], [[slice]], [[quantity]], [[portion]]
**2** the prescribed number or [[quantity]] allowed, required, or admitted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share]], [[allowance]], [[ration]], [[part]], [[limit]], [[slice]], [[quantity]], [[portion]]
