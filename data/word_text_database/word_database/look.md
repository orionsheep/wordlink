**look** *vb*
**1** (often foll. by *at*) to direct the eyes (towards): he turned to look at her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[see]], [[view]], [[consider]], [[watch]], [[eye]], [[study]], [[survey]], [[examine]]
**2** (often foll. by *at*) to [[consider]]: let's look at the issues involved
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consider]], [[contemplate]]
**3** to give the [[impression]] of being; [[seem]]: Luxembourg's timetable looks a winner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seem]], [[appear]], [[look like]], [[strike you as]]
**4** to [[face]] in a particular direction: Morgan's Rock looks south
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[face]], [[overlook]]
**5** (foll. by *for*) to [[search]] or [[seek]]: the department looks for reputable firms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[search]], [[seek]], [[hunt]], [[forage]], [[fossick]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**6** (foll. by *into*) to carry out an investigation
**7** to direct a look at (someone) in a specified way: she looks at Teresina suspiciously
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[see]], [[view]], [[consider]], [[watch]], [[eye]], [[study]], [[survey]], [[examine]]
**8** to match in [[appearance]] with (something): looking your best
**9** to [[expect]] or [[hope]] (to do something): we would look to derive a procedure that would account for most cases
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hope]], [[expect]], [[await]], [[anticipate]], [[reckon on]]
**10** **look alive *or* lively *or* sharp *or* smart** to hurry up; get busy
**11** **look here** an [[expression]] used to attract someone's attention or add emphasis to a statement
 *n*
**12** an instance of looking: a look of icy contempt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glimpse]], [[view]], [[glance]], [[observation]], [[sight]], [[examination]], [[gaze]], [[inspection]]
**13** a [[view]] or [[sight]] (of something): take a look at my [[view]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glimpse]], [[view]], [[glance]], [[observation]], [[sight]], [[examination]], [[gaze]], [[inspection]]
**14** (*often pl*) [[appearance]] to the [[eye]] or mind; [[aspect]]: I'm not happy with the look of things here; better than you by the looks of it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appearance]], [[bearing]], [[air]], [[style]], [[aspect]], [[manner]], [[expression]], [[impression]]
**15** [[style]] or fashion: the look made famous by the great Russian
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appearance]], [[bearing]], [[air]], [[style]], [[aspect]], [[manner]], [[expression]], [[impression]]
 *conj*
**16** an [[expression]] demanding attention or showing annoyance: look, I won't be coming back
