**source** *n*
**1** the [[origin]] or starting point: the source of discontent among fishermen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cause]], [[origin]], [[derivation]], [[beginning]], [[author]]
**2** any person, book, or organization that provides information for a news report or for research
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[informant]], [[authority]]
**3** the area or spring where a river or stream begins
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[origin]], fount
 *vb*
**4** to establish a supplier of (a product, etc)
**5** (foll. by *from*) to originate from
