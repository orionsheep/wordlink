**terrible** *adj*
**1** very [[serious]] or [[extreme]]: war is a terrible thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serious]], [[desperate]], [[severe]], [[extreme]], [[dangerous]], [[insufferable]]
**2** (*informal*) very [[bad]], unpleasant, or unsatisfactory: terrible books
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bad]], [[awful]], [[dreadful]], [[dire]], [[abysmal]], [[poor]], [[rotten]] (*informal*)
**3** causing fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[awful]], [[shocking]] (*informal*), [[terrifying]], [[horrible]], [[dreadful]], [[horrifying]], [[fearful]] (*informal*), [[horrendous]]
 **[[terribly]]** *adv*
