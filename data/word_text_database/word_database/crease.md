**crease** *n*
**1** a [[line]] made by folding or pressing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fold]], [[line]], [[ridge]], [[groove]], [[corrugation]]
**2** a [[wrinkle]] or furrow, esp. on the face
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrinkle]], [[line]], [[crow's-foot]]
**3** (*cricket*) any of four lines near each wicket marking positions for the bowler or batsman
 *vb* **creasing**, **creased**
**4** to make or become wrinkled or furrowed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crumple]], [[rumple]], [[fold]], [[double up]], [[corrugate]]
 **[[creasy]]** *adj*
