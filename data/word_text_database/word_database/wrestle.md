**wrestle** *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**1** to [[fight]] (someone) by grappling and trying to throw or pin him or her to the ground, often as a sport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[battle]], [[struggle]], [[combat]], [[grapple]], [[tussle]], [[scuffle]]
**2** **wrestle with** to [[struggle]] hard with (a person, problem, or thing): I wrestled with my conscience
 **[[wrestler]]** *n*
