**deceive** *vb* **-ceiving**, **-ceived**
**1** to [[mislead]] by lying
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take in]], [[trick]], [[fool]] (*informal*), [[cheat]], [[con]] (*informal*), [[mislead]], [[dupe]], [[swindle]], [[scam]] (*slang*)
**2** **deceive oneself** to refuse to acknowledge something one knows to be true
**3** to be unfaithful to (one's sexual partner)
