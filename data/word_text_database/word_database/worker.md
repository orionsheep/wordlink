**worker** *n*
**1** a person who works in a specified way: a hard worker
**2** a person who works at a specific job: a government worker
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[employee]], [[hand]], [[labourer]], workman *or* woman *or* person, craftsman *or* woman *or* person, [[artisan]], [[tradesperson]]
**3** an [[employee]], as opposed to an employer
**4** a sterile female bee, ant, or wasp, that works for the colony
