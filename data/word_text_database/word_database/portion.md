**portion** *n*
**1** a [[part]] of a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[bit]], [[piece]], [[section]], [[scrap]], [[segment]], [[fragment]], [[chunk]]
**2** a [[part]] belonging to a person or group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share]], [[allowance]], [[lot]], [[measure]], [[quantity]], [[quota]], [[ration]], [[allocation]]
**3** a [[helping]] of food served to one person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[helping]], [[serving]], [[piece]], [[plateful]]
**4** (*law*) a dowry
**5** (*literary*) someone's fate or destiny: utter disaster was my portion
 *vb*
**6** to divide (something) into shares
