**bronze** *n*
**1** an alloy of [[copper]] and smaller proportions of tin
**2** a statue, medal, or other object made of bronze
 *adj*
**3** made of or resembling bronze
**4** [[yellowish-brown]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[yellowish-brown]], [[reddish-brown]], [[copper]], [[tan]], [[rust]], [[chestnut]], brownish
 *vb* **bronzing**, **[[bronzed]]**
**5** (esp. of the skin) to make or become brown; [[tan]]
