**between** *prep*
**1** at a point intermediate to two other points in space, time, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amidst]], [[among]], [[mid]], [[in the middle of]], [[betwixt]]
**2** in combination; together: between them, they saved enough money to buy a car
**3** confined to: between you and me
**4** indicating a linking relation or comparison
**5** indicating alternatives, strictly only two alternatives
 *adv* *also* **in between**
**6** between one specified thing and another
