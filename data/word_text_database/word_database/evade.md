**evade** *vb* **evading**, **evaded**
**1** to [[get away from]] or [[avoid]] (imprisonment, captors, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[avoid]], [[escape]], [[dodge]], [[get away from]], [[elude]], [[steer clear of]], [[sidestep]], [[duck]] (*informal*)
**2** to get around, shirk, or [[dodge]] (the law, a duty, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[avoid]], [[escape]], [[dodge]], [[get away from]], [[elude]], [[steer clear of]], [[sidestep]], [[duck]] (*informal*)
**3** to [[avoid answering]] (a question)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[avoid answering]], [[parry]], [[fend off]], [[fudge]], [[hedge]], [[equivocate]]
