**possess** *vb*
**1** to [[have]] as one's property; [[own]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[own]], [[have]], [[hold]], [[be in possession of]], [[be the owner of]], [[have in your possession]]
**2** to [[have]] as a quality or attribute: he possessed an innate elegance, authority, and wit on screen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be endowed with]], [[have]], [[enjoy]], [[benefit from]], [[be possessed of]], [[be gifted with]]
**3** to gain [[control]] over or [[dominate]]: absolute terror possessed her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seize]], [[hold]], [[control]], [[dominate]], [[occupy]], [[take someone over]], [[have power over]], [[have mastery over]]
 **[[possessor]]** *n*
