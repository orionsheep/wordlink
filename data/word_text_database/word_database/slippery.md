**slippery** *adj*
**1** liable or tending to cause objects to slip: the road was slippery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smooth]], [[icy]], [[greasy]], [[glassy]], slippy (*informal*, *dialect*), [[unsafe]]
**2** liable to slip from one's grasp: a bar of slippery soap
**3** not to be trusted: slippery politicians
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untrustworthy]], [[tricky]], [[cunning]], [[dishonest]], [[devious]], [[crafty]], [[evasive]], [[shifty]] (*informal*) 
 **[[slipperiness]]** *n*
