**gloomy** *adj* **gloomier**, **gloomiest**
**1** despairing or [[sad]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[miserable]], [[sad]], [[pessimistic]], [[melancholy]], [[glum]], [[dejected]], [[dispirited]], [[downcast]]
**2** causing depression or gloom: gloomy economic forecasts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depressing]], [[bad]], [[dreary]], [[sombre]], [[dispiriting]], [[disheartening]], [[cheerless]]
**3** [[dark]] or [[dismal]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dark]], [[dull]], [[dim]], [[dismal]], [[black]], [[grey]], [[murky]], [[dreary]]
 **[[gloomily]]** *adv*
