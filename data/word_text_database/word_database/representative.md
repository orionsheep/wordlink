**representative** *n*
**1** a person chosen to act for or represent a group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delegate]], [[member]], [[agent]], [[deputy]], [[proxy]], spokesman *or* woman *or* person
**2** a person who tries to sell the products or services of a firm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agent]], [[salesperson]], [[rep]], [[commercial traveller]]
**3** a [[typical]] example
 *adj*
**4** [[typical]] of a class or kind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[typical]], [[characteristic]], [[archetypal]], [[exemplary]]
**5** representing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[symbolic]]
**6** including examples of all the interests or types in a group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[typical]], [[characteristic]], [[archetypal]], [[exemplary]]
**7** acting as [[deputy]] for another
**8** of a political system in which people choose a person to make decisions on their behalf
