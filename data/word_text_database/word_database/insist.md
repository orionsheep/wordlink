**insist** *vb* (often foll. by *on* or *upon*)
**1** to make a determined [[demand]] (for): he insisted on his rights
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[demand]], [[order]], [[require]], [[command]], [[dictate]], [[entreat]]
**2** to express a convinced belief (in) or assertion (of): she insisted that she had been given permission
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assert]], [[state]], [[maintain]], [[claim]], [[declare]], [[repeat]], [[vow]], [[swear]]
