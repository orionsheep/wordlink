**bandage** *n*
**1** a piece of material used to [[dress]] a wound or wrap an injured limb
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dressing]], [[plaster]], [[compress]], gauze
 *vb* **-daging**, **-daged**
**2** to [[cover]] or wrap with a bandage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dress]], [[cover]], [[bind]], [[swathe]]
