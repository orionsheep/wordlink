**blink** *vb*
**1** to close and immediately reopen (the eyes), usually involuntarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flutter]], [[wink]], [[bat]]
**2** to shine intermittently or unsteadily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flash]], [[flicker]], [[wink]], [[shimmer]], [[twinkle]], glimmer
 *n*
**3** the act or an instance of blinking
**4** a glance; glimpse
**5** **[[on the blink]]** (*slang*) not working properly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on the blink]]**: not working (properly), faulty, defective, playing up, out of action, malfunctioning, out of order
