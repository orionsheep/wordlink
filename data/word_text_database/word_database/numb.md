**numb** *adj*
**1** deprived of feeling through cold, shock, or fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unfeeling]], [[dead]], [[frozen]], [[paralysed]], [[insensitive]], [[deadened]], [[immobilized]], [[torpid]]
**2** unable to move; [[paralysed]]
 *vb*
**3** to make numb
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stun]], [[knock out]], [[paralyse]], [[daze]]
 **[[numbly]]** *adv* 
 **[[numbness]]** *n*
