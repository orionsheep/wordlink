**wonder** *vb*
**1** to [[think]] about something with [[curiosity]] or doubt: I wonder why she did that
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[question]], [[puzzle]], [[speculate]], [[query]], [[ponder]], [[meditate]], [[conjecture]]
**2** to [[be amazed]]: I did wonder at her leaving valuable china on the shelves
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be amazed]], [[stare]], [[marvel]], [[be astonished]], gape
 *n*
**3** something that causes [[surprise]] or [[awe]]: it's a wonder she isn't speechless with fright
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[phenomenon]], [[sight]], [[miracle]], [[spectacle]], [[curiosity]], [[marvel]], [[prodigy]], [[rarity]]
**4** the feeling of [[surprise]] or [[awe]] caused by something strange: the wonder of travel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amazement]], [[surprise]], [[admiration]], [[awe]], [[fascination]], [[astonishment]], [[bewilderment]], [[wonderment]]
**5** **do *or* work wonders** to achieve spectacularly good results
**6** **no *or* small wonder** it is not surprising: no wonder you're going broke
 *adj*
**7** causing [[surprise]] or [[awe]] because of spectacular results achieved: a new wonder drug for treating migraine
 **[[wonderingly]]** *adv* 
 **[[wonderment]]** *n*
