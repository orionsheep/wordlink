**bruise** *vb* **bruising**, **bruised**
**1** to [[injure]] (body tissue) without breaking the skin, usually with [[discoloration]], or (of body tissue) to be injured in this way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hurt]], [[injure]], [[mark]]
**2** to [[hurt]] (someone's feelings)
**3** to [[damage]] (fruit)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[mark]], [[mar]], discolour
 *n*
**4** a bodily [[injury]] without a break in the skin, usually with [[discoloration]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discoloration]], [[mark]], [[injury]], [[blemish]], [[contusion]] (*formal*)
