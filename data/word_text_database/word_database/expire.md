**expire** *vb* **-piring**, **-pired**
**1** to [[finish]] or [[run out]]; come to an [[end]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[become invalid]], [[end]], [[finish]], [[conclude]], [[close]], [[stop]], [[run out]], [[cease]]
**2** to breathe out air
**3** to [[die]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[die]], [[depart]], [[perish]], [[kick the bucket]] (*informal*), [[depart this life]], [[meet your maker]], [[cark it]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*), pass away *or* on
