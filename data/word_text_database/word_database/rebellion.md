**rebellion** *n*
**1** organized opposition to a government or other authority involving the use of violence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resistance]], [[rising]], [[revolution]], [[revolt]], [[uprising]], [[mutiny]]
**2** nonviolent opposition to a government or other authority: a Tory backbenchers' rebellion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonconformity]], [[defiance]], [[heresy]], [[schism]]
**3** rejection of accepted conventions of behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonconformity]], [[defiance]], [[heresy]], [[schism]]
