**parody** *n*, *pl* **-dies**
**1** a piece of music or literature that mimics the style of another composer or author in a humorous way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[takeoff]] (*informal*), [[satire]], [[caricature]], [[send-up]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[spoof]] (*informal*), [[skit]], [[burlesque]], [[piss-take]] (*informal*)
**2** something done so badly that it seems like an intentional mockery
 *vb* **-dies**, **-dying**, **-died**
**3** to make a parody of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take off]] (*informal*), [[caricature]], [[send up]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[burlesque]], [[satirize]], [[do a takeoff of]] (*informal*) 
 **[[parodist]]** *n*
