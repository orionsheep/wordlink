**tradition** *n*
**1** the handing down from generation to generation of [[customs]], beliefs, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[customs]], [[institution]], [[ritual]], [[folklore]], [[lore]], [[tikanga]] (*NZ*)
**2** the unwritten body of beliefs, [[customs]], etc. handed down from generation to generation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[customs]], [[institution]], [[ritual]], [[folklore]], [[lore]], [[tikanga]] (*NZ*)
**3** a [[custom]] or practice of long standing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[established practice]], [[custom]], [[convention]], [[habit]], [[ritual]]
**4** **in the tradition of** having many features similar to those of a person or thing in the past: a thriller writer in the tradition of Chandler
