**delicate** *adj*
**1** [[fine]] or [[subtle]] in quality or workmanship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fine]], [[elegant]], [[exquisite]], [[graceful]]
**2** having a [[fragile]] beauty
**3** (of colour, smell, or taste) pleasantly [[subtle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[subtle]], [[fine]], [[delicious]], [[faint]], [[refined]], [[understated]], [[dainty]]
**4** easily damaged; [[fragile]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fragile]], [[weak]], [[frail]], [[brittle]], [[tender]], [[flimsy]], [[dainty]], [[breakable]]
**5** precise or [[sensitive]] in action: the delicate digestive system
**6** requiring tact: a delicate matter
**7** showing consideration for the feelings of other people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diplomatic]], [[sensitive]], [[thoughtful]], [[discreet]], [[considerate]], [[tactful]]
 **[[delicately]]** *adv*
