**classification** *n*
**1** placing things systematically in categories
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[categorization]], [[grading]], [[taxonomy]], [[sorting]], [[analysis]], [[arrangement]], [[profiling]]
**2** a [[division]] or category in a classifying system
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[class]], [[grouping]], [[heading]], [[sort]], [[department]], [[type]], [[division]], [[section]]
 **[[classificatory]]** *adj*
