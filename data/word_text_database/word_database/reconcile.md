**reconcile** *vb* **-ciling**, **-ciled**
**1** to make (two apparently conflicting things) compatible or consistent with each other: in many cases science and religion are reconciled
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resolve]], [[settle]], [[square]], [[adjust]], [[compose]], [[rectify]], [[put to rights]]
**2** to re-establish friendly relations with (a person or people) or between (people)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reunite]], [[bring back together]], [[conciliate]]
**3** to accept or cause to accept an unpleasant situation: we reconciled ourselves to a change
