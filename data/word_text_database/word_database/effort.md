**effort** *n*
**1** physical or mental [[energy]] needed to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exertion]], [[work]], [[trouble]], [[energy]], [[struggle]], [[application]], [[graft]], [[toil]]
**2** a determined [[attempt]] to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attempt]], [[try]], [[endeavour]], [[shot]] (*informal*), [[bid]], [[essay]] (*formal*), [[go]] (*informal*), [[stab]] (*informal*)
**3** an achievement or creation: his earliest literary efforts
 **[[effortless]]** *adj* 
 **[[effortlessly]]** *adv*
