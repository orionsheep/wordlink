**unity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state of being one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wholeness]], [[integrity]], [[oneness]], [[union]], [[entity]], [[singleness]]
**2** mutual [[agreement]]: unity of intention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[accord]], [[consensus]], [[harmony]], [[solidarity]], [[unison]], [[assent]], [[concord]]
**3** the state of being a single thing that is composed of separate parts, organizations, etc.: moves towards church unity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[union]], [[unification]], [[coalition]], [[federation]], [[integration]], [[confederation]], [[amalgamation]]
**4** (*maths*) the number or numeral one
