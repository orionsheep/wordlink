**perimeter** (per-**rim**-it-er) *n*
**1** (*maths*)
**A** the curve or line enclosing a plane area
 **B** the length of this curve or line
**2** any [[boundary]] around something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boundary]], [[edge]], [[border]], [[bounds]], [[limit]], [[margin]], [[confines]], [[periphery]]
