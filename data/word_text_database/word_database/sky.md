**sky** *n*, *pl* **skies**
**1** the upper atmosphere as seen from earth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heavens]], [[firmament]], [[rangi]] (*NZ*)
**2** **praise to the skies** praise rather excessively
 *vb* **skies**, **skying**, **skied**
**3** (*informal*) to hit (a ball) high in the air: the blond-haired forward skied the ball high over the bar
