**contradict** *vb*
**1** to declare the opposite of (a statement) to be true
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[negate]], [[deny]], [[rebut]], [[controvert]]
**2** (of a fact or statement) to suggest that (another fact or statement) is wrong
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[deny]], [[challenge]], [[belie]], [[fly in the face of]], [[be at variance with]]
 **[[contradiction]]** *n*
