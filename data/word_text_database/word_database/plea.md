**plea** *n*
**1** an emotional [[appeal]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appeal]], [[request]], [[suit]], [[prayer]], [[petition]], [[entreaty]], [[intercession]], [[supplication]] (*formal*)
**2** (*law*) a statement by or on behalf of a defendant
**3** an [[excuse]]: his plea of poverty rings a little hollow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excuse]], [[defence]], [[explanation]], [[justification]]
