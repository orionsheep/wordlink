**devour** *vb*
**1** to [[eat]] up greedily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eat]], [[consume]], [[swallow]], [[wolf]], [[gulp]], [[gobble]], [[guzzle]], [[polish off]] (*informal*)
**2** to engulf and destroy
**3** to read avidly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enjoy]], [[take in]], read compulsively *or* voraciously
 **[[devouring]]** *adj*
