**prestige** *n*
**1** high [[status]] or respect resulting from success or achievements: a symbol of French power and prestige
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[status]], [[standing]], [[credit]], [[reputation]], [[honour]], [[importance]], [[fame]], [[distinction]], [[mana]] (*NZ*)
**2** the power to impress: a humdrum family car with no prestige
 **[[prestigious]]** *adj*
