**monarchy** *n*, *pl* **-chies**
**1** a form of government in which supreme authority is held by a single hereditary ruler, such as a king
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sovereignty]], [[autocracy]], [[kingship]], [[queenship]], [[royalism]], [[monocracy]]
**2** a country reigned over by a monarch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kingdom]], [[empire]], [[realm]], [[principality]]
