**mention** *vb*
**1** to [[refer to]] or speak about briefly or incidentally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refer to]], [[point out]], [[bring up]], [[state]], [[reveal]], [[declare]], [[disclose]], [[intimate]]
**2** to include in a report, list, etc., because of high standards or an outstanding achievement: the hotel is mentioned in all the guidebooks; he was twice mentioned in dispatches during the war
**3** **not to mention (something)** to say nothing of (something too obvious to mention)
 *n*
**4** a slight [[reference]] or [[allusion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reference]], [[observation]], [[indication]], [[remark]], [[allusion]]
**5** a [[recognition]] or [[acknowledgment]] of high quality or an outstanding achievement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acknowledgment]], [[recognition]], [[tribute]], [[citation]], [[honourable mention]]
