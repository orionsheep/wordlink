**clutter** *vb*
**1** to [[scatter]] objects about (a place) in an untidy manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[litter]], [[scatter]], [[strew]], [[mess]] up
 *n*
**2** an untidy heap or mass of objects
**3** a state of [[untidiness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untidiness]], [[mess]], [[disorder]], [[confusion]], [[litter]], [[muddle]], [[disarray]], [[jumble]]
