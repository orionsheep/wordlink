**maiden** *n*
**1** (*archaic*, *or* *literary*) a young [[unmarried]] [[girl]], esp. a [[virgin]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[girl]], maid (*archaic*, *literary*), [[lass]], damsel (*archaic*, *poetic*), [[virgin]], [[lassie]] (*informal*)
**2** (*horse racing*) a horse that has never won a race
 *adj*
**3** [[unmarried]]: a maiden aunt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmarried]], [[unwed]], [[single]]
**4** [[first]] or earliest: maiden voyage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[first]], [[initial]], [[inaugural]], [[introductory]]
 **[[maidenhood]]** *n* 
 **[[maidenly]]** *adj*
