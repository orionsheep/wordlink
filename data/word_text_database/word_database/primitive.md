**primitive** *adj*
**1** of or belonging to the beginning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[early]], [[first]], [[earliest]], [[original]], [[primary]], [[elementary]], [[primordial]], [[primeval]]
**2** (*biol*) of an [[early]] stage in development: primitive amphibians
**3** characteristic of an [[early]] [[simple]] state, esp. in being [[crude]] or basic: a primitive dwelling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crude]], [[simple]], [[rough]], [[rudimentary]], unrefined
 *n*
**4** a primitive person or thing
**5** a painter of any era whose work appears childlike or untrained
**6** a work by such an artist
