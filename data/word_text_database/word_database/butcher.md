**butcher** *n*
**1** a person who sells meat
**2** a person who kills animals for meat
**3** a brutal [[murderer]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[murderer]], [[killer]], [[slaughterer]], [[slayer]], [[destroyer]], [[executioner]], [[cut-throat]], exterminator
 *vb*
**4** to [[kill]] and [[prepare]] (animals) for meat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slaughter]], [[prepare]], [[carve]], [[cut up]], [[dress]], [[cut]], [[clean]], [[joint]]
**5** to [[kill]] (people) at random or brutally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kill]], [[slaughter]], [[massacre]], [[destroy]], [[cut down]], [[assassinate]], slay (*archaic*, *literary*), [[liquidate]]
**6** to make a mess of; botch
