**phase** *n*
**1** any distinct or characteristic [[stage]] in a sequence of events: two distinct phases in the singer's career
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stage]], [[time]], [[point]], [[position]], [[step]], [[development]], [[period]], [[chapter]]
**2** (*astron*) one of the recurring shapes of the portion of the moon, Mercury, or Venus illuminated by the sun
**3** (*physics*) a particular [[stage]] in a periodic process or phenomenon
**4** (*physics*) **in *or* out of phase** (of two waves or signals) reaching or not reaching corresponding phases at the same [[time]]
 *vb* **phasing**, **phased**
**5** to do or introduce gradually: the redundancies will be phased over two years
 See also **phase in**
