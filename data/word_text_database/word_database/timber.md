**timber** *n*
**1** [[wood]] as a building material
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wood]], [[logs]]
**2** trees collectively
**3** a wooden beam in the frame of a house, boat, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beams]], [[boards]], planks
 *adj*
**4** made out of timber: timber houses
**5** of or involved in the production or sale of [[wood]] as a building material: a timber merchant
 **[[timbered]]** *adj* 
 **[[timbering]]** *n*
