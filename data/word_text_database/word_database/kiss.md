**kiss** *vb*
**1** to [[touch]] with the lips as an expression of love, greeting, or respect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peck]] (*informal*), [[osculate]], [[neck]] (*informal*)
**2** to join lips with another person as an act of love or desire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peck]] (*informal*), [[osculate]], [[neck]] (*informal*)
**3** (*literary*) to [[touch]] lightly: a long high free kick that kissed the top of the crossbar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brush]], [[touch]], [[shave]], [[scrape]], [[graze]], [[glance off]], stroke
 *n*
**4** a caress with the lips
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peck]] (*informal*), [[snog]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[smacker]] (*slang*), [[French kiss]], [[osculation]]
**5** a light [[touch]]
 **[[kissable]]** *adj*
