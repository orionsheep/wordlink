**feather** *n*
**1** any of the flat light structures that form the plumage of birds, each consisting of a shaft with soft thin hairs on either side
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plume]]
**2** **feather in one's cap** a cause for pleasure at one's achievements
 *vb*
**3** to fit, cover, or supply with feathers
**4** (*rowing*) to turn an oar parallel to the water between strokes, in order to lessen wind resistance
**5** **feather one's nest** to collect possessions and money to make one's life comfortable, often dishonestly
 **[[feathered]]** *adj* 
 **[[feathery]]** *adj*
