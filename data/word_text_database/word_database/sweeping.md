**sweeping** *adj*
**1** affecting many people to a great extent: sweeping financial reforms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wide-ranging]], [[global]], [[comprehensive]], [[wide]], [[broad]], [[extensive]], [[all-inclusive]], [[all-embracing]]
**2** (of a statement) making general assumptions about an issue without considering the details
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indiscriminate]], [[blanket]], [[wholesale]], [[exaggerated]], [[overstated]], [[unqualified]]
**3** decisive or overwhelming: to suffer sweeping losses
**4** taking in a [[wide]] area: a sweeping view of the area
