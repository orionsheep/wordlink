**lover** *n*
**1** a person having a sexual relationship with another person outside marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sweetheart]], [[beloved]], [[loved one]], [[flame]] (*informal*), [[mistress]], [[admirer]], [[suitor]]
**2** (*often pl*) either of the people involved in a love affair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sweetheart]], [[beloved]], [[loved one]], [[flame]] (*informal*), [[mistress]], [[admirer]], [[suitor]]
**3** someone who loves a specified person or thing: an animal-lover
