**allowance** *n*
**1** an amount of money given at regular intervals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pocket money]], [[grant]], [[fee]], [[payment]], [[ration]], [[handout]], [[remittance]]
**2** (in Britain) an amount of a person's income that is not subject to income tax
**3** **make allowances for**
**A** to treat or judge someone less severely because he or she has special problems
 **B** to take into account in one's plans
