**alternative** *n*
**1** a possibility of [[choice]] between two or more things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substitute]], [[choice]], [[other]], [[option]], [[preference]], [[recourse]]
**2** either or any of such choices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substitute]], [[choice]], [[other]], [[option]], [[preference]], [[recourse]]
 *adj*
**3** presenting a [[choice]] between two or more possibilities
**4** of a lifestyle, etc. that is less conventional or materialistic than is usual
