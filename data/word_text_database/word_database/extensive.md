**extensive** *adj*
**1** covering a [[large]] area: extensive moorland
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[large]], [[considerable]], [[substantial]], [[spacious]], [[wide]], [[broad]], [[expansive]]
**2** very [[great]] in effect: the bomb caused extensive damage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[great]], [[vast]], [[widespread]], [[large-scale]], [[far-reaching]], [[far-flung]], [[voluminous]]
**3** containing many details, ideas, or items on a particular subject: an extensive collection of modern art
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comprehensive]], [[complete]], [[wide]], [[pervasive]]
 **[[extensively]]** *adv*
