**1** to [[inspect]] carefully or in detail; [[investigate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inspect]], [[study]], [[survey]], [[investigate]], [[explore]], [[analyse]], [[scrutinize]], [[peruse]]
**2** (*education*) to [[test]] the knowledge of (a candidate) in (a subject)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[test]], [[question]], [[assess]], [[quiz]], [[evaluate]], [[appraise]]
**3** (*med*) to [[investigate]] the state of health of (a patient)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[check]], [[analyse]], [[check over]]
**4** (*law*) to formally [[question]] (someone) on oath
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[question]], [[quiz]], [[interrogate]], [[cross-examine]], [[grill]] (*informal*), [[give the third degree to]] (*informal*)
