**interpret** *vb*
**1** to [[explain]] the meaning of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explain]], [[make sense of]], [[decode]], [[decipher]], [[elucidate]]
**2** to work out the significance of: his remarks were widely interpreted as a promise not to raise taxes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take]], [[understand]], [[explain]], [[construe]]
**3** to convey the meaning of (a poem, song, etc.) in performance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[portray]], [[present]], [[perform]], [[render]], [[depict]], [[enact]], [[act out]]
**4** to act as an interpreter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[translate]], [[transliterate]]
 **[[interpretive]]** *adj*
