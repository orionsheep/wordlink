**whatever** *pron*
**1** everything or anything that: I can handle whatever comes up
**2** no matter what: whatever you do, keep your temper
**3** (*informal*) other similar or related things: a block of wood, rock, or whatever
**4** an intensive form of *what*, used in questions: whatever gave you that impression?
 *adj*
**5** an intensive form of *what*: I can take whatever actions I deem necessary
**6** at all: there is no foundation whatever for such opinions
 *interj*
**7** an expression of indifference
