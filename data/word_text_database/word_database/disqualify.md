**disqualify** *vb* **-fies**, **-fying**, **-fied**
**1** to officially [[ban]] (someone) from doing something: he was disqualified from driving for ten years
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[rule out]], [[prohibit]], [[preclude]], [[debar]], [[declare ineligible]]
**2** to make ineligible, as for entry to an examination
 **[[disqualification]]** *n*
