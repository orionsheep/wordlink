**glitter** *vb*
**1** (of a surface) to reflect light in bright flashes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shine]], [[flash]], [[sparkle]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[shimmer]], [[twinkle]], [[glint]]
**2** (of light) to be reflected in bright flashes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shine]], [[flash]], [[sparkle]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[shimmer]], [[twinkle]], [[glint]]
**3** to be brilliant in a showy way: she glitters socially
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shine]], [[flash]], [[sparkle]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[shimmer]], [[twinkle]], [[glint]]
 *n*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sparkle]], [[flash]], [[shine]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[sheen]], [[shimmer]], [[brightness]]
