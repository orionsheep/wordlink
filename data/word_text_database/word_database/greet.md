**greet** *vb*
**1** to address or [[meet]] with expressions of friendliness or [[welcome]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[welcome]], [[meet]], [[receive]], [[karanga]] (*NZ*), [[mihi]] (*NZ*), [[haeremai]] (*NZ*)
**2** to [[receive]] in a specified manner: a direct request would be greeted coolly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[receive]], [[take]], [[respond to]], [[react to]]
**3** to be immediately noticeable to: the scene of devastation which greeted him
