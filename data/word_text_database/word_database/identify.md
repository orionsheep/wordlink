**identify** *vb* **-fies**, **-fying**, **-fied**
**1** to prove or [[recognize]] as being a certain person or thing; determine the identity of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recognize]], [[place]], [[name]], [[remember]], [[spot]], [[diagnose]], [[make out]], [[pinpoint]]
**2** (often foll. by *with*) to understand and sympathize with a person or group because one regards oneself as being similar or similarly situated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[identify with someone]]**: relate to, respond to, feel for, empathize with
**3** to consider or treat as the same
**4** to connect or associate closely: he was closely identified with the community charge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[identify something or someone with something or someone]]**: equate with, associate with
 **[[identifiable]]** *adj* 
 **[[identification]]** *n*
