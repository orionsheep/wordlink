**stifle** *vb* **-fling**, **-fled**
**1** to [[stop]] oneself from expressing (a yawn or cry)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[suppress]], [[repress]], [[smother]]
**2** to [[stop]] (something) from continuing: the new leadership stifled all internal debate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suppress]], [[repress]], [[stop]], [[check]], [[silence]], [[restrain]], [[hush]], [[smother]]
**3** to feel discomfort and difficulty in breathing
**4** to kill (someone) by preventing him or her from breathing
