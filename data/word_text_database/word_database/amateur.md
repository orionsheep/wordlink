**amateur** *n*
**1** a person who engages in a sport or other activity as a pastime rather than as a profession
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonprofessional]], [[outsider]], [[layperson]], layman *or* woman, [[dilettante]], [[non-specialist]], [[dabbler]]
**2** a person unskilled in a subject or activity
 *adj*
**3** doing something out of interest, not for money
**4** amateurish
 **[[amateurism]]** *n*
