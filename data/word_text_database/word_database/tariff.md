**tariff** *n*
**1**
**A** a [[tax]] levied by a government on imports or occasionally exports
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tax]], [[duty]], [[toll]], [[levy]], excise
 **B** a list of such taxes
**2** a list of fixed prices, for example in a hotel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[price list]], [[schedule]]
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a method of charging for services such as gas and electricity by setting a price per unit
