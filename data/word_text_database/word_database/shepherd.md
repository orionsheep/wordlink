**shepherd** *n*
**1** a person employed to tend sheep
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drover]], [[stockman]], herdsman *or* woman (*Brit*), [[herder]], [[grazier]]
**2** (*Christianity*) a member of the clergy when considered as the moral and spiritual [[guide]] of the people in the parish
 *vb*
**3** to [[guide]] or watch over (people)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[guide]], [[conduct]], [[steer]], [[herd]], [[usher]]
 **[[shepherdess]]** *fem n*
