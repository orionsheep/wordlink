**functional** *adj*
**1** of or performing a function
**2** [[practical]] rather than decorative
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[practical]], [[utilitarian]], [[serviceable]], [[hard-wearing]], [[useful]]
**3** in [[working]] order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[working]], [[operative]], [[operational]], [[going]], [[prepared]], [[ready]], [[viable]], [[up and running]]
**4** (*med*) affecting a function of an organ without structural change
 **[[functionally]]** *adv*
