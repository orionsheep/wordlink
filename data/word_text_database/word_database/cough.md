**cough** *vb*
**1** to expel air abruptly and noisily from the lungs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clear your throat]], [[bark]], [[hack]] (*informal*)
**2** (of an engine or other machine) to make a sound similar to this
 *n*
**3** an act or sound of coughing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: frog *or* tickle in your throat, [[bark]], [[hack]] (*informal*)
**4** an illness which causes frequent coughing
