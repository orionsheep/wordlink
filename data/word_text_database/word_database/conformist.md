**conformist** *adj*
**1**
**A** (of a person) behaving or thinking like most other people rather than in an original or unconventional way: a shy and conformist type of boy
 **B** (of an organization or society) expecting everyone to behave in the same way: the school was a dull, conformist place for staff and students alike
 *n*
**2** a person who behaves or thinks like most other people rather than in an original or unconventional way
