**image** *n*
**1** a [[mental picture]] of someone or something produced by the imagination or memory
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thought]], [[idea]], [[vision]], [[concept]], [[impression]], [[perception]], [[mental picture]], [[conceptualization]]
**2** the appearance or [[impression]] given to the public by a person or organization
**3** a simile or metaphor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[figure of speech]]
**4** a representation of a person or thing in a work of art or literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[figure]], [[idol]], [[icon]], [[fetish]], [[talisman]], [[avatar]]
**5** an optical [[reproduction]] of an object, formed by the lens of an eye or camera, or by a mirror
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reflection]], [[likeness]], [[mirror image]]
**6** a person or thing that resembles another closely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[replica]], [[copy]], [[reproduction]], [[counterpart]], [[clone]], [[facsimile]], [[spitting image]] (*informal*), [[Doppelgänger]]
**7** a personification of a specified quality; epitome: the image of good breeding
 *vb* **imaging**, **imaged**
**8** to picture in the mind
**9** to mirror or reflect an image of
**10** to portray or describe
