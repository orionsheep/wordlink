**blunt** *adj*
**1** (esp. of a knife) lacking sharpness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[rounded]], [[dulled]], [[edgeless]], [[unsharpened]]
**2** not having a sharp edge or point: a blunt instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[rounded]], [[dulled]], [[edgeless]], [[unsharpened]]
**3** (of people, manner of speaking, etc.) [[straightforward]] and uncomplicated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frank]], [[forthright]], [[straightforward]], [[rude]], [[outspoken]], [[bluff]], [[brusque]], plain-spoken
 *vb*
**4** to make less sharp
**5** to diminish the sensitivity or perception of: prison life has blunted his mind
 **[[bluntly]]** *adv*
