**twitch** *vb*
**1** (of a person or part of a person's body) to move in a jerky spasmodic way: his left eyelid twitched involuntarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jerk]], [[flutter]], [[jump]], [[squirm]]
**2** to [[pull]] (something) with a quick jerky movement: she twitched the curtains shut
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pull]] (at), [[tug]] (at), [[pluck]] (at), yank (at)
 *n*
**3** a sharp jerking movement, esp. one caused by a nervous condition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jerk]], [[tic]], [[spasm]], [[jump]], [[flutter]]
