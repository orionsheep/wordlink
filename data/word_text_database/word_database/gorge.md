**gorge** *n*
**1** a deep narrow steep-sided valley
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ravine]], [[canyon]], [[pass]], [[chasm]], [[cleft]], [[fissure]], [[defile]], [[gulch]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**2** **one's gorge rises** one feels disgusted or nauseated
 *vb* **gorging**, **gorged**
**3** Also: **gorge oneself** to eat greedily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stuff]], [[feed]], [[cram]], [[glut]]
