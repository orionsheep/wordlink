**insulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to prevent or reduce the transfer of electricity, heat, or sound by surrounding or lining with a nonconducting material
**2** to [[isolate]] or set apart
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[isolate]], [[protect]], [[screen]], [[defend]], [[shelter]], [[shield]], [[cut off]], [[cushion]]
 **[[insulator]]** *n*
