◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moral code]], [[standards]], [[principles]], [[morals]], [[conscience]], [[morality]], [[moral values]], [[moral principles]], [[tikanga]] (*NZ*)
**2** the moral fitness of a decision, course of action, etc.
 *n*
**3** the study of the moral value of human conduct
