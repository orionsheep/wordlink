**scheme** *n*
**1** a systematic [[plan]] for a course of action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[programme]], [[strategy]], [[system]], [[project]], [[proposal]], [[tactics]]
**2** a systematic arrangement of parts or features: colour scheme
**3** a secret [[plot]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[ploy]], [[ruse]], [[intrigue]], [[conspiracy]], [[manoeuvre]], [[subterfuge]], [[stratagem]]
**4** a chart, diagram, or outline
**5** a [[plan]] formally adopted by a government or organization: a pension scheme
 *vb* **[[scheming]]**, **schemed**
**6** to [[plan]] in an underhand manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[plan]], [[intrigue]], [[manoeuvre]], [[conspire]], [[contrive]], [[collude]], [[machinate]]
 **[[schemer]]** *n* 
 **[[scheming]]** *adj*, *n*
