**hunt** *vb*
**1** to seek out and kill (animals) for food or sport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stalk]], [[track]], [[chase]], [[pursue]], [[trail]], [[hound]]
**2** **hunt down** to [[track]] in an attempt to capture (someone): [[hunting]] down villains
**3** **hunt for** to [[search]] for: Western companies are [[hunting]] for opportunities to invest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hunt for something or someone]]**: [[search]] for, look for, seek for, forage for, scour for, fossick for (*Austral** & **NZ*), ferret about for
 *n*
**4** the act or an instance of [[hunting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[search]], [[hunting]], [[investigation]], [[chase]], [[pursuit]], [[quest]]
**5** a party organized for the [[pursuit]] of wild animals for sport
**6** the members of such a party
 **[[hunting]]** *n*
