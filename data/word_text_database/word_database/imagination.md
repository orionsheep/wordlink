**imagination** *n*
**1** the faculty or action of producing mental images of what is not present or in one's experience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mind's eye]], fancy (*old-fashioned*, *literary*)
**2** creative mental ability
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creativity]], [[vision]], [[invention]], [[ingenuity]], [[enterprise]], [[originality]], [[inventiveness]], [[resourcefulness]]
