**wax**^2 *vb*
**1** to [[increase]] gradually in size, strength, or power: trading has waxed and waned with the economic cycle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[grow]], [[develop]], [[expand]], [[swell]], [[enlarge]], [[magnify]]
**2** (of the moon) to show a gradually increasing area of brightness from new moon until full moon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[become fuller]], [[enlarge]]
**3** to become: he waxed eloquent on the disadvantages of marriage
