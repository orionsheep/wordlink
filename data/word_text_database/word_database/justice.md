**justice** *n*
**1** the quality of being just
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fairness]], [[equity]], [[integrity]], [[honesty]], [[decency]], [[rightfulness]], [[right]]
**2** the administration of law according to prescribed and accepted principles
**3** a [[judge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[judge]], [[magistrate]], [[beak]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[His]], Her *or* Your Honour
**4** **bring to justice** to capture, try, and punish (a criminal)
**5** **do justice to** to show to full advantage: the choir was not able to do justice to Handel's music
