**frank** *adj*
**1** honest and [[straightforward]] in speech or attitude
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[candid]], [[open]], [[direct]], [[straightforward]], [[blunt]], [[sincere]], [[outspoken]], honest
 *vb*
**2** to put a mark on (a letter), ensuring free carriage
 *n*
**3** an official mark stamped to a letter ensuring free delivery
 **[[frankly]]** *adv* 
 **[[frankness]]** *n*
