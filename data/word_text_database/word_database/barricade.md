**barricade** *n*
**1** a [[barrier]], esp. one erected hastily for defence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[barrier]], [[wall]], [[fence]], [[blockade]], [[obstruction]], [[rampart]], [[bulwark]], palisade
 *vb* **-cading**, **-caded**
**2** to erect a barricade across (an entrance)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bar]], [[block]], [[defend]], [[secure]], [[lock]], [[bolt]], [[blockade]], [[fortify]]
