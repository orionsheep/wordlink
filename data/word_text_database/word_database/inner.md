**inner** *adj*
**1** happening or located [[inside]] or further [[inside]]: the door to the inner office
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inside]], [[internal]], [[interior]], [[inward]]
**2** of the mind or spirit: her inner self
**3** exclusive or private: the inner sanctum of the party secretariat
**4** more profound; less apparent: the inner meaning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hidden]], [[deep]], [[secret]], [[underlying]], [[obscure]], [[repressed]], unrevealed
 *n*
**5** (*archery*)
**A** the red innermost ring on a target
 **B** a shot which hits this ring
