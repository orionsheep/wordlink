**while** *conj*
**1** at the same time that: anti-inflammatory remedies may be used to alleviate the condition while background factors are investigated
**2** at some point during the time that: her father had died while she was gone
**3** although or whereas: while she tossed and turned, he fell into a dreamless sleep
 *n*
**4** a period of time: I'd like to stay a while
