**clever** *adj*
**1** displaying sharp intelligence or mental alertness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intelligent]], [[bright]], [[talented]], [[gifted]], [[smart]], [[knowledgeable]], [[quick-witted]]
**2** [[skilful]] with one's hands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skilful]], [[talented]], [[gifted]]
**3** [[smart]] in a superficial way
**4** (*Brit* *informal*) sly or cunning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shrewd]], [[bright]] (*informal*), [[ingenious]], [[resourceful]], [[canny]]
 **[[cleverly]]** *adv* 
 **[[cleverness]]** *n*
