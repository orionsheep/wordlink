**individual** *adj*
**1** of, relating to, or meant for a single [[person]] or thing: small sums from individual donors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[independent]], [[isolated]], [[lone]], [[solitary]]
**2** [[separate]] or distinct from others of its kind: please mark the individual pages
**3** characterized by unusual and striking qualities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unique]], [[special]], [[fresh]], [[novel]], [[exclusive]], [[singular]], [[idiosyncratic]], unorthodox
 *n*
**4** a single [[person]], esp. when regarded as distinct from others: respect for the individual
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[person]], [[being]], [[human]], [[unit]], [[character]] (*informal*), [[soul]], [[creature]]
**5** (*informal*) a [[person]]: a most annoying individual
**6** (*biol*) a single animal or plant, esp. as distinct from a species
 **[[individually]]** *adv*
