**enough** *adj*
**1** as much or as many as necessary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sufficient]], [[adequate]], [[ample]], [[abundant]], [[as much as you need]], [[as much as is necessary]]
**2** **that's enough!** used to stop someone behaving in a particular way
 *pron*
**3** an [[adequate]] amount or number: I don't know enough about the subject to be able to speak about it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sufficiency]], [[plenty]], [[sufficient]], [[abundance]], [[adequacy]], [[right amount]], [[ample]] supply
 *adv*
**4** as much as necessary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sufficiently]], [[amply]], [[reasonably]], [[adequately]], [[satisfactorily]], [[abundantly]], [[tolerably]]
**5** fairly or quite: that's a common enough experience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sufficiently]], [[amply]], [[reasonably]], [[adequately]], [[satisfactorily]], [[abundantly]], [[tolerably]]
**6** very: used to give emphasis to the preceding word: funnily enough, I wasn't alarmed
**7** just [[adequately]]: he sang well enough
