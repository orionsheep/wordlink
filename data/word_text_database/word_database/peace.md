**peace** *n*
**1** [[stillness]] or [[silence]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stillness]], [[rest]], [[quiet]], [[silence]], [[calm]], [[hush]], [[tranquillity]], [[seclusion]]
**2** absence of mental anxiety: peace of mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serenity]], [[calm]], [[composure]], [[contentment]], [[repose]], [[equanimity]], [[peacefulness]], [[harmoniousness]]
**3** absence of war
**4** [[harmony]] between people or groups
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harmony]], [[accord]], [[agreement]], [[concord]]
**5** a [[treaty]] marking the end of a war
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[truce]], [[ceasefire]], [[treaty]], [[armistice]]
**6** law and order within a state: a breach of the peace
**7** **at peace**
**A** dead: the woman is at peace now
 **B** in a state of [[harmony]] or [[serenity]]
**8** **hold *or* keep one's peace** to keep silent
**9** **keep the peace** to maintain law and order
