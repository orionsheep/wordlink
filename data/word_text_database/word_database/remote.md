**1** [[far]] away
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[distant]], [[far]], [[isolated]], [[out-of-the-way]], [[secluded]], [[inaccessible]], [[in the middle of nowhere]]

◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[aloof]], [[cold]], [[reserved]], [[withdrawn]], [[distant]], [[abstracted]], [[detached]], [[uncommunicative]]
