**root**^2 *vb*
**1** (*Brit*) to [[dig]] up the earth in search of food, using the snout: dogs were rooting in the rushes for bones
**2** (*informal*) to search vigorously but unsystematically: she was rooting around in her large untidy purse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dig]], [[burrow]], [[ferret]]
