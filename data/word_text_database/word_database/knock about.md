**knock about** * or ***knock around** *vb*
**1** to wander or travel about: I have knocked about the world through three continents
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[knock about or around]]**: wander, travel, roam, rove, range, drift, stray, ramble, go walkabout (*Austral*)
**2** (foll. by *with*) to associate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[knock about or around with someone]]**: mix with, associate with, mingle with, consort with, hobnob with, socialize with, accompany
**3** to treat brutally: they used to knock me about a bit and try and make me cry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[knock someone about or around]]**: hit, attack, beat, strike, abuse, injure, assault, batter
**4** to consider or discuss informally
 *adj* **knockabout**
**5** (of comedy) lively, boisterous, and physical
