**debit** *n*
**1** the money, or a record of the money, withdrawn from a person's bank account
**2** (*accounting*)
**A** acknowledgment of a sum owing by entry on the left side of an account
 **B** an entry or the total of entries on this side
 *vb* **-iting**, **-ited**
**3** to charge (an account) with a debt: they had debited our account
**4** (*accounting*) to record (an item) as a debit in an account
