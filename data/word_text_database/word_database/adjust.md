**adjust** *vb*
**1** to [[adapt]] to a new environment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adapt]], [[change]], [[alter]], [[accustom]], [[conform]]
**2** to [[alter]] slightly, so as to be accurate or suitable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[modify]], [[alter]], [[adapt]]
**3** (*insurance*) to determine the amount payable in settlement of a claim
 **[[adjustable]]** *adj* 
 **[[adjuster]]** *n*
