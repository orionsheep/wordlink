**bold** *adj*
**1** [[courageous]], [[confident]], and [[fearless]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fearless]], [[enterprising]], [[brave]], [[daring]], [[heroic]], [[adventurous]], [[courageous]], [[audacious]]
**2** immodest or [[impudent]]: she gave him a bold look
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impudent]], [[forward]], [[confident]], [[rude]], [[cheeky]], [[feisty]] (*informal*), [[brazen]], [[shameless]], [[insolent]]
**3** (*Irish*) (of a child) naughty; badly behaved
**4** standing out distinctly; conspicuous: a figure carved in bold relief
 **[[boldly]]** *adv* 
 **[[boldness]]** *n*
