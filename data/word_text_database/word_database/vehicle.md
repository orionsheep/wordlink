**vehicle** *n*
**1** a [[machine]] such as a bus or car for transporting people or goods
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conveyance]] (*old-fashioned*), [[machine]], [[motor vehicle]]
**2** something used to achieve a particular purpose or as a [[means]] of expression: the newspaper was a vehicle for explaining government policies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[medium]], [[means]], [[channel]], [[mechanism]], [[organ]], [[apparatus]]
**3** (*pharmacol*) an inactive substance mixed with the active ingredient in a medicine
**4** a liquid, such as oil, in which a pigment is mixed before it is applied to a surface
 **[[vehicular]]** *adj*
