**uncertain** *adj*
**1** not able to be accurately known or predicted: an uncertain future
**2** not definitely decided: they are uncertain about the date
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsure]], [[undecided]], [[vague]], [[unclear]], [[dubious]], [[hazy]], [[irresolute]]
**3** not to be depended upon: an uncertain career
**4** changeable: an uncertain sky
 **[[uncertainty]]** *n*
