**jolly** *adj* **-lier**, **-liest**
**1** full of good humour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happy]], [[cheerful]], [[merry]], [[upbeat]], [[playful]], [[cheery]], [[genial]], [[chirpy]] (*informal*)
**2** involving a lot of fun: big jolly birthday parties
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happy]], [[cheerful]], [[merry]], [[upbeat]], [[playful]], [[cheery]], [[genial]], [[chirpy]] (*informal*)
 *adv*
**3** (*Brit* *informal*) very: I'm going to have a jolly good try
 *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
**4** **jolly along** (*informal*) to try to keep (someone) [[cheerful]] by flattery or [[cheerful]] chat
