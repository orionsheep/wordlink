**such** *adj*
**1** of the sort specified or understood: such places
**2** so great or so much: such a mess
 *adv*
**3** extremely: such a powerful friend
 *pron*
**4** a person or thing of the sort specified or understood: such is the law of the land; fruitcakes and puddings and such
**5** **as such** in itself or themselves: the Nordic countries are not lifting sanctions as such
**6** **such as** for example: other socialist groups, such as the Fabians
