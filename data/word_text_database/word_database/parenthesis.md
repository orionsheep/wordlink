**parenthesis** (par-**en**-thiss-iss) *n*, *pl* **-ses** (-seez)
**1** a word or phrase inserted into a passage, and marked off by brackets or dashes
**2** Also called: **bracket** either of a pair of characters (), used to enclose such a phrase
 **[[parenthetical]]** *adj* 
 **[[parenthetically]]** *adv*
