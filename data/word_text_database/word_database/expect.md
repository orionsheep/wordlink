**expect** *vb*
**1** to regard as likely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[believe]], [[suppose]], [[assume]], [[trust]], [[imagine]], [[reckon]] (*informal*), [[presume]]
**2** to [[look forward to]] or be waiting for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anticipate]], [[look forward to]], [[predict]], [[envisage]], [[await]], [[hope for]], [[contemplate]]
**3** to [[require]] (something) as an obligation: he expects an answer by January
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[require]], [[demand]], [[want]], [[call for]], [[ask for]], [[hope for]], [[insist on]]
**4** **be expecting** (*informal*) to be pregnant
