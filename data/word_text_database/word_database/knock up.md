**knock up** *vb*
**1** Also: **knock together** (*informal*) to make or assemble quickly: my boyfriend can knock up a wonderful lasagne
**2** (*Brit* *informal*) to waken: to knock someone up early
**3** (*slang*) to make pregnant
**4** to practise before a game of tennis, squash, or badminton
 *n* **knock-up**
**5** a practice session at tennis, squash, or badminton
