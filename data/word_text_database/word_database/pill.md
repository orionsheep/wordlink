**pill** *n*
**1** a small mass of medicine intended to be swallowed whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tablet]], [[capsule]], [[pellet]]
**2** **the pill** (*informal*) an oral contraceptive taken by a woman
**3** something unpleasant that must be endured: her reinstatement was a bitter pill to swallow; the pill was sweetened by a reduction in interest
