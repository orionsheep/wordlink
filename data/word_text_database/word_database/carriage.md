**carriage** *n*
**1** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) one of the sections of a train for passengers
**2** the way a person holds and moves his or her head and body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bearing]], [[posture]], [[gait]], [[deportment]], [[air]]
**3** a four-wheeled horse-drawn passenger [[vehicle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vehicle]], [[coach]], [[trap]], [[gig]], [[cab]], [[wagon]], [[hackney]], [[conveyance]] (*old-fashioned*)
**4** the moving part of a machine, such as a typewriter, that supports and shifts another part
**5** the charge made for conveying goods
