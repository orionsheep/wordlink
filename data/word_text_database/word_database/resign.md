**resign** *vb*
**1** to [[give up]] office or a job
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quit]], [[leave]], [[step down]] (*informal*), [[vacate]], [[abdicate]], give *or* hand in your notice
**2** to accept (an unpleasant fact): he resigned himself to the inevitable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[resign yourself to something]]**: accept, succumb to, submit to, give in to, [[yield]] to, acquiesce to
**3** to [[give up]] (a right or claim)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give up]], [[abandon]], [[yield]], [[surrender]], [[relinquish]], [[renounce]], [[forsake]], [[forgo]]
