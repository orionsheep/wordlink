**merge** *vb* **merging**, **merged**
**1** to [[combine]], esp. so as to become part of a larger whole: the two airlines merged in 1983
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[combine]], [[blend]], [[fuse]], [[amalgamate]], [[unite]], [[join]], [[mix]], [[mingle]]
**2** to [[blend]] gradually, without any sudden change being apparent: late afternoon merged imperceptibly into early evening
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[melt]], [[blend]], [[mingle]]
