**nature** *n*
**1** the whole system of the existence, forces, and events of the physical [[world]] that are not controlled by human beings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creation]], [[world]], [[earth]], [[environment]], [[universe]], [[cosmos]], [[natural world]]
**2** fundamental or essential qualities: the theory and nature of science
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quality]], [[character]], [[make-up]], [[constitution]], [[essence]], [[complexion]]
**3** [[kind]] or [[sort]]: problems of a financial nature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kind]], [[sort]], [[style]], [[type]], [[variety]], [[species]], [[category]], [[description]]
**4** [[temperament]] or [[personality]]: an amiable and pleasant nature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[temperament]], [[character]], [[personality]], [[disposition]], [[outlook]], [[mood]], [[humour]], [[temper]]
**5** **by nature** essentially: he was by nature a cautious man
**6** **in the nature of** essentially; by way of: it was in the nature of a debate rather than an argument
