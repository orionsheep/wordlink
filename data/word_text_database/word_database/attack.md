**attack** *vb*
**1** to launch a physical [[assault]] (against)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assault]], [[strike]] (at), [[mug]] (*informal*), [[ambush]], [[tear into]], [[set upon]], [[lay into]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[criticize]] vehemently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[criticize]], [[blame]], [[abuse]], [[condemn]], [[knock]] (*informal*), [[put down]], [[slate]] (*informal*), [[have a go]] (at) (*informal*)
**3** to set about (a job or problem) with vigour
**4** to affect adversely: BSE attacks the animal's brain
**5** to take the initiative in a game or sport
 *n*
**6** the act of attacking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assault]], [[charge]], [[campaign]], [[strike]], [[raid]], [[invasion]], [[offensive]], [[blitz]]
**7** any sudden appearance of a disease or symptoms: a bad attack of mumps
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bout]], [[fit]], [[stroke]], [[seizure]], [[spasm]], [[convulsion]], [[paroxysm]]
