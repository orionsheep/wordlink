**stimulus** (**stim**-myew-luss) *n*, *pl* **-li** (-lie)
**1** something that acts as an [[incentive]] to (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incentive]], [[spur]], [[encouragement]], [[impetus]], [[inducement]], [[goad]], [[incitement]], [[fillip]]
**2** something, such as a drug or electrical impulse, that is capable of causing a response in a person or an animal
