**landscape** *n*
**1** an extensive area of [[land]] regarded as being visually distinct
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scenery]], [[country]], [[view]], [[land]], [[scene]], [[prospect]], [[countryside]], [[outlook]]
**2** a painting, drawing, or photograph depicting natural [[scenery]]
 *vb* **-scaping**, **-scaped**
**3** to improve the natural features of (an area of [[land]])
 *adj*
**4** (of a page or screen) having greater width than height
