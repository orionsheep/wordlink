**kettle** *n*
**1** a metal container with a handle and spout, for boiling water
**2** any of various metal containers for heating liquid, cooking, etc.
**3** **a different kettle of fish** a different matter entirely
**4** **a fine kettle of fish** a difficult or awkward situation
 *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**5** (of police) to force (demonstrators) into an enclosed space
