**picturesque** *adj*
**1** visually pleasing, as in being [[striking]] or [[quaint]]: a small picturesque harbour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interesting]], [[pretty]], [[beautiful]], [[attractive]], [[charming]], [[scenic]], [[quaint]]
**2** (of language) [[graphic]] or [[vivid]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vivid]], [[striking]], [[graphic]], [[colourful]], [[memorable]]
