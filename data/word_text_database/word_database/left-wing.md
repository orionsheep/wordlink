**left-wing** *adj*
**1** [[socialist]] or [[radical]]: the party ditched many of its more left-wing policies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[socialist]], [[communist]], [[red]] (*informal*), [[radical]], [[revolutionary]], [[militant]], [[Bolshevik]], [[Leninist]]
**2** belonging to the more [[radical]] part of a political party: a group of left-wing Conservatives
 *n* **left wing**
**3** the more [[radical]] or progressive section, esp. of a political party: the left wing of the Labour Party
**4** (*sport*)
**A** the left-hand side of the field of play
 **B** a player positioned in this area in certain games
 **[[left-winger]]** *n*
