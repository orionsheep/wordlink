**ton** *n*
**1** (*Brit*) a unit of weight equal to 2240 pounds or 1016.046 kilograms
**2** (*US** & **Canad*) a unit of weight equal to 2000 pounds or 907.184 kilograms
**3** **come down on someone like a ton of bricks** to scold someone very severely
 *adv*
**4** **tons** a lot: I've got tons of things to do before going on holiday
