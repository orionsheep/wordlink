**hungry** *adj* **-grier**, **-griest**
**1** desiring food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[starving]], [[ravenous]], [[famished]], [[starved]], [[empty]] (*informal*), [[voracious]], peckish (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*)
**2** (foll. by *for*) having a [[craving]], desire, or need for: hungry for revenge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eager]], [[keen]], [[craving]], [[yearning]], [[greedy]], [[avid]], [[desirous]], [[covetous]]
**3** expressing greed, [[craving]], or desire: the media's hungry search for impact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eager]], [[keen]], [[craving]], [[yearning]], [[greedy]], [[avid]], [[desirous]], [[covetous]]
 **[[hungrily]]** *adv*
