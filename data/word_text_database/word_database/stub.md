**stub** *n*
**1** a short piece remaining after something has been used: a cigarette stub
**2** the section of a ticket or cheque which the purchaser keeps as a receipt
 *vb* **stubbing**, **stubbed**
**3** to strike (one's toe or foot) painfully against a hard surface
**4** **stub out** to put out (a cigarette or cigar) by pressing the end against a surface
