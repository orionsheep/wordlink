**cramp**^2 *vb*
**1** to confine or [[restrict]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrict]], [[hamper]], [[inhibit]], [[hinder]], [[handicap]], [[constrain]], [[obstruct]], [[impede]]
**2** **cramp someone's style** (*informal*) to prevent someone from impressing another person or from behaving naturally: shyness will cramp their style
