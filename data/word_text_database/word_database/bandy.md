**bandy** *adj* **-dier**, **-diest**
**1** Also: **bandy-legged** having legs curved outwards at the knees
**2** (of legs) curved outwards at the knees
 *vb* **-dies**, **-dying**, **-died**
**3** to exchange (words), sometimes in a heated manner
**4** **bandy about** to use (a name, term, etc.) frequently
