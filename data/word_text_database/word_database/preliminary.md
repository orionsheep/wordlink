**preliminary** *adj*
**1** occurring before or in preparation; introductory
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[first]], [[opening]], [[trial]], [[initial]], [[test]], [[pilot]], [[prior]], introductory
 *n*, *pl* **-naries**
**2** an action or event occurring before or in preparation for an activity: the discussions are a preliminary to the main negotiations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[introduction]], [[opening]], [[beginning]], [[start]], [[prelude]], [[preface]], [[overture]], [[preamble]]
**3** a qualifying contest held before a main competition
