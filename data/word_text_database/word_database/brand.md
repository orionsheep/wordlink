**brand** *n*
**1** a particular product or a characteristic that identifies a particular producer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trademark]]
**2** a trade name or [[trademark]]
**3** a particular kind or variety
**4** an identifying [[mark]] made, usually by burning, on the skin of animals as a proof of ownership
**5** an iron used for branding animals
**6** a [[mark]] of [[disgrace]]
**7** (*archaic*, *or* *poetic*) a flaming torch
 *vb*
**8** to [[label]], [[burn]], or [[mark]] with or as if with a brand
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[burn]], [[label]], [[stamp]], [[scar]]
**9** to [[label]] (someone): he was branded a war criminal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stigmatize]], [[mark]], [[expose]], [[denounce]], [[disgrace]], [[discredit]], [[censure]]
