**assembly** *n*, *pl* **-blies**
**1** a number of people gathered together for a [[meeting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gathering]], [[group]], [[meeting]], [[council]], [[conference]], [[crowd]], [[congress]], [[collection]], [[hui]] (*NZ*), [[runanga]] (*NZ*)
**2** a [[group]] of people who meet regularly to make decisions or laws for a particular region or country: the National Assembly for Wales
**3** the act of assembling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[putting together]], [[setting up]], [[construction]], [[building up]], [[connecting]], [[piecing together]]
