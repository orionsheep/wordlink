**wanting** *adj*
**1** [[lacking]]: I would be wanting in charity if I did not explain the terms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lacking]], [[missing]], [[absent]], [[incomplete]], [[short]], [[shy]]
**2** not meeting requirements or expectations: she compares herself to her sister and finds herself wanting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deficient]], [[poor]], [[inadequate]], [[insufficient]], [[faulty]], [[defective]], [[imperfect]], [[unsound]], bodger *or* bodgie (*Austral* *slang*)
