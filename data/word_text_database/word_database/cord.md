**cord** *n*
**1** [[string]] or thin [[rope]] made of twisted strands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rope]], [[line]], [[string]], [[twine]]
**2** (*anatomy*) a structure in the body resembling a [[rope]]: the vocal cords
**3** a ribbed fabric like corduroy
**4** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*) an electrical flex
**5** a unit for measuring cut wood, equal to 128 cubic feet
 *adj*
**6** (of fabric) ribbed
