**nut** *n*
**1** a dry one-seeded fruit that grows inside a hard shell
**2** the edible inner part of such a fruit
**3** a small piece of metal with a hole in it, that screws on to a bolt
**4** (*slang*) a foolish or eccentric person
**5** (*offensive*) a person with a mental illness
**6** (*slang*) an enthusiast: a health nut
**7** (*slang*) the [[head]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[head]], [[skull]]
**8** (*Brit*) a small piece of coal
**9** **a hard *or* tough nut to crack** a person or thing that presents difficulties
**10** **do one's nut** (*Brit** & **Austral* *slang*) to be very angry
