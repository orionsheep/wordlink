**omission** *n*
**1** something that has been left out or passed over
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gap]], [[space]], [[exclusion]], [[lacuna]]
**2** an act of missing out or failing to do something: we regret the omission of these and the names of the other fine artists
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exclusion]], [[removal]], [[elimination]], [[deletion]], [[excision]]
