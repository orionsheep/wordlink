**pity** *n*, *pl* **pities**
**1** sorrow felt for the sufferings of others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compassion]], [[charity]], [[sympathy]], [[kindness]], [[fellow feeling]]
**2** a cause of regret: it's a great pity he did not live longer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shame]], [[sin]] (*informal*), [[misfortune]], [[bummer]] (*slang*), [[crying shame]]
**3** **have *or* take pity on** to have [[sympathy]] or show mercy for
 *vb* **pities**, **[[pitying]]**, **pitied**
**4** to feel pity for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feel sorry for]], [[feel for]], [[sympathize with]], [[grieve for]], [[weep for]], [[bleed for]], [[have compassion for]]
 **[[pitying]]** *adj*
