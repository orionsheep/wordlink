**differential** *adj*
**1** of, relating to, or using a difference
**2** (*maths*) involving differentials
 *n*
**3** a factor that differentiates between two comparable things
**4** (*maths*) a minute difference between values in a scale
**5** (*chiefly* *Brit*) the difference between rates of pay for different types of labour, esp. within a company or industry
