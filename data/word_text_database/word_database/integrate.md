**integrate** *vb* **-grating**, **-grated**
**1** to make or be made into a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[join]], [[unite]], [[combine]], [[blend]], [[incorporate]], [[merge]], [[fuse]], [[assimilate]]
**2** to amalgamate (an ethnic or a religious group) with an existing community
**3** to designate (an institution) for use by all ethnicities or groups
**4** (*maths*) to determine the integral of a function or variable
 **[[integration]]** *n*
