**harbour** * or * (*US*) **harbor** *n*
**1** a sheltered [[port]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[port]], [[haven]], [[dock]], [[mooring]], [[marina]], [[pier]], [[wharf]], [[anchorage]]
**2** a place of refuge or safety
 *vb*
**3** to [[maintain]] secretly in the mind: he might be harbouring a death wish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold]], [[bear]], [[maintain]], [[nurse]], [[retain]], [[foster]], [[entertain]], [[nurture]]
**4** to give [[shelter]] or protection to: the government accused her of harbouring criminals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shelter]], [[protect]], [[hide]], [[shield]], [[provide refuge]], [[give asylum to]]
