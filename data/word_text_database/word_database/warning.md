**warning** *n*
**1** a hint, threat, or advance [[notice]] of a possible danger or problem
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[notice]], [[notification]], [[sign]], [[alarm]], [[announcement]], [[alert]], [[tip-off]] (*informal*), [[heads up]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**2** [[advice]] not to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[caution]], [[information]], [[advice]], [[injunction]], [[notification]]
 *adj*
**3** giving or serving as a warning: warning signs
 **[[warningly]]** *adv*
