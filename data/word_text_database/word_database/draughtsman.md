**draughtsman** * or * (*US*) **draftsman** *n*, *pl* **-men**
**1** a person employed to prepare detailed scale drawings of equipment, machinery, or buildings
**2** a person skilled in drawing
**3** (*Brit*) a flat disc used in the game of draughts. US and Canad equivalent: **checker**
 **[[draughtsmanship]]** *n*
