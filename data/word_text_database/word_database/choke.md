**choke** *vb* **choking**, **choked**
**1** to hinder or [[stop]] the breathing of (a person or animal) by strangling or smothering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strangle]], [[throttle]], [[asphyxiate]]
**2** to have trouble in breathing, swallowing, or speaking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suffocate]], [[stifle]], [[smother]], [[overpower]], [[asphyxiate]]
**3** to [[block]] or [[clog]] up: the old narrow streets become choked to a standstill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[block]], [[clog]], [[obstruct]], [[bung]], [[constrict]], [[congest]], [[stop]], [[bar]]
**4** to hinder the growth of: weeds would outgrow and choke the rice crop
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[block]], [[clog]], [[obstruct]], [[bung]], [[constrict]], [[congest]], [[stop]], [[bar]]
 *n*
**5** a device in a vehicle's engine that enriches the petrol-air mixture by reducing the air supply
