**shade** *n*
**1** relative darkness produced by blocking out sunlight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shadow]]
**2** a place sheltered from the sun by trees, buildings, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shadow]]
**3** something used to provide a [[shield]] or protection from a direct source of light, such as a lamp shade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[screen]], [[covering]], [[cover]], [[blind]], [[curtain]], [[shield]], [[veil]], [[canopy]]
**4** a [[shaded]] area in a painting or drawing
**5** any of the different hues of a [[colour]]: a much darker shade of grey
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hue]], [[tone]], [[colour]], [[tint]]
**6** a slight amount: a shade of reluctance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dash]], [[trace]], [[hint]], [[suggestion]]
**7** **put someone *or* something in the shade** to be so impressive as to make another person or thing seem unimportant by comparison
**8** (*literary*) a [[ghost]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ghost]], [[spirit]], [[phantom]], [[spectre]], [[apparition]], kehua (*NZ*)
 *vb* **shading**, **[[shaded]]**
**9** to [[screen]] or [[protect]] from heat or light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[protect]], [[screen]], [[hide]], [[shield]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]], [[veil]]
**10** to make darker or dimmer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[darken]], [[shadow]], [[cloud]], [[dim]]
**11** to represent (a darker area) in (a painting or drawing), by graded areas of [[tone]], lines, or dots
**12** to change slightly or by degrees
