**jack** *n*
**1** a mechanical device used to raise a motor vehicle or other heavy object
**2** a playing card with a picture of a pageboy on it
**3** (*bowls*) a small white bowl at which the players aim their bowls
**4** (*electrical engineering*) a socket into which a plug can be inserted
**5** a flag flown at the bow of a ship, showing nationality
**6** one of the pieces used in the game of jacks
**7** **every man jack** everyone without exception
 See also **jack up**
