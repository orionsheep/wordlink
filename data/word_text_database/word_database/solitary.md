**solitary** *adj*
**1** experienced or performed [[alone]]: a solitary dinner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lone]], [[alone]]
**2** living a life of solitude: a solitary child
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsociable]], [[reclusive]], [[unsocial]], [[isolated]], [[lonely]], [[cloistered]], [[lonesome]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[friendless]]
**3** single; [[alone]]: the solitary cigarette in the ashtray
**4** having few friends; [[lonely]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsociable]], [[reclusive]], [[unsocial]], [[isolated]], [[lonely]], [[cloistered]], [[lonesome]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[friendless]]
**5** (of a place) without people; empty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[isolated]], [[remote]], [[out-of-the-way]], [[hidden]], unfrequented
 *n*, *pl* **-taries**
**6** a person who lives on his or her own; hermit
 **[[solitariness]]** *n*
