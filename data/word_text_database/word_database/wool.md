**wool** *n*
**1** the soft curly [[hair]] of sheep and some other animals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fleece]], [[hair]], [[coat]]
**2** [[yarn]] spun from this, used in weaving and knitting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[yarn]]
**3** cloth made from this [[yarn]]
**4** **pull the wool over someone's eyes** to deceive someone
