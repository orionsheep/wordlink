**nail** *n*
**1** a piece of metal with a point at one end and a head at the other, hit with a [[hammer]] to join two objects together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tack]], [[spike]], [[rivet]], [[hobnail]], [[brad]] (*technical*)
**2** the hard covering of the upper tips of the fingers and toes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fingernail]], [[toenail]], [[talon]], [[thumbnail]], [[claw]]
**3** **hit the nail on the head** to say something exactly correct or accurate
**4** **on the nail** at once: he paid always in cash, always on the nail
 *vb*
**5** to [[attach]] (something) with nails
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fasten]], [[fix]], [[secure]], [[attach]], [[pin]], [[hammer]] (*informal*), [[tack]]
**6** (*informal*) to [[arrest]] or [[catch]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[catch]], [[arrest]], [[capture]], [[apprehend]], [[trap]], [[snare]], [[ensnare]], [[entrap]]
**7** (*informal*) to execute (an act or performance) to the highest level: you nailed that song!
