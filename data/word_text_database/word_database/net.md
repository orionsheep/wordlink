**net**^2 * or ***nett**^2 *adj*
**1** remaining after all deductions, as for taxes and expenses: net income
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[after taxes]], [[final]], [[clear]], [[take-home]]
**2** (of weight) excluding the weight of wrapping or container
**3** [[final]] or conclusive: the net effect
 *vb* **netting**, **netted**
**4** to yield or [[earn]] as a [[clear]] profit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[earn]], [[make]], [[clear]], [[gain]], [[realize]], [[bring in]], [[accumulate]], [[reap]]
