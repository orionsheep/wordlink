**crest** *n*
**1** the [[top]] of a mountain, hill, or wave
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[top]], [[summit]], [[peak]], [[ridge]], [[highest point]], [[pinnacle]], [[apex]], [[crown]]
**2** a [[tuft]] or growth of feathers or skin on the [[top]] of a bird's or animal's head
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tuft]], [[crown]], [[comb]], [[plume]], [[mane]]
**3** a heraldic design or figure used on a coat of arms and elsewhere
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[emblem]], [[badge]], [[symbol]], [[insignia]], [[bearings]], [[device]]
**4** an ornamental [[plume]] or [[emblem]] on [[top]] of a helmet
 *vb*
**5** to come or rise to a high point
**6** to lie at the [[top]] of
**7** to reach the [[top]] of (a hill or wave)
 **[[crested]]** *adj*
