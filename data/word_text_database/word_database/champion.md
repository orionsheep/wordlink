**champion** *n*
**1** a person, plant, or animal that has defeated all others in a competition: the Olympic 100 metres champion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[winner]], [[hero]], [[victor]], [[conqueror]], [[title holder]]
**2** someone who defends a person or cause: a champion of the downtrodden
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defender]], [[guardian]], [[patron]], [[backer]], [[protector]], upholder
 *vb*
**3** to [[support]]: he unceasingly championed equal rights and opportunities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[back]], [[defend]], [[promote]], [[advocate]], [[fight for]], [[uphold]], espouse
 *adj*
**4** (*N English* *dialect*) excellent
