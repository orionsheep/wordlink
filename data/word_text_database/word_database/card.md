**card** *n*
**1** a piece of stiff paper or thin cardboard used for reference, identification, or sending greetings or messages: a business card; a Christmas card
**2** one of a set of small pieces of cardboard, marked with figures or symbols, used for playing games or for fortune-telling
**3** a small rectangle of stiff plastic used for identification or for making financial transactions: a library card; a charge card
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *informal*) a witty or eccentric person
