**integrity** *n*
**1** [[honesty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[honesty]], [[principle]], [[honour]], [[virtue]], [[goodness]], [[morality]], [[purity]], [[probity]] (*formal*)
**2** the quality of being whole or united: respect for a state's territorial integrity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unity]], [[unification]], [[cohesion]], [[coherence]], [[wholeness]], [[soundness]], [[completeness]]
**3** the quality of being unharmed or sound: the integrity of the cell membrane
