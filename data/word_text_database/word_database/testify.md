◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bear witness]], [[state]], [[swear]], [[certify]], [[assert]], [[affirm]], [[attest]], [[corroborate]]
**2** **testify to** to be evidence of: a piece of paper testifying to their educational qualifications
