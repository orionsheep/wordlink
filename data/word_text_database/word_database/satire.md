**satire** *n*
**1** the use of [[ridicule]] to expose incompetence, evil, or corruption
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mockery]], [[irony]], [[ridicule]]
**2** a play, novel, or poem containing satire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[parody]], [[mockery]], [[caricature]], [[lampoon]], [[burlesque]]
 **[[satirical]]** *adj*
