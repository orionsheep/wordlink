**spontaneous** *adj*
**1** not planned or arranged; [[impulsive]]: a spontaneous celebration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unplanned]], [[impromptu]], [[unprompted]], [[willing]], [[natural]], [[voluntary]], [[instinctive]], [[impulsive]]
**2** occurring through [[natural]] processes without outside influence: a spontaneous explosion
 **[[spontaneously]]** *adv* 
 **[[spontaneity]]** *n*
