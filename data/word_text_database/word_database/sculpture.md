**sculpture** *n*
**1** the art of making figures or designs in wood, plaster, stone, or metal
**2** works or a work made in this way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[statue]], [[figure]], [[model]], [[bust]], [[effigy]], [[figurine]], statuette
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**3** to [[carve]] (a material) into figures or designs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carve]], [[form]], [[model]], [[fashion]], [[shape]], [[mould]], [[sculpt]], [[chisel]]
**4** to represent (a person or thing) in sculpture
**5** to [[form]] or be formed in the manner of sculpture: limestone sculptured by fast-flowing streams
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carve]], [[form]], [[model]], [[fashion]], [[shape]], [[mould]], [[sculpt]], [[chisel]]
 **[[sculptural]]** *adj*
