**federation** *n*
**1** the [[union]] of several provinces, states, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[union]], [[league]], [[association]], [[alliance]], [[combination]], [[coalition]], [[partnership]], [[consortium]]
**2** any [[alliance]] or [[association]] of organizations which have freely joined together for a common purpose: a federation of twenty regional unions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[union]], [[league]], [[association]], [[alliance]], [[combination]], [[coalition]], [[partnership]], [[consortium]]
