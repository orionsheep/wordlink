**carpet** *n*
**1** a heavy fabric for covering floors
**2** a covering like a carpet: a carpet of leaves
**3** **on the carpet** (*informal*) being or about to be reprimanded
**4** **sweep something under the carpet** to conceal or keep silent about something that one does not want to be discovered
 *vb* **-peting**, **-peted**
**5** to cover with a carpet or a covering like a carpet
