**tout** (rhymes with **shout**) *vb*
**1** to seek (business, customers, etc.) or try to sell (goods), esp. in a persistent or direct manner: he went from door to door touting for business
**2** to put forward or recommend (a person or thing) as a good or suitable example or candidate: the plant was once touted as a showcase factory
 *n*
**3** a person who sells tickets for a heavily booked event at inflated prices
