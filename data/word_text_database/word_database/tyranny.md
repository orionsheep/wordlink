**tyranny** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1**
**A** government by a tyrant
 **B** oppressive and unjust government by more than one person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oppression]], [[cruelty]], [[dictatorship]], [[authoritarianism]], [[despotism]], [[autocracy]], [[absolutism]], [[high-handedness]]
**2** the condition or state of being dominated or controlled by something that makes unpleasant or harsh demands: the tyranny of fashion drives many people to diet
 **[[tyrannous]]** *adj*
