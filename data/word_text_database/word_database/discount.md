**discount** *vb*
**1** to leave (something) out of account as being unreliable, prejudiced, or irrelevant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disregard]], [[reject]], [[ignore]], [[overlook]], [[discard]], [[set aside]], [[dispel]], [[pass over]]
**2** to deduct (an amount or percentage) from the price of something
 *n*
**3** a [[deduction]] from the full amount of a price
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deduction]], [[cut]], [[reduction]], [[concession]], [[rebate]]
**4** **at a discount** below the regular price
