**rod** *n*
**1** a thin straight [[pole]] made of wood or metal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick]], [[bar]], [[pole]], [[shaft]], [[cane]]
**2** a [[cane]] used to beat people as a punishment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[staff]], [[baton]], [[wand]]
**3** a type of cell in the retina, sensitive to dim light
 **[[rodlike]]** *adj*
