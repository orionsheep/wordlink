**defensive** *adj*
**1** intended for defence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protective]], [[watchful]], [[on the defensive]], [[on guard]]
**2** guarding against criticism or exposure of one's failings: he can be highly defensive and wary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oversensitive]], uptight (*informal*)
 *n*
**3** **[[on the defensive]]** in a position of defence, as in being ready to reject criticism
 **[[defensively]]** *adv*
