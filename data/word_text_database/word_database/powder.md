**powder** *n*
**1** a substance in the form of tiny [[loose particles]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dust]], [[talc]], [[fine grains]], [[loose particles]]
**2** a medicine or cosmetic in this form
 *vb*
**3** to [[cover]] or [[sprinkle]] with powder
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dust]], [[cover]], [[scatter]], [[sprinkle]], [[strew]], [[dredge]]
 **[[powdery]]** *adj*
