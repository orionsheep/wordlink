**division** *n*
**1** the [[separation]] of something into two or more distinct parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separation]], [[dividing]], [[splitting up]], [[partition]], [[cutting up]]
**2** the act of [[dividing]] or [[sharing]] out
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sharing]], [[distribution]], [[assignment]], [[rationing]], [[allocation]], [[allotment]], [[apportionment]]
**3** one of the parts into which something is divided
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[bit]], [[piece]], [[section]], [[class]], [[category]], [[fraction]]
**4** the mathematical operation of [[dividing]]
**5** a difference of opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagreement]], [[split]], [[rift]], [[rupture]], [[abyss]], [[chasm]], [[variance]], [[discord]]
**6** a [[part]] of an organization that has been made into a unit for administrative or other reasons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[department]], [[group]], [[branch]]
**7** a formal vote in Parliament
**8** one of the groups of teams that make up a football or other sports league
**9** (*army*) a major formation containing the necessary arms to sustain independent combat
**10** (*biol*) one of the major groups into which the plant kingdom is divided, corresponding to a phylum
 **[[divisional]]** *adj*
