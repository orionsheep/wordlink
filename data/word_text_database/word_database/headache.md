**headache** *n*
**1** a continuous pain in the [[head]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[migraine]], [[head]] (*informal*), [[neuralgia]]
**2** (*informal*) any cause of [[worry]], difficulty, or annoyance: financial headaches
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[worry]], [[trouble]], [[bother]], [[nuisance]], [[inconvenience]], [[bane]], [[vexation]]
