**instinct** *n*
**1** the inborn [[tendency]] to behave in a particular way without the need for thought: maternal instinct
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[natural inclination]], [[talent]], [[tendency]], [[faculty]], [[inclination]], [[knack]], [[predisposition]], [[proclivity]] (*formal*)
**2** natural reaction: my first instinct was to get out of the car
**3** [[intuition]]: Mr Barr's mother said she knew by instinct that her son was safe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intuition]], [[impulse]]
