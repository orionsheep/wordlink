**glory** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** [[fame]], [[praise]], or [[honour]]: tales of glory
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[honour]], [[praise]], [[fame]], [[distinction]], [[acclaim]], [[prestige]], [[eminence]], [[renown]]
**2** beauty: the glory of the tropical day
**3** something worthy of [[praise]]: the Lady Chapel is the great glory of Lichfield
**4** adoration or worship: the greater glory of God
 *vb* **-ries**, **-rying**, **-ried**
**5** **glory in** to take great pleasure in: the workers were glorying in their new-found freedom
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[triumph]], [[boast]], [[relish]], [[revel]], [[exult]], [[take delight in]], [[pride yourself on]]
