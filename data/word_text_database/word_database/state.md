**state** *n*
**1** the [[condition]] or circumstances of a person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condition]], [[shape]]
**2** a sovereign political power or community
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[country]], [[nation]], [[land]], [[republic]], [[territory]], [[federation]], [[commonwealth]], [[kingdom]]
**3** the [[territory]] of such a community
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[country]], [[nation]], [[land]], [[republic]], [[territory]], [[federation]], [[commonwealth]], [[kingdom]]
**4** the sphere of power in such a community: matters of state
**5** (*often cap*) one of a number of areas or communities having their own governments and forming a [[federation]] under a sovereign [[government]], such as in the US or Australia
**6** (*often cap*) the [[government]], civil service, and armed forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[government]], [[ministry]], [[administration]], [[executive]], [[regime]], [[powers-that-be]]
**7** **in a state** (*informal*) in an emotional or very worried [[condition]]
**8** **lie in state** (of a body) to be placed on public view before burial
**9** **state of affairs** circumstances or [[condition]]: this wonderful state of affairs
**10** grand and luxurious lifestyle, as enjoyed by royalty, aristocrats, or the wealthy: living in state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[glory]], [[grandeur]], [[splendour]], [[majesty]], pomp
 *adj*
**11** controlled or financed by a state: state ownership
**12** of or concerning the State: state secrets
**13** involving [[ceremony]]: a state visit
 *vb* **stating**, **stated**
**14** to [[express]] (something) in words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[say]], [[declare]], [[present]], [[voice]], [[express]], [[assert]], [[utter]]
