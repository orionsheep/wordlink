**essence** *n*
**1** the most important and distinctive feature of something, which determines its identity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fundamental nature]], [[nature]], [[being]], [[heart]], [[spirit]], [[soul]], [[core]], [[substance]]
**2** a concentrated liquid used to flavour food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[concentrate]], [[spirits]], [[extract]], [[tincture]], [[distillate]]
**3** **in essence** essentially
**4** **of the essence** vitally important
