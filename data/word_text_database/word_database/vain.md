**vain** *adj*
**1** excessively [[proud]] of one's appearance or achievements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conceited]], [[narcissistic]], [[proud]], [[arrogant]], [[swaggering]], [[egotistical]], [[self-important]]
**2** [[senseless]] or [[unsuccessful]]: he made a vain attempt to lighten the atmosphere
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[futile]], [[useless]], [[pointless]], [[unsuccessful]], [[idle]], [[worthless]], [[senseless]], fruitless
 *n*
**3** **[[in vain]]** without achieving the desired effects or results: the old man searched [[in vain]] for his son
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in vain]]**: [[useless]], to no avail, [[unsuccessful]], fruitless, vain
 **[[vainly]]** *adv*
