**polite** *adj*
**1** having good manners; [[courteous]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mannerly]], [[civil]], [[courteous]], [[gracious]], [[respectful]], [[well-behaved]], [[complaisant]], [[well-mannered]]
**2** cultivated or [[refined]]: polite society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refined]], [[cultured]], [[civilized]], [[polished]], [[sophisticated]], [[elegant]], [[genteel]], [[well-bred]]
**3** socially correct but insincere: he smiled a polite response and stifled an urge to scream
 **[[politely]]** *adv* 
 **[[politeness]]** *n*
