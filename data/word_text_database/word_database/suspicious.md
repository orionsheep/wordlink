**suspicious** *adj*
**1** causing one to [[suspect]] something is wrong: suspicious activities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suspect]], [[dubious]], [[questionable]], [[doubtful]], [[dodgy]] (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[fishy]] (*informal*), [[shonky]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**2** unwilling to trust: I'm suspicious of his motives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distrustful]], [[sceptical]], [[doubtful]], [[unbelieving]], [[wary]]
 **[[suspiciously]]** *adv*
