**compensation** *n*
**1** payment made as [[reparation]] for loss or injury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reparation]], [[damages]], [[recompense]], [[remuneration]], [[restitution]], [[reimbursement]]
**2** the act of making [[amends]] for something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recompense]], [[amends]], [[reparation]], [[restitution]], [[atonement]]
