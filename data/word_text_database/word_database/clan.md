**clan** *n*
**1** a [[group]] of families with a common surname and a common ancestor, esp. among Scottish Highlanders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[family]], [[group]], [[society]], [[tribe]], [[fraternity]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[brotherhood]], [[ainga]] (*NZ*), ngai *or* ngati (*NZ*)
**2** an extended [[family]] related by ancestry or marriage: America's leading political clan, the Kennedys
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[family]], [[group]], [[society]], [[tribe]], [[fraternity]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[brotherhood]], [[ainga]] (*NZ*), ngai *or* ngati (*NZ*)
**3** a [[group]] of people with common characteristics, aims, or interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[set]], [[circle]], [[gang]], [[faction]], [[coterie]], [[cabal]]
 **[[clansman]]** * or * (*fem*) **[[clanswoman]]** *n*
