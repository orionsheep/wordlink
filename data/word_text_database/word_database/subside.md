**subside** *vb* **-siding**, **-sided**
**1** to become less loud, excited, or violent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decrease]], [[diminish]], [[lessen]], [[ease]], [[wane]], [[ebb]], [[abate]], [[slacken]]
**2** to [[sink]] to a [[lower]] level
**3** (of the surface of the earth) to [[cave in]]; [[collapse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collapse]], [[sink]], [[cave in]], [[drop]], [[lower]], [[settle]]
 **[[subsidence]]** *n*
