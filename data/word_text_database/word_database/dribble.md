**dribble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to flow or allow to flow in a thin stream or drops
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[drip]], [[trickle]], [[drop]], [[leak]], [[ooze]], [[seep]], [[fall in drops]]
**2** to allow saliva to [[trickle]] from the mouth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drool]], [[drivel]], [[slaver]], [[slobber]]
**3** (in football, hockey, etc.) to propel (the ball) by kicking or tapping in quick succession
 *n*
**4** a small quantity of liquid falling in drops or flowing in a thin stream
**5** a small supply: there's only a dribble of milk left
**6** an act or instance of dribbling
 **[[dribbler]]** *n*
