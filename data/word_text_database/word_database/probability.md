**probability** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the condition of being probable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[likelihood]], [[prospect]], [[chance]], [[odds]], [[expectation]], [[liability]], [[likeliness]]
**2** an event or other thing that is likely to happen or be true
**3** (*statistics*) a measure of the [[likelihood]] of an event happening
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[odds]], [[possibility]], [[likelihood]]
