**right-wing** *adj*
**1** conservative or reactionary: there's a very fast-growing right-wing feeling in our country
**2** belonging to the more conservative part of a political party: a group of right-wing Labour MPs
 *n* **right wing**
**3** the more conservative or reactionary section, esp. of a political party: the right wing of the Conservative Party
**4** (*sport*)
**A** the right-hand side of the field of play
 **B** a player positioned in this area in certain games
 **[[right-winger]]** *n*
