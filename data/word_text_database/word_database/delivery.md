**delivery** *n*, *pl* **-eries**
**1**
**A** the act of delivering [[goods]] or mail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handing over]], [[transfer]], [[distribution]], [[transmission]], [[dispatch]], [[consignment]], conveyance
 **B** something that is delivered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consignment]], [[goods]], [[shipment]], [[batch]]
**2** the act of giving birth to a baby
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[childbirth]], [[labour]], [[confinement]], [[parturition]]
**3** manner or style in public speaking: her delivery was clear and humorous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speech]], [[utterance]], [[articulation]], [[intonation]], [[elocution]], [[enunciation]]
**4** (*cricket*) the act or manner of bowling a ball
**5** (*S African*) a semi-official slogan for the provision of services to previously disadvantaged communities
