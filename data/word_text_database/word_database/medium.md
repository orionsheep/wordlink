**medium** *adj*
**1** [[midway]] between extremes of size, amount, or degree: fry over a medium heat; a man of medium height
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[average]], [[mean]], [[middle]], [[middling]], [[fair]], [[intermediate]], [[midway]], mediocre
 *n*, *pl* **-dia*** or ***-diums**
**2** a [[middle]] state, degree, or condition: the happy medium
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[middle]], [[mean]], [[centre]], [[average]], [[compromise]], [[midpoint]]
**3** a substance which has a particular effect or can be used for a particular purpose: linseed oil is used as a thinning medium for oil paint
**4** a means for communicating information or news to the public
**5** a person who can supposedly communicate with the dead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spiritualist]], [[seer]], [[clairvoyant]], [[fortune teller]], [[channeller]]
**6** the substance or surroundings in which an organism naturally lives or grows
**7** (*art*) the category of a work of art, as determined by its materials: his works in the photographic medium
