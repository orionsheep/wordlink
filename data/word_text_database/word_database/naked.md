**naked** *adj*
**1** without clothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nude]], [[stripped]], [[exposed]], [[bare]], [[undressed]], [[starkers]] (*informal*), [[stark-naked]], [[unclothed]]
**2** not concealed: naked aggression
**3** without any covering: it was dimly lit by naked bulbs
**4** **the naked eye** the eye unassisted by any optical instrument: difficult to spot with the naked eye
 **[[nakedly]]** *adv* 
 **[[nakedness]]** *n*
