**zoom** *vb*
**1** to move very rapidly: the first rocket zoomed into the sky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speed]], [[shoot]], [[fly]], [[rush]], [[flash]], [[dash]], [[whizz]] (*informal*), [[hurtle]]
**2** to increase or rise rapidly: stocks zoomed on the American exchange
**3** to move with or make a continuous buzzing or humming sound
 *n*
**4** the sound or act of zooming
**5** a zoom lens
