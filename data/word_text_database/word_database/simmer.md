**simmer** *vb*
**1** to cook (food) gently at just below boiling point
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bubble]], [[boil gently]], [[seethe]]
**2** (of violence or conflict) to threaten to break out: revolt simmering among rural MPs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fume]], [[seethe]], [[smoulder]], [[rage]], be angry
 *n*
**3** the state of simmering
