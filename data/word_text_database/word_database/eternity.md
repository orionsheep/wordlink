**eternity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** endless or infinite time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perpetuity]], [[immortality]], [[infinity]], [[timelessness]], [[endlessness]]
**2** a seemingly endless period of time: it seemed an eternity before he could feel his heart beating again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ages]]
**3** the timeless existence after death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the afterlife]], [[heaven]] (*informal*), [[paradise]], [[the next world]], [[the hereafter]]
**4** the state of being eternal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perpetuity]], [[immortality]], [[infinity]], [[timelessness]], [[endlessness]]
