**calm** *adj*
**1** not showing or not feeling agitation or excitement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cool]], [[relaxed]], [[composed]], [[sedate]], [[collected]], [[dispassionate]], [[unemotional]], [[self-possessed]], [[chilled]] (*informal*)
**2** not ruffled by the wind: a flat calm sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[still]], [[quiet]], [[smooth]], [[mild]], [[serene]], [[tranquil]], [[balmy]], [[windless]]
**3** (of weather) [[windless]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[still]], [[quiet]], [[smooth]], [[mild]], [[serene]], [[tranquil]], [[balmy]], [[windless]]
 *n*
**4** a peaceful state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peacefulness]], [[peace]], serenity
 *vb*
**5** (often foll. by *down*) to make or become calm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soothe]], [[quiet]], [[relax]], [[appease]], [[still]], [[allay]], [[assuage]], [[quieten]]
 **[[calmly]]** *adv* 
 **[[calmness]]** *n*
