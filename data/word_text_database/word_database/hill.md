**hill** *n*
**1** a natural elevation of the earth's surface, less high than a mountain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mount]], [[fell]], [[height]], [[mound]], [[hilltop]], [[tor]], [[knoll]], [[hillock]], kopje *or* koppie (*S African*)
**2** a heap or [[mound]]
**3** an incline or slope
 **[[hilly]]** *adj*
