**study** *vb* **studies**, **studying**, **studied**
**1** to be engaged in the [[learning]] or understanding of (a subject)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[learn]], [[cram]] (*informal*), [[swot]] (up) (*Brit* *informal*), [[read up]], [[mug up]] (*Brit* *slang*)
**2** to investigate or [[examine]] (something) by observation and [[research]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contemplate]], [[read]], [[examine]], [[consider]], [[go into]], [[pore over]]
**3** to [[look at]] (something or someone) closely; scrutinize
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examine]], [[survey]], [[look at]], scrutinize
 *n*, *pl* **studies**
**4** the act or process of studying
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[learning]], [[lessons]], [[school work]], [[reading]], [[research]], [[swotting]] (*Brit* *informal*)
**5** a room used for studying, [[reading]], or writing
**6** (*often pl*) work relating to a particular area of [[learning]]: environmental studies
**7** an [[investigation]] and [[analysis]] of a particular subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examination]], [[investigation]], [[analysis]], [[consideration]], [[inspection]], [[scrutiny]], [[contemplation]]
**8** a paper or book produced as a result of study
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[piece of research]], [[survey]], [[report]], [[review]], [[inquiry]], [[investigation]]
**9** a work of art, such as a drawing, done for practice or in preparation for another work
**10** a musical composition designed to develop playing technique
