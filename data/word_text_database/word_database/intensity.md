**intensity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state or quality of being intense
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passion]], [[emotion]], [[fervour]], [[force]], [[strength]], [[fanaticism]], [[ardour]], [[vehemence]]
**2** extreme [[force]], degree, or amount
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[strength]], [[fierceness]]
**3** (*physics*) the amount or degree of [[strength]] of electricity, heat, light, or sound per unit area of volume
