**grovel** (**grov**-el) *vb* **-elling**, **-elled** (*US*) **-eling**, **-eled**
**1** to behave excessively humbly towards someone, esp. a superior, in an attempt to win his or her favour
**2** to crawl on the floor, often in search of something: [[grovelling]] on the floor for missing cards
 **[[grovelling]]** * or * (*US*) **[[groveling]]** *adj*, *n*
