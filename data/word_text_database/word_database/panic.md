**panic** *n*
**1** a sudden overwhelming feeling of [[terror]] or [[anxiety]], sometimes affecting a whole group of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fear]], [[alarm]], [[terror]], [[anxiety]], [[hysteria]], [[fright]], [[trepidation]] (*formal*), a flap (*informal*)
 *adj*
**2** of or resulting from such [[terror]]: panic measures
 *vb* **-nicking**, **-nicked**
**3** to feel or cause to feel panic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go to pieces]], [[become hysterical]], [[lose your nerve]]
 **[[panicky]]** *adj*
