**opening** *n*
**1** the [[beginning]] or first part of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beginning]], [[start]], [[launch]], [[dawn]] (*literary*), [[outset]], [[initiation]], [[inception]], [[commencement]]
**2** the first performance of a theatrical production
**3** a [[chance]] or [[opportunity]]: an opening into show business
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opportunity]], [[chance]], [[time]], [[moment]], [[occasion]], [[look-in]] (*informal*)
**4** a [[hole]] or [[gap]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hole]], [[space]], [[tear]], [[crack]], [[gap]], [[slot]], [[puncture]], [[aperture]]
