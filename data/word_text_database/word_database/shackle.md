**shackle** *n*
**1** one of a pair of metal rings joined by a chain for securing someone's wrists or ankles
**2** **shackles** anything that confines or restricts freedom: free from the shackles of its feudal past
**3** a metal loop or link closed by a bolt, used for securing ropes or chains
 *vb* **-ling**, **-led**
**4** to fasten with shackles
**5** to restrict or hamper: an economy shackled by central control
