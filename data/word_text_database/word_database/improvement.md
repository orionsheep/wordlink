**improvement** *n*
**1** the act of improving or the state of being improved
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enhancement]], [[advancement]], [[betterment]]
**2** a change that makes something better or adds to its value: home improvements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advance]], [[development]], [[progress]], [[recovery]], [[upswing]]
**3** (*Austral** & **NZ*) a building on a piece of land, adding to its value
