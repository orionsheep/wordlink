**ration** *n*
**1** a fixed [[allowance]] of something that is scarce, such as food or petrol in wartime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allowance]], [[quota]], [[allotment]], [[helping]], [[part]], [[share]], [[measure]], [[portion]]
**2** **rations** a fixed daily [[allowance]] of food, such as that given to a soldier
 *vb*
**3** to [[restrict]] the distribution of (something): the government has rationed petrol
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[limit]], [[control]], [[restrict]], [[budget]]
**4** to distribute a fixed amount of (something) to each person in a group
 **[[rationing]]** *n*
