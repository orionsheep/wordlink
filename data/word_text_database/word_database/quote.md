**quote** *vb* **quoting**, **quoted**
**1** to [[repeat]] (words) exactly from (an earlier work, speech, or conversation), usually with an acknowledgment of their source
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[repeat]], [[recite]], [[recall]]
**2** to state a price for goods or a job of work
**3** to put quotation marks round (words)
 *n*
**4** (*informal*) a quotation
**5** **quotes** (*informal*) quotation marks
 *interj*
**6** an expression used to indicate that the words that follow are a quotation
 **[[quotable]]** *adj*
