**immerse** *vb* **-mersing**, **-mersed**
**1** to [[plunge]] or [[dip]] into liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plunge]], [[dip]], [[submerge]], [[sink]], [[duck]], [[bathe]], [[douse]], [[dunk]]
**2** to [[involve]] deeply: he immersed himself in the history of Rome
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engross]], [[involve]], [[absorb]], [[busy]], [[occupy]], [[engage]]
**3** to baptize by dipping the whole body into water
 **[[immersion]]** *n*
