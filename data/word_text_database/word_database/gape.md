**gape** *vb* **[[gaping]]**, **gaped**
**1** to [[stare]] in [[wonder]] with the mouth [[open]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stare]], [[wonder]], [[goggle]], [[gawp]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[gawk]]
**2** to [[open]] the mouth wide, as in yawning
**3** to be or become wide [[open]]: a hole gaped in the roof
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[open]], [[split]], [[crack]], [[yawn]]
 **[[gaping]]** *adj*
