**space** *n*
**1** the unlimited three-dimensional [[expanse]] in which all objects exist
**2** an [[interval]] of distance or [[time]] between two points, objects, or events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[interval]], [[time]], [[while]], [[span]], [[duration]], [[time frame]], [[timeline]]
**3** a [[blank]] portion or area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blank]], [[gap]], [[interval]]
**4** unoccupied area or [[room]]: barely enough space to walk around
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[room]], [[capacity]], [[extent]], [[margin]], [[scope]], [[play]], [[expanse]], [[leeway]]
**5** the region beyond the earth's atmosphere containing other planets, stars, and galaxies; [[the universe]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outer space]], [[the universe]], [[the galaxy]], [[the solar system]], the cosmos
 *vb* **spacing**, **[[spaced]]**
**6** (often foll. by *out*) to place or arrange (things) at intervals or with spaces between them
