**corrupt** *adj*
**1** open to or involving bribery or other [[dishonest]] practices: corrupt practices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dishonest]], [[bent]] (*slang*), [[crooked]] (*informal*), [[fraudulent]], [[unscrupulous]], [[venal]], [[unprincipled]]
**2** morally [[depraved]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depraved]], [[vicious]], [[degenerate]], [[debased]], [[profligate]], [[dissolute]]
**3** (of a text or data) made unreliable by errors or alterations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distorted]], [[doctored]], [[altered]], falsified
 *vb*
**4** to make corrupt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deprave]], [[pervert]], [[subvert]], [[debauch]]
 **[[corruptive]]** *adj*
