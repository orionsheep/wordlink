**trot** *vb* **trotting**, **trotted**
**1** (of a horse) to move in a manner faster than a walk but slower than a gallop, in which diagonally opposite legs come down together
**2** (of a person) to move fairly quickly, with small quick steps
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[jog]], [[scamper]], [[lope]], [[canter]]
 *n*
**3** a medium-paced gait of a horse, in which diagonally opposite legs come down together
**4** a steady brisk pace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[jog]], [[lope]], [[canter]]
**5** **on the trot** (*informal*) one after the other: ten years on the trot
**6** **the trots** (*slang*) diarrhoea
