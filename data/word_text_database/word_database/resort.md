**resort** *vb*
**1** **resort to** to have recourse to for help, use, etc.: some people have resorted to begging for food
**2** to go, esp. often or habitually: to resort to the beach
 *n*
**3** a place to which many people go for holidays
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holiday centre]], [[spot]], [[retreat]], [[haunt]], [[tourist centre]]
**4** the use of something as a means or aid
**5** **last resort** the last possible course of action open to a person
