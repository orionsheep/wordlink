**member** *n*
**1** a person who belongs to a group or organization such as a club or political party
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representative]], [[associate]], [[supporter]], [[fellow]], [[subscriber]], [[comrade]], [[disciple]]
**2** any part of a plant or animal, such as a limb or petal
**3** a Member of Parliament: the member for Glasgow Central
 *adj*
**4** (of a country or group) belonging to an organization or alliance: a summit of the member countries' heads of state is due
