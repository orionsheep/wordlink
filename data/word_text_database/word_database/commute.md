**commute** *vb* **-muting**, **-muted**
**1** to travel some distance regularly between one's home and one's place of work
**2** (*law*) to reduce (a sentence) to one less severe
**3** to substitute
**4** to pay (an annuity or pension) at one time, instead of in instalments
 *n*
**5** a journey made by commuting
