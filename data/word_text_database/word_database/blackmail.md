**blackmail** *n*
**1** the act of attempting to obtain money by threatening to reveal shameful information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[threat]], [[intimidation]], [[ransom]], [[extortion]], [[hush money]] (*slang*)
**2** the use of unfair pressure in an attempt to influence someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[threat]], [[intimidation]], [[ransom]], [[extortion]], [[hush money]] (*slang*)
 *vb*
**3** to obtain or attempt to obtain money by [[intimidation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[threaten]], [[squeeze]], [[compel]], [[intimidate]], [[coerce]], [[dragoon]], [[extort]], [[hold to ransom]]
**4** to attempt to influence (a person) by unfair pressure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[threaten]], [[squeeze]], [[compel]], [[intimidate]], [[coerce]], [[dragoon]], [[extort]], [[hold to ransom]]
 **[[blackmailer]]** *n*
