**dodgy** *adj* **dodgier**, **dodgiest** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**1** [[dangerous]], [[risky]], or unreliable: he's in a very dodgy political position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[risky]], [[difficult]], [[tricky]], [[dangerous]], [[delicate]], [[uncertain]], dicey (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[chancy]] (*informal*), [[shonky]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**2** untrustworthy: they considered him a very dodgy character
