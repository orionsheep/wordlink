**guess** *vb*
**1** to form an [[estimate]] or conclusion about (something), without proper knowledge: a competition to guess the weight of the cake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimate]], [[predict]], [[work out]], [[speculate]], [[conjecture]], [[postulate]], [[hypothesize]]
**2** to arrive at a correct [[estimate]] of (something) by guessing: I had a notion that he guessed my thoughts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimate]], [[predict]], [[work out]], [[speculate]], [[conjecture]], [[postulate]], [[hypothesize]]
**3** (*informal*) to [[think]] or [[suppose]]: I guess he must have been a great athlete
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suppose]], [[think]], [[believe]], [[suspect]], [[judge]], [[imagine]], [[reckon]] (*informal*), fancy
 *n*
**4** an [[estimate]] or conclusion arrived at by guessing: we can hazard a guess at the answer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimate]], [[speculation]], [[judgment]], [[hypothesis]], [[conjecture]], [[shot in the dark]]
