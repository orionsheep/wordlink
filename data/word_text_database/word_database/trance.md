**trance** *n*
**1** a hypnotic state resembling sleep in which a person is unable to move or act of his or her own will
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[daze]], [[dream]], [[abstraction]], [[rapture]], [[reverie]], [[stupor]], [[unconsciousness]]
**2** a dazed or stunned state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[daze]], [[dream]], [[abstraction]], [[rapture]], [[reverie]], [[stupor]], [[unconsciousness]]
