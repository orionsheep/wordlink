**appropriate** *adj*
**1** right or [[suitable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suitable]], [[fitting]], [[relevant]], [[to the point]], [[apt]], [[pertinent]], [[befitting]], well-suited
 *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**2** to take for one's own use without permission
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seize]], [[claim]], [[acquire]], [[confiscate]], [[usurp]], [[impound]], [[commandeer]], [[take possession of]]
**3** to put (money) aside for a particular purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allocate]], [[allow]], [[budget]], [[devote]], [[assign]], [[designate]], [[set aside]], [[earmark]]
 **[[appropriately]]** *adv* 
 **[[appropriateness]]** *n*
