**1** a [[pile]] of things lying one on top of another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pile]], [[lot]], [[collection]], [[mass]], [[stack]], [[mound]], [[accumulation]], [[hoard]]
**2** (*often pl*, *informal*) a large number or quantity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[a lot]], [[lots]], [[plenty]], [[masses]], [[load]](s) (*informal*), [[great deal]], [[tons]] (*informal*), [[stack]](s)
 *adv*
**3** **heaps** (*informal*) much: he was heaps better
 *vb*
**4** to [[collect]] into a [[pile]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pile]], [[collect]], [[gather]], [[stack]], [[accumulate]], [[amass]], [[hoard]]
**5** to give freely (to): film roles were heaped on her
