**mind** *n*
**1** the part of a person responsible for thought, feelings, and [[intention]]. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**mental**
**2** [[intelligence]] as opposed to feelings or wishes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intelligence]], [[reason]], [[reasoning]], [[understanding]], [[sense]], [[brain]](s) (*informal*), [[wits]], [[intellect]]
**3** [[memory]] or [[recollection]]: his name didn't spring to mind immediately
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[memory]], [[recollection]], [[remembrance]], [[powers of recollection]]
**4** a person considered as an intellectual being: one of Europe's greatest minds
**5** the condition or state of a person's feelings or thoughts: a confused state of mind
**6** an [[intention]] or [[desire]]: I have a mind to go
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intention]], [[wish]], [[desire]], [[urge]], [[fancy]], [[leaning]], [[notion]], [[inclination]]
**7** attention or thoughts: keep your mind on the job
**8** a sound mental state; [[sanity]]: he's out of his mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sanity]], [[reason]], [[senses]], [[judgment]], [[wits]], [[marbles]] (*informal*), [[rationality]], [[mental balance]]
**9** **change one's mind** to alter one's decision or opinion
**10** **give someone a piece of one's mind** to scold someone severely
**11** **in two minds** undecided or wavering
**12** **make up one's mind** to reach a decision
**13** **on one's mind** in one's thoughts
**14** **to my mind** in my opinion
 *vb*
**15** to [[take offence at]]: do you mind if I open a window?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take offence at]], [[dislike]], [[care about]], [[object to]], [[resent]], [[disapprove of]], [[be bothered by]], [[be affronted by]]
**16** to [[pay attention to]]: to mind one's own business
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pay attention to]], [[mark]], [[note]], [[listen to]], [[observe]], [[obey]], [[heed]], [[take heed of]]
**17** to make certain; ensure: mind you tell him
**18** to [[take care of]]: mind the shop
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look after]], [[watch]], [[protect]], [[tend]], [[guard]], [[take care of]], [[attend to]], [[keep an eye on]]
**19** to [[be cautious]] or careful about: mind how you go
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be careful]], [[watch]], [[take care]], [[be wary]], [[be cautious]], [[be on your guard]]
**20** (*dialect*) to remember
