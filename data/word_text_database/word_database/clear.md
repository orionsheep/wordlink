**clear** *adj*
**1** [[free]] from doubt or confusion: clear instructions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comprehensible]], [[explicit]], [[understandable]]
**2** [[certain]] in the mind; [[sure]]: I am still not clear about what they can and cannot do
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[certain]], [[sure]], [[convinced]], [[positive]], [[satisfied]], [[resolved]], [[definite]], [[decided]]
**3** easy to see or hear; [[distinct]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distinct]]
**4** perceptive; alert: clear thinking
**5** [[evident]] or [[obvious]]: it is not clear how she died
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obvious]], [[plain]], [[apparent]], [[evident]], [[distinct]], [[pronounced]], [[manifest]], [[blatant]]
**6** [[transparent]]: clear glass doors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transparent]], [[see-through]], [[translucent]], [[crystalline]], [[glassy]], [[limpid]], [[pellucid]]
**7** [[free]] from darkness or obscurity; [[bright]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bright]], [[fine]], [[fair]], [[shining]], [[sunny]], [[luminous]], [[cloudless]], [[light]]
**8** (of sounds or the voice) not harsh or hoarse
**9** even and [[pure]] in tone or colour
**10** [[free]] of obstruction; [[open]]: a clear path runs under the trees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unobstructed]], [[open]], [[free]], [[empty]], [[unhindered]], [[unimpeded]]
**11** (of weather) [[free]] from dullness or clouds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bright]], [[fine]], [[fair]], [[shining]], [[sunny]], [[luminous]], [[cloudless]], [[light]]
**12** without blemish or defect: a clear skin
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untroubled]], [[clean]], [[pure]], [[innocent]], [[immaculate]], [[unblemished]], [[untarnished]]
**13** [[free]] of suspicion, guilt, or blame: a clear conscience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untroubled]], [[clean]], [[pure]], [[innocent]], [[immaculate]], [[unblemished]], [[untarnished]]
**14** (of money) without deduction; net
**15** [[free]] from debt or obligation
**16** without qualification or limitation; complete: a clear lead
