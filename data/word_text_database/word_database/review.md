**review** *n*
**1** a critical assessment of a book, film, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[critique]], [[commentary]], [[evaluation]], [[notice]], [[criticism]], [[judgment]]
**2** a publication containing such articles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[magazine]], [[journal]], [[periodical]], [[zine]] (*informal*)
**3** a general [[survey]] or report: the new curriculum is to be set up a year after the conclusions of the review are due
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[survey]], [[study]], [[analysis]], [[examination]], [[scrutiny]]
**4** a formal or official [[inspection]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inspection]], [[parade]], [[march past]]
**5** the act or an instance of reviewing
**6** a second consideration; re-[[examination]]
**7** a retrospective [[survey]]
**8** (*law*) a re-[[examination]] of a case
 *vb*
**9** to hold or write a review of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assess]], [[study]], [[judge]], [[evaluate]], [[criticize]]
**10** to [[examine]] again: the committee will review the ban in the summer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reconsider]], [[revise]], [[rethink]], [[reassess]], [[re-examine]], [[re-evaluate]], [[think over]]
**11** to look back upon (a period of time or sequence of events): he reviewed his achievements with pride
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look back on]], [[remember]], [[recall]], [[reflect on]], [[recollect]]
**12** to [[inspect]] formally or officially: when he reviewed the troops they cheered him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inspect]], [[check]], [[survey]], [[examine]], [[vet]]
**13** (*law*) to [[re-examine]] (a decision) judicially
