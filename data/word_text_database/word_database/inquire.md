**inquire** * or ***enquire** *vb* **-quiring**, **-quired**
**1** to seek information (about)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ask]], [[question]], [[query]], [[quiz]]
**2** **inquire after** to [[ask]] about the health or progress of (a person)
**3** **[[inquire into]]** to make an investigation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[inquire into]]**: investigate, study, examine, research, explore, look into, probe into, make inquiries into
**4** **inquire of** to [[ask]] (a person) for information: I'll inquire of my aunt when she is coming
 **[[inquirer]]** * or ***[[enquirer]]** *n*
