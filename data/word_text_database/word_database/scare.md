**scare** *vb* **scaring**, **scared**
**1** to [[frighten]] or be frightened
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frighten]], [[alarm]], [[terrify]], [[panic]], [[shock]], [[startle]], [[intimidate]], [[dismay]]
**2** **scare away *or* off** to drive away by frightening
 *n*
**3** a sudden attack of fear or [[alarm]]: you gave me a scare
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fright]], [[shock]], [[start]]
**4** a period of general fear or [[alarm]]: the bird flu scare
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[panic]], [[hysteria]]
