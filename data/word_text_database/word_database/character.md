**character** *n*
**1** the combination of qualities distinguishing an individual person, group of people, or place: the unique charm and character of this historic town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[personality]], [[nature]], [[attributes]], [[temperament]], [[complexion]], [[disposition]]
**2** a distinguishing [[quality]] or characteristic: bodily movements of a deliberate character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nature]], [[kind]], [[quality]], [[calibre]]
**3** [[reputation]], esp. good [[reputation]]: a man of my Dad's character and standing in the community
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reputation]], [[honour]], [[integrity]], [[good name]], [[rectitude]]
**4** an attractively unusual or interesting [[quality]]: the little town was full of life and character
**5** a person represented in a play, film, or story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[role]], [[part]], [[persona]]
**6** an unusual or amusing person: quite a character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eccentric]], [[card]] (*informal*), [[original]], [[oddball]] (*informal*)
**7** (*informal*) a person: a flamboyant character
**8** a single [[letter]], numeral, or [[symbol]] used in writing or printing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[symbol]], [[mark]], [[sign]], [[letter]], [[figure]], [[device]], [[rune]], [[hieroglyph]]
**9** **in *or* out of character** typical *or* not typical of the apparent character of a person
 **[[characterless]]** *adj*
