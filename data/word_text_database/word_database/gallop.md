**gallop** *vb*
**1** (of a horse) to [[run]] fast with a two-beat stride in which all four legs are off the ground at once
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[race]], [[career]], [[speed]], [[bolt]]
**2** to ride (a horse) at a gallop
**3** to move or progress rapidly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dash]], [[run]], [[race]], [[career]], [[speed]], [[rush]], sprint
 *n*
**4** the fast two-beat gait of horses
**5** an instance of galloping
