**depart** *vb*
**1** to [[leave]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leave]], [[go]], [[withdraw]], [[retire]], [[disappear]], [[quit]], [[retreat]], [[exit]], [[rack off]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
**2** to [[differ]] or [[deviate]]: to depart from the original concept
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deviate]], [[vary]], [[differ]], [[stray]], [[veer]], [[swerve]], [[diverge]], [[digress]]
