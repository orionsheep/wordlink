**satisfaction** *n*
**1** the [[pleasure]] obtained from the [[fulfilment]] of a desire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fulfilment]], [[pleasure]], [[achievement]], [[relish]], [[gratification]], [[pride]]
**2** something that brings [[fulfilment]]: craft workers get satisfaction from their work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contentment]], [[content]], [[comfort]], [[pleasure]], [[happiness]], [[enjoyment]], [[satiety]], [[repletion]]
**3** compensation or an apology for a wrong done: consumers unable to get satisfaction from their gas supplier
