**2** a [[meeting]] of a court, parliament, or council
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting]], [[hearing]], [[sitting]], [[period]], [[conference]], [[congress]], [[discussion]], [[assembly]]
**3** a series or [[period]] of such meetings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting]], [[hearing]], [[sitting]], [[period]], [[conference]], [[congress]], [[discussion]], [[assembly]]
**4** a school or university term or year
 **[[sessional]]** *adj*
