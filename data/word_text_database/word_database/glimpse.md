**glimpse** *n*
**1** a brief [[view]]: a glimpse of a rare snow leopard
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look]], [[sighting]], [[sight]], [[glance]], [[peep]], [[peek]]
**2** a vague indication: glimpses of insecurity
 *vb* **glimpsing**, **glimpsed**
**3** to [[catch sight of]] momentarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[catch sight of]], [[spot]], [[sight]], [[view]], [[spy]], [[espy]]
