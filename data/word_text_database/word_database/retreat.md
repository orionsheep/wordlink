**retreat** *vb*
**1** (*military*) to [[withdraw]] or retire in the face of or from action with an enemy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[withdraw]], [[back off]], [[draw back]], [[leave]], [[go back]], [[depart]], [[fall back]], [[pull back]]
**2** to retire or [[withdraw]] to [[seclusion]] or [[shelter]]
**3** to alter one's opinion about something
 *n*
**4** the act of retreating or withdrawing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flight]], [[retirement]], [[departure]], [[withdrawal]], [[evacuation]]
**5** (*military*)
**A** a [[withdrawal]] or [[retirement]] in the face of the enemy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flight]], [[retirement]], [[departure]], [[withdrawal]], [[evacuation]]
 **B** a bugle call signifying [[withdrawal]] or [[retirement]]
**6** a place to which one may retire, esp. for religious contemplation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refuge]], [[haven]], [[shelter]], [[sanctuary]], [[hideaway]], [[seclusion]]
**7** a period of [[seclusion]], esp. for religious contemplation
**8** the act of altering one's opinion about something
