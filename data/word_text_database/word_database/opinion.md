**opinion** *n*
**1** [[belief]] not founded on certainty or proof but on what seems probable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[belief]], [[feeling]], [[view]], [[idea]], [[theory]], [[conviction]], [[point of view]], [[sentiment]]
**2** evaluation or [[estimation]] of a person or thing: they seemed to share my high opinion of her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimation]], [[view]], [[impression]], [[assessment]], [[judgment]], [[appraisal]], [[considered opinion]]
**3** a [[judgment]] given by an expert: medical opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[estimation]], [[view]], [[impression]], [[assessment]], [[judgment]], [[appraisal]], [[considered opinion]]
**4** **a matter of opinion** a point open to question
