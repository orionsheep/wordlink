**level** *adj*
**1** on a [[horizontal]] [[plane]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[horizontal]], [[even]], [[flat]], [[smooth]], [[uniform]]
**2** having an [[even]] surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[horizontal]], [[even]], [[flat]], [[smooth]], [[uniform]]
**3** being of the same height as something else: the floor of the lean-to was level with the patio
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equal]], [[balanced]], [[at the same height]]
**4** [[equal]] to or [[even]] with (something or someone else): Johnson was level with the overnight leader
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[even]], [[tied]], [[equal]], [[drawn]], [[neck and neck]], [[all square]], [[level pegging]]
**5** not exceeding the upper edge of (a spoon, etc.)
**6** consistent or regular: a level pulse
**7** **one's level best** the best one can do
 *vb* **-velling**, **-velled** (*US*) **-veling**, **-veled**
**8** (sometimes foll. by *off*) to make [[horizontal]] or [[even]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flatten]], [[plane]], [[smooth]], [[even]] off *or* out
**9** to make [[equal]] in [[position]] or [[status]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equalize]], [[balance]], [[even up]]
**10** to [[direct]] (an accusation or criticism) emphatically at someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[direct]], [[point]], [[turn]], [[train]], [[aim]], [[focus]]
**11** to [[focus]] (a look) directly at someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[direct]], [[point]], [[turn]], [[train]], [[aim]], [[focus]]
**12** to [[aim]] (a weapon) horizontally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[direct]], [[point]], [[turn]], [[train]], [[aim]], [[focus]]
**13** to [[demolish]] completely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destroy]], [[devastate]], [[demolish]], [[flatten]], [[knock down]], [[pull down]], [[tear down]], [[bulldoze]], [[kennet]] (*Austral* *slang*), jeff (*Austral* *slang*)
 *n*
**14** a [[horizontal]] line or [[plane]]
**15** a device, such as a spirit level, for determining whether a surface is [[horizontal]]
**16** [[position]] or [[status]] in a scale of values: a high-level delegation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[standard]], [[degree]] (*archaic*), [[grade]], [[standing]], [[stage]], [[rank]], [[status]]
**17** [[stage]] or [[degree]] of progress: primary-school level
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[standard]], [[degree]] (*archaic*), [[grade]], [[standing]], [[stage]], [[rank]], [[status]]
**18** a specified vertical [[position]]: floor level
**19** the topmost [[horizontal]] line or [[plane]] from which the height of something is calculated: sea level
**20** a [[flat]] [[even]] surface or area of land
**21** a [[degree]] or intensity reached on a measurable or notional scale: noise level
**22** **[[on the level]]** (*informal*) sincere or genuine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on the level]]**: honest, genuine, straight, fair, square, dinkum (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), above board
