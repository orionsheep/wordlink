**ambush** *n*
**1** the act of waiting in a concealed position to make a [[surprise]] [[attack]]
**2** an [[attack]] from such a position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trap]], [[snare]], [[lure]], waylaying
 *vb*
**3** to [[attack]] suddenly from a concealed position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trap]], [[attack]], [[surprise]], [[deceive]], [[dupe]], [[ensnare]], [[waylay]], [[bushwhack]] (*US*)
