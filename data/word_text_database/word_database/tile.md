**tile** *n*
**1** a thin piece of ceramic, plastic, etc., used with others to cover a surface, such as a floor or wall
**2** a rectangular block used as a playing piece in mah jong and other games
**3** **on the tiles** (*informal*) out having a good time and drinking a lot
 *vb* **tiling**, **[[tiled]]**
**4** to cover (a surface) with tiles
 **[[tiled]]** *adj* 
 **[[tiler]]** *n*
