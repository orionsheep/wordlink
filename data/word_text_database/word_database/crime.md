**crime** *n*
**1** an act prohibited and punished by law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offence]], [[violation]], [[trespass]], [[felony]], [[misdemeanour]], [[misdeed]], [[transgression]], [[unlawful act]]
**2** unlawful acts collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lawbreaking]], [[corruption]], [[illegality]], [[vice]], [[misconduct]], [[wrongdoing]], e-crime *or* ecrime, [[cybercrime]]
**3** (*informal*) a disgraceful act: it would be a crime to travel to Australia and not stop in Sydney
