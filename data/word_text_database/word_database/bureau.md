**bureau** (**byew**-roe) *n*, *pl* **-reaus*** or ***-reaux** (-rose)
**1** an [[office]] or agency, esp. one providing services for the public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[office]], [[department]], [[section]], [[branch]], [[station]], [[unit]], [[division]], [[subdivision]]
**2** (*US*) a government [[department]]
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a [[writing desk]] with pigeonholes and drawers against which the writing surface can be closed when not in use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desk]], [[writing desk]]
**4** (*US*) a chest of drawers
