◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farm]], [[work]], [[plant]], [[tend]], [[till]], [[plough]]
**2** to grow (plants)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farm]], [[work]], [[plant]], [[tend]], [[till]], [[plough]]
**3** to [[develop]] or improve (something) by giving special attention to it: he tried to cultivate a reputation for fairness

**4** to try to [[develop]] a friendship with (a person)
