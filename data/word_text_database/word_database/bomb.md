**bomb** *n*
**1** a hollow projectile containing [[explosive]], incendiary, or other destructive substance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explosive]], [[mine]], [[shell]], [[missile]], [[device]], [[rocket]], [[grenade]], [[torpedo]]
**2** an object in which an [[explosive]] [[device]] has been planted: a car bomb
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit* *slang*) a large sum of money: it cost a bomb
**4** (*slang*) a disastrous failure: the new play was a total bomb
**5** **like a bomb** (*informal*) with great speed or success
**6** **the bomb** a hydrogen or an atom bomb considered as the ultimate destructive weapon
 *vb*
**7** to [[attack]] with a bomb or bombs; drop bombs (on)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow up]], [[attack]], [[destroy]], [[assault]], [[shell]], [[blitz]], [[bombard]], [[torpedo]]
**8** (foll. by *along*) (*informal*) to move or drive very quickly
**9** (*slang*) to fail disastrously
 See also **bomb out**
 **[[bombing]]** *n*
