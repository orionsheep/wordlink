**duplicate** *adj*
**1** copied exactly from an original: he had a duplicate key to the front door
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[identical]], [[matched]], [[matching]], [[twin]], [[corresponding]], twofold
 *n*
**2** an exact [[copy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]], [[facsimile]]
**3** **in duplicate** in two exact copies: submit the draft in duplicate, please
 *vb* **-cating**, **-cated**
**4** to make an exact [[copy]] of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]]
**5** to do again (something that has already been done)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[repeat]], [[reproduce]], [[copy]], [[clone]], [[replicate]]
 **[[duplication]]** *n* 
 **[[duplicator]]** *n*
