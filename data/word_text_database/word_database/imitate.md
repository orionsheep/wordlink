**imitate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to [[copy]] the manner or style of, or take as a model: he remains rock's most imitated guitarist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]], [[follow]], [[repeat]], [[echo]], [[emulate]], [[ape]], [[simulate]], [[mirror]]
**2** to [[mimic]] or impersonate, esp. for amusement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[do an impression of]], [[mimic]], [[copy]]
**3** to make a [[copy]] or reproduction of; duplicate
 **[[imitable]]** *adj* 
 **[[imitator]]** *n*
