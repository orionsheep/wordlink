**licence** * or * (*US*) **license** *n*
**1** a [[document]] giving official [[permission]] to do, use, or own something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[certificate]], [[document]], [[permit]], [[charter]], [[warrant]]
**2** formal [[permission]] or exemption
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[permission]], [[the right]], [[authority]], [[leave]], [[sanction]], [[liberty]], [[immunity]], [[entitlement]]
**3** intentional disregard of conventional rules to achieve a certain effect: poetic licence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[freedom]], [[creativity]], [[latitude]], [[independence]], [[liberty]], [[deviation]], [[leeway]], [[free rein]]
**4** excessive [[freedom]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[laxity]], [[excess]], [[indulgence]], [[irresponsibility]], [[licentiousness]], [[immoderation]]
