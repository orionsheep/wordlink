**tumour** * or * (*US*) **tumor** (**tew**-mer) *n* (*pathol*)
**A** any abnormal [[swelling]]
 **B** a mass of tissue formed by a new [[growth]] of cells
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[growth]], [[cancer]], [[swelling]], [[lump]], [[carcinoma]] (*pathol*), [[sarcoma]] (*med*) 
 **[[tumorous]]** *adj*
