**plenty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** (often foll. by *of*) a great number or amount: plenty of time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lots of]] (*informal*), [[enough]], [[a great deal of]], [[masses of]], [[piles of]] (*informal*), [[stacks of]], [[heaps of]] (*informal*), [[an abundance of]]
**2** [[abundance]]: an age of plenty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abundance]], [[wealth]], [[prosperity]], [[fertility]], [[profusion]], [[affluence]], [[plenitude]], fruitfulness
 *adj*
**3** very many: there's plenty more fish in the sea
 *adv*
**4** (*informal*) more than adequately: that's plenty fast [[enough]] for me
