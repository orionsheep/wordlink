**betray** *vb*
**1** to hand over or [[expose]] (one's nation, friend, etc.) treacherously to an enemy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be disloyal to]], [[dob in]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[double-cross]] (*informal*), [[stab in the back]], [[be unfaithful to]], inform on *or* against
**2** to [[disclose]] (a secret or confidence) treacherously
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give away]], [[reveal]], [[expose]], [[disclose]], [[uncover]], [[divulge]], [[unmask]], [[let slip]]
**3** to [[reveal]] unintentionally: his singing voice betrays his origins
 **[[betrayal]]** *n* 
 **[[betrayer]]** *n*
