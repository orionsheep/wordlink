**inflation** *n*
**1** an inflating or being inflated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[expansion]], [[extension]], [[swelling]], [[escalation]], [[enlargement]]
**2** (*econ*) a progressive [[increase]] in the general level of prices brought about by an [[increase]] in the amount of money in circulation or by increases in costs
**3** (*informal*) the rate of [[increase]] of prices
 **[[inflationary]]** *adj*
