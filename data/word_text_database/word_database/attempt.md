**attempt** *vb*
**1** to make an [[effort]] (to do or achieve something); [[try]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[try]], [[seek]], [[aim]], [[struggle]], [[venture]], [[undertake]], [[strive]], endeavour
 *n*
**2** an endeavour to achieve something; [[effort]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[try]], [[go]] (*informal*), [[shot]] (*informal*), [[effort]], [[trial]], [[bid]], [[crack]] (*informal*), [[stab]] (*informal*)
**3** **attempt on someone's life** an attack on someone with the intention to kill
