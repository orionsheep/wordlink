**logic** *n*
**1** the branch of philosophy that analyses the patterns of [[reasoning]]
**2** a particular system of [[reasoning]]
**3** reasoned thought or argument, as distinguished from irrationality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reason]], [[reasoning]], [[sense]], [[good sense]]
**4** the interdependence of a series of events or facts
**5** (*electronics*, *computers*) the principles underlying the units in a computer system that produce results from data
