**slender** *adj*
**1** (esp. of a person's figure) [[slim]] and graceful
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[slim]], [[narrow]], [[slight]], [[lean]], [[willowy]]
**2** of [[small]] width relative to length or height
**3** [[small]] or inadequate in amount or size: a slender advantage
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[meagre]], [[little]], [[small]], [[scant]], [[scanty]]
