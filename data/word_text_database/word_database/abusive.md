**abusive** *adj*
**1** [[rude]] or [[insulting]]: he was alleged to have used abusive language towards spectators
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insulting]], [[offensive]], [[rude]], [[degrading]], [[scathing]], [[contemptuous]], [[disparaging]], [[scurrilous]]
**2** involving cruelty and ill-treatment: an abusive relationship
 **[[abusively]]** *adv*
