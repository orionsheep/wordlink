**flop** *vb* **flopping**, **flopped**
**1** to bend, [[fall]], or [[collapse]] loosely or carelessly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hang down]], [[hang]], [[dangle]], [[sag]], [[droop]]
**2** (*informal*) to [[fail]]: his first big film flopped
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fail]], [[fold]] (*informal*), [[founder]], [[fall flat]], [[come unstuck]], [[misfire]], [[go belly-up]] (*slang*)
**3** to [[fall]] or move with a sudden noise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slump]], [[fall]], [[drop]], [[collapse]], sink
 *n*
**4** (*informal*) a complete [[failure]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[failure]], [[disaster]], [[fiasco]], [[debacle]], [[washout]] (*informal*), [[nonstarter]]
**5** the act of flopping
 **[[floppy]]** *adj*
