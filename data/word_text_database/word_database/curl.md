**curl** *vb*
**1** to [[twist]] (hair) or (of hair) to grow in coils or ringlets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crimp]], [[wave]], [[perm]]
**2** to [[twist]] into a [[spiral]] or [[curve]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[twirl]], [[turn]], [[bend]], [[twist]], [[curve]], [[loop]], [[spiral]], [[coil]]
**3** to play the game of curling
**4** **curl one's lip** to show contempt by raising a corner of the lip
 *n*
**5** a [[coil]] of hair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ringlet]], [[lock]]
**6** a curved or [[spiral]] shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[twist]], [[spiral]], [[coil]], [[kink]], whorl
 **[[curly]]** *adj*
