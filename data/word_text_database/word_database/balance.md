**balance** *n*
**1** [[stability]] of mind or body: lose one's balance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equilibrium]], [[stability]], [[steadiness]], [[evenness]]
**2** a state of being in balance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stability]], [[equanimity]], [[steadiness]]
**3** harmony in the parts of a whole
**4** the power to influence or control: the balance of power
**5** something that remains: the balance of what you owe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remainder]], [[rest]], [[difference]], [[surplus]], [[residue]]
**6** (*accounting*)
**A** the matching of debit and credit totals in an account
 **B** a [[difference]] between such totals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remainder]], [[rest]], [[difference]], [[surplus]], [[residue]]
**7** a weighing device
**8** **in the balance** in an undecided condition
**9** **on balance** after weighing up all the factors
 *vb* **-ancing**, **-anced**
**10** to [[weigh]] in or as if in a balance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weigh]], [[consider]], [[compare]], [[estimate]], [[contrast]], [[assess]], [[evaluate]], [[set against]]
**11** to be or come into [[equilibrium]]
**12** to bring into or hold in [[equilibrium]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stabilize]], [[level]], [[steady]]
**13** to [[compare]] the relative weight or importance of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weigh]], [[consider]], [[compare]], [[estimate]], [[contrast]], [[assess]], [[evaluate]], [[set against]]
**14** to arrange so as to create a state of harmony
**15** (*accounting*) to [[compare]] or equalize the credit and debit totals of (an account)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calculate]], [[total]], [[determine]], [[estimate]], [[settle]], [[count]], [[square]], [[reckon]]
