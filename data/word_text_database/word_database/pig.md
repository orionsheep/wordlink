**pig** *n*
**1** a mammal with a long head, a snout, and bristle-covered skin, which is kept and killed for pork, ham, and bacon. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**porcine**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hog]] (*US*), [[sow]], [[boar]], [[swine]], [[porker]]
**2** (*informal*) a dirty, greedy, or bad-mannered person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slob]], [[glutton]]
**3** (*offensive*, *slang*) a police officer
**4** a mass of metal cast into a simple shape
**5** (*Brit* *informal*) something that is difficult or unpleasant: the coast is a pig for little boats
**6** **a pig in a poke** something bought or received without previous sight or knowledge
**7** **make a pig of oneself** (*informal*) to overeat
 *vb* **pigging**, **pigged**
**8** (of a [[sow]]) to give birth
**9** (often foll. by *out*) (*slang*) to eat greedily or to excess: she had pigged out on pizza before the show
