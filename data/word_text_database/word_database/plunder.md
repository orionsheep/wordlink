**plunder** *vb*
**1** to seize (valuables or goods) from (a place) by force, usually in wartime; [[loot]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steal]], [[rob]], [[take]], [[nick]] (*informal*), [[pinch]] (*informal*), [[embezzle]], [[pilfer]], thieve
 *n*
**2** anything plundered; [[booty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loot]], [[spoils]], [[booty]], [[swag]] (*slang*), [[ill-gotten gains]]
**3** the act of plundering; [[pillage]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pillage]]
