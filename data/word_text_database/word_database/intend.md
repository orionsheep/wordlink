**intend** *vb*
**1** to [[propose]] or [[plan]] (something or to do something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[mean]], [[aim]], [[propose]], [[purpose]], have in mind *or* view
**2** to have as one's [[purpose]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[mean]], [[aim]], [[propose]], [[purpose]], have in mind *or* view
**3** to [[mean]] to express or indicate: no criticism was intended
**4** (often foll. by *for*) to design or destine (for a certain [[purpose]] or person): the plane was never intended for combat
