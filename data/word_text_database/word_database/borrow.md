**borrow** *vb*
**1** to obtain (something, such as money) on the understanding that it will be returned to the lender
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take on loan]], touch (someone) for (*slang*), [[scrounge]] (*informal*), [[cadge]], [[use temporarily]]
**2** to [[adopt]] (ideas, words, etc.) from another source
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steal]], [[take]], [[copy]], [[adopt]], [[pinch]] (*informal*) 
 **[[borrower]]** *n* 
 **[[borrowing]]** *n*
