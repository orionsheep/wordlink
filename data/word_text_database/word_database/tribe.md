**tribe** *n*
**1** a group of families or clans believed to have a common ancestor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[race]], [[people]], [[family]], [[clan]], [[hapu]] (*NZ*), [[iwi]] (*NZ*)
**2** (*informal*) a group of [[people]] who do the same type of thing: a tribe of German yachtsmen
 **[[tribal]]** *adj*
