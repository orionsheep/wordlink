**reveal** *vb*
**1** to [[disclose]] or divulge (a secret)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make known]], [[disclose]], [[give away]], [[make public]], [[tell]], [[announce]], [[proclaim]], [[let out]]
**2** to expose to view or [[show]] (something concealed)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[display]], [[exhibit]], [[unveil]], [[uncover]], [[manifest]], [[unearth]], [[unmask]]
**3** (of God) to [[disclose]] (divine truths)
