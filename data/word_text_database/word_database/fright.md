**fright** *n*
**1** sudden [[fear]] or [[alarm]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fear]], [[shock]], [[alarm]], [[horror]], [[panic]], [[dread]], [[consternation]], [[trepidation]] (*formal*)
**2** a sudden alarming [[shock]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scare]], [[start]], [[turn]] (*informal*), [[surprise]], [[shock]], [[jolt]], [[the creeps]] (*informal*), [[the willies]] (*slang*)
**3** (*informal*) a very strange or unattractive person or thing
