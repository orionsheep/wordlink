**crawl** *vb*
**1** to move on one's hands and knees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creep]], [[slither]], [[inch]], [[wriggle]], [[writhe]], [[worm your way]], [[advance slowly]]
**2** (of insects, worms, or snakes) to [[creep]] slowly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creep]], [[slither]], [[inch]], [[wriggle]], [[writhe]], [[worm your way]], [[advance slowly]]
**3** to move very slowly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creep]], [[slither]], [[inch]], [[wriggle]], [[writhe]], [[worm your way]], [[advance slowly]]
**4** to act in a servile manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grovel]], [[creep]], [[humble yourself]]
**5** to be or feel as if covered with crawling creatures: the kind of smile that made your hair stand on end and your flesh crawl
 *n*
**6** a slow creeping pace or motion
**7** (*swimming*) a stroke in which the feet are kicked like paddles while each arm in turn reaches forward and pulls back through the water
 **[[crawler]]** *n*
