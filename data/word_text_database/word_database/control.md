**control** *n*
**1** [[power]] to [[direct]] something: the province is mostly under guerrilla control
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[power]], [[authority]], [[management]], [[command]], [[guidance]], [[supervision]], [[supremacy]], [[charge]]
**2** a [[curb]] or [[check]]: import controls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restraint]], [[check]], [[regulation]], [[brake]], [[limitation]], [[curb]]
**3** **controls** [[instruments]] used to operate a machine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instruments]], [[dash]], [[dials]], [[console]], [[dashboard]], [[control panel]]
**4** a standard of comparison used in an experiment
**5** an experiment used to verify another by having all aspects identical except for the one that is being tested
 *vb* **-trolling**, **-trolled**
**6** to [[have power over]]: the gland which controls the body's metabolic rate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[have power over]], [[manage]], [[direct]], [[handle]], [[command]], [[govern]], [[administer]], [[supervise]]
**7** to [[limit]] or [[restrain]]: he could not control his jealousy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[limit]], [[check]], [[contain]], [[curb]], [[hold back]], [[subdue]], [[repress]]
**8** to regulate or operate (a machine)
**9** to restrict the authorized supply of (certain drugs)
 **[[controllable]]** *adj*
