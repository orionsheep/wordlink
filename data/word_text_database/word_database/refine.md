**refine** *vb* **-fining**, **-fined**
**1** to make free from impurities; [[purify]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purify]], [[process]], [[filter]], [[cleanse]], [[clarify]], [[distil]]
**2** to [[improve]]: surgical techniques are constantly being refined
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[improve]], [[perfect]], [[polish]], [[hone]]
**3** to separate (a mixture) into pure constituents: molasses is a residual syrup obtained during sugar refining
