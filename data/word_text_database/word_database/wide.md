**wide** *adj*
**1** having a great extent from side to side: the wide main street
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spacious]], [[broad]], [[extensive]], [[roomy]], [[commodious]]
**2** having a specified extent from side to side: three metres wide
**3** covering or including many different things: a wide range of services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[broad]], [[extensive]], [[wide-ranging]], [[large]], [[sweeping]], [[vast]], [[immense]], [[expansive]]
**4** covering a [[large]] distance or extent: the proposal was voted down by a wide margin
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[large]], [[broad]], [[vast]], [[immense]]
**5** (of eyes) opened [[fully]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expanded]], [[dilated]], distended
 *adv*
**6** to a [[large]] or full extent: he swung the door wide
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fully]], [[completely]]
**7** **far and wide** See **far**
 *n*
**8** (*cricket*) a ball bowled outside the batsman's reach, which scores a run for the batting side
 **[[widely]]** *adv*
