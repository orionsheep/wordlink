**relate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to establish a relation between
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[relate to something or someone]]**: connect with, associate with, link with, couple with, join with, correlate to
**2** to have reference or relation to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[relate to something or someone]]**: concern, refer to, apply to, have to do with, pertain to, be relevant to
**3** to have an understanding (of people or ideas): the inability to relate to others
**4** to [[tell]] (a story) or [[describe]] (an event)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tell]], [[recount]], [[report]], [[detail]], [[describe]], [[recite]], [[narrate]]
