**rocket** *n*
**1** a self-propelling device, usually cylindrical, which produces thrust by expelling through a nozzle the gases produced by burning fuel, such as one used as a firework or distress signal
**2** any vehicle propelled by a rocket engine, as a weapon or carrying a spacecraft
**3** (*informal*) a severe reprimand: my sister gave me a rocket for writing such dangerous nonsense
 *vb* **-eting**, **-eted**
**4** to increase rapidly: within six years their turnover had rocketed
**5** to attack with rockets
