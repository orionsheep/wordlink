**circulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to send, go, or pass from place to place or person to person: rumours were circulating that he was about to resign
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread]], [[issue]], [[publish]], [[broadcast]], [[distribute]], [[publicize]], [[disseminate]], [[promulgate]]
**2** to move through a circuit or system, returning to the starting point: regular exercise keeps the blood circulating around the body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[revolve]], [[rotate]], [[radiate]]
**3** to move around the guests at a party, talking to different people: it wasn't like her not to circulate among all the guests
 **[[circulatory]]** *adj*
