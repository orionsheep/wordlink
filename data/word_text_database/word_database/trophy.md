**trophy** *n*, *pl* **-phies**
**1** a [[cup]], shield, etc., given as a [[prize]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prize]], [[cup]], [[award]], [[laurels]]
**2** a [[memento]] of success, esp. one taken in war or hunting: stuffed animal heads and other hunting trophies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[souvenir]], [[spoils]], [[relic]], [[memento]], [[booty]], keepsake
 *adj*
**3** (*informal*) regarded as a highly desirable symbol of wealth or success: a trophy wife
