**dense** *adj*
**1** thickly crowded or closely packed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thick]], [[heavy]], [[solid]], [[compact]], [[condensed]], [[impenetrable]], [[close-knit]]
**2** difficult to see through: dense clouds of smoke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heavy]], [[thick]], [[opaque]], [[impenetrable]]
**3** (*informal*) [[stupid]] or [[dull]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stupid]] (*informal*), [[thick]], [[dull]], [[dumb]] (*informal*), [[dozy]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[stolid]], [[dopey]] (*informal*), [[moronic]]
**4** (of a film, book, etc.) difficult to follow or understand: the content should be neither too dense nor too abstract
 **[[densely]]** *adv*
