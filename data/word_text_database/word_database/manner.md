**manner** *n*
**1** the [[way]] a thing happens or is done
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[style]], [[way]], [[fashion]], [[method]], [[custom]], [[mode]]
**2** a person's [[bearing]] and [[behaviour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[behaviour]], [[air]], [[bearing]], [[conduct]], [[aspect]], [[demeanour]]
**3** the [[style]] or customary [[way]] of doing something: sculpture in the Greek manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[style]], [[way]], [[fashion]], [[method]], [[custom]], [[mode]]
**4** [[type]] or [[kind]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[type]], [[form]], [[sort]], [[kind]], [[variety]], [[brand]], [[category]]
**5** **in a manner of speaking** in a [[way]]; so to speak
**6** **to the manner born** naturally fitted to a specified role or activity
