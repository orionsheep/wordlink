**warn** *vb*
**1** to make (someone) aware of a possible danger or problem
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[notify]], [[tell]], [[remind]], [[inform]], [[alert]], [[tip off]], [[give notice]], [[make someone aware]]
**2** to [[inform]] (someone) in advance: you'd better warn your girlfriend that you'll be working at the weekend
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[notify]], [[tell]], [[remind]], [[inform]], [[alert]], [[tip off]], [[give notice]], [[make someone aware]]
**3** **warn off** to advise (someone) to go away or not to do something
