**wrinkle** *n*
**1** a slight ridge in the smoothness of a surface, such as a [[crease]] in the skin as a result of age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[line]], [[fold]], [[crease]], [[furrow]], [[crow's-foot]], corrugation
 *vb* **-kling**, **-kled**
**2** to develop or cause to develop wrinkles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crease]], [[gather]], [[fold]], [[crumple]], [[furrow]], [[rumple]], [[pucker]], [[corrugate]]
 **[[wrinkled]]** * or ***[[wrinkly]]** *adj*
