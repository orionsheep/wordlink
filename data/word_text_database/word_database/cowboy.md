**cowboy** *n*
**1** (in the US and Canada) a ranch worker who herds and tends cattle, usually on horseback
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cowhand]], [[drover]], [[rancher]], [[stockman]], [[cattleman]], herdsman *or* woman (*Brit*), [[gaucho]]
**2** a conventional character of Wild West folklore or films
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cowhand]], [[drover]], [[rancher]], [[stockman]], [[cattleman]], herdsman *or* woman (*Brit*), [[gaucho]]
**3** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) an irresponsible or unscrupulous worker or businessperson
 **[[cowgirl]]** *fem n*
