**two** *n*
**1** the cardinal number that is the sum of one and one
**2** a numeral, 2 or II, representing this number
**3** something representing or consisting of two units
**4** **in two** in or into two parts: cut the cake in two and take a bit each
**5** **put two and two together** to reach an obvious conclusion by considering the evidence available
**6** **that makes two of us** the same applies to me
 *adj*
**7** amounting to two: two years
