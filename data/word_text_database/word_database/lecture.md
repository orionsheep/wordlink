**lecture** *n*
**1** a [[talk]] on a particular subject delivered to an audience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[talk]], [[address]], [[speech]], [[lesson]], [[instruction]], [[presentation]], [[discourse]], [[sermon]], [[webinar]]
**2** a lengthy [[scolding]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[telling-off]] (*informal*), [[rebuke]], [[reprimand]], [[talking-to]] (*informal*), [[scolding]], [[dressing-down]] (*informal*), reproof
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**3** to deliver a lecture (to an audience or class)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[talk]], [[speak]], [[teach]], [[address]], [[discourse]], [[spout]] (*informal*), [[expound]], [[hold forth]]
**4** to [[scold]] (someone) at length
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tell off]] (*informal*), [[berate]], [[scold]], [[reprimand]], [[censure]], [[castigate]], [[admonish]], [[reprove]]
