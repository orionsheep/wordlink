**survey** *vb*
**1** to [[view]] or consider (something) as a whole: she surveyed her purchases anxiously
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look over]], [[view]], [[examine]], [[observe]], [[contemplate]], [[inspect]], [[eyeball]] (*slang*), [[scrutinize]]
**2** to make a detailed map of (an area of land) by measuring or calculating distances and height
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[measure]], [[estimate]], [[assess]], [[appraise]]
**3** (*Brit*) to [[inspect]] (a building) to [[assess]] its condition and value
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[measure]], [[estimate]], [[assess]], [[appraise]]
**4** to make a detailed [[investigation]] of the behaviour, opinions, etc., of (a group of people)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interview]], [[question]], [[poll]], [[research]], investigate
 *n*
**5** a detailed [[investigation]] of the behaviour, opinions, etc., of a group of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[poll]], [[study]], [[research]], [[review]], [[inquiry]], [[investigation]]
**6** the act of making a detailed map of an area of land by measuring or calculating distance and height
**7** (*Brit*) an inspection of a building to [[assess]] its condition and value
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[valuation]], [[estimate]], [[assessment]], [[appraisal]]
 **[[surveying]]** *n* 
 **[[surveyor]]** *n*
