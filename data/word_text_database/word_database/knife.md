**knife** *n*, *pl* **knives**
**1** a cutting instrument or weapon consisting of a sharp-edged [[blade]] of metal fitted into a handle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blade]], [[carver]], cutter
 *vb* **knifing**, **[[knifed]]**
**2** to [[stab]] or kill with a knife
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut]], [[wound]], [[stab]], [[slash]], [[thrust]], [[pierce]], [[spear]], [[jab]]
 **[[knifelike]]** *adj*
