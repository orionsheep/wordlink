**rely** *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
 **rely on *or* upon**
**A** to be dependent on: the organization relies on voluntary contributions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depend on]], lean on
 **B** to have [[trust]] or confidence in: you can rely on his judgment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: be confident of, [[bank on]], [[trust]], [[count on]], [[bet on]]
