**technical** *adj*
**1** of or specializing in industrial, practical, or mechanical arts and applied sciences: a technical school
**2** [[skilled]] in practical activities rather than abstract thinking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scientific]], [[technological]], [[skilled]], [[specialist]], [[specialized]], hi-tech *or* high-tech
**3** relating to a particular field of activity: technical jargon
**4** according to the letter of the law: a last-minute penalty awarded to the Irish for a technical offence
**5** showing technique: technical perfection
 **[[technically]]** *adv*
