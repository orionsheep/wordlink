**subdue** *vb* **-duing**, **-dued**
**1** to [[overcome]] and bring (a person or people) under [[control]] by persuasion or force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overcome]], [[defeat]], [[master]], [[break]], [[control]], [[crush]], [[conquer]], [[tame]]
**2** to make (feelings, colour, or lighting) less intense
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moderate]], [[suppress]], [[soften]], [[mellow]], [[tone down]], [[quieten down]]
