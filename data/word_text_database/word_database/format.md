**format** *n*
**1** the shape, size, and general appearance of a publication
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrangement]], [[form]], [[style]], [[make-up]], [[look]], [[plan]], [[design]], [[type]]
**2** [[style]] or [[arrangement]], such as of a television programme: a chat-show format
**3** (*computers*) the [[arrangement]] of data on disk or magnetic tape to comply with a computer's input device
 *vb* **-matting**, **-matted**
**4** to arrange in a specified format
