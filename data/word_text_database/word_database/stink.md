**stink** *n*
**1** a strong unpleasant smell
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stench]], [[pong]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[foul smell]], [[fetor]]
**2** **make *or* create *or* kick up a stink** (*slang*) to make a fuss
 *vb* **stinking**, **stank*** or ***stunk**, **stunk**
**3** to give off a strong unpleasant smell
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reek]], [[pong]] (*Brit* *informal*)
**4** (*slang*) to be thoroughly bad or unpleasant: the script stinks, the casting stinks
 **[[stinky]]** *adj*
