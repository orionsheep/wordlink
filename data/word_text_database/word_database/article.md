**article** *n*
**1** a written [[composition]] in a magazine or newspaper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feature]], [[story]], [[paper]], [[piece]], [[item]], [[creation]], [[essay]], [[composition]]
**2** an [[item]] or [[object]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thing]], [[piece]], [[unit]], [[item]], [[object]], [[device]], [[tool]], [[implement]]
**3** a [[clause]] in a written document
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clause]], [[point]], [[part]], [[section]], [[item]], [[passage]], [[portion]], [[paragraph]]
**4** (*grammar*) any of the words *a, an,* or *the*
