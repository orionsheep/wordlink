**interview** *n*
**1** a formal discussion, esp. one in which an employer assesses a job applicant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting]]
**2** a conversation in which a well-known person is asked about his or her views, career, etc., by a reporter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[audience]], [[talk]], [[conference]], [[exchange]], [[dialogue]], [[consultation]], press [[conference]]
 *vb*
**3** to [[question]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[question]], [[interrogate]], [[examine]], [[investigate]], [[pump]], [[grill]] (*informal*), [[quiz]], [[cross-examine]]
 **[[interviewee]]** *n* 
 **[[interviewer]]** *n*
