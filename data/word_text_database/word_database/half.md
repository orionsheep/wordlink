**half** *n*, *pl* **halves**
**1** either of two equal or corresponding parts that together make up a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fifty per cent]], [[equal part]]
**2** the fraction equal to one divided by two
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fifty per cent]], [[equal part]]
**3** half a pint, esp. of beer
**4** (*sport*) one of two equal periods of play in a game
**5** a half-price ticket
**6** **by half** to an excessive degree: too clever by half
**7** **by halves** without being thorough: they rarely do things by halves
**8** **go halves** to share expenses
 *adj*
**9** denoting one of two equal parts: a half chicken
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[partial]], [[limited]], [[moderate]], halved
 *adv*
**10** half in degree or quantity: half as much
**11** [[partially]]; to an extent: half hidden in the trees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[partially]], [[partly]], [[in part]]
**12** **not half** (*Brit* *informal*)
**A** very; indeed: it isn't half hard to look at these charts
 **B** yes, indeed
