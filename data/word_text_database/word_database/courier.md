**courier** *n*
**1** a person who looks after and guides travellers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[guide]], [[representative]], [[escort]], [[conductor]]
**2** a person paid to deliver urgent messages or parcels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[messenger]], [[runner]], [[carrier]], [[bearer]], envoy
 *vb*
**3** to transport (a message or parcel) by courier
