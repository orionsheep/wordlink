**heavenly** *adj*
**1** (*informal*) [[wonderful]] or very enjoyable: a heavenly meal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wonderful]], [[lovely]], [[delightful]], [[beautiful]], [[divine]] (*informal*), [[exquisite]], [[sublime]], [[blissful]]
**2** of or occurring in space: a heavenly body
**3** of or relating to heaven
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[celestial]], [[holy]], [[divine]], [[blessed]], [[immortal]], [[angelic]]
