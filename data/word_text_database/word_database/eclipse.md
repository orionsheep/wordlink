**eclipse** *n*
**1** the [[obscuring]] of reflected light from a celestial body as it passes through the shadow of another; a **lunar eclipse** occurs when the moon passes through the shadow of the earth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obscuring]], [[covering]], [[blocking]], [[shading]], [[dimming]], [[extinction]], [[darkening]], [[blotting out]]
**2** a loss of importance, power, or fame: communism eventually went into eclipse
 *vb* **eclipsing**, **eclipsed**
**3** to [[overshadow]] or [[surpass]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surpass]], [[exceed]], [[overshadow]], [[excel]], [[transcend]], [[outdo]], [[outclass]], [[outshine]]
**4** (of a star or planet) to hide (another planet or star) from view
