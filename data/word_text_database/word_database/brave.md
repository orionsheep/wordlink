**brave** *adj*
**1** having or displaying courage, resolution, or [[daring]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[courageous]], [[daring]], [[bold]], [[heroic]], [[adventurous]], [[fearless]], [[resolute]], [[audacious]]
**2** fine; splendid: a brave sight
 *n*
**3** (*old-fashioned*) a warrior of a Native American tribe of N America
 *vb* **braving**, **braved**
**4** to [[confront]] with resolution or courage: she braved the 21 miles of Lake Tahoe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confront]], [[face]], [[suffer]], [[tackle]], [[endure]], [[defy]], [[withstand]], [[stand up to]]
 **[[bravery]]** *n*
