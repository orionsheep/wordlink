**grey** * or * (*US*) **gray** *adj*
**1** of a colour between black and white; of the colour of ashes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[dark]], [[dim]], [[gloomy]], [[drab]]
**2**
**A** (of hair) having partly turned white
 **B** (of a person) having grey hair
**3** dismal, [[dark]], or [[gloomy]]: a grey and misty morning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[dark]], [[dim]], [[gloomy]], [[drab]]
**4** [[dull]] or [[boring]]: in 1948 life generally was grey
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boring]], [[dull]], [[anonymous]], [[faceless]], [[colourless]], [[nondescript]], characterless
 *n*
**5** a colour between black and white
**6** anything grey, such as grey paint or grey clothing: available in grey or brown
**7** a grey or whitish horse
