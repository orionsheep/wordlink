**deny** *vb* **-nies**, **-nying**, **-nied**
**1** to declare (a statement) to be untrue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contradict]], [[disagree with]], [[rebuff]], [[negate]], [[rebut]], [[refute]]
**2** to [[refuse]] to give or allow: we have been denied permission
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refuse]], [[forbid]], [[reject]], [[rule out]], [[turn down]], [[prohibit]], [[withhold]], [[preclude]]
**3** to [[refuse]] to acknowledge: the baron denied his wicked son
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[renounce]], [[reject]], [[retract]], [[repudiate]], [[disown]], [[recant]], [[disclaim]]
