**disperse** *vb* **-persing**, **-persed**
**1** to [[scatter]] over a wide area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scatter]], [[spread]], [[distribute]], [[strew]], [[diffuse]], [[disseminate]], [[throw about]]
**2** to leave or cause to leave a gathering: police dispersed rioters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break up]], [[separate]], [[scatter]], [[dissolve]], [[disband]]
**3** to [[separate]] (light) into its different wavelengths
**4** to [[separate]] (particles) throughout a solid, liquid, or gas
 **[[dispersal]]** * or ***[[dispersion]]** *n*
