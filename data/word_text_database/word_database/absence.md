**absence** *n*
**1** the state of being away
**2** the time during which a person or thing is away
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[time off]], [[leave]], [[break]], [[vacation]], [[recess]], [[truancy]], [[absenteeism]], [[nonattendance]]
**3** the fact of being without something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lack]], [[deficiency]], [[omission]], [[scarcity]], [[want]], [[need]], [[shortage]], [[dearth]]
