**interference** *n*
**1** the act of interfering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intrusion]], [[intervention]], [[meddling]], [[opposition]], [[conflict]], [[obstruction]], [[prying]]
**2** any undesired signal that interferes with the reception of radio waves
**3** (*physics*) the meeting of two waves which reinforce or neutralize each other depending on whether they are in or out of phase
