**continue** *vb* **-tinuing**, **-tinued**
**1** to [[remain]] or cause to [[remain]] in a particular condition or place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remain]], [[last]], [[stay]], [[survive]], [[carry on]], [[live on]], [[endure]], [[persist]]
**2** to [[carry on]] (doing something): we continued kissing; heavy fighting continued until Thursday afternoon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[keep on]], [[go on]], [[maintain]], [[sustain]], [[carry on]], [[persist in]], [[persevere]], [[stick at]]
**3** to [[resume]] after an interruption: we'll continue after lunch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resume]], [[return to]], [[take up again]], [[proceed]], [[carry on]], [[recommence]], [[pick up where you left off]]
**4** to [[go on]] to a further place: the road continues on up the hill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go on]], [[progress]], [[proceed]], [[carry on]], [[keep going]], [[crack on]] (*informal*)
