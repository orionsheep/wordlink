**disappear** *vb*
**1** to [[cease]] to be visible; [[vanish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vanish]], [[recede]], [[evanesce]] (*formal*)
**2** to go away or become lost, esp. without explanation
**3** to [[cease]] to exist: the pain has disappeared
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cease]], [[dissolve]], [[evaporate]], [[perish]], [[die out]], [[pass away]], [[melt away]], [[leave no trace]]
 **[[disappearance]]** *n*
