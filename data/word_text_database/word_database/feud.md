**feud** *n*
**1** long and bitter [[hostility]] between two families, clans, or individuals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hostility]], [[row]], [[conflict]], [[argument]], [[disagreement]], [[rivalry]], [[quarrel]], vendetta
 *vb*
**2** to carry on a feud
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quarrel]], [[row]], [[clash]], [[dispute]], [[fall out]] (*informal*), [[contend]], [[war]], [[squabble]]
