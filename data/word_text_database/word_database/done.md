**done** *vb*
**1** the past participle of **do**
 *interj*
**2** an expression of agreement: £60 seems reasonable, done!
 *adj*
**3** (of a task) completed
**4** (of food) cooked enough
**5** used up: the milk is done
**6** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) socially acceptable: the done thing
**7** (*informal*) cheated or tricked
**8** **done in *or* up** (*informal*) exhausted
