**mantle** *n*
**1** (*old-fashioned*) a loose [[wrap]] or [[cloak]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cloak]], [[wrap]], [[cape]], [[hood]], [[shawl]]
**2** anything that covers completely or envelops: a mantle of snow covered the ground
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covering]], [[screen]], [[curtain]], [[blanket]], [[veil]], [[shroud]], [[canopy]], [[pall]]
**3** the responsibilities and duties which go with a particular job or position: he refuses to accept the mantle of leader
**4** a small mesh dome used to increase illumination in a gas or oil lamp by becoming incandescent
**5** (*geol*) the part of the earth between the crust and the core
 *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**6** to spread over or become spread over: mountains mantled in lush vegetation
