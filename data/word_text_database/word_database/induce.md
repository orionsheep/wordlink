**induce** *vb* **-ducing**, **-duced**
**1** to [[persuade]] or use [[influence]] on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persuade]], [[encourage]], [[influence]], [[convince]], [[urge]], [[prompt]], [[sway]], [[entice]]
**2** to [[cause]] or [[bring about]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cause]], [[produce]], [[create]], [[effect]], [[lead to]], [[occasion]] (*formal*), [[generate]], [[bring about]]
**3** (*med*) to [[cause]] (labour) to begin by the use of drugs or other means
**4** (*logic*, *obsolete*) to draw (a general conclusion) from particular instances
**5** to [[produce]] (an electromotive force or electrical current) by induction
**6** to transmit (magnetism) by induction
 **[[inducible]]** *adj*
