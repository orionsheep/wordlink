**plaster** *n*
**1** a mixture of lime, sand, and water that is applied to a wall or ceiling as a soft paste and dries as a hard coating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mortar]], [[stucco]], [[gypsum]], [[plaster of Paris]]
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) an adhesive strip of material for [[dressing]] a cut or wound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bandage]], [[dressing]], [[sticking plaster]], [[Elastoplast]] (*trademark*), adhesive plaster
 *vb*
**3** to [[coat]] (a wall or ceiling) with plaster
**4** to apply like plaster: he plastered his face with shaving cream
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[spread]], [[coat]], [[smear]], [[overlay]], [[daub]]
**5** to cause to lie flat or to adhere: his hair was plastered to his forehead
 **[[plasterer]]** *n*
