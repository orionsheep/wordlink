**tip**^3 *vb* **tipping**, **tipped**
**1** to tilt: he tipped back his chair
**2** **tip over** to tilt so as to overturn or fall: the box tipped over and the clothes in it spilled out
**3** (*Brit*) to [[dump]] (rubbish)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dump]], [[empty]], [[unload]], [[pour out]]
**4** to [[pour out]] (the contents of a container): he tipped the water from the basin down the sink
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pour]], [[drop]], [[empty]], [[dump]], [[drain]], [[discharge]], [[unload]], jettison
 *n*
**5** a rubbish [[dump]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dump]], [[midden]], [[rubbish heap]], [[refuse heap]]
