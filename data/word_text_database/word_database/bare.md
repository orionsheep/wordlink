**bare** *adj*
**1** [[unclothed]]: used esp. of a part of the body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[naked]], [[nude]], [[stripped]], [[uncovered]], [[undressed]], [[unclothed]], [[unclad]], [[without a stitch on]] (*informal*)
**2** without the natural, conventional, or usual covering: bare trees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[simple]], [[spare]], [[stark]], [[austere]], [[spartan]], [[unadorned]], [[unembellished]], [[unornamented]], [[bare-bones]]
**3** lacking appropriate furnishings, etc.: a bare room
**4** [[simple]]: the bare facts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plain]], [[simple]], [[basic]], [[obvious]], [[sheer]], [[patent]], [[evident]], [[stark]]
**5** just sufficient: the bare minimum
 *vb* **baring**, **bared**
**6** to uncover
 **[[bareness]]** *n*
