**pest** *n*
**1** an annoying person or thing; [[nuisance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nuisance]], [[trial]], [[pain]] (*informal*), [[drag]] (*informal*), [[bother]], [[irritation]], [[annoyance]], [[bane]]
**2** any organism that damages crops, or injures or irritates livestock or humans
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[infection]], [[bug]], [[insect]], [[plague]], [[epidemic]], [[blight]], [[scourge]], [[pestilence]], [[gogga]] (*S African* *informal*)
