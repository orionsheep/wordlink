**soar** *vb*
**1** to [[rise]] or [[fly]] upwards into the air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fly]], [[wing]], [[climb]], [[ascend]]
**2** (of a bird or aircraft) to glide while maintaining altitude
**3** to [[rise]] or [[increase]] suddenly above the usual level: television ratings soared
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rise]], [[increase]], [[grow]], [[mount]], [[climb]], [[go up]], [[rocket]], [[escalate]]
