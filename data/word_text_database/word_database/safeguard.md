**safeguard** *vb*
**1** to [[protect]] (someone or something) from being harmed or destroyed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]], [[guard]], [[defend]], [[save]], [[preserve]], [[look after]], keep safe
 *n*
**2** a person or thing that ensures [[protection]] against danger or harm: safeguards to prevent air collisions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[security]], [[defence]], [[guard]]
