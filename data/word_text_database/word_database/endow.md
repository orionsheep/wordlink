**endow** *vb*
**1** to provide with a source of permanent income, esp. by leaving money in a will
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[finance]], [[fund]], [[pay for]], [[award]], [[confer]], [[bestow]], [[bequeath]], [[donate money to]]
**2** **endowed with** [[provided]] with or possessing (a quality or talent)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[provided]], [[favoured]], [[graced]], [[blessed]], [[supplied]], [[furnished]]
