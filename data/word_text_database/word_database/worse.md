**worse** *adj*
**1** the comparative of **bad**
**2** **none the worse for** not harmed by (adverse events or circumstances)
**3** **the worse for wear** (*informal*) in a poor condition; not at one's best: returning the worse for wear from the pub
 *n*
**4** **for the worse** into a worse condition: taking a turn for the worse
 *adv*
**5** the comparative of **badly**
**6** **worse off** in a worse condition, esp. financially
