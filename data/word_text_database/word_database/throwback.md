**throwback** *n*
**1** a person or thing that is like something that existed or was common long ago: his ideas were a throwback to old colonial attitudes
**2** (*informal*) an occasion when someone is reminded of a past time
 *vb* **throw back**
**3** to remind someone of (something he or she said or did previously) in order to upset him or her: he threw back at me everything I'd said the week before
