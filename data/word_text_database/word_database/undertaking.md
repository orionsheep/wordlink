**undertaking** *n*
**1** a [[task]] or enterprise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[task]], [[business]], [[operation]], [[project]], [[attempt]], [[effort]], [[affair]], [[venture]]
**2** an agreement to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise]], [[commitment]], [[pledge]], [[word]], [[vow]], [[assurance]]
**3** (*informal*) the practice of overtaking on an inner lane a vehicle which is travelling in an outer lane
