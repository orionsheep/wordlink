**junior** *adj*
**1** [[lower]] in rank or position: junior officers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[minor]], [[lower]], [[secondary]], [[lesser]], [[subordinate]], [[inferior]]
**2** [[younger]]: world junior champion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[younger]]
**3** (in England and Wales) of schoolchildren between the ages of 7 and 11 approximately
**4** (*US*) of the third year of a four-year course at college or high school
 *n*
**5** a person holding a low rank or position
**6** a person who is [[younger]] than another person: the man she is to marry is 20 years her junior
**7** (in England and Wales) a junior schoolchild
**8** (*US*) a junior student
