**night** *n*
**1** the period of [[darkness]] that occurs each 24 hours, between sunset and sunrise. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**nocturnal**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[darkness]], [[dark]], [[night-time]]
**2** the period between sunset and bedtime; evening
**3** the time between bedtime and morning
**4** nightfall or dusk
**5** an evening designated for a specific activity: opening night
**6** **make a night of it** to celebrate the whole evening
 *adj*
**7** of, occurring, or working at night: the night sky
