**sweet** *adj*
**1** tasting of or like sugar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sugary]], [[cloying]], [[saccharine]], [[icky]] (*informal*)
**2** [[kind]] and [[charming]]: that was really sweet of you
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charming]], [[kind]], [[agreeable]]
**3** attractive and [[delightful]]: a sweet child
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delightful]], [[appealing]], [[cute]], [[winning]], [[engaging]], [[lovable]], likable *or* likeable
**4** (of a sound) pleasant and tuneful: sweet music
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[melodious]], [[musical]], [[harmonious]], [[mellow]], [[dulcet]]
**5** (of wine) having a high sugar content; not dry
**6** [[fresh]], clear, and [[clean]]: sweet water; sweet air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fresh]], [[clean]], [[pure]]
**7** **sweet on someone** fond of or infatuated with someone
 *n* (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*)
**8** a shaped piece of [[confectionery]] consisting mainly of sugar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confectionery]], [[candy]] (*US*), [[lolly]] (*Austral** & **NZ*), [[bonbon]]
**9** a [[dessert]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dessert]], [[pudding]]
 **[[sweetly]]** *adv* 
 **[[sweetness]]** *n*
