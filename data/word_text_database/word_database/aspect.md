**aspect** *n*
**1** a distinct [[feature]] or element in a problem or [[situation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feature]], [[side]], [[factor]], [[angle]], [[characteristic]], [[facet]]
**2** a [[position]] facing a particular direction: the room's east-facing aspect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[view]], [[situation]], [[scene]], [[prospect]], [[point of view]], [[outlook]]
**3** [[appearance]] or [[look]]: a room with a somewhat gloomy aspect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appearance]], [[look]], [[air]], [[condition]], [[quality]], [[bearing]], [[attitude]], [[cast]]
