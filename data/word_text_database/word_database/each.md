**each** *adj*
**1** [[every one]] of two or more people or things considered [[individually]]: each year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[every]], [[every]] single
 *pron*
**2** [[every one]] of two or more people or things: each had been given one room to design
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[every one]], [[all]], [[each one]], [[each and every one]], one and [[all]]
 *adv*
**3** for, to, or from each person or thing: twenty pounds each
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[apiece]], [[individually]], [[for each]], [[to each]], [[respectively]], [[per person]], [[per head]], [[per capita]]
**4** **each other** (of two or more people) [[each one]] to or at the other or others; one another: they stared at each other
