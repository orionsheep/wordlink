**relic** *n*
**1** an object or custom that has survived from the past
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remnant]], [[vestige]], [[memento]], [[trace]], [[fragment]], [[souvenir]], [[keepsake]]
**2** something valued for its past associations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remnant]], [[vestige]], [[memento]], [[trace]], [[fragment]], [[souvenir]], [[keepsake]]
**3** **relics** remaining parts or traces
**4** (*RC Church*, *Eastern churches*) a body part or possession of a saint, venerated as holy
