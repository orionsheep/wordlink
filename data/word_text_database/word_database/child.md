**child** *n*, *pl* **children**
**1** a young human being; boy or girl
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[youngster]], [[baby]], [[kid]] (*informal*), [[infant]], [[babe]], [[juvenile]], [[toddler]], [[tot]], [[littlie]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[ankle-biter]] (*Austral** & **US* *slang*), [[tacker]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** a son or daughter. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**filial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offspring]]
**3** a childish or immature person
**4** the product of an influence or environment: a child of the Army
**5** **with child** (*old-fashioned*) pregnant

 **[[childlessness]]** *n*
