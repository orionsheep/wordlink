**shout** *n*
**1** a loud [[call]] or [[cry]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cry]], [[call]], [[yell]], [[scream]], [[roar]], [[bellow]]
**2** (*informal*) one's turn to buy a round of drinks
 *vb*
**3** to [[cry]] out loudly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cry]] (out), [[call]] (out), [[yell]], [[scream]], [[roar]], [[bellow]], [[bawl]], [[holler]] (*informal*)
**4** (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) to treat (someone) to (something, such as a drink)
