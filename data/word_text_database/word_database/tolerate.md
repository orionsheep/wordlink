**tolerate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to [[allow]] something to exist or happen, even though one does not approve of it: you must learn to tolerate opinions other than your own
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allow]], [[accept]], [[permit]], [[take]], [[brook]], [[put up with]] (*informal*), [[condone]]
**2** to [[put up with]] (someone or something): he found the pain hard to tolerate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[endure]], [[stand]], [[take]], [[stomach]], [[put up with]] (*informal*) 
 **[[toleration]]** *n*
