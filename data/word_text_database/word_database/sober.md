**sober** *adj*
**1** not drunk
**2** tending to drink only [[moderate]] quantities of alcohol
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abstinent]], [[temperate]], [[abstemious]], [[moderate]]
**3** [[serious]] and thoughtful: a sober and [[serious]] fellow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serious]], [[cool]], [[grave]], [[reasonable]], [[steady]], [[composed]], [[rational]], [[solemn]], [[grounded]]
**4** (of colours) [[plain]] and dull
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plain]], [[dark]], [[sombre]], [[quiet]], [[subdued]], [[drab]]
**5** free from exaggeration: a fairly sober version of what happened
 *vb*
**6** (usually foll. by *up*) to make or become less drunk
 **[[sobering]]** *adj*
