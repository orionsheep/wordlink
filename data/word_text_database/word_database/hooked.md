**hooked** *adj*
**1** [[bent]] like a hook
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bent]], [[curved]], [[aquiline]], [[hook-shaped]]
**2** (often foll. by *on*)
**A** (*slang*) [[addicted]] (to): hooked on drugs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[addicted]], [[dependent]], [[using]] (*informal*), having a habit
 **B** obsessed (with): hooked on football
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: obsessed, [[addicted]], [[taken]], [[devoted]], [[turned on]] (*slang*), [[enamoured]]
