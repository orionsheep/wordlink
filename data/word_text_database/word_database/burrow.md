**burrow** *n*
**1** a [[hole]] dug in the ground by a rabbit or other small animal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hole]], [[shelter]], [[tunnel]], [[den]], [[lair]], retreat
 *vb*
**2** to [[dig]] (a [[tunnel]] or [[hole]]) in, through, or under ground
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dig]], [[tunnel]], [[excavate]]
**3** to move through a place by or as if by digging
**4** to [[delve]] deeply: he burrowed into his coat pocket
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delve]], [[search]], [[probe]], [[ferret]], [[rummage]], [[forage]], [[fossick]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**5** to live in or as if in a burrow
