**discomfort** *n*
**1** a mild [[pain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pain]], [[hurt]], [[ache]], [[throbbing]], [[irritation]], [[tenderness]], [[pang]], [[malaise]]
**2** a feeling of [[worry]] or embarrassment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneasiness]], [[worry]], [[anxiety]], [[doubt]], [[distress]], [[misgiving]], [[qualms]], [[trepidation]] (*formal*)
**3** **discomforts** conditions that cause physical uncomfortableness: the physical discomforts of pregnancy
