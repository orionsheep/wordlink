**expectation** *n*
**1** the state of expecting or of being expected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[projection]], [[supposition]], [[assumption]], [[belief]], [[forecast]], [[likelihood]], [[probability]], [[presumption]]
**2** something looked forward to, whether feared or hoped for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anticipation]], [[hope]], [[promise]], [[excitement]], [[expectancy]], [[apprehension]], [[suspense]]
**3** [[belief]] that someone should behave in a particular way: unrealistic expectations of yourself and others
