**wink** *vb*
**1** to close and open one eye quickly as a signal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blink]], [[bat]], [[flutter]]
**2** (of a light) to [[shine]] brightly and intermittently; [[twinkle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[twinkle]], [[flash]], [[shine]], [[sparkle]], [[gleam]], [[shimmer]], glimmer
 *n*
**3** the act or an instance of winking, esp. as a signal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blink]], [[flutter]]
**4** a twinkling of light
**5** (*informal*) the smallest amount of sleep: I didn't sleep a wink last night
**6** **tip someone the wink** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) to give someone a hint or warning
