**minimize** * or ***-mise** *vb* **-mizing**, **-mized*** or ***-mising**, **-mised**
**1** to [[reduce]] to the lowest possible degree or amount: these measures should help minimize our costs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reduce]], [[decrease]], [[shrink]], [[diminish]], [[prune]], [[curtail]], [[miniaturize]]
**2** to regard or treat as less important than it really is; [[belittle]]: I don't want to minimize the importance of her contribution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[play down]], [[discount]], [[belittle]], [[disparage]], [[decry]], [[underrate]], [[deprecate]], make light *or* little of
