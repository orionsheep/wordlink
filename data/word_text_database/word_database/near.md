**near** *prep*
**1** at or to a place or time not far away from
 *adv*
**2** at or to a place or time not far away
**3** short for **nearly**: the pain damn near crippled him
 *adj*
**4** at or in a place or time not far away: in the near future
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[close]], [[neighbouring]], [[nearby]], [[adjacent]], [[adjoining]]
**5** closely connected or intimate: a near relation
**6** almost being the thing specified: a mood of near rebellion
 *vb*
**7** to draw [[close]] (to): the participants are nearing agreement
 **[[nearness]]** *n*
