**discover** *vb*
**1** to be the first to [[find]] or [[find out]] about
**2** to [[learn]] about for the first time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[find out]], [[learn]], [[notice]], [[realize]], [[recognize]], [[perceive]], [[detect]], [[uncover]]
**3** to [[find]] after study or search
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[find]], [[come across]], [[uncover]], [[unearth]], [[turn up]], [[dig up]], [[come upon]]
 **[[discoverer]]** *n*
