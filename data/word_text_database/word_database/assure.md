**assure** *vb* **-suring**, **-sured**
**1** to promise or [[guarantee]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise to]], [[pledge to]], [[vow to]], [[guarantee to]], [[swear to]], [[confirm to]], [[certify to]], [[give your word to]]
**2** to [[convince]]: they assured me that they had not seen the document
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[convince]], [[encourage]], [[persuade]], [[satisfy]], [[comfort]], [[reassure]], [[hearten]], [[embolden]]
**3** to make (something) certain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make certain]], [[ensure]], [[confirm]], [[guarantee]], [[secure]], [[make sure]], [[complete]], [[seal]]
**4** (*chiefly* *Brit*) to insure against loss of life
