**reconstruct** *vb*
**1** to build again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rebuild]], [[restore]], [[re-create]], [[remake]], [[renovate]], [[remodel]], [[regenerate]]
**2** to reorganize: three works proved useful in reconstructing the training routine
**3** to form a picture of (a past event, esp. a crime) by piecing together evidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[build up a picture of]], [[build up]], [[piece together]], [[deduce]]
 **[[reconstruction]]** *n*
