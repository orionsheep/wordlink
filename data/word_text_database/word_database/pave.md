**pave** *vb* **paving**, **paved**
**1** to [[cover]] (a road or area of ground) with a firm [[surface]] to make it suitable for walking or travelling on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[floor]], [[surface]], [[concrete]], [[tile]]
**2** **pave the way for** to prepare or make easier: the arrests paved the way for the biggest-ever Mafia trial
