**chamois** *n*, *pl* **-ois**
**1** (**sham**-wah) a small mountain antelope of Europe and SW Asia
**2** (**sham**-ee) a soft suede leather made from the skin of this animal or from sheep or goats
**3** (**sham**-ee) Also: **chamois leather**, **shammy**, **chammy** a piece of such leather or similar material, used for cleaning and polishing
