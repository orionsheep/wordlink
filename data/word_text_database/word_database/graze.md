**graze**^2 *vb* **grazing**, **grazed**
**1** to break the [[skin]] of (a part of the body) by scraping
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scratch]], [[skin]], [[scrape]], [[chafe]], [[abrade]]
**2** to [[brush]] against someone gently in passing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[touch]], [[brush]], [[rub]], [[scrape]], [[shave]], [[skim]], glance off
 *n*
**3** an injury on the [[skin]] caused by scraping
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scratch]], [[scrape]], [[abrasion]]
