**otherwise** *conj*
**1** [[or else]]; [[if not]], then: you need to leave by ten o'clock, otherwise you'll be late
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[or else]], [[or]], [[if not]], [[or]] then
 *adv*
**2** [[differently]]: it was fruitless to pretend [[or]] to hope otherwise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[differently]], [[any other way]], [[contrarily]]
**3** in other respects: shrewd psychological twists perk up an otherwise predictable storyline
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[apart from that]], [[in other ways]], in (all) other respects
 *adj*
**4** different: circumstances beyond our control dictated that it should be otherwise
 *pron*
**5** **[[or]] otherwise** [[or]] not; [[or]] the opposite: he didn't want company, talkative [[or]] otherwise
