**throng** *n*
**1** a great number of people or things crowded together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowd]], [[mob]], [[horde]], [[host]], [[pack]], [[mass]], [[crush]], swarm
 *vb*
**2** to gather in or fill (a place) in large numbers: streets thronged with shoppers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pack]], [[crowd]]
