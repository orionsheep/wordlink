**beside** *prep*
**1** [[next to]]; at, by, or to the side of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[next to]], [[near]], [[close to]], [[neighbouring]], [[alongside]], [[adjacent to]], [[at the side of]], [[abreast of]]
**2** as compared with
**3** away from: beside the point
**4** **beside oneself** overwhelmed; overwrought: beside oneself with grief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[beside yourself]]**: distraught, desperate, distressed, frantic, frenzied, unhinged, overwrought
 *adv*
**5** at, by, to, or along the side of something or someone
