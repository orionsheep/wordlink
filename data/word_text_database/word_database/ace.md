**ace** *n*
**1** a playing card with one symbol on it
**2** (*informal*) an [[expert]]: an American stock car ace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expert]], [[star]], [[champion]], [[authority]], [[professional]], [[master]], [[specialist]], [[guru]]
**3** (*tennis*) a winning serve that the opponent fails to reach
**4** a fighter pilot who has destroyed several enemy aircraft
 *adj*
**5** (*informal*) [[superb]] or [[excellent]]: an ace skateboarder
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[great]] (*informal*), [[brilliant]], [[fine]], [[wonderful]], [[excellent]], [[outstanding]], [[superb]], [[fantastic]] (*informal*), [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[ka pai]] (*NZ*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*)
