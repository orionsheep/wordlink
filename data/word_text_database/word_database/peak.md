**peak** *n*
**1** a pointed [[tip]] or projection: the peak of the roof
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[top]], [[tip]], [[summit]], [[brow]], [[crest]], [[pinnacle]], [[apex]]
**2**
**A** the pointed [[summit]] of a mountain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[top]], [[tip]], [[summit]], [[brow]], [[crest]], [[pinnacle]], [[apex]]
 **B** a mountain with a pointed [[summit]]
**3** the [[point]] of greatest success or achievement: the peak of his career
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[high point]], [[crown]], [[climax]], [[culmination]], [[zenith]], [[acme]]
**4** a projecting piece on the front of some caps
 *vb*
**5** to form or reach a peak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[culminate]], [[climax]], come to a head
 *adj*
**6** of or relating to a period of greatest demand: hotels are generally dearer in peak season
