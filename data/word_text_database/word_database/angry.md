**angry** *adj* **-grier**, **-griest**
**1** feeling or expressing annoyance or rage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[furious]], [[cross]], [[mad]] (*informal*), [[outraged]], [[annoyed]], [[infuriated]], [[incensed]], [[enraged]], [[tooshie]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[off the air]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[aerated]]
**2** severely inflamed: he had angry welts on his forehead
**3** dark and stormy: angry waves
 **[[angrily]]** *adv*
