**rotate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to [[turn]] around a centre or [[pivot]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[revolve]], [[turn]], [[wheel]], [[spin]], [[reel]], [[go round]], [[swivel]], [[pivot]]
**2** to follow or cause to follow a set sequence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[follow in sequence]], [[switch]], [[alternate]], [[take turns]]
**3** to regularly change the type of crop grown on a piece of land in order to preserve the fertility of the soil
 **[[rotation]]** *n* 
 **[[rotational]]** *adj*
