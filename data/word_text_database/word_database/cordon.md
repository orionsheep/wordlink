**cordon** *n*
**1** a [[chain]] of police, soldiers, or vehicles guarding an area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chain]], [[line]], [[ring]], [[barrier]], [[picket line]]
**2** an ornamental braid or ribbon
**3** (*horticulture*) a fruit tree trained to grow as a single stem bearing fruit
 *vb*
**4** **cordon off** to put or form a cordon round
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[cordon something off]]**: surround, isolate, close off, fence off, separate, enclose, picket, encircle
