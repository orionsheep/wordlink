**differentiate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to perceive or show the difference (between)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distinguish]], [[separate]], [[discriminate]], [[contrast]], [[mark off]], [[make a distinction]], [[tell apart]], set off *or* apart
**2** to make (one thing) distinct from other such things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make different]], [[separate]], [[distinguish]], [[characterize]], [[single out]], [[segregate]], [[individualize]], [[mark off]]
**3** (*maths*) to determine the derivative of a function or variable
 **[[differentiation]]** *n*
