**derelict** *adj*
**1** [[abandoned]] or unused and falling into ruins
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abandoned]], [[deserted]], [[ruined]], [[neglected]], [[discarded]], [[forsaken]], dilapidated
 *n*
**2** a social [[outcast]] or [[vagrant]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vagrant]], [[tramp]] (*old-fashioned*), [[outcast]], [[drifter]], [[down-and-out]], [[bag lady]], [[derro]] (*Austral* *slang*)
