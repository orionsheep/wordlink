**topple** *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**1** to fall over or cause (something) to fall over, esp. from a height: he staggered back against the railing and toppled over into the river
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[knock over]]
**2** to [[overthrow]] or [[oust]]: few believe the scandal will topple the government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overthrow]], [[overturn]], [[bring down]], [[oust]], [[unseat]], [[bring low]]
