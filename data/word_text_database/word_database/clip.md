**clip**^2 *n*
**1** a device for attaching or holding things together
**2** an article of jewellery that can be clipped onto a dress or hat
 *vb* **clipping**, **clipped**
**3** to [[attach]] or [[hold]] together with a clip
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attach]], [[fix]], [[secure]], [[connect]], [[pin]], [[staple]], [[fasten]], [[hold]]
