**fluid** *n*
**1** a substance, such as a [[liquid]] or gas, that can flow and has no fixed shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liquid]], [[solution]], [[juice]], [[liquor]], sap
 *adj*
**2** capable of [[flowing]] and easily changing shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liquid]], [[flowing]], [[watery]], [[molten]], [[melted]], [[runny]], [[liquefied]]
**3** constantly changing or apt to change
 **[[fluidity]]** *n*
