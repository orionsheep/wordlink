**hire** *vb* **[[hiring]]**, **hired**
**1** to acquire the temporary use of (a thing) or the services of (a person) in exchange for payment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[employ]], [[commission]], [[take on]], [[engage]], [[appoint]], [[sign up]], [[enlist]]
**2** to [[employ]] (a person) for wages
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[employ]], [[commission]], [[take on]], [[engage]], [[appoint]], [[sign up]], [[enlist]]
**3** to provide (something) or the services of (oneself or others) for payment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rent]], [[charter]], [[lease]], [[let]], [[engage]]
**4** **hire out** (*chiefly* *Brit*) to pay independent contractors for (work to be done)
 *n*
**5** the act of [[hiring]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rental]], [[hiring]], [[rent]], [[lease]]
**6** **for hire** available to be hired
