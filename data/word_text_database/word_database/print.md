**print** *vb*
**1** to [[reproduce]] (a newspaper, book, etc.) in large quantities by mechanical or electronic means
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[publish]], [[release]], [[circulate]], [[issue]], [[disseminate]]
**2** to [[reproduce]] (text or pictures) by applying ink to paper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run off]], [[publish]], [[copy]], [[reproduce]], [[issue]], [[engrave]]
**3** to write in letters that are not joined up
**4** to [[stamp]] (fabric) with a design
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[impress]], [[stamp]], [[imprint]]
**5** to produce (a [[photograph]]) from a negative
**6** to fix in the mind or memory
 *n*
**7** printed content, such as newsprint
**8** a printed publication, such as a book
**9** a [[picture]] printed from an engraved [[plate]] or wood block
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[picture]], [[plate]], [[etching]], [[engraving]], [[lithograph]], [[woodcut]], [[linocut]]
**10** printed text, with regard to the typeface: italic print
**11** a [[photograph]] produced from a negative
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[photograph]], [[photo]], [[snap]]
**12** a fabric with a printed design
**13** a [[mark]] made by pressing something onto a surface
**14** See **fingerprint**
**15** **in print**
**A** in printed or published form
 **B** (of a book) available from a publisher
**16** **out of print** no longer available from a publisher
