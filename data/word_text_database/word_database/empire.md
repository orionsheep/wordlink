**empire** *n*
**1** a group of countries under the rule of a single person or sovereign state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kingdom]], [[territory]], [[province]], [[federation]], [[commonwealth]], [[realm]], [[domain]]
**2** a large industrial [[organization]] that is controlled by one person: the heiress to a jewellery empire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[organization]], [[company]], [[business]], [[firm]], [[concern]], [[corporation]], [[consortium]], [[syndicate]]
