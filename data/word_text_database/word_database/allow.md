**allow** *vb*
**1** to [[permit]] someone to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[permit]], [[approve]], [[enable]], [[sanction]], [[endure]], [[license]], [[tolerate]], [[authorize]]
**2** to [[set aside]]: I allowed plenty of time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give]], [[provide]], [[grant]], [[spare]], [[devote]], [[assign]], [[allocate]], [[set aside]]
**3** to [[acknowledge]] (a point or claim)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acknowledge]], [[accept]], [[admit]], [[grant]], [[recognize]], [[yield]], [[concede]], [[confess]]
**4** **allow for** to take into account
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[allow for something]]**: take into account, consider, plan for, accommodate, [[provide]] for, make provision for, make allowances for, make concessions for
 **[[allowable]]** *adj*
