**revenge** *n*
**1** [[vengeance]] for wrongs or injury received
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[retaliation]], [[vengeance]], [[reprisal]], [[retribution]], [[an eye for an eye]]
**2** something done as a means of [[vengeance]]
 *vb* **-venging**, **-venged**
**3** to inflict equivalent injury or damage for (injury received)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[avenge]], [[repay]], [[take revenge for]], [[get your own back for]] (*informal*)
**4** to take [[vengeance]] for (oneself or another)
 **[[revengeful]]** *adj*
