**negotiate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to talk with others in order to reach (an agreement)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bargain]], [[deal]], [[discuss]], [[debate]], [[mediate]], [[hold talks]], [[cut a deal]], [[conciliate]]
**2** to succeed in passing round or over (a place or a problem)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrange]], [[work out]], [[bring about]], [[transact]]
 **[[negotiation]]** *n* 
 **[[negotiator]]** *n*
