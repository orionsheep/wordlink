**worst** *adj*, *adv*
**1** the superlative of **bad**, **badly**
 *n*
**2** the least good or the most terrible person, thing, or part: the worst is yet to come
**3** **at one's worst** in the worst condition or aspect of a thing or person: the British male is at his worst in July and August
**4** **at worst** in the least favourable interpretation or conditions: all the questions should ideally be answered 'no', or at worst 'sometimes'
 *vb*
**5** (*old-fashioned*) to defeat or beat
