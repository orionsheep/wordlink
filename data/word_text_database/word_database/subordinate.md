**subordinate** *adj*
**1** of [[lesser]] rank or importance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inferior]], [[lesser]], [[lower]], [[junior]], [[subject]], [[minor]], [[secondary]], dependent
 *n*
**2** a person or thing that is of [[lesser]] rank or importance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inferior]], [[junior]], [[assistant]], [[aide]], [[second]], attendant
 *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**3** (usually foll. by *to*) to regard (something) as less important than another: the army's interests were subordinated to those of the air force
 **[[subordination]]** *n*
