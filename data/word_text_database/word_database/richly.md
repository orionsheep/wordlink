**richly** *adv*
**1** in a rich or elaborate manner: the rooms are richly decorated with a variety of classical motifs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[elaborately]], [[lavishly]], [[elegantly]], [[splendidly]], [[exquisitely]], [[expensively]], [[luxuriously]], [[gorgeously]]
**2** [[fully]] and [[appropriately]]: he left the field to a richly deserved standing ovation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fully]], [[well]], [[thoroughly]], [[amply]], [[appropriately]], [[properly]], [[suitably]]
