**magazine** *n*
**1** a periodic [[publication]] containing written pieces and illustrations, usually on a particular subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[journal]], [[publication]], [[supplement]], [[rag]] (*informal*), [[issue]], [[glossy]] (*informal*), [[pamphlet]], [[periodical]], ezine *or* e-zine
**2** a television or radio programme made up of short nonfictional items
**3** a metal case holding several cartridges used in some firearms
**4** a rack for automatically feeding slides through a projector
**5** a place for storing weapons, explosives, or military equipment
