**menu** *n*
**1** a list of dishes served at a meal or that can be ordered in a restaurant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bill of fare]], [[tariff]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[set menu]], [[table d'hôte]], [[carte du jour]] (*French*)
**2** a list of options displayed on a visual display unit from which the operator can choose
