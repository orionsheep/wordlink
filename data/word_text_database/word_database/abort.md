**abort** *vb*
**1** (of a pregnancy) to [[end]] before the fetus is viable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terminate]]
**2** to perform an abortion on a pregnant woman
**3** to [[end]] a plan or process before completion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[end]], [[finish]], [[check]], [[arrest]], [[halt]], [[cease]], [[axe]] (*informal*)
