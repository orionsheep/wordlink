**fiction** *n*
**1** literary works invented by the imagination, such as novels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tale]], [[story]], [[novel]], [[legend]], [[myth]], [[romance]], [[narration]], [[creative writing]]
**2** an invented [[story]] or explanation: the fiction that cricket is more noble than other sports
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lie]], [[invention]], [[fabrication]], [[falsehood]], [[untruth]], [[urban myth]], [[tall story]], [[urban legend]]
**3** (*law*) something assumed to be true for the sake of convenience, though probably false
 **[[fictional]]** *adj*
