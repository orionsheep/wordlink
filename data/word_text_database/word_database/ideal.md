**ideal** *n*
**1** (*often pl*) a principle or [[model]] of ethical behaviour
**2** a conception of something that is [[perfect]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[model]], [[prototype]], [[paradigm]]
**3** a person or thing considered to represent [[perfection]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[epitome]], [[standard]], [[dream]], [[pattern]], [[perfection]], [[last word]], [[paragon]]
**4** something existing only as an idea
 *adj*
**5** most suitable: the ideal person to head the delegation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perfect]], [[best]], [[model]], [[classic]], [[supreme]], [[ultimate]], [[archetypal]], [[exemplary]]
**6** of, involving, or existing only as an idea; imaginary: an ideal world
 **[[ideally]]** *adv*
