◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[record]], [[story]], [[history]], [[account]], [[register]], [[journal]], [[diary]], [[narrative]], blog
 *vb* **-cling**, **-cled**
**2** to [[record]] in or as if in a chronicle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[record]], [[tell]], [[report]], [[enter]], [[relate]], [[register]], [[recount]], [[set down]]
 **[[chronicler]]** *n*
