**ancient** *adj*
**1** dating from very long ago
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[classical]], [[old]], [[former]], [[past]], [[bygone]], [[primordial]], [[primeval]], [[olden]]
**2** [[very old]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[very old]], [[aged]], [[antique]], [[archaic]], [[timeworn]]
**3** of the far [[past]], esp. before the collapse of the Western Roman Empire (476 <small>AD</small>)
 *n*
**4** **ancients** people who lived very long ago, such as the Romans and Greeks
