**objective** *n*
**1** an [[aim]] or [[purpose]]: the objective is to highlight the environmental threat to the planet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purpose]], [[aim]], [[goal]], [[end]], [[plan]], [[hope]], [[idea]], [[target]]
**2** (*grammar*) a grammatical case in some languages that identifies the direct object of a verb or preposition
**3** (*optics*) the lens nearest to the object observed in an optical instrument
 *adj*
**4** not distorted by personal feelings or bias: I have tried to be as objective as possible in my presentation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unbiased]], [[detached]], [[fair]], [[open-minded]], [[impartial]], [[impersonal]], [[disinterested]], [[even-handed]]
**5** of or relating to actual facts as opposed to thoughts or feelings: stand back and try to take a more objective view of your life as a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unbiased]], [[detached]], [[fair]], [[open-minded]], [[impartial]], [[impersonal]], [[disinterested]], [[even-handed]]
**6** existing independently of the mind; [[real]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[factual]], [[real]]
 **[[objectival]]** *adj* 
 **[[objectively]]** *adv* 
 **[[objectivity]]** *n*
