**cracking** *adj*
**1** **get cracking** (*informal*) to start doing something immediately
**2** **a cracking pace** (*informal*) a high speed
 *adv*, *adj*
**3** (*Brit* *informal*) first-class: five cracking good saves
 *n*
**4** the oil-refining process in which heavy oils are broken down into smaller molecules by heat or catalysis
