**ashamed** *adj*
**1** overcome with shame or remorse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[embarrassed]], [[sorry]], [[guilty]], [[distressed]], [[humiliated]], [[self-conscious]], [[red-faced]], [[mortified]]
**2** unwilling through fear of humiliation or shame: she'd be ashamed to admit to jealousy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reluctant]], [[embarrassed]]
