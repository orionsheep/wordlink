**ask** *vb*
**1** to say or write (something) in a form that requires an answer: I asked him his name; 'do you think we'll have trouble landing?' he asked
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inquire]], [[question]], [[quiz]], [[query]], [[interrogate]]
**2** to make a request or demand: the chairman asked for a show of hands
**3** to [[invite]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[invite]], [[bid]], [[summon]]
**4** to [[inquire]] about: I pretended to be lost and asked for directions
**5** to expect: is that too much to ask?
