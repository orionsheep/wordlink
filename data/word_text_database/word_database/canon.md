**canon**^2 *n*
**1** (*Christianity*) a Church decree regulating morals or religious practices
**2** a general [[rule]] or [[standard]]: the Marx-Engels canon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rule]], [[standard]], [[principle]], [[regulation]], [[formula]], [[criterion]], [[dictate]], [[statute]]
**3** a [[list]] of the works of an author that are accepted as authentic: the Yeats canon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[index]], [[catalogue]], [[roll]]
**4** a piece of music in which a melody in one part is taken up in one or more other parts successively
