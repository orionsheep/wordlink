**delegate** *n*
**1** a person chosen to represent others at a conference or meeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representative]], [[agent]], [[deputy]], [[ambassador]], [[commissioner]], [[envoy]], [[proxy]], legate
 *vb* **-gating**, **-gated**
**2** to [[entrust]] (duties or powers) to another person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entrust]], [[transfer]], [[hand over]], [[give]], [[pass on]], [[assign]], [[consign]], [[devolve]]
**3** to [[appoint]] as a [[representative]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appoint]], [[commission]], [[select]], [[contract]], [[engage]], [[nominate]], [[designate]], [[mandate]]
