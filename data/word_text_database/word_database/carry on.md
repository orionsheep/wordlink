**[[carry on]]** *vb*
**1** to continue: we'll [[carry on]] exactly where we left off
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[carry on]]**: continue, last, endure, persist, keep going, persevere, crack on (*informal*)
**2** to do, run, or take part in: the vast trade carried on in the city
**3** (*informal*) to cause a [[fuss]]: I don't want to [[carry on]] and make a big scene
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[carry on]]**: make a [[fuss]], misbehave, create (*slang*), raise Cain
 *n* **carry-on**
**4** (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*) a [[fuss]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fuss]], [[disturbance]], [[racket]], [[commotion]]
