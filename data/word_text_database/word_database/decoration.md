**decoration** *n*
**1** an addition that makes something more attractive or ornate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ornament]], [[trimmings]], [[garnish]], [[frill]], [[bauble]]
**2** the way in which a room or building is decorated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adornment]], [[trimming]], [[enhancement]], [[elaboration]], [[embellishment]], [[ornamentation]], [[beautification]]
**3** something, esp. a [[medal]], conferred as a mark of honour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[medal]], [[award]], [[star]], [[ribbon]], [[badge]]
