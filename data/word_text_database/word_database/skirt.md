**skirt** *n*
**1** a woman's or girl's garment hanging from the waist
**2** the part of a dress or coat below the waist
**3** a circular hanging flap, for example round the base of a hovercraft
**4** (*Brit** & **NZ*) a cut of beef from the [[flank]]
**5** **bit of skirt** (*offensive*, *slang*) a girl or woman
 *vb*
**6** to lie along or form the [[edge]] of (something): a track skirting the foot of the mountain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[border]], [[edge]], [[flank]]
**7** to go around the outer [[edge]] of (something): we skirted the township
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go round]], [[circumvent]]
**8** to [[avoid]] dealing with (an issue): I was skirting around the real issues
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[avoid]], [[evade]], [[steer clear of]], [[circumvent]] (*formal*)
