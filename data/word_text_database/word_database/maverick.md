**maverick** *n*
**1** a person of independent or unorthodox views
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rebel]], [[radical]], [[dissenter]], [[individualist]], [[protester]], [[eccentric]], [[heretic]], [[nonconformist]]
**2** (in the US and Canada) an unbranded stray calf
 *adj*
**3** (of a person or his or her views) independent and unorthodox
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rebel]], [[radical]], [[dissenting]], [[individualistic]], [[eccentric]], [[heretical]], [[iconoclastic]], [[nonconformist]]
