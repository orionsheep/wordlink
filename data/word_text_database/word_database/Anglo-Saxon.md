**Anglo-Saxon** *n*
**1** a member of any of the West Germanic tribes that settled in Britain from the 5th century <small>AD</small>
**2** any White person whose native language is English
**3** (*informal*) plain, blunt, and often rude English
 *adj*
**4** of the Anglo-Saxons or the Old English language
**5** of the White Protestant culture of Britain and the US
