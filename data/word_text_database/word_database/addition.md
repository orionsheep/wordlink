**addition** *n*
**1** the act of [[adding]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclusion]], [[adding]], [[increasing]], [[extension]], [[attachment]], [[insertion]], [[incorporation]], [[augmentation]]
**2** a person or thing that is added
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extra]], [[supplement]], [[increase]], [[gain]], [[bonus]], [[extension]], [[accessory]], [[additive]]
**3** a mathematical operation in which the total of two or more numbers or quantities is calculated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[counting up]], [[totalling]], [[adding up]], [[computation]], [[totting up]]
**4** **in addition (to)** besides; as well (as)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in addition to]]**: as well as, along with, on top of, besides, to boot, [[additionally]], over and above, to say nothing of
 **[[additional]]** *adj* 
 **[[additionally]]** *adv*
