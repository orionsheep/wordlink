**formation** *n*
**1** the act of having or taking form or existence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[establishment]], [[founding]], [[forming]], [[setting up]], [[starting]], [[production]], [[generation]], [[manufacture]]
**2** something that is formed
**3** the manner in which something is arranged
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[development]], [[shaping]], [[constitution]], [[moulding]], [[genesis]]
**4** an [[arrangement]] of people or things acting as a unit, such as a troop of soldiers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrangement]], [[grouping]], [[design]], [[structure]], [[pattern]], [[organization]], [[array]], [[configuration]]
**5** a series of rocks or clouds of a particular [[structure]] or shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrangement]], [[grouping]], [[design]], [[structure]], [[pattern]], [[organization]], [[array]], [[configuration]]
