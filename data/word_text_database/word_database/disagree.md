**disagree** *vb* **-greeing**, **-greed**
**1** to have differing opinions or [[argue]] about (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[differ]] (in opinion), [[argue]], [[clash]], [[dispute]], [[dissent]], [[quarrel]], [[take issue with]], [[cross swords]]
**2** to fail to correspond; conflict
**3** to cause physical [[discomfort]] to: curry disagrees with me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make ill]], [[upset]], [[sicken]], [[trouble]], [[hurt]], [[bother]], [[distress]], [[discomfort]]
