**resignation** *n*
**1** the act of resigning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leaving]], [[departure]], [[abandonment]], [[abdication]]
**2** a formal document stating one's intention to resign
**3** passive [[endurance]] of difficulties: full of quiet resignation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acceptance]], [[patience]], [[submission]], [[compliance]], [[endurance]], [[passivity]], [[acquiescence]], [[sufferance]]
