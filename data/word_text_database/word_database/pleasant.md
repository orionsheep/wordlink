**pleasant** *adj*
**1** [[pleasing]] or [[enjoyable]]: what a pleasant surprise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pleasing]], [[nice]], [[fine]], [[lovely]], [[amusing]], [[delightful]], [[enjoyable]], [[agreeable]], [[lekker]] (*S African* *slang*)
**2** having [[pleasing]] manners or appearance: he was a pleasant boy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[friendly]], [[nice]], [[agreeable]], likable *or* likeable, [[engaging]], [[charming]], [[amiable]], [[genial]]
 **[[pleasantly]]** *adv*
