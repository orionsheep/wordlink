**experiment** *n*
**1** a [[test]] or [[investigation]] to provide evidence for or against a theory: a scientific experiment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[test]], [[trial]], [[investigation]], [[examination]], [[procedure]], [[demonstration]], [[observation]], [[try-out]]
**2** the trying out of a new idea or method
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[research]], [[investigation]], [[analysis]], [[observation]], [[research and development]], [[experimentation]]
 *vb*
**3** to carry out an experiment or experiments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[test]], [[investigate]], [[trial]], [[research]], [[try]], [[examine]], [[pilot]], [[sample]]
 **[[experimentation]]** *n* 
 **[[experimenter]]** *n*
