**band**^2 *n*
**1** a [[strip]] of some material, used to hold objects together: a rubber band
**2** a [[strip]] of fabric used as an ornament or to reinforce clothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[headband]], [[strip]], [[ribbon]]
**3** a stripe of contrasting colour or texture
**4** a driving belt in machinery
**5** (*physics*) a range of frequencies or wavelengths between two limits
 *vb*
**6** to fasten or mark with a band
