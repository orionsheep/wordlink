**cut** *vb* **cutting**, **cut**
**1** to open up or [[penetrate]] (a person or thing) with a sharp instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slit]], [[score]], [[slice]], [[slash]], [[pierce]], [[penetrate]]
**2** (of a sharp instrument) to [[penetrate]] or open up (a person or thing)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slit]], [[score]], [[slice]], [[slash]], [[pierce]], [[penetrate]]
**3** to divide or be divided with or as if with a sharp instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chop]], [[split]], [[slice]], [[dissect]]
**4** to [[trim]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clip]], [[mow]], [[trim]], [[prune]], [[snip]], [[pare]], [[lop]]
**5** to [[abridge]] or [[shorten]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abridge]], [[edit]], [[shorten]], [[curtail]], [[condense]], [[abbreviate]]
**6** to [[reduce]] or restrict: cut your intake of fried foods
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reduce]], [[lower]], [[slim]] (down), [[diminish]], [[slash]], [[decrease]], [[cut back]], [[kennet]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[jeff]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**7** to [[form]] or [[shape]] by cutting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shape]], [[carve]], [[engrave]], [[chisel]], [[form]], [[score]], [[fashion]], [[whittle]]
**8** to reap or [[mow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clip]], [[mow]], [[trim]], [[prune]], [[snip]], [[pare]], [[lop]]
**9** (*sport*) to hit (the ball) so that it spins and swerves
**10** to [[hurt]] the feelings of (a person): her rudeness cut me to the core
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hurt]], [[wound]], [[upset]], [[sting]], [[hurt someone's feelings]]
**11** (*informal*) to pretend not to recognize
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ignore]], [[avoid]], [[slight]], [[blank]] (*slang*), [[snub]], [[spurn]], [[cold-shoulder]], [[turn your back on]]
**12** (*informal*) to absent oneself from without permission: he found the course boring, and was soon cutting classes
**13** to stop (doing something): cut the nonsense
**14** to dilute or adulterate: heroin cut with talcum powder
**15** to make a sharp or sudden change in direction: the path cuts to the right just after you pass the quarry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cross]], [[bisect]]
**16** to grow (teeth) through the gums
**17** (*films*)
**A** to call a halt to a shooting sequence
 **B** **cut to** to move quickly to (another scene)
**18** (*films*) to [[edit]] (film)
**19** to switch off (a light or engine)
**20** to make (a commercial recording): he cut his first solo album in 1971
**21** (*cards*)
**A** to divide (the pack) at random into two parts after shuffling
 **B** to pick cards from a spread pack to decide the dealer or who plays first
**22** **cut a dash** to make a stylish impression
**23** **cut a person dead** (*informal*) to [[ignore]] a person completely
**24** **cut and run** (*informal*) to escape quickly from a difficult situation
**25** **cut both ways**
**A** to have both good and bad effects
 **B** to serve both sides of an argument
**26** **cut it fine** (*informal*) to allow little margin of time or space
**27** **cut no ice** (*informal*) to fail to make an impression
**28** **cut one's teeth on** (*informal*) to get experience from
 *n*
**29** the act of cutting
**30** a [[stroke]] or [[incision]] made by cutting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incision]], [[nick]], [[stroke]], [[slash]], [[slit]]
**31** a [[piece]] cut off
**32** a channel or path cut or hollowed out
**33** a [[reduction]]: a pay cut
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reduction]], [[fall]], [[lowering]], [[slash]], [[decrease]], [[cutback]]
**34** a deletion in a text, film, or play
**35** (*informal*) a [[portion]] or [[share]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share]], [[piece]], [[slice]], [[percentage]], [[portion]]
**36** the [[style]] in which hair or a garment is cut
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[style]], [[look]], [[fashion]], [[shape]]
**37** a direct route; short cut
**38** (*sport*) a [[stroke]] which makes the ball spin and swerve
**39** (*films*) an immediate transition from one shot to the next
**40** (*Brit*) a canal
**41** **a cut above** (*informal*) superior to; better than
 *adj*
**42** made or shaped by cutting
**43** reduced by cutting: the shop has hundreds of suits, all at cut prices
**44** adulterated or diluted
**45** **cut and dried** (*informal*) settled in advance
