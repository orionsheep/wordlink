**migrant** *n*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wanderer]], [[immigrant]], [[traveller]], [[rover]], [[nomad]], [[emigrant]], [[itinerant]], drifter
 *adj*
**2** moving from one place to another: migrant farm labourers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[itinerant]], [[wandering]], [[drifting]], [[roving]], [[travelling]], [[shifting]], [[immigrant]], [[transient]]
