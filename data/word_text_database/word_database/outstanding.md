**outstanding** *adj*
**1** very [[good]]; [[excellent]]: an outstanding performance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellent]], [[good]], [[great]] (*informal*), [[important]], [[special]], [[fine]], [[brilliant]], [[impressive]], [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**2** still to be dealt with or paid: outstanding bills; a few outstanding problems have to be put right
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unpaid]], [[remaining]], [[due]], [[pending]], [[payable]], [[unsettled]], [[uncollected]]
**3** very obvious or [[important]]: there are significant exceptions, of which oil is the outstanding example
 **[[outstandingly]]** *adv*
