**unstable** *adj*
**1** not firmly fixed and likely to wobble or fall: an unstable pile of books
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insecure]], [[shaky]], [[precarious]], [[unsettled]], [[wobbly]], [[tottering]], [[unsteady]]
**2** likely to change suddenly and create difficulties or danger: the unstable political climate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[changeable]], [[volatile]], [[unpredictable]], [[variable]], [[fluctuating]], [[fitful]], [[inconstant]]
**3** (of a person) having abrupt changes of mood or behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unpredictable]], [[irrational]], [[erratic]], [[inconsistent]], [[temperamental]], [[capricious]], [[changeable]]
**4** (*chem*, *physics*) readily decomposing
