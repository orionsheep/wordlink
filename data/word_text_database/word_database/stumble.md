**stumble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to [[trip]] and almost [[fall]] while walking or running
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trip]], [[fall]], [[slip]], [[reel]], [[stagger]], [[falter]], [[lurch]]
**2** to walk in an unsteady or unsure way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[totter]], [[reel]], [[lurch]], [[wobble]]
**3** to make mistakes or hesitate in speech
**4** **stumble across *or* on *or* upon** to encounter or discover (someone or something) by accident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[stumble across or on something or someone]]**: discover, find, come across, chance upon
 *n*
**5** an act of stumbling
