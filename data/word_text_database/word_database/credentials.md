**credentials** *pl n*
**1** something that entitles a person to credit or confidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[qualifications]], [[ability]], [[skill]], [[fitness]], [[attribute]], [[capability]], [[eligibility]], [[aptitude]]
**2** a [[document]] giving evidence of the bearer's identity or [[qualifications]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[certification]], [[document]], [[reference]](s), [[papers]], [[licence]], [[passport]], [[testimonial]], [[authorization]]
