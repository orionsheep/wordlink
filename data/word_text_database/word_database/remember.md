**remember** *vb*
**1** to become aware of (something forgotten) again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recall]], [[think back to]], [[recollect]], [[reminisce about]], [[call to mind]]
**2** to keep (an idea, intention, etc.) in one's mind: remember to write
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bear in mind]], [[keep in mind]]
**3** to give money to (someone), as in a will or in tipping
**4** **remember to** to mention (a person's name) to another person, by way of greeting: remember me to her
**5** to [[commemorate]]: we are here to remember the dead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look back]] (on), [[commemorate]]
