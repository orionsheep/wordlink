**offence** * or * (*US*) **offense** *n*
**1** a breaking of a law or rule; [[crime]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crime]], [[sin]], [[fault]], [[violation]], [[wrongdoing]], [[trespass]], [[felony]], [[misdemeanour]]
**2** annoyance or [[anger]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outrage]], [[shock]], [[anger]], [[trouble]], [[bother]], [[resentment]], [[irritation]], [[hassle]] (*informal*)
**3** a cause of annoyance or [[anger]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insult]], [[slight]], [[hurt]], [[outrage]], [[injustice]], [[snub]], [[affront]], [[indignity]]
**4** **give offence** to cause to feel upset or angry
**5** **take offence** to feel [[hurt]] or offended
