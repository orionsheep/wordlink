**conscious** *adj*
**1** alert and [[awake]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[awake]], [[wide-awake]], [[sentient]], [[alive]]
**2** [[aware of]] one's surroundings and of oneself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[awake]], [[wide-awake]], [[sentient]], [[alive]]
**3** aware (of something): he was conscious of a need to urinate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aware of]], [[alert to]], [[responsive to]], [[sensible of]]
**4** [[deliberate]] or [[intentional]]: a conscious attempt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deliberate]], [[knowing]], [[studied]], [[calculated]], [[self-conscious]], [[intentional]], [[wilful]], [[premeditated]]
**5** of the part of the mind that is [[aware of]] a person's self, surroundings, and thoughts, and that to a certain extent determines choices of action
 *n*
**6** the conscious part of the mind
 **[[consciously]]** *adv* 
 **[[consciousness]]** *n*
