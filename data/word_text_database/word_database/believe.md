◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[judge]], [[suppose]], [[estimate]], [[imagine]], [[assume]], [[gather]], [[reckon]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accept]], [[trust]], [[credit]], [[depend on]], [[rely on]], [[have faith in]], [[swear by]], [[be certain of]]
