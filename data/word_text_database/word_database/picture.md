**picture** *n*
**1** a visual [[representation]] produced on a surface, such as in a [[photograph]] or [[painting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representation]], [[drawing]], [[painting]], [[portrait]], [[image]], [[print]], [[illustration]], [[sketch]], [[avatar]]
**2** a [[mental image]]: neither had any clear picture of whom they were looking for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idea]], [[vision]], [[concept]], [[impression]], [[notion]], [[visualization]], [[mental picture]], [[mental image]]
**3** a [[description]] or [[account]] of a situation considered as an observable scene: the reports do not provide an accurate picture of the spread of the disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[description]], [[impression]], [[explanation]], [[report]], [[account]], [[image]], [[sketch]], [[depiction]]
**4** a person or thing resembling another: he is the picture of a perfect host
**5** a person or scene typifying a particular state: his face was a picture of dejection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[personification]], [[embodiment]], [[essence]], [[epitome]], [[avatar]]
**6** the [[image]] on a television screen
**7** a cinema [[film]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[film]], [[movie]] (*US* *informal*), [[flick]] (*slang*), [[feature film]], [[motion picture]]
**8** **in the picture** informed about a situation
**9** **the pictures** a cinema or [[film]] [[show]]
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**10** to [[visualize]] or [[imagine]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imagine]], [[see]], [[envision]], [[visualize]], [[conceive of]], [[fantasize about]], [[conjure up an image of]]
**11** to describe or [[depict]] vividly: a documentary that had pictured them as good-natured dolts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[represent]], [[show]], [[draw]], [[paint]], [[illustrate]], [[sketch]], [[depict]]
**12** to put in a picture or make a picture of: the people pictured above are all the same age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[photograph]], [[capture on film]]
