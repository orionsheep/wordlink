**illuminate** *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**1** to [[light up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[light up]], [[brighten]]
**2** to make easily understood; [[explain]]: the report obscures rather than illuminates the most relevant facts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explain]], [[interpret]], [[make clear]], [[clarify]], [[clear up]], [[enlighten]], [[shed light on]], [[elucidate]]
**3** to decorate with lights
**4** to decorate (an initial letter or manuscript) with designs of gold, silver, or bright colours
 **[[illuminating]]** *adj* 
 **[[illuminative]]** *adj*
