**turkey** *n*, *pl* **-keys*** or ***-key**
**1** a large bird of North America bred for its meat
**2** (*informal*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) something, esp. a theatrical production, that fails
**3** **cold turkey** (*slang*) a method of curing drug addiction by abrupt withdrawal of all doses
**4** **talk turkey** (*informal*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to discuss, esp. business, frankly and practically
