**uncover** *vb*
**1** to remove the cover or top from
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[open]], [[unveil]], [[unwrap]], [[show]], [[strip]], [[expose]], [[bare]], [[lay bare]]
**2** to [[reveal]] or [[disclose]]: they have [[uncovered]] a plot to overthrow the government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reveal]], [[expose]], [[disclose]], [[divulge]], [[make known]]
 **[[uncovered]]** *adj*
