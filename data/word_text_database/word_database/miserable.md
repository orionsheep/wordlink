**miserable** *adj*
**1** unhappy or [[depressed]]; [[wretched]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sad]], [[depressed]], [[gloomy]], [[forlorn]], [[dejected]], [[despondent]], [[sorrowful]], [[wretched]]
**2** causing misery or discomfort: a miserable existence
**3** sordid or squalid: miserable living conditions
**4** mean or ungenerous: a miserable pension
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pathetic]], [[sorry]], [[shameful]], [[despicable]], [[deplorable]], [[lamentable]]
 **[[miserableness]]** *n* 
 **[[miserably]]** *adv*
