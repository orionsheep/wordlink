**item** *n*
**1** a single [[thing]] in a list or collection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[article]], [[thing]], [[object]], [[piece]], [[unit]], [[component]]
**2** a [[piece]] of information: a news item
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[report]], [[story]], [[piece]], [[account]], [[note]], [[feature]], [[notice]], [[article]]
**3** (*accounting*) an entry in an [[account]]
**4** (*informal*) a couple
