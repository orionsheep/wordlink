**bus** *n*
**1** a large motor vehicle designed to carry passengers between stopping places along a regular route
**2** (*informal*) a car or aircraft that is old and shaky
**3** (*electronics*, *computers*) an electrical conductor used to make a common connection between several circuits
 *vb* **bussing**, **bussed*** or ***busing**, **bused**
**4** to travel or transport by bus
**5** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to transport (children) by bus from one area to another in order to create racially integrated schools
