**escort** *n*
**1** people or vehicles accompanying another to protect or [[guard]] them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[guard]], [[bodyguard]], [[train]], [[convoy]], [[entourage]], [[retinue]], [[cortege]]
**2** a person who accompanies someone on a social occasion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[companion]], [[partner]], [[attendant]], [[guide]], [[beau]] (*old-fashioned*), [[chaperon]]
 *vb*
**3** to act as an escort to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accompany]], [[lead]], [[partner]], [[conduct]], [[guide]], [[shepherd]], [[usher]], [[chaperon]]
