**constrain** *vb*
**1** to [[compel]] or [[force]]: he felt constrained to apologize
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[bind]], [[compel]], [[oblige]], [[necessitate]], [[coerce]], [[impel]], [[pressurize]]
**2** to limit, [[restrict]], or inhibit: the mobility of workers is constrained by the serious housing shortage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrict]], [[confine]], [[curb]], [[restrain]], [[constrict]], [[straiten]], [[check]]
