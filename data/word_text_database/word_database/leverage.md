**leverage** *n*
**1** the mechanical advantage gained by using a lever
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[hold]], [[pull]], [[strength]], [[grip]], [[grasp]]
**2** the ability to [[influence]] people or events: information gives leverage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[influence]], [[authority]], [[pull]] (*informal*), [[weight]], [[clout]] (*informal*)
