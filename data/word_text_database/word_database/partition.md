**partition** *n*
**1** a large [[screen]] or thin [[wall]] that divides a room
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[screen]], [[wall]], [[barrier]]
**2** the [[division]] of a country into two or more independent countries
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[division]], [[separation]], segregation
 *vb*
**3** to [[separate]] (a room) into sections: the shower is partitioned off from the rest of the bathroom
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[screen]], [[divide]]
**4** to [[divide]] (a country) into [[separate]] self-governing parts: the subcontinent was partitioned into India and Pakistan
