**healthy** *adj* **healthier**, **healthiest**
**1** having or showing good health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well]], [[fit]], [[strong]], [[active]], [[robust]], [[in good shape]] (*informal*), [[in the pink]], [[in fine fettle]]
**2** likely to produce good health: healthy seaside air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wholesome]], [[beneficial]], [[nourishing]], [[nutritious]], [[salutary]], [[hygienic]], [[salubrious]]
**3** functioning [[well]] or being sound: this is a very healthy business to be in
**4** (*informal*) considerable: healthy profits
**5** sensible: a healthy scepticism about his promises
 **[[healthily]]** *adv* 
 **[[healthiness]]** *n*
