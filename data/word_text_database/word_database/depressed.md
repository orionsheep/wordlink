**depressed** *adj*
**1** (of a person) having depression
**2** low in spirits; downcast
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sad]], [[blue]], [[unhappy]], [[discouraged]], [[fed up]], [[mournful]], [[dejected]], [[despondent]]
**3** suffering from economic hardship, such as unemployment: the current depressed conditions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[poverty-stricken]], [[poor]], [[deprived]], [[disadvantaged]], [[rundown]], [[impoverished]], [[needy]]
**4** pressed down or flattened
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sunken]], [[hollow]], [[recessed]], [[indented]], [[concave]]
