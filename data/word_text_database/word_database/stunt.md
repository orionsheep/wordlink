**stunt**^2 *n*
**1** an acrobatic or dangerous piece of action in a film or television programme
**2** anything spectacular or unusual done to gain publicity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feat]], [[act]], [[trick]], [[exploit]], deed
 *adj*
**3** of or relating to acrobatic or dangerous pieces of action in films or television programmes: a stunt man
