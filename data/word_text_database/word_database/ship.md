**ship** *n*
**1** a large seagoing [[vessel]] with engines or sails
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vessel]], [[boat]], [[craft]]
**2** **when one's ship comes in** when one has become successful
 *vb* **shipping**, **shipped**
**3** to send or transport by any carrier, esp. a ship
**4** (*naut*) to take in (water) over the side
**5** to bring or go aboard a [[vessel]]: to ship oars
**6** (often foll. by *off*) (*informal*) to send away: they were shipped off to foreign countries
**7** to be hired to serve aboard a ship: I shipped aboard a Liverpool liner
