**wither** *vb*
**1** to make or become dried up or shrivelled: the leaves had [[withered]] but not fallen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wilt]], [[decline]], [[decay]], [[disintegrate]], [[perish]], [[shrivel]]
**2** to fade or [[waste]]: deprived of the nerve supply the muscles wither
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waste]], [[decline]], [[shrivel]]
**3** to humiliate (someone) with a scornful look or remark
 **[[withered]]** *adj*
