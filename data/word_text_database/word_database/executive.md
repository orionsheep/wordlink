**executive** *n*
**1** a person or group responsible for the [[administration]] of a project or business
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administrator]], [[official]], [[director]], [[manager]], [[managing director]], [[controller]], [[chief executive officer]]
**2** the branch of [[government]] responsible for carrying out laws, decrees, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administration]], [[government]], [[directors]], [[management]], [[leadership]], [[hierarchy]], directorate
 *adj*
**3** having the function of carrying plans, orders, laws, etc. into effect: the executive producer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administrative]], [[controlling]], [[directing]], [[governing]], [[regulating]], [[decision-making]], [[managerial]]
**4** of or for executives: the executive car park
**5** (*informal*) very expensive or exclusive: executive cars
