**scandal** *n*
**1** a disgraceful action or event: the manager resigned after a loans scandal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disgrace]], [[crime]], [[offence]], [[sin]], [[embarrassment]], [[wrongdoing]], [[dishonourable behaviour]], [[discreditable behaviour]]
**2** [[shame]] or [[outrage]] arising from a disgraceful action or event: the figures were a national scandal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outrage]], [[shame]], [[insult]], [[disgrace]], [[injustice]], [[crying shame]]
**3** malicious [[gossip]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gossip]], [[goss]] (*informal*), [[talk]], [[rumours]], [[dirt]] (*informal*), [[slander]], [[tattle]], [[aspersion]]
 **[[scandalous]]** *adj* 
 **[[scandalously]]** *adv*
