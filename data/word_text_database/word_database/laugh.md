**laugh** *vb*
**1** to express amusement or happiness by producing a series of inarticulate sounds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chuckle]], [[giggle]], [[snigger]], [[cackle]], [[chortle]], [[guffaw]], [[titter]], [[be in stitches]]
**2** to utter or express with laughter: he laughed his derision at the play
**3** to bring or force (oneself) into a certain condition by laughter: laughing herself silly
**4** **laugh at** to make fun of; jeer at
**5** **laugh up one's sleeve** to laugh secretly
 *n*
**6** the act or an instance of laughing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chortle]], [[giggle]], [[chuckle]], [[snigger]], [[guffaw]], [[titter]]
**7** (*informal*) a person or thing that causes laughter: he's a laugh, that one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clown]], [[character]] (*informal*), [[scream]] (*informal*), [[entertainer]], [[card]] (*informal*), [[joker]], [[hoot]] (*informal*)
**8** **the last laugh** final success after previous defeat
 See also **laugh off**
 **[[laughingly]]** *adv*
