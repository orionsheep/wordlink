**economical** *adj*
**1** not requiring a lot of money to use: low fuel consumption makes this car very economical
**2** (of a person) spending money carefully and sensibly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thrifty]], [[sparing]], [[careful]], [[prudent]], [[provident]], [[frugal]], [[parsimonious]], [[scrimping]]
**3** using no more time, effort, or resources than is necessary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[efficient]], [[sparing]], [[cost-effective]], [[money-saving]], [[time-saving]]
**4** **economical with the truth** (*euphemistic*) deliberately withholding information
 **[[economically]]** *adv*
