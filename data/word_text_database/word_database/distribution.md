**distribution** *n*
**1** the delivering of leaflets, mail, etc. to individual people or organizations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delivery]], [[mailing]], [[transportation]], [[handling]]
**2** the [[sharing]] out of something among a particular group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sharing]], [[division]], [[assignment]], [[rationing]], [[allocation]], [[allotment]], [[apportionment]]
**3** the [[arrangement]] or [[spread]] of anything over an area, space, or period of time: the unequal distribution of wealth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread]], [[organization]], [[arrangement]], [[placement]]
**4** (*commerce*) the process of satisfying the demand for goods and services
