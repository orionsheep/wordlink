**decision** *n*
**1** a choice or [[judgment]] made about something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[judgment]], [[finding]], [[ruling]], [[sentence]], [[resolution]], [[conclusion]], [[verdict]], [[decree]]
**2** the act of making up one's mind
**3** the ability to make quick and definite decisions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decisiveness]], [[purpose]], [[resolution]], [[resolve]], [[determination]], [[firmness]], [[forcefulness]], strength of mind *or* will
