◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crafty]], [[sly]], [[devious]], [[artful]], [[sharp]], [[wily]], [[Machiavellian]], [[shifty]] (*informal*)
**2** made with [[skill]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ingenious]], [[imaginative]], [[sly]], [[devious]], [[artful]], [[Machiavellian]]
 *n*
**3** [[cleverness]] at deceiving
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[craftiness]], [[guile]], [[trickery]], [[deviousness]], [[artfulness]], [[slyness]]
**4** [[skill]] or [[ingenuity]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skill]], [[subtlety]], [[ingenuity]], [[artifice]], [[cleverness]]
 **[[cunningly]]** *adv*
