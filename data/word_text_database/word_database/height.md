**height** *n*
**1** the vertical distance from the bottom of something to the [[top]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[altitude]], [[measurement]], [[highness]], [[elevation]], [[tallness]]
**2** the vertical distance of a place above sea level
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[altitude]], [[measurement]], [[highness]], [[elevation]], [[tallness]]
**3** relatively great distance from bottom to [[top]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tallness]], [[stature]], [[highness]], [[loftiness]]
**4** the topmost point; [[summit]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peak]], [[top]], [[crown]], [[summit]], [[crest]], [[pinnacle]], [[apex]]
**5** the period of greatest intensity: the height of the shelling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[culmination]], [[climax]], [[zenith]], [[limit]], [[maximum]], [[ultimate]]
**6** an extreme example: the height of luxury
**7** **heights** extremes: dizzy heights of success
