**grit** *n*
**1** small hard particles of [[sand]], earth, or stone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gravel]], [[sand]], [[dust]], [[pebbles]]
**2** [[courage]] and [[determination]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[courage]], [[spirit]], [[resolution]], [[determination]], [[guts]] (*informal*), [[backbone]], [[fortitude]], tenacity
 *vb* **gritting**, **[[gritted]]**
**3** to cover (an icy road) with grit
**4** **grit one's teeth**
**A** to rub one's upper and lower teeth against each other, as if chewing
 **B** to decide to carry on in a difficult situation: he urged the Cabinet to grit its teeth and continue cutting public spending
