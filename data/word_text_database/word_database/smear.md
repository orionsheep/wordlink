**smear** *vb*
**1** to spread with a greasy or sticky substance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread over]], [[daub]], [[rub on]], [[cover]], [[coat]], [[bedaub]]
**2** to apply (a greasy or sticky substance) thickly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread over]], [[daub]], [[rub on]], [[cover]], [[coat]], [[bedaub]]
**3** to rub so as to produce a [[smudge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smudge]], [[soil]], [[dirty]], [[stain]], [[sully]]
**4** to spread false and damaging rumours (about)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slander]], [[malign]], [[blacken]], besmirch
 *n*
**5** a [[dirty]] mark or [[smudge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smudge]], [[daub]], [[streak]], [[blot]], [[blotch]], [[splotch]]
**6** a false but damaging rumour spread by a rival or enemy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slander]], [[libel]], [[defamation]], [[calumny]]
**7** (*med*) a small amount of a substance smeared onto a glass slide for examination under a microscope
 **[[smeary]]** *adj*
