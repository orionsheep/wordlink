**billion** *n*, *pl* **-lions*** or ***-lion**
**1** one thousand million: 1 000 000 000 or 10^9
**2** (in Britain, originally) one million million: 1 000 000 000 000 or 10^12
**3** (*often pl*, *informal*) an extremely large but unspecified number: billions of dollars
 **[[billionth]]** *adj*, *n*
