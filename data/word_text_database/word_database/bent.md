**bent** *adj*
**1** not straight; [[curved]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misshapen]], [[twisted]], [[angled]], [[bowed]], [[curved]], [[arched]], [[crooked]], [[distorted]]
**2** (*slang*)
**A** dishonest; corrupt: bent officials
 **B** (*Brit** & **Austral* *offensive*, *slang*) homosexual
**3** **[[bent on]]** determined to pursue (a course of action)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[bent on]]**: intent on, set on, fixed on, predisposed to, resolved on, insistent on
 *n*
**4** personal [[inclination]] or [[aptitude]]: he had a strong practical bent in his nature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclination]], [[ability]], [[leaning]], [[tendency]], [[preference]], [[penchant]], [[propensity]], [[aptitude]]
