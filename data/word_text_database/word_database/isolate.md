**isolate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to place apart or alone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[break up]], [[cut off]], [[detach]], [[split up]], [[insulate]], [[segregate]], [[disconnect]]
**2** (*chem*) to obtain (a substance) in an uncombined form
**3** (*med*) to [[quarantine]] (a person or animal) with a contagious disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quarantine]]
 **[[isolation]]** *n*
