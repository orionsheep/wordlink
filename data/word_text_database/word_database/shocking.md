**shocking** *adj*
**1** (*informal*) very [[bad]] or [[terrible]]: a shocking match at Leicester
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terrible]], [[appalling]], [[dreadful]], [[bad]], [[horrendous]], [[ghastly]], [[deplorable]], [[abysmal]]
**2** causing dismay or disgust: a shocking lack of concern
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appalling]], [[outrageous]], [[disgraceful]], [[disgusting]], [[dreadful]], [[horrifying]], [[revolting]], [[sickening]]
**3** **shocking pink** (of) a very bright shade of pink
