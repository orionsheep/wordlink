**anxiety** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a state of [[uneasiness]] about what may happen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneasiness]], [[concern]], [[worry]], [[doubt]], [[tension]], [[angst]], [[apprehension]], [[misgiving]]
**2** eagerness: she was uneasy with his mixture of diffidence and anxiety to please
**3** (*psychol*) a state of intense [[worry]]
