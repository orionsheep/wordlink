**sentiment** *n*
**1** a mental [[attitude]] based on a mixture of thoughts and feelings: anti-American sentiment
**2** (*often pl*) a thought, [[opinion]], or [[attitude]] expressed in words: his sentiments were echoed by subsequent speakers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[idea]], [[view]], [[opinion]], [[attitude]], [[belief]], [[judgment]]
**3** feelings such as [[tenderness]], romance, and sadness, esp. when exaggerated: someone without the softness of sentiment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sentimentality]], [[emotion]], [[tenderness]], [[romanticism]], [[sensibility]], [[emotionalism]], [[mawkishness]]
