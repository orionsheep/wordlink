**reprieve** *vb* **-prieving**, **-prieved**
**1** to postpone the execution of (a condemned person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grant a stay of execution to]], [[pardon]], [[let off the hook]] (*slang*)
**2** to give temporary relief to
 *n*
**3** a postponement or cancellation of a punishment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stay of execution]], [[amnesty]], [[pardon]], [[remission]], [[deferment]], [[postponement of punishment]]
**4** a warrant granting a postponement or cancellation
**5** a temporary relief from pain or harm
