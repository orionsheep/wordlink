**professional** *adj*
**1** of a profession
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[qualified]], [[trained]], [[skilled]], [[white-collar]]
**2** taking part in an activity, such as sport or music, as a means of livelihood
**3** displaying a high level of competence or skill: a professional and polished performance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expert]], [[experienced]], [[skilled]], [[masterly]], [[efficient]], [[competent]], [[adept]], [[proficient]]
**4** undertaken or performed by people who are paid: professional golf
 *n*
**5** a professional person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expert]], [[master]], [[pro]] (*informal*), [[specialist]], [[guru]], [[adept]], [[maestro]], [[virtuoso]], [[fundi]] (*S African*) 
 **[[professionalism]]** *n* 
 **[[professionally]]** *adv*
