**affinity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a feeling of [[closeness]] to and understanding of a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attraction]], [[liking]], [[leaning]], [[sympathy]], [[inclination]], [[rapport]], [[fondness]], [[partiality]], [[aroha]] (*NZ*)
**2** a close [[similarity]] in appearance, structure, or quality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[similarity]], [[relationship]], [[connection]], [[correspondence]], [[analogy]], [[resemblance]], [[closeness]], [[likeness]]
**3** a chemical [[attraction]]
