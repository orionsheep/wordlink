**leave**^2 *n*
**1** [[permission]] to be absent, for instance from work: so I asked for leave
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holiday]], [[break]], [[vacation]], [[time off]], [[sabbatical]], [[leave of absence]], [[furlough]], [[schoolie]] (*Austral*), accumulated day off *or* ADO (*Austral*)
**2** the length of such absence: weekend leave
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holiday]], [[break]], [[vacation]], [[time off]], [[sabbatical]], [[leave of absence]], [[furlough]], [[schoolie]] (*Austral*), accumulated day off *or* ADO (*Austral*)
**3** [[permission]] to do something: they were refused leave to appeal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[permission]], [[freedom]], [[sanction]], [[liberty]], [[concession]], [[consent]], [[allowance]], [[warrant]]
**4** **on leave** officially excused from work or duty
**5** **take (one's) leave of** to say farewell to
