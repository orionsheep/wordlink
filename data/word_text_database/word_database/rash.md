**rash**^2 *n*
**1** an [[outbreak of spots]] or patches on the skin, caused by illness or allergy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outbreak of spots]], (skin) [[eruption]]
**2** an [[outbreak]] of occurrences: a rash of censorship trials
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spate]], [[series]], [[wave]], [[flood]], [[plague]], [[outbreak]]
