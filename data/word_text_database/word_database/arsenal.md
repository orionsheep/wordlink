**arsenal** *n*
**1** a building in which arms and ammunition are made or stored
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[armoury]], [[storehouse]], [[ammunition dump]], [[arms depot]], [[ordnance depot]]
**2** a [[store]] of anything regarded as weapons: this new weapon in the medical arsenal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[store]], [[supply]], [[stockpile]]
