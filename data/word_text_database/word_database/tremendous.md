**tremendous** *adj*
**1** very large or impressive: a tremendous amount of money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[huge]], [[great]], [[enormous]], [[terrific]], [[formidable]], [[immense]], [[gigantic]], [[colossal]]
**2** very exciting or unusual: a tremendous feeling of elation
**3** very good or pleasing: my wife has given me tremendous support
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellent]], [[great]], [[wonderful]], [[brilliant]], [[amazing]], [[extraordinary]], [[fantastic]], [[marvellous]], [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*) 
 **[[tremendously]]** *adv*
