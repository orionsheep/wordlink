**device** *n*
**1** a [[machine]] or [[tool]] used for a particular purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gadget]], [[machine]], [[tool]], [[instrument]], [[implement]], [[appliance]], [[apparatus]], [[contraption]] (*informal*)
**2** (*euphemistic*) a bomb
**3** a [[scheme]] or [[plan]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ploy]], [[scheme]], [[plan]], [[trick]], [[manoeuvre]], [[gambit]], [[stratagem]], [[wile]]
**4** a design or emblem
**5** **leave someone to his *or* her own devices** to leave someone alone to do as he or she wishes
