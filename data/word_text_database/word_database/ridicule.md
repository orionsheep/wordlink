**ridicule** *n*
**1** language or behaviour intended to humiliate or [[mock]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mockery]], [[scorn]], [[derision]], [[laughter]], [[jeer]], [[chaff]], [[gibe]], raillery
 *vb* **-culing**, **-culed**
**2** to [[make fun of]] or [[mock]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[laugh at]], [[mock]], [[make fun of]], [[sneer at]], [[jeer at]], [[deride]], [[poke fun at]], [[chaff]]
