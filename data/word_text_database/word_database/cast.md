**cast** *n*
**1** the [[actors]] in a play collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[actors]], [[company]], [[players]], [[characters]], [[troupe]], [[dramatis personae]]
**2**
**A** an object made of material that has been shaped, while molten, by a [[mould]]
 **B** the [[mould]] used to [[shape]] such an object
**3** (*surgery*) a rigid casing made of plaster of Paris for immobilizing broken bones while they heal
**4** a [[sort]], [[kind]], or [[style]]: people of an academic cast of mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[type]], [[sort]], [[kind]], [[style]], [[stamp]]
**5** a slight squint in the eye
 *vb* **casting**, **cast**
**6** to [[select]] (an actor) to play a part in a play or film
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[choose]], [[name]], [[pick]], [[select]], [[appoint]], [[assign]], [[allot]]
**7** to [[give]] or [[deposit]] (a vote)
**8** to express (doubts or aspersions)
**9** to cause to appear: a shadow cast by the grandstand; the gloom cast by the recession
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give out]], [[spread]], [[deposit]], [[shed]], [[distribute]], [[scatter]], [[emit]], [[radiate]]
**10**
**A** to [[shape]] (molten material) by pouring it into a [[mould]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mould]], [[set]], [[found]], [[form]], [[model]], [[shape]]
 **B** to make (an object) by such a process
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mould]], [[set]], [[found]], [[form]], [[model]], [[shape]]
**11** to [[throw]] (a fishing line) into the water
**12** to [[throw]] with force: cast into a bonfire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throw]], [[launch]], [[pitch]], [[toss]], [[thrust]], [[hurl]], [[fling]], [[sling]]
**13** to [[direct]] (a glance): he cast his eye over the horse-chestnut trees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bestow]], [[give]], [[level]], [[direct]]
**14** to roll or [[throw]] (a dice)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throw]], [[launch]], [[pitch]], [[toss]], [[thrust]], [[hurl]], [[fling]], [[sling]]
**15** **cast aside** to abandon or reject: cast aside by her lover
**16** **cast a spell**
**A** to perform magic
 **B** to have an irresistible influence
