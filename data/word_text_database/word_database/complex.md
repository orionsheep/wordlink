**complex** *adj*
**1** made up of interconnected parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compound]], [[multiple]], [[composite]], [[manifold]] (*formal*), [[heterogeneous]], [[multifarious]]
**2** [[intricate]] or [[complicated]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complicated]], [[difficult]], [[involved]], [[elaborate]], [[tangled]], [[intricate]], [[tortuous]], [[convoluted]]
**3** (*maths*) of or involving complex numbers
 *n*
**4** a whole made up of related parts: a leisure complex including a gymnasium, squash courts, and a 20-metre swimming pool
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[system]], [[scheme]], [[network]], [[organization]], [[aggregate]], [[composite]]
**5** (*psychoanalysis*) a group of unconscious feelings that influences a person's behaviour
**6** (*informal*) an [[obsession]] or [[phobia]]: I have never had a complex about my height
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obsession]], [[preoccupation]], [[phobia]], [[fixation]], [[fixed idea]], [[idée fixe]] (*French*)
