**breathless** *adj*
**1** [[out of breath]]; [[gasping]], etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[out of breath]], [[panting]], [[gasping]], [[gulping]], [[wheezing]], [[short-winded]]
**2** holding one's breath or having it taken away by excitement, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excited]], [[curious]], [[eager]], [[enthusiastic]], [[impatient]], [[on tenterhooks]], [[in suspense]]
**3** (esp. of the atmosphere) motionless and stifling
 **[[breathlessness]]** *n*
