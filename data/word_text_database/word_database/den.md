**den** *n*
**1** the home of a wild animal; [[lair]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lair]], [[hole]], [[shelter]], [[cave]], [[haunt]], [[cavern]], [[hide-out]]
**2** (*chiefly* *US*) a small secluded room in a home, often used for a hobby
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[study]], [[retreat]], [[sanctuary]], [[hideaway]], [[sanctum]], [[cubbyhole]]
**3** a place where people indulge in criminal or immoral activities: a den of iniquity
