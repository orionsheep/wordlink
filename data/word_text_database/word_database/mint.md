**mint**^2 *n*
**1** a factory where the official coins of a country are made
**2** a very large amount of money
 *adj*
**3** **in mint condition** in perfect condition; as if new
 *vb*
**4** to [[make]] coins by stamping metal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[produce]], [[strike]], [[cast]], [[stamp]], [[punch]], [[coin]]
**5** to invent or create: no-one knows who first minted the term 'yuppie'
