**blister** *n*
**1** a small bubble on the skin filled with a watery fluid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sore]], [[boil]], [[swelling]], [[cyst]], [[pimple]], [[carbuncle]], [[pustule]]
**2** a [[swelling]] containing air or liquid, such as on a painted surface
 *vb*
**3** to have or cause to have blisters
**4** to attack verbally with great scorn
 **[[blistering]]** *adj*
