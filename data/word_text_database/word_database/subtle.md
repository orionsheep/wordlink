**subtle** *adj*
**1** not immediately obvious: a subtle change in his views
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faint]], [[slight]], [[implied]], [[delicate]], [[understated]]
**2** (of a colour, taste, or smell) [[delicate]] or [[faint]]: the subtle aroma
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faint]], [[slight]], [[implied]], [[delicate]], [[understated]]
**3** using [[shrewd]] and indirect methods to achieve an objective
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crafty]], [[cunning]], [[sly]], [[shrewd]], [[ingenious]], [[devious]], [[wily]], [[artful]]
**4** having or requiring the ability to make [[fine]] distinctions: a subtle argument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fine]], [[minute]], [[narrow]], [[tenuous]], [[hair-splitting]]
 **[[subtly]]** *adv*
