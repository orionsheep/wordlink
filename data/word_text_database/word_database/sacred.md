**sacred** *adj*
**1** exclusively devoted to a god or gods; [[holy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holy]], [[hallowed]], [[blessed]], [[divine]], [[revered]], [[sanctified]]
**2** connected with religion or intended for [[religious]] use: sacred music
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[religious]], [[holy]], [[ecclesiastical]], [[hallowed]]
**3** regarded as too important to be changed or interfered with: sacred principles of free speech
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inviolable]], [[protected]], [[sacrosanct]], [[hallowed]], [[inalienable]], [[unalterable]]
**4** **sacred to** dedicated to: the site is sacred to Vishnu
