**hue** *n*
**1** the feature of [[colour]] that enables an observer to classify it as red, blue, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[colour]], [[tone]], [[shade]], [[dye]], [[tint]], [[tinge]]
**2** a [[shade]] of a [[colour]]
