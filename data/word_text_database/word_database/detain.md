**detain** *vb*
**1** to [[delay]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delay]], [[hold up]], [[hamper]], [[hinder]], [[retard]], [[impede]], [[keep back]], slow up *or* down
**2** to force (someone) to stay: the police detained him for questioning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold]], [[arrest]], [[confine]], [[restrain]], [[imprison]], [[intern]], [[take prisoner]], [[hold in custody]]
 **[[detainee]]** *n* 
 **[[detainment]]** *n*
