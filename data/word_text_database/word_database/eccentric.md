**eccentric** *adj*
**1** [[unconventional]] or [[odd]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[odd]], [[strange]], [[peculiar]], [[irregular]], [[quirky]], [[unconventional]], [[idiosyncratic]], [[outlandish]], [[daggy]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**2** (of circles) not having the same centre
 *n*
**3** a person who behaves unconventionally or oddly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crank]] (*informal*), [[character]] (*informal*), [[oddball]] (*informal*), [[nonconformist]]
 **[[eccentrically]]** *adv*
