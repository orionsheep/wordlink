**backfire** *vb* **-firing**, **-fired**
**1** (of a plan or scheme) to [[fail]] to have the desired effect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fail]], [[founder]], [[flop]] (*informal*), [[rebound]], [[boomerang]], [[miscarry]], [[misfire]]
**2** (of an internal-combustion engine) to make a loud noise as a result of an explosion of unburnt gases in the exhaust system
