**moment** *n*
**1** a short period of [[time]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instant]], [[second]], [[flash]], [[twinkling]], [[split second]], [[jiffy]] (*informal*), [[trice]]
**2** a specific [[instant]] or [[point]] in [[time]]: at that moment the phone rang
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[time]], [[point]], [[stage]], [[juncture]]
**3** **the moment** the present [[point]] of [[time]]: for the moment he is out of prison
**4** importance, significance, or value: a matter of greatest moment
**5** (*physics*)
**A** a tendency to produce motion, esp. rotation about a [[point]] or axis
 **B** the product of a physical quantity, such as force or mass, and its distance from a fixed reference [[point]]
