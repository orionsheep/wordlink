**assume** *vb* **-suming**, **-sumed**
**1** to take to be true without proof
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[presume]], [[think]], [[believe]], [[expect]], [[suppose]], [[imagine]], [[fancy]], [[take for granted]]
**2** to undertake or [[take on]]: every general staff officer was able to assume control of the army
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take on]], [[accept]], [[shoulder]], [[take over]], [[put on]], [[enter upon]]
**3** to make a pretence of: the man had assumed a debonair attitude
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[simulate]], [[affect]], [[adopt]], [[put on]], [[imitate]], [[mimic]], [[feign]], [[impersonate]]
**4** to [[take on]]: her eyes assumed a scared haunted look
