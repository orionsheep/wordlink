**peddle** *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**1** to [[sell]] (goods) from place to place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sell]], [[trade]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[market]], [[hawk]], [[flog]] (*slang*)
**2** to [[sell]] (illegal drugs)
**3** to advocate (an idea or information) persistently: the version of events being peddled by his opponents
