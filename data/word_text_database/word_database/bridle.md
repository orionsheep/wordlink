**bridle** *n*
**1** headgear for controlling a horse, consisting of straps and a bit and reins
**2** something that curbs or restrains
 *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**3** to show anger or indignation: he bridled at the suggestion that he should resign
**4** to put a bridle on (a horse)
**5** to restrain; curb
