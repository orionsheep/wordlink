**arrival** *n*
**1** the act of [[arriving]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appearance]], [[coming]], [[arriving]], [[entrance]], [[advent]], [[materialization]]
**2** a person or thing that has just arrived
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[newcomer]], [[incomer]], [[visitor]], [[caller]], [[entrant]]
**3** (*informal*) a recently born baby
