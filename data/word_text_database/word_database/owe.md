**owe** *vb* **owing**, **owed**
**1** to be under an obligation to pay an amount of money to (someone): he owes me a lot of money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be in debt]] (to), [[be in arrears]] (to), [[be overdrawn]] (by), be obligated *or* indebted (to)
**2** to feel an obligation to do or give: I think I owe you an apology
**3** **owe something to** to have something as a result of: many serving officers owe their present position to the former president
