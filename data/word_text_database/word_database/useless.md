**useless** *adj*
**1** having no practical use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pointless]], [[futile]], [[vain]]
**2** (*informal*) [[ineffectual]], weak, or stupid: I'm useless at most things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worthless]], [[valueless]], [[impractical]], [[fruitless]], [[unproductive]], [[ineffectual]], [[unsuitable]]
 **[[uselessly]]** *adv* 
 **[[uselessness]]** *n*
