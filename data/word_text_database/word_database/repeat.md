**repeat** *vb*
**1** to say, write, or do again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reiterate]] (*formal*), [[restate]]
**2** to tell to another person (the secrets told to one by someone else)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[retell]], [[echo]], [[replay]], [[reproduce]], [[rerun]], [[reshow]]
**3** to recite (a poem, etc.) from memory
**4** to occur more than once: this pattern repeats itself many times
**5** (of food) to be tasted again after eating as the result of belching
**6** to say (the words or sounds) uttered by someone else; [[echo]]
 *n*
**7** the act or an instance of repeating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[repetition]], [[echo]], [[reiteration]]
**8** a word, action, pattern, etc., that is [[repeated]]
**9** (*radio*, *television*) a broadcast of a programme which has been broadcast before
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rerun]], [[replay]], [[reshowing]]
**10** (*music*) a passage that is an exact restatement of the passage preceding it
 **[[repeated]]** *adj* 
 **[[repeatedly]]** *adv* 
 **[[repeatable]]** *adj*
