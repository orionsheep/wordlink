**stand for** *vb*
**1** to represent: AIDS stands for Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[stand for something]]**: represent, mean, signify, denote, indicate, symbolize, betoken
**2** to support and represent (an idea or a belief): to stand for liberty and truth
**3** (*informal*) to tolerate or bear: I won't stand for this!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[stand for something]]**: tolerate, bear, endure, put up with, brook
