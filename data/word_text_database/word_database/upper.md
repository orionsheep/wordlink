**[[upper]]** *adj*
**1** higher or highest in physical position, wealth, rank, or status
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[topmost]], [[top]]
**2** **[[Upper]]** (*geol*) denoting the late part of a period or formation: [[Upper]] Cretaceous
 *n*
**3** the part of a shoe above the sole
**4** **on one's uppers** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) very poor; penniless
