**brittle** *adj*
**1** easily cracked or broken; [[fragile]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fragile]], [[delicate]], [[crisp]], [[crumbling]], [[frail]], [[crumbly]], [[breakable]], [[friable]]
**2** curt or irritable: a brittle reply
**3** hard or sharp in quality: a brittle laugh
 **[[brittly]]** *adv*
