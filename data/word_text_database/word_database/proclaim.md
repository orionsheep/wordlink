**1** to [[announce]] publicly; [[declare]]: Greece was proclaimed an independent kingdom in 1832
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pronounce]], [[announce]], [[declare]]
**2** to [[indicate]] plainly: the sharp hard glint in the eye proclaimed her determination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[announce]], [[declare]], [[advertise]], [[publish]], [[indicate]], [[herald]], [[circulate]], [[profess]]
 **[[proclamation]]** *n*
