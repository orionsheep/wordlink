**obey** *vb*
**1** to [[carry out]] instructions or orders; be obedient
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry out]], [[follow]], [[implement]], [[act upon]], [[carry through]]
**2** to act in accordance with one's feelings, an impulse, etc.: I had obeyed the impulse to open the gate and had walked up the drive
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abide by]], [[keep]], [[follow]], [[comply with]], [[observe]], [[heed]], [[conform to]], [[keep to]]
