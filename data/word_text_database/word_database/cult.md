◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sect]], [[faction]], [[school]], [[religion]], [[clique]], [[hauhau]] (*NZ*)
**2** a [[sect]] devoted to the beliefs of a cult
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sect]], [[faction]], [[school]], [[religion]], [[clique]], [[hauhau]] (*NZ*)
**3** devoted attachment to a person, idea, or activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obsession]], [[worship]], [[devotion]], [[idolization]]
**4** a popular [[fashion]]: the bungee-jumping cult
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[craze]], [[fashion]], [[trend]], fad
 *adj*
**5** very popular among a limited group of people: a cult TV series
