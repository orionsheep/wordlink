**frozen** *vb*
**1** the past participle of **freeze**
 *adj*
**2** turned into or covered with ice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[icy]], [[hard]], [[solid]], [[frosted]], [[arctic]] (*informal*), [[ice-covered]], [[icebound]]
**3** killed or stiffened by extreme [[cold]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ice-cold]], [[freezing]], [[numb]], [[very cold]], [[frigid]], [[frozen stiff]]
**4** (of food) preserved by a [[freezing]] process
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chilled]], [[cold]], [[iced]], [[refrigerated]], [[ice-cold]]
**5**
**A** (of prices or wages) officially fixed at a certain level
 **B** (of business assets) not convertible into cash
**6** motionless: she was frozen in horror
