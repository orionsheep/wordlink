**dazzle** *vb* **-zling**, **-zled**
**1** to [[impress]] greatly: she was dazzled by his wit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impress]], [[amaze]], [[overwhelm]], [[astonish]], [[overpower]], [[bowl over]] (*informal*), [[take your breath away]]
**2** to [[blind]] for a short time by sudden excessive light: he passed two cars and they dazzled him with their headlights
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blind]], [[confuse]], [[daze]], bedazzle
 *n*
**3** bright light that dazzles
 **[[dazzling]]** *adj* 
 **[[dazzlingly]]** *adv*
