**mistress** *n*
**1** a woman who has a continuing sexual relationship with a man who is usually married to somebody else
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lover]], [[girlfriend]], [[concubine]] (*old-fashioned*), [[kept woman]], [[paramour]] (*old-fashioned*)
**2** a woman in a position of authority, ownership, or control
**3** a woman having control over something specified: she is a mistress of disguise
**4** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a female teacher
