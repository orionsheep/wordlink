**contemporary** *adj*
**1** existing or occurring at the [[present]] time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[modern]], [[recent]], [[current]], [[up-to-date]], [[present-day]], [[à la mode]], [[newfangled]], [[present]]
**2** living or occurring in the same period
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coexisting]], [[concurrent]], [[contemporaneous]]
**3** [[modern]] in style or fashion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[modern]], [[recent]], [[current]], [[up-to-date]], [[present-day]], [[à la mode]], [[newfangled]], [[present]]
**4** of approximately the same age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coexisting]], [[concurrent]], [[contemporaneous]]
 *n*, *pl* **-raries**
**5** a person or thing living at the same time or of approximately the same age as another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peer]], [[fellow]], [[equal]]
