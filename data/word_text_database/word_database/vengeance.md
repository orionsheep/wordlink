**vengeance** *n*
**1** the act of killing, injuring, or harming someone for [[revenge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[revenge]], [[retaliation]], [[reprisal]], [[retribution]], [[requital]]
**2** **with a vengeance** to a much greater extent or with much greater force than expected: my career was beginning to take off with a vengeance
