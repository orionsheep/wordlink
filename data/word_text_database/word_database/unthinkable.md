**unthinkable** *adj*
**1** so shocking or unpleasant that one cannot believe it to be true
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impossible]], [[out of the question]], [[inconceivable]], [[absurd]], [[unreasonable]]
**2** [[unimaginable]] or [[inconceivable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inconceivable]], [[incredible]], [[unimaginable]]
