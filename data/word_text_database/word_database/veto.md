**veto** (**vee**-toe) *n*, *pl* **-toes**
**1** the power to prevent legislation or action proposed by others: no single state has a veto
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[dismissal]], [[rejection]], [[vetoing]], [[boycott]], [[embargo]], [[prohibiting]], [[prohibition]]
**2** the exercise of this power
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[dismissal]], [[rejection]], [[vetoing]], [[boycott]], [[embargo]], [[prohibiting]], [[prohibition]]
 *vb* **-toing**, **-toed**
**3** to refuse consent to (a proposal, such as a government bill)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[block]], [[reject]], [[rule out]], [[turn down]], [[forbid]], [[boycott]], [[prohibit]]
**4** to [[prohibit]] or [[forbid]]: the minister vetoed the appointments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[block]], [[reject]], [[rule out]], [[turn down]], [[forbid]], [[boycott]], [[prohibit]]
