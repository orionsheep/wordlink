**fuse**^2 *n*
**1** a protective device for safeguarding electric circuits, containing a wire that melts and breaks the circuit when the current exceeds a certain value
 *vb* **fusing**, **fused**
**2** (*Brit*) to fail or cause to fail as a result of a fuse blowing
**3** to equip (a plug or circuit) with a fuse
**4** to join or become combined: the two ideas fused in his mind
**5** to unite or become united by melting
**6** to become or cause to become liquid, esp. by the action of heat
