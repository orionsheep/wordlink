**filling** *n*
**1** a substance or thing used to fill something: a sandwich filling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stuffing]], [[padding]], [[filler]], [[wadding]], [[inside]], [[insides]], [[contents]]
**2** (*dentistry*) a substance that fills a gap or cavity of a tooth
 *adj*
**3** (of food or a meal) [[substantial]] and [[satisfying]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satisfying]], [[heavy]], [[square]], [[substantial]], [[ample]]
