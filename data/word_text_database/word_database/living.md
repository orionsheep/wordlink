**living** *adj*
**1** possessing life; not dead or inanimate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alive]], [[existing]], [[moving]], [[active]], [[breathing]], [[animate]]
**2** currently [[in use]] or valid: a living alliance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[current]], [[present]], [[active]], [[contemporary]], [[in use]], [[extant]]
**3** seeming to be real: a living doll
**4** (of people or animals) [[existing]] in the [[present]] age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alive]], [[existing]], [[moving]], [[active]], [[breathing]], [[animate]]
**5** very: the living image
**6** of or like everyday life: living costs
**7** of or involving those now [[alive]]: one of our greatest living actors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alive]], [[existing]], [[moving]], [[active]], [[breathing]], [[animate]]
 *n*
**8** the condition of being [[alive]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alive]], [[existing]], [[moving]], [[active]], [[breathing]], [[animate]]
**9** the manner of one's life: high living
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lifestyle]], [[ways]], [[situation]], [[conduct]], [[behaviour]], [[customs]], [[way of life]]
**10** one's financial means
**11** (*Church of England*) a benefice
