**visit** *vb* **-siting**, **-sited**
**1** to go or come to see (a person or place)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call on]], [[drop in on]] (*informal*), [[stop by]], [[look up]], [[go see]] (*US*), [[swing by]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[stay with]] (someone) as a guest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stay at]], [[stay with]], [[spend time with]]
**3** (*old-fashioned*) (of a disease or disaster) to afflict
**4** **visit on *or* upon** to inflict (punishment) on
**5** **visit with** (*US* *informal*) to chat with (someone)
 *n*
**6** the act or an instance of visiting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call]], [[social call]]
**7** a professional or official [[call]]
**8** a [[stay]] as a guest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trip]], [[stop]], [[stay]], [[break]], [[tour]], [[holiday]], [[vacation]], [[stopover]]
