**confront** *vb*
**1** (of a problem or task) to present itself to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trouble]], [[face]], [[perturb]], [[bedevil]]
**2** to meet [[face]] to [[face]] in hostility or defiance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[challenge]], [[face]], [[oppose]], [[tackle]], [[encounter]], [[defy]], [[stand up to]], [[accost]]
**3** to present (someone) with something, esp. in order to accuse or criticize: she finally confronted him with her suspicions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[challenge]], [[face]], [[oppose]], [[tackle]], [[encounter]], [[defy]], [[stand up to]], [[accost]]
