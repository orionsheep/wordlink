**scenery** *n*, *pl* **-eries**
**1** the natural features of a [[landscape]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[landscape]], [[view]], [[surroundings]], [[terrain]], [[vista]]
**2** (*theatre*) the painted backcloths or screens used to represent a location in a theatre or studio
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[set]], [[setting]], [[backdrop]], [[flats]], [[stage set]]
