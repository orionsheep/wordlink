**elevate** *vb* **-vating**, **-vated**
**1** to [[raise]] in rank or status: he had elevated flirting to an art form
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promote]], [[raise]], [[advance]], [[upgrade]], [[exalt]], [[kick upstairs]] (*informal*), [[aggrandize]], [[give advancement to]]
**2** to [[lift]] to a higher place: this action elevates the upper back
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[raise]], [[lift]], [[heighten]], [[uplift]], [[hoist]], [[lift up]], [[raise up]], [[hike up]]
