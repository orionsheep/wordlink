**incubate** (**in**-cube-ate) *vb* **-bating**, **-bated**
**1** (of birds) to hatch (eggs) by sitting on them
**2** to cause (bacteria) to develop, esp. in an incubator or culture medium
**3** (of disease germs) to remain inactive in an animal or human before causing disease
**4** to develop gradually
 **[[incubation]]** *n*
