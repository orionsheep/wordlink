**bathe** *vb* **bathing**, **bathed**
**1** to [[swim]] in open water for pleasure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swim]]
**2** to apply liquid to (the skin or a wound) in order to [[cleanse]] or soothe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cleanse]], [[clean]], [[wash]], [[soak]], [[rinse]]
**3** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to [[wash]] in a [[bath]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wash]], [[clean]], [[bath]], [[shower]], [[soak]], [[cleanse]], [[scrub]], [[rinse]]
**4** to spread over: bathed in moonlight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[flood]], [[steep]], [[engulf]], [[immerse]], [[overrun]], [[suffuse]], [[wash]] over
