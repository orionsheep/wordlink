**berth** *n*
**1** a [[bunk]] in a ship or train
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bunk]], [[bed]], [[hammock]], [[billet]]
**2** (*naut*) a place assigned to a ship at a mooring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anchorage]], [[haven]], [[port]], [[harbour]], [[dock]], [[pier]], [[wharf]], [[quay]]
**3** (*naut*) sufficient room for a ship to manoeuvre
**4** **give a wide berth to** to keep clear of
 *vb*
**5** (*naut*) to [[dock]] (a ship)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anchor]], [[land]], [[dock]], [[moor]], [[tie up]], [[drop anchor]]
**6** to provide with a sleeping place
**7** (*naut*) to pick up a mooring in an [[anchorage]]
