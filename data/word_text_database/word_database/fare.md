**fare** *n*
**1** the amount charged or paid for a journey in a bus, train, or plane
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]], [[price]], [[ticket price]], [[ticket money]]
**2** a paying passenger
**3** a range of [[food]] and drink: marvellous picnic fare
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[food]], [[provisions]], [[board]], [[rations]], [[kai]] (*NZ* *informal*), [[nourishment]], [[sustenance]], [[victuals]], nutriment
 *vb* **faring**, **[[fared]]**
**4** to [[get on]] (in a specified way): he [[fared]] well in the exam
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get on]], [[do]], [[manage]], [[make out]], [[prosper]], [[get along]]
