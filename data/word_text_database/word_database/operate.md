◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[function]], [[work]], [[act]]
**2** to [[control]] the working of (a machine)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[work]], [[use]], [[control]], [[manoeuvre]]
**3** to [[manage]], [[direct]], or [[run]] (a business or system)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manage]], [[run]], [[direct]], [[handle]], [[supervise]], [[be in charge of]]
**4** to perform a surgical operation (upon a person or animal)
