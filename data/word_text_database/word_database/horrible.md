**horrible** *adj*
**1** disagreeable and [[unpleasant]]: a horrible hotel room
**2** causing fear, shock, or disgust: he died a horrible death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dreadful]], [[terrible]], [[awful]], [[nasty]], [[cruel]], [[mean]], [[unpleasant]], [[horrid]] (*informal*) 
 **[[horribly]]** *adv*
