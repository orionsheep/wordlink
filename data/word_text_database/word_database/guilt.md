**guilt** *n*
**1** the fact or state of having done wrong: the court was unable to establish guilt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[culpability]], [[blame]], [[responsibility]], [[misconduct]], [[wickedness]], [[sinfulness]], [[guiltiness]]
**2** [[remorse]] or [[self-reproach]] caused by feeling that one has done something wrong: he feels no guilt about the planned cutbacks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shame]], [[regret]], [[remorse]], [[contrition]], [[guilty conscience]], [[self-reproach]]
