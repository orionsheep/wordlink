**invade** *vb* **-vading**, **-vaded**
**1** to enter (a country or territory) by military force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[storm]], [[assault]], [[capture]], [[occupy]], [[seize]], [[raid]], [[overwhelm]]
**2** to enter in large numbers: the town was invaded by rugby supporters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[infest]], [[swarm]], [[overrun]], [[ravage]], [[beset]], [[pervade]], [[permeate]]
**3** to disturb (privacy, etc.)
 **[[invader]]** *n*
