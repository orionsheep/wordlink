**distract** *vb*
**1** to draw (a person or his or her attention) away from something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[divert]], [[sidetrack]], [[draw away]], [[turn aside]], [[lead astray]], draw *or* lead away from
**2** to [[amuse]] or [[entertain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amuse]], [[occupy]], [[entertain]], [[beguile]], [[engross]]
