**overturn** *vb*
**1** to turn over or upside down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tip over]], [[topple]], [[upturn]], [[capsize]], [[upend]], [[keel over]], [[overbalance]]
**2** to overrule or [[reverse]] (a legal decision)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverse]], [[change]], [[cancel]], [[abolish]], [[overthrow]], [[set aside]], [[repeal]], [[quash]]
**3** to [[overthrow]] or [[destroy]] (a government)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overthrow]], [[defeat]], [[destroy]], [[overcome]], [[bring down]], [[oust]], [[topple]], [[depose]]
