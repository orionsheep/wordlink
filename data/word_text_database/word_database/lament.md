**lament** *vb*
**1** to feel or express sorrow or [[regret]] (for or over)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bemoan]], [[grieve]], [[mourn]], [[weep over]], [[complain about]], [[regret]], [[wail about]], deplore
 *n*
**2** an expression of sorrow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complaint]], [[moan]], [[wailing]], [[lamentation]]
**3** a poem or song in which a death is lamented
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dirge]], [[requiem]], [[elegy]], [[threnody]] (*formal*) 
 **[[lamentation]]** *n*
