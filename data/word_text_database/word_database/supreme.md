**supreme** *adj*
**1** of [[highest]] status or power: the Supreme Council
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paramount]], [[surpassing]]
**2** of [[highest]] quality or importance: a supreme player
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chief]], [[leading]], [[principal]], [[highest]], [[head]], [[top]], [[prime]], [[foremost]]
**3** [[greatest]] in degree; extreme: supreme happiness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ultimate]], [[highest]], [[greatest]]
 **[[supremely]]** *adv*
