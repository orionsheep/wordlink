◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[total]], [[body]], [[whole]], [[amount]], [[collection]], [[cluster]], [[mass]], [[sum]], [[combination]]
**2** (*geol*) a rock, such as granite, consisting of a mixture of minerals
**3** the sand and stone [[mixed]] with cement and water to make concrete
 *adj*
**4** formed of separate units [[collected]] into a [[whole]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collective]], [[mixed]], [[combined]], [[collected]], [[accumulated]], [[composite]], cumulative
 *vb* **-gating**, **-gated**
**5** to [[combine]] or be [[combined]] into a [[whole]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[combine]], [[mix]], [[collect]], [[assemble]], [[heap]], [[accumulate]], [[pile]], [[amass]]
**6** to [[amount]] to (a particular number)
