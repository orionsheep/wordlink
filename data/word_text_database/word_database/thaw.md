**thaw** *vb*
**1** to [[melt]] or cause to [[melt]]: snow thawing in the gutter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[melt]], [[dissolve]], [[soften]], [[defrost]], [[warm]], [[liquefy]], [[unfreeze]]
**2** (of frozen food) to become or cause to become unfrozen; [[defrost]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[melt]], [[dissolve]], [[soften]], [[defrost]], [[warm]], [[liquefy]], [[unfreeze]]
**3** (of weather) to be [[warm]] enough to cause ice or snow to [[melt]]: it's not freezing, it's thawing again
**4** to become more relaxed or friendly: only with Llewelyn did he thaw, let his defences down
 *n*
**5** the act or process of thawing
**6** a spell of relatively [[warm]] weather, causing snow or ice to [[melt]]
