**fellowship** *n*
**1** the state of sharing mutual interests or activities
**2** a [[society]] of people sharing mutual interests or activities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[society]], [[club]], [[league]], [[association]], [[organization]], [[guild]], [[fraternity]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[sorority]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[brotherhood]]
**3** [[companionship]] or friendship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[camaraderie]], [[brotherhood]], [[sisterhood]], [[companionship]], [[sociability]]
**4** (*education*) a financed research post providing study facilities
