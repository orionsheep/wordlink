**disadvantage** *n*
**1** an unfavourable or harmful circumstance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drawback]], [[trouble]], [[handicap]], [[nuisance]], [[snag]], [[inconvenience]], [[downside]]
**2** **at a disadvantage** in a less favourable position than other people: he continued to insist that he was at a disadvantage at the hearings
 **[[disadvantageous]]** *adj*
