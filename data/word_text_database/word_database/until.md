**until** *conj*
**1** [[up to]] a time that: he lifted the wire until it was taut
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[till]], [[up to]], [[up till]], [[up to the time]], [[as late as]]
**2** [[before]] (a time or event): I won't speak to him until he apologizes
 *prep*
**3** (often preceded by *up*) in or throughout the period [[before]]: up until then I'd never thought about having kids
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[till]], [[up to]], [[up till]], [[up to the time]], [[as late as]]
**4** [[before]]: Baker does not get to Israel until Sunday
