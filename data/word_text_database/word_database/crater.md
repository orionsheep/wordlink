**crater** *n*
**1** the bowl-shaped opening in a volcano or a geyser
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hollow]], [[hole]], [[depression]], [[dip]], [[cavity]]
**2** a [[cavity]] made by the impact of a meteorite or an explosion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hollow]], [[hole]], [[depression]], [[dip]], [[cavity]]
**3** a roughly circular [[cavity]] on the surface of the moon and some planets
 *vb*
**4** to make or form craters in (a surface, such as the ground)
 **[[cratered]]** *adj*
