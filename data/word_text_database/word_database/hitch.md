**hitch** *n*
**1** a temporary or minor [[problem]] or [[difficulty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[catch]] (*informal*), [[difficulty]], [[hold-up]], [[obstacle]], [[drawback]], [[snag]], [[uphill]] (*S African*), [[impediment]]
**2** a knot that can be undone by pulling against the direction of the strain that holds it
 *vb*
**3** (*informal*)
**A** to obtain (a ride) by hitchhiking
 **B** to [[hitchhike]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hitchhike]], [[thumb a lift]]
**4** to [[fasten]] with a knot or [[tie]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fasten]], [[join]], [[attach]], [[couple]], [[tie]], [[connect]], [[harness]], [[tether]]
**5** **get hitched** (*slang*) to get married
**6** **hitch up** to pull up (one's trousers, etc.) with a quick jerk
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hitch something up]]**: pull up, tug, jerk, yank
