**coat** *n*
**1** an outer garment with sleeves, [[covering]] the body from the shoulders to below the waist
**2** the [[hair]], [[wool]], or [[fur]] of an animal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fur]], [[hair]], [[skin]], [[hide]], [[wool]], [[fleece]], [[pelt]]
**3** any [[layer]] that covers a surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[layer]], [[covering]], [[coating]], overlay
 *vb*
**4** to [[cover]] with a [[layer]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[spread]], [[plaster]], [[smear]]
