**pray** *vb*
**1** to say prayers (to one's God)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[say your prayers]], [[offer a prayer]], [[recite the rosary]]
**2** to [[ask]] earnestly; [[beg]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beg]], [[ask]], [[plead]], [[petition]], [[request]], [[solicit]], [[implore]], beseech
 *adv*
**3** (*archaic*) I [[beg]] you; please: pray, leave us alone
