**irony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** the mildly sarcastic use of words to imply the opposite of what they normally mean
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sarcasm]], [[mockery]], [[ridicule]], [[satire]], [[cynicism]], [[derision]]
**2** a situation or result that is the direct opposite of what was expected or intended
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paradox]], [[incongruity]]
