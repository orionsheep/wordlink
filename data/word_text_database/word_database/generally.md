**generally** *adv*
**1** [[usually]]; [[as a rule]]: these protests have generally been peaceful
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usually]], [[commonly]], [[typically]], [[normally]], [[on the whole]], [[by and large]], [[ordinarily]], [[as a rule]]
**2** [[commonly]] or [[widely]]: it's generally agreed he has performed well
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commonly]], [[widely]], [[publicly]], [[universally]], [[extensively]], [[popularly]], [[conventionally]], [[customarily]]
**3** not specifically; broadly: what are your thoughts generally about the war?
