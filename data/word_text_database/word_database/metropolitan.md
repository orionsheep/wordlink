**metropolitan** *adj*
**1** of or characteristic of a metropolis
**2** of or consisting of a city and its suburbs: the Tokyo metropolitan region
**3** of or belonging to the home territories of a country, as opposed to overseas territories: metropolitan France
 *n*
**4** (*Christianity*) the senior member of the clergy, esp. an archbishop, in charge of an ecclesiastical province
**5** an inhabitant of a large city
