**sentence** *n*
**1** a sequence of words constituting a statement, question, or a command that begins with a capital letter and ends with a full stop when written down
**2**
**A** the [[decision]] of a law court as to what punishment is passed on a convicted person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[verdict]], [[order]], [[ruling]], [[decision]], [[judgment]], decree
 **B** the punishment passed on a convicted person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: punishment, condemnation
 *vb* **-tencing**, **-tenced**
**3** to pronounce sentence on (a convicted person) in a law court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condemn]], [[doom]]
 **[[sentential]]** *adj*
