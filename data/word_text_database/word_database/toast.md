**toast**^2 *n*
**1** a proposal of [[health]] or success given to a person or thing and marked by people raising glasses and drinking together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tribute]], [[compliment]], [[salute]], [[health]], [[pledge]], [[salutation]] (*formal*)
**2** a person or thing that is honoured: his success made him the toast of the British film industry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[favourite]], [[celebrity]], [[darling]], [[talk]], [[pet]], [[focus of attention]], hero *or* heroine, blue-eyed boy *or* girl (*Brit* *informal*)
 *vb*
**3** to propose or drink a toast to (a person or thing)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drink to]], [[honour]], [[salute]], drink (to) the [[health]] of
