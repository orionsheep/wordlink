**charity** *n*
**1** *pl* **-ties** an organization set up to provide [[help]] to those in need
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charitable organization]], [[fund]], [[movement]], [[trust]], [[endowment]]
**2** the giving of [[help]], such as money or food, to those in need
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[donations]], [[help]], [[relief]], [[gift]], [[contributions]], [[assistance]], [[hand-out]], [[philanthropy]], [[koha]] (*NZ*)
**3** [[help]] given to those in need; alms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[donations]], [[help]], [[relief]], [[gift]], [[contributions]], [[assistance]], [[hand-out]], [[philanthropy]], [[koha]] (*NZ*)
**4** a kindly attitude towards people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kindness]], [[humanity]], [[goodwill]], [[compassion]], [[generosity]], [[indulgence]], [[altruism]], [[benevolence]], [[aroha]] (*NZ*)
