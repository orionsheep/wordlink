**overnight** *adv*
**1** during the night
**2** in or as if in the course of one night; suddenly: we are not saying that a change like this would happen overnight
 *adj*
**3** done in, occurring in, or lasting the night: the army has ordered an overnight curfew
**4** staying for one night: overnight guests
**5** for use during a single night: should I pack an overnight case?
**6** happening very quickly; sudden: he doesn't expect the programme to be an overnight success
