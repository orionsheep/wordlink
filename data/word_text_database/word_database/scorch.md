**scorch** *vb*
**1** to [[burn]] or become burnt slightly on the surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[burn]], [[sear]], [[roast]], [[wither]], [[shrivel]], [[parch]], [[singe]]
**2** to [[parch]] or [[shrivel]] from heat
**3** (*informal*) to criticize harshly
 *n*
**4** a slight [[burn]]
**5** a mark caused by the application of excessive heat
 **[[scorching]]** *adj*
