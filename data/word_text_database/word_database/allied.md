**[[allied]]** *adj*
**1** [[united]] by a common aim or common characteristics: the [[allied]] areas of telepathy and clairvoyance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[united]], [[linked]], [[related]], [[combined]], [[integrated]], [[affiliated]], [[cooperating]], [[in league]]
**2** **[[Allied]]** relating to the countries that fought against Germany and Japan in the Second World War: the [[Allied]] bombing of German cities
