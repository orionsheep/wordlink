**ebb** *vb*
**1** (of the sea or the tide) to [[flow back]] from its highest point
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow back]], [[go out]], [[withdraw]], [[retreat]], [[wane]], [[recede]]
**2** to fall away or [[decline]]: her anger ebbed away
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decline]], [[flag]], [[diminish]], [[decrease]], [[dwindle]], [[lessen]], [[subside]], fall away
 *n*
**3** the [[flowing back]] of the tide from high to [[low water]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flowing back]], [[going out]], [[withdrawal]], [[retreat]], [[wane]], [[low water]], [[low tide]], [[outgoing tide]]
**4** **at a low ebb** in a weak state: her creativity was at a low ebb
