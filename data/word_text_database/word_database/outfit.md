**outfit** *n*
**1** a set of [[clothes]] worn together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[costume]], [[dress]], [[clothes]], [[clothing]], [[suit]], [[get-up]] (*informal*), [[kit]], [[ensemble]]
**2** (*informal*) a [[group]] of people working together as a [[unit]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[company]], [[team]], [[party]], [[unit]], [[crowd]], [[squad]], [[organization]]
**3** a set of equipment for a particular task; [[kit]]: a complete anti-snakebite outfit
