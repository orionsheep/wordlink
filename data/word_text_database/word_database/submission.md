**submission** *n*
**1** an act or instance of [[capitulation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surrender]], [[yielding]], [[giving in]], [[cave-in]] (*informal*), [[capitulation]]
**2** the act of submitting (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[presentation]], [[handing in]], [[entry]], [[tendering]]
**3** something submitted, such as a proposal
**4** the state in which someone has to accept the control of another person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compliance]], [[obedience]], [[meekness]], [[resignation]], [[deference]], [[passivity]], [[docility]]
