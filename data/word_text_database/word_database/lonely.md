**lonely** *adj* **-lier**, **-liest**
**1** unhappy as a result of solitude
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solitary]], [[alone]], [[isolated]], [[abandoned]], [[lone]], [[withdrawn]], [[single]], [[forsaken]], [[lonesome]] (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*)
**2** resulting from the state of being [[alone]]: command can be a lonely business
**3** [[isolated]] and not much visited by people: a lonely beach
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desolate]], [[deserted]], [[remote]], [[isolated]], [[out-of-the-way]], [[secluded]], [[uninhabited]], [[godforsaken]]
 **[[loneliness]]** *n*
