**advertise** *vb* **-tising**, **-tised**
**1** to present or praise (goods or a service) to the public, in order to encourage sales
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[publicize]], [[promote]], [[plug]] (*informal*), [[announce]], [[inform]], [[hype]], [[notify]], [[tout]]
**2** to make (a vacancy, an event, or an article for sale) publicly known
