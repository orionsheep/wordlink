**underestimate** *vb* **-mating**, **-mated**
**1** to make too low an estimate of: the trust had underestimated the cost of work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[underrate]], [[undervalue]], [[belittle]]
**2** to not be aware or take account of the full abilities or potential of: the police had underestimated him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[undervalue]], [[understate]], [[diminish]], [[play down]], [[minimize]], [[downgrade]], [[miscalculate]], trivialize
 *n*
