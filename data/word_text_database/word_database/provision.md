**provision** *n*
**1** the act of [[supplying]] something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supplying]], [[giving]], [[providing]], [[supply]], [[delivery]], [[distribution]], [[catering]], [[presentation]]
**2** something supplied
**3** **provisions** [[food]] and other necessities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[food]], [[supplies]], [[stores]], [[fare]], [[rations]], [[foodstuff]], [[kai]] (*NZ* *informal*), [[victuals]] (*old-fashioned*), [[edibles]]
**4** a [[condition]] incorporated in a document
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condition]], [[term]], [[requirement]], [[demand]], [[rider]], [[restriction]], [[qualification]], [[clause]]
**5** **make provision for** to make arrangements for beforehand: the company has made provision for future losses
 *vb*
**6** to [[supply]] with provisions
