◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[simple]], [[rudimentary]], [[basic]], [[primitive]], [[coarse]], [[clumsy]], [[rough-and-ready]]
**2** [[tasteless]] or [[vulgar]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vulgar]], [[dirty]], [[rude]], [[obscene]], [[coarse]], [[indecent]], [[tasteless]], [[smutty]]
**3** in a [[natural]] or [[unrefined]] state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unrefined]], [[natural]], [[raw]], [[unprocessed]]
