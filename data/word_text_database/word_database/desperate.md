**desperate** *adj*
**1** willing to do anything to improve one's situation
**2** (of an action) undertaken as a last resort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[last-ditch]], [[daring]], [[furious]], [[risky]], [[frantic]], [[audacious]]
**3** very [[grave]]: in desperate agony
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grave]], [[pressing]], [[serious]], [[severe]], [[extreme]], [[urgent]], [[drastic]]
**4** having a great need or desire: I was desperate for a child
 **[[desperately]]** *adv*
