**interruption** *n*
**1** something that interrupts, such as a comment or question
**2** an interval or intermission
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disruption]], [[break]], [[disturbance]], [[hitch]], [[intrusion]]
**3** the act of interrupting or the state of being interrupted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stoppage]], [[pause]], [[suspension]]
