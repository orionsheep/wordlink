**shudder** *vb*
**1** to [[shake]] or [[tremble]] suddenly and violently from horror or fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shiver]], [[shake]], [[tremble]], [[quake]], [[quiver]], [[convulse]]
**2** (of a machine) to [[shake]] violently
 *n*
**3** a [[shiver]] of fear or horror
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shiver]], [[tremor]], [[quiver]], [[spasm]]
