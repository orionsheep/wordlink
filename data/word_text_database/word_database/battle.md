**battle** *n*
**1** a [[fight]] between large armed forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[attack]], [[action]], [[struggle]], [[conflict]], [[clash]], [[encounter]], [[combat]], [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[boilover]] (*Austral*)
**2** [[conflict]] or [[struggle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[campaign]], [[drive]], [[movement]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[struggle]]
 *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**3** to [[fight]] in or as if in military [[combat]]: shop stewards battling to improve conditions at [[work]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrestle]], [[war]], [[fight]], [[argue]], [[dispute]], [[grapple]], [[clamour]], [[lock horns]]
**4** to [[struggle]]: she battled through the crowd
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[struggle]], [[work]], [[labour]], [[strain]], [[strive]], [[toil]], [[go all out]] (*informal*), [[give it your best shot]] (*informal*)
