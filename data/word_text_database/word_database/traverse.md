**traverse** *vb* **-versing**, **-versed**
**1** to move over or back and forth over; cross: he once traversed San Francisco harbour in a balloon
**2** to reach across
**3** to walk, climb, or ski diagonally up or down a slope
 *n*
**4** something being or lying across, such as a crossbar
**5** the act or an instance of traversing or crossing
**6** a path or road across
 *adj*
**7** being or lying across
 **[[traversal]]** *n*
