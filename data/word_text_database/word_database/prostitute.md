**prostitute** *n*
**1** a person who offers sexual intercourse in return for payment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sex worker]], [[call girl]], hooker (*US* *slang*, *derogatory*), pro (*slang*)
 *vb* **-tuting**, **-tuted**
**2** to offer (oneself or another) in sexual intercourse for money
**3** to offer (oneself or one's talent) for unworthy purposes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cheapen]], [[sell out]], [[pervert]], [[degrade]], [[devalue]], [[squander]], [[demean]], [[debase]]
 **[[prostitution]]** *n*
