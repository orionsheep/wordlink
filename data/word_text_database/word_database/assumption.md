**assumption** *n*
**1** something that is taken for granted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[presumption]], [[belief]], [[guess]], [[hypothesis]], [[inference]], [[conjecture]], [[surmise]], [[supposition]]
**2** the act of assuming power or possession
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[taking on]], [[managing]], [[handling]], [[shouldering]], [[putting on]], [[taking up]], [[takeover]], [[acquisition]]
