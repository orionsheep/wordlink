**grand** *adj*
**1** [[large]] or [[impressive]] in size or appearance; [[magnificent]]: the grand hall
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impressive]], [[great]], [[large]], [[magnificent]], [[imposing]], [[splendid]], [[regal]], [[stately]]
**2** [[ambitious]] or important: grand themes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ambitious]], [[great]], [[grandiose]]
**3** [[dignified]] or haughty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[superior]], [[great]], [[dignified]], [[stately]]
**4** (*informal*) [[excellent]] or [[wonderful]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellent]], [[great]] (*informal*), [[fine]], [[wonderful]], [[outstanding]], [[smashing]] (*informal*), [[first-class]], [[splendid]]
**5** comprehensive or complete: the grand total
 *n*
**6** *pl* **grand** (*slang*) a thousand pounds or dollars
**7** short for **grand piano**
 **[[grandly]]** *adv*
