**forgive** *vb* **-giving**, **-gave**, **-given**
**1** to stop feeling anger and resentment towards (a person) or at (an action that has caused upset or harm)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excuse]], [[pardon]], [[not hold something against]], [[understand]], [[acquit]], [[condone]], [[let off]] (*informal*), [[turn a blind eye to]]
**2** to [[pardon]] (a mistake)
**3** to free from (a debt)
