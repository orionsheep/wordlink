**convert** *vb*
**1** to change or [[adapt]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adapt]], [[modify]], [[remodel]], [[reorganize]], [[customize]], [[restyle]]
**2** to cause (someone) to change in opinion or belief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reform]], [[convince]], [[proselytize]]
**3** to change (a measurement) from one system of units to another
**4** to change (money) into a different currency
**5** (*rugby*) to make a conversion after (a try)
 *n*
**6** a person who has been converted to another belief or religion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[neophyte]] (*formal*), [[disciple]], [[proselyte]]
 **[[converter]]** * or ***[[convertor]]** *n*
