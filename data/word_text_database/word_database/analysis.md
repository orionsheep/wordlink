**analysis** (an-**nal**-liss-iss) *n*, *pl* **-ses** (-seez)
**1** the separation of a whole into its parts for study or [[interpretation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examination]], [[test]], [[inquiry]], [[investigation]], [[interpretation]], [[breakdown]], [[scanning]], [[evaluation]]
**2** a statement of the results of this
**3** short for **psychoanalysis**
