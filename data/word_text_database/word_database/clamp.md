**clamp** *n*
**1** a mechanical device with movable jaws for holding things together tightly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vice]], [[press]], [[grip]], [[bracket]], [[fastener]]
**2** See **wheel clamp**
 *vb*
**3** to [[fix]] or [[fasten]] with a clamp
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fasten]], [[fix]], [[secure]], [[brace]], [[make fast]]
**4** to immobilize (a car) by means of a wheel clamp
