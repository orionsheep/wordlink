**sketch** *n*
**1** a quick rough [[drawing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drawing]], [[design]], [[draft]], [[delineation]]
**2** a brief descriptive piece of writing
**3** a short funny piece of acting forming part of a show
**4** any brief [[outline]]
 *vb*
**5** to make a quick rough [[drawing]] (of)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[draw]], [[outline]], [[represent]], [[draft]], [[depict]], [[delineate]], [[rough out]]
**6** **sketch out** to make a brief description of: they sketched out plans for the invasion
