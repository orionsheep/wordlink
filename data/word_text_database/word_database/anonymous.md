**anonymous** *adj*
**1** by someone whose name is [[unknown]] or withheld: an anonymous letter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsigned]], [[uncredited]], [[unattributed]]
**2** having no known name: an anonymous writer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unnamed]], [[unknown]], [[unidentified]], [[nameless]], [[unacknowledged]], [[incognito]]
**3** lacking distinguishing characteristics: an anonymous little town
**4** **Anonymous** of an organization that helps applicants who remain anonymous: Alcoholics Anonymous
 **[[anonymously]]** *adv* 
 **[[anonymity]]** *n*
