**atmosphere** *n*
**1** the mass of gases surrounding the earth or any other heavenly body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[air]], [[sky]], [[heavens]], [[aerosphere]]
**2** the [[air]] in a particular place
**3** a pervasive [[feeling]] or [[mood]]: the atmosphere was tense
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[character]], [[environment]], [[spirit]], [[surroundings]], [[tone]], [[mood]], [[climate]]
**4** a unit of pressure equal to the normal pressure of the [[air]] at sea level
 **[[atmospheric]]** *adj* 
 **[[atmospherically]]** *adv*
