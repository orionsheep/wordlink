**infancy** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** the state or period of being an infant
**2** an early stage of growth or development: the technology is still in its infancy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beginnings]], [[start]], [[birth]], [[roots]], [[seeds]], [[origins]], [[dawn]] (*literary*), [[outset]]
**3** (*law*) the state or period of being a minor
