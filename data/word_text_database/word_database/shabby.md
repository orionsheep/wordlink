**1** old and [[worn]] in appearance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tatty]] (*Brit*), [[worn]], [[ragged]], [[scruffy]], [[tattered]], [[threadbare]]
**2** wearing [[worn]] and [[dirty]] clothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tatty]] (*Brit*), [[worn]], [[ragged]], [[scruffy]], [[tattered]], [[threadbare]]
**3** behaving in a [[mean]] or unfair way: shabby manoeuvres
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mean]], [[low]], [[rotten]] (*informal*), [[cheap]], [[dirty]], [[despicable]], [[contemptible]], [[scurvy]] (*old-fashioned*)
