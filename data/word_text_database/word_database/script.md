**script** *n*
**1** the [[text]] of a play, TV programme, or film for the use of performers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[text]], [[lines]], [[words]], [[book]], [[copy]], [[dialogue]], [[libretto]]
**2** an alphabet or system of [[writing]]: Cyrillic script
**3** a candidate's answer paper in an examination
**4** [[handwriting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handwriting]], [[writing]], [[calligraphy]], [[penmanship]] (*formal*)
**5** a typeface which looks like [[handwriting]]
 *vb*
**6** to [[write]] a script for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[write]], [[draft]]
