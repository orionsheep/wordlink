**clown** *n*
**1** a [[comic]] entertainer, usually bizarrely dressed and made up, appearing in the circus
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comedian]], [[fool]], [[harlequin]], [[jester]], [[buffoon]]
**2** an amusing person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[joker]], [[comic]], [[prankster]]
**3** a clumsy rude person
 *vb*
**4** to behave foolishly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[play the fool]], [[mess about]], [[jest]], [[act the fool]]
**5** to perform as a clown
 **[[clownish]]** *adj*
