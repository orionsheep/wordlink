**steam** *n*
**1** the vapour into which water changes when boiled
**2** the mist formed when such vapour condenses in the atmosphere
**3** (*informal*) power, energy, or speed
**4** **let off steam** (*informal*) to release pent-up energy or feelings
**5** **pick up steam** (*informal*) to gather momentum
 *adj*
**6** operated, heated, or powered by steam: a steam train
 *vb*
**7** to give off steam
**8** (of a vehicle) to move by steam power
**9** (*informal*) to proceed quickly and often forcefully
**10** to cook (food) in steam
**11** to treat (something) with steam, such as in cleaning or pressing clothes
**12** **steam open *or* off** to use steam in order to open or remove (something): let me steam open this letter
