**conflict** *n*
**1** [[opposition]] between ideas or interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[difference]], [[opposition]], [[hostility]], [[disagreement]], [[friction]], [[strife]], [[fighting]], [[cyberwar]]
**2** a struggle or [[battle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[battle]], [[war]], [[fight]], [[clash]], [[contest]], [[encounter]], [[combat]], [[strife]], boilover (*Austral*)
 *vb*
**3** to [[be incompatible]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be incompatible]], [[clash]], [[differ]], [[disagree]], [[collide]], [[be at variance]]
 **[[conflicting]]** *adj*
