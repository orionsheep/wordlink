**punk** *n*
**1** (*US* *informal*) a worthless person
**2** a youth movement of the late 1970s, characterized by anti-Establishment slogans, short spiky hair, and the wearing of worthless articles such as safety pins for decoration
**3** a follower of the punk movement or of punk rock
 *adj*
**4** relating to the punk youth movement of the late 1970s: a punk band
**5** (*US* *informal*) worthless or insignificant
