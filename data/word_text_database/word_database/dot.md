**dot** *n*
**1** a small round [[mark]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spot]], [[point]], [[mark]], [[fleck]], [[jot]], [[speck]], [[speckle]]
**2** the small round [[mark]] used to represent the short sound in Morse code
**3** **[[on the dot]]** at exactly the arranged time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on the dot]]**: on time, promptly, precisely, exactly (*informal*), to the minute, on the button (*informal*), punctually
 *vb* **dotting**, **dotted**
**4** to [[mark]] with a dot
**5** to scatter or intersperse: there are numerous churches dotted around Rome
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spot]], [[stud]], [[fleck]], [[speckle]]
**6** **dot one's i's and cross one's t's** (*informal*) to pay meticulous attention to detail
