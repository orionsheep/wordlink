**road** *n*
**1** a [[route]], usually surfaced, used by travellers and vehicles to get from one place to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[roadway]], [[highway]], [[motorway]], [[track]], [[route]], [[path]], [[lane]], [[pathway]]
**2** a street
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[roadway]], [[highway]], [[motorway]], [[track]], [[route]], [[path]], [[lane]], [[pathway]]
**3** a [[way]] or course: on the road to recovery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[path]]
**4** **one for the road** (*informal*) a last alcoholic drink before leaving
**5** **on the road** travelling about
