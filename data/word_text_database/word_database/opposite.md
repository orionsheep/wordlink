**opposite** *adj*
**1** situated on the [[other]] or further side
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[facing]], [[other]], [[opposing]]
**2** [[facing]] or going in [[contrary]] directions: he saw another small craft heading the opposite way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[facing]], [[other]], [[opposing]]
**3** completely [[different]]: I have a [[different]], in fact, opposite view on this subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[different]], [[conflicting]], [[contrasted]], [[contrasting]], [[unlike]], [[contrary]], [[dissimilar]], [[divergent]]
**4** (*maths*) (of a side in a triangle) [[facing]] a specified angle
 *n*
**5** a person or thing that is opposite; [[antithesis]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverse]], [[contrary]], [[converse]], [[antithesis]], [[contradiction]], [[inverse]], obverse
 *prep*
**6** [[facing]]; [[across from]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[facing]], [[face to face with]], [[across from]], eyeball to eyeball with (*informal*)
 *adv*
**7** in an opposite position: fragments smashed through the windows of the house opposite
