**neither** *adj*
**1** not one nor the other (of two): neither enterprise went well
 *pron*
**2** not one nor the other (of two): neither completed the full term
 *conj*
**3**

 **B** same as **nor**
 *adv*
**4** (*not standard*) same as **either**
