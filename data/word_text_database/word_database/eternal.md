**2** unchanged by time: eternal truths

**3** seemingly unceasing: his eternal whingeing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interminable]], [[endless]], [[infinite]], [[continual]], [[immortal]], [[never-ending]], [[everlasting]]
**4** of or like God or a god: the Eternal Buddha
