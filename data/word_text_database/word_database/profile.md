**profile** *n*
**1** an [[outline]], esp. of the human face, as seen from the side
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[lines]], [[form]], [[figure]], [[silhouette]], [[contour]], [[side view]]
**2** a short biographical [[sketch]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[biography]], [[sketch]], [[vignette]], [[characterization]], [[thumbnail sketch]]
**3** an area of a social media site containing a person's name, picture, and personal information
