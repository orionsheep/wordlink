**interfere** *vb* **-fering**, **-fered**
**1** to try to influence other people's affairs where one is not involved or wanted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meddle]], [[intervene]], [[intrude]], [[butt in]], [[tamper]], [[pry]], [[encroach]], [[stick your oar in]] (*informal*)
**2** (*physics*) to produce or cause to produce interference
**3** **interfere with**
**A** to clash with or hinder: I try not to let personal feelings interfere with my work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[interfere with something or someone]]**: conflict with, check, clash, handicap, hamper, disrupt, inhibit, thwart
 **B** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *euphemistic*) to abuse sexually
 **[[interfering]]** *adj*
