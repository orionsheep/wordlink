**replace** *vb* **-placing**, **-placed**
**1** to [[take the place of]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take the place of]], [[follow]], [[succeed]], [[oust]], [[take over from]], [[supersede]], [[supplant]]
**2** to [[substitute]] a person or thing for (another): we need to replace that chair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substitute]], [[change]], [[exchange]], [[switch]], [[swap]]
**3** to put (something) back in its rightful place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put back]], [[restore]]
