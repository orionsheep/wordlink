**definitive** *adj*
**1** [[final]] and unable to be questioned or altered: a definitive verdict
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[final]], [[convincing]], [[absolute]], [[clinching]], [[decisive]], [[definite]], [[conclusive]], [[irrefutable]]
**2** most complete, or the best of its kind: the book was hailed as the definitive Dickens biography
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[authoritative]], [[greatest]], [[ultimate]], [[reliable]], [[exhaustive]], [[superlative]]
 **[[definitively]]** *adv*
