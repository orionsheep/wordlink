**inward** *adj*
**1** directed towards the middle of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incoming]], [[entering]], [[inbound]], [[ingoing]]
**2** situated within
**3** of the mind or spirit: inward meditation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[internal]], [[inner]], [[private]], [[personal]], [[inside]], [[secret]], [[hidden]], [[interior]]
**4** of one's own country or a specific country: inward investment
 *adv*
