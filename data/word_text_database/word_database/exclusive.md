**exclusive** *adj*
**1** excluding or incompatible with anything else: these two theories are mutually exclusive
**2** not shared: exclusive rights
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entire]], [[full]], [[whole]], [[complete]], [[total]], [[absolute]], [[undivided]]
**3** used or lived in by a privileged minority, esp. a [[fashionable]] clique: an exclusive skiing resort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[select]], [[fashionable]], [[stylish]], [[restricted]], posh (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[chic]], [[high-class]], [[up-market]]
**4** not including the numbers, dates, etc. mentioned
**5** **exclusive of** except for; not taking account of
**6** **exclusive to** limited to; found only in
 *n*
**7** a story reported in only one newspaper
 **[[exclusively]]** *adv* 
 **[[exclusivity]]** * or ***[[exclusiveness]]** *n*
