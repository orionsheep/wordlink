**conception** *n*
**1** a [[notion]], [[idea]], or [[plan]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idea]], [[plan]], [[design]], [[image]], [[concept]], [[notion]]
**2** the [[fertilization]] of an egg by a sperm in the Fallopian tube followed by implantation in the womb
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impregnation]], [[insemination]], [[fertilization]], [[germination]]
**3** origin or beginning: the gap between the conception of an invention and its production
