**goodbye** *interj*
**1** an expression used on [[parting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farewell]], [[see you]], [[see you later]], [[ciao]] (*Italian*), [[cheerio]], [[adieu]], [[ta-ta]], [[au revoir]] (*French*), haere ra (*NZ*)
 *n*
**2** the act of saying goodbye: he said his goodbyes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farewell]], [[parting]], [[leave-taking]]
