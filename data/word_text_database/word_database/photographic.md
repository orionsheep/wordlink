**photographic** *adj*
**1** of or like photography or a photograph
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pictorial]], [[visual]], [[graphic]], [[cinematic]], [[filmic]]
**2** (of a person's memory) able to retain facts or appearances in [[precise]] detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accurate]], [[exact]], [[precise]], [[faithful]], [[retentive]]
 **[[photographically]]** *adv*
