**contrast** *n*
**1** a [[difference]] which is clearly seen when two things are compared
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difference]], [[opposition]], [[comparison]], [[distinction]], [[foil]], [[disparity]], [[divergence]], [[dissimilarity]]
**2** a person or thing showing differences when compared with another
**3** the degree of [[difference]] between the colours in a photograph or television picture
 *vb*
**4** to [[compare]] or be compared in order to show the differences between (things): he contrasts that society with contemporary America
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[differentiate]], [[compare]], [[oppose]], [[distinguish]], [[set in opposition]]
**5** **contrast with** to be very different from: her speed of reaction contrasted with her husband's vagueness
 **[[contrasting]]** *adj*
