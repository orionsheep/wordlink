**iron** *n*
**1** a [[strong]] silvery-white metallic element, widely used for structural and engineering purposes. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**Fe**
**2** a tool made of iron
**3** a small electrically heated device with a weighted flat bottom for pressing clothes
**4** (*golf*) a club with an angled metal head
**5** a splintlike support for a malformed leg
**6** great strength or resolve: a will of iron
**7** **strike while the iron is hot** to act at a suitable moment
 *adj*
**8** made of iron
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ferrous]], [[ferric]]
**9** very [[hard]] or merciless: iron determination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inflexible]], [[hard]], [[strong]], [[tough]], [[rigid]], [[adamant]], [[unconditional]], [[steely]]
**10** very [[strong]]: an iron constitution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inflexible]], [[hard]], [[strong]], [[tough]], [[rigid]], [[adamant]], [[unconditional]], [[steely]]
 *vb*
**11** to smooth (clothes or fabric) by removing (creases) with an iron
