**take over** *vb*
**1** to gain control or management of
**2** to become responsible for (a job) after another person has stopped doing it: I'll take over the driving if you want a break
**3** **take over from** to become more successful or important than (something), and eventually replace it: cars gradually took over from horses
 *n* **takeover**
**4** the act of gaining control of a company by buying its shares
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merger]], [[coup]], [[incorporation]]
**5** the act of seizing and taking control of something: the rebel takeover in Ethiopia
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merger]], [[coup]], [[incorporation]]
