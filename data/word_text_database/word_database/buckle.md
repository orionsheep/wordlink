**buckle** *n*
**1** a [[clasp]] for fastening together two loose ends, esp. of a belt or strap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fastener]], [[catch]], [[clip]], [[clasp]], hasp
 *vb* **-ling**, **-led**
**2** to [[fasten]] or be fastened with a buckle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fasten]], [[close]], [[secure]], [[hook]], [[clasp]]
**3** to [[bend]] or cause to [[bend]] out of shape, esp. as a result of pressure or heat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distort]], [[bend]], [[warp]], [[crumple]], [[contort]]
