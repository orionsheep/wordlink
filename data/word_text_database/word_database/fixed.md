**fixed** *adj*
**1** attached or placed so as to be [[immovable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immovable]], [[set]], [[established]], [[secure]], [[rooted]], [[permanent]], [[rigid]]
**2** stable: fixed rates
**3** unchanging and appearing artificial: a fixed smile
**4** [[established]] as to relative position: a fixed point
**5** always at the same time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreed]], [[set]], [[planned]], [[decided]], [[established]], [[settled]], [[arranged]], [[resolved]]
**6** (of ideas) firmly maintained
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inflexible]], [[set]], [[steady]], [[resolute]], [[unwavering]]
**7** (*informal*) equipped or provided for, esp. with money or possessions
**8** (*informal*) illegally [[arranged]]: a fixed trial
 **[[fixedly]]** (**fix**-id-lee) *adv*
