**society** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** human beings considered as a [[group]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the community]], [[people]], [[the public]], [[humanity]], [[civilization]], [[humankind]], [[mankind]]
**2** a [[group]] of [[people]] forming a single [[community]] with its own distinctive [[culture]] and institutions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[culture]], [[community]], [[population]]
**3** the structure, [[culture]], and institutions of such a [[group]]
**4** an organized [[group]] of [[people]] sharing a common aim or interest: a dramatic society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[organization]], [[group]], [[club]], [[union]], [[league]], [[association]], [[institute]], [[circle]]
**5** the rich and fashionable class of society collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upper classes]], [[gentry]], [[elite]], [[high society]], [[beau monde]]
**6** (*old-fashioned*) [[companionship]]: I enjoy her society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[companionship]], [[company]], [[fellowship]], [[friendship]]
