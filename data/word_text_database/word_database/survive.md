**survive** *vb* **-viving**, **-vived**
**1**
**A** to continue to live or exist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remain alive]], [[last]], [[live on]], endure
 **B** to continue to live or exist after (a passage of time or a difficult or dangerous experience)
**2** to live after the death of (another)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[live longer than]], [[outlive]], [[outlast]]
 **[[survivor]]** *n*
