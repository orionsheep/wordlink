**frantic** *adj*
**1** [[distracted]] with fear, pain, joy, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frenzied]], [[wild]], [[furious]], [[distracted]], [[distraught]], [[berserk]], [[at the end of your tether]], [[beside yourself]], [[berko]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** hurried and disorganized: frantic activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hectic]], [[desperate]], [[frenzied]], [[fraught]] (*informal*), [[frenetic]]
 **[[frantically]]** *adv*
