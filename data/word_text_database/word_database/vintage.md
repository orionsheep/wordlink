**vintage** *n*
**1** the wine obtained from a particular [[harvest]] of grapes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harvest]]
**2** the [[harvest]] from which such a wine is obtained
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harvest]]
**3** a time of origin: an open-necked shirt of uncertain vintage
 *adj*
**4** (of wine) of an outstandingly good year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[high-quality]], [[best]], [[prime]], [[quality]], [[choice]], [[select]], [[superior]]
**5** representative of the [[best]] and most typical: a vintage Saint Laurent dress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[classic]], [[old]], [[veteran]], [[historic]], [[heritage]], [[enduring]], [[antique]], [[timeless]]
