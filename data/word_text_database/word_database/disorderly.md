◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untidy]], [[confused]], [[chaotic]], [[messy]], [[jumbled]], [[shambolic]] (*informal*), [[disorganized]], [[higgledy-piggledy]] (*informal*)
**2** uncontrolled; [[unruly]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unruly]], [[disruptive]], [[rowdy]], [[turbulent]], [[tumultuous]], [[lawless]], [[riotous]], [[ungovernable]]
**3** (*law*) violating public peace
