**rumour** * or * (*US*) **rumor** *n*
**1** information, often a mixture of truth and untruth, told by one person to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[story]], [[news]], [[report]], [[talk]], [[word]], [[whisper]] (*informal*), [[buzz]], [[gossip]], [[goss]] (*informal*)
**2** [[gossip]] or common [[talk]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[story]], [[news]], [[report]], [[talk]], [[word]], [[whisper]] (*informal*), [[buzz]], [[gossip]], [[goss]] (*informal*)
 *vb*
**3** **be rumoured** to be circulated as a rumour: he is rumoured to have at least 53 yachts
