**alienate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to cause (a friend) to become unfriendly or hostile
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[antagonize]], [[anger]], [[annoy]], [[offend]], [[irritate]], [[hassle]] (*informal*), [[estrange]], [[hack off]] (*informal*)
**2** to cause (someone) to feel that he or she does not belong somewhere
**3** (*law*) to transfer the ownership of property to another person
 **[[alienation]]** *n*
