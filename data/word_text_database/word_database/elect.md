**elect** *vb*
**1** to [[choose]] (someone) to fill a position by voting for him or her: she was elected President in 1990
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vote for]], [[choose]], [[pick]], [[determine]], [[select]], [[appoint]], [[opt for]], [[settle on]]
**2** to [[choose]] or [[decide]]: those who elected to stay
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[choose]], [[decide]], [[prefer]], [[select]], opt
 *adj*
**3** voted into office but not yet having taken over from the current office-bearer: the President elect
 *pl n*
**4** **the elect** any group of people specially chosen for some privilege
 **[[electable]]** *adj*
