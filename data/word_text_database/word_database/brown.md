**brown** *adj*
**1** of the colour of wood or the earth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brunette]], [[bay]], [[coffee]], [[chocolate]], [[chestnut]], [[hazel]], [[dun]], [[auburn]]
**2** (of bread) made from wheatmeal or wholemeal flour
**3** deeply [[tanned]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tanned]], [[bronze]], [[tan]], sunburnt
 *n*
**4** the colour of wood or the earth
**5** anything brown, such as brown paint or brown clothing: clad in brown
 *vb*
**6** to make or become brown or browner, for example as a result of cooking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fry]], [[cook]], [[grill]], [[sear]], [[sauté]]
 **[[brownish]]** *adj*
