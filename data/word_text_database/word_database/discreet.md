**discreet** *adj*
**1** [[careful]] to avoid embarrassment when dealing with secret or private matters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tactful]], [[diplomatic]], [[guarded]], [[careful]], [[cautious]], [[wary]], [[prudent]], [[considerate]]
**2** unobtrusive: there was a discreet entrance down a side alley
 **[[discreetly]]** *adv*
