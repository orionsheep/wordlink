**doctor** *n*
**1** a person licensed to practise medicine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physician]], [[medic]] (*informal*), [[general practitioner]], [[medical practitioner]], G.P.
**2** a person who has been awarded a doctorate
**3** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) a person licensed to practise dentistry or veterinary medicine
 *vb*
**4** to [[change]] in order to deceive: she confessed to having doctored the figures
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[alter]], [[interfere with]], [[disguise]], [[pervert]], [[tamper with]], [[tinker with]], [[misrepresent]]
**5** to poison or drug (food or drink)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[add to]], [[spike]], [[cut]], [[mix something with something]], [[dilute]], [[water down]], [[adulterate]]
**6** to castrate (an animal)
 **[[doctoral]]** *adj*
