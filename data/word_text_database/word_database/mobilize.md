**mobilize** * or ***-lise** *vb* **-lizing**, **-lized*** or ***-lising**, **-lised**
**1** to [[prepare]] for war or another emergency by organizing resources and the armed services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deploy]], [[prepare]], [[ready]], [[rally]], [[assemble]], [[call up]], [[marshal]], [[muster]]
**2** to [[organize]] for a purpose: we must mobilize local residents behind our campaign
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rally]], [[organize]], [[stimulate]], [[excite]], [[prompt]], [[marshal]], [[activate]], [[awaken]]
 **[[mobilization]]** * or ***-lisation** *n*
