**niche** (**neesh**) *n*
**1** a [[recess]] in a wall for a statue or ornament
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recess]], [[opening]], [[corner]], [[hollow]], [[nook]], [[alcove]]
**2** a [[position]] exactly suitable for the person occupying it: perhaps I will find my niche in a desk job
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[calling]], [[place]], [[slot]] (*informal*), [[vocation]], pigeonhole (*informal*)
 *adj*
**3** of or aimed at a specialist group or market: niche retailing ventures
