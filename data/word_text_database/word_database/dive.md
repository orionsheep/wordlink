**dive** *vb* **diving**, **dived** (*US*) **dove**, **dived**
**1** to [[plunge]] headfirst into water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plunge]], [[drop]], [[duck]], [[dip]], [[descend]], [[plummet]]
**2** (of a submarine or diver) to submerge under water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go underwater]]
**3** (of a bird or aircraft) to fly in a steep nose-down descending path
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nose-dive]], [[plunge]], [[crash]], [[swoop]], [[plummet]]
**4** to move quickly in a specified direction: he dived for the door
**5** **dive in *or* into**
**A** to put (one's hand) quickly or forcefully into
 **B** to start doing (something) enthusiastically
 *n*
**6** a headlong [[plunge]] into water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plunge]], [[spring]], [[jump]], [[leap]], [[lunge]], [[nose dive]]
**7** the act of diving
**8** a steep nose-down descent of a bird or aircraft
**9** (*slang*) a disreputable bar or club
