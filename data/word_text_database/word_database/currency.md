**currency** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** the system of [[money]] or the actual [[coins]] and banknotes in use in a particular country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[money]], [[coinage]], [[legal tender]], [[notes]], [[coins]]
**2** general [[acceptance]] or use: ideas that had gained currency during the early 1960s
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acceptance]], [[popularity]], [[circulation]], [[vogue]], [[prevalence]]
