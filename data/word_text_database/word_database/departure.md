**departure** *n*
**1** the act of departing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leaving]], [[going]], [[retirement]], [[withdrawal]], [[exit]], [[going away]], [[removal]], [[exodus]]
**2** a [[divergence]] from previous custom, rule, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shift]], [[change]], [[difference]], [[variation]], [[innovation]], [[novelty]], [[deviation]], [[divergence]]
**3** a course of action or venture: the album represents a new departure for them
