**pagan** *adj*
**1** having, being, or relating to religious beliefs, esp. ancient ones, which are not part of any of the world's major religions: this was the site of a pagan temple to the sun
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heathen]], [[infidel]], [[polytheistic]], [[idolatrous]]
**2** irreligious
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heathen]], [[infidel]], [[polytheistic]], [[idolatrous]]
 *n*
**3** a person who does not belong to any of the world's major religions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heathen]] (*old-fashioned*), [[infidel]], [[polytheist]], [[idolater]]
**4** a person without any religion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heathen]] (*old-fashioned*), [[infidel]], [[polytheist]], [[idolater]]
 **[[paganism]]** *n*
