**unload** *vb*
**1** to remove cargo from a ship, lorry, or plane
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empty]], [[clear]], [[unpack]], [[dump]], [[discharge]]
**2** to express worries or problems by telling someone about them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unburden]]
**3** to remove the ammunition from a gun
