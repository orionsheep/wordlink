**pillar** *n*
**1** an [[upright]] [[support]] of stone, brick, or metal; [[column]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[post]], [[column]], [[prop]], [[shaft]], [[upright]], [[pier]], [[stanchion]]
**2** something resembling this: a pillar of smoke
**3** a prominent [[supporter]] or member: a pillar of society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[leader]], [[mainstay]], [[leading light]] (*informal*), [[upholder]]
**4** **from pillar to [[post]]** from one place to another
