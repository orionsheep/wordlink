**tour** *n*
**1** an extended [[journey]] visiting places of interest along the route
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[journey]], [[expedition]], [[excursion]], [[trip]], [[outing]], [[jaunt]], [[junket]]
**2** a [[trip]], by a band, theatre company, etc., to perform in several places
**3** an overseas [[trip]] made by a cricket team, rugby team, etc., to play in several places
**4** (*military*) a period of service, esp. in one place: the regiment has served several tours in Northern Ireland
 *vb*
**5** to make a tour of (a place)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[visit]], [[explore]], [[go round]], [[inspect]], [[walk round]], [[drive round]], [[sightsee]]
