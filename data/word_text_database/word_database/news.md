**news** *n*
**1** important or interesting new happenings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[information]], [[latest]] (*informal*), [[report]], [[story]], [[exposé]], [[intelligence]], [[rumour]], [[revelation]], [[goss]] (*informal*)
**2** [[information]] about such events, reported in the mass media
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[information]], [[latest]] (*informal*), [[report]], [[story]], [[exposé]], [[intelligence]], [[rumour]], [[revelation]], [[goss]] (*informal*)
**3** **the news** a television or radio programme presenting such [[information]]
**4** interesting or important new [[information]]: it's news to me
**5** a person or thing widely reported in the mass media: reggae is suddenly big news again
