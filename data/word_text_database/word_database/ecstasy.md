**ecstasy** *n*, *pl* **-sies**
**1** a state of extreme [[delight]] or [[joy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rapture]], [[delight]], [[joy]], [[bliss]], [[euphoria]], [[fervour]], [[elation]]
**2** (*slang*) a strong drug that acts as a stimulant and can cause hallucinations
 **[[ecstatic]]** *adj* 
 **[[ecstatically]]** *adv*
