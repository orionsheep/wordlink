**blunder** *n*
**1** a stupid or clumsy [[mistake]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mistake]], [[slip]], [[fault]], [[error]], [[oversight]], [[gaffe]], [[slip-up]] (*informal*), [[indiscretion]], barry *or* Barry Crocker (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb*
**2** to make stupid or clumsy mistakes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make a mistake]], [[blow it]] (*slang*), [[err]], [[slip up]] (*informal*), [[foul up]], [[put your foot in it]] (*informal*)
**3** to act clumsily; [[stumble]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stumble]], [[fall]], [[reel]], [[stagger]], [[lurch]]
 **[[blundering]]** *n*, *adj*
