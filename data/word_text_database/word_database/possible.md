◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feasible]], [[viable]], [[workable]], [[achievable]], [[practicable]], [[attainable]], [[doable]], [[realizable]]
**3** having potential: a possible buyer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aspiring]], [[would-be]], [[promising]], [[hopeful]], [[prospective]], [[wannabe]] (*informal*)
