**debate** *n*
**1** a [[discussion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discussion]], [[talk]], [[argument]], [[dispute]], [[analysis]], [[conversation]], [[controversy]], [[dialogue]]
**2** a formal [[discussion]], as in a parliament, in which opposing arguments are put forward
 *vb* **-bating**, **-bated**
**3** to [[discuss]] (something) formally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discuss]], [[question]], [[talk about]], [[argue about]], [[dispute]], [[examine]], [[deliberate]]
**4** to [[consider]] (possible courses of action)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consider]], [[reflect]], [[think about]], [[weigh]], [[contemplate]], [[deliberate]], [[ponder]], [[ruminate]]
