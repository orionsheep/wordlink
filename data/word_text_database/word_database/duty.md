**duty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the [[work]] performed as part of one's [[job]]: it is his duty to supervise the memorial services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsibility]], [[job]], [[task]], [[work]], [[role]], [[function]], [[obligation]], [[assignment]]
**2** a [[obligation]] to fulfil one's responsibilities: it's my duty as a doctor to keep it confidential
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsibility]], [[job]], [[task]], [[work]], [[role]], [[function]], [[obligation]], [[assignment]]
**3** a government [[tax]] on imports
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tax]], [[toll]], [[levy]], [[tariff]], [[excise]]
**4** **on *or* off duty** at (*or* not at) [[work]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on duty]]**: at [[work]], busy, engaged, on active service
