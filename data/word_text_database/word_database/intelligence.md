**intelligence** *n*
**1** the ability to understand, learn, and think things out quickly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intellect]], [[understanding]], [[brains]] (*informal*), [[sense]], [[knowledge]], [[judgment]], [[wit]], [[perception]]
**2** the collection of secret [[information]], esp. for military purposes
**3** a group or department collecting military [[information]]
**4** (*old-fashioned*) [[news]] or [[information]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[information]], [[news]], [[facts]], [[report]], [[findings]], [[knowledge]], [[data]], [[notification]], [[heads up]]
