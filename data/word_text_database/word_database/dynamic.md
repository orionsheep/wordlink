**dynamic** *adj*
**1** (of a person) full of energy, ambition, or new ideas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[energetic]], [[powerful]], [[vital]], [[go-ahead]], [[lively]], [[animated]], [[high-powered]], [[forceful]]
**2** relating to a force of society, history, or the mind that produces a change: the government needs a more dynamic policy towards the environment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[energetic]], [[powerful]], [[vital]], [[go-ahead]], [[lively]], [[animated]], [[high-powered]], [[forceful]]
**3** (*physics*) relating to energy or forces that produce motion
 **[[dynamically]]** *adv*
