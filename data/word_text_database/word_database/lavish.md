**lavish** *adj*
**1** great in quantity or richness: lavish banquets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grand]], [[magnificent]], [[splendid]], [[abundant]], [[copious]], [[profuse]]
**2** very [[generous]] in giving
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[generous]], [[free]], [[liberal]], [[bountiful]], [[open-handed]], [[unstinting]], [[munificent]]
**3** [[extravagant]]; [[wasteful]]: lavish spending habits
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extravagant]], [[wild]], [[excessive]], [[exaggerated]], [[wasteful]], [[prodigal]], [[unrestrained]], immoderate
 *vb*
**4** to give or spend very generously or in great quantities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shower]], [[pour]], [[heap]], [[deluge]], [[dissipate]]
 **[[lavishly]]** *adv*
