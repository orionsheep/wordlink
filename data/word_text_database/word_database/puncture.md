**puncture** *n*
**1** a small [[hole]] made by a sharp object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hole]], [[opening]], [[break]], [[cut]], [[nick]], [[leak]], [[slit]]
**2** a tear and loss of pressure in a tyre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flat tyre]], [[flat]], [[flattie]] (*NZ*)
**3** the act of puncturing or perforating
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**4** to [[pierce]] a [[hole]] in (something) with a sharp object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pierce]], [[cut]], [[nick]], [[penetrate]], [[prick]], [[rupture]], [[perforate]], [[bore a hole]] (in)
**5** to cause (a tyre, etc.) to lose pressure by piercing
