**gulp** *vb*
**1** to swallow (a drink or food) rapidly in large mouthfuls
**2** to gasp or breathe in violently, for example when nervous or when swimming
**3** **gulp back** to stifle or suppress: he gulped back the tears as he said his goodbyes
 *n*
**4** the act of gulping
**5** the quantity taken in a gulp
