**prayer** *n*
**1** a thanksgiving or an [[appeal]] spoken to one's God
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[orison]], [[litany]], [[invocation]], [[intercession]]
**2** a set form of words used in praying: the Lord's Prayer
**3** an earnest [[request]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plea]], [[appeal]], [[request]], [[petition]], [[entreaty]], [[supplication]] (*formal*)
**4** the practice of praying: call the faithful to prayer
**5** (*often pl*) a form of [[devotion]] spent mainly praying: morning prayers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supplication]], [[devotion]]
**6** something prayed for
