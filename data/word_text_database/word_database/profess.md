**profess** *vb*
**1** to [[claim]] (something as true), often falsely: he professes not to want the job of Prime Minister
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[claim]], [[allege]], [[pretend]], [[fake]], [[make out]], [[purport]], [[feign]]
**2** to acknowledge openly: he [[professed]] great relief at getting some rest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state]], [[admit]], [[announce]], [[declare]], [[confess]], [[assert]], [[proclaim]], [[affirm]]
**3** to have as one's belief or religion: most Indonesians profess the Islamic faith
 **[[professed]]** *adj*
