**counsel** *n*
**1** [[advice]] or [[guidance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advice]], [[information]], [[warning]], [[direction]], [[suggestion]], [[recommendation]], [[guidance]]
**2** discussion or consultation: when it was over they took counsel of their consciences
**3** a barrister or group of barristers who conduct cases in court and [[advise]] on legal matters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legal adviser]], [[lawyer]], [[attorney]], [[solicitor]], [[advocate]], barrister
 *vb* **-selling**, **-selled** (*US*) **-seling**, **-seled**
**4** to give [[advice]] or [[guidance]] to
**5** to [[recommend]] or [[urge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advise]], [[recommend]], [[advocate]], [[warn]], [[urge]], [[instruct]], [[exhort]] (*formal*) 
 **[[counselling]]** * or * (*US*) **[[counseling]]** *n*
