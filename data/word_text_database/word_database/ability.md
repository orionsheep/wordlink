**ability** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** possession of the necessary [[skill]] or power to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[capability]], [[potential]], [[competence]], [[proficiency]]
**2** great [[skill]] or [[competence]]: his ability as a speaker was legendary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skill]], [[talent]], [[expertise]], [[competence]], [[aptitude]], [[proficiency]], [[cleverness]]
