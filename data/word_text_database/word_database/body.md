**body** *n*, *pl* **bodies**
**1** the entire physical structure of an animal or human. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**corporal**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physique]], [[build]], [[form]], [[figure]], [[shape]], [[frame]], [[constitution]]
**2** the [[trunk]] or [[torso]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[torso]], [[trunk]]
**3** a [[corpse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corpse]], [[dead body]], [[remains]], [[carcass]], [[cadaver]], [[stiff]] (*slang*)
**4** a [[group]] regarded as a single entity: a local voluntary body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[organization]], [[company]], [[group]], [[society]], [[association]], [[band]], [[congress]], [[institution]]
**5** the [[main part]] of anything: the body of a car
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[main part]], [[matter]], [[material]], [[mass]], [[substance]], [[bulk]], [[essence]]
**6** a separate [[mass]] of water or land
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expanse]], [[mass]]
**7** the flesh as opposed to the spirit
**8** fullness in the appearance of the hair
**9** the characteristic full quality of certain wines
**10** (*informal*) a person: all the important bodies from the council were present
**11** a woman's one-piece undergarment
**12** **keep body and soul together** to manage to survive
