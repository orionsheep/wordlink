**guest** *n*
**1** a person who receives hospitality at someone else's home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[visitor]], [[company]], [[caller]], manu(w)hiri (*NZ*)
**2** a person who is taken out socially by someone else who pays all the expenses
**3** a performer or speaker taking part in an event, show, or programme by special invitation
**4** a person who is staying in a hotel
 *vb*
**5** to be a guest in an event, show, or programme: she guested in concert with Kylie Minogue
