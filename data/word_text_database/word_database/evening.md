**evening** *n*
**1** the latter part of the day, esp. from late afternoon until nightfall
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dusk]] (*archaic*), [[night]], [[sunset]], [[twilight]], [[sundown]], [[gloaming]] (*Scot* *poetic*), [[close of day]], evo (*Austral* *slang*)
 *adj*
**2** of or in the evening: the evening meal
