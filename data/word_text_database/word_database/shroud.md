**shroud** *n*
**1** a piece of cloth used to wrap a dead body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[winding sheet]], [[grave clothes]]
**2** anything that hides things: a shroud of smoke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covering]], [[veil]], [[mantle]], [[screen]], pall
 *vb*
**3** to [[hide]] or obscure (something): shrouded in uncertainty; shrouded by smog
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conceal]], [[cover]], [[screen]], [[hide]], [[blanket]], [[veil]], [[cloak]], [[envelop]]
