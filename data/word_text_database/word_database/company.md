**company** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a [[business]] organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[business]], [[firm]], [[association]], [[corporation]], [[partnership]], [[establishment]], [[syndicate]], [[house]]
**2** a [[group]] of actors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[set]], [[community]], [[band]], [[crowd]], [[collection]], [[gathering]], [[assembly]]
**3** a small [[unit]] of troops
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[troop]], [[unit]], [[squad]], [[team]]
**4** the officers and crew of a ship
**5** the fact of being with someone: I enjoy her company
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[companionship]], [[society]] (*old-fashioned*), [[presence]], [[fellowship]]
**6** a number of people gathered together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[set]], [[community]], [[band]], [[crowd]], [[collection]], [[gathering]], [[assembly]]
**7** a guest or [[guests]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[guests]], [[party]], [[visitors]], [[callers]]
**8** a person's associates
**9** **keep someone company** to accompany someone
**10** **part company** to disagree or separate
