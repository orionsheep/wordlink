**add** *vb*
**1** to combine (numbers or quantities) so as to make a larger number or quantity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[count up]], [[total]], [[reckon]], [[compute]], [[add up]], [[tot up]]
**2** to join something to something else so as to increase its size, effect, or scope: these new rules will add an extra burden on already overworked officials
**3** to say or write something further
**4** **add in** to include
