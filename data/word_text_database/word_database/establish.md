**establish** *vb*
**1** to [[create]] or [[set up]] (an organization, link, etc.): the regime wants to establish better relations with neighbouring countries
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[set up]], [[found]], [[create]], [[institute]], [[constitute]], [[inaugurate]]
**2** to become firmly associated with a particular activity or reputation: the play that established him as a major dramatist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secure]], [[form]], [[ground]], [[settle]]
**3** to [[prove]]: a test to establish if your baby has any chromosomal disorder
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prove]], [[confirm]], [[demonstrate]], [[certify]], [[verify]], [[substantiate]], [[corroborate]], [[authenticate]]
**4** to cause (a principle) to be accepted: our study establishes the case for further research
