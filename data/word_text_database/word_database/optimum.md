**optimum** *n*, *pl* **-ma*** or ***-mums**
**1** the most favourable conditions or [[best]] compromise possible
 *adj*
**2** most favourable or advantageous; [[best]]: balance is a critical part of an optimum diet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ideal]], [[best]], [[highest]], [[finest]], [[perfect]], [[supreme]], [[peak]], [[outstanding]]
