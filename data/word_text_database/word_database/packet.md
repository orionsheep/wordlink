**packet** *n*
**1** a [[container]], together with its contents: a packet of crisps
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[container]], [[box]], [[package]], [[carton]]
**2** a small [[parcel]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[package]], [[parcel]]
**3** Also: **packet boat** a boat that transports mail, passengers, or goods on a fixed short route
**4** (*slang*) a large sum of money: she was paid a packet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[a fortune]], [[a bomb]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[a pile]] (*informal*), [[a small fortune]], [[a tidy sum]] (*informal*), [[a king's ransom]] (*informal*), [[top whack]] (*informal*)
**5** (*computers*) a unit into which a larger piece of data is broken down for more efficient transmission
