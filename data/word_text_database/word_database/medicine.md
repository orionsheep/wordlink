**medicine** *n*
**1** any substance used in treating or alleviating the symptoms of disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remedy]], [[drug]], [[cure]], [[prescription]], [[medication]], [[nostrum]], [[medicament]]
**2** the science of preventing, diagnosing, or curing disease
**3** any nonsurgical branch of medical science
**4** **take one's medicine** to accept a deserved punishment
