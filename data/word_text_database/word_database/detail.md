**detail** *n*
**1** an item that is considered separately
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[fact]], [[feature]], [[particular]], [[respect]], [[factor]], [[element]], [[aspect]]
**2** an item considered to be unimportant: a mere detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fine point]], [[particular]], [[nicety]], [[triviality]]
**3** treatment of individual parts: the census provides a considerable amount of detail
**4** a small section of a work of art often enlarged to make the smaller features more distinct
**5** (*chiefly* *military*)
**A** personnel assigned a specific [[duty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[party]], [[force]], [[body]], [[duty]], [[squad]], [[assignment]], [[fatigue]], detachment
 **B** the [[duty]]
**6** **in detail** including all the important particulars
 *vb*
**7** to [[list]] fully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[relate]], [[catalogue]], [[recount]], [[rehearse]], [[recite]], [[enumerate]], [[itemize]]
**8** (*chiefly* *military*) to select (personnel) for a specific [[duty]]
