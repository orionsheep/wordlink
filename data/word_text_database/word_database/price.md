**price** *n*
**1** the [[amount]] of money for which a thing is bought or sold
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cost]], [[value]], [[rate]], [[charge]], [[figure]], [[worth]], [[damage]] (*informal*), [[amount]]
**2** the [[cost]] at which something is obtained: the price of making the wrong decision
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consequences]], [[penalty]], [[cost]], [[result]], [[toll]], [[forfeit]]
**3** (*gambling*) odds
**4** **at any price** whatever the price or [[cost]]
**5** **at a price** at a high price
**6** **what price (something)?** what are the chances of (something) happening now?
 *vb* **pricing**, **priced**
**7** to fix the price of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evaluate]], [[value]], [[estimate]], [[rate]], [[cost]], [[assess]]
**8** to discover the price of
