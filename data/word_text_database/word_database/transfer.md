**transfer** *vb* **-ferring**, **-ferred**
**1** to [[change]] or [[move]] from one thing, person, place, etc., to another: he was transferred from prison to hospital
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[move]], [[transport]], [[shift]], [[relocate]], [[transpose]], [[change]], [[download]], [[upload]]
**2** to [[move]] (money or property) from the control of one person or organization to that of another: the money has been transferred into your account
**3** (of a football club) to sell or release (a player) to another club: he was transferred to Juventus for a world record fee
**4** to [[move]] (a drawing or design) from one surface to another
 *n*
**5** the act, process, or system of transferring, or the state of being transferred
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transference]], [[move]], [[handover]], [[change]], [[shift]], [[transmission]], [[translation]], [[relocation]]
**6** a person or thing that transfers or is transferred
**7** a design or drawing that is transferred from one surface to another
**8** the moving of (money or property) from the control of one person or organization to that of another
 **[[transferable]]** * or ***[[transferrable]]** *adj* 
 **[[transference]]** *n*
