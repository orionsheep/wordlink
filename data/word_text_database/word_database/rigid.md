**rigid** *adj*
**1** [[inflexible]] or strict: the talks will be general, without a rigid agenda
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inflexible]], [[uncompromising]], [[unbending]]
**2** physically unyielding or [[stiff]]: use only rigid plastic containers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stiff]], [[inflexible]], [[inelastic]]
 **[[rigidity]]** *n* 
 **[[rigidly]]** *adv*
