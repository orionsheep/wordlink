**traffic** *n*
**1** the [[vehicles]] travelling on roads
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transport]], [[vehicles]], [[transportation]], [[freight]]
**2** the movement of [[vehicles]] or people in a particular place or for a particular purpose: air traffic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transport]], [[vehicles]], [[transportation]], [[freight]]
**3** [[trade]], esp. of an illicit kind: drug traffic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trade]], [[commerce]], [[business]], [[exchange]], [[truck]], [[dealings]], [[peddling]]
**4** the [[exchange]] of ideas between people or organizations: a lively traffic in ideas
 *vb* **-ficking**, **-ficked**
**5** to carry on [[trade]] or [[business]], esp. of an illicit kind: he confessed to trafficking in gold and ivory
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trade]], [[deal]], [[exchange]], [[bargain]], [[do business]], [[peddle]], [[cut a deal]], [[have dealings]]
 **[[trafficker]]** *n*
