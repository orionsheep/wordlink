**sample** *n*
**1** a small part of anything, taken as being representative of a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[specimen]], [[example]], [[model]], [[pattern]], instance
 *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**2** to take a sample or samples of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[test]], [[try]], [[experience]], [[taste]], [[inspect]]
**3** (*music*)
**A** to take a short extract from (one record) and mix it into a different backing track
 **B** to record (a sound) and feed it into a computerized synthesizer so that it can be reproduced at any pitch
 **[[sampling]]** *n*
