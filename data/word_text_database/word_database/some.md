**some** *adj*
**1** unknown or unspecified: some man called for you
**2** an unknown or unspecified quantity or number of: I've got some money
**3**
**A** a considerable number or amount of: he lived some years afterwards
 **B** a little: show some respect
**4** (*informal*) an impressive or remarkable: that was some game!
 *pron*
**5** certain unknown or unspecified people or things: some can teach and others can't
**6** an unknown or unspecified quantity of something or number of people or things: he will sell some in his pub
 *adv*
**7** approximately: some thirty pounds
