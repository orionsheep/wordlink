**very** *adv*
**1** used to add emphasis to adjectives and adverbs that are able to be graded: I'm very happy; he'll be home very soon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extremely]], [[highly]], [[greatly]], [[really]], [[deeply]], [[unusually]], [[profoundly]], decidedly
 *adj*
**2** used with nouns to give emphasis or exaggerated intensity: the very end of his visit; the very thing I need
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exact]], [[precise]], [[selfsame]]
