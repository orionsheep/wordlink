**prepare** *vb* **-paring**, **-pared**
**1** to [[make or get ready]]: the army prepared for battle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]] *or* [[get]] ready, [[arrange]], [[jack up]] (*NZ* *informal*)
**2** to [[put together]] using parts or ingredients: he had spent most of the afternoon preparing the meal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[cook]], [[put together]], [[get]], [[produce]], [[assemble]], [[muster]], [[concoct]]
**3** to equip or outfit, as for an expedition
**4** **be prepared to** to be willing and able to: I'm not prepared to say
