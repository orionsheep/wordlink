**demonstration** *n*
**1** a [[march]] or public meeting to demonstrate opposition to something or support for something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[march]], [[protest]], [[rally]], [[sit-in]], [[parade]], [[picket]], [[mass lobby]], [[hikoi]] (*NZ*)
**2** an [[explanation]], [[display]], or experiment showing how something works
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[show]], [[performance]], [[explanation]], [[description]], [[presentation]], [[exposition]]
**3** [[proof]] or evidence leading to [[proof]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indication]], [[proof]], [[testimony]], [[confirmation]], [[substantiation]]
