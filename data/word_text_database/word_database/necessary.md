**necessary** *adj*
**1** [[needed]] in order to obtain the desired result: the necessary skills
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[needed]], [[required]], [[essential]], [[vital]], [[compulsory]], [[mandatory]], [[imperative]], [[indispensable]]
**2** [[certain]] or [[unavoidable]]: the necessary consequences
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inevitable]], [[certain]], [[unavoidable]], inescapable
 *n*
**3** **do the necessary** (*informal*) to do something that is necessary in a particular situation
**4** **the necessary** (*informal*) the money [[required]] for a particular purpose
