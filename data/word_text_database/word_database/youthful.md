**youthful** *adj*
**1** vigorous or active: the intermediate section was won by a youthful grandmother
**2** of, relating to, possessing, or associated with youth: youthful good looks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[young]], [[juvenile]], [[childish]], [[immature]], [[boyish]], [[girlish]]
 **[[youthfully]]** *adv* 
 **[[youthfulness]]** *n*
