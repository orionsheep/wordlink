**keep up** *vb*
**1** to maintain at the present level
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[keep something up]]**: maintain, sustain, perpetuate, retain, preserve, prolong
**2** to maintain in good condition
**3** **keep up with**
**A** to maintain a pace set by (someone)
 **B** to remain informed about: he liked to think he kept up with current musical trends
 **C** to remain in contact with (someone)
**4** **keep up with the Joneses** (*informal*) to compete with one's friends or neighbours in material possessions
