**impose** *vb* **-posing**, **-posed**
**1** to establish (a rule, condition, etc.) as something to be obeyed or complied with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[impose something on someone]]**: levy, introduce, charge, establish, fix, institute, decree, ordain (*formal*)
**2** to force (oneself) on others
**3** (*printing*) to arrange (pages) in the correct order for printing
**4** to pass off (something) deceptively on someone
**5** **impose on** to take advantage of (a person or quality): she imposed on his kindness
