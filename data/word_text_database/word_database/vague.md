**vague** *adj*
**1** not expressed or explained clearly: he thought of his instructions, so vague and [[imprecise]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unclear]], [[indefinite]], [[hazy]], [[confused]], [[loose]], [[uncertain]], [[unsure]], [[superficial]]
**2** deliberately withholding information: she was rather vague about the whole deal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imprecise]], [[unspecified]], [[generalized]], [[rough]], [[loose]], [[ambiguous]], [[hazy]], [[equivocal]]
**3** (of a sound or shape) unable to be heard or seen clearly: he heard some vague sound from downstairs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indistinct]], [[unclear]], [[faint]], [[hazy]], [[indeterminate]], [[nebulous]], [[ill-defined]]
**4** (of a person) not concentrating or thinking clearly: she was mumbling to herself in a vague way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[absent-minded]], [[distracted]], [[vacant]], [[preoccupied]], [[oblivious]], [[inattentive]]
**5** not clearly established or known: it was a vague rumour which would fade away and be forgotten
 **[[vaguely]]** *adv* 
 **[[vagueness]]** *n*
