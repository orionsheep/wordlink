**moratorium** *n*, *pl* **-ria*** or ***-riums**
**1** a legally authorized [[postponement]] of the payment of a debt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[postponement]], [[freeze]], [[halt]], [[suspension]], [[standstill]]
**2** an agreed [[suspension]] of activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[postponement]], [[freeze]], [[halt]], [[suspension]], [[standstill]]
