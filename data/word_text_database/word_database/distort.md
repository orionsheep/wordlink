**distort** *vb*
**1** to alter or [[misrepresent]] (facts)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misrepresent]], [[twist]], [[bias]], [[disguise]], [[pervert]], [[slant]], [[colour]], [[misinterpret]]
**2** to [[twist]] out of shape; [[deform]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deform]], [[bend]], [[twist]], [[warp]], [[buckle]], [[mangle]], [[mangulate]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[disfigure]], [[contort]]
**3** (*electronics*) to reproduce or amplify (a signal) inaccurately
 **[[distorted]]** *adj* 
 **[[distortion]]** *n*
