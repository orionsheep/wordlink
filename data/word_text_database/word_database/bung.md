**bung** *n*
**1** a stopper, esp. of cork or rubber, used to close something such as a cask or flask
 *vb*
**2** (foll. by *up*) (*informal*) to close or seal (something) with or as if with a bung
**3** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *slang*) to throw (something) somewhere in a careless manner; sling
