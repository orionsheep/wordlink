**rusty** *adj* **rustier**, **rustiest**
**1** affected by rust: a rusty old freighter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corroded]], [[rusted]], [[oxidized]], [[rust-covered]]
**2** [[reddish-brown]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reddish-brown]], [[chestnut]], [[reddish]], [[russet]], [[coppery]], [[rust-coloured]]
**3** [[out of practice]] in a skill or subject: your skills may be a little rusty, but your past experience will more than make up for that
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[out of practice]], [[weak]], [[stale]], [[unpractised]]
 **[[rustily]]** *adv* 
 **[[rustiness]]** *n*
