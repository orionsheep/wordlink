**[[break out]]** *vb*
**1** to begin or arise suddenly: fighting broke out between the two factions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[break out]]**: begin, start, happen, occur, arise, set in, commence, spring up
**2** to make an escape, esp. from prison
**3** **[[break out]] in** to erupt in (a rash or spots)
 *n* **break-out**
**4** an escape, esp. from prison
