**truth** *n*
**1** the quality of being true, genuine, or factual: there is no truth in the allegations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[truthfulness]], [[fact]], [[accuracy]], [[precision]], [[validity]], [[legitimacy]], [[veracity]], [[genuineness]]
**2** something that is true: he finally learned the truth about his parents' marriage
**3** a proven or verified [[fact]], principle, etc.: some profound truths about biology have come to light
