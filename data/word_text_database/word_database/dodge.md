**dodge** *vb* **dodging**, **dodged**
**1** to [[avoid]] being hit, caught, or seen by moving suddenly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[duck]] (*informal*), [[dart]], [[swerve]], [[sidestep]], [[shoot]], [[turn aside]]
**2** to [[evade]] by cleverness or trickery: the Government will not be able to dodge the issue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evade]], [[avoid]], [[escape]], [[get away from]], elude
 *n*
**3** a cunning and deceitful [[trick]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trick]], [[scheme]], [[ploy]], [[trap]], [[device]], [[fraud]], [[manoeuvre]], [[deception]], [[fastie]] (*Austral* *slang*)
