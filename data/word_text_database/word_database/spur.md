**spur** *n*
**1** an [[incentive]] to get something done
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stimulus]], [[incentive]], [[impetus]], [[motive]], [[impulse]], [[inducement]], [[incitement]]
**2** a sharp spiked wheel on the heel of a rider's boot used to [[urge]] the horse on
**3** a sharp horny part sticking out from a cock's leg
**4** a ridge sticking out from a mountain side
**5** **[[on the spur of the moment]]** suddenly and without planning; on [[impulse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on the spur of the moment]]**: on [[impulse]], impulsively, on the spot, impromptu, without planning
**6** **win one's spurs** to prove one's ability
 *vb* **spurring**, **spurred**
**7** (often foll. by *on*) to encourage (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incite]], [[drive]], [[prompt]], [[urge]], [[stimulate]], [[animate]], [[prod]], [[prick]]
