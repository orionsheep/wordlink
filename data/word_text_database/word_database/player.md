**player** *n*
**1** a person who takes part in a game or sport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: sportsman *or* sportswoman, [[competitor]], [[participant]], [[contestant]]
**2** a person who plays a musical instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[musician]], [[artist]], [[performer]], [[virtuoso]], [[instrumentalist]]
**3** (*informal*) a leading [[participant]] in a particular field or activity: one of the key players in Chinese politics
**4** an actor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[performer]], [[entertainer]], [[Thespian]], [[trouper]], actor *or* actress
