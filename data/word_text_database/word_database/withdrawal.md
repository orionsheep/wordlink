**withdrawal** *n*
**1** the act or an instance of withdrawing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[removal]], [[ending]], [[stopping]], [[taking away]], [[abolition]], [[elimination]], [[cancellation]], [[termination]]
**2** the period that a drug addict goes through after [[stopping]] using drugs, during which he or she may experience symptoms such as tremors, sweating, and vomiting
 *adj*
**3** of or relating to withdrawal from an addictive drug: withdrawal symptoms
