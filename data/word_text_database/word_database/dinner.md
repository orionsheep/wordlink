**dinner** *n*
**1** the [[main meal]] of the day, eaten either in the evening or at midday
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meal]], [[main meal]], [[spread]] (*informal*), [[repast]]
**2** a formal social occasion at which an evening [[meal]] is served
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[banquet]], [[feast]], [[repast]], [[hakari]] (*NZ*)
