**manual** *adj*
**1** of a [[hand]] or hands: manual dexterity
**2** [[physical]] as opposed to mental: manual labour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physical]], [[human]]
**3** operated or done by [[human]] labour rather than automatic or computer-aided means: a manual gearbox
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hand-operated]], [[hand]], non-automatic
 *n*
**4** a book of [[instructions]] or information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handbook]], [[guide]], [[instructions]], [[bible]]
**5** (*music*) one of the keyboards on an organ
 **[[manually]]** *adv*
