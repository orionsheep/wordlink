**destiny** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** the future destined for a person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fate]], [[fortune]], [[lot]], [[portion]], [[doom]], [[nemesis]]
**2** the predetermined course of events
**3** the power that predetermines the course of events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fortune]], [[chance]], [[karma]], [[providence]], [[kismet]], [[predestination]], [[divine will]]
