**influence** *n*
**1** an effect of one person or thing on another
**2** the [[power]] of a person or thing to have such an effect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[power]], [[authority]], [[pull]] (*informal*), [[importance]], [[prestige]], [[clout]] (*informal*), [[leverage]]
**3** [[power]] resulting from ability, wealth, or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[power]], [[authority]], [[direction]], [[command]], [[domination]], [[supremacy]], [[mastery]], [[mana]] (*NZ*)
**4** a person or thing with influence
**5** **under the influence** (*informal*) drunk
 *vb* **-encing**, **-enced**
**6** to have an effect upon (actions or events)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affect]], [[have an effect on]], [[have an impact on]], [[control]], [[concern]], [[direct]], [[guide]], [[bear upon]]
**7** to [[persuade]] or [[induce]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persuade]], [[prompt]], [[urge]], [[induce]], [[entice]], [[coax]], [[incite]], [[instigate]]
 **[[influencer]]** *n*
