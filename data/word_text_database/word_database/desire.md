**desire** *vb* **-siring**, **-sired**
**1** to [[want]] very much
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[want]], [[long for]], [[crave]], [[hope for]], [[ache for]], [[wish for]], [[yearn for]], [[thirst for]]
**2** (*formal*) to request: we desire your company at the wedding of our daughter
 *n*
**3** a [[wish]] or [[longing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wish]], [[want]], [[longing]], [[hope]], [[urge]], [[aspiration]], [[craving]], [[thirst]]
**4** sexual [[appetite]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lust]], [[passion]], [[libido]], [[appetite]], [[lasciviousness]]
**5** a person or thing that is desired
