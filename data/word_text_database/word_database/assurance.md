**assurance** *n*
**1** a [[statement]] or [[assertion]] intended to inspire [[confidence]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise]], [[statement]], [[guarantee]], [[commitment]], [[pledge]], [[vow]], [[declaration]], [[assertion]]
**2** feeling of [[confidence]]; [[certainty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confidence]], [[conviction]], [[certainty]], [[self-confidence]], [[poise]], [[faith]], [[nerve]], [[aplomb]]
**3** insurance that provides for events that are certain to happen, such as death
