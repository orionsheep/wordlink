**occult** *adj*
**1** involving [[mystical]] or [[supernatural]] phenomena or powers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supernatural]], [[magical]], [[mysterious]], [[psychic]], [[mystical]], [[unearthly]], [[esoteric]], [[uncanny]]
**2** beyond ordinary human understanding
**3** secret or [[mysterious]]
 *n*
**4** **[[the occult]]** the knowledge and study of occult phenomena and powers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the occult]]**: magic, witchcraft, sorcery, wizardry, enchantment, black art, necromancy
