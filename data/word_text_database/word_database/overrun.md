**overrun** *vb* **-running**, **-ran**, **-run**
**1** to conquer (territory) rapidly by force of numbers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overwhelm]], [[attack]], [[assault]], [[occupy]], [[raid]], [[invade]], [[penetrate]], [[rout]]
**2** to [[spread over]] (a place) rapidly: dirty tenements, overrun by lice, rats, and roaches
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread over]], [[overwhelm]], [[choke]], [[swamp]], [[infest]], [[inundate]], [[permeate]], [[swarm over]]
**3** to extend or run beyond a set limit: Tuesday's lunch overran by three-quarters of an hour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exceed]], [[go beyond]], [[surpass]], [[overshoot]], run over *or* on
