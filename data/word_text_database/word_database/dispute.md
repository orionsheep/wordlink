**dispute** *n*
**1** a [[disagreement]] between workers and their employer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagreement]], [[conflict]], [[argument]], [[dissent]], [[altercation]]
**2** an [[argument]] between two or more people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[argument]], [[row]], [[clash]], [[controversy]], [[contention]], [[feud]], [[quarrel]], [[squabble]]
**3** **beyond dispute** unable to be questioned or denied: it's beyond dispute that tensions already existed between them
 *vb* **-puting**, **-puted**
**4** to argue or [[quarrel]] about (something)
**5** to [[doubt]] the validity of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contest]], [[question]], [[challenge]], [[deny]], [[doubt]], [[oppose]], [[object to]], [[contradict]]
**6** to fight over possession of
 **[[disputation]]** *n* 
 **[[disputatious]]** *adj*
