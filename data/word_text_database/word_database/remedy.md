**remedy** *n*, *pl* **-edies**
**1** a drug or [[treatment]] for curing pain or disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cure]], [[treatment]], [[medicine]], [[nostrum]]
**2** a way of solving a problem: every statesman promised a remedy for unemployment
 *vb* **-edies**, **-edying**, **-edied**
**3** to [[put right]] or improve
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put right]], [[rectify]], [[fix]], [[correct]], [[set to rights]]
 **[[remediable]]** *adj*
