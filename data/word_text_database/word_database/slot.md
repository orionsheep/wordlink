**slot** *n*
**1** a narrow [[opening]] or [[groove]], such as one in a vending machine for inserting a coin
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opening]], [[hole]], [[groove]], [[vent]], [[slit]], [[aperture]]
**2** (*informal*) a [[place]] in a series or scheme: the late-night slot when people stop watching TV
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[place]], [[time]], [[space]], [[opening]], [[position]], vacancy
 *vb* **slotting**, **[[slotted]]**
**3** to make a slot or slots in
**4** (usually foll. by *in* or *into*) to [[fit]] or be fitted into a slot: I [[slotted]] my card into the machine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fit]], [[insert]]
