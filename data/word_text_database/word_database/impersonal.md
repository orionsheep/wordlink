**impersonal** *adj*
**1** without reference to any individual person; objective: Buddhism began as a very impersonal doctrine
**2** without human warmth or sympathy: an impersonal manner
**3** (*grammar*)
**A** (of a verb) having no subject, as in *it is raining*
 **B** (of a pronoun) not referring to a person
 **[[impersonality]]** *n* 
 **[[impersonally]]** *adv*
