**thrift** *n*
**1** wisdom and caution with money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[economy]], [[prudence]], [[frugality]], [[saving]], [[parsimony]], [[carefulness]], [[thriftiness]]
**2** a low-growing plant of Europe, W Asia, and North America, with narrow leaves and round heads of pink or white flowers
 **[[thriftless]]** *adj*
