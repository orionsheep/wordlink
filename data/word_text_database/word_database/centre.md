**centre** * or * (*US*) **center** *n*
**1** the [[middle]] point or part of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[middle]], [[heart]], [[focus]], [[core]], [[nucleus]], [[hub]], [[pivot]], [[kernel]]
**2** a place where a specified activity takes place: a shopping centre
**3** a person or thing that is a [[focus]] of interest: the centre of a long-running dispute
**4** a place of activity or influence: the parliament building was the centre of resistance
**5** a political party or group that favours moderation
**6** (*sport*) a player who plays in the [[middle]] of the field rather than on a wing
 *vb* **-tring**, **-tred** (*US*) **-tering**, **-tered**
**7** to put in the centre of something
**8** **centre on** to have as a centre or main theme: the summit is expected to centre on expanding the role of the UN
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[centre on something or someone]]**: [[focus]], concentrate, cluster, revolve, converge
