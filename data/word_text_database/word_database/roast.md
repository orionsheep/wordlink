**roast** *vb*
**1** to cook (food) by dry heat in an oven or over a fire
**2** to brown or dry (coffee or nuts) by exposure to heat
**3** to make or be extremely hot
**4** (*informal*) to criticize severely
 *n*
**5** a roasted joint of meat
 *adj*
**6** cooked by roasting: roast beef
