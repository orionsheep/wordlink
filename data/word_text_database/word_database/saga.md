**saga** (**sah**-ga) *n*
**1** a medieval Scandinavian legend telling the adventures of a hero or a family
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[epic]], [[story]], [[tale]], [[narrative]], [[yarn]] (*informal*)
**2** (*informal*) a long [[story]] or series of events: the long-running saga of the hostage issue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry-on]] (*informal*), [[performance]] (*informal*), [[pantomime]] (*informal*)
