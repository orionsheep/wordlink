**diary** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** a book containing a record of daily events, appointments, or observations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[journal]], [[chronicle]], [[blog]] (*informal*)
**2** a written record of daily events, appointments, or observations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engagement book]], [[appointment book]], [[personal organizer]]
