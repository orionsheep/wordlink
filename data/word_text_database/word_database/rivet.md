**rivet** (**riv**-vit) *n*
**1** a short metal pin for fastening metal plates, with a head at one end, the other end being hammered flat after being put through holes in the plates
 *vb* **-veting**, **-veted**
**2** to join by riveting
**3** to cause a person's attention to be fixed in fascination or horror: he was riveted by the story
 **[[riveter]]** *n*
