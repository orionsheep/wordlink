**tarnish** *vb*
**1** (of a metal) to become stained or less bright, esp. by exposure to air or moisture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stain]], [[discolour]], [[darken]], [[blot]], [[blemish]]
**2** to [[damage]] or [[taint]]: the affair could tarnish the reputation of the prime minister
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[taint]], [[blacken]], [[sully]], smirch
 *n*
**3** a [[tarnished]] condition, surface, or film on a surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stain]], [[taint]], [[discoloration]], [[spot]], [[blot]], [[blemish]]
 **[[tarnished]]** *adj*
