**best** *adj*
**1** the superlative of **good**
**2** most excellent of a particular group, category, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[finest]], [[leading]], [[supreme]], [[principal]], [[foremost]], [[pre-eminent]], [[unsurpassed]], [[most accomplished]]
**3** most suitable, desirable, etc.
 *adv*
**4** the superlative of **well**
**5** in a manner surpassing all others; most attractively, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[most highly]], [[most fully]], most deeply
 *n*
**6** **[[the best]]** the most outstanding or excellent person, thing, or group in a category
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the best]]**: the [[finest]], the pick, the flower, the cream, the elite, the crème de la crème
**7** the utmost effort: I did my best
**8** a person's [[finest]] clothes
**9** **at best**
**A** in the most favourable interpretation
 **B** under the most favourable conditions
**10** **for [[the best]]**
**A** for an ultimately good outcome
 **B** with good intentions
**11** **get [[the best]] of** to defeat or outwit
**12** **make [[the best]] of** to cope as well as possible with
 *vb*
**13** to defeat
