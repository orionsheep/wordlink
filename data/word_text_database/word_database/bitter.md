**bitter** *adj*
**1** having an unpalatable [[harsh]] taste, as the peel of an orange
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sour]], [[sharp]], [[acid]], [[harsh]], [[tart]], [[astringent]], [[acrid]], [[unsweetened]]
**2** showing or caused by hostility or resentment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resentful]], [[angry]], [[offended]], [[sour]], [[sore]], [[acrimonious]], [[sullen]], [[miffed]] (*informal*)
**3** difficult to accept: a bitter blow
**4** sarcastic: bitter words
**5** bitingly cold: a bitter night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[freezing]], [[biting]], [[severe]], [[intense]], [[raw]], [[fierce]], [[chill]], stinging
 *n*
**6** (*Brit*) beer with a slightly bitter taste
 **[[bitterly]]** *adv* 
 **[[bitterness]]** *n*
