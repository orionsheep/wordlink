**varnish** *n*
**1** a liquid painted onto a surface to give it a hard glossy finish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lacquer]], [[polish]], [[glaze]], [[gloss]]
**2** a smooth surface, coated with or as if with varnish
**3** an artificial, superficial, or deceptively pleasing manner or appearance: those who aspired to become civil servants acquired a varnish of university education
**4** (*chiefly* *Brit*) short for **nail varnish**
 *vb*
**5** to apply varnish to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lacquer]], [[polish]], [[glaze]], [[gloss]]
**6** to try to make (something unpleasant) appear more attractive: when did we start equivocating, camouflaging, varnishing the truth?
