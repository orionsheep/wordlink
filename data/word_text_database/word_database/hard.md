**3** showing or requiring a lot of effort or application: hard work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhausting]], [[tough]], [[exacting]], [[rigorous]], [[gruelling]], [[strenuous]], [[arduous]], [[laborious]]
**4** [[unkind]] or unfeeling: she's very hard, no pity for anyone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harsh]], [[cold]], [[cruel]], [[stern]], [[callous]], [[unkind]], [[unsympathetic]], [[pitiless]]
**5** causing pain, sorrow, or hardship: the hard life of a northern settler

**6** [[tough]] or violent: a hard man
**7** forceful: a hard knock
**8** cool or uncompromising: we took a long hard look at our profit factor
**9** indisputable and proven to be true: hard facts
**10** (of water) containing calcium salts which stop soap lathering freely
**11** practical, shrewd, or calculating: I am a hard businesswoman
**12** [[harsh]]: hard light
**13** (of currency) high and stable in exchange value

**16** hard-core

**19** **hard of hearing** slightly deaf
**20** **hard up** (*informal*) in need of money
