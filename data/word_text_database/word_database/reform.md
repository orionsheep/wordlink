**reform** *n*
**1** correction of abuses or malpractices: a programme of economic reforms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[improvement]], [[amendment]], [[rehabilitation]], [[betterment]]
**2** [[improvement]] of morals or behaviour
 *vb*
**3** to [[improve]] (a law or institution) by correcting abuses
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[improve]], [[correct]], [[restore]], [[amend]], [[mend]], [[rectify]]
**4** to give up or cause to give up a bad habit or way of life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mend your ways]], [[go straight]] (*informal*), [[shape up]] (*informal*), [[turn over a new leaf]], [[clean up your act]] (*informal*), [[pull your socks up]] (*Brit* *informal*)
