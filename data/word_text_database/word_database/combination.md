**combination** *n*
**1** the act of combining or state of being combined
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[association]], [[union]], [[alliance]], [[coalition]], [[federation]], [[consortium]], [[syndicate]], [[confederation]]
**2** people or things combined
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mixture]], [[mix]], [[blend]], [[composite]], [[amalgamation]], [[coalescence]]
**3** the set of numbers or letters that opens a combination lock
**4** a motorcycle with a sidecar
**5** (*maths*) an arrangement of the members of a set into specified groups without regard to order in the group
