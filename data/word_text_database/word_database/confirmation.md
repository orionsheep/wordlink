**confirmation** *n*
**1** the act of confirming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affirmation]], [[approval]], [[acceptance]], [[endorsement]], [[ratification]], [[assent]], [[agreement]]
**2** something that confirms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proof]], [[evidence]], [[testimony]], [[verification]], [[ratification]], [[validation]], [[corroboration]], [[authentication]]
**3** a rite in several Christian churches that admits a baptized person to full church membership
