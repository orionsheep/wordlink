**activate** *vb* **-vating**, **-vated**
**1** to make something active
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[start]], [[move]], [[initiate]], [[rouse]], [[mobilize]], [[set in motion]], [[galvanize]]
**2** (*physics*) to make something radioactive
**3** (*chem*) to increase the rate of a reaction
 **[[activation]]** *n*
