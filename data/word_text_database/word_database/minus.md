**minus** *prep*
**1** reduced by the subtraction of: six minus two equals four
**2** (*informal*) without or lacking: he returned minus his jacket
 *adj*
**3** indicating or involving subtraction: a minus sign
**4** Also: **negative** less than zero: it's minus eight degrees in Montreal today
**5** (*education*) slightly below the standard of a particular grade: a C minus for maths
 *n*
**6** a negative quantity
**7** (*informal*) something detrimental or negative
