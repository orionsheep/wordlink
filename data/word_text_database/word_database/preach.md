**preach** *vb*
**1** to talk on a religious theme as part of a church service
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deliver a sermon]], [[address]], [[evangelize]], [[preach a sermon]]
**2** to speak in support of (something) in a moralizing way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[urge]], [[teach]], [[champion]], [[recommend]], [[advise]], [[counsel]], [[advocate]], [[exhort]]
