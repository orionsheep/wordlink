**manufacture** *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**1** to process or [[make]] goods on a large scale, esp. using machinery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[build]], [[produce]], [[construct]], [[create]], [[turn out]], [[assemble]], [[put together]]
**2** to [[invent]] or [[concoct]] evidence, an excuse, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[concoct]], [[make up]], [[invent]], [[devise]], [[fabricate]], [[think up]], [[cook up]] (*informal*), trump up
 *n*
**3** the [[production]] of goods, esp. by industrial processes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[making]], [[production]], [[construction]], [[assembly]], [[creation]]
 **[[manufacturer]]** *n* 
 **[[manufacturing]]** *n*, *adj*
