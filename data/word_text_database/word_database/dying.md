**dying** *vb*
**1** the present participle of **die**
 *adj*
**2** occurring at the moment of death: in accordance with his dying wish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[final]], [[last]], [[parting]], [[departing]]
**3** (of a person or animal) very ill and likely to die soon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[near death]], [[moribund]], [[in extremis]] (*Latin*), [[at death's door]], [[not long for this world]]
**4** becoming less important or less current: coal mining is a dying industry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[failing]], [[declining]], [[foundering]], [[diminishing]], [[decreasing]], [[dwindling]], [[subsiding]]
