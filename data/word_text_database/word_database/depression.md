**depression** *n*
**1** a mental health condition in which a person has strong feelings of dejection and inadequacy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[despair]], [[misery]], [[sadness]], [[the dumps]] (*informal*), [[the blues]], [[melancholy]], [[unhappiness]], [[despondency]]
**2** an economic condition in which there is substantial unemployment, low output and low investment; [[slump]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recession]], [[slump]], [[economic decline]], [[credit crunch]], [[stagnation]], [[inactivity]], hard *or* bad times
**3** (*meteorol*) a mass of air below normal atmospheric pressure, which often causes rain
**4** a sunken place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hollow]], [[pit]], [[dip]], [[bowl]], [[valley]], [[dent]], [[cavity]], [[indentation]]
