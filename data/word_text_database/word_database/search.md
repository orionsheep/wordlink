**search** *vb*
**1** to [[look]] through (a place) thoroughly in order to find someone or something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examine]], [[investigate]], [[explore]], [[inspect]], [[comb]], [[scour]], [[ransack]], [[scrutinize]], [[fossick]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**2** to [[examine]] (a person) for hidden objects
**3** to [[look]] at or [[examine]] (something) closely: I searched my heart for one good thing she had done
**4** **search out** to find by searching
**5** to make a search
**6** **search me** (*informal*) I don't know
 *n*
**7** an attempt to find something by looking somewhere
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hunt]], [[look]], [[investigation]], [[examination]], [[pursuit]], [[quest]], [[inspection]], [[exploration]], [[Google]] (*computers*), googlewhack (*computers*, *informal*)
