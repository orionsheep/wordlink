**browse** *vb* **browsing**, **browsed**
**1** to [[look through]] (a book or articles for sale) in a casual leisurely manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skim]], [[scan]], [[glance at]], [[survey]], [[look through]], [[look round]], [[dip into]], [[leaf through]]
**2** (*computers*) to read hypertext, esp. on the internet
**3** (of deer, goats, etc.) to [[feed]] upon vegetation by continual nibbling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[graze]], [[eat]], [[feed]], nibble
 *n*
**4** an instance of browsing
