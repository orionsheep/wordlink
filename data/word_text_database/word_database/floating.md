**floating** *adj*
**1** (of a population) moving about; not settled
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[free]], [[wandering]], [[variable]], [[fluctuating]], [[unattached]], [[movable]]
**2** (of an organ or part) displaced or abnormally [[movable]]: a floating kidney
**3** (of a voter) not committed to one party
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncommitted]], [[wavering]], [[undecided]], [[indecisive]], [[vacillating]], [[sitting on the fence]] (*informal*), [[unaffiliated]], [[independent]]
**4** (*finance*)
**A** (of capital) available for current use
 **B** (of a currency) [[free]] to fluctuate against other currencies
