**attract** *vb*
**1** to arouse the interest or admiration of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allure]], [[draw]], [[persuade]], [[charm]], [[appeal to]], [[win over]], [[tempt]], [[lure]] (*informal*)
**2** (of a magnet) to [[draw]] (something) closer by exerting a force on it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pull]] (*informal*), [[draw]], [[magnetize]]
