◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corrode]], [[eat into]], [[burn into]]
**2** to [[cut]] (a design or pattern) into a printing plate with acid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engrave]], [[cut]], [[impress]], [[stamp]], [[carve]], [[imprint]], [[inscribe]]
