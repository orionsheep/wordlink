**reaction** *n*
**1** a physical or emotional [[response]] to a stimulus
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[response]], [[answer]], [[reply]]
**2** any action resisting another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[counteraction]], [[backlash]], [[recoil]]
**3** opposition to change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conservatism]], [[the right]]
**4** (*med*) any effect produced by a drug or by a substance (allergen) to which a person is allergic
**5** (*chem*) a process that involves changes in the structure and energy content of atoms, molecules, or ions
**6** the equal and opposite force that acts on a body whenever it exerts a force on another body
**7** **reactions** someone's ability to act in [[response]] to something that happens
