**fraction** *n*
**1** (*maths*) a numerical quantity that is not a whole number
**2** any [[part]] or subdivision
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fragment]], [[part]], [[piece]], [[section]], [[sector]], [[segment]]
**3** a very small proportion or amount of something
**4** (*chem*) a component of a mixture separated by distillation
 **[[fractional]]** *adj* 
 **[[fractionally]]** *adv*
