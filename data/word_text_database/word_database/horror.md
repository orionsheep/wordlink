**horror** *n*
**1** extreme [[fear]] or [[terror]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terror]], [[fear]], [[alarm]], [[panic]], [[dread]], [[fright]], [[consternation]], [[trepidation]] (*formal*)
**2** intense [[hatred]]: she had a horror of violence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hatred]], [[disgust]], [[loathing]], [[aversion]], [[revulsion]], [[repugnance]], [[odium]] (*formal*), [[detestation]]
**3** a thing or person causing [[fear]], [[loathing]], or distaste
 *adj*
**4** having a frightening subject, usually concerned with the supernatural: a horror film
