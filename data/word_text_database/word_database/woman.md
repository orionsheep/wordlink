**woman** *n*, *pl* **women**
**1** an [[adult female]] human being
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lady]], [[girl]], [[female]] (*sometimes* *derogatory*), [[sheila]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[vrou]] (*S African*), [[adult female]], [[femme]], [[wahine]] (*NZ*)
**2** [[adult female]] human beings collectively: the very image of woman pared of the trappings of 'femininity'
**3** an [[adult female]] human being with qualities associated with the [[female]], such as tenderness or maternalism: she's more woman than you know
**4** a [[female]] servant or domestic help
**5** (*informal*) a wife or girlfriend
 *adj*
**6** [[female]]: a woman doctor
