**1** to move or [[sway]] unsteadily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shake]], [[rock]], [[sway]], [[tremble]], [[teeter]], [[totter]]
**2** to [[shake]]: she was having difficulty in controlling her voice, which wobbled about
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tremble]], [[shake]]
 *n*
**3** a wobbling movement or sound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsteadiness]], [[shake]], [[tremble]]
