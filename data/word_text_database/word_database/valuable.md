**valuable** *adj*
**1** worth a large amount of money: his house was furnished with valuable antique furniture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[precious]], [[expensive]], [[costly]], [[dear]], [[high-priced]], [[priceless]], [[irreplaceable]]
**2** of great use or importance: the investigations will provide valuable information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[useful]], [[important]], [[profitable]], [[worthwhile]], [[beneficial]], helpful
 *n*
**3** **valuables** valuable articles of personal property, such as jewellery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[treasures]], [[prized possessions]], [[precious items]], [[heirlooms]], [[personal effects]], [[costly articles]]
