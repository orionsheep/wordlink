**fight** *vb* **[[fighting]]**, **fought**
**1** to [[struggle]] against (an enemy) in [[battle]] or physical combat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oppose]], [[campaign against]], [[dispute]], [[contest]], [[resist]], [[defy]], [[contend]], [[withstand]]
**2** to [[struggle]] to overcome or destroy: to fight drug trafficking
**3** to [[carry on]] (a [[battle]] or [[contest]])
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engage in]], [[conduct]], [[wage]], [[pursue]], [[carry on]]
**4** to make (one's way) somewhere with difficulty: they fought their way upstream
**5** **fight for** to uphold (a cause) by struggling: fight for your rights
**6** **fight it out** to [[struggle]] or compete until a decisive result is obtained
**7** **fight shy of** to avoid: they fought shy of direct involvement in the conflict
 *n*
**8** a [[battle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[battle]], [[campaign]], [[movement]], [[struggle]]
**9** a [[quarrel]] or [[contest]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[row]], [[argument]], [[dispute]], [[quarrel]], [[squabble]]
**10** a boxing match
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brawl]], [[scrap]] (*informal*), [[confrontation]], [[rumble]] (*US** & **NZ* *slang*), [[duel]], [[skirmish]], [[tussle]], [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[boilover]] (*Austral*)
**11** **put up a fight** to offer resistance
 **[[fighting]]** *n*
