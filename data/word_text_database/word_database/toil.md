**toil** *n*
**1** hard or exhausting [[work]]: hours of toil beneath the Catalan sun
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hard work]], [[effort]], [[application]], [[sweat]], [[graft]] (*informal*), [[slog]], [[exertion]], drudgery
 *vb*
**2** to [[work]] hard: workers toiling in the fields to produce tea for westerners to drink
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[labour]], [[work]], [[struggle]], [[strive]], [[sweat]] (*informal*), [[slave]], [[graft]] (*informal*), [[slog]]
**3** to move slowly and with difficulty, for instance because of exhaustion or the steepness of a slope: Joanna toiled up the steps to the church
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[struggle]], [[trek]], [[slog]], [[trudge]], [[fight your way]], [[footslog]]
