**juice** *n*
**1** a drink made from the [[liquid]] part of a fruit or vegetable: grapefruit juice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liquid]], [[extract]], [[fluid]], [[liquor]], [[sap]], [[nectar]]
**2** (*informal*)
**A** petrol
 **B** electricity
**3** **juices**
**A** the fluids in a person's or animal's body: digestive juices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: secretion
 **B** the [[liquid]] that comes out of meat when it is cooked
