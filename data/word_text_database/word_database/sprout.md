**sprout** *vb*
**1** (of a plant or seed) to produce (new leaves or shoots)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[germinate]], [[bud]], [[shoot]], [[spring]]
**2** (often foll. by *up*) to begin to [[grow]] or [[develop]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grow]], [[develop]], ripen
 *n*
**3** a new [[shoot]] or [[bud]]
**4** same as **Brussels sprout**
