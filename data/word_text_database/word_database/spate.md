**spate** *n*
**1** a large number of things happening within a period of time: a spate of bombings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[series]], [[sequence]], [[course]], [[chain]], [[succession]], [[run]], [[train]]
**2** a fast [[flow]] or [[outpouring]]: an incomprehensible spate of words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flood]], [[flow]], [[torrent]], [[rush]], [[deluge]], [[outpouring]]
**3** **in spate** (*chiefly* *Brit*) (of a river) flooded
