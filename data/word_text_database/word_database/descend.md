**descend** *vb*
**1** to [[move down]] (a slope, staircase, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go down]], [[come down]], [[walk down]], [[move down]], [[climb down]]
**2** to move or [[fall]] to a lower level, pitch, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fall]], [[drop]], [[sink]], [[go down]], [[plunge]], [[dive]], [[tumble]], [[plummet]]
**3** **[[be descended from]]** to be connected by a blood relationship to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[be descended from]]**: originate from, derive from, spring from, proceed from, issue from
**4** **descend on** to visit unexpectedly
**5** **descend to** to stoop to (unworthy behaviour)
