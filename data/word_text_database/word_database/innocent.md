**innocent** *adj*
**1** [[not guilty]] of a particular crime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[not guilty]], [[in the clear]], [[blameless]], [[clean]], [[honest]], [[uninvolved]], [[irreproachable]], [[guiltless]]
**2** without experience of evil
**3** [[harmless]] or [[innocuous]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harmless]], [[innocuous]], [[inoffensive]], [[well-meant]], [[unobjectionable]], [[well-intentioned]]
**4** **innocent of** without or lacking: innocent of prejudice
 *n*
**5** an innocent person, esp. a young child or a naive adult
 **[[innocently]]** *adv*
