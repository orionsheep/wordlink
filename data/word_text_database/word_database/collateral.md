**collateral** *n*
**1** security pledged for the repayment of a loan
**2** a person, animal, or plant descended from the same ancestor as another but through a different line
 *adj*
**3** descended from a common ancestor but through different lines
**4** additional but subordinate: a spokeswoman said that there was no collateral information to dispute the assurances the government had been given
**5** situated or running side by side: collateral ridges of mountains
