**imply** *vb* **-plies**, **-plying**, **-plied**
**1** to express or [[indicate]] by a [[hint]]; [[suggest]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suggest]], [[hint]], [[insinuate]], [[indicate]], [[intimate]], [[signify]]
**2** to [[suggest]] or [[involve]] as a necessary consequence: a spending commitment implies a corresponding tax imposition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[involve]], [[mean]], [[entail]], [[require]], [[indicate]], [[point to]], [[signify]], [[presuppose]]
