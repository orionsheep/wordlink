**love** *vb* **loving**, **loved**
**1** to have a great [[affection]] for a person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adore]], [[care for]], [[treasure]], [[cherish]], [[prize]], [[worship]], [[be devoted to]], [[dote on]]
**2** to have passionate desire for someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adore]], [[care for]], [[treasure]], [[cherish]], [[prize]], [[worship]], [[be devoted to]], [[dote on]]
**3** to [[like]] (to do something) very much
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enjoy]], [[like]], [[appreciate]], [[relish]], [[delight in]], [[savour]], [[take pleasure in]], have a soft spot for
 *n*
**4** an intense emotion of [[affection]] towards a person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passion]], [[affection]], [[warmth]], [[attachment]], [[intimacy]], [[devotion]], [[tenderness]], [[adoration]], [[aroha]] (*NZ*)
**5** a deep feeling of sexual attraction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passion]], [[affection]], [[warmth]], [[attachment]], [[intimacy]], [[devotion]], [[tenderness]], [[adoration]], [[aroha]] (*NZ*)
**6** wholehearted [[liking]] for or pleasure in something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liking]], [[taste]], [[bent for]], [[weakness for]], [[relish for]], [[enjoyment]], [[devotion to]], [[penchant for]]
**7** a [[beloved]] person: often used as an endearment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beloved]], [[dear]], [[dearest]], [[lover]], [[darling]], [[honey]], [[sweetheart]], [[truelove]]
**8** (*Brit* *informal*) a commonplace term of address, not necessarily restricted to people one knows or has regard for
**9** (in tennis, squash, etc.) a score of zero
**10** **fall in love** to become in love
**11** **for love or money** in any circumstances
**12** **in love** feeling a strong emotional and sexual attraction
**13** **make love to**
**A** to have sexual intercourse with
 **B** (*archaic*) to court
