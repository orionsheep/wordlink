**trimming** *n*
**1** an extra piece added to a garment for [[decoration]]: a pink nightie with lace trimming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decoration]], [[edging]], [[border]], [[piping]], [[frill]], [[embellishment]], [[adornment]], [[ornamentation]]
**2** **trimmings** usual or traditional [[accompaniments]]: bacon and eggs with all the trimmings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extras]], [[accessories]], [[ornaments]], [[accompaniments]], [[frills]], [[trappings]], [[paraphernalia]]
