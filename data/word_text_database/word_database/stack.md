**stack** *n*
**1** a [[pile]] of things, one on top of the other
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pile]], [[heap]], [[mountain]], [[mass]], [[load]] (*informal*), [[mound]]
**2** a large neat [[pile]] of hay or straw
**3** **stacks** a large amount: there's still stacks for us to do
**4** an area in a computer memory for temporary storage
 *vb*
**5** to place (things) in a stack
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pile]], [[heap up]], [[load]], [[assemble]], [[accumulate]], [[amass]]
**6** to [[load]] or fill (something) up with piles of objects: Henry was watching her stack the dishwasher
**7** to control (a number of aircraft) waiting to land at an airport so that each flies at a different altitude
