**slam** *vb* **slamming**, **slammed**
**1** to close violently and noisily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bang]], [[crash]], [[smash]]
**2** to [[throw]] (something or someone) down violently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throw]], [[dash]], [[hurl]], [[fling]]
**3** (*slang*) to criticize harshly: his new proposals were slammed by the opposition
**4** to strike with violent force: he slammed the ball into the back of the net
 *n*
**5** the act or noise of slamming
