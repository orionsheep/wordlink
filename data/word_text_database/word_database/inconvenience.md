**inconvenience** *n*
**1** a state or instance of [[trouble]] or [[difficulty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trouble]], [[difficulty]], [[bother]], [[fuss]], [[disadvantage]], [[disturbance]], [[disruption]], [[nuisance]], uphill (*S African*)
 *vb* **-iencing**, **-ienced**
**2** to cause [[trouble]] or [[difficulty]] to (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trouble]], [[bother]], [[disturb]], [[upset]], [[disrupt]], [[put out]], [[discommode]]
 **[[inconvenient]]** *adj*
