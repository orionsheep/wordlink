**offend** *vb*
**1** to hurt the feelings of (a person); [[insult]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distress]], [[upset]], [[outrage]], [[wound]], [[slight]], [[insult]], [[annoy]], [[snub]]
**2** to be disagreeable to; disgust: she was offended by what she saw
**3** to commit a crime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break the law]], [[sin]], [[err]], [[do wrong]], [[fall]], [[go astray]]
 **[[offender]]** *n* 
 **[[offending]]** *adj*
