**applaud** *vb*
**1** to show approval of by clapping one's hands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clap]], [[encourage]], [[praise]], [[cheer]], [[acclaim]]
**2** to express approval of: we applaud her determination and ambition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[praise]], [[celebrate]], [[approve]], [[acclaim]], [[compliment]], [[salute]], [[commend]], [[extol]]
