**represent** *vb*
**1** to act as the authorized delegate for (a person, country, etc.): she represented her country at the Olympic Games
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[act for]], [[speak for]]
**2** to act as a substitute (for)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stand for]], [[serve as]]
**3** to stand as an equivalent of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[express]], [[correspond to]], [[symbolize]], [[mean]]
**4** to be a means of expressing: the lights are relit to represent resurrection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depict]], [[show]], [[describe]], [[picture]], [[illustrate]], [[outline]], [[portray]], [[denote]]
**5** to display the characteristics of: romanticism in music is represented by Liszt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exemplify]], [[embody]], [[symbolize]], [[typify]], [[personify]], [[epitomize]]
**6** to [[describe]] as having a specified character or quality: the magical bird was often represented as having two heads
**7** to state or explain
**8** to present an image of through a [[picture]] or sculpture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depict]], [[show]], [[describe]], [[picture]], [[illustrate]], [[outline]], [[portray]], [[denote]]
**9** to bring clearly before the mind
