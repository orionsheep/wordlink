**pioneer** *n*
**1** an [[explorer]] or [[settler]] of a new land or region
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settler]], [[explorer]], [[colonist]]
**2** an originator or [[developer]] of something new
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[founder]], [[leader]], [[developer]], [[innovator]], trailblazer
 *vb*
**3** to be a pioneer (in or of)
**4** to [[initiate]] or [[develop]]: the new technique was pioneered in France
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[develop]], [[create]], [[establish]], [[start]], [[discover]], [[institute]], [[invent]], [[initiate]]
