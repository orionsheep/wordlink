**choose** *vb* **choosing**, **chose**, **chosen**
**1** to [[select]] (a person, thing, or course of action) from a number of alternatives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pick]], [[prefer]], [[select]], [[elect]], [[adopt]], [[opt for]], [[designate]], [[settle upon]]
**2** to like or please: when she did choose to reveal her secret, the group were initially hushed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wish]], [[want]]
**3** to consider it desirable or proper: I don't choose to read that sort of book
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wish]], [[want]]
