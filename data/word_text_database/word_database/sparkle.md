**sparkle** *vb* **-kling**, **-kled**
**1** to [[glitter]] with many bright points of light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glitter]], [[flash]], [[shine]], [[gleam]], [[shimmer]], [[twinkle]], [[dance]], [[glint]]
**2** (of wine or mineral water) to be slightly fizzy
**3** to be lively, witty, and intelligent
 *n*
**4** a small bright point of light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glitter]], [[flash]], [[gleam]], [[flicker]], [[brilliance]], [[twinkle]], [[glint]]
**5** [[liveliness]] and wit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vivacity]], [[life]], [[spirit]], [[dash]], [[vitality]], [[élan]], [[liveliness]]
 **[[sparkling]]** *adj*
