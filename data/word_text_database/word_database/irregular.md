**irregular** *adj*
**1** [[uneven]] in shape, position, arrangement, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneven]], [[rough]], [[ragged]], [[crooked]], [[jagged]], [[bumpy]], [[contorted]], [[lopsided]]
**2** not conforming to accepted practice or routine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inappropriate]], [[unconventional]], [[unethical]], [[unusual]], [[extraordinary]], [[exceptional]], [[peculiar]], [[unofficial]]
**3** (of a word) not following the usual pattern of formation in a language
**4** not occurring at expected or equal intervals: an irregular pulse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[variable]], [[erratic]], [[occasional]], [[random]], [[casual]], [[shaky]], [[sporadic]], [[haphazard]]
**5** (of troops) not belonging to regular forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unofficial]], [[underground]], [[guerrilla]], [[resistance]], [[partisan]], [[rogue]], [[paramilitary]], mercenary
 *n*
**6** a soldier not in a regular army
 **[[irregularity]]** *n* 
 **[[irregularly]]** *adv*
