**latitude** *n*
**1**
**A** an angular distance measured in degrees north or south of the equator
 **B** (*often pl*) a region considered with regard to its distance from the equator
**2** [[scope]] for [[freedom]] of action and thought
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scope]], [[liberty]], [[freedom]], [[play]], [[space]], [[licence]], [[leeway]], [[laxity]]
 **[[latitudinal]]** *adj*
