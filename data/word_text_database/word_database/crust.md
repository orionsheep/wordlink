**crust** *n*
**1** the hard outer part of bread
**2** the baked [[shell]] of a pie or tart
**3** any hard outer [[layer]]: a thin crust of snow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[layer]], [[covering]], [[coating]], [[skin]], [[surface]], [[shell]]
**4** the solid outer [[shell]] of the earth
 *vb*
**5** to cover with or form a crust
