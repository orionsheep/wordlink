**fake** *vb* **faking**, **faked**
**1** to cause (something not genuine) to appear real or more valuable by [[fraud]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forge]], [[copy]], [[reproduce]], [[fabricate]], [[counterfeit]], [[falsify]]
**2** to [[pretend]] to have (an illness, emotion, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sham]], [[put on]], [[pretend]], [[simulate]], [[feign]], go through the motions of
 *n*
**3** an object, person, or act that is not genuine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forgery]], [[copy]], [[fraud]] (*informal*), [[reproduction]], [[dummy]], [[imitation]], [[hoax]], [[counterfeit]]
 *adj*
**4** not genuine: fake fur
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[artificial]], [[false]], [[forged]], [[counterfeit]], [[put-on]], [[pretend]] (*informal*), [[mock]], [[imitation]]
