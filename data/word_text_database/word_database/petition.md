**petition** *n*
**1** a written document signed by a large number of people demanding some form of action from a government or other authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appeal]], [[round robin]], [[list of signatures]]
**2** any formal [[request]] to a higher authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entreaty]], [[appeal]], [[suit]], [[application]], [[request]], [[prayer]], [[plea]], [[solicitation]]
**3** (*law*) a formal [[application]] in writing made to a court asking for some specific judicial action: she filed a petition for divorce
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entreaty]], [[appeal]], [[suit]], [[application]], [[request]], [[prayer]], [[plea]], [[solicitation]]
 *vb*
**4** to address or present a petition to (a government or to someone in authority): he petitioned the Crown for mercy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appeal]], [[plead]], [[ask]], [[pray]], [[beg]], [[solicit]], [[beseech]], [[entreat]]
**5** (foll. by *for*) to seek by petition: the firm's creditors petitioned for liquidation
 **[[petitioner]]** *n*
