**minority** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the smaller of two parts, factions, or groups
**2** a group that is different, esp. racially or politically, from a larger group of which it is a part
**3** **in the minority** forming or part of the group of people or things made up of less than half of a larger group
 *adj*
**4** relating to or being a minority: a minority sport
