**sturdy** *adj* **-dier**, **-diest**
**1** (of a person) healthy, strong, and unlikely to tire or become injured
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[robust]], [[hardy]], [[powerful]], [[athletic]], [[muscular]], [[lusty]], [[brawny]]
**2** (of a piece of furniture, shoes, etc.) strongly built or made
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substantial]], [[solid]], [[durable]], [[well-made]], [[well-built]]
 **[[sturdily]]** *adv*
