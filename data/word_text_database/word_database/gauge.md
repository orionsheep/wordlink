**gauge** (**gayj**) *vb* **gauging**, **gauged**
**1** to [[estimate]] or [[judge]] (people's feelings or reactions)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[judge]], [[estimate]], [[guess]], [[assess]], [[evaluate]], [[rate]], [[appraise]], [[reckon]]
**2** to [[measure]] using a gauge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[measure]], [[calculate]], [[evaluate]], [[value]], [[determine]], [[count]], [[weigh]], compute
 *n*
**3** an instrument for measuring quantities: a petrol gauge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meter]], [[dial]], [[measuring instrument]]
**4** a scale or standard of measurement
**5** a standard for estimating people's feelings or reactions: a gauge of public opinion
**6** the diameter of the barrel of a gun
**7** the distance between the rails of a railway track
