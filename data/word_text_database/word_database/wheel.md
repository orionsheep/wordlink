**wheel** *n*
**1** a circular object mounted on a shaft around which it can [[turn]], fixed under vehicles to enable them to move
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disc]], [[ring]], [[hoop]]
**2** anything like a wheel in shape or function: the steering wheel; a spinning wheel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disc]], [[ring]], [[hoop]]
**3** something that is repeated in cycles: the wheel of fashion would [[turn]], and the clothes would be back in style
**4** **at *or* behind the wheel** driving a vehicle
 *vb*
**5** to [[push]] (a bicycle, wheelchair, or pram) along
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[push]], [[trundle]], [[roll]]
**6** to [[turn]] in a circle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[turn]], [[swing]], [[spin]], [[revolve]], [[rotate]], [[whirl]], [[swivel]]
**7** **wheel and deal** to operate shrewdly and sometimes unscrupulously in order to advance one's own interests
**8** **wheel round** to change direction or [[turn]] round suddenly
