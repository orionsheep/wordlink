**truss** *vb*
**1** to tie or bind (someone) up
**2** to bind the wings and legs of (a fowl) before cooking
 *n*
**3** (*med*) a device for holding a hernia in place
**4** a framework of wood or metal used to support a roof, bridge, etc.
**5** a cluster of flowers or fruit growing at the end of a single stalk
