**scan** *vb* **scanning**, **scanned**
**1** to [[scrutinize]] carefully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[survey]], [[search]], [[investigate]], [[sweep]], [[scour]], [[scrutinize]]
**2** to [[glance over]] quickly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glance over]], [[skim]], [[look over]], [[eye]], [[check]], [[examine]], [[check out]] (*informal*), [[run over]], [[surf]] (*computers*)
**3** (*prosody*) to analyse (verse) by examining its rhythmic structure
**4** (*prosody*) (of a line or verse) to be metrically correct
**5** to [[examine]] or [[search]] (an area) by systematically moving a beam of light or electrons, or a radar or sonar beam over it
**6** (*med*) to obtain an image of (a part of the body) by means of ultrasound or a scanner
 *n*
**7** an instance of scanning
**8** (*med*)
**A** the examination of part of the body by means of a scanner
 **B** the image produced by a scanner
