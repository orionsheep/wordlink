**drink** *vb* **drinking**, **drank**, **drunk**
**1** to [[swallow]] (a [[liquid]])
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swallow]], [[sip]], [[suck]], [[gulp]], [[sup]], [[guzzle]], [[imbibe]] (*formal*), [[quaff]]
**2** to consume [[alcohol]], esp. to excess
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[booze]] (*informal*), [[tipple]], [[tope]] (*literary*), [[hit the bottle]] (*informal*)
**3** to bring (oneself) into a specified condition by consuming [[alcohol]]: he drank himself senseless every night
**4** **drink someone's health** to wish someone health or happiness with a toast
**5** **drink in** to pay close attention to: I drank in what the speaker said
**6** **drink to** to drink a toast to: I drank to their engagement
 *n*
**7** [[liquid]] suitable for drinking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beverage]], [[refreshment]], [[potion]], [[liquid]]
**8** a portion of [[liquid]] for drinking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glass]], [[cup]], [[draught]]
**9** [[alcohol]], or the habit of drinking too much of it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alcohol]], [[booze]] (*informal*), [[liquor]], [[spirits]], [[the bottle]] (*informal*), hooch *or* hootch (*informal*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) 
 **[[drinkable]]** *adj* 
 **[[drinker]]** *n*
