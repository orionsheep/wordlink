**gear** *n*
**1** a set of toothed wheels that engages with another or with a rack in order to change the speed or direction of transmitted motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mechanism]], [[works]], [[machinery]], [[cogs]], [[cogwheels]], [[gearwheels]]
**2** a [[mechanism]] for transmitting motion by gears
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mechanism]], [[works]], [[machinery]], [[cogs]], [[cogwheels]], [[gearwheels]]
**3** the setting of a gear to suit engine speed or direction: a higher gear; reverse gear
**4** [[clothing]] or personal belongings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clothing]], [[wear]], [[dress]], [[clothes]], [[outfit]], [[costume]], [[garments]], [[togs]]
**5** [[equipment]] for a particular task: police in riot gear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equipment]], [[supplies]], [[tackle]], [[tools]], [[instruments]], [[apparatus]], [[paraphernalia]], [[accoutrements]]
**6** **in *or* out of gear** with the gear [[mechanism]] engaged or disengaged
 *vb*
**7** to prepare or organize for something: to gear for war
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equip]], [[fit]], [[adjust]], adapt
 See also **gear up**
