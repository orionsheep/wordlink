**lost** *vb*
**1** the past of **lose**
 *adj*
**2** unable to find one's way
**3** unable to be found or recovered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[missing]], [[disappeared]], [[vanished]], [[wayward]], [[misplaced]], [[mislaid]]
**4** confused or bewildered: she seemed a bit lost
**5** (sometimes foll. by *on*) not used, noticed, or understood by: not that the propaganda value of the game was lost on the authorities
**6** no longer possessed or existing: lost credit
**7** (foll. by *in*) engrossed (in): he remained lost in his own thoughts
**8** morally ruined: in a lost condition
**9** damned: a lost soul
