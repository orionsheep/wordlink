**maid** *n*
**1** a [[female servant]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[servant]], [[chambermaid]], [[housemaid]], [[menial]], [[maidservant]], [[female servant]], [[domestic]] (*archaic*), [[parlourmaid]]
**2** (*archaic*, *or* *literary*) a young unmarried [[girl]]; maiden
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[girl]], maiden (*archaic*, *literary*), [[lass]], [[damsel]], [[lassie]] (*informal*)
