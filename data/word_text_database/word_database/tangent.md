**tangent** *n*
**1** a line, curve, or plane that touches another curve or surface at one point but does not cross it
**2** (in trigonometry) the ratio of the length of the opposite side to that of the adjacent side of a right-angled triangle
**3** **go off at a tangent** suddenly take a completely different line of thought or action
 *adj*
**4** of or involving a tangent
**5** touching at a single point
