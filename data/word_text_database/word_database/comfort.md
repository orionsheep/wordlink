**comfort** *n*
**1** a state of physical [[ease]] or well-being
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ease]], [[luxury]], [[wellbeing]], [[opulence]]
**2** [[relief]] from suffering or grief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consolation]], [[succour]], [[help]], [[support]], [[relief]], [[compensation]]
**3** a person or thing that brings [[ease]]
**4** **comforts** things that make life easier or more pleasant: the comforts of home
 *vb*
**5** to [[soothe]] or [[console]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[console]], [[reassure]], [[soothe]], [[hearten]], [[commiserate with]]
**6** to bring physical [[ease]] to
 **[[comforting]]** *adj*
