◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conscientious]], [[faithful]], [[rigid]], [[meticulous]], [[scrupulous]], [[punctilious]] (*formal*)
**4** (*Christianity*) relating to the way of life of monks and nuns
 *n*
**5** (*Christianity*) a monk or nun
