**admission** *n*
**1** permission or the right to enter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[admittance]], [[access]], [[entry]], [[introduction]], [[entrance]], [[acceptance]], [[initiation]], [[entrée]]
**2** permission to join an organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[admittance]], [[access]], [[entry]], [[introduction]], [[entrance]], [[acceptance]], [[initiation]], [[entrée]]
**3** the price charged for [[entrance]]
**4** a [[confession]]: she was, by her own admission, not educated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confession]], [[declaration]], [[revelation]], [[allowance]], [[disclosure]], [[acknowledgement]], [[unburdening]], [[divulgence]]
