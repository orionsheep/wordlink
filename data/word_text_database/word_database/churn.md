**churn** *n*
**1** a machine in which cream is shaken to make butter
**2** a large container for milk
 *vb*
**3** to stir (milk or cream) vigorously in order to make butter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stir up]], [[beat]], [[disturb]], [[swirl]], [[agitate]]
**4** to move about violently: a hot tub of churning water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swirl]], [[toss]]
