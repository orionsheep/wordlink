**premature** *adj*
**1** happening or done before the normal or expected time: premature ageing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[early]], [[untimely]], [[before time]], [[unseasonable]]
**2** impulsive or [[hasty]]: a premature judgment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hasty]], [[rash]], [[too soon]], [[untimely]], [[ill-timed]], [[overhasty]]
**3** (of a baby) born weeks before the date when it was due to be born
 **[[prematurely]]** *adv*
