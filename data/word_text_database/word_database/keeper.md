**keeper** *n*
**1** a person in charge of animals in a zoo
**2** a person in charge of a museum, collection, or section of a museum
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curator]], [[guardian]], [[steward]], [[attendant]], [[caretaker]], [[preserver]]
**3** a person who supervises a person or thing: the self-appointed keeper of the village conscience
**4** short for **gamekeeper**, **goalkeeper**
