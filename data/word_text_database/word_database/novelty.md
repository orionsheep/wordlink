**novelty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the quality of being new and interesting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[newness]], [[originality]], [[freshness]], [[innovation]], [[surprise]], [[uniqueness]], [[strangeness]], [[unfamiliarity]]
**2** a new or unusual experience or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curiosity]], [[rarity]], [[oddity]], [[wonder]]
**3** a small cheap toy or [[trinket]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trinket]], [[souvenir]], [[memento]], [[bauble]], [[trifle]], [[knick-knack]]
