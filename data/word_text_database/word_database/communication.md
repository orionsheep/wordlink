**communication** *n*
**1** the exchange of [[information]], ideas, or feelings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contact]], [[conversation]], [[correspondence]], [[link]], [[relations]]
**2** something communicated, such as a [[message]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[message]], [[news]], [[report]], [[word]], [[information]], [[statement]], [[announcement]], [[disclosure]], email *or* e-mail, [[text]]
**3** **communications** means of travelling or sending messages
