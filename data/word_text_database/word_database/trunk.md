**trunk** *n*
**1** the main [[stem]] of a tree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stem]], [[stalk]], [[bole]]
**2** a large strong [[case]] or [[box]] used to contain clothes when travelling and for storage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chest]], [[case]], [[box]], [[crate]], [[coffer]], [[casket]]
**3** a person's [[body]] excluding the head, neck, and limbs; [[torso]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[body]], [[torso]]
**4** the long [[nose]] of an elephant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[snout]], [[nose]], [[proboscis]]
**5** (*US*) the boot of a car
