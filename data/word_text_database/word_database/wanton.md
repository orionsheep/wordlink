**wanton** *adj*
**1** without motive, provocation, or justification: sheer wanton destruction
**2** (of a person) maliciously and unnecessarily cruel
**3** (*old-fashioned*, *derogatory*) (of a woman) sexually unrestrained or immodest
 *n*
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *derogatory*) a sexually unrestrained or immodest woman
