**velvet** *n*
**1** a fabric with a thick close soft pile on one side
**2** the furry covering of the newly formed antlers of a deer
 *adj*
**3** made of velvet
**4** soft or smooth like velvet
**5** **an iron fist *or* hand in a velvet glove** determination concealed by a gentle manner
