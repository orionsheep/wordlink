**[[show off]]** *vb*
**1** to exhibit or display (something) so as to invite admiration: he was eager to [[show off]] his new car
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[show something off]]**: exhibit, display, parade, demonstrate, flaunt
**2** (*informal*) to flaunt skills, knowledge, or looks in order to attract attention or impress people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[show off]]**: boast, brag, blow your own trumpet, swagger
 *n* **show-off**
**3** (*informal*) a person who flaunts his or her skills, knowledge, or looks in order to attract attention or impress people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhibitionist]], [[boaster]], [[poseur]], [[braggart]], [[figjam]] (*Austral* *slang*)
