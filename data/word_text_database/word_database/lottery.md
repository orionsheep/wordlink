**lottery** *n*, *pl* **-teries**
**1** a method of raising money by selling tickets by which a winner is selected at random
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[raffle]], [[draw]], [[lotto]] (*Brit** & **NZ** & **S African*), [[sweepstake]]
**2** a venture whose outcome is a matter of luck: hospital treatment is a lottery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gamble]], [[chance]], [[risk]], [[hazard]], [[toss-up]] (*informal*)
