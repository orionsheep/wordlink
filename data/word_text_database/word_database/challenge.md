**challenge** *n*
**1** a demanding or stimulating situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[test]], [[trial]], [[opposition]], [[confrontation]], [[ultimatum]]
**2** a call to engage in a contest, fight, or argument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dare]], [[provocation]], [[wero]] (*NZ*)
**3** a questioning of a statement or fact
**4** a demand by a sentry for identification or a password
**5** (*law*) a formal objection to a juror
 *vb* **-lenging**, **-lenged**
**6** to [[invite]] or call (someone) to take part in a contest, fight, or argument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dare]], [[invite]], [[defy]], [[throw down the gauntlet]]
**7** to call (a decision or action) into [[question]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[question]], [[tackle]], [[confront]], [[defy]], [[object to]], [[disagree with]], [[take issue with]]
**8** to order (a person) to stop and be identified
**9** (*law*) to make a formal objection to (a juror)
 **[[challenger]]** *n* 
 **[[challenging]]** *adj*
