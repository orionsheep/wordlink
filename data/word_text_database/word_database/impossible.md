**impossible** *adj*
**1** not able to be done or to happen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[not possible]], [[out of the question]], [[impracticable]], [[unfeasible]]
**2** [[absurd]] or [[unreasonable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[absurd]], [[crazy]] (*informal*), [[ridiculous]], [[outrageous]], [[ludicrous]], [[unreasonable]], [[preposterous]], [[farcical]]
**3** (*informal*) intolerable or [[outrageous]]: those children are impossible
 **[[impossibly]]** *adv*
