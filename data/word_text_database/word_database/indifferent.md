**indifferent** *adj*
**1** showing no concern or interest: he was indifferent to politics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unconcerned]], [[detached]], [[cold]], [[cool]], [[callous]], [[aloof]], [[unmoved]], [[unsympathetic]]
**2** of only average standard or quality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mediocre]], [[ordinary]], [[moderate]], [[so-so]] (*informal*), [[passable]], [[undistinguished]], [[no great shakes]] (*informal*), [[half-pie]] (*NZ* *informal*)
**3** not at all good: she had starred in several very indifferent movies
**4** unimportant
**5** showing or having no preferences
