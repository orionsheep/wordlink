**[[pay off]]** *vb*
**1** to pay the complete amount of (a debt)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pay something off]]**: settle, clear, square, discharge, pay in full
**2** to pay (someone) all that is due in wages and dismiss him or her from employment
**3** to turn out successfully: her persistence finally paid off
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pay off]]**: succeed, work, be effective
**4** (*informal*) to give a bribe to
 *n* **payoff**
**5** (*informal*) the climax or outcome of events
**6** (*informal*) a bribe
**7** the final payment of a debt
**8** the final settlement, esp. in retribution: the payoff came when the gang besieged the squealer's house
