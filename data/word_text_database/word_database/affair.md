**affair** *n*
**1** an [[event]] or [[happening]]: the Watergate affair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[matter]], [[business]], [[happening]], [[event]], [[activity]], [[incident]], [[episode]], [[topic]]
**2** a sexual [[relationship]] outside marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relationship]], [[romance]], [[intrigue]], [[fling]], [[liaison]], [[flirtation]], [[amour]], [[dalliance]]
**3** a thing to be done or attended to: your choice of career is your own affair
**4** something previously specified: lunch was a subdued affair
