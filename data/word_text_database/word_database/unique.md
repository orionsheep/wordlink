**unique** (yew-**neek**) *adj*
**1** being the [[only]] one of a particular type
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distinct]], [[special]], [[exclusive]], [[peculiar]], [[only]], [[single]], [[lone]], [[solitary]]
**2** **unique to** concerning or belonging to a particular person, thing, or group: certain dishes are unique to this restaurant
**3** [[without equal]] or like
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unparalleled]], [[unmatched]], [[unequalled]], [[matchless]], [[without equal]]
**4** (*informal*) remarkable
 **[[uniquely]]** *adv*
