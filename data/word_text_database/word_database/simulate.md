**simulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to [[pretend]] to feel or perform (an emotion or action); imitate: I tried to simulate anger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pretend]], [[act]], [[feign]], [[affect]], [[put on]], [[sham]]
**2** to imitate the conditions of (a situation), as in carrying out an experiment: we can then simulate global warming
**3** to have the appearance of: the wood had been painted to simulate stone
 **[[simulated]]** *adj* 
 **[[simulation]]** *n*
