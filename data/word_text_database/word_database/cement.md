**cement** *n*
**1**
**A** a fine grey powder made of limestone and clay, mixed with water and sand to make [[mortar]] or concrete
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mortar]], [[plaster]], [[paste]]
 **B** [[mortar]] or concrete
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mortar]], [[plaster]], [[paste]]
**2** something that unites, binds, or joins things or people: bone cement; the cement of fear and hatred of the Left
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sealant]], [[glue]], [[gum]], [[adhesive]]
**3** (*dentistry*) a material used for filling teeth
 *vb*
**4** to [[join]], bind, or cover with cement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick]], [[join]], [[bond]], [[attach]], [[seal]], [[glue]], [[plaster]], [[weld]]
**5** to make (a relationship) stronger: this would cement a firm alliance between the army and rebels
