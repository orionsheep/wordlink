**grudge** *n*
**1** a persistent feeling of [[resentment]] against a person who has caused harm or upset
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resentment]], [[bitterness]], [[grievance]], [[dislike]], [[animosity]], [[antipathy]], [[enmity]], rancour
 *vb* **grudging**, **[[grudged]]**
**2** to give unwillingly: the rich men who [[grudged]] pennies for poor people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resent]], [[mind]], [[envy]], [[covet]], [[begrudge]]
**3** to [[resent]] or [[envy]] the success or possessions of: none of their guests [[grudged]] them this celebration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resent]], [[mind]], [[envy]], [[covet]], [[begrudge]]
 *adj*
**4** planned or carried out in order to settle a grudge: a grudge match
