**aid** *n*
**1** money, equipment, or services provided for people in need; [[assistance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[help]], [[backing]], [[support]], [[benefit]], [[favour]], [[relief]], [[promotion]], [[assistance]]
**2** a person or device that helps or assists
 *vb*
**3** to [[help]] financially or in other ways
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[help]], [[support]], [[serve]], [[sustain]], [[assist]], [[avail]], [[subsidize]], [[be of service to]]
