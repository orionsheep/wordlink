**strait** *n*
**1** (*often pl*) a narrow [[channel]] of the sea linking two larger areas of sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[channel]], [[sound]], [[narrows]]
**2** **straits** a position of extreme [[difficulty]]: in desperate straits
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difficulty]], [[dilemma]], [[plight]], [[hardship]], [[uphill]] (*S African*), [[predicament]], [[extremity]]
