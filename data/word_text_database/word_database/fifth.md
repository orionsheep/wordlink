**fifth** *adj*
**1** of or being number five in a series
**2** denoting the fifth from lowest (usually the highest) forward gear in a motor vehicle
 *n*
**3** one of five equal parts of something
**4** (*music*) the interval between one note and the note three-and-a-half tones higher or lower than it
**5** the fifth from lowest (usually the highest) forward gear in a motor vehicle
