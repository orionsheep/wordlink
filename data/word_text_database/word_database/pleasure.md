**pleasure** *n*
**1** a feeling of [[happiness]] and contentment: the pleasure of hearing good music
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happiness]], [[delight]], [[satisfaction]], [[enjoyment]], [[bliss]], [[gratification]], [[gladness]], [[delectation]] (*formal*)
**2** something that gives [[enjoyment]]: his garden was his only pleasure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amusement]], [[joy]]
**3** the activity of enjoying oneself: business before pleasure
**4** (*euphemistic*) sexual [[gratification]]: he took his pleasure of her
**5** a person's preference
