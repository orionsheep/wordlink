**pitfall** *n*
**1** an unsuspected [[difficulty]] or [[danger]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[danger]], [[difficulty]], [[peril]], [[catch]] (*informal*), [[trap]], [[hazard]], [[drawback]], [[snag]], [[uphill]] (*S African*)
**2** a [[trap]] in the form of a concealed pit, designed to [[catch]] people or wild animals
