**halve** *vb* **halving**, **halved**
**1** to divide (something) into two equal parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[split in two]], [[cut in half]], [[bisect]], [[divide in two]], [[share equally]], [[divide equally]]
**2** to reduce (the size or amount of something) by half
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut in half]], [[reduce by fifty per cent]], [[decrease by fifty per cent]], [[lessen by fifty per cent]]
**3** (*golf*) to draw with one's opponent on (a hole or round)
