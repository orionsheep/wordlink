**proportion** *n*
**1** relative [[size]] or [[extent]]: a large proportion of our revenue comes from advertisements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[share]], [[amount]], [[division]], [[percentage]], [[segment]], [[quota]], [[fraction]]
**2** correct [[relationship]] between parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[balance]], [[harmony]], [[correspondence]], [[symmetry]], [[concord]], [[congruity]]
**3** a [[part]] considered with respect to the whole: the proportion of women in the total workforce
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relative amount]], [[relationship]], [[ratio]]
**4** **proportions** [[dimensions]] or [[size]]: a building of vast proportions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dimensions]], [[size]], [[volume]], [[capacity]], [[extent]], [[expanse]]
**5** (*maths*) a [[relationship]] between four numbers in which the [[ratio]] of the first pair equals the [[ratio]] of the second pair
**6** **in proportion**
**A** comparable in [[size]], rate of increase, etc.
 **B** without exaggerating
 *vb*
**7** to adjust in [[relative amount]] or [[size]]: the [[size]] of the crops are very rarely proportioned to the wants of the inhabitants
**8** to cause to be harmonious in [[relationship]] of parts
