**damper** *n*
**1** **put a damper on** to produce a depressing or inhibiting effect on
**2** a movable plate to regulate the draught in a stove or furnace
**3** the pad in a piano or harpsichord that deadens the vibration of each string as its key is released
**4** (*chiefly* *Austral** & **NZ*) any of various unleavened loaves and scones, typically cooked on an open fire
