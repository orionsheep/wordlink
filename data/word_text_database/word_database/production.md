**production** *n*
**1** the act of [[producing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[producing]], [[making]], [[manufacture]], [[manufacturing]], [[construction]], [[formation]], [[fabrication]]
**2** anything that is produced
**3** the amount produced or the rate at which it is produced
**4** (*econ*) the creation or [[manufacture]] of goods and services
**5** any work created as a result of literary or artistic effort
**6** the [[presentation]] of a play, opera, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[presentation]], [[staging]], [[mounting]]
**7** the artistic [[direction]] of a play
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[management]], [[administration]], [[direction]]
**8** the overall sound of a recording
