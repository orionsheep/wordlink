**Lord** *n*
**1** (*Christianity*) a title given to God or Jesus Christ
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the Lord or Our Lord]]**: Jesus Christ, God, Christ, Messiah, Jehovah, the Almighty
**2** (*Brit*) a title given to certain male peers
**3** (*Brit*) a title given to certain high officials and judges
 *interj*
**4** an exclamation of dismay or surprise: Good Lord!
