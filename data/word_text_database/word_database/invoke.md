**invoke** *vb* **-voking**, **-voked**
**1** to put (a law or penalty) into [[use]]: chapter 8 of the UN charter was invoked
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[apply]], [[use]], [[implement]], [[initiate]], [[resort to]], [[put into effect]]
**2** to bring about: the hills invoked a feeling of serenity
**3** to call on (God or another deity) for help, inspiration, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call upon]], [[appeal to]], [[pray to]], [[petition]], [[beseech]], [[entreat]], [[supplicate]]
**4** to summon (a spirit) by uttering magic words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call upon]], [[appeal to]], [[pray to]], [[petition]], [[beseech]], [[entreat]], [[supplicate]]
