**toy with** *vb*
**1** to consider an idea without being serious about it or being able to decide about it: I've been toying with the idea of setting up my own firm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[toy with something]]**: play with, consider, trifle with, dally with, entertain the possibility of, amuse yourself with, think idly of
**2** to keep moving (an object) about with one's fingers, esp. when thinking about something else: Jessica sat toying with her glass
