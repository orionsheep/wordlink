**web** *n*
**1** a mesh of fine tough threads built by a spider to trap insects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cobweb]], [[spider's web]]
**2** anything that is intricately formed or complex: a web of relationships
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tangle]], [[network]]
**3** a membrane connecting the toes of some water birds and water-dwelling animals such as frogs
**4** **the web** (*often cap*) short for **World Wide Web**
 *adj*
**5** of or situated on the World Wide Web: a web server; web pages
 **[[webbed]]** *adj*
