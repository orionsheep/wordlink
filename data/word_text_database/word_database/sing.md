**sing** *vb* **[[singing]]**, **sang**, **sung**
**1** to produce musical sounds with the voice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[croon]], [[carol]], [[chant]], [[warble]], [[yodel]], [[pipe]]
**2** to perform (a song)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[croon]], [[carol]], [[chant]], [[warble]], [[yodel]], [[pipe]]
**3** (of certain birds and insects) to make musical calls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trill]], [[chirp]], [[warble]]
**4** **sing of** to tell a story in song about: the minstrels sang of courtly love
**5** to make a humming, ringing, or whistling sound: the arrow sang past his ear
**6** (of one's ears) to be filled with a continuous ringing sound
**7** to bring (someone) to a given state by [[singing]]: I sang him to sleep
**8** (*slang*, *chiefly* *US*) to act as an informer
 **[[singer]]** *n* 
 **[[singing]]** *adj*, *n*
