**estate** *n*
**1** a large piece of landed [[property]], esp. in the country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lands]], [[property]], [[area]], [[grounds]], [[domain]], [[manor]], [[holdings]], [[homestead]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral*) a large [[area]] of land with houses or factories built on it: an industrial estate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[area]], [[centre]], [[park]], [[development]], [[site]], [[zone]], [[plot]]
**3** (*law*) [[property]] or [[possessions]], esp. of a deceased person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[property]], [[capital]], [[assets]], [[fortune]], [[goods]], [[effects]], [[wealth]], [[possessions]]
**4** (*history*) any of the orders or classes making up a society
