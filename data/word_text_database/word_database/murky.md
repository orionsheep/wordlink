**murky** *adj* **murkier**, **murkiest**
**1** [[gloomy]] or [[dark]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dark]], [[gloomy]], [[grey]], [[dull]], [[dim]], [[cloudy]], [[misty]], [[overcast]]
**2** [[cloudy]] or hard to see through: a murky stagnant pond
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dark]], [[cloudy]]
**3** obscure and suspicious; shady: murky goings-on; his murky past
 **[[murkily]]** *adv* 
 **[[murkiness]]** *n*
