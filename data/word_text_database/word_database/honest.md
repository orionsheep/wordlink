**honest** *adj*
**1** [[truthful]] and moral in behaviour; [[trustworthy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trustworthy]], [[upright]], [[ethical]], [[honourable]], [[reputable]], [[truthful]], [[virtuous]], [[law-abiding]]
**2** [[open]] and [[sincere]] in relationships and attitudes; without pretensions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[open]], [[direct]], [[frank]], [[plain]], [[sincere]], [[candid]], [[forthright]], [[upfront]] (*informal*)
**3** gained or earned fairly: an honest income
