**obtain** *vb*
**1** to [[gain]] possession of; [[get]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get]], [[gain]], [[acquire]], [[land]] (*informal*), [[net]], [[pick up]], [[secure]], [[procure]]
**2** (*formal*) to be customary or accepted: silence obtains from eight in the evening
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prevail]], [[hold]], [[exist]], [[be the case]], [[abound]], [[predominate]], [[be in force]], [[be current]]
 **[[obtainable]]** *adj*
