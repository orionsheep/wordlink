**team** *n*
**1** a [[group]] of players forming one of the sides in a sporting contest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[side]], [[squad]]
**2** a [[group]] of people organized to work together: a team of scientists
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[company]], [[set]], [[body]], [[band]], [[gang]], [[line-up]], [[bunch]] (*informal*)
**3** two or more animals working together: a sledge pulled by a team of dogs
 *vb*
**4** **team up with** to join with (someone) in order to work together
**5** **team with** to match (something) with something else: navy skirts teamed with various coloured blouses
