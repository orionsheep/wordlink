**divorce** *n*
**1** the legal ending of a marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separation]], [[split]], [[break-up]], [[parting]], [[split-up]], [[rift]], [[dissolution]], [[annulment]]
**2** a [[separation]], esp. one that is permanent
 *vb* **-vorcing**, **-vorced**
**3** to [[separate]] or be separated by divorce
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[split up]], [[separate]], [[part company]], [[dissolve your marriage]]
**4** to remove or [[separate]]
