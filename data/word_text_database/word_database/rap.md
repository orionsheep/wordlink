**rap** *vb* **rapping**, **rapped**
**1** to [[hit]] with a sharp quick [[blow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hit]], [[strike]], [[knock]], [[crack]] (*informal*), [[tap]]
**2** to [[knock]] loudly and sharply
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hit]], [[strike]], [[knock]], [[crack]] (*informal*), [[tap]]
**3** **rap out** to utter in sharp rapid speech: he rapped out his address
**4** to perform a rhythmic monologue with musical backing
**5** (*slang*) to talk in a relaxed and friendly way
**6** to [[rebuke]] or criticize sharply
**7** **rap over the knuckles** to reprimand
 *n*
**8** a sharp quick [[blow]] or the sound produced by it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow]], [[knock]], [[crack]] (*informal*), [[tap]], [[clout]] (*informal*)
**9** a fast rhythmic monologue over a musical backing
**10** a sharp [[rebuke]] or criticism
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rebuke]], [[blame]], [[responsibility]], [[punishment]]
**11** (*slang*) a legal charge: a murder rap
