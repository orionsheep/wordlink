**lounge** *n*
**1** a [[living room]] in a private house
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sitting room]] (*Brit*), [[living room]], [[parlour]] (*old-fashioned*), [[drawing room]], [[front room]], [[reception room]], [[television room]]
**2** same as **lounge bar**
**3** a communal room in a hotel, ship, or airport, used for waiting or relaxing in
**4** the act of lounging
 *vb* **lounging**, **lounged**
**5** (often foll. by *about* or *around*) to sit or lie in a relaxed manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relax]], [[loaf]], [[sprawl]], [[lie about]], [[take it easy]], [[loiter]], [[loll]], [[laze]], [[outspan]] (*S African*)
**6** to pass time lazily or idly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relax]], [[loaf]], [[sprawl]], [[lie about]], [[take it easy]], [[loiter]], [[loll]], [[laze]], [[outspan]] (*S African*)
