**[[look up]]** *vb*
**1** to discover or confirm by checking in a reference book
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[look something up]]**: research, find, search for, hunt for, track down, seek out
**2** to improve: things were looking up
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[look up]]**: improve, develop, advance, pick up, progress, get better, shape up (*informal*), perk up
**3** **[[look up]] to** to have respect for: she looked up to him as a kind of father
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[look up to someone]]**: respect, honour, admire, esteem, revere, defer to, think highly of
**4** to visit (a person): I'll look you up when I'm in town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[look someone up]]**: visit, call on, drop in on (*informal*), look in on
