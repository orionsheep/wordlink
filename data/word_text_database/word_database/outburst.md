**outburst** *n*
**1** a sudden strong expression of emotion, esp. of anger: such emotional outbursts do nothing to help calm discussion of the matter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explosion]], [[surge]], [[outbreak]], [[eruption]], [[flare-up]]
**2** a sudden period of violent activity: this sudden outburst of violence has come as a shock
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fit]], [[flare-up]], [[eruption]], [[spasm]], [[outpouring]]
