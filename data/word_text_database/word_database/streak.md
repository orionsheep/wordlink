**streak** *n*
**1** a long thin stripe or [[trace]] of some contrasting colour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[touch]], [[element]], [[strain]], [[dash]], [[vein]]
**2** (of lightning) a sudden [[flash]]
**3** a quality or characteristic: a nasty streak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[touch]], [[element]], [[strain]], [[dash]], [[vein]]
**4** a short stretch of good or bad luck: a losing streak
**5** (*informal*) an instance of running naked through a public place
 *vb*
**6** to mark (something) with a streak or streaks: sweat streaking the grime of his face
**7** to move quickly in a straight line
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speed]], [[fly]], [[tear]], [[flash]], [[sprint]], [[dart]], [[zoom]], [[whizz]] (*informal*)
