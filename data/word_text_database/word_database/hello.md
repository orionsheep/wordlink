**hello** * or ***hallo** * or ***hullo** *interj*
**1** an expression of greeting or surprise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hi]] (*informal*), [[greetings]], how do you do?, [[good morning]], [[good evening]], [[good afternoon]], [[welcome]], [[kia ora]] (*NZ*), gidday *or* g'day (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**2** a call used to attract attention
 *n*, *pl* **-los**
**3** the act of saying 'hello'
