**dwarf** *vb*
**1** to cause (someone or something) to seem [[small]] by being much larger
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: tower above *or* over, [[dominate]], [[overlook]], [[stand over]], [[loom over]], stand head and shoulders above
 *adj*
**2** (of an animal or plant) much below the average size for the species: a dwarf evergreen shrub
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[miniature]], [[small]], [[baby]], [[tiny]], [[diminutive]], [[bonsai]], undersized
 *n*, *pl* **dwarfs*** or ***dwarves**
**3** a person who is smaller than average size as a result of a medical condition
**4** (in folklore) a [[small]] ugly manlike creature, often possessing magical powers
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[gnome]], [[midget]], [[Lilliputian]], [[Tom Thumb]], pygmy *or* pigmy
