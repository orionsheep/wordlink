**overcome** *vb* **-coming**, **-came**, **-come**
**1** to deal successfully with or control (a problem or feeling): once I'd overcome my initial nerves I discovered hang-gliding was great fun
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conquer]], [[beat]], [[master]], [[subdue]], [[triumph over]], [[vanquish]] (*literary*)
**2** (of an emotion or a feeling) to affect (someone) strongly or make (someone) powerless: he was overcome by a sudden surge of jealousy
**3** to [[defeat]] (someone) in a conflict
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defeat]], [[beat]], [[conquer]], [[master]], [[overwhelm]], [[subdue]], [[rout]], [[overpower]]
