**prevent** *vb*
**1** to keep from happening: vitamin C prevented scurvy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[avoid]], [[frustrate]], [[hamper]], [[foil]], [[inhibit]], [[avert]], [[thwart]]
**2** (often foll. by *from*) to keep (someone from doing something): circumstances prevented her from coming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[avoid]], [[frustrate]], [[hamper]], [[foil]], [[inhibit]], [[avert]], [[thwart]]
 **[[preventable]]** *adj* 
 **[[prevention]]** *n*
