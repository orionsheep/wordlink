**legend** *n*
**1** a popular [[story]] handed down from earlier times which may or may not be true
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[myth]], [[story]], [[tale]], [[fiction]], [[saga]], [[fable]], [[folk tale]], [[folk story]]
**2** such stories collectively
**3** a person whose fame makes him or her seem exceptional: he is a living legend
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[celebrity]], [[star]], [[phenomenon]], [[genius]], [[prodigy]], [[luminary]], [[megastar]] (*informal*)
**4** modern stories about a famous person which may or may not be true: no Garland fan could complain about sordid revelations tarnishing the legend
**5** words written on something to explain it: a pub mirror spelling out the legend 'Saloon Bar'
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inscription]], [[title]], [[caption]], [[device]], [[motto]], [[rubric]]
**6** an explanation on a table, map, or chart, of the symbols used
