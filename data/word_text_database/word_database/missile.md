**missile** *n*
**1** a [[rocket]] with an exploding warhead, used as a [[weapon]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[projectile]], [[weapon]], [[shell]], [[rocket]]
**2** an object or [[weapon]] that is thrown, launched, or fired at a target
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[projectile]], [[weapon]], [[shell]], [[rocket]]
