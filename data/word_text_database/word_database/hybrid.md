**hybrid** *n*
**1** an animal or plant resulting from a [[cross]] between two different types of animal or plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crossbreed]], [[cross]], [[mixture]], [[compound]], [[composite]], [[amalgam]], [[mongrel]], [[half-breed]]
**2** a vehicle that is powered by an internal-combustion engine and another source of power
**3** anything that is a [[mixture]] of two different things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mixture]], [[compound]], [[composite]], [[amalgam]]
 *adj*
**4** of mixed origin
**5** (of a vehicle) powered by an internal-combustion engine and another source of power
