**merry** *adj* **-rier**, **-riest**
**1** [[cheerful]] and [[jolly]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cheerful]], [[happy]], [[carefree]], [[jolly]], [[festive]], [[joyous]], [[convivial]], [[blithe]] (*archaic*)
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral* *informal*) slightly drunk
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tipsy]], [[happy]], [[mellow]], tiddly (*rare*, *chiefly* *Brit*), squiffy (*Brit* *informal*, *old-fashioned*)
**3** **make merry** to take part in noisy [[cheerful]] celebrations or fun
 **[[merrily]]** *adv* 
 **[[merriment]]** *n*
