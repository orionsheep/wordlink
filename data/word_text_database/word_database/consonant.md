**consonant** *n*
**1**
**A** a speech sound made by partially or completely blocking the breath streams, for example *b* or *f*
 **B** a letter representing this
 *adj*
**2** **consonant with** in keeping or agreement with: an individualistic style of religion, more consonant with liberal society
**3** harmonious: this highly dissonant chord is followed by a more consonant one
