**seethe** *vb* **[[seething]]**, **seethed**
**1** to be in a state of extreme anger or indignation without publicly showing these feelings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be furious]], [[rage]], [[fume]], [[simmer]], [[see red]] (*informal*), [[be livid]], [[go ballistic]] (*slang*)
**2** (of a liquid) to [[boil]] or [[foam]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boil]], [[bubble]], [[foam]], [[fizz]], [[froth]]
 **[[seething]]** *adj*
