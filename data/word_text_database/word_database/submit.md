**submit** *vb* **-mitting**, **-mitted**
**1** to accept the will of another person or a superior force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surrender]], [[yield]], [[give in]], [[agree]], [[endure]], [[tolerate]], [[comply]], [[succumb]]
**2** to send (an application or proposal) to someone for judgment or consideration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[present]], [[hand in]], [[tender]], [[put forward]], [[table]] (*Brit*), [[proffer]]
**3** to be voluntarily subjected (to medical or psychiatric treatment)
