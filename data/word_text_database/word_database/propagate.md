**propagate** *vb* **-gating**, **-gated**
**1** to spread (information or ideas)
**2** (*biol*) to reproduce or breed
**3** (*horticulture*) to produce (plants)
**4** (*physics*) to transmit, esp. in the form of a wave: the electrical signal is propagated through a specialized group of conducting fibres
 **[[propagation]]** *n* 
 **[[propagator]]** *n*
