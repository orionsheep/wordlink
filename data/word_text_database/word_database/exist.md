**exist** *vb*
**1** to have being or reality; [[be]]: does God exist?
**2** to only just [[be]] able to keep oneself alive, esp. because of poverty or hunger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[survive]], [[stay alive]], [[make ends meet]], [[subsist]], [[eke out a living]], [[scrape by]], [[scrimp and save]], [[support yourself]]
**3** to [[be]] living; [[live]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[live]], [[be present]], [[survive]], [[endure]], [[be in existence]], [[be]], [[have breath]]
**4** to [[be present]] under specified conditions or in a specified place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occur]], [[be present]]
 **[[existing]]** *adj*
