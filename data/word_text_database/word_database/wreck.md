**wreck** *vb*
**1** to [[break]], [[spoil]], or [[destroy]] completely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destroy]], [[break]], [[smash]], [[ruin]], [[devastate]], [[shatter]], [[spoil]], [[demolish]], [[kennet]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[jeff]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** to cause the accidental sinking or destruction of (a ship) at sea
 *n*
**3** something that has been destroyed or badly damaged, such as a crashed car or aircraft
**4** a ship that has been sunk or destroyed at sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shipwreck]], [[hulk]]
**5** a person in a poor mental or physical state
