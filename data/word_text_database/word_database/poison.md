**poison** *n*
**1** a substance that causes death or injury when swallowed or absorbed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[toxin]], [[venom]], [[bane]] (*archaic*)
**2** something that destroys or corrupts: the poison of Nazism
 *vb*
**3** to give poison to someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give someone poison]], [[murder]], [[kill]], [[administer poison to]]
**4** to add poison to (something)
**5** to have a harmful or evil effect on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contaminate]], [[foul]], [[infect]], [[spoil]], [[pollute]], [[blight]], [[taint]], [[befoul]]
**6** (foll. by *against*) to turn (a person's mind) against: he poisoned her mind against me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corrupt]], [[colour]], [[undermine]], [[bias]], [[sour]], [[pervert]], [[warp]], [[taint]]
 **[[poisoner]]** *n*
