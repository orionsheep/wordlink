**trigger** *n*
**1** a small lever that releases a catch on a gun or machine
**2** any event that sets a course of action in motion: his murder was the trigger for a night of rioting
 *vb*
**3** Also: **trigger off** to set (an action or process) in motion: various factors can trigger off a migraine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring about]], [[start]], [[cause]], [[produce]], [[generate]], [[prompt]], [[provoke]], [[set off]]
