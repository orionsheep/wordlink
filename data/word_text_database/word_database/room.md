**room** *n*
**1** an [[area]] within a building enclosed by a floor, a ceiling, and walls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chamber]], [[office]]
**2** the people present in a room: the whole room was laughing
**3** unoccupied or unobstructed [[space]]: there wasn't enough room
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[space]], [[area]], [[capacity]], [[extent]], [[expanse]]
**4** **room for** opportunity or scope for: there was no room for acts of heroism
**5** **rooms** lodgings
 *vb*
**6** (*US*) to occupy or share a rented room: I roomed with him for five years
