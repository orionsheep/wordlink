**erosion** *n*
**1** the wearing away of rocks or soil by the action of water, ice, or wind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disintegration]], [[deterioration]], wearing down *or* away, [[grinding down]]
**2** a gradual lessening or reduction: an erosion of national sovereignty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deterioration]], [[undermining]], [[destruction]], [[weakening]], [[attrition]], [[eating away]], [[abrasion]], [[grinding down]]
 **[[erosive]]** * or ***[[erosional]]** *adj*
