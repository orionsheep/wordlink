**structure** *n*
**1** something that has been built or organized
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[building]], [[construction]], [[erection]], [[edifice]]
**2** the way the individual parts of something are made, built, or organized into a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrangement]], [[form]], [[make-up]], [[design]], [[organization]], [[construction]], [[formation]], [[configuration]]
**3** the pattern of interrelationships within an [[organization]], society, etc.
**4** an organized method of working, thinking, or behaving
**5** (*chem*) the [[arrangement]] of atoms in a molecule of a chemical compound
**6** (*geol*) the way in which a rock is made up of its component parts
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**7** to [[arrange]] (something) into an organized system or pattern: a structured school curriculum
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrange]], [[organize]], [[design]], [[shape]], [[build up]], [[assemble]]
