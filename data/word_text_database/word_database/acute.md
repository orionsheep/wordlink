**acute** *adj*
**1** [[severe]] or intense: acute staff shortages
**2** penetrating in perception or insight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perceptive]], [[sharp]], [[keen]], [[smart]], [[sensitive]], [[clever]], [[astute]], [[insightful]]
**3** [[sensitive]] or [[keen]]: it was amazing how acute your hearing got in the bush
**4** (of a disease) sudden and [[severe]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serious]], [[important]], [[dangerous]], [[critical]], [[crucial]] (*informal*), [[severe]], [[grave]], [[urgent]]
**5** (*maths*) (of an angle) of less than 90°
 *n*
**6** an acute accent
 **[[acutely]]** *adv* 
 **[[acuteness]]** *n*
