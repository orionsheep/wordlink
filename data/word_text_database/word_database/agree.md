**agree** *vb* **agreeing**, **agreed**
**1** to [[be of the same opinion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[concur]], [[be as one]], [[sympathize]], [[assent]], [[see eye to eye]], [[be of the same opinion]]
**2** to give [[assent]]; consent
**3** to be consistent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[correspond]], [[match]], [[coincide]], [[tally]], [[conform]]
**4** **agree on** to reach a joint decision about: the ministers agreed on a strategy
**5** **agree with** to be agreeable or suitable to (one's health or appearance): you look good – your new job must agree with you
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[agree with someone]]**: suit, get on with, befit
**6** to concede: they agreed that the price they were asking was too high
**7** (*grammar*) to be the same in number, gender, and case as a connected word
