**crusade** *n*
**1** any of the medieval military expeditions undertaken by European Christians to recapture the Holy Land from the Muslims
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holy war]]
**2** a vigorous [[campaign]] in favour of a [[cause]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[campaign]], [[drive]], [[movement]], [[cause]], [[push]] (*informal*)
 *vb* **-sading**, **-saded**
**3** to take part in a crusade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[campaign]], [[fight]], [[push]], [[struggle]], [[lobby]], [[agitate]], [[work]]
 **[[crusader]]** *n*
