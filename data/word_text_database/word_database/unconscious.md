**unconscious** *adj*
**1** unable to notice or respond to things which one would normally be aware of through the senses; insensible or comatose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[senseless]], [[knocked out]], [[out cold]] (*informal*), [[out]], [[stunned]], [[dazed]], [[in a coma]], [[stupefied]]
**2** not aware of one's actions or behaviour: unconscious of his failure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unaware]], [[ignorant]], [[oblivious]], [[unknowing]]
**3** not realized or intended: unconscious duplicity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unintentional]], [[unwitting]], [[inadvertent]], [[accidental]]
**4** coming from or produced by the unconscious: unconscious mental processes
 *n*
**5** (*psychoanalysis*) the part of the mind containing instincts, impulses, and ideas that are not available for direct examination
 **[[unconsciously]]** *adv* 
 **[[unconsciousness]]** *n*
