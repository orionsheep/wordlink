**overdue** *adj*
**1** not having arrived or happened by the time expected or desired: a reassessment of policy on this issue is long overdue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delayed]], [[belated]], [[late]], [[behind schedule]], [[tardy]], [[unpunctual]], [[behindhand]]
**2** (of money) not having been paid by the required date: by this time his rent was three weeks overdue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unpaid]], [[owing]]
**3** (of a library book) not having been returned to the library by the required date
