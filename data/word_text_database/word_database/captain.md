**captain** *n*
**1** the person in charge of a ship, boat, or civil aircraft
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commander]], [[skipper]]
**2** a middle-ranking naval officer
**3** a junior officer in the army
**4** the [[leader]] of a team or group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leader]], [[boss]], [[master]], [[skipper]], [[head]], chief
 *vb*
**5** to be captain of
 **[[captaincy]]** *n*
