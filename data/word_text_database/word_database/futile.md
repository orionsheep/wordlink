**futile** (**fyew**-tile) *adj*
**1** [[useless]] or having no chance of success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[useless]], [[vain]], [[unsuccessful]], [[pointless]], [[worthless]], [[fruitless]], [[ineffectual]], [[unprofitable]]
**2** foolish and of no value: her futile remarks began to annoy me
 **[[futility]]** *n*
