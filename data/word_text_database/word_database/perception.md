**perception** *n*
**1** the act of perceiving
**2** [[insight]] or intuition: his acute perception of other people's emotions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[understanding]], [[intelligence]], [[observation]], [[discrimination]], [[insight]], [[sharpness]], [[cleverness]], [[keenness]]
**3** the ability to perceive
**4** way of viewing: advertising affects the customer's perception of a product
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[awareness]], [[understanding]], [[sense]], [[impression]], [[feeling]], [[idea]], [[notion]], [[consciousness]]
 **[[perceptual]]** *adj*
