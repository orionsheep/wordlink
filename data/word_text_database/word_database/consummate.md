**consummate** *vb* (**kon**-sum-mate) **-mating**, **-mated**
**1** to make (a marriage) legal by sexual intercourse
**2** to complete or fulfil
 *adj* (kon-**sum**-mit)
**3** supremely skilled: a consummate craftsman
**4** complete or extreme: consummate skill; consummate ignorance
 **[[consummation]]** *n*
