**rob** *vb* **robbing**, **robbed**
**1** to take something from (a person or place) illegally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steal from]], [[hold up]], [[mug]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[deprive]], esp. of something deserved: I can't forgive him for robbing me of an Olympic gold
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deprive]], [[do out of]] (*informal*) 
 **[[robber]]** *n*
