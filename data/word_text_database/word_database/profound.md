**profound** *adj*
**1** showing or needing [[great]] knowledge: a profound knowledge of Greek literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wise]], [[learned]], [[deep]], [[penetrating]], [[philosophical]], [[sage]], [[abstruse]], [[sagacious]] (*formal*)
**2** strongly felt; [[intense]]: profound relief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sincere]], [[acute]], [[intense]], [[great]], [[keen]], [[extreme]], [[heartfelt]], [[deeply felt]]
**3** extensive: profound changes
**4** situated at or having a [[great]] depth
 **[[profoundly]]** *adv* 
 **[[profundity]]** *n*
