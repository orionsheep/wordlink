**monster** *n*
**1** an imaginary [[beast]], usually frightening in appearance
**2** a very large person, animal, or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[giant]], [[mammoth]], [[titan]], [[colossus]], [[monstrosity]]
**3** an exceptionally cruel or wicked person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brute]], [[devil]], [[beast]], [[demon]], [[villain]], [[fiend]]
**4** a person, animal, or plant with a marked deformity
