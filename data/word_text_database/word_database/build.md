**build** *vb* **building**, **built**
**1** to [[make]] or [[construct]] by joining parts or materials: more than 100 bypasses have been built in the past decade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[construct]], [[make]], [[raise]], [[put up]], [[assemble]], [[erect]], [[fabricate]], [[form]]
**2** to establish and develop: it took ten years to build the business
**3** to [[make]] in a particular way or for a particular purpose: she's built for speed, not stamina
**4** (often foll. by *up*) to increase in intensity
 *n*
**5** physical [[form]], [[figure]], or proportions: he has an athletic build
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physique]], [[form]], [[body]], [[figure]], [[shape]], [[structure]], [[frame]]
