**suggest** *vb*
**1** to put forward (a plan or an idea) for consideration: he didn't suggest a meeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recommend]], [[propose]], [[advise]], [[advocate]], [[prescribe]]
**2** to bring (a person or thing) to the mind by the association of ideas: a man whose very name suggests blandness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring to mind]], [[evoke]]
**3** to give a hint of: her grey eyes suggesting a livelier mood than usual
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indicate]]
