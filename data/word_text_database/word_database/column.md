**column** *n*
**1** an [[upright]] [[pillar]] usually having a cylindrical [[shaft]], a base, and a capital
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pillar]], [[support]], [[post]], [[shaft]], [[upright]], [[obelisk]]
**2** a form or structure in the shape of a column: a column of smoke
**3** a vertical division of a page of text, esp. in a newspaper or dictionary
**4** a regular feature in a paper: a cookery column
**5** a vertical arrangement of numbers
**6** (*military*) a narrow formation in which individuals or units follow one behind the other
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[line]], [[row]], [[file]], [[rank]], [[procession]], [[cavalcade]]
 **[[columnar]]** *adj*
