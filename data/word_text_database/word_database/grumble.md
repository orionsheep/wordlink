◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complain]], [[moan]] (*informal*), [[gripe]] (*informal*), [[whinge]] (*informal*), [[carp]], [[whine]], [[grouse]], [[bleat]]
**2** to make low rumbling sounds: the storm grumbled in the distance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rumble]], [[growl]], [[gurgle]]
 *n*
**3** a [[complaint]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complaint]], [[protest]], [[objection]], [[moan]] (*informal*), [[grievance]], [[grouse]], [[gripe]] (*informal*), [[grouch]] (*informal*)
