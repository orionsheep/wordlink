**what** *pron*
**1** used in requesting further information about the identity or categorization of something: what was he wearing?; I knew what would happen
**2** the person, thing, people, or things that: all was not what it seemed
**3** used in exclamations to add emphasis: what a creep!
**4** **what for?** for what reason?
**5** **what have you** other similar or related things: qualifications, interests, profession, what have you
 *adj*
**6** used with a noun in requesting further information about the identity or categorization of something: what difference can it make now?
**7** to any degree or in any amount: they provided what financial support they could
