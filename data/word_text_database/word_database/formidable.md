**formidable** *adj*
**1** frightening because very difficult to deal with or overcome: the Finnish winter presents formidable problems to drivers
**2** extremely [[impressive]]: a formidable Juventus squad
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impressive]], [[great]] (*informal*), [[powerful]], [[tremendous]], [[mighty]], [[terrific]], [[awesome]], [[invincible]]
 **[[formidably]]** *adv*
