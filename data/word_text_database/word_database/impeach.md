**impeach** *vb*
**1** (*chiefly* *US*) to charge (a public official) with an offence committed in office
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral*, *criminal law*) to accuse of treason or serious crime
**3** to challenge or question (a person's honesty or honour)
 **[[impeachable]]** *adj* 
 **[[impeachment]]** *n*
