**provocation** *n*
**1** the act of provoking or inciting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cause]], [[reason]], [[grounds]], [[motivation]], [[stimulus]], [[incitement]]
**2** something that causes indignation or anger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offence]], [[challenge]], [[insult]], [[taunt]], [[injury]], [[dare]], [[grievance]], [[annoyance]]
