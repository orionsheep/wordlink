**foam** *n*
**1** a mass of small [[bubbles]] of gas formed on the surface of a liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[froth]], [[spray]], [[bubbles]], [[lather]], [[suds]], [[spume]], [[head]]
**2** frothy saliva
**3** a light spongelike solid used for insulation, packing, etc.
 *vb*
**4** to produce or cause to produce foam
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bubble]], [[boil]], [[fizz]], [[froth]], [[lather]], [[effervesce]]
**5** **foam at the mouth** to be very angry
 **[[foamy]]** *adj*
