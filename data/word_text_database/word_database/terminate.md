**terminate** *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**1** to bring or come to an [[end]]: his flying career was terminated by this crash
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cease]], [[end]], [[close]], [[finish]]
**2** to put an [[end]] to (a pregnancy) by inducing an abortion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abort]], [[end]]
**3** (of the route of a train, bus, etc.) to stop at a particular place and not go any further: this train terminates at Leicester
 **[[termination]]** *n*
