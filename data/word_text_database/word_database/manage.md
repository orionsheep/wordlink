**manage** *vb* **-naging**, **-naged**
**1** to [[succeed]] in doing something: we finally managed to sell our old house
**2** to [[be in charge of]]; [[administer]]: the company is badly managed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be in charge of]], [[run]], [[handle]], [[direct]], [[conduct]], [[command]], [[administer]], [[supervise]]
**3** to have room or time for: can you manage lunch tomorrow?
**4** to keep under [[control]]: she disapproved of taking drugs to manage stress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[handle]], [[manipulate]]
**5** to struggle on despite difficulties, esp. financial ones: most people cannot manage on a cleaner's salary
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cope]], [[survive]], [[succeed]], [[carry on]], [[make do]], [[get by]] (*informal*), [[muddle through]]
 **[[manageable]]** *adj*
