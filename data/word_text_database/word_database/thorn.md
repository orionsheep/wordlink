**thorn** *n*
**1** a sharp pointed woody projection from a stem or leaf
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prickle]], [[spike]], [[spine]], [[barb]]
**2** any of various trees or shrubs having thorns, esp. the hawthorn
**3** **a thorn in one's side *or* flesh** a source of irritation: he was sufficiently bright at school to become a thorn in the side of his maths teacher
 **[[thornless]]** *adj*
