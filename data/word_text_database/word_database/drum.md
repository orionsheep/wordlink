**drum** *n*
**1** a percussion instrument sounded by striking a skin stretched across the opening of a hollow cylinder
**2** the sound produced by a drum
**3** an object shaped like a drum: an oil drum
**4** same as **eardrum**
 *vb* **drumming**, **drummed**
**5** to play (music) on a drum
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pound]], [[beat]], [[tap]], [[rap]], [[thrash]], [[tattoo]], [[throb]], [[pulsate]]
**6** to [[tap]] rhythmically or regularly: he drummed his fingers on the desk
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pound]], [[beat]], [[tap]], [[rap]], [[thrash]], [[tattoo]], [[throb]], [[pulsate]]
**7** to fix in someone's mind by constant repetition: my father always drummed into us how privileged we were
 See also **drum up**
