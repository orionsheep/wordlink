**[[catch on]]** *vb* (*informal*)
**1** to become popular or fashionable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[catch on]]**: become popular, take off, become trendy, come into fashion
**2** to understand: I was slow to [[catch on]] to what she was trying to tell me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[catch on]]**: understand, see, find out, grasp, see through, comprehend, twig (*Brit* *informal*), get the picture
