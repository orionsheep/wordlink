**fee** *n*
**1** a [[charge]] paid to be allowed to do something: many people resent the licence fee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]], [[price]], [[cost]], [[bill]], [[payment]], [[wage]], [[salary]], [[toll]]
**2** a [[payment]] asked by professional people for their services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]], [[price]], [[cost]], [[bill]], [[payment]], [[wage]], [[salary]], [[toll]]
**3** (*property law*) an interest in land that can be inherited. The interest can be with unrestricted rights (**fee simple**) or restricted (**fee tail**)
