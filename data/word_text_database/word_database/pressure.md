**pressure** *n*
**1** the state of pressing or being pressed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[crushing]], [[squeezing]], [[compressing]], [[weight]], [[compression]]
**2** the application of [[force]] by one body on the surface of another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[crushing]], [[squeezing]], [[compressing]], [[weight]], [[compression]]
**3** urgent claims or [[demands]]: to work under pressure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stress]], [[demands]], [[strain]], [[heat]], [[load]], [[burden]], [[urgency]], [[hassle]] (*informal*), [[uphill]] (*S African*)
**4** a condition that is hard to bear: the pressure of grief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[power]], [[influence]], [[force]], [[constraint]], [[sway]], [[compulsion]], [[coercion]]
**5** (*physics*) the [[force]] applied to a unit area of a surface
**6** **bring pressure to bear on** to use [[influence]] or authority to persuade
 *vb* **-suring**, **-sured**
**7** to persuade forcefully: he was pressured into resignation
