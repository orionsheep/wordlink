**accessory** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** a supplementary part or object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extra]], [[addition]], [[supplement]], [[attachment]], [[adjunct]], [[appendage]]
**2** a small item, such as a bag or belt, worn or carried by someone to complete his or her outfit
**3** a person who is involved in a crime but who was not present when it took place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accomplice]], [[partner]], [[ally]], [[associate]], [[assistant]], [[helper]], [[colleague]], [[collaborator]]
