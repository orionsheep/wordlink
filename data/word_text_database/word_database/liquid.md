**liquid** *n*
**1** a substance in a physical state which can change shape but not size
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fluid]], [[solution]], [[juice]], sap
 *adj*
**2** of or being a liquid: liquid medicines
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fluid]], [[running]], [[flowing]], [[melted]], [[watery]], [[molten]], [[runny]], [[aqueous]]
**3** shining and clear: liquid sunlight days
**4** [[flowing]], fluent, or smooth
**5** (of assets) in the form of money or easily [[convertible]] into money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[convertible]], [[disposable]], [[negotiable]], [[realizable]]
