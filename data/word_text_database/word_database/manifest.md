**manifest** *adj*
**1** easily noticed, [[obvious]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obvious]], [[apparent]], [[patent]], [[evident]], [[clear]], [[glaring]], [[noticeable]], blatant
 *vb*
**2** to [[reveal]] or [[display]]: an additional symptom now manifested itself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[show]], [[reveal]], [[express]], [[demonstrate]], [[expose]], [[exhibit]]
**3** to [[show]] by the way one behaves: he manifested great personal bravery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[show]], [[reveal]], [[express]], [[demonstrate]], [[expose]], [[exhibit]]
**4** (of a disembodied spirit) to appear in visible form
 *n*
**5** a customs document containing particulars of a ship and its cargo
**6** a list of the cargo and passengers on an aeroplane
 **[[manifestation]]** *n*
