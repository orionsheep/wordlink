**anatomy** *n*, *pl* **-mies**
**1** the science of the physical [[structure]] of animals and plants
**2** the [[structure]] of an animal or plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[build]], [[make-up]], [[frame]], [[framework]], [[composition]]
**3** (*informal*) a person's body: the male anatomy
**4** a detailed [[analysis]]: an anatomy of the massacre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examination]], [[study]], [[division]], [[inquiry]], [[investigation]], [[analysis]], [[dissection]]
 **[[anatomical]]** *adj* 
 **[[anatomically]]** *adv*
