**decorate** *vb* **-rating**, **-rated**
**1** to make more attractive by adding some [[ornament]] or colour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adorn]], [[trim]], [[embroider]], [[ornament]], [[embellish]], [[festoon]], [[beautify]], [[grace]]
**2** to [[paint]] or [[wallpaper]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[do up]], [[paper]], [[paint]], [[wallpaper]], [[renovate]] (*informal*), [[furbish]]
**3** to confer a mark of distinction, esp. a medal, upon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pin a medal on]], [[cite]], confer an honour on *or* upon
 **[[decorative]]** *adj* 
 **[[decorator]]** *n*
