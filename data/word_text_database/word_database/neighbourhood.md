**neighbourhood** * or * (*US*) **neighborhood** *n*
**1** a [[district]] where people live
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[district]], [[community]], [[quarter]], [[region]], [[locality]], [[locale]]
**2** the immediate environment; surroundings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vicinity]], [[environs]]
**3** the people in a [[district]]
**4** **in the neighbourhood of** approximately
 *adj*
**5** in and for a [[district]]: our neighbourhood cinema
