◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promote]], [[raise]], [[advance]], [[boost]], [[move up]], [[elevate]], [[kick upstairs]] (*informal*), [[give promotion to]]
**2** to [[raise]] in value, importance, or esteem
