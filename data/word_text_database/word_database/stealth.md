**2** cunning or underhand behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secrecy]], [[furtiveness]], [[slyness]], [[sneakiness]], [[unobtrusiveness]], [[stealthiness]], [[surreptitiousness]]
 *adj*
**3** (of technology) able to render an aircraft almost invisible to radar
**4** disguised or hidden
