**bogey** * or ***bogy** *n*
**1** an evil or mischievous spirit
**2** something that worries or annoys
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bugbear]], [[bête noire]], [[horror]], [[nightmare]], [[bugaboo]]
**3** (*golf*) a score of one stroke over par on a hole
**4** (*slang*) a piece of dried mucus from the nose
