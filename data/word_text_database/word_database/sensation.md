**sensation** *n*
**1** the power of [[feeling]] things physically: I lose all sensation in my hands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[sense]], [[impression]], [[perception]], [[awareness]], [[consciousness]]
**2** a physical [[feeling]]: a burning sensation in the throat
**3** a general [[feeling]] or [[awareness]]: a sensation of vague resentment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[sense]], [[impression]], [[perception]], [[awareness]], [[consciousness]]
**4** a state of [[excitement]]: imagine the sensation in Washington!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excitement]], [[thrill]], [[stir]], [[furore]], [[commotion]]
**5** an exciting person or thing: you'll be a sensation
