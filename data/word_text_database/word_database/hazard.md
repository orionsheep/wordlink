**hazard** *n*
**1** a thing likely to cause injury, loss, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[danger]], [[risk]], [[threat]], [[problem]], [[menace]], [[peril]], [[jeopardy]], [[pitfall]]
**2** [[risk]] or likelihood of injury, loss, etc.: evaluate the level of hazard in a situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[danger]], [[risk]], [[threat]], [[problem]], [[menace]], [[peril]], [[jeopardy]], [[pitfall]]
**3** (*golf*) an obstacle such as a bunker
**4** **at hazard** at [[risk]]
 *vb*
**5** to [[risk]]: hazarding the health of his crew
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jeopardize]], [[risk]], [[endanger]], [[threaten]], [[expose]], [[imperil]], [[put in jeopardy]]
**6** **[[hazard a guess]]** to make a guess
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hazard a guess]]**: guess, conjecture, presume, take a guess
