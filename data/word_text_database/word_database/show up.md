**show up** *vb*
**1** to reveal or be revealed clearly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[show something up]]**: reveal, expose, highlight, lay bare
**2** to expose the faults or defects of (someone or something) by comparison
**3** (*informal*) to put (someone) to shame; embarrass
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[show someone up]]**: embarrass, let down, mortify, put to shame
**4** (*informal*) to arrive
