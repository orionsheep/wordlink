**dreadful** *adj*
**1** extremely disagreeable or [[shocking]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terrible]], [[shocking]] (*informal*), [[awful]], [[appalling]], [[horrible]], [[fearful]] (*informal*), [[hideous]], [[atrocious]]
**2** extreme: there were dreadful delays
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serious]], [[terrible]], [[awful]], [[horrendous]], [[monstrous]], [[abysmal]]
 **[[dreadfully]]** *adv*
