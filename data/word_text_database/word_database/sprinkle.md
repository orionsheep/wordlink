**sprinkle** *vb* **-kling**, **-kled**
**1** to [[scatter]] (liquid or [[powder]]) in tiny drops over (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scatter]], [[dust]], [[strew]], [[pepper]], [[shower]], [[spray]], [[powder]], [[dredge]]
**2** to distribute over (something): a dozen mud huts sprinkled around it
 **[[sprinkler]]** *n*
