**shuffle** *vb* **-fling**, **-fled**
**1** to walk or move (the feet) with a slow dragging motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shamble]], [[stagger]], [[stumble]], [[dodder]]
**2** to [[mix]] together in a jumbled mass: the newsreader shuffled his papers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rearrange]], [[jumble]], [[mix]], [[disorder]], [[disarrange]]
**3** to [[mix]] up (playing cards) so as to change their order
 *n*
**4** an instance of shuffling
**5** a rearrangement: a shuffle of top management
**6** a dance with short dragging movements of the feet
