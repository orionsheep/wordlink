**orchestrate** *vb* **-trating**, **-trated**
**1** to [[score]] or [[arrange]] (a piece of music) for orchestra
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[score]], [[set]], [[arrange]], [[adapt]]
**2** to [[arrange]] (something) in order to produce a particular result: he had orchestrated today's meeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[organize]], [[plan]], [[run]], [[set up]], [[arrange]], [[put together]], [[marshal]], [[coordinate]]
 **[[orchestration]]** *n*
