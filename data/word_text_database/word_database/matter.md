**matter** *n*
**1** the [[substance]] of which something, esp. a physical object, is made; [[material]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substance]], [[material]], [[body]], [[stuff]]
**2** [[substance]] that occupies space and has mass, as distinguished from [[substance]] that is mental or spiritual
**3** [[substance]] of a specified type: vegetable matter
**4** an [[event]], [[situation]], or [[subject]]: a matter of taste; the break-in is a matter for the police
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[situation]], [[concern]], [[business]], [[question]], [[event]], [[subject]], [[affair]], [[incident]]
**5** a quantity or amount: a matter of a few pounds
**6** the content of written or verbal [[material]] as distinct from its style or form
**7** written [[material]] in general: advertising matter
**8** a secretion or discharge, such as pus
**9** **for that matter** as regards that
**10** **no matter** regardless of; irrespective of: you have to leave, no matter what she thinks
**11** **the matter** wrong; the trouble: there's nothing the matter
 *vb*
**12** to be of importance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be important]], [[make a difference]], [[count]], [[be relevant]], [[make any difference]], [[carry weight]], [[cut any ice]] (*informal*), be of account
 *interj* **no matter**
**13** it is unimportant
