**afford** *vb*
**1** **can afford** to be able to do or spare something without risking financial difficulties or undesirable consequences: she can't afford to be choosy
**2** to [[give]] or [[supply]]: afford me an opportunity to judge for myself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give]], [[offer]], [[provide]], [[produce]], [[supply]], [[yield]], [[render]]
 **[[affordable]]** *adj* 
 **[[affordability]]** *n*
