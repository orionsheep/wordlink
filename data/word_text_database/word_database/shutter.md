**shutter** *n*
**1** a hinged doorlike cover, usually one of a pair, for closing off a window
**2** **put up the shutters** to close business at the end of the day or permanently
**3** (*photog*) a device in a camera that opens to allow light through the lens so as to expose the film when a photograph is taken
 *vb*
**4** to close or equip with a shutter or shutters
