**acid** *n*
**1** (*chem*) one of a class of compounds, corrosive and [[sour]] when dissolved in water, that combine with a base to form a salt
**2** (*slang*) LSD
**3** a [[sour]]-tasting substance
 *adj*
**4** (*chem*) of, from, or containing acid
**5** [[sharp]] or [[sour]] in taste
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sour]], [[tart]], [[pungent]], [[acerbic]], [[acrid]], [[vinegary]]
**6** [[sharp]] in speech or manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sharp]], [[cutting]], [[biting]], [[bitter]], [[harsh]], [[barbed]], [[caustic]], [[vitriolic]]
 **[[acidly]]** *adv*
