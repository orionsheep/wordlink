**heaven** *n*
**1** (in some religions) the place where God is believed to live and where those leading good lives are believed to go when they die
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paradise]], [[next world]], [[hereafter]], nirvana (*Buddhism*, *Hinduism*), [[bliss]], [[Zion]] (*Christianity*), [[life everlasting]], Elysium *or* Elysian fields (*Greek myth*)
**2** a place or state of [[happiness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happiness]], [[paradise]], [[ecstasy]], [[bliss]], [[utopia]], [[rapture]], [[seventh heaven]]
**3** **heavens** the sky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the heavens]]**: sky, ether, firmament
**4** Also: **heavens** God or the gods, used in exclamatory phrases: for heaven's sake!
