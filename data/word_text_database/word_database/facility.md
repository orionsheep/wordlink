**facility** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** **facilities** the [[means]] or [[equipment]] needed for an activity: leisure and shopping facilities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amenity]], [[means]], [[aid]], [[opportunity]], [[advantage]], [[resource]], [[equipment]], [[provision]]
**2** the [[ability]] to do things easily and well
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ability]], [[skill]], [[efficiency]], [[fluency]], [[proficiency]], [[dexterity]], [[adroitness]]
**3** [[skill]] or [[ease]]: grown human beings can forget with remarkable facility
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ease]], [[fluency]], [[effortlessness]]
