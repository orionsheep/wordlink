**eject** *vb*
**1** to push or send out forcefully
**2** to compel (someone) to leave a place or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throw out]], [[remove]], [[turn out]], [[expel]], [[oust]], [[banish]], [[drive out]], [[evict]]
**3** to leave an aircraft rapidly in mid-flight, using an [[ejector]] seat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bail out]], [[escape]], [[get out]]
 **[[ejection]]** *n* 
 **[[ejector]]** *n*
