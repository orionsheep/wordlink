**catapult** *n*
**1** a Y-shaped device with a loop of elastic fastened to the ends of the prongs, used by children for firing stones
**2** a device used to launch aircraft from a warship
 *vb*
**3** to shoot forwards or upwards violently: traffic catapulted forward with a roar
**4** to cause (someone) suddenly to be in a particular situation: catapulted to stardom
