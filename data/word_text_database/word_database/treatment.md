**treatment** *n*
**1** the medical or surgical care given to a patient
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cure]], [[remedy]], [[medication]], [[medicine]]
**2** a way of [[handling]] a person or thing: the party has had unfair treatment in the press
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handling]], [[dealings with]], [[behaviour towards]], [[conduct towards]], [[management]], [[manipulation]], [[action towards]]
