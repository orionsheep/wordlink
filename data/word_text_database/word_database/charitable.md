◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kind]], [[understanding]], [[forgiving]], [[sympathetic]], [[favourable]], [[tolerant]], [[indulgent]], [[lenient]]
**2** of or for charity: a charitable organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[benevolent]], [[liberal]], [[generous]], [[lavish]], [[philanthropic]], [[bountiful]], [[beneficent]]
 **[[charitably]]** *adv*
