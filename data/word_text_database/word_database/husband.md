**husband** *n*
**1** the man to whom a person is married
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[partner]], [[spouse]], [[mate]], better half (*humorous*)
 *vb*
**2** to use (resources, finances, etc.) economically
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conserve]], [[budget]], [[save]], [[store]], [[hoard]], [[economize on]], [[use economically]]
