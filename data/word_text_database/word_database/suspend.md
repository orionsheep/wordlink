**suspend** *vb*
**1** to [[hang]] (something) from a high place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hang]], [[attach]], [[dangle]]
**2** to cause (something) to remain floating or hanging: a huge orange sun suspended above the horizon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hang]], [[attach]], [[dangle]]
**3** to cause (something) to stop temporarily: the discussions have been suspended
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[postpone]], [[put off]], [[cease]], [[interrupt]], [[shelve]], [[defer]], [[cut short]], [[discontinue]]
**4** to remove (someone) temporarily from a job or position, usually as a punishment
