**trolley** *n*
**1** a small table on casters used for carrying food or drink
**2** a wheeled cart or stand used for moving heavy items, such as shopping in a supermarket or luggage at a railway station
**3** (*Brit*) See **trolley bus**
**4** a device, such as a wheel that collects the current from an overhead wire, to drive the motor of an electric vehicle
**5** (*Brit** & **Austral*) a low truck running on rails, used in factories, mines, etc.
