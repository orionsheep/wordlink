**2** free from serious mental or physical health problems
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sane]], [[reasonable]], [[rational]], [[well-adjusted]], [[compos mentis]] (*Latin*), [[in your right mind]], [[mentally sound]]
**3** (*geom*) same as **perpendicular**
 *n*
