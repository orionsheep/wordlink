**tourist** *n*
**1** a person who travels for pleasure, usually sightseeing and staying in hotels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[traveller]], [[voyager]], [[tripper]] (*Brit*), [[globetrotter]], [[holiday-maker]], [[sightseer]], [[excursionist]]
**2** a member of a sports team which is visiting a country to play a series of matches: the tourists were bowled out for 135
**3** the lowest class of accommodation on a passenger ship
 *adj*
**4** of or relating to tourists or tourism: a popular tourist attraction
**5** of the lowest class of accommodation on a passenger ship or aircraft
