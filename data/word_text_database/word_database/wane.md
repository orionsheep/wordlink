**wane** *vb* **[[waning]]**, **waned**
**1** to [[decrease]] gradually in size, strength, or power: the influence of the extremists is [[waning]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decline]], [[weaken]], [[diminish]], [[fail]], [[fade]], [[decrease]], [[dwindle]], [[lessen]]
**2** (of the moon) to show a gradually decreasing area of brightness from full moon until new moon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diminish]], [[decrease]], [[dwindle]]
 *n*
**3** **on the wane** decreasing in size, strength, or power: his fame was on the wane
 **[[waning]]** *adj*
