**spite** *n*
**1** deliberate nastiness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[malice]], [[malevolence]], [[ill will]], [[hatred]], [[animosity]], [[venom]], [[spleen]], [[spitefulness]]
**2** **[[in spite of]]** regardless of: he loved them [[in spite of]] their shortcomings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in spite of]]**: despite, regardless of, notwithstanding, (even) though
 *vb* **spiting**, **spited**
**3** to [[annoy]] (someone) deliberately, out of spite: it was to spite his father
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annoy]], [[hurt]], [[injure]], [[harm]], [[vex]]
 **[[spiteful]]** *adj* 
 **[[spitefully]]** *adv*
