**humour** * or * (*US*) **humor** *n*
**1** the quality of being funny
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comedy]], [[funniness]], [[fun]], [[amusement]], [[funny side]], [[jocularity]], [[facetiousness]], [[ludicrousness]]
**2** the ability to appreciate or express things that are humorous: a sense of humour
**3** situations, speech, or writings that are humorous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[joking]], [[comedy]], [[wit]], [[farce]], [[jesting]], [[wisecracks]] (*informal*), [[witticisms]]
**4** a state of mind; [[mood]]: in astoundingly good humour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mood]], [[spirits]], [[temper]], [[disposition]], [[frame of mind]]
**5** (*archaic*) any of various fluids in the body: aqueous humour
 *vb*
**6** to be kind and indulgent to: he decided the patient needed to be humoured
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indulge]], [[accommodate]], [[go along with]], [[flatter]], [[gratify]], [[pander to]], [[mollify]]
 **[[humourless]]** *adj*
