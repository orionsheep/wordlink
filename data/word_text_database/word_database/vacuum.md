**vacuum** *n*, *pl* **vacuums*** or ***vacua**
**1** a [[space]] which contains no air or other gas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[emptiness]], [[space]], [[void]], [[gap]], [[nothingness]], [[vacuity]]
**2** a vacant place or position that needs to be filled by someone or something else: the army moved in to fill the power vacuum
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gap]], [[lack]], [[absence]], [[space]], [[deficiency]], [[void]]
**3** short for **vacuum cleaner**
 *vb*
**4** to clean (something) with a vacuum cleaner
