**old-fashioned** *adj*
**1** in the style of a previous period; [[outdated]]: she wore her hair in a strangely old-fashioned tight hairdo
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[out of date]], [[dated]], [[outdated]], [[unfashionable]], [[outmoded]], [[passé]], [[old hat]], [[behind the times]]
**2** favouring or denoting the styles or ideas of a former time: old-fashioned values
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oldfangled]], [[square]] (*informal*), [[outdated]], [[unfashionable]], [[obsolescent]]
