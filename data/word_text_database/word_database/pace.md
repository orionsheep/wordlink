**pace** *n*
**1**
**A** a single [[step]] in walking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[footstep]], [[step]], [[stride]]
 **B** the length of a [[step]]
**2** [[speed]] of walking or running
**3** [[speed]] of doing some other activity: efforts to accelerate the pace of change are unlikely to succeed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speed]], [[rate]], [[tempo]], [[velocity]]
**4** manner of walking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[step]], [[walk]], [[stride]], [[tread]], [[gait]]
**5** **keep pace with** to advance at the same [[speed]] as
**6** **put someone through his *or* her paces** to test someone's ability
**7** **set the pace** to determine the [[speed]] at which a group advances
 *vb* **pacing**, **paced**
**8** to [[walk]] with regular steps, often in anxiety or impatience: he paced up and down the foyer impatiently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stride]], [[walk]], [[pound]], [[patrol]], [[march up and down]]
**9** to set the [[speed]] for (the competitors) in a race
**10** **pace out** to measure by paces
