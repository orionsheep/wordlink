**attitude** *n*
**1** the way a person thinks and behaves
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opinion]], [[view]], [[position]], [[approach]], [[mood]], [[perspective]], [[point of view]], [[stance]]
**2** a [[position]] of the body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[bearing]], [[pose]], [[stance]], [[carriage]], [[posture]]
**3** (*informal*) a hostile manner
**4** the orientation of an aircraft or spacecraft in relation to some plane or direction
