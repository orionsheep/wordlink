**loud** *adj*
**1** (of sound) relatively great in volume: loud applause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noisy]], [[booming]], [[roaring]], [[thundering]], [[forte]] (*music*), [[resounding]], [[deafening]], [[thunderous]]
**2** making or able to make sounds of relatively great volume: a loud voice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noisy]], [[booming]], [[roaring]], [[thundering]], [[forte]] (*music*), [[resounding]], [[deafening]], [[thunderous]]
**3** insistent and emphatic: loud appeals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noisy]], [[booming]], [[roaring]], [[thundering]], [[forte]] (*music*), [[resounding]], [[deafening]], [[thunderous]]
**4** (of colours or patterns) harsh to look at
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[garish]], [[bold]], [[glaring]], [[flamboyant]], [[brash]], [[flashy]], [[lurid]], [[gaudy]]
**5** [[noisy]], vulgar, and offensive
 *adv*
