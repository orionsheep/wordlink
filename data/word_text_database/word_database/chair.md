**chair** *n*
**1** a seat with a back and four legs, for one person to sit on
**2** an official position of authority or the person holding it: the chair of the Security Council
**3** a professorship
**4** **in the chair** presiding over a meeting
**5** **the chair** (*informal*) the electric chair
 *vb*
**6** to preside over (a meeting)
