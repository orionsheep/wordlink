**robbery** *n*, *pl* **-beries**
**1** (*criminal law*) the [[stealing]] of property from a person by using or threatening to use force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[burglary]], [[raid]], [[hold-up]], [[rip-off]] (*slang*), stick-up (*slang*, *chiefly* *US*), [[home invasion]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**2** the act or an instance of robbing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theft]], [[stealing]], [[mugging]] (*informal*), [[plunder]], [[swindle]], [[pillage]], [[larceny]]
