**drip** *vb* **dripping**, **dripped**
**1** to fall or let fall in drops
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drop]], [[splash]], [[sprinkle]], [[trickle]], [[dribble]], [[exude]], plop
 *n*
**2** a [[drop]] of liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drop]], [[bead]], [[trickle]], [[dribble]], [[droplet]], [[globule]], [[pearl]]
**3** the falling of drops of liquid
**4** the sound made by falling drops
**5** (*informal*) a weak or foolish person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weakling]], [[wet]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[weed]] (*informal*), [[softie]] (*informal*), [[mummy's boy]] (*informal*), [[namby-pamby]]
**6** (*med*) a device that administers a liquid [[drop]] by [[drop]] into a vein
