**poll** *n*
**1** Also called: **opinion poll** the questioning of a random [[sample]] of people to find out the general opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[survey]], [[figures]], [[count]], [[sampling]], [[returns]], [[ballot]], [[tally]], [[census]]
**2** the casting, recording, or counting of votes in an [[election]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[election]], [[vote]], [[voting]], [[referendum]], [[ballot]], [[plebiscite]]
**3** the result of such a [[voting]]: a marginal poll
**4** the head
 *vb*
**5** to receive (a certain number of votes)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gain]], [[return]], [[record]], [[register]], [[tally]]
**6** to [[record]] the votes of: he polled the whole town
**7** to [[question]] (a person, etc.) as part of an opinion poll
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[question]], [[interview]], [[survey]], [[sample]], [[ballot]], [[canvass]]
**8** to [[vote]] in an [[election]]
**9** to clip or shear
**10** to remove or cut short the horns of (cattle)
