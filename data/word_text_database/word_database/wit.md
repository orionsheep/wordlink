**wit** *n*
**1** the ability to use words or ideas in a clever, amusing, and imaginative way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humour]], [[quips]], [[banter]], [[puns]], [[repartee]], [[wordplay]], [[witticisms]], [[badinage]]
**2** a person possessing this ability
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humorist]], [[card]] (*informal*), [[comedian]], [[wag]], [[joker]], [[dag]] (*NZ* *informal*)
**3** practical intelligence: do credit me with some wit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cleverness]], [[sense]], [[brains]], [[wisdom]], [[common sense]], [[intellect]], [[ingenuity]], [[acumen]]
