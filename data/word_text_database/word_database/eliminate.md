**eliminate** *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**1** to [[get rid of]] (something or someone unwanted, unnecessary, or not meeting the requirements needed): he can be eliminated from the list of suspects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remove]], [[end]], [[stop]], [[withdraw]], [[get rid of]], [[abolish]], [[cut out]], [[dispose of]]
**2** to [[remove]] (a competitor or team) from a contest, esp. following a defeat: they were eliminated in the third round
**3** (*slang*) to murder in cold blood: Stalin had thousands of his former comrades eliminated
 **[[elimination]]** *n*
