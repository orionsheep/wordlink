**bankrupt** *n*
**1** a person, declared by a court to be unable to pay his or her debts, whose property is sold and the proceeds distributed among the creditors
**2** a person no longer having a particular quality: a spiritual bankrupt
 *adj*
**3** declared [[insolvent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insolvent]], [[broke]] (*informal*), [[ruined]], [[wiped out]] (*informal*), [[impoverished]], [[in the red]], [[destitute]], [[gone bust]] (*informal*)
**4** financially [[ruined]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insolvent]], [[broke]] (*informal*), [[ruined]], [[wiped out]] (*informal*), [[impoverished]], [[in the red]], [[destitute]], [[gone bust]] (*informal*)
**5** no longer having a particular quality: morally bankrupt
 *vb*
**6** to make bankrupt
