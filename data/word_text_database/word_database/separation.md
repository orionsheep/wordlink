**separation** *n*
**1** the act of separating: the separation of child from mother
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[division]], [[break]], [[dissociation]], [[disconnection]], [[disunion]]
**2** (*family law*) the living apart of a married couple without [[divorce]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[split-up]], [[parting]], [[split]], [[divorce]], [[break-up]], [[rift]]
**3** a mark, line, or object that separates one thing from another
