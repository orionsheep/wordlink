**Christmas** *n*
**1**
**A** a Christian festival commemorating the birth of Christ, held by most Churches to have occurred on December 25
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the festive season]], [[Noël]], [[Xmas]] (*informal*), [[Yule]] (*archaic*), Yuletide (*archaic*)
 **B** Also: **Christmas Day** December 25, as a day of secular celebrations when gifts and greetings are exchanged
 *adj*
**2** connected with or taking place at the time of year when this festival is celebrated: the Christmas holidays
 **[[Christmassy]]** *adj*
