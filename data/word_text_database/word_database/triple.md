**triple** *adj*
**1** made up of three parts or things: a triple murder
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[three-way]], [[threefold]], [[tripartite]]
**2** (of musical time or rhythm) having three beats in each bar
**3** three times as great or as much: a triple brandy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[treble]], three times
 *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**4** to make or become three times as much or as many: the company has tripled its sales over the past five years
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[treble]], increase [[threefold]]
 *n*
**5** something that is, or contains, three times as much as normal
**6** a group of three
 **[[triply]]** *adv*
