**valid** *adj*
**1** based on [[sound]] reasoning: I think that's a very valid question
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sound]], [[good]], [[reasonable]], [[telling]], [[convincing]], [[rational]], [[logical]], [[viable]]
**2** legally acceptable: she must produce a valid driving licence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legal]], [[official]], [[legitimate]], [[genuine]], [[authentic]], [[lawful]], [[bona fide]]
**3** important or serious enough to say or do: religious broadcasting has a valid purpose
 **[[validity]]** *n*
