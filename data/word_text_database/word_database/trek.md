**trek** *n*
**1** a long and often difficult [[journey]], esp. on foot
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[journey]], [[hike]], [[expedition]], [[safari]], [[march]], [[odyssey]]
**2** (*S African*) a [[journey]] or stage of a [[journey]], esp. a migration by ox wagon
 *vb* **trekking**, **trekked**
**3** to make a trek
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trudge]], [[traipse]] (*informal*), [[footslog]], [[slog]]
