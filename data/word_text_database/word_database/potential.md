**potential** *adj*
**1**
**A** [[possible]] but not yet actual: potential buyers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[possible]], [[future]], [[likely]], [[promising]], probable
 **B** capable of being or becoming; latent: potential danger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[possible]], [[future]], [[likely]], [[promising]], probable
 *n*
**2** [[ability]] or talent not yet in full use: she has great potential as a painter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ability]], [[possibilities]], [[capacity]], [[capability]], [[aptitude]], [[wherewithal]], [[potentiality]]
**3** In full: **electric potential** the work required to transfer a unit positive electric charge from an infinite distance to a given point
 **[[potentially]]** *adv*
