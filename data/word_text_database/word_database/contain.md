**contain** *vb*
**1** to [[hold]] or be capable of holding: the bag contained a selection of men's clothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold]], [[incorporate]], [[accommodate]], [[enclose]], [[have capacity for]]
**2** to have as one of its ingredients or constituents: tea and coffee both contain appreciable amounts of caffeine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[include]], [[consist of]], [[embrace]], [[comprise]], [[embody]], [[comprehend]]
**3** to [[consist of]]: the book contains 13 very different and largely separate chapters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[include]], [[consist of]], [[embrace]], [[comprise]], [[embody]], [[comprehend]]
**4** to check or [[restrain]] (feelings or behaviour)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[control]], [[hold in]], [[curb]], [[suppress]], [[hold back]], [[stifle]], [[repress]]
**5** to prevent from spreading or going beyond fixed limits: the blockade was too weak to contain the French fleet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[control]], [[hold in]], [[curb]], [[suppress]], [[hold back]], [[stifle]], [[repress]]
 **[[containable]]** *adj*
