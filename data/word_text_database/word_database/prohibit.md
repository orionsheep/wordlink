**prohibit** *vb* **-biting**, **-bited**
**1** to [[forbid]] by law or other authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forbid]], [[ban]], [[veto]], [[outlaw]], [[disallow]], [[proscribe]], [[debar]]
**2** to [[hinder]] or [[prevent]]: the paucity of information prohibits us from drawing reliable conclusions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prevent]], [[restrict]], [[stop]], [[hamper]], [[hinder]], [[impede]]
 **[[prohibitor]]** *n*
