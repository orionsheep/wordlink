**campaign** *n*
**1** a series of coordinated activities designed to achieve a goal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drive]], [[appeal]], [[movement]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[offensive]], [[crusade]]
**2** (*military*) a number of operations aimed at achieving a single objective
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[operation]], [[drive]], [[attack]], [[movement]], [[push]], [[offensive]], [[expedition]], [[crusade]]
 *vb*
**3** to take part in a campaign: he paid tribute to all those who'd campaigned for his release
 **[[campaigner]]** *n*
