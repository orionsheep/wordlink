**twin** *n*
**1** one of a [[pair]] of people or animals conceived at the same time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[double]], [[counterpart]], [[mate]], [[match]], [[fellow]], [[clone]], [[duplicate]], [[lookalike]]
**2** one of a [[pair]] of people or things that are identical or very similar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[double]], [[counterpart]], [[mate]], [[match]], [[fellow]], [[clone]], [[duplicate]], [[lookalike]]
 *vb* **twinning**, **[[twinned]]**
**3** to [[pair]] or be paired together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pair]], [[match]], [[join]], [[couple]], [[link]], [[yoke]]
