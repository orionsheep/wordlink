**absent** *adj*
**1** not present in a place or situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[away]], [[missing]], [[gone]], [[elsewhere]], [[unavailable]], [[nonexistent]]
**2** lacking
**3** not paying attention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[absent-minded]], [[blank]], [[vague]], [[distracted]], [[vacant]], [[preoccupied]], [[oblivious]], inattentive
 *vb*
**4** **absent oneself** to stay [[away]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[absent yourself]]**: stay [[away]], withdraw, keep [[away]], play truant
 **[[absently]]** *adv*
