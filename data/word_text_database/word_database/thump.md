**thump** *n*
**1** the sound of something heavy hitting a comparatively soft surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thud]], [[crash]], [[bang]], [[clunk]], [[thwack]]
**2** a heavy [[blow]] with the hand
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow]], [[knock]], [[punch]], [[rap]], [[smack]], [[clout]] (*informal*), [[whack]], swipe (*informal*)
 *vb*
**3** to place (something) on or [[bang]] against (something) with a loud dull sound: thumping the table is aggressive
**4** to [[hit]] or [[punch]] (someone): stop that at once or I'll thump you!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strike]], [[hit]], [[punch]], [[pound]], [[beat]], [[knock]], [[smack]], [[clout]] (*informal*)
**5** to throb or [[beat]] violently: he could feel his heart thumping
