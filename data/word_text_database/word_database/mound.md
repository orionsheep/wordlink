**mound** *n*
**1** a [[heap]] of earth, debris, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heap]], [[pile]], [[drift]], [[stack]], [[rick]]
**2** any [[heap]] or [[pile]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heap]], [[pile]], [[drift]], [[stack]], [[rick]]
**3** a small natural [[hill]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hill]], [[bank]], [[rise]], [[dune]], [[embankment]], [[knoll]], [[hillock]], kopje *or* koppie (*S African*)
