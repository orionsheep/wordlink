**lane** *n*
**1** a narrow [[road]], esp. in the country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[road]], [[street]], [[track]], [[path]], [[way]], [[passage]], [[trail]], [[pathway]]
**2** one of the parallel strips into which the carriageway of a major [[road]] or motorway is divided
**3** any well-defined route or course, such as for ships or aircraft
**4** one of the parallel strips into which a running [[track]] or swimming pool is divided for races
