**violence** *n*
**1** the use of physical [[force]], usually intended to cause injury or destruction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brutality]], [[bloodshed]], [[savagery]], [[fighting]], [[terrorism]]
**2** great [[force]] or [[strength]] in action, feeling, or expression
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[power]], [[strength]], [[might]], [[ferocity]], [[forcefulness]], [[powerfulness]]
