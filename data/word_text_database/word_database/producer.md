**producer** *n*
**1** a person with the financial and administrative responsibility for a film or television programme
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[director]], [[promoter]], [[impresario]]
**2** a person responsible for the artistic direction of a play
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[director]], [[promoter]], [[impresario]]
**3** a person who supervises the arrangement, performance, and mixing of a recording
**4** a person or thing that produces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[maker]], [[manufacturer]], [[builder]], [[creator]], [[fabricator]]
