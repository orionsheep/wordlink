**grain** *n*
**1** the small hard seedlike fruit of a [[cereal]] plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seed]], [[kernel]], [[grist]]
**2** a mass of such fruits gathered for food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cereal]], [[corn]]
**3** [[cereal]] plants in general
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cereal]], [[corn]]
**4** a small hard [[particle]]: a grain of salt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bit]], [[piece]], [[trace]], [[scrap]], [[particle]], [[fragment]], [[speck]], [[morsel]]
**5** a very small amount: a grain of compassion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bit]], [[piece]], [[trace]], [[scrap]], [[particle]], [[fragment]], [[speck]], [[morsel]]
**6**
**A** the arrangement of the fibres, layers, or particles in wood, leather, or stone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[texture]], [[pattern]], [[surface]], [[fibre]], [[weave]], [[nap]]
 **B** the [[pattern]] or [[texture]] resulting from this
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[texture]], [[pattern]], [[surface]], [[fibre]], [[weave]], [[nap]]
**7** **go against the grain** to be contrary to one's natural inclinations
 **[[grainy]]** *adj*
