**bless** *vb* **blessing**, **blessed*** or ***blest**
**1** to make holy by means of a religious rite
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sanctify]], [[dedicate]], [[ordain]], [[exalt]], [[anoint]], [[consecrate]], [[hallow]]
**2** to give honour or glory to (a person or thing) as holy
**3** to call upon God to protect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sanctify]], [[dedicate]], [[ordain]], [[exalt]], [[anoint]], [[consecrate]], [[hallow]]
**4** to worship or adore (God)
**5** **be blessed with** to be endowed with: she is blessed with immense energy
**6** **bless me!** an exclamation of surprise
**7** **bless you!** said to a person who has just sneezed
