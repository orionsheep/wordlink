**thesis** (**theess**-siss) *n*, *pl* **-ses** (-seez)
**1** a written work resulting from original research, esp. one submitted for a higher degree in a university
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dissertation]], [[paper]], [[treatise]], [[essay]], [[monograph]]
**2** an [[opinion]] supported by reasoned argument: it is the author's thesis that Britain has yet to come to terms with the loss of its Empire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proposition]], [[theory]], [[hypothesis]], [[idea]], [[view]], [[opinion]], [[proposal]], [[contention]]
**3** (*logic*) an unproved statement put forward as a premise in an argument
