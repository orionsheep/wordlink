**mirror** *n*
**1** a sheet of [[glass]] with a metal coating on its back, that reflects an image of an object placed in front of it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[looking-glass]], [[glass]] (*Brit*), [[reflector]]
**2** a thing that reflects or depicts something else
 *vb*
**3** to [[reflect]] or represent faithfully: the book inevitably mirrors my own interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reflect]], [[follow]], [[copy]], [[echo]], [[emulate]]
