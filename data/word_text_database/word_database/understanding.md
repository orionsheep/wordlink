**understanding** *n*
**1** the ability to learn, judge, or make decisions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perception]], [[knowledge]], [[grasp]], [[sense]], [[know-how]] (*informal*), [[judgment]], [[awareness]], [[appreciation]]
**2** personal [[opinion]] or [[interpretation]] of a subject: my understanding of what he said
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[belief]], [[view]], [[opinion]], [[impression]], [[interpretation]], [[feeling]], [[idea]], [[notion]]
**3** a mutual [[agreement]], usually an informal or private one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[deal]] (*informal*), [[promise]], [[arrangement]], [[accord]], [[contract]], [[bond]], pledge
 *adj*
**4** [[kind]], [[sympathetic]], or [[tolerant]] towards people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sympathetic]], [[kind]], [[compassionate]], [[considerate]], [[patient]], [[sensitive]], [[tolerant]]
