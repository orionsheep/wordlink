**understand** *vb* **-standing**, **-stood**
**1** to know and [[comprehend]] the nature or meaning of: I understand what you are saying
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comprehend]], [[get]], [[take in]], [[perceive]], [[grasp]], [[see]], [[follow]], [[realize]]
**2** to know what is happening or why it is happening: in order to understand the problems that can occur
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comprehend]], [[get]], [[take in]], [[perceive]], [[grasp]], [[see]], [[follow]], [[realize]]
**3** to [[assume]], infer, or [[believe]]: I understand he is based in this town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believe]], [[gather]], [[think]], [[see]], [[suppose]], [[notice]], [[assume]], [[fancy]]
**4** to know how to translate or read: don't you understand Russian?
**5** to be sympathetic to or compatible with: she needed him to understand her completely
 **[[understandable]]** *adj* 
 **[[understandably]]** *adv*
