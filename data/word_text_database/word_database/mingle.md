**mingle** *vb* **-gling**, **-gled**
**1** to [[mix]] or [[blend]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mix]], [[combine]], [[blend]], [[merge]], [[unite]], [[join]], [[interweave]], [[intermingle]]
**2** to [[associate]] or [[mix]] with a group of people: the performers mingled with the audience after the show
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[associate]], [[consort]], [[socialize]], [[rub shoulders]] (*informal*), [[hobnob]], [[fraternize]], hang about *or* around
