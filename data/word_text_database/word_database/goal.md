**goal** *n*
**1** (*sport*) the space into which players try to propel the ball or puck to score
**2** (*sport*)
**A** a successful attempt at scoring
 **B** the score so made
**3** an [[aim]] or [[purpose]]: the goal is to get homeless people on their feet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aim]], [[end]], [[target]], [[purpose]], [[object]], [[intention]], [[objective]], [[ambition]]
 **[[goalless]]** *adj*
