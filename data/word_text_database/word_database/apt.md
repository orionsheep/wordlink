**apt** *adj*
**1** having a specified tendency: they are apt to bend the rules
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclined]], [[likely]], [[ready]], [[disposed]], [[prone]], [[liable]], [[given]], [[predisposed]]
**2** [[suitable]] or [[appropriate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appropriate]], [[fitting]], [[suitable]], [[relevant]], [[to the point]], [[pertinent]]
**3** [[quick]] to learn: she was turning out to be a more apt pupil than he had expected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gifted]], [[skilled]], [[quick]], [[talented]], [[sharp]], [[capable]], [[smart]], [[clever]]
 **[[aptly]]** *adv* 
 **[[aptness]]** *n*
