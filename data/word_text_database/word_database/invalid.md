**invalid**^2 *adj*
**1** having no legal force: an invalid cheque
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[null and void]], [[void]], [[worthless]], [[inoperative]]
**2** (of an argument, result, etc.) not valid because it has been based on a mistake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unfounded]], [[false]], [[illogical]], [[irrational]], [[unsound]], [[fallacious]], [[untenable]]
 **[[invalidity]]** *n*
