**decisive** *adj*
**1** having great influence on the result of something: the decisive goal was scored in the closing minutes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crucial]], [[significant]], [[critical]], [[influential]], [[momentous]], [[conclusive]], [[fateful]]
**2** having the ability to make quick decisions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resolute]], [[decided]], [[firm]], [[determined]], [[forceful]], [[incisive]], [[trenchant]], [[strong-minded]]
 **[[decisively]]** *adv* 
 **[[decisiveness]]** *n*
