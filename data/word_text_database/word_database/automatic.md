**automatic** *adj*
**1** (of a device or mechanism) able to activate or regulate itself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mechanical]], [[automated]], [[mechanized]], [[push-button]], [[self-propelling]]
**2** (of a process) performed by automatic equipment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mechanical]], [[automated]], [[mechanized]], [[push-button]], [[self-propelling]]
**3** done without conscious thought
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[involuntary]], [[natural]], [[unconscious]], [[mechanical]], [[spontaneous]], [[reflex]], [[instinctive]], [[unwilled]]
**4** (of a firearm) utilizing some of the force of each explosion to reload and fire continuously
**5** occurring as a necessary consequence: the certificate itself carries no automatic legal benefits
 *n*
**6** an automatic firearm
**7** a motor vehicle with automatic transmission
 **[[automatically]]** *adv*
