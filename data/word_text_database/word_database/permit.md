**permit** *vb* **-mitting**, **-mitted**
**1** to [[allow]] (something) to be done or to happen: smoking is not permitted in the office
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allow]], [[grant]], [[sanction]], [[let]], [[entitle]], [[license]], [[authorize]], [[consent to]]
**2** to [[allow]] (someone) to do something: her father does not permit her to eat sweets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allow]], [[grant]], [[sanction]], [[let]], [[entitle]], [[license]], [[authorize]], [[consent to]]
**3** to [[allow]] the possibility (of): they saw each other as often as time and circumstances permitted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enable]], [[let]], [[allow]], cause
 *n*
**4** an official [[document]] granting permission to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[licence]], [[pass]], [[document]], [[certificate]], [[passport]], [[visa]], [[warrant]], [[authorization]]
