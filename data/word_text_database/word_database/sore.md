**sore** *adj*
**1** (of a wound, injury, etc.) painfully [[sensitive]]; [[tender]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[painful]], [[smarting]], [[raw]], [[tender]], [[burning]], [[angry]], [[sensitive]], [[irritated]]
**2** causing annoyance and resentment: a sore point
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annoying]], [[troublesome]]
**3** [[upset]] and angered: she's still sore about last night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annoyed]], [[cross]], [[angry]], [[pained]], [[hurt]], [[upset]], [[stung]], [[irritated]], [[tooshie]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[hoha]] (*NZ*)
**4** (*literary*) [[urgent]]; [[pressing]]: in sore need of firm government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[urgent]], [[desperate]], [[extreme]], [[dire]], [[pressing]], [[critical]], acute
 *n*
**5** a [[painful]] or [[sensitive]] wound or injury
 *adv*
**6** **sore afraid** (*archaic*) greatly frightened
 **[[soreness]]** *n*
