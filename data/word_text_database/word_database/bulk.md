**bulk** *n*
**1** [[volume]] or [[size]], esp. when great
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[size]], [[volume]], [[dimensions]], [[magnitude]], [[substance]], [[immensity]], [[largeness]]
**2** the main part: he spends the bulk of his time abroad
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[majority]], [[mass]], [[most]], [[body]], [[best part]], [[lion's share]], [[better part]], [[preponderance]]
**3** a large [[body]], esp. of a person
**4** the part of food which passes unabsorbed through the digestive system
**5** **in bulk** in large quantities: how frequently do you buy food in bulk for your family?
 *vb*
**6** **bulk large** to be or seem important or prominent
