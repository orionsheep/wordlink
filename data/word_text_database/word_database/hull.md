**hull** *n*
**1** the main [[body]] of a boat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[framework]], [[casing]], [[body]], [[covering]], [[frame]]
**2** the outer [[covering]] of a fruit or seed such as a pea or bean
**3** the leaves round the stem of a strawberry, raspberry, or similar fruit
 *vb*
**4** to remove the hulls from (fruit or seeds)
