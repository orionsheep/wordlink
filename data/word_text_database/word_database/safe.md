**safe** *adj*
**1** giving security or protection from harm: a safe environment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protected]], [[secure]], [[impregnable]], [[out of danger]], [[safe and sound]], [[in safe hands]], [[out of harm's way]]
**2** free from danger: she doesn't feel safe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[all right]], [[intact]], [[unscathed]], [[unhurt]], [[unharmed]], [[undamaged]], O.K. *or* okay (*informal*)
**3** taking or involving no risks: a safe bet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[risk-free]], [[sound]], [[secure]], [[certain]], [[impregnable]]
**4** not dangerous: the beef is safe to eat
**5** **on the safe side** as a precaution
 *n*
**6** a strong metal container with a [[secure]] lock, for storing money or valuables
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strongbox]], [[vault]], [[coffer]], [[repository]], [[deposit box]], [[safe-deposit box]]
 **[[safely]]** *adv*
