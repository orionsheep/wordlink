**handcuff** *n*
**1** **handcuffs** a linked pair of locking metal rings used for securing prisoners
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shackles]], [[cuffs]] (*informal*), [[fetters]], manacles
 *vb*
**2** to put handcuffs on (a person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shackle]], [[secure]], [[restrain]], [[fetter]], [[manacle]]
