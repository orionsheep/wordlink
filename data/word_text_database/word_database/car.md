**car** *n*
**1** a motorized road [[vehicle]] designed to carry a small number of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vehicle]], [[motor]], [[wheels]] (*informal*), [[auto]] (*US*), [[automobile]], [[jalopy]] (*informal*), [[motor car]], [[machine]]
**2** the passenger compartment of a [[cable car]], airship, lift, or balloon
**3** (*US** & **Canad*) a railway [[carriage]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: (railway) [[carriage]], [[coach]], [[cable car]], [[dining car]], [[sleeping car]], [[buffet car]], [[van]]
