**puppet** *n*
**1** a small [[doll]] or figure moved by strings attached to its limbs or by the hand inserted in its cloth body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[marionette]], [[doll]], [[glove puppet]], [[sock puppet]], [[finger puppet]]
**2** a person or state that appears independent but is controlled by another: the former cabinet ministers have denied that they are puppets of a foreign government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pawn]], [[tool]], [[instrument]] (*informal*), [[mouthpiece]], [[stooge]] (*slang*), [[cat's-paw]]
