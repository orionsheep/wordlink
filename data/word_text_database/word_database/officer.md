**officer** *n*
**1** a person in the armed services, or on a non-naval ship, who holds a position of authority
**2** a member of a police force, esp. a constable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[police officer]], [[detective]], [[PC]], [[police constable]], [[policeman]], [[policewoman]]
**3** a person holding a position of authority in a government or organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[official]], [[executive]], [[agent]], [[representative]], [[appointee]], [[functionary]], [[office-holder]], [[office bearer]]
