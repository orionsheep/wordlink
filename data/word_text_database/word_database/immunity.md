**immunity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the ability of an organism to resist disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resistance]], [[protection]], [[resilience]], [[inoculation]], [[immunization]]
**2** [[freedom]] from prosecution, tax, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exemption]], [[amnesty]], [[indemnity]], [[release]], [[freedom]], [[invulnerability]]
