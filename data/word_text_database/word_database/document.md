◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paper]], [[form]], [[certificate]], [[report]], [[record]], [[testimonial]], [[authorization]]
**2** an item of text or graphics that is stored as a file on a computer

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[certify]], [[verify]], [[detail]], [[validate]], [[substantiate]], [[corroborate]], [[authenticate]]
