**comedy** *n*, *pl* **-dies**
**1** a humorous film, play, or broadcast
**2** such works as a genre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humour]], [[fun]], [[joking]], [[farce]], [[jesting]], [[hilarity]]
**3** the humorous aspect of life or of events
**4** (in classical literature) a play that ends happily
