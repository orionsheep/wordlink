**indicator** *n*
**1** something that acts as a [[sign]] or indication: an indicator of the moral decline of our society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sign]], [[mark]], [[measure]], [[guide]], [[signal]], [[symbol]], [[meter]], [[gauge]]
**2** a device for indicating that a motor vehicle is about to turn left or right, esp. two pairs of lights that flash
**3** an instrument, such as a [[gauge]], that registers or measures something
**4** (*chem*) a substance used to indicate the completion of a chemical reaction, usually by a change of colour
