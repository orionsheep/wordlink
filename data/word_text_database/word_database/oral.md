**oral** *adj*
**1** [[spoken]] or [[verbal]]; using [[spoken]] words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spoken]], [[vocal]], [[verbal]], [[unwritten]]
**2** of or for use in the mouth: an oral thermometer
**3** (of a drug) to be taken by mouth: an oral contraceptive
 *n*
**4** an examination in which the questions and answers are [[spoken]] rather than written
 **[[orally]]** *adv*
