**render** *vb*
**1** to [[cause to become]]: he was rendered unconscious by his wound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[cause to become]], [[leave]]
**2** to [[give]] or [[provide]] (aid, a service, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[provide]], [[give]], [[pay]], [[present]], [[supply]], [[submit]], [[tender]], [[hand out]]
**3** (*formal*) to [[present]] or [[submit]] (a bill)
**4** to translate
**5** to [[represent]] in painting, music, or acting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[represent]], [[portray]], [[depict]], [[do]], [[give]], [[play]], [[act]], [[perform]]
**6** to yield or [[give]]: he rendered up his soul to God
**7** to cover with plaster
**8** to melt down (fat)
 **[[rendering]]** *n*
