**daily** *adj*
**1** occurring [[every day]] or every weekday: there have been daily airdrops of food, blankets, and water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[everyday]], [[diurnal]], [[quotidian]]
**2** of or relating to a single day or to one day at a time: her home help comes in on a daily basis; exercise has become part of our daily lives
 *adv*
**3** [[every day]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[every day]], [[day by day]], once a day
 *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**4** (*Brit** & **Austral*) a daily newspaper
**5** (*Brit* *informal*) a person employed to keep someone's house clean
