**silence** *n*
**1** the state or quality of being silent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quiet]], [[peace]], [[calm]], [[hush]], [[lull]], [[stillness]]
**2** the absence of sound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quiet]], [[peace]], [[calm]], [[hush]], [[lull]], [[stillness]]
**3** refusal or failure to speak or communicate when expected: he's broken his silence on the issue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reticence]], [[dumbness]], [[taciturnity]], muteness
 *vb* **-lencing**, **-lenced**
**4** to cause (someone or something) to become silent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quieten]], [[still]], [[quiet]], [[cut off]], [[stifle]], [[cut short]], [[muffle]], [[deaden]]
**5** to put a stop to: a way of silencing criticism
