**cheer** *vb*
**1** to [[applaud]] or encourage with shouts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[applaud]], [[hail]], [[acclaim]], [[clap]]
**2** **cheer up** to make or become happy or hopeful; comfort or be comforted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[cheer someone up]]**: comfort, encourage, hearten, enliven, gladden, gee up, jolly along (*informal*)
 *n*
**3** a shout of [[applause]] or encouragement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[applause]], [[ovation]]
**4** a feeling of cheerfulness: the news brought little cheer
