**sector** *n*
**1** a [[part]] or subdivision, esp. of a society or an economy: the public sector
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[division]]
**2** (*geom*) either portion of a circle bounded by two radii and the arc cut off by them
**3** a portion into which an [[area]] is divided for military operations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[area]], [[part]], [[region]], [[district]], [[zone]], [[quarter]]
