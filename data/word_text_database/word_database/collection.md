**collection** *n*
**1** things collected or accumulated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accumulation]], [[set]], [[store]], [[mass]], [[pile]], [[heap]], [[stockpile]], [[hoard]], [[aggregate]]
**2** a [[group]] of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[company]], [[crowd]], [[assembly]], [[cluster]], [[assortment]]
**3** the act or process of collecting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gathering]]
**4** a selection of clothes usually presented by a particular designer
**5** a sum of money collected, as in church
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contribution]], [[donation]], [[alms]]
**6** a regular removal of letters from a postbox
