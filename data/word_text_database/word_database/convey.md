**convey** *vb*
**1** to [[communicate]] (information)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communicate]], [[impart]], [[reveal]], [[relate]], [[disclose]], [[make known]], [[tell]]
**2** to [[carry]] or [[transport]] from one place to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry]], [[transport]], [[move]], [[bring]], [[bear]], [[conduct]], [[fetch]]
**3** (of a channel or path) to transfer or transmit
**4** (*law*) to transfer (the title to property)
 **[[conveyable]]** *adj* 
 **[[conveyor]]** *n*
