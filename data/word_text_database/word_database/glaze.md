**glaze** *vb* **[[glazing]]**, **[[glazed]]**
**1** to fit or cover with glass
**2** to cover (a piece of pottery) with a protective shiny coating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coat]], [[polish]], [[gloss]], [[varnish]], [[enamel]], [[lacquer]]
**3** to cover (food) with beaten egg or milk before cooking, in order to produce a shiny coating
 *n*
**4** a protective shiny coating applied to a piece of pottery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coat]], [[finish]], [[polish]], [[shine]], [[gloss]], [[varnish]], [[enamel]], [[lacquer]]
**5** a shiny coating of beaten egg or milk applied to food
 **[[glazed]]** *adj* 
 **[[glazing]]** *n*
