**marriage** *n*
**1** the state or relationship of being married: the institution of marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wedding]], [[match]], [[nuptials]], [[wedlock]], [[matrimony]]
**2** the contract made by two people to live together in a partnership. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVES: </small>**connubial**, **nuptial**
**3** the ceremony formalizing this union; [[wedding]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wedding]], [[match]], [[nuptials]], [[wedlock]], [[matrimony]]
**4** a close union or relationship: the marriage of scientific knowledge and industry
