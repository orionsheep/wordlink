**park** *n*
**1** a large area of open land for recreational use by the public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recreation ground]], [[garden]], [[playground]], [[pleasure garden]], [[playpark]], [[domain]] (*NZ*), [[forest park]] (*NZ*)
**2** a piece of open land for public recreation in a town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[parkland]], [[grounds]], [[estate]] (*Brit*), [[lawns]], [[woodland]], [[grassland]]
**3** (*Brit*) a large area of private land surrounding a country house
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[parkland]], [[grounds]], [[estate]] (*Brit*), [[lawns]], [[woodland]], [[grassland]]
**4** an area designed to accommodate a number of related enterprises: a science park
**5** (*US** & **Canad*) a [[playing field]] or sports stadium
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[field]], [[pitch]], [[playing field]]
**6** **the park** (*Brit* *informal*) the [[pitch]] in soccer
 *vb*
**7** to stop and leave (a vehicle) temporarily: I parked between the two cars already outside; police vans were parked on every street corner
**8** (*informal*) to leave or put (someone or something) somewhere: she parked herself on the sofa and stayed there all evening
 **[[parking]]** *n*
