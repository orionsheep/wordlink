**thus** *adv*
**1** as a result or consequence: the platforms provided a new floor and thus improved and enlarged the premises
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[therefore]], [[so]], [[hence]], [[consequently]], [[accordingly]], [[for this reason]], [[ergo]], [[on that account]]
**2** in this manner: I sat thus for nearly half an hour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[in this way]], [[so]], [[like this]], [[as follows]]
**3** to such a degree: the competition has been almost bereft of surprise thus far
