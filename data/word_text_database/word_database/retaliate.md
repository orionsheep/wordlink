◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pay someone back]], [[hit back]], [[strike back]], [[reciprocate]], [[take revenge]], [[get even with]] (*informal*), [[get your own back]] (*informal*)
**2** to cast (accusations) back upon a person
