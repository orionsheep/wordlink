**pickle** *n*
**1** (*often pl*) food, esp. vegetables preserved in vinegar or brine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chutney]], [[relish]], [[piccalilli]]
**2** a liquid or [[marinade]], such as spiced vinegar, for preserving vegetables, meat, or fish
**3** (*informal*) an awkward or difficult situation: to be in a pickle; they are in a pickle over what to do with toxic waste
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[predicament]], [[fix]] (*informal*), [[difficulty]], [[bind]] (*informal*), [[jam]] (*informal*), [[dilemma]], [[scrape]] (*informal*), [[hot water]] (*informal*), uphill (*S African*)
 *vb* **-ling**, **-led**
**4** to [[preserve]] or treat in a pickling liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[preserve]], [[marinade]], [[steep]]
