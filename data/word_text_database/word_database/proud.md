**proud** *adj*
**1** feeling pleasure or satisfaction: she was proud of her daughter's success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satisfied]], [[pleased]], [[content]], [[thrilled]], [[glad]], [[gratified]], [[joyful]], [[well-pleased]]
**2** feeling honoured
**3** [[haughty]] or [[arrogant]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conceited]], [[arrogant]], [[lordly]], [[imperious]], [[overbearing]], [[haughty]], [[snobbish]], [[self-satisfied]]
**4** causing pride: the city's proud history
**5** dignified: too proud to accept charity
**6** (of a surface or edge) projecting or protruding
 *adv*
**7** **do someone proud** to entertain someone on a grand scale: Mum did us all proud last Christmas
 **[[proudly]]** *adv*
