**stump** *n*
**1** the base of a tree trunk left standing after the tree has been cut down or has fallen
**2** the part of something, such as a tooth or limb, that remains after a larger part has been removed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tail end]], [[end]], [[remnant]], [[remainder]]
**3** (*cricket*) any of three upright wooden sticks that, with two bails laid across them, form a wicket
 *vb*
**4** to [[baffle]] or [[confuse]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[baffle]], [[confuse]], [[puzzle]], [[bewilder]], [[perplex]], [[mystify]], [[flummox]], [[nonplus]]
**5** (*cricket*) to dismiss (a batsman) by breaking his or her wicket with the ball
**6** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to campaign or canvass (an area), by political speech-making
**7** to walk with heavy steps; trudge
