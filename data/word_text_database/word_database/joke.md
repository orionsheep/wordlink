**joke** *n*
**1** something that is said or done to amuse people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jest]], [[gag]] (*informal*), [[wisecrack]] (*informal*), [[witticism]], [[crack]] (*informal*), [[quip]], [[pun]], [[one-liner]] (*informal*)
**2** someone or something that is ridiculous: the country's inexperienced leaders are regarded as something of a joke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[laughing stock]], [[clown]], [[buffoon]]
**3** **no joke** (*informal*) a serious or difficult matter: getting over mountain passes at ten thousand feet is no joke
 *vb* **joking**, **joked**
**4** to say or do something to amuse people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jest]], [[kid]] (*informal*), [[mock]], [[tease]], [[taunt]], [[quip]], [[banter]], play the fool
 **[[jokey]]** *adj* 
 **[[jokingly]]** *adv*
