**horse** *n*
**1** a four-footed mammal with hooves, a mane, and a tail, used for riding and pulling carts, etc.. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVES: </small>**equestrian**, **equine**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nag]], [[mount]], [[mare]], [[colt]], [[filly]], [[stallion]], steed (*archaic*, *literary*), [[moke]] (*Austral* *slang*), yarraman *or* yarramin (*Austral*), [[gee-gee]] (*slang*)
**2** the adult male of this species; [[stallion]]
**3** (*gymnastics*) a padded apparatus on legs, used for vaulting
**4** **be *or* get on one's high horse** (*informal*) to act in a haughty manner
**5** **the horses** (*informal*) horse races on which bets may be placed: an occasional flutter on the horses
**6** **the horse's mouth** the most reliable source: I'll tell you straight from the horse's mouth
 See also **horse around**
