**nickname** *n*
**1** a familiar, pet, or derisory name given to a person or place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pet name]], [[label]], [[diminutive]], [[epithet]], [[sobriquet]], moniker *or* monicker (*slang*)
 *vb* **-naming**, **-named**
**2** to call (a person or place) by a nickname: Gaius Caesar Augustus Germanicus, nicknamed Caligula
