**suppress** *vb*
**1** to [[put an end to]] (something) by physical or legal force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stamp out]], [[stop]], [[check]], [[crush]], [[conquer]], [[subdue]], [[put an end to]], [[overpower]]
**2** to prevent the circulation or publication of (information or books)
**3** to hold (an emotion or a response) in [[check]]; [[restrain]]: he could barely suppress a groan
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrain]], [[stifle]], [[contain]], [[silence]], [[conceal]], [[curb]], [[repress]], [[smother]]
**4** (*electronics*) to reduce or eliminate (interference) in a circuit
 **[[suppression]]** *n*
