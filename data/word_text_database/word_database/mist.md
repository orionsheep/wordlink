**mist** *n*
**1** a thin [[fog]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fog]], [[cloud]], [[steam]], [[spray]], [[film]], [[haze]], [[vapour]], [[smog]]
**2** a fine [[spray]] of liquid, such as that produced by an aerosol container
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fog]], [[cloud]], [[steam]], [[spray]], [[film]], [[haze]], [[vapour]], [[smog]]
**3** condensed water [[vapour]] on a surface
**4** something that causes haziness or lack of clarity, such as a [[film]] of tears
 *vb*
**5** to cover or be covered with mist: the windscreen has misted up again; his eyes misted over and he shook with rage
 **[[misty]]** *adj* 
 **[[mistiness]]** *n*
