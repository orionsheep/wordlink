**fiery** (**fire**-ee) *adj* **fierier**, **fieriest**
**1** consisting of or like fire: a fiery explosion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[burning]], [[flaming]], [[blazing]], [[on fire]], [[ablaze]], [[aflame]], [[afire]]
**2** displaying strong passion, esp. anger: a fiery speech
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excitable]], [[fierce]], [[passionate]], [[irritable]], [[impetuous]], [[irascible]], [[hot-headed]]
**3** (of food) very spicy
 **[[fierily]]** *adv* 
 **[[fieriness]]** *n*
