**parcel** *n*
**1** something wrapped up; a [[package]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[package]], [[case]], [[box]], [[pack]], [[bundle]]
**2** a group of people or things sharing something in common: a parcel of fools
**3** a distinct portion of land: he was the recipient of a substantial parcel of land
 *vb* **-celling**, **-celled** (*US*) **-celing**, **-celed**
**4** (often foll. by *up*) to [[wrap]] (something) up into a parcel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrap]], [[pack]], [[package]], [[tie up]], [[do up]], [[gift-wrap]], [[box up]], [[fasten together]]
**5** (foll. by *out*) to divide (something) into portions: the children were parcelled out to relatives
