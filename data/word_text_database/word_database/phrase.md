**phrase** *n*
**1** a group of words forming a unit of meaning in a sentence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expression]], [[saying]], [[remark]], [[construction]], [[quotation]], [[maxim]], [[idiom]], [[adage]]
**2** an idiomatic or original [[expression]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expression]], [[saying]], [[remark]], [[construction]], [[quotation]], [[maxim]], [[idiom]], [[adage]]
**3** (*music*) a small group of notes forming a coherent unit of melody
 *vb* **phrasing**, **phrased**
**4** to [[express]] orally or in a phrase: I could have phrased that better
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[express]], [[say]], [[word]], [[put]], [[voice]], [[communicate]], [[convey]], [[put into words]]
**5** (*music*) to divide (a melodic line or part) into musical phrases, esp. in performance
 **[[phrasal]]** *adj*
