**perpetual** *adj*
**1** never ending or never changing: Mexico's colourful scenery and nearly perpetual sunshine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[everlasting]], [[permanent]], [[endless]], [[eternal]], [[lasting]], [[perennial]], [[infinite]], [[never-ending]]
**2** continually [[repeated]]: his mother's perpetual worries about his health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continual]], [[repeated]], [[constant]], [[endless]], [[continuous]], [[persistent]], [[recurrent]], [[never-ending]]
 **[[perpetually]]** *adv*
