**how** *adv*
**1** in what way, by what means: how did you spend the evening?; observing how elderly people coped
**2** to what extent: they don't know how tough I am
**3** how good, how well, what ... like: how good are the copies?; so that's how things are
**4** **how about?** used to suggest something: how about some tea?
**5** **how are you?** what is your state of health?
**6** **how's that?**
**A** what is your opinion?: we'll go out for a late-night supper – how's that?
 **B** (*cricket*) Also written: **howzat** (an appeal to the umpire) is the batsman out?
