**joy** *n*
**1** deep happiness and contentment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delight]], [[pleasure]], [[satisfaction]], [[ecstasy]], [[enjoyment]], [[bliss]], [[glee]], [[rapture]]
**2** something that brings deep happiness: a thing of beauty is a joy for ever
**3** (*informal*) success or [[satisfaction]]: we checked ports and airports without any joy
