**reclaim** *vb*
**1** to get back possession of: the club is now trying to reclaim the money from the blockaders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[retrieve]], [[regain]]
**2** to convert (unusable or submerged land) into land suitable for farming or building on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regain]], [[salvage]], [[recapture]]
**3** to recover (useful substances) from waste products
 **[[reclamation]]** *n*
