**family** *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**1** a social [[group]] consisting of parents and their [[offspring]]. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**familial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relations]], [[relatives]], [[household]], [[folk]] (*informal*), [[kin]], [[nuclear family]], [[next of kin]], [[kith and kin]], [[ainga]] (*NZ*), cuzzies *or* cuzzie-bros (*NZ*), [[rellies]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** one's wife or husband and one's [[children]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relations]], [[relatives]], [[household]], [[folk]] (*informal*), [[kin]], [[nuclear family]], [[next of kin]], [[kith and kin]], [[ainga]] (*NZ*), cuzzies *or* cuzzie-bros (*NZ*), [[rellies]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**3** one's [[children]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[children]], [[kids]] (*informal*), [[offspring]], [[little ones]], [[littlies]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**4** a [[group]] descended from a common ancestor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ancestors]], [[house]], [[race]], [[tribe]], [[clan]], [[dynasty]], [[line of descent]]
**5** all the people living together in one [[household]]
**6** any [[group]] of related objects or beings: a family of chemicals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[species]], [[group]], [[class]], [[system]], [[order]], [[network]], [[genre]], [[subdivision]]
**7** (*biol*) one of the groups into which an [[order]] is divided, containing one or more genera: the cat family
 *adj*
**8** of or suitable for a family or any of its members: films for a family audience
**9** **in the family way** (*informal*) pregnant
