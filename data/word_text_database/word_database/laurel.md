**laurel** *n*
**1** a small Mediterranean evergreen tree with glossy leaves
**2** **laurels** a wreath of laurel, worn on the head as an emblem of victory or honour in classical times
**3** **laurels** honour, distinction, or fame
**4** **look to one's laurels** to be on guard against one's rivals
**5** **rest on one's laurels** to be satisfied with what one has already achieved and stop striving for further success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[rest on your laurels]]**: sit back, relax, take it easy, relax your efforts
