**energy** *n*, *pl* **-gies**
**1** capacity for intense activity; [[vigour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strength]], [[might]], [[stamina]], [[forcefulness]]
**2** intensity or [[vitality]] of action or expression; [[forcefulness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liveliness]], [[drive]], [[determination]], [[pep]], [[vitality]], [[vigour]], [[verve]], [[resilience]]
**3** (*physics*) the capacity to do work and overcome resistance
**4** a source of [[power]], such as electricity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[power]]
