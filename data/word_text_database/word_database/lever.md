**lever** *n*
**1** a [[handle]] used to operate machinery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handle]], [[bar]]
**2** a [[bar]] used to move a heavy object or to [[prise]] something open
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handle]], [[bar]]
**3** a rigid [[bar]] that turns on a fixed support (fulcrum) to transfer effort and motion, for instance to move a load
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handle]], [[bar]]
**4** a means of exerting pressure in order to achieve an aim: using the hostages as a lever to gain concessions from the government
 *vb*
**5** to open or move with a lever
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prise]], [[force]]
