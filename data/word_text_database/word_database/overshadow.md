**overshadow** *vb*
**1** to make (someone or something) seem insignificant or less important by comparison
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outshine]], [[eclipse]], [[surpass]], [[dwarf]], [[tower above]], leave *or* put in the shade
**2** to sadden the atmosphere of: news of their team-mate's injury overshadowed the victory celebrations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spoil]], [[ruin]], [[mar]], [[wreck]], [[blight]], crool *or* cruel (*Austral* *slang*), [[mess up]], [[put a damper on]]
