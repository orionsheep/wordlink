**late** *adj*
**1** occurring or arriving after the correct or expected time: the plane will be late
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overdue]], [[delayed]], [[last-minute]], [[belated]], [[tardy]], [[behind time]], [[behindhand]]
**2** towards or near the end: the late afternoon
**3** occurring or being at a relatively [[advanced]] time: a late starter, his first novel was effectively his last
**4** at an [[advanced]] time in the evening or at night: it's late, I have to get back
**5** having died recently: her late father
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dead]], [[deceased]], [[departed]] (*euphemistic*), [[passed on]], [[former]], [[defunct]]
**6** [[recent]]: recollect the late defeats which your enemies have experienced
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recent]], [[new]], [[advanced]], [[fresh]]
**7** [[former]]: the late manager of the team
