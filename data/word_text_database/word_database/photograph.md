**photograph** *n*
**1** a [[picture]] made using a camera, either by the chemical action of light on sensitive [[film]] or by the creation of a digital image
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[picture]], [[photo]] (*informal*), [[shot]], [[print]], [[snap]] (*informal*), [[snapshot]], [[selfie]] (*informal*), [[transparency]], [[JPEG]], [[thumbnail]], avatar
 *vb*
**2** to take a photograph of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take a picture of]], [[record]], [[film]], [[shoot]], [[snap]] (*informal*), take (someone's) [[picture]]
