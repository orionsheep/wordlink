**scenario** *n*, *pl* **-narios**
**1** a [[summary]] of the plot and characters of a play or film
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[story line]], [[résumé]], [[outline]], [[summary]], [[synopsis]]
**2** an imagined sequence of future events: the likeliest scenario is another general election
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[situation]]
