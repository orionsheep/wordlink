**cringe** *vb* **cringing**, **cringed**
**1** to shrink or flinch in fear: he cringed and shrank against the wall
**2** to behave in a submissive or timid way: an organization that tends to cringe before political authority 
**3** (*informal*) to be very embarrassed: I cringe every time I see that old photo of me
 *n*
**4** the act of cringing
