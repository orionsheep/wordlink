**disguise** *vb* **-guising**, **-guised**
**1** to change the appearance or manner in order to [[conceal]] the identity of (someone or something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hide]], [[cover]], [[conceal]], [[screen]], [[mask]], [[suppress]], [[withhold]], [[veil]]
**2** to misrepresent (something) in order to obscure its actual nature or meaning
 *n*
**3** a [[mask]], [[costume]], or manner that disguises
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[costume]], [[mask]], [[camouflage]]
**4** the state of being [[disguised]]
 **[[disguised]]** *adj*
