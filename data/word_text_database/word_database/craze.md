**craze** *n*
**1** a short-lived [[fashion]] or [[enthusiasm]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fad]], [[fashion]], [[trend]], [[rage]], [[enthusiasm]], [[vogue]], [[mania]], infatuation
 *vb* **crazing**, **[[crazed]]**
**2** to make mad
**3** (*ceramics*, *metallurgy*) to develop or cause to develop fine cracks: you must prevent the drill crazing the glazed surface of the tile
