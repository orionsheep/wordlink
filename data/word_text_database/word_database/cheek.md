**cheek** *n*
**1** either side of the face below the eye
**2** (*informal*) [[impudence]], boldness, or lack of respect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impudence]], [[nerve]] (*informal*), [[disrespect]], [[audacity]], [[lip]] (*slang*), [[temerity]], [[chutzpah]] (*US** & **Canad* *informal*), [[insolence]]
**3** (*informal*) a buttock
**4** **cheek by jowl** close together
**5** **turn the other cheek** to refuse to retaliate
 *vb*
**6** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) to speak or behave disrespectfully to someone
