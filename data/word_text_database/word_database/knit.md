**knit** *vb* **knitting**, **knitted*** or ***knit**
**1** to make (a garment) by looping (wool) using long eyeless needles or a knitting machine
**2** to [[join]] together closely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[join]], [[unite]], [[link]], [[tie]], [[bond]], [[combine]], [[bind]], [[weave]]
**3** to draw (one's eyebrows) together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[furrow]], [[tighten]], [[knot]], [[wrinkle]], [[crease]], [[screw up]], [[pucker]], scrunch up
 *n*
**4** a fabric made by knitting
 **[[knitter]]** *n*
