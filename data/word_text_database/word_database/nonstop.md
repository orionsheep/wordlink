**nonstop** *adj*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continuous]], [[constant]], [[relentless]], [[uninterrupted]], [[endless]], [[unbroken]], [[interminable]], incessant
 *adv*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continuously]], [[constantly]], [[endlessly]], [[relentlessly]], [[perpetually]], [[incessantly]], [[ceaselessly]], [[interminably]]
