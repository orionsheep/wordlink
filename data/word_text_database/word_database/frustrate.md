**frustrate** *vb* **-trating**, **-trated**
**1** to upset or anger (a person) by presenting difficulties that cannot be overcome: his lack of ambition frustrated me
**2** to hinder or prevent (the efforts, plans, or desires of)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thwart]], [[stop]], [[check]], [[block]], [[defeat]], [[disappoint]], [[counter]], [[spoil]], crool *or* cruel (*Austral* *slang*) 
 **[[frustrating]]** *adj* 
 **[[frustration]]** *n*
