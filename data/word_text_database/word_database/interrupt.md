**interrupt** *vb*
**1** to break into (a conversation or discussion) by questions or comment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intrude]], [[disturb]], [[intervene]], [[interfere]] (with), [[break in]], [[heckle]], [[butt in]], [[barge in]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[stop]] (a process or activity) temporarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suspend]], [[stop]], [[end]], [[delay]], [[cease]], [[postpone]], [[shelve]], [[put off]]
 **[[interrupted]]** *adj* 
 **[[interruptive]]** *adj*
