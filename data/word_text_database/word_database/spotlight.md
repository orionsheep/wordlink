**spotlight** *n*
**1** a powerful light focused so as to light up a small area
**2** **the spotlight** the centre of [[attention]]: the spotlight moved to the president
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attention]], [[limelight]], [[public eye]], fame
 *vb* **-lighting**, **-lit*** or ***-lighted**
**3** to direct a spotlight on (something)
**4** to focus [[attention]] on (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[highlight]], [[draw attention to]], [[accentuate]]
