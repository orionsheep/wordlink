**status** *n*
**1** a person's [[position]] in society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[rank]], [[grade]]
**2** the esteem in which people hold a person: priests feel they have lost some of their status in society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prestige]], [[standing]], [[authority]], [[influence]], [[weight]], [[honour]], [[importance]], [[fame]], [[mana]] (*NZ*)
**3** the legal or official [[standing]] or classification of a person or country: the status of refugees; Ireland's non-aligned status
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state of play]], [[development]], [[progress]], [[condition]], [[evolution]]
**4** degree of [[importance]]
