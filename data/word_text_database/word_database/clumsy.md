**clumsy** *adj* **-sier**, **-siest**
**1** lacking in skill or physical coordination: an extraordinarily clumsy player
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[awkward]], [[lumbering]], [[bumbling]], [[ponderous]], [[ungainly]], [[gauche]], [[gawky]], [[uncoordinated]], [[unco]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** badly made or done
**3** said or done without thought or tact: I took the clumsy hint and left
 **[[clumsily]]** *adv* 
 **[[clumsiness]]** *n*
