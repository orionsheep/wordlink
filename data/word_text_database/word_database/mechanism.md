**mechanism** *n*
**1** a [[system]] of moving parts that performs some function, esp. in a [[machine]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[machine]], [[device]], [[tool]], [[instrument]], [[appliance]], [[apparatus]], [[contrivance]]
**2** any mechanical [[device]] or part of such a [[device]]
**3** a [[process]] or [[technique]]: the body's defence mechanisms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[process]], [[way]], [[means]], [[system]], [[operation]], [[agency]] (*old-fashioned*), [[method]], [[technique]]
 **[[mechanistic]]** *adj*
