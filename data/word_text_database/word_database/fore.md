**fore** *adj*
**1** at, in, or towards the front: the fore foot
 *n*
**2** the front part
**3** **fore and aft** located at both ends of a vessel: two double cabins fore and aft
**4** **to the fore** to the front or prominent position
 *interj*
**5** a golfer's shouted warning to a person in the path of a flying ball
