**yearn** *vb*
**1** to have an intense [[desire]] or longing: he often yearned for life in a country town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[long]], [[desire]], [[hunger]], [[ache]], [[crave]], [[covet]], [[itch]], [[hanker after]]
**2** to feel tenderness or affection: I yearn for you
 **[[yearning]]** *n*, *adj*
