**sea** *n*
**1** **the sea** the [[mass]] of salt water that covers three-quarters of the earth's surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ocean]], [[the deep]], [[the waves]], [[main]]
**2**
**A** one of the smaller areas of this: the Irish Sea
 **B** a large inland area of water: the Caspian Sea
**3** the area on or close to the edge of the sea, esp. as a place where holidays are taken: a day by the sea
**4** strong and uneven swirling movement of waves: rough seas
**5** anything resembling the sea in size or movement: a sea of red and yellow flags
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mass]], [[army]], [[host]], [[crowd]], [[mob]], [[abundance]], [[swarm]], [[horde]]
**6** **[[at sea]]**
**A** on the [[ocean]]
 **B** in a state of confusion or uncertainty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at sea]]**: bewildered, lost, confused, puzzled, baffled, perplexed, mystified, flummoxed
**7** **go to sea** to become a sailor
**8** **put out to sea** to start a sea voyage
