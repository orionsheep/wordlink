◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regarding]], [[on]], [[concerning]], [[dealing with]], [[referring to]], [[relating to]], [[as regards]]
**2** [[near]] to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[near]], [[around]], [[close to]], [[nearby]], [[beside]], [[adjacent to]], [[in the neighbourhood of]]
**3** carried [[on]]: I haven't any money about me
**4** [[on]] every side of
 *adv*
**5** [[near]] in number, time, or degree; [[approximately]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[approximately]], [[around]], [[almost]], [[nearly]], [[approaching]], [[close to]], [[roughly]], [[just about]]
**6** [[nearby]]
**7** here and there: there were some fifteen other people scattered about [[on]] the first floor

 *adj*
