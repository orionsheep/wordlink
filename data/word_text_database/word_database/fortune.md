**fortune** *n*
**1** a very large sum of money
**2** [[luck]], esp. when favourable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[luck]], [[fluke]] (*informal*), [[stroke of luck]], [[serendipity]], [[twist of fate]], [[run of luck]]
**3** (*often pl*) a person's [[destiny]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destiny]], [[lot]], [[experiences]], [[history]], [[condition]], [[success]], [[means]], [[adventures]]
**4** a power regarded as being responsible for human affairs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[fate]], [[destiny]], [[providence]], [[the stars]], [[Lady Luck]], [[kismet]]
**5** [[wealth]] or material prosperity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wealth]], [[means]], [[property]], [[riches]], [[resources]], [[assets]], [[possessions]], [[treasure]]
