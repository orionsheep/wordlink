**academic** *adj*
**1** relating to a college or university
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scholastic]], [[educational]]
**2** (of pupils) having an aptitude for study
**3** relating to studies such as languages and pure science rather than technical or professional studies
**4** of [[theoretical]] interest only: the argument is academic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theoretical]], [[abstract]], [[speculative]], [[hypothetical]], [[impractical]], [[notional]], conjectural
 *n*
**5** a member of the teaching or research staff of a college or university
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scholar]], [[intellectual]], [[don]], [[master]], [[professor]], [[fellow]], [[lecturer]], [[tutor]], [[acca]] (*Austral* *slang*) 
 **[[academically]]** *adv*
