**discourse** *n*
**1** [[conversation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conversation]], [[talk]], [[discussion]], [[speech]], [[communication]], [[chat]], [[dialogue]]
**2** a formal treatment of a subject in [[speech]] or writing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speech]], [[essay]], [[lecture]], [[sermon]], [[treatise]], [[dissertation]], [[homily]], [[oration]], whaikorero (*NZ*)
 *vb* **-coursing**, **-coursed**
**3** to speak or write (about) at length
