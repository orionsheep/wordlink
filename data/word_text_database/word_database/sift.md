**sift** *vb*
**1** to [[sieve]] (a powdery substance) in order to remove the coarser particles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sieve]], [[filter]], [[strain]], [[separate]], [[part]]
**2** to [[examine]] (information or evidence) carefully to select what is important
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examine]], [[investigate]], [[go through]], [[research]], [[analyse]], [[work over]], [[scrutinize]]
