**punish** *vb*
**1** to force (someone) to undergo a penalty for some crime or misbehaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discipline]], [[correct]], [[castigate]], [[chastise]], [[sentence]], [[chasten]], [[penalize]]
**2** to inflict punishment for (some crime or misbehaviour)
**3** to treat harshly, esp. by overexertion: he continued to punish himself in the gym
 **[[punishable]]** *adj* 
 **[[punishing]]** *adj*
