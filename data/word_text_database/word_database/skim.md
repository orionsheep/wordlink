**skim** *vb* **skimming**, **skimmed**
**1** to [[remove]] (floating material) from the surface of (a liquid): skim any impurities off the surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remove]], [[separate]], [[cream]]
**2** to [[glide]] smoothly over (a surface)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glide]], [[fly]], [[coast]], [[sail]], [[float]]
**3** to throw (a flat stone) across a surface, so that it bounces: two men skimmed stones on the surface of the sea
**4** (often foll. by *through*) to read (a piece of writing) quickly and without taking in the details
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scan]], [[glance]], [[run your eye over]]
