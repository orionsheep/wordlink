**rhythm** *n*
**1** any regular movement or [[beat]]: the side-effects can cause changes in the rhythm of the heart [[beat]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[swing]], [[accent]], [[pulse]], [[tempo]], [[cadence]], [[lilt]]
**2** any regular pattern that occurs over a period of [[time]]: the seasonal rhythm of the agricultural year
**3**
**A** the arrangement of the durations of and stress on the notes of a piece of music, usually laid out in regular groups (**bars**) of beats
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[swing]], [[accent]], [[pulse]], [[tempo]], [[cadence]], [[lilt]]
 **B** any specific arrangement of such groupings: waltz rhythm
**4** (in poetry) the arrangement of words to form a regular pattern of stresses
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[metre]], [[time]]
 **[[rhythmic]]** * or ***[[rhythmical]]** *adj* 
 **[[rhythmically]]** *adv*
