**declaration** *n*
**1** a firm, emphatic statement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affirmation]], [[profession]], [[assertion]], [[revelation]], [[disclosure]], [[acknowledgment]], [[protestation]] (*formal*), [[avowal]]
**2** an official [[announcement]] or statement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[announcement]], [[proclamation]], [[decree]], [[notice]], [[notification]], [[edict]], [[pronouncement]]
 **[[declaratory]]** *adj*
