**freak** *n*
**1** a person, animal, or plant that is [[abnormal]] or deformed
**2** an object, event, etc., that is [[abnormal]]: a statistical freak
**3** (*informal*) a person whose appearance or behaviour is very [[unusual]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eccentric]], [[character]] (*informal*), [[oddball]] (*informal*), [[nonconformist]]
**4** (*informal*) a person who is very enthusiastic about something specified: a health freak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enthusiast]], [[fan]], [[nut]] (*slang*), [[addict]], [[buff]] (*informal*), [[fanatic]], [[devotee]], fiend (*informal*)
 *adj*
**5** [[abnormal]] or [[unusual]]: a freak accident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abnormal]], [[chance]], [[unusual]], [[exceptional]], [[unparalleled]]
 **[[freakish]]** *adj* 
 **[[freaky]]** *adj*
