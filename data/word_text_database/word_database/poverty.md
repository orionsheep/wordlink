**poverty** *n*
**1** the state of lacking adequate food or money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pennilessness]], [[want]], [[need]], [[hardship]], [[insolvency]], [[privation]] (*formal*), [[penury]], [[destitution]]
**2** [[lack]] or [[scarcity]]: a poverty of information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scarcity]], [[lack]], [[absence]], [[want]], [[deficit]], [[shortage]], [[deficiency]], [[inadequacy]]
**3** inferior quality or [[inadequacy]]: the poverty of political debate in this country
