**sum** *n*
**1** the result of the addition of numbers or quantities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[total]], [[aggregate]]
**2** one or more columns or rows of numbers to be added, subtracted, multiplied, or divided
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calculation]], [[figures]], [[arithmetic]], [[mathematics]], [[maths]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[tally]], [[math]] (*US* *informal*), [[arithmetical problem]]
**3** a [[quantity]] of money: they can win enormous sums
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amount]], [[quantity]], [[volume]]
**4** **in sum** as a summary; in short: in sum, it's been a bad week for the government
 *adj*
**5** complete or final: the sum [[total]]
 *vb* **summing**, **summed**
**6** See **sum up**
