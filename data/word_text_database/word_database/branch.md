**branch** *n*
**1** a secondary woody stem extending from the trunk or main branch of a tree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bough]], [[shoot]], [[arm]], [[spray]], [[limb]], [[sprig]], [[offshoot]]
**2** one of a number of shops, offices, or groups that belongs to a central organization: she was transferred to their Japanese branch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[office]], [[department]], [[unit]], [[wing]], [[chapter]], [[bureau]]
**3** a [[subdivision]] or subsidiary [[section]] of something larger or more complex: branches of learning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[division]], [[part]], [[section]], [[subdivision]], subsection
 *vb*
**4** to divide, then develop in different directions
 **[[branchlike]]** *adj*
