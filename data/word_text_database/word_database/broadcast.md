**broadcast** *n*
**1** a [[transmission]] or [[programme]] on [[radio]] or television
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transmission]], [[show]], [[programme]], [[telecast]], [[podcast]], [[webcast]], [[vodcast]], mobcast
 *vb* **-casting**, **-cast*** or ***-casted**
**2** to [[transmit]] (announcements or programmes) on [[radio]] or television
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transmit]], [[show]], [[air]], [[radio]], [[cable]], [[beam]], [[send out]], [[relay]], [[stream]], [[podcast]], [[open-line]] (*Canad*)
**3** to take part in a [[radio]] or television [[programme]]
**4** to make widely known throughout an area: to broadcast news
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make public]], [[report]], [[announce]], [[publish]], [[spread]], [[advertise]], [[proclaim]], [[circulate]]
**5** to scatter (seed, etc.)
