**sluice** *n*
**1** a channel that carries a rapid current of water, with a sluicegate to control the flow
**2** the water controlled by a sluicegate
**3** (*mining*) a sloping trough for washing ore
 *vb* **sluicing**, **sluiced**
**4** to draw off or drain with a sluice
**5** to wash with a stream of water
**6** (often foll. by *away* or *out*) (of water) to run or flow from or as if from a sluice
