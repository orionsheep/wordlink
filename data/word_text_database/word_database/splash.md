**splash** *vb*
**1** to [[scatter]] (liquid) on (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scatter]], [[shower]], [[spray]], [[sprinkle]], [[wet]], [[spatter]], [[slop]]
**2** to cause (liquid) to fall or (of liquid) to be scattered in drops
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scatter]], [[shower]], [[spray]], [[sprinkle]], [[wet]], [[spatter]], [[slop]]
**3** to display (a photograph or story) prominently in a newspaper
 *n*
**4** a splashing sound
**5** an amount splashed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dash]], [[touch]], [[spattering]]
**6** a [[patch]] (of colour or light)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spot]] (*Brit*), [[burst]], [[patch]], [[spurt]]
**7** **make a splash** (*informal*) to attract a lot of attention
**8** a small amount of liquid added to a drink
