**passing** *adj*
**1** [[momentary]] or [[short-lived]]: a passing fad
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[momentary]], [[fleeting]], [[short-lived]], [[transient]], [[ephemeral]], [[brief]], [[temporary]], [[transitory]]
**2** [[casual]] or [[superficial]]: a passing resemblance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[superficial]], [[short]], [[quick]], [[glancing]], [[casual]], [[summary]], [[cursory]], perfunctory
 *n*
**3** (*euphemistic*) death
**4** the ending of something: the passing of the old order in Eastern Europe
**5** **in passing** briefly and without going into detail; incidentally: this fact is only noted in passing
