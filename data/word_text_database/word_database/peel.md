**peel** *vb*
**1** to remove the [[skin]] or [[rind]] of (a fruit or vegetable)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skin]], [[scale]], [[strip]], [[pare]], [[shuck]], [[flake off]], take the [[skin]] *or* [[rind]] off
**2** to come off in flakes
**3** (of a person or part of the body) to shed [[skin]] in flakes as a result of sunburn
 *n*
**4** the [[skin]] or [[rind]] of a fruit, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rind]], [[skin]], [[peeling]]
