**why** *adv*
**1** for what reason?: why did he marry her?; she avoided asking him why he was there
 *pron*
**2** for or because of which: you can think of all kinds of reasons why you should not believe it
 *n*, *pl* **whys**
**3** **the whys and wherefores** See **wherefore**
 *interj*
**4** an exclamation of surprise, indignation, or impatience: why, I listen to you on the radio twice a week
