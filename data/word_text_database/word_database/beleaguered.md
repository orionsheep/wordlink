**beleaguered** *adj*
**1** struggling against difficulties or criticism: the country's beleaguered health system
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harassed]], [[troubled]], [[plagued]], [[hassled]] (*informal*), [[badgered]], [[persecuted]], [[pestered]], [[vexed]]
**2** [[besieged]] by an enemy: a ship bringing food to the beleaguered capital of Monrovia
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[besieged]], [[surrounded]], [[blockaded]], [[beset]], [[encircled]], [[assailed]], [[hemmed in]]
