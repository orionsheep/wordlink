**invite** *vb* **-viting**, **-vited**
**1** to [[ask]] (a person) in a friendly or polite way (to do something, attend an event, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ask]]
**2** to make a [[request]] for, esp. publicly or formally: we invite applications for six scholarships
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[request]], [[look for]], [[bid for]], [[appeal for]]
**3** to bring on or provoke: his theory invites disaster
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[encourage]], [[attract]], [[cause]], [[court]], [[ask for]] (*informal*), [[generate]], [[foster]], [[tempt]]
**4** to [[tempt]]
 *n*
**5** (*informal*) an invitation
