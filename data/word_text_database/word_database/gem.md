**gem** *n*
**1** a [[precious stone]] used for decoration. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**lapidary**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[precious stone]], [[jewel]], [[stone]]
**2** a person or thing regarded as precious or special: a perfect gem of a hotel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[treasure]], [[prize]], [[jewel]], [[pearl]], [[masterpiece]], [[humdinger]] (*slang*), [[taonga]] (*NZ*)
