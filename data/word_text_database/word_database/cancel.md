**cancel** *vb* **-celling**, **-celled** (*US*) **-celing**, **-celed**
**1** to stop (something that has been arranged) from taking place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call off]], [[drop]], [[forget about]]
**2** to mark (a cheque or stamp) with an official stamp to prevent further use
**3** **cancel out** to make ineffective by having the opposite effect: economic vulnerability cancels out any possible political gain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[cancel something out]]**: counterbalance, offset, make up for, compensate for, neutralize, nullify, balance out
 **[[cancellation]]** *n*
