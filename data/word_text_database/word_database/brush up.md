**brush up** *vb*
**1** (often foll. by *on*) to refresh one's knowledge or memory of (a subject)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[brush something up or brush up on something]]**: revise, study, go over, cram, polish up, read up on, relearn, bone up on (*informal*)
 *n* **brush-up**
**2** (*Brit*) the act of tidying one's appearance: have a wash and brush-up
