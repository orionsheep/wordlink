**hunger** *n*
**1** a feeling of [[emptiness]] or weakness caused by lack of food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appetite]], [[emptiness]], [[hungriness]], [[ravenousness]]
**2** a lack of food that causes suffering or death: refugees dying of hunger and disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[starvation]], [[famine]], [[malnutrition]], [[undernourishment]]
**3** [[desire]] or [[craving]]: Europe's hunger for bullion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desire]], [[appetite]], [[craving]], [[ache]], [[lust]], [[yearning]], [[itch]], thirst
 *vb*
**4** **hunger for *or* after** to have a great [[desire]] for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hunger for or after something]]**: want, [[desire]], crave, long for, wish for, yearn for, hanker after, [[ache]] for
