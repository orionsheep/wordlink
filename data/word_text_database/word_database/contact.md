**contact** *n*
**1** the state or act of [[communication]]: the airport lost contact with the plane shortly before the crash
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communication]], [[link]], [[association]], [[connection]], [[correspondence]]
**2** the state or act of touching: rugby is a game of hard physical contact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[touch]], [[contiguity]]
**3** an [[acquaintance]] who might be useful in business
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[connection]], [[colleague]], [[associate]], [[liaison]], [[acquaintance]], [[confederate]]
**4** a [[connection]] between two electrical conductors in a circuit
**5** a person who has been exposed to a contagious disease
 *vb*
**6** to come or be in [[communication]] or [[touch]] with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: get *or* be in [[touch]] with, [[call]], [[reach]], [[approach]], [[write to]], [[speak to]], [[communicate with]], email *or* e-mail, [[text]], [[message]]
 **[[contactless]]** *adj*
