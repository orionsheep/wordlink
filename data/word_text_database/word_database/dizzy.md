**dizzy** *adj* **-zier**, **-ziest**
**1** feeling [[giddy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[giddy]], [[faint]], [[light-headed]], [[swimming]], [[reeling]], [[shaky]], [[wobbly]], [[off balance]]
**2** unable to think clearly; [[confused]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confused]], [[dazzled]], [[at sea]], [[bewildered]], [[muddled]], [[bemused]], [[dazed]], [[disorientated]]
**3** tending to cause giddiness or confusion
 *vb* **-zies**, **-zying**, **-zied**
**4** to cause to feel [[giddy]] or [[confused]]
 **[[dizzily]]** *adv* 
 **[[dizziness]]** *n*
