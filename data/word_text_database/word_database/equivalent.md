◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equal]], [[counterpart]], [[twin]], [[parallel]], [[match]], opposite number
 *adj*
**2** [[equal]] in value, quantity, significance, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equal]], [[same]], [[comparable]], [[parallel]], [[identical]], [[alike]], [[corresponding]], [[tantamount]]
**3** having the [[same]] or a similar effect or meaning
