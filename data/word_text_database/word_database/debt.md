**debt** *n*
**1** a sum of money owed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[debit]], [[commitment]], [[obligation]], [[liability]]
**2** **bad debt** a debt that is unlikely to be paid
**3** **[[in debt]]** owing money
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in debt]]**: owing, liable, in the red (*informal*), in arrears
**4** **in someone's debt** grateful to someone for his or her help: I couldn't have managed without you – I'm in your debt
