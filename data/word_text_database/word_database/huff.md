**huff** *n*
**1** a passing mood of anger or resentment: in a huff
 *vb*
**2** to blow or puff heavily
**3** (*draughts*) to remove (an opponent's draught) from the board for failure to make a capture
**4** **huffing and puffing** empty threats or objections
 **[[huffy]]** *adj* 
 **[[huffily]]** *adv*
