**sublime** *adj*
**1** causing deep emotions and feelings of wonder or joy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noble]], [[glorious]], [[high]], [[great]], [[grand]], [[elevated]], [[lofty]], [[exalted]]
**2** without equal; supreme
**3** of [[great]] moral, artistic, or spiritual value
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noble]], [[glorious]], [[high]], [[great]], [[grand]], [[elevated]], [[lofty]], [[exalted]]
 *n*
**4** **the sublime** something that is sublime
 *vb* **-liming**, **-limed**
**5** (*chem*, *physics*) to change directly from a solid to a vapour without first melting
 **[[sublimely]]** *adv*
