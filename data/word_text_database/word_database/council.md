**council** *n*
**1** a group meeting for discussion or consultation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[committee]], [[governing body]], [[board]]
**2** a legislative or advisory body: the United Nations Security Council
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[governing body]], [[parliament]], [[congress]], [[cabinet]], [[panel]], [[assembly]], [[convention]], [[conference]], [[runanga]] (*NZ*)
**3** (*Brit*) the local governing authority of a town or county
**4** (*Austral*) the local governing authority of a district or shire
 *adj*
**5** of or provided by a local council: a council house
