**paradise** *n*
**1** [[heaven]]; where the good go after death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heaven]], [[Promised Land]], [[Elysian fields]]
**2** the Garden of Eden
**3** any place or condition that fulfils a person's desires
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bliss]], [[delight]], [[heaven]] (*informal*), [[felicity]], [[utopia]]
