**celebrate** *vb* **-brating**, **-brated**
**1** to hold festivities: let's celebrate!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rejoice]], [[party]], [[enjoy yourself]], [[carouse]], [[live it up]] (*informal*), [[make merry]], [[put the flags out]], [[kill the fatted calf]]
**2** to hold festivities to mark (a happy event, birthday, or anniversary)
**3** to [[perform]] (a solemn or religious ceremony)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perform]], [[observe]], [[preside over]], [[officiate at]], [[solemnize]]
**4** to praise publicly: the novel is justly celebrated as a masterpiece
 **[[celebration]]** *n* 
 **[[celebratory]]** *adj*
