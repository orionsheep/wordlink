**barren** *adj*
**1** incapable of producing offspring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[infertile]], [[sterile]], [[unproductive]]
**2** unable to support the growth of crops, fruit, etc.: barren land
**3** unprofitable or unsuccessful: the team has had a barren season
**4** dull
 **[[barrenness]]** *n*
