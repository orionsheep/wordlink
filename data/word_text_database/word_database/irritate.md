**irritate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to [[annoy]] or [[anger]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annoy]], [[anger]], [[bother]], [[needle]] (*informal*), [[infuriate]], [[exasperate]], [[nettle]], [[irk]]
**2** (*pathol*) to cause (an organ or part of the body) to become inflamed or tender
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inflame]], [[pain]], [[rub]], [[scratch]], [[scrape]], [[chafe]]
**3** (*biol*) to stimulate (an organ) to respond in a characteristic manner
 **[[irritation]]** *n*
