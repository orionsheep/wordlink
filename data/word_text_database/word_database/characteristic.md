◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feature]], [[mark]], [[quality]], [[property]], [[attribute]], [[faculty]], [[trait]], [[quirk]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[typical]], [[special]], [[individual]], [[representative]], [[distinguishing]], [[distinctive]], [[peculiar]], [[singular]]
 **[[characteristically]]** *adv*
