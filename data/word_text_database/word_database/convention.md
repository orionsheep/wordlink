**convention** *n*
**1** the established view of what is thought to be proper behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[custom]], [[practice]], [[tradition]], [[code]], [[usage]], [[protocol]], [[etiquette]], [[propriety]], [[kawa]] (*NZ*), [[tikanga]] (*NZ*)
**2** an accepted rule or method: a convention used by printers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[custom]], [[practice]], [[tradition]], [[code]], [[usage]], [[protocol]], [[etiquette]], [[propriety]], [[kawa]] (*NZ*), [[tikanga]] (*NZ*)
**3** a formal [[agreement]] or [[contract]] between people and nations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[contract]], [[treaty]], [[bargain]], [[pact]], [[protocol]]
**4** a large formal [[assembly]] of a group with common interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assembly]], [[meeting]], [[council]], [[conference]], [[congress]], [[convocation]] (*formal*), [[hui]] (*NZ*), [[runanga]] (*NZ*)
