◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[follow]], [[track]], [[hunt]], [[chase]], [[dog]], [[shadow]], [[tail]] (*informal*), [[hound]]
**2** to try hard to achieve (some [[desire]] or aim)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[try for]], [[seek]], [[desire]], [[search for]], [[aim for]], [[work towards]], [[strive for]]
**3** to [[follow]] the guidelines of (a plan or policy)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engage in]], [[perform]], [[conduct]], [[carry on]], [[practise]]
**4** to apply oneself to (studies or interests)
**5** to [[follow]] persistently or [[seek]] to become acquainted with

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continue]], [[maintain]], [[carry on]], [[keep on]], [[persist in]], [[proceed in]], [[persevere in]]
