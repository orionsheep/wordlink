**disarm** *vb*
**1** to deprive of weapons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[demilitarize]], [[disband]], [[demobilize]], [[deactivate]]
**2** to win the confidence or affection of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[win over]], [[persuade]]
**3** (of a country) to decrease the size and capability of one's armed forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[demilitarize]], [[disband]], [[demobilize]], [[deactivate]]
