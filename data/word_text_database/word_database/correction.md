**correction** *n*
**1** an act or instance of correcting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rectification]], [[improvement]], [[amendment]], [[adjustment]], [[modification]], [[alteration]], [[emendation]]
**2** an [[alteration]] correcting something: corrections to the second proofs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rectification]], [[improvement]], [[amendment]], [[adjustment]], [[modification]], [[alteration]], [[emendation]]
**3** a [[reproof]] or [[punishment]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[punishment]], [[discipline]], [[reformation]], [[admonition]], [[chastisement]], [[reproof]], [[castigation]]
 **[[correctional]]** *adj*
