**omnibus** *n*, *pl* **-buses**
**1** a collection of works by one author or several works on a similar topic, reprinted in one volume
**2** Also called: **omnibus edition** a television or radio programme consisting of two or more episodes of a serial broadcast earlier in the week
**3** (*old-fashioned*) a bus
 *adj*
**4** consisting of or dealing with several different things at once: this year's version of an omnibus crime bill
