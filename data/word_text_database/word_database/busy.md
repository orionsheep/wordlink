**busy** *adj* **busier**, **busiest**
**1** actively or fully engaged; occupied
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[active]], [[industrious]], [[rushed off your feet]]
**2** crowded with or characterized by activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hectic]], [[full]], [[exacting]], [[energetic]]
**3** (of a telephone line) in use; engaged
 *vb* **busies**, **busying**, **busied**
**4** to make or keep (someone, esp. oneself) busy; occupy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[busy yourself]]**: occupy yourself, be engrossed, immerse yourself, involve yourself, absorb yourself, employ yourself, engage yourself
 **[[busily]]** *adv*
