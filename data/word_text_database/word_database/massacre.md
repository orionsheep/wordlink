**massacre** (**mass**-a-ker) *n*
**1** the wanton or savage killing of large numbers of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slaughter]], [[murder]], [[holocaust]], [[carnage]], [[extermination]], [[annihilation]], [[butchery]], [[blood bath]]
**2** (*informal*) an overwhelming defeat
 *vb* **-cring**, **-cred**
**3** to [[kill]] people indiscriminately in large numbers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slaughter]], [[kill]], [[murder]], [[butcher]], [[wipe out]], [[exterminate]], [[mow down]], [[cut to pieces]]
**4** (*informal*) to defeat overwhelmingly
