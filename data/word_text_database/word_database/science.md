**science** *n*
**1** the study of the nature and behaviour of the physical universe, based on observation, experiment, and measurement
**2** the knowledge obtained by these methods
**3** any particular branch of this knowledge: medical science
**4** any [[body of knowledge]] organized in a way resembling that of the physical sciences but concerned with other subjects: political science
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discipline]], [[body of knowledge]], [[branch of knowledge]]
