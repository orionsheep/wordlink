**slum** *n*
**1** an overcrowded and badly maintained house
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hovel]], [[ghetto]], [[shanty]]
**2** (*often pl*) a poor rundown overpopulated section of a city
 *vb* **slumming**, **slummed**
**3** to visit slums, esp. for curiosity
**4** **slum it** to temporarily and deliberately experience poorer places or conditions
 **[[slummy]]** *adj*
