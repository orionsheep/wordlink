**arrow** *n*
**1** a long slender pointed weapon, with feathers at one end, that is shot from a bow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dart]], [[flight]], [[bolt]], [[shaft]] (*archaic*), [[quarrel]]
**2** an arrow-shaped sign or symbol used to show the direction to a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pointer]], [[indicator]], [[marker]]
