**undo** *vb* **-does**, **-doing**, **-did**, **-done**
**1** to [[open]], unwrap or [[untie]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[open]], [[unfasten]], [[loose]], [[untie]], [[unbutton]], [[disentangle]]
**2** to [[reverse]] the effects of: all the work of the congress would be undone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverse]], [[cancel]], [[offset]], [[neutralize]], [[invalidate]], [[annul]]
**3** to cause the downfall of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ruin]], [[defeat]], [[destroy]], [[wreck]], [[shatter]], [[upset]], [[undermine]], [[overturn]]
