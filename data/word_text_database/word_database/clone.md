**clone** *n*
**1** a group of organisms or cells of the same genetic constitution that have been reproduced asexually from a single plant or animal
**2** (*informal*) a person who closely resembles another
**3** (*slang*) a mobile phone that has been given the electronic identity of an existing mobile phone, so that calls made on it are charged to that owner
 *vb* **[[cloning]]**, **cloned**
**4** to produce as a clone
**5** (*informal*) to produce near copies of (a person)
**6** (*slang*) to give (a mobile phone) the electronic identity of an existing mobile phone so that calls made on it are charged to that owner
