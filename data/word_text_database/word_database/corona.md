**corona** (kor-**rone**-a) *n*, *pl* **-nas*** or ***-nae** (-nee)
**1** a circle of light around a luminous body, usually the moon
**2** the outermost part of the sun's atmosphere, visible as a faint halo during a total eclipse
**3** a long cigar with blunt ends
**4** (*botany*) a crownlike part of some flowers on top of the seed or on the inner side of the corolla
**5** (*physics*) an electrical glow appearing around the surface of a charged conductor
