**1** the act of [[investing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[investing]], [[backing]], [[funding]], [[financing]], [[contribution]], [[speculation]], [[transaction]], [[expenditure]]
**2** money invested
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stake]], [[interest]], [[share]], [[concern]], [[portion]], [[ante]] (*informal*)
**3** something in which money is invested
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[buy]], [[asset]], [[acquisition]], [[venture]], [[risk]], [[gamble]]
