**country** *n*, *pl* **-tries**
**1** an area distinguished by its [[people]], culture, language, or government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nation]], [[state]], [[land]], [[commonwealth]], [[kingdom]], [[realm]], [[people]]
**2** the territory of a [[nation]] or [[state]]
**3** the [[people]] of a [[nation]] or [[state]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[people]], [[community]], [[nation]], [[society]], [[citizens]], [[inhabitants]], [[populace]], [[public]]
**4** the part of the [[land]] that is away from cities or industrial areas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[countryside]], [[provinces]], [[sticks]] (*informal*), [[farmland]], [[outback]] (*Austral** & **NZ*), [[green belt]], [[backwoods]], [[bush]] (*NZ** & **S African*)
**5** a person's native [[land]]
**6** same as **country and western**
**7** **across country** not keeping to roads
**8** **go to the country** (*Brit** & **NZ*) to dissolve Parliament and hold a general election
