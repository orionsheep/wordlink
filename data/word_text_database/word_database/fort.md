**fort** *n*
**1** a fortified building or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fortress]], [[keep]], [[camp]], [[tower]], [[castle]], [[garrison]], [[stronghold]], [[citadel]], [[fortified pa]] (*NZ*)
**2** **[[hold the fort]]** (*informal*) to [[keep]] things in operation during someone's absence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hold the fort]]**: take responsibility, cover, stand in, carry on, take over the reins, deputize, [[keep]] things on an even keel
