**mental** *adj*
**1** of, done by, or involving the mind: mental alertness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intellectual]], [[rational]], [[theoretical]], [[cognitive]], [[brain]], [[conceptual]], [[cerebral]]
**2** done in the mind without using speech or writing: mental arithmetic
**3** concerned with the care of people with mental health problems: a mental hospital
**4** (*slang*) extremely foolish or eccentric
 **[[mentally]]** *adv*
