**tin** *n*
**1** a soft silvery-white metallic element. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**Sn**
**2** a sealed airtight metal container used for preserving and storing food or drink: a cupboard full of packets and tins
**3** any metal container: a tin of paint
**4** the contents of a tin
**5** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) galvanized iron, used to make roofs
 *vb* **tinning**, **tinned**
**6** to put (food) into tins
