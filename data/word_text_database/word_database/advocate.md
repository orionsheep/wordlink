**advocate** *vb* **-cating**, **-cated**
**1** to [[recommend]] a course of action publicly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recommend]], [[support]], [[champion]], [[encourage]], [[propose]], [[promote]], [[advise]], endorse
 *n*
**2** a person who upholds or defends a cause or course of action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], spokesman *or* woman *or* person, [[champion]], [[defender]], [[campaigner]], [[promoter]], [[counsellor]], [[proponent]]
**3** a person who speaks on behalf of another in a court of law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lawyer]], [[attorney]], [[solicitor]], [[counsel]], [[barrister]]
**4** (*Scots law*) a [[barrister]]
