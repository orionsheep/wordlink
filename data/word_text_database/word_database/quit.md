**1** to [[stop]] (doing something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[give up]], [[cease]], [[end]], [[drop]], [[abandon]], [[halt]], [[discontinue]]
**2** to [[resign]] (from): the Prime Minister's decision to quit; he quit his job as an editor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resign]] (from), [[leave]], [[retire]] (from), [[pull out]] (of), [[step down]] (from) (*informal*), [[abdicate]]
**3** to [[leave]] (a place)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leave]], [[depart from]], [[go out of]], [[go away from]], [[pull out from]]
 **[[quitter]]** *n*
