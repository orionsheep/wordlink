**feature** *n*
**1** **features** any one of the parts of the [[face]], such as the nose, chin, or mouth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[face]], [[countenance]] (*literary*), [[physiognomy]], [[lineaments]]
**2** a prominent or distinctive part of something: regular debates were a feature of our final year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aspect]], [[quality]], [[characteristic]], [[property]], [[factor]], [[trait]], [[hallmark]], [[facet]]
**3** the main film in a cinema programme
**4** an [[item]] appearing regularly in a newspaper or magazine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[article]], [[report]], [[story]], [[piece]], [[item]], [[column]]
**5** a prominent [[story]] in a newspaper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[article]], [[report]], [[story]], [[piece]], [[item]], [[column]]
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**6** to have as a feature or make a feature of: this cooker features a fan-assisted oven
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spotlight]], [[present]], [[emphasize]], [[play up]], [[foreground]], [[give prominence to]]
**7** to give special prominence to: the film features James Mason as Rommel
 **[[featureless]]** *adj*
