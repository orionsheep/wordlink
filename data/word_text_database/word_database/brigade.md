**brigade** *n*
**1** a military formation smaller than a [[division]] and usually commanded by a brigadier
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corps]], [[company]], [[force]], [[unit]], [[division]], [[troop]], [[squad]], [[team]]
**2** a [[group]] of people organized for a certain task: a rescue brigade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[band]], [[squad]], [[organization]]
