**crumb** *n*
**1** a small [[fragment]] of bread or other dry food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bit]], [[grain]], [[fragment]], [[shred]], [[morsel]]
**2** a small [[bit]] or [[scrap]]: a crumb of comfort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[morsel]], [[scrap]], [[shred]], [[snippet]], [[soupçon]] (*French*)
