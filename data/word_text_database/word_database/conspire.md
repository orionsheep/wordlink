**conspire** *vb* **-spiring**, **-spired**
**1** to [[plan]] a crime together in secret
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[scheme]], [[intrigue]], [[manoeuvre]], [[contrive]], [[machinate]], [[plan]]
**2** to act together as if by design: the weather and the recession conspired to hit wine production and sales
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[work together]], [[combine]], [[contribute]], [[cooperate]], [[concur]], [[tend]]
 **[[conspirator]]** *n* 
 **[[conspiratorial]]** *adj*
