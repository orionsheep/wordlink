**again** *adv*
**1** another or a second time: I want to look at that atlas again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[once more]], [[another time]], [[anew]], [[afresh]]
**2** [[once more]] in a previously experienced state or condition: he pictured her again as she used to be
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[once more]], [[another time]], [[anew]], [[afresh]]
**3** [[in addition]] to the original amount: twice as much again
**4** on the other hand
**5** [[moreover]] or [[furthermore]]: she is talented and, again, hard-working
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[also]], [[in addition]], [[moreover]], [[besides]], [[furthermore]]
**6** **again and again** continually or repeatedly
