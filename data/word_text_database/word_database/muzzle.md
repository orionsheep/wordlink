**muzzle** *n*
**1** the projecting part of an animal's face, usually the jaws and nose
**2** a guard, made of plastic or strap of strong material, fitted over an animal's nose and jaws to prevent it biting or eating
**3** the front end of a gun barrel
 *vb* **-zling**, **-zled**
**4** to prevent from being heard or noticed: an attempt to muzzle the press
**5** to put a muzzle on an animal
