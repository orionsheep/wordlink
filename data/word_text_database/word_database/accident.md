**accident** *n*
**1** an unpleasant event that causes damage, injury, or death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crash]], [[smash]], [[wreck]], [[collision]]
**2** an unforeseen event or one without apparent cause: they had met in town by accident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[fortune]], [[luck]], [[fate]], [[hazard]], [[coincidence]], [[fluke]], [[fortuity]]
