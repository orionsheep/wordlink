**chant** *vb*
**1** to repeat (a slogan) over and over
**2** to [[sing]] or [[recite]] (a [[psalm]])
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sing]], [[chorus]], [[recite]], [[intone]], [[carol]]
 *n*
**3** a rhythmic or repetitious slogan repeated over and over, usually by more than one person
**4** a religious [[song]] with a short simple [[melody]] in which several words or syllables are sung on one note
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[song]], [[carol]], [[chorus]], [[melody]], [[psalm]]
