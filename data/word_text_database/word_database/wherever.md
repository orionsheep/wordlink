**wherever** *pron*
**1** at, in, or to every place or point which: I got a wonderful reception wherever I went
 *conj*
**2** in, to, or at whatever place: wherever they went, the conditions were harsh
 *adv*
**3** no matter where: we're going to find him, wherever he is
**4** (*informal*) at, in, or to an unknown or unspecified place: the jungles of Borneo or wherever
**5** an intensive form of *where,* used in questions: wherever have you been?
