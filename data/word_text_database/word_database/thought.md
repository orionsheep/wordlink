**thought** *vb*
**1** the past of **think**
 *n*
**2** the act or process of [[thinking]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thinking]], [[consideration]], [[reflection]], [[deliberation]], [[musing]], [[meditation]], [[rumination]], [[cogitation]]
**3** a [[concept]] or [[idea]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opinion]], [[view]], [[idea]], [[concept]], [[notion]], [[judgment]]
**4** ideas typical of a particular time or place: the development of Western intellectual thought
**5** detailed [[consideration]]: he appeared to give some sort of thought to the question
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consideration]], [[study]], [[attention]], [[care]], [[regard]], [[scrutiny]], [[heed]]
**6** an intention, [[hope]], or reason for doing something: his first thought was to call the guard and have the man arrested
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hope]], [[expectation]], [[prospect]], [[aspiration]], [[anticipation]]
