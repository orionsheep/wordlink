**sensational** *adj*
**1** causing intense feelings of shock, anger, or excitement: sensational allegations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amazing]], [[dramatic]], [[thrilling]], [[astounding]]
**2** (*informal*) extremely good: the views are sensational
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellent]], [[superb]] (*old-fashioned*), [[mean]] (*slang*), [[impressive]], [[smashing]] (*old-fashioned*), [[fabulous]] (*informal*), [[marvellous]], [[out of this world]] (*informal*), [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[funky]]
**3** of the senses or sensation
 **[[sensationally]]** *adv*
