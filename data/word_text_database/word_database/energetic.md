**energetic** *adj*
**1** having or showing energy and enthusiasm: an energetic campaigner for democracy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forceful]], [[determined]], [[active]], [[aggressive]], [[dynamic]], [[vigorous]], [[hard-hitting]], [[strenuous]]
**2** involving a lot of movement and physical effort: energetic exercise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strenuous]], [[hard]], [[taxing]], [[demanding]], [[tough]], [[exhausting]], [[vigorous]], [[arduous]]
 **[[energetically]]** *adv*
