**remain** *vb*
**1** to [[continue]] to be: the situation remains alarming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stay]], [[continue]], [[go on]], [[stand]], dwell (*formal*, *literary*)
**2** to [[stay behind]] or in the same place: to remain at home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stay behind]], [[wait]], [[delay]]
**3** to be left after use or the passage of time
**4** to be left to be done, said, etc.: whether this will be a long-term trend remains to be seen
