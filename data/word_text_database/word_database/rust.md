**rust** *n*
**1** a reddish-brown oxide coating formed on iron or steel by the action of oxygen and moisture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corrosion]], [[oxidation]]
**2** a fungal disease of plants which produces a reddish-brown discoloration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mildew]], [[must]], [[mould]], [[rot]], blight
 *adj*
**3** reddish-brown
 *vb*
**4** to become coated with a layer of rust
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corrode]], [[oxidize]]
**5** to deteriorate through lack of use: my brain had rusted up
