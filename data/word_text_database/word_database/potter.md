**potter**^2 * or * (*esp* *US** & **Canad*) **putter**^2 *vb*
**1** to move with little energy or direction: I saw him pottering off to see to his canaries
**2** **potter about *or* around *or* away** to be busy in a pleasant but aimless way: he potters away doing God knows what all day
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mess about]], [[tinker]], [[dabble]], [[footle]] (*informal*)
