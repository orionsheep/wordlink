**quantity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a specified or definite [[amount]] or [[number]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amount]], [[lot]], [[total]], [[sum]], [[part]], [[number]]
**2** the aspect of anything that can be measured, weighed, or counted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[size]], [[measure]], [[mass]], [[volume]], [[length]], [[capacity]], [[extent]], [[bulk]]
**3** a large [[amount]]
**4** (*maths*) an entity having a magnitude that may be denoted by a numerical expression
