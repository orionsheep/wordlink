**reproduction** *n*
**1** (*biol*) a process by which an animal or plant produces one or more individuals similar to itself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breeding]], [[increase]], [[generation]], [[multiplication]]
**2** a [[copy]] of a work of art
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]], [[picture]], [[print]], [[replica]], [[imitation]], [[duplicate]], [[facsimile]]
**3** the quality of sound from an audio system
**4** the act or process of reproducing
 *adj*
**5** made in [[imitation]] of an earlier style: reproduction furniture
 **[[reproductive]]** *adj*
