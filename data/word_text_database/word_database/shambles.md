**shambles** *n*
**1** a disorderly or badly organized event or place: the bathroom was a shambles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chaos]], [[mess]], [[disorder]], [[confusion]], [[muddle]], [[havoc]] (*informal*), [[disarray]], [[madhouse]] (*informal*)
**2** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a butcher's slaughterhouse
**3** (*old-fashioned*) any scene of great slaughter
