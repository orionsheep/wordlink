**barrel** *n*
**1** a cylindrical container, usually with rounded sides and flat ends, and held together by metal hoops
**2** a unit of capacity of varying amount in different industries
**3** the tube through which the bullet of a firearm is fired
**4** **over a barrel** (*informal*) powerless
 *vb* **-relling**, **-relled** (*US*) **-reling**, **-reled**
**5** to put into a barrel or barrels
