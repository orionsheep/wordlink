◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pictures]], [[movies]], [[picture-house]], [[flicks]] (*slang*)
**2** **the cinema**
**A** the art or business of making [[films]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[films]], [[pictures]], [[movies]], [[the big screen]] (*informal*), [[motion pictures]], the silver screen
 **B** [[films]] collectively
 **[[cinematic]]** *adj*
