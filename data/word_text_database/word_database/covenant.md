**covenant** (**kuv**-ven-ant) *n*
**1** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a formal [[agreement]] to make an annual payment to charity
**2** (*law*) a formal sealed [[agreement]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise]], [[contract]], [[agreement]], [[commitment]], [[arrangement]], [[pledge]], [[pact]]
**3** (*Bible*) God's [[promise]] to the Israelites and their [[commitment]] to worship him alone
 *vb*
**4** to agree by a legal covenant
 **[[covenanter]]** *n*
