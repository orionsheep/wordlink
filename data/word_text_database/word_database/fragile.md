**fragile** *adj*
**1** able to be broken or damaged easily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unstable]], [[weak]], [[vulnerable]], [[delicate]], [[uncertain]], [[insecure]], [[precarious]], [[flimsy]]
**2** in a weakened physical state: you're looking a bit fragile this morning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unwell]], [[poorly]] (*informal*), [[weak]], [[delicate]], [[crook]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[shaky]], [[frail]], [[feeble]], [[sickly]]
 **[[fragility]]** *n*
