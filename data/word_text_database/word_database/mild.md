**mild** *adj*
**1** (of a taste or sensation) not strong; [[bland]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bland]], [[thin]], [[smooth]], [[tasteless]], [[insipid]], [[flavourless]]
**2** gentle or [[temperate]] in character, climate, or behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[temperate]], [[warm]], [[calm]], [[moderate]], [[tranquil]], [[balmy]]
**3** not extreme; [[moderate]]: mild criticism of senior officers
**4** feeble; unassertive: a mild protest
 *n*
**5** (*Brit*) a dark beer flavoured with fewer hops than bitter
 **[[mildly]]** *adv*
