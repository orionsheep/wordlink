**cripple** *n* (*offensive*)
**1** a person who is unable to walk or walk easily
**2** a person who is or seems deficient in some way: an emotional cripple
 *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**3** to [[disable]] (a person or animal)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disable]], [[paralyse]], [[lame]], [[maim]], [[incapacitate]], [[weaken]], [[hamstring]]
**4** to [[damage]] (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[destroy]], [[ruin]], [[spoil]], [[impair]], [[put paid to]], [[put out of action]]
 **[[crippled]]** *adj* 
 **[[crippling]]** *adj*
