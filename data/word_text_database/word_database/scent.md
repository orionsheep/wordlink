**scent** *n*
**1** a distinctive [[smell]], esp. a pleasant one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fragrance]], [[smell]], [[perfume]], [[bouquet]], [[aroma]], [[odour]]
**2** a [[smell]] left in passing, by which a person or animal may be traced
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trail]], [[track]], [[spoor]]
**3** a [[trail]] or series of clues by which something is followed: he must have got on to the scent of the story through you
**4** [[perfume]]
 *vb*
**5** to become aware of by smelling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smell]], [[sense]], [[detect]], [[sniff]], [[discern]], [[nose out]]
**6** to suspect: he [[scented]] the beginnings of irritation in the car
**7** to fill with [[odour]] or [[fragrance]]
 **[[scented]]** *adj*
