**constitution** *n*
**1** the principles on which a state is governed
**2** **the Constitution** (in certain countries) the statute embodying such principles
**3** a person's [[state of health]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state of health]], [[build]], [[body]], [[frame]], [[physique]], [[physical condition]]
**4** the [[make-up]] or [[structure]] of something: changes in the very constitution of society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[form]], [[nature]], [[make-up]], [[composition]], [[character]], [[disposition]]
