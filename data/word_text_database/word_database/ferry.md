**ferry** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** a [[boat]] for transporting passengers and vehicles across a body of water, esp. as a regular service
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ferry boat]], [[boat]], [[ship]], [[passenger boat]], [[packet boat]], [[packet]]
**2** such a service
 *vb* **-ries**, **-rying**, **-ried**
**3** to [[transport]] or go by ferry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transport]], [[bring]], [[carry]], [[ship]], [[take]], [[run]], [[shuttle]], [[convey]]
**4** to [[transport]] (passengers or goods) on a regular basis
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transport]], [[bring]], [[carry]], [[ship]], [[take]], [[run]], [[shuttle]], [[convey]]
 **[[ferryman]]** *n*
