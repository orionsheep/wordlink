**liquor** *n*
**1** [[spirits]] or other alcoholic drinks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alcohol]], [[drink]], [[spirits]], [[booze]] (*informal*), [[hard stuff]] (*informal*), [[strong drink]]
**2** any [[liquid]] in which food has been cooked
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[juice]], [[stock]], [[liquid]], [[extract]], [[broth]]
