**notch** *n*
**1** a V-shaped [[cut]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut]], [[nick]], [[incision]], [[indentation]], [[mark]], [[score]], [[cleft]]
**2** (*informal*) a [[step]] or [[level]]: the economy moved up another notch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[level]], [[step]], [[degree]], grade
 *vb*
**3** to [[cut]] a notch in
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut]], [[mark]], [[score]], [[nick]], [[scratch]], [[indent]]
**4** **notch up** (*informal*) to [[score]] or achieve: he notched up a hat trick of wins
