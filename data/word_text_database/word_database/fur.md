**fur** *n*
**1** the dense coat of fine silky hairs on many mammals
**2** the skin of certain animals, with the hair left on
**3** a garment made of fur
**4** **make the fur fly** to cause a scene or disturbance
**5** (*informal*) a whitish coating on the tongue, caused by illness
**6** (*Brit*) a deposit on the insides of water pipes or kettles, caused by hard water
 *vb* **furring**, **furred**
**7** Also: **fur up** to cover or become covered with a furlike deposit
