**lack** *n*
**1** [[shortage]] or [[absence]] of something required or desired: a lack of confidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shortage]], [[want]], [[absence]], [[deficiency]], [[need]], [[inadequacy]], [[scarcity]], dearth
 *vb*
**2** (often foll. by *in*) to be short (of) or have [[need]] (of): lacking in sparkle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[miss]], [[want]], [[need]], [[require]], [[not have]], [[be without]], [[be short of]], [[be in need of]]
