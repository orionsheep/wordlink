**picnic** *n*
**1** an [[excursion]] on which people bring food to be eaten in the open air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excursion]], [[barbecue]], [[barbie]] (*informal*), [[cookout]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[alfresco meal]], [[clambake]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[outdoor meal]], [[outing]]
**2** an informal meal eaten out-of-doors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excursion]], [[barbecue]], [[barbie]] (*informal*), [[cookout]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[alfresco meal]], [[clambake]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[outdoor meal]], [[outing]]
**3** **no picnic** (*informal*) a hard or disagreeable task
 *vb* **-nicking**, **-nicked**
**4** to eat or take part in a picnic
 **[[picnicker]]** *n*
