**envelope** *n*
**1** a flat [[covering]] of paper, that can be sealed, used to enclose a letter, etc.
**2** any [[covering]], [[wrapper]], or enclosing structure: an envelope of filo pastry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrapping]], [[casing]], [[case]], [[covering]], [[cover]], [[jacket]], [[sleeve]], [[wrapper]]
**3** (*geom*) a curve that is tangential to each one of a group of curves
