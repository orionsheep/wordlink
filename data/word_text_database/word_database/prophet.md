**prophet** *n*
**1** a person supposedly chosen by God to pass on His message
**2** a person who predicts the future: a prophet of doom
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soothsayer]], [[forecaster]], [[diviner]], [[oracle]], [[seer]], [[sibyl]], [[prophesier]]
**3** a spokesman for, or advocate of, some cause: a prophet of revolution
 **[[prophetess]]** *fem n*
