**river** *n*
**1** a large natural [[stream]] of fresh water flowing along a definite course into the sea, a lake, or a larger river. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**fluvial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stream]], [[brook]], [[creek]] (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*), [[waterway]], [[tributary]], [[burn]] (*Scot*)
**2** an abundant [[stream]] or [[flow]]: rivers of blood
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[rush]], [[flood]], [[spate]], [[torrent]]
