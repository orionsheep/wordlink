**running** *adj*
**1** maintained continuously: a running battle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continuous]], [[constant]], [[perpetual]], [[uninterrupted]], [[incessant]]
**2** without interruption: for the third day running
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[in succession]], [[unbroken]]
**3**
**A** [[flowing]]: rinse them under cold running water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flowing]], [[moving]], [[streaming]], coursing
 **B** supplied through a tap: there is no electricity, [[no running water]], [[and no telephone]]
**4** operating: running costs
**5** discharging pus: a running sore
**6** accomplished at a run: a running jump
**7** [[moving]] or slipping easily, as a rope or a knot
 *n*
**8** the act of [[moving]] or [[flowing]] quickly
**9** [[management]] or [[organization]]: the running of the farm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[management]], [[control]], [[administration]], [[direction]], [[leadership]], [[organization]], [[supervision]]
**10** the [[operation]] or [[maintenance]] of a machine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[working]], [[performance]], [[operation]], [[functioning]], [[maintenance]]
**11** **in *or* out of the running** having *or* not having a good chance in a competition
**12** **make the running** to set the pace in a competition or race
