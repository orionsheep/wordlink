**perish** *vb*
**1** to [[be destroyed]] or die
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be destroyed]], [[fall]], [[decline]], [[collapse]], [[disappear]], [[vanish]]
**2** to cause to suffer: we were perished with cold
**3** to [[rot]] or cause to [[rot]]: to prevent your swimsuit from perishing, rinse it in clean water before it dries
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rot]], [[waste away]], [[decay]], [[disintegrate]], [[decompose]], [[moulder]]
