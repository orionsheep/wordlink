**rain** *n*
**1**
**A** water falling from the sky in drops formed by the condensation of water vapour in the atmosphere
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rainfall]], [[fall]], [[showers]], [[deluge]], [[drizzle]], [[downpour]], [[raindrops]], cloudburst
 **B** a [[fall]] of rain <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**pluvial**
**2** a large quantity of anything falling rapidly: a rain of stones descended on the police
**3** **(come) rain or shine** regardless of circumstances
**4** **right as rain** (*informal*) perfectly all right
 *vb*
**5** to [[fall]] as rain: it's raining back home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pour]], [[pelt]] (down), [[teem]], [[bucket down]] (*informal*), [[drizzle]], [[come down in buckets]] (*informal*)
**6** to [[fall]] rapidly and in large quantities: steel rungs and sawdust raining down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fall]], [[shower]], [[be dropped]], [[sprinkle]], [[be deposited]]
**7** **rained off** cancelled or postponed because of rain. US and Canad term: **rained out**
 **[[rainy]]** *adj*
