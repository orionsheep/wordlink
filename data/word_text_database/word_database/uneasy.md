**2** (of a condition) [[precarious]] or [[insecure]]: an uneasy peace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[precarious]], [[strained]], [[uncomfortable]], [[tense]], [[awkward]], [[shaky]], [[insecure]]
**3** (of a thought or feeling) disquieting
