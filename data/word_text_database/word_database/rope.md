**rope** *n*
**1** a fairly thick [[cord]] made of intertwined fibres or wire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cord]], [[line]], [[cable]], [[strand]], [[hawser]]
**2** a row of objects fastened to form a [[line]]: a twenty-inch rope of pearls
**3** **[[know the ropes]]** to have a thorough understanding of a particular activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[know the ropes]]**: be experienced, be knowledgeable, be an old hand
**4** **the rope**
**A** a rope noose used for hanging someone
 **B** death by hanging
 *vb* **roping**, **roped**
**5** to tie with a rope
**6** **rope off** to enclose or divide with a rope
