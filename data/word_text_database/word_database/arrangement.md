**arrangement** *n*
**1** a [[preparation]] or [[plan]] made for an event: travel arrangements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[planning]], [[provision]], [[preparation]]
**2** an [[agreement]] or a [[plan]] to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[contract]], [[settlement]], [[appointment]], [[compromise]], [[deal]] (*informal*), [[pact]], [[compact]]
**3** a thing composed of various ordered parts: a flower arrangement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[system]], [[structure]], [[organization]], [[exhibition]], [[presentation]], [[classification]], [[alignment]]
**4** the form in which things are arranged
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[system]], [[structure]], [[organization]], [[exhibition]], [[presentation]], [[classification]], [[alignment]]
**5** an [[adaptation]] of a piece of music for performance in a different way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adaptation]], [[score]], [[version]], [[interpretation]], [[instrumentation]], [[orchestration]], [[harmonization]]
