**rally** *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**1** a large [[gathering]] of people for a [[meeting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gathering]], [[convention]], [[meeting]], [[congress]], [[assembly]], [[hui]] (*NZ*)
**2** a marked [[recovery]] of strength, as during illness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recovery]], [[improvement]], [[revival]], [[recuperation]]
**3** (*Stock Exchange*) a sharp increase in price or trading activity after a decline
**4** (*tennis*, *squash*, *etc.*) an exchange of several shots before one player wins the point
**5** a car-driving competition on public roads
 *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
**6** to bring or come together after being dispersed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gather together]], [[unite]], [[regroup]], [[reorganize]], [[reassemble]]
**7** to bring or come together for a common cause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gather together]], [[unite]], [[regroup]], [[reorganize]], [[reassemble]]
**8** to summon up (one's strength or spirits)
**9** to [[recover]] (sometimes only temporarily) from an illness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recover]], [[improve]], [[revive]], [[get better]], [[recuperate]]
**10** (*Stock Exchange*) to increase sharply after a decline
