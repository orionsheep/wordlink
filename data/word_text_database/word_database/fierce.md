**fierce** *adj*
**1** very [[aggressive]] or angry: a fierce dog
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ferocious]], [[wild]], [[dangerous]], [[cruel]], [[savage]], [[brutal]], [[aggressive]], [[menacing]], [[aggers]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** [[intense]] or [[strong]]: a fierce wind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intense]], [[strong]], [[keen]], [[relentless]], [[cut-throat]]
 **[[fiercely]]** *adv*
