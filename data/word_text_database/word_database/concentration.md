**concentration** *n*
**1** intense mental [[application]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attention]], [[application]], [[absorption]], [[single-mindedness]], [[intentness]]
**2** the act of concentrating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[focusing]], [[centring]], [[consolidation]], [[convergence]], [[bringing to bear]], [[intensification]], [[centralization]]
**3** something that is concentrated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[convergence]], [[collection]], [[mass]], [[cluster]], [[accumulation]], [[aggregation]]
**4** the amount or proportion of a substance in a mixture or solution
