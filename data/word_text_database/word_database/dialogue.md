**dialogue** * or * (*US*, *sometimes*) **dialog** *n*
**1** [[conversation]] between two people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conversation]], [[discussion]], [[communication]], [[discourse]]
**2** a [[conversation]] in a literary or dramatic work
**3** a [[discussion]] between representatives of two nations or groups
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discussion]], [[conference]], [[exchange]], [[debate]]
