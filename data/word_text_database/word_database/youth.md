**youth** *n*
**1** the period between childhood and maturity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immaturity]], [[adolescence]], boyhood *or* girlhood, [[salad days]]
**2** the quality or condition of being young, immature, or inexperienced: his youth told against him in the contest
**3** a [[young man]] or [[boy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boy]], [[lad]], [[youngster]], [[kid]] (*informal*), [[teenager]], [[young man]], [[adolescent]], [[teen]] (*informal*)
**4** young people collectively: there is still hope for today's youth
**5** the freshness, vigour, or vitality associated with being young
