**imprint** *n*
**1** a [[mark]] or [[impression]] produced by pressing, printing, or stamping
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[impression]], [[stamp]], [[indentation]]
**2** the publisher's name and address, often with the date of publication, printed on the title page of a book
 *vb*
**3** to produce (a [[mark]]) by pressing, printing, or stamping: T-shirts imprinted with slogans
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engrave]], [[print]], [[stamp]], [[impress]], [[etch]], [[emboss]]
**4** to establish firmly; [[impress]]: he couldn't dislodge the images imprinted on his brain
