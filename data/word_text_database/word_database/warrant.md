**warrant** *n*
**1** an official [[authorization]] for some action or decision: Scotland Yard today issued a warrant for the arrest of this man
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[authorization]], [[permit]], [[licence]], [[permission]], [[authority]], [[sanction]]
**2** a document that certifies or guarantees something, such as a receipt or [[licence]]
 *vb*
**3** to make necessary: we've no hard evidence to warrant a murder investigation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call for]], [[demand]], [[require]], [[merit]], [[rate]], [[earn]], [[deserve]], [[permit]]
