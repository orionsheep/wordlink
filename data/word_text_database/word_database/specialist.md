**specialist** *n*
**1** a person who is an [[expert]] in a particular activity or subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expert]], [[authority]], [[professional]], [[master]], [[consultant]], [[guru]], [[buff]] (*informal*), [[connoisseur]], [[fundi]] (*S African*)
**2** a doctor who concentrates on treating one particular category of diseases or the diseases of one particular part of the body: an eye specialist
 *adj*
**3** particular to or concentrating on one subject or activity: a specialist comic shop
