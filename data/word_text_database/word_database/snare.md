**snare** *n*
**1** a [[trap]] for birds or small animals, usually a flexible loop that is drawn tight around the prey
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trap]], [[net]], [[wire]], [[gin]], [[noose]]
**2** anything that traps someone or something unawares
 *vb* **snaring**, **snared**
**3** to [[catch]] in or as if in a snare
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trap]], [[catch]], [[net]], [[wire]], [[seize]], [[entrap]]
