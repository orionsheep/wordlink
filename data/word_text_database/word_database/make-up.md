**make-up** *n*
**1** [[cosmetics]], such as [[powder]] or lipstick
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cosmetics]], [[paint]] (*informal*), [[powder]], [[face]] (*informal*), [[greasepaint]] (*theatre*)
**2** the [[cosmetics]] used by an actor to adapt his or her appearance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cosmetics]], [[paint]] (*informal*), [[powder]], [[face]] (*informal*), [[greasepaint]] (*theatre*)
**3** the [[arrangement]] of the parts of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[organization]], [[arrangement]], [[construction]], [[assembly]], [[constitution]], [[format]], [[composition]]
**4** mental or physical [[constitution]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nature]], [[character]], [[constitution]], [[temperament]], disposition
 *vb* **[[make up]]**
**5** to form or constitute: these arguments [[make up]] the case for the defence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make up something]]**: form, account for, constitute, compose, comprise
**6** to devise or compose, sometimes with the intent to deceive: she was well known for making up stories about herself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make something up]]**: invent, create, construct, compose, frame, coin, devise, originate
**7** to supply what is lacking in; complete: I'll [[make up]] the difference
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make up something]]**: complete, supply, fill, round off
**8** Also: **make it up** to settle differences amicably
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make up]]**: settle your differences, bury the hatchet, call it quits, declare a truce, be friends again
**9** **[[make up]] for** to compensate for: one good year can [[make up]] for several bad ones
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make up for something]]**: compensate for, make amends for, atone for, balance out, offset, make recompense for
**10** to apply [[cosmetics]] to the [[face]]
**11** **[[make up]] to** (*informal*)
**A** to make friendly overtures to
 **B** to flirt with
