**handicap** *n*
**1** (*old-fashioned*, *offensive*) a physical or mental disability
**2** something that makes progress difficult
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disadvantage]], [[barrier]], [[restriction]], [[obstacle]], [[limitation]], [[drawback]], [[stumbling block]], [[impediment]]
**3**
**A** a contest in which competitors are given advantages or disadvantages in an attempt to equalize their chances
 **B** the [[advantage]] or [[disadvantage]] given
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advantage]], [[head start]]
**4** (*golf*) the number of strokes by which a player's averaged score exceeds par for the course
 *vb* **-capping**, **-capped**
**5** to make it difficult for (someone) to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hinder]], [[limit]], [[restrict]], [[burden]], [[hamstring]], [[hamper]], [[hold back]], [[impede]]
