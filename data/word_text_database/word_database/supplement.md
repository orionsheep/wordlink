**supplement** *n*
**1** an [[addition]] designed to make something more adequate
**2** a magazine distributed free with a newspaper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pull-out]], [[insert]]
**3** a section added to a publication to supply further information or correct errors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appendix]], [[add-on]], [[postscript]]
**4** (of money) an additional payment to obtain special services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[addition]], extra
 *vb*
**5** to provide an [[addition]] to (something), esp. in order to make up for an inadequacy: she supplements her salary by renting her spare bedroom 
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[add to]], [[reinforce]], [[augment]], [[extend]]
 **[[supplementary]]** *adj*
