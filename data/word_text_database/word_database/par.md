**par** *n*
**1** the usual or average condition: I feel slightly below par most of the time
**2** (*golf*) a standard score for a hole or course that a good player should make: four under par with two holes to play
**3** (*finance*) the established value of the unit of one national currency in terms of the unit of another
**4** **on a par with** equal or equivalent to: an environmental disaster on a par with Chernobyl
**5** **par for the course** to be expected: random acts of violence were par for the course in the capital
