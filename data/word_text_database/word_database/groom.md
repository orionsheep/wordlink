**groom** *n*
**1** a person employed to [[clean]] and look after horses
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stableman]], [[stableboy]], hostler *or* ostler (*archaic*)
**2** short for **bridegroom**
 *vb*
**3** to [[clean]] and smarten (a horse or other animal)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brush]], [[clean]], [[tend]], [[rub down]], [[curry]]
**4** to keep (oneself or one's appearance) [[clean]] and [[tidy]]: carefully groomed hair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smarten up]], [[clean]], [[tidy]], [[preen]], [[spruce up]], [[primp]]
**5** to [[train]] (someone) for a particular task or occupation: groomed for future leadership
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[train]], [[prime]], [[prepare]], [[coach]], [[ready]], [[educate]], [[drill]], [[nurture]]
 **[[grooming]]** *n*
