**fetch** *vb*
**1** to go after and [[bring]] back
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring]], [[pick up]], [[collect]], [[go and get]], [[get]], [[carry]], [[deliver]], [[transport]]
**2** to be sold for (a certain price): Impressionist pictures fetch very high prices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sell for]], [[make]], [[raise]], [[earn]], [[realize]], [[go for]], [[yield]], [[bring in]]
**3** (*informal*) to give someone (a blow or slap)
**4** **fetch and [[carry]]** to perform menial tasks
