**fit**^2 *n*
**1** a sudden [[attack]] or [[convulsion]], such as an epileptic [[seizure]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seizure]], [[attack]], [[bout]], [[spasm]], [[convulsion]], [[paroxysm]]
**2** a sudden short [[burst]] or [[spell]]: fits of laughter; a fit of pique
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bout]], [[burst]], [[outbreak]], [[outburst]], [[spell]]
**3** **in fits and starts** in spasmodic spells
**4** **have a fit** (*informal*) to become very angry
