**union** *n*
**1** the act of merging two or more things to become one, or the state of being merged in such a way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[joining]], [[uniting]], [[unification]], [[combination]], [[coalition]], [[merger]], [[mixture]], [[blend]]
**2** short for **trade union**
**3** an [[association]] of individuals or groups for a common purpose: the Scripture Union
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alliance]], [[league]], [[association]], [[coalition]], [[federation]], [[confederacy]]
**4**
**A** an [[association]] or society: the Students' union
 **B** the buildings of such an organization
**5** marriage or sexual intercourse
**6** (*maths*) a set containing all the members of two given sets
**7** (in 19th-century England) a workhouse maintained by a number of parishes
 *adj*
**8** of a trade union
