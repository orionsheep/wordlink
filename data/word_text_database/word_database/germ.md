**germ** *n*
**1** a tiny living thing, esp. one that causes disease: a diphtheria germ
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[microbe]], [[virus]], [[bug]] (*informal*), [[bacterium]], [[bacillus]], [[microorganism]]
**2** the [[beginning]] from which something may develop: the germ of a book
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beginning]], [[root]], [[seed]], [[origin]], [[spark]], [[embryo]], [[rudiment]]
