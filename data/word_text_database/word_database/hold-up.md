**hold-up** *n*
**1** an armed [[robbery]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[robbery]], [[theft]], [[mugging]] (*informal*), stick-up (*slang*, *chiefly* *US*)
**2** a [[delay]]: a traffic hold-up
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delay]], [[wait]], [[hitch]], [[setback]], [[snag]], [[traffic jam]], [[stoppage]], bottleneck
 *vb* **hold up**
**3** to [[delay]]
**4** to support (an object)
**5** to stop and rob (someone), using a weapon
**6** to exhibit or present (something) as an example: he was held up as a model professional
