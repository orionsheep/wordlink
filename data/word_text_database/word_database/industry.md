**industry** *n*, *pl* **-tries**
**1** the work and process involved in manufacture: Japanese industry increased output considerably last year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[business]], [[production]], [[manufacturing]], [[trade]], [[commerce]]
**2** a branch of commercial enterprise concerned with the manufacture of a specified product: the steel industry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trade]], [[world]], [[business]], [[service]], [[line]], [[field]], [[profession]], [[occupation]]
**3** the quality of working hard
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diligence]], [[effort]], [[labour]], [[hard work]], [[trouble]], [[activity]], [[application]], [[endeavour]]
