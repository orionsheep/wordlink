**twiddle** *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**1** to twirl or fiddle, often in an idle way: twiddling the knobs of a radio
**2** **twiddle one's thumbs**
**A** to rotate one's thumbs around one another, when bored or impatient
 **B** to be bored, with nothing to do
 *n*
**3** an unnecessary decoration, esp. a curly one
