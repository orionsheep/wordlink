**ignorant** *adj*
**1** lacking in knowledge or education
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneducated]], [[illiterate]]
**2** [[rude]] through lack of knowledge of good manners: an ignorant remark
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insensitive]], [[rude]], [[crass]]
**3** **ignorant of** lacking in awareness or knowledge of: ignorant of Asian culture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uninformed of]], [[unaware of]], [[oblivious to]], [[innocent of]], [[unconscious of]], [[inexperienced of]], [[uninitiated about]], [[unenlightened about]]
