**bang** *n*
**1** a short loud explosive noise, such as the report of a gun
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explosion]], [[pop]], [[clash]], [[crack]], [[blast]], [[slam]], [[discharge]], [[thump]]
**2** a hard [[blow]] or loud [[knock]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow]], [[knock]], [[stroke]], [[punch]], [[bump]], [[sock]] (*slang*), [[smack]], [[thump]]
**3** (*vulgar*, *slang*) an act of sexual intercourse
**4** **with a bang** successfully: the party went with a bang
 *vb*
**5** to [[hit]] or [[knock]], esp. with a loud noise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hit]], [[strike]], [[knock]], [[belt]] (*informal*), [[slam]], [[thump]], [[clatter]], beat *or* [[knock]] seven bells out of (*informal*)
**6** to close (a door) noisily
**7** to make or cause to make a loud noise, as of an [[explosion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resound]], [[boom]], [[explode]], [[thunder]], [[thump]], [[clang]]
**8** (*vulgar*, *slang*) to have sexual intercourse with
 *adv*
**9** with a sudden impact: the car drove bang into a lamppost
**10** [[precisely]]: bang in the middle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exactly]], [[straight]], [[square]], [[squarely]], [[precisely]], [[slap]], [[smack]] (*informal*), [[plumb]] (*informal*)
