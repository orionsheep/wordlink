**police** *n*
**1** (often preceded by *the*) the organized civil force in a state which keeps law and order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the law]] (*informal*), [[police force]], [[constabulary]], [[the fuzz]] (*slang*), boys in blue (*informal*, *archaic*), [[the Old Bill]] (*slang*), [[the rozzers]] (*slang*)
**2** the men and women who are members of such a force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the law]] (*informal*), [[police force]], [[constabulary]], [[the fuzz]] (*slang*), boys in blue (*informal*, *archaic*), [[the Old Bill]] (*slang*), [[the rozzers]] (*slang*)
**3** an organized body with a similar function: security police
 *vb* **-licing**, **-liced**
**4** to maintain order or [[control]] by means of a [[police force]] or similar body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[patrol]], [[guard]], [[watch]], [[protect]], [[regulate]]
