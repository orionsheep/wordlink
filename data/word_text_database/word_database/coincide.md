**coincide** *vb* **-ciding**, **-cided**
**1** to happen at the same time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occur simultaneously]], [[coexist]], [[synchronize]], [[be concurrent]]
**2** to [[agree]] or [[correspond]] exactly: what she had said coincided exactly with his own thinking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agree]], [[match]], [[accord]], [[square]], [[correspond]], [[tally]], [[concur]], [[harmonize]]
**3** to occupy the same place in space
