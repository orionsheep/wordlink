**spout** *vb*
**1** (of a liquid or flames) to pour out in a stream or jet
**2** (*informal*) to talk about (something) in a boring way or without much thought
 *n*
**3** a projecting tube or lip for pouring liquids
**4** a stream or jet of liquid: a spout of steaming water
**5** **up the spout** (*slang*)
**A** ruined or lost: the motor industry is up the spout
 **B** pregnant
