**attend** *vb*
**1** to [[be present]] at (an event)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be present]], [[go to]], [[visit]], [[frequent]], [[haunt]], [[appear at]], [[turn up at]], [[patronize]]
**2** to go regularly to a school, college, etc.
**3** to look after: the actors lounged in their canvas chairs, attended by sycophants
**4** to [[pay attention]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pay attention]], [[listen]], [[hear]], [[mark]], [[note]], [[observe]], [[heed]], [[pay heed]]
**5** **attend to** to apply oneself to: I've a few things I must attend to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[attend to something]]**: apply yourself to, concentrate on, look after, take care of, see to, get to work on, devote yourself to, occupy yourself with
