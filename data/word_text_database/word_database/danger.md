**danger** *n*
**1** the possibility that someone may be injured or killed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jeopardy]], [[vulnerability]]
**2** someone or something that may cause injury or harm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hazard]], [[risk]], [[threat]], [[menace]], [[peril]], [[pitfall]]
**3** a likelihood that something unpleasant will happen: the danger of flooding
