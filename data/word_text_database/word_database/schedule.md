**schedule** *n*
**1** a timed [[plan]] of procedure for a project
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[programme]], [[agenda]], [[calendar]], [[timetable]]
**2** a list of details or items: the schedule of priorities
**3** a [[timetable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[programme]], [[agenda]], [[calendar]], [[timetable]]
 *vb* **-uling**, **-uled**
**4** to [[plan]] and [[arrange]] (something) to happen at a certain time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[set up]], [[book]], [[programme]], [[arrange]], [[organize]]
**5** to make a schedule or include in a schedule
