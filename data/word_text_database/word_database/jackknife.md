**jackknife** *vb* **-knifing**, **-knifed**
**1** (of an articulated lorry) to go out of control in such a way that the trailer swings round at a sharp angle to the cab
 *n*, *pl* **-knives**
**2** a knife with a blade that can be folded into the handle
**3** a dive in which the diver bends at the waist in midair
