**grief** *n*
**1** deep or intense [[sorrow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sadness]], [[suffering]], [[regret]], [[distress]], [[misery]], [[sorrow]], [[woe]], [[anguish]]
**2** (*informal*) trouble or annoyance: people were giving me grief for leaving ten minutes early
**3** **come to grief** to have an unfortunate or unsuccessful end or outcome
