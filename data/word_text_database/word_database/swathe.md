**swathe** *vb* **swathing**, **swathed**
**1** to [[wrap]] a bandage, garment, or piece of cloth around (a person or part of the body)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrap]], [[drape]], [[envelop]], [[cloak]], [[shroud]], bundle up
 *n*
**2** a long strip of cloth wrapped around something
**3** the width of one sweep of a scythe or of the blade of a mowing machine
**4** the strip cut in one sweep
**5** the quantity of cut crops left in one sweep
**6** a long narrow strip of land
