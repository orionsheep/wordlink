**pine**^2 *vb* **pining**, **pined**
**1** (often foll. by *for*) to feel great longing (for)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pine for something or someone]]**: long, ache, crave, yearn, eat your heart out over
**2** (often foll. by *away*) to become ill or thin through grief or longing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waste]], [[decline]], [[sicken]], [[fade]], [[languish]]
