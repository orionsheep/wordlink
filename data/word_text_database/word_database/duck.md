**duck**^2 *vb*
**1** to move (the head or body) quickly downwards, to [[escape]] being seen or [[avoid]] a blow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bob]], [[drop]], [[lower]], [[bend]], [[bow]], [[dodge]], [[crouch]], [[stoop]]
**2** to [[plunge]] suddenly under water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dunk]], [[wet]], [[plunge]], [[dip]], [[submerge]], [[immerse]], [[douse]], [[souse]]
**3** (*informal*) to [[dodge]] (a duty or responsibility)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dodge]], [[avoid]], [[escape]], [[evade]], [[elude]], [[sidestep]], [[shirk]]
