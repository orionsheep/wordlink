**welfare** *n*
**1** [[health]], happiness, prosperity, and general [[wellbeing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wellbeing]], [[good]], [[interest]], [[health]], [[security]], [[benefit]], [[safety]], [[protection]]
**2** financial and other assistance given, usually by the government, to people in need
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state benefit]], [[support]], [[benefits]], [[pensions]], [[dole]] (*slang*), [[social security]], [[unemployment benefit]], [[state benefits]], [[pogey]] (*Canad*)
