**chatter** *vb*
**1** to speak quickly and continuously about unimportant things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prattle]], [[chat]], [[rabbit on]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[babble]], [[gab]] (*informal*), [[natter]], [[blather]], [[schmooze]] (*slang*)
**2** (of birds or monkeys) to make rapid repetitive high-pitched noises
**3** (of the teeth) to click together rapidly through cold or fear
 *n*
**4** idle talk or [[gossip]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prattle]], [[chat]], [[gossip]], [[babble]], [[gab]] (*informal*), [[natter]], [[blather]], [[blether]] (*Scot*)
**5** the high-pitched repetitive noise made by a bird or monkey
