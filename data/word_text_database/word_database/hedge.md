**hedge** *n*
**1** a row of shrubs or bushes forming a boundary
**2** a barrier or protection against something, esp. against the risk of loss on an investment
 *vb* **hedging**, **hedged**
**3** to avoid making a decision by making noncommittal statements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prevaricate]], [[evade]], [[sidestep]], [[duck]] (*informal*), [[dodge]], [[flannel]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[equivocate]], [[temporize]]
**4** **hedge against** to guard against the risk of loss in (a bet or disagreement), by supporting the opposition as well
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hedge against something]]**: protect, insure, guard, safeguard, shield, cover
