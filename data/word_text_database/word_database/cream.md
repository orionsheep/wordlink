**cream** *n*
**1** the fatty part of milk, which rises to the top
**2** a [[cosmetic]] or medication that resembles cream in consistency
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lotion]], [[ointment]], [[oil]], [[essence]], [[cosmetic]], [[paste]], [[emulsion]], [[salve]]
**3** any of various foods resembling or containing cream
**4** the [[best]] part of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[best]], [[elite]], [[prime]], [[pick]], [[flower]], [[the crème de la crème]]
**5** **cream sherry** a full-bodied sweet sherry
 *adj*
**6** yellowish-white
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[off-white]], [[ivory]], yellowish-white
 *vb*
**7** to beat (foodstuffs) to a light [[creamy]] consistency
**8** to remove the cream from (milk)
**9** to prepare or cook (foodstuffs) with cream or milk
