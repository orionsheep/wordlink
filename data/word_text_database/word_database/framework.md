**framework** *n*
**1** a particular set of beliefs, ideas, or rules referred to in [[order]] to solve a problem: a moral framework
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[system]], [[plan]], [[order]], [[scheme]], [[arrangement]], [[the bare bones]]
**2** a [[structure]] supporting something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[body]], [[frame]], [[foundation]], [[shell]], [[skeleton]]
