**evaporate** *vb* **-rating**, **-rated**
**1** to change from a liquid or solid to a vapour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disappear]], [[vaporize]], [[dematerialize]], [[vanish]], [[dissolve]], [[dry up]], [[fade away]], [[melt away]]
**2** to become less and less and finally [[disappear]]: faith in the government evaporated rapidly after the election
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fade away]], [[disappear]], [[vanish]], [[dissolve]], [[melt away]]
 **[[evaporable]]** *adj* 
 **[[evaporation]]** *n*
