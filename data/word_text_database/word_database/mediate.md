**mediate** (**mee**-dee-ate) *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to [[intervene]] between people or in a dispute in order to bring about agreement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intervene]], [[step in]] (*informal*), [[intercede]], [[referee]], [[umpire]], [[reconcile]], [[arbitrate]], [[conciliate]]
**2** to resolve differences by [[mediation]]
**3** to be changed slightly by (an experience or event): clients' attitudes to social workers have often been mediated by their past experiences
 **[[mediation]]** *n* 
 **[[mediator]]** *n*
