**garland** *n*
**1** a [[wreath]] of flowers and leaves worn round the head or neck or hung up
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wreath]], [[band]], [[bays]], [[crown]], [[honours]], [[laurels]], [[festoon]], chaplet
 *vb*
**2** to decorate with a garland or garlands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adorn]], [[crown]], [[deck]], [[festoon]], [[wreathe]]
