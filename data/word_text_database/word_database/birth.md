**birth** *n*
**1** the process of bearing young; [[childbirth]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[childbirth]], [[delivery]], [[nativity]], [[parturition]]
**2** the act of being born
**3** the beginning of something; origin
**4** [[ancestry]]: of noble birth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ancestry]], [[stock]], [[blood]], [[background]], [[breeding]], [[pedigree]], [[lineage]], [[parentage]]
**5** **give birth to**
**A** to bear (offspring)
 **B** to produce or originate (an idea, plan, etc.)
