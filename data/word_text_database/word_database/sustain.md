**sustain** *vb*
**1** to [[maintain]] or [[continue]] for a period of time: I managed to sustain a conversation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[maintain]], [[continue]], [[keep up]], [[prolong]], [[protract]]
**2** to [[keep up]] the strength or energy of (someone): one mouthful of water to sustain him; the merest drop of comfort to sustain me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[keep alive]], [[nourish]], [[provide for]]
**3** to [[suffer]] (an injury or loss): he [[sustained]] a spinal injury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suffer]], [[experience]], [[undergo]], [[feel]], [[bear]], [[endure]], [[withstand]]
**4** to [[support]] (something) from below
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[bear]], [[uphold]]
**5** to [[support]] or agree with (a decision or statement): objection [[sustained]]
 **[[sustained]]** *adj*
