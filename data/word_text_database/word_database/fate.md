**fate** *n*
**1** the ultimate force that supposedly predetermines the course of events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destiny]], [[chance]], [[fortune]], [[luck]], [[the stars]], [[providence]], [[nemesis]], [[kismet]]
**2** the inevitable [[fortune]] that happens to a person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fortune]], [[destiny]], [[lot]], [[portion]], [[cup]], [[horoscope]]
**3** death or downfall: Custer met his fate at Little Bighorn
