**curve** *n*
**1** a continuously bending line with no straight parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bend]], [[turn]], [[loop]], [[arc]], [[curvature]]
**2** something that curves or is curved
**3** [[curvature]]
**4** (*maths*) a system of points whose coordinates satisfy a given equation
**5** a line representing data on a graph
 *vb* **curving**, **curved**
**6** to form into or move in a curve
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bend]], [[turn]], [[wind]], [[twist]], [[arch]], [[snake]], [[arc]], coil
 **[[curvy]]** *adj*
