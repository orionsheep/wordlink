**purple** *n*
**1** a colour between red and blue
**2** cloth of this colour, often used to symbolize royalty or nobility
**3** the official robe of a cardinal
**4** anything purple, such as purple paint or purple clothing: dressed in purple
 *adj*
**5** of a colour between red and blue
**6** (of writing) excessively elaborate: purple prose
 **[[purplish]]** *adj*
