**lyric** *adj*
**1**
**A** (of poetry) expressing the writer's personal feelings
 **B** (of poetry) having the form and manner of a song
**2** of or relating to such poetry
**3** (of a singing voice) light and melodic
 *n*
**4** a short poem of songlike quality
**5** **lyrics** the words of a popular song: Cole invests all her lyrics with a touch of drama
