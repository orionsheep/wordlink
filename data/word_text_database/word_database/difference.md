**difference** *n*
**1** the state or quality of being unlike
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dissimilarity]], [[contrast]], [[variation]], [[change]], [[variety]], [[diversity]], [[alteration]], [[discrepancy]]
**2** a [[disagreement]] or [[argument]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagreement]], [[conflict]], [[argument]], [[clash]], [[dispute]], [[quarrel]], [[contretemps]]
**3** the result of the subtraction of one number or quantity from another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remainder]], [[rest]], [[balance]], [[remains]], [[excess]]
**4** **make a difference** to have an effect
**5** **split the difference**
**A** to compromise
 **B** to divide a [[remainder]] equally
