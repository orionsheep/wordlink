**glue** *n*
**1** a substance used for sticking things together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adhesive]], [[cement]], [[gum]], [[paste]]
 *vb* **gluing*** or ***glueing**, **[[glued]]**
**2** to join or [[stick]] together with glue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick]], [[fix]], [[seal]], [[cement]], [[gum]], [[paste]], [[affix]]
**3** **[[glued]] to** paying full attention to: she lay on the sofa, [[glued]] to her phone
 **[[gluey]]** *adj*
