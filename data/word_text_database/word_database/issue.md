**issue** *n*
**1** a [[topic]] of interest or discussion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[topic]], [[point]], [[matter]], [[problem]], [[question]], [[subject]], [[theme]]
**2** an important [[subject]] requiring a decision
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[question]], [[bone of contention]]
**3** a particular [[edition]] of a magazine or newspaper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edition]], [[printing]], [[copy]], [[publication]], [[number]], [[version]]
**4** a consequence or result
**5** (*law*) the [[descendants]] of a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[children]], [[offspring]], [[babies]], [[kids]] (*informal*), [[heirs]], [[descendants]], [[progeny]]
**6** the act of sending or giving out something
**7** the act of emerging; outflow
**8** something flowing out, such as a river
**9** **at issue**
**A** under discussion
 **B** in disagreement
**10** **force the issue** to compel decision on some [[matter]]
**11** **join issue** to join in controversy
**12** **take issue (with)** to disagree (with)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[take issue with something or someone]]**: disagree with, [[question]], challenge, oppose, dispute, object to, argue with, take exception to
 *vb* **-suing**, **-sued**
**13** to make (a statement, etc.) publicly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give out]], [[release]], [[publish]], [[announce]], [[deliver]], [[spread]], [[broadcast]], [[distribute]]
**14** to supply officially (with)
**15** to send out or [[distribute]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give out]], [[release]], [[publish]], [[announce]], [[deliver]], [[spread]], [[broadcast]], [[distribute]]
**16** to [[publish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give out]], [[release]], [[publish]], [[announce]], [[deliver]], [[spread]], [[broadcast]], [[distribute]]
**17** to come forth or emerge
 **[[issuable]]** *adj*
