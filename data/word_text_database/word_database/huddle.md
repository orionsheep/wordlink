**huddle** *n*
**1** a small group of people or things standing or lying close together
**2** **go into a huddle** (*informal*) to have a private conference
 *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**3** (of a group of people) to [[crowd]] or nestle closely together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowd]], [[press]], [[gather]], [[collect]], [[squeeze]], [[cluster]], [[flock]], [[herd]]
**4** to [[curl up]] one's arms and legs close to one's body through cold or fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curl up]], [[crouch]], [[hunch up]]
