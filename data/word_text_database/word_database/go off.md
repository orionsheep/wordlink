**[[go off]]** *vb*
**1** to stop functioning: the heating went off
**2** to make a sudden loud noise: a bomb went off
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[go off]]**: explode, fire, blow up, detonate
**3** to occur as specified: the actual launch went off perfectly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[go off]]**: take place, come about
**4** (*informal*) (of food) to become stale or rotten
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[go off]]**: go bad, turn, spoil, rot, go stale
**5** (*Brit* *informal*) to stop liking
