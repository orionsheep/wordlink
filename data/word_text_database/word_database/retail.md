**retail** *n*
**1** the sale of goods individually or in small quantities to the public
 *adj*
**2** of or engaged in such selling: auctioneers have been successful in cornering the retail market
 *adv*
**3** in small amounts or at a retail price
 *vb*
**4** to sell or be sold in small quantities to the public
**5** to relate (gossip or scandal) in detail: he gleefully retailed the story
 **[[retailer]]** *n*
