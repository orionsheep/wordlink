**uplift** *vb*
**1** to [[raise]] or lift up
**2** to [[raise]] morally or spiritually
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[improve]], [[better]], [[raise]], [[advance]], [[inspire]], [[refine]], [[edify]]
**3** (*Scot*) to collect or pick up
 *n*
**4** the act or process of bettering moral, social, or cultural conditions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[improvement]], [[enlightenment]], [[advancement]], [[refinement]], [[enhancement]], [[enrichment]], edification
 *adj*
**5** (of a bra) designed to lift and support the breasts
