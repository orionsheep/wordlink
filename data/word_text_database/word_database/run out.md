**[[run out]]** *vb*
**1** to use up or (of a supply) to be used up: we soon ran out of gas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[run out]]**: be used up, dry up, give out, fail, finish, be exhausted
**2** to become invalid: my passport has [[run out]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[run out]]**: expire, end, terminate
**3** **[[run out]] on** (*informal*) to desert or abandon
**4** (*cricket*) to dismiss (a running batsman) by breaking the wicket with the ball while he or she is running between the wickets
 *n* **run-out**
**5** (*cricket*) dismissal of a batsman by running him or her out
