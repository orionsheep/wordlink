**surround** *vb*
**1** to [[encircle]] or [[enclose]] (something or someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enclose]], [[ring]], [[encircle]], [[encompass]], [[envelop]], [[hem in]]
**2** to exist around (someone or something): the family members who surround him
 *n*
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a border, such as the area of uncovered floor between the walls of a room and the carpet
 **[[surrounding]]** *adj*
