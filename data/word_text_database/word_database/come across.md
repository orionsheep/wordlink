**come across** *vb*
**1** to meet or find by accident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[come across someone]]**: meet, encounter, run into, bump into (*informal*)
**2** to communicate the intended meaning or impression
**3** **come across as** to give a certain impression
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[come across as something or someone]]**: seem, look, seem to be, appear to be, give the impression of being
