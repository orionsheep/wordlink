**2** to [[present]] to view: he produced his passport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[show]], [[present]], [[proffer]]
**3** to [[make]]: this area produces much of Spain's best wine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[create]], [[develop]], [[manufacture]], [[construct]], [[invent]], [[fabricate]]
**4** to [[give birth to]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring forth]], [[bear]], [[deliver]], [[breed]], [[give birth to]], [[beget]] (*old-fashioned*), [[bring into the world]]
**5** to [[present]] on [[stage]], film, or television: the girls and boys write and produce their own plays
