**monumental** *adj*
**1** large, impressive, or likely to last or be remembered for a long time: a monumental three-volume biography
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[important]], [[significant]], [[enormous]], [[historic]], [[memorable]], [[awesome]], [[majestic]], [[unforgettable]]
**2** of or being a monument
**3** (*informal*) extreme: a monumental gamble
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immense]], [[great]], [[massive]], [[staggering]], [[colossal]]
