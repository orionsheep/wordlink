**chemical** *n*
**1** any [[substance]] used in or resulting from a reaction involving changes to atoms or molecules
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compound]], [[drug]], [[substance]], [[synthetic substance]], potion
 *adj*
**2** of or used in chemistry
**3** of, made from, or using chemicals: a chemical additive found in many foods
 **[[chemically]]** *adv*
