**weld** *vb*
**1** to [[join]] (two pieces of metal or plastic) by softening with heat and hammering or by fusion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[join]], [[link]], [[bond]], [[bind]], [[connect]], [[fuse]], [[solder]]
**2** to [[unite]] closely: the diverse ethnic groups had been welded together by the anti-Fascist cause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unite]], [[combine]], [[blend]], [[unify]], [[fuse]]
 *n*
**3** a joint formed by welding
 **[[welder]]** *n*
