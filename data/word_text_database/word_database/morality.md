◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[virtue]], [[justice]], [[morals]], [[honour]], [[integrity]], [[goodness]], [[honesty]], [[decency]]

**3** a system of moral [[principles]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ethics]], [[conduct]], [[principles]], [[morals]], [[manners]], [[philosophy]], [[mores]]
