**gate** *n*
**1** a movable [[barrier]], usually hinged, for closing an [[opening]] in a wall or fence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[barrier]], [[opening]], [[door]], [[entrance]], [[exit]], [[gateway]], [[portal]] (*literary*)
**2**
**A** the number of people admitted to a sporting event or entertainment
 **B** the total [[entrance]] money received from them
**3** an [[exit]] at an airport by which passengers get to an aircraft
**4** (*electronics*) a circuit with one or more input terminals and one output terminal, the output being determined by the combination of input signals
**5** a slotted metal frame that controls the positions of the gear lever in a motor vehicle
