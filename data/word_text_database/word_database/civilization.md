**civilization** * or ***-lisation** *n*
**1** the total [[culture]] and way of life of a particular [[people]], [[nation]], region, or period
**2** a human [[society]] that has a complex cultural, political, and legal organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[society]], [[people]], [[community]], [[nation]], [[polity]]
**3** the races collectively who have achieved such a state
**4** cities or populated areas, as contrasted with sparsely inhabited areas
**5** intellectual, cultural, and moral refinement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[culture]], [[development]], [[education]], [[progress]], [[enlightenment]], [[sophistication]], [[advancement]], [[cultivation]]
