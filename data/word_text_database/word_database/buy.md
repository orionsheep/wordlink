**buy** *vb* **buying**, **bought**
**1** to [[acquire]] (something) by paying a sum of money for it; [[purchase]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purchase]], [[get]], [[pay for]], [[obtain]], [[acquire]], [[invest in]], [[shop for]], [[procure]]
**2** to be capable of purchasing: money can't buy love
**3** to [[acquire]] by any exchange or sacrifice: the rise in interest rates was just to buy time until the weekend
**4** to bribe (someone)
**5** (*slang*) to accept (something) as true
**6** (foll. by *into*) to [[purchase]] shares of (a company)
 *n*
**7** a [[purchase]]: a good buy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purchase]], [[deal]] (*informal*), [[bargain]], [[acquisition]], [[steal]] (*informal*), [[snip]] (*informal*), [[giveaway]]
