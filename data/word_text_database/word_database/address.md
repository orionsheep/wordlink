**address** *n*
**1** the [[place]] at which someone lives
**2** the conventional form by which the [[location]] of a building is described
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[location]], [[home]], [[place]], [[house]], [[point]], [[position]], [[situation]], [[site]]
**3** a formal [[speech]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speech]], [[talk]], [[lecture]], [[discourse]], [[sermon]], [[dissertation]], [[homily]], [[oration]]
**4** (*computers*)
**A** a number giving the [[location]] of a piece of stored information
 **B** a series of characters that represents a [[location]] on the internet
 *vb*
**5** to mark (a letter or parcel) with an address
**6** to [[speak to]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speak to]], [[talk to]], [[greet]], [[hail]], [[approach]], [[converse with]], [[korero]] (*NZ*)
**7** to direct one's attention to (a problem or an issue)
**8** **address oneself to**
**A** to speak or write to
 **B** to apply oneself to: we have got to address ourselves properly to this problem
