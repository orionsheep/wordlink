**wood** *n*
**1** the hard fibrous substance beneath the bark in trees and shrubs, which is used in building and carpentry and as fuel. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**ligneous**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[timber]], [[planks]], [[planking]], [[lumber]] (*US*)
**2** an area of trees growing together that is smaller than a [[forest]]: a track leading into a wood <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**sylvan**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[woodland]], [[forest]], [[grove]], [[thicket]], [[copse]], [[coppice]], [[bushland]]
**3** (*golf*) a long-shafted club with a wooden head
 *adj*
**4** made of, using, or for use with wood: wood fires
