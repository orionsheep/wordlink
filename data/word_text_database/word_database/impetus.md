**impetus** (**imp**-it-uss) *n*, *pl* **-tuses**
**1** an [[incentive]] or [[impulse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incentive]], [[push]], [[spur]], [[motivation]], [[impulse]], [[stimulus]], [[catalyst]], [[goad]]
**2** (*physics*) the [[force]] that starts a body moving or that tends to resist changes in its speed or direction once it is moving
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[power]], [[energy]], [[momentum]]
