**collar** *n*
**1** the part of a garment round the neck
**2** a band of leather, rope, or metal placed around an animal's neck
**3** (*biol*) a ringlike marking around the neck of a bird or animal
**4** a cut of meat, esp. bacon, from the neck of an animal
**5** a ring or band around a pipe, rod, or shaft
 *vb* (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**6** to [[seize]]; [[arrest]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seize]], [[catch]], [[arrest]], [[grab]], [[capture]], [[nail]] (*informal*), [[nab]] (*informal*), [[apprehend]]
**7** to [[catch]] in order to speak to
**8** to take for oneself
