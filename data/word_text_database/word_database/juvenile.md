**juvenile** *adj*
**1** [[young]]; not fully adult: juvenile offenders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[young]], [[junior]], [[adolescent]], [[youthful]], [[immature]]
**2** of or for [[young]] people: juvenile court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merely]], [[only]], [[simply]], [[solely]]
**3** [[immature]] in behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immature]], [[childish]], [[infantile]], [[puerile]], [[young]], [[youthful]], [[inexperienced]], callow
 *n*
**4** a [[young]] person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[child]], [[youth]], [[minor]], [[girl]], [[boy]], [[teenager]], [[infant]], [[adolescent]]
