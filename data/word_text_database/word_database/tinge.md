**tinge** *n*
**1** a slight [[tint]] or colouring: his skin had an unhealthy greyish tinge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tint]], [[colour]], [[shade]]
**2** a very small amount: both goals had a tinge of fortune
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[bit]], [[drop]], [[touch]], [[suggestion]], [[dash]], [[sprinkling]], smattering
 *vb* **tingeing*** or ***tinging**, **[[tinged]]**
**3** to [[colour]] or [[tint]] faintly: the sunset [[tinged]] the lake with pink
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tint]], [[colour]]
**4** **[[tinged]] with** having a small amount of a particular quality: the victory was [[tinged]] with sadness
