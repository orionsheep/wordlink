**advantage** *n*
**1** a more favourable position or state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lead]], [[sway]], [[dominance]], [[precedence]]
**2** [[benefit]] or [[profit]]: they could make this work to their advantage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[benefit]], [[help]], [[profit]], [[favour]]
**3** (*tennis*) the point scored after deuce
**4** **take advantage of**
**A** to use a person unfairly
 **B** to use an opportunity
**5** **to advantage** to good effect: her hair was shaped to display her features to advantage
