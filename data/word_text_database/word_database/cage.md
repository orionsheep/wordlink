**cage** *n*
**1** an [[enclosure]] made of bars or wires, for keeping birds or animals in
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enclosure]], [[pen]], [[coop]], [[hutch]], [[pound]]
**2** the enclosed platform of a lift in a mine
 *vb* **caging**, **[[caged]]**
**3** to confine in a cage
 **[[caged]]** *adj*
