**wish** *vb*
**1** to [[want]] or [[desire]] (something impossible or improbable): he wished he'd kept quiet
**2** to [[desire]] or prefer to be or do something: the next person who wished to speak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[want]], [[feel]], [[choose]], [[please]], [[desire]], [[think fit]]
**3** to [[feel]] or express a [[hope]] concerning the welfare, health, or success of: we wished him well
**4** to greet as specified: I wished her a Merry Christmas
 *n*
**5** a [[desire]], often for something impossible or improbable: a desperate wish to succeed as a professional artist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desire]], [[want]], [[hope]], [[urge]], [[intention]], [[fancy]] (*informal*), [[ambition]], [[yen]] (*informal*)
**6** something desired or wished for: your wishes will come true
**7** the expression of a [[hope]] for someone's welfare, health, or success: give him our best wishes
