**comment** *n*
**1** a [[remark]], criticism, or [[observation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remark]], [[statement]], [[observation]]
**2** a situation or event that expresses some feeling: a sad comment on the nature of many relationships
**3** talk or gossip
**4** a [[note]] explaining or criticizing a passage in a text
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[note]], [[explanation]], [[illustration]], [[commentary]], [[exposition]], [[annotation]], [[elucidation]]
**5** **no comment** I decline to [[say]] anything about the matter
 *vb*
**6** to [[remark]] or express an opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remark]], [[say]], [[note]], [[mention]], [[point out]], [[observe]], [[utter]]
