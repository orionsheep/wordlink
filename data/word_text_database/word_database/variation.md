**variation** *n*
**1** something presented in a slightly different form: his books are all variations on a basic theme
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alternative]], [[variety]], [[modification]], [[departure]], [[innovation]], [[variant]]
**2** a [[change]] in level, amount, or quantity: there was a variation in the figures
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[variety]], [[change]], [[deviation]], [[difference]], [[diversity]], [[diversion]] (*Brit*), [[novelty]]
**3** (*music*) the repetition of a simple tune with the addition of new harmonies or a [[change]] in rhythm: Variations on a Hussar's Song
