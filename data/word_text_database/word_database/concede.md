**concede** *vb* **-ceding**, **-ceded**
**1** to [[admit]] (something) as true or correct
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[admit]], [[allow]], [[accept]], [[acknowledge]], [[own]], [[grant]], [[confess]]
**2** to [[give up]] or [[grant]] (something, such as a right)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give up]], [[yield]], [[hand over]], [[surrender]], [[relinquish]], [[cede]]
**3** to [[acknowledge]] defeat in (a contest or argument)
