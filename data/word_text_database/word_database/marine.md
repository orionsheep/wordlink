**marine** *adj*
**1** of, found in, or relating to the sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nautical]], [[maritime]], [[naval]], [[seafaring]], [[seagoing]]
**2** of shipping or navigation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nautical]], [[maritime]], [[naval]], [[seafaring]], [[seagoing]]
**3** used or adapted for use at sea
 *n*
**4** (esp. in Britain and the US) a soldier trained for land and sea combat
**5** a country's shipping or navy collectively: the merchant marine
