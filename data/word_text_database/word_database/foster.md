**foster** *adj*
**1** of or involved in the bringing up of a child not one's own: foster care
 *vb*
**2** to [[bring up]] (a child not one's own)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring up]], [[raise]], [[parent]], [[nurse]], [[look after]], [[rear]], [[care for]], [[take care of]]
**3** to [[promote]] the growth or development of: Catherine fostered knowledge and patronized the arts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[develop]], [[support]], [[further]], [[encourage]], [[feed]], [[promote]], [[stimulate]], [[uphold]]
 **[[fostering]]** *n*
