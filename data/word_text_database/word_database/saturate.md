**saturate** *vb* **-rating**, **-rated**
**1** to [[soak]] completely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soak]], [[steep]], [[drench]], [[imbue]], [[suffuse]], [[wet through]], [[waterlog]], [[souse]]
**2** to fill so completely that no more can be added: saturating the area with their men
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flood]], [[overwhelm]], [[swamp]], [[overrun]]
**3** (*chem*) to combine (a substance) or (of a substance) to be combined with the greatest possible amount of another substance
