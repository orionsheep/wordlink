◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[over]], [[upon]], [[beyond]], [[on top of]], [[exceeding]], [[higher than]]
**2** greater than in quantity or degree: above average

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[senior to]], [[over]], [[ahead of]], [[in charge of]], [[higher than]], [[superior to]], [[more powerful than]]
**4** too high-minded for: she considered herself above the task of working
**5** too respected for; [[beyond]]: his fleet was above suspicion
**6** too difficult to be understood by: a discussion that was way above my head
**7** louder or [[higher than]] (other noise)
**8** in preference to
**9** **above all** most of all; especially
 *adv*
**10** in or to a higher place: the hills above
**11** in a previous place (in something written or printed)
**12** higher in rank or position
