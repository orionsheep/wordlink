**emotional** *adj*
**1** of or relating to the emotions: emotional abuse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[psychological]], [[private]], [[personal]], [[hidden]], [[spiritual]], [[inner]]
**2** influenced by feelings rather than rational thinking: he was too emotional to be a good doctor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passionate]], [[sentimental]], [[temperamental]], [[excitable]], [[demonstrative]], [[hot-blooded]]
**3** appealing to the emotions: emotional appeals for public support
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moving]], [[touching]], [[affecting]], [[stirring]], [[sentimental]], [[poignant]], [[emotive]], [[heart-rending]]
**4** showing one's feelings openly, esp. when upset: he became very emotional and burst into tears
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passionate]], [[sentimental]], [[temperamental]], [[excitable]], [[demonstrative]], [[hot-blooded]]
 **[[emotionalism]]** *n* 
 **[[emotionally]]** *adv*
