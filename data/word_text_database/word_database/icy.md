**1** [[freezing]] or very [[cold]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cold]], [[freezing]], [[bitter]], [[biting]], [[raw]], [[chill]], [[chilly]], [[frosty]]
**2** covered with ice: an icy runway
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slippery]], [[glassy]], slippy (*informal*, *dialect*), [[like a sheet of glass]]
**3** [[cold]] or reserved in manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unfriendly]], [[cold]], [[distant]], [[aloof]], [[frosty]], [[frigid]], [[unwelcoming]]
 **[[icily]]** *adv*
