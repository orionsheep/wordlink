**get** *vb* **getting**, **got**
**1** to come into possession of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obtain]], [[receive]], [[gain]], [[acquire]], [[win]], [[land]] (*informal*), [[net]], [[pick up]]
**2** to [[bring]] or [[fetch]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fetch]], [[bring]], [[collect]]
**3** to [[catch]] (an illness)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[catch]], [[develop]], [[contract]], [[succumb to]], [[fall victim to]], [[go down with]], [[come down with]]
**4** to [[become]]: they get frustrated and angry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[become]], [[grow]], [[turn]], [[come to be]]
**5** to cause to be done or to happen: he got a wart removed; to get steamed up
**6** to hear or [[understand]]: did you get that joke?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[understand]], [[follow]], [[catch]], [[see]], [[realize]], [[take in]], [[perceive]], [[grasp]]
**7** to reach (a place or point): we could not get to the airport in time
**8** to [[catch]] (a bus or train)
**9** to [[persuade]]: she was trying to get him to give secrets away
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persuade]], [[convince]], [[induce]], [[influence]], [[entice]], [[incite]], [[impel]], [[prevail upon]]
**10** (*informal*) to [[annoy]]: you know what really gets me?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annoy]], [[upset]], [[anger]], [[disturb]], [[trouble]], [[bug]] (*informal*), [[irritate]], [[gall]]
**11** (*informal*) to baffle: now you've got me
**12** (*informal*) to hit: a bit of grenade got me on the left hip
**13** (*informal*) to be revenged on
**14** (*informal*) to start: we got talking about it; it got me thinking
