**vary** *vb* **varies**, **[[varying]]**, **varied**
**1** to [[change]] in appearance, character, or form
**2** to [[be different]] or cause to [[be different]]: the age of appearance of underarm and body hair varies greatly from person to person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[differ]], [[be different]], [[be dissimilar]], [[disagree]], [[diverge]]
**3** to give variety to: you can vary the type of exercise you do
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alternate]]
**4** to [[change]] in accordance with another variable: an individual's calorie requirement varies with age, sex, and physical activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[shift]], [[swing]], [[alter]], [[fluctuate]], [[oscillate]], [[seesaw]]
 **[[varying]]** *adj*
