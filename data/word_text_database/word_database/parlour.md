**parlour** * or * (*US*) **parlor** *n*
**1** (*old-fashioned*) a [[living room]] for receiving visitors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sitting room]] (*Brit*), [[lounge]], [[living room]], [[drawing room]], [[front room]]
**2** a room or [[shop]] equipped as a place of business: an ice-cream parlour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[establishment]], [[shop]], [[store]], [[salon]]
