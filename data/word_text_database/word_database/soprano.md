**soprano** *n*, *pl* **-pranos**
**1** the highest adult female voice
**2** the voice of a young boy before puberty
**3** a singer with such a voice
**4** the highest or second highest instrument in a family of instruments
 *adj*
**5** denoting a musical instrument that is the highest or second highest pitched in its family: the soprano saxophone
**6** of or relating to the highest female voice, or the voice of a young boy: the part is quite possibly the most demanding soprano role Wagner ever wrote
