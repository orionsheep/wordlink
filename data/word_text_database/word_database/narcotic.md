**narcotic** *n*
**1** a [[drug]], such as opium or morphine, that produces numbness and drowsiness, used medicinally but addictive
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drug]], [[anaesthetic]], [[painkiller]], [[sedative]], [[opiate]], [[tranquillizer]], [[anodyne]], [[analgesic]]
 *adj*
**2** of narcotics or narcosis
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sedative]], [[calming]], [[hypnotic]], [[analgesic]], [[soporific]], [[painkilling]]
