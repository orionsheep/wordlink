**suspicion** *n*
**1** the act or an instance of suspecting; belief without sure proof that something is wrong
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idea]], [[notion]], [[hunch]], [[guess]], [[impression]]
**2** a feeling of [[mistrust]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distrust]], [[scepticism]], [[mistrust]], [[doubt]], [[misgiving]], [[qualm]], [[wariness]], [[dubiety]]
**3** a slight [[trace]]: the merest suspicion of a threat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[touch]], [[hint]], [[suggestion]], [[shade]], [[streak]], [[tinge]], [[soupçon]] (*French*)
**4** **above suspicion** not possibly guilty of anything, through having a good reputation
**5** **under suspicion** suspected of doing something wrong
