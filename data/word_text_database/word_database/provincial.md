**provincial** *adj*
**1** of a province
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regional]], [[state]], [[local]], [[county]], [[district]], [[territorial]], [[parochial]]
**2** [[unsophisticated]] or [[narrow-minded]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[parochial]], [[insular]], [[narrow-minded]], [[unsophisticated]], [[limited]], [[narrow]], [[small-town]] (*chiefly* *US*), [[inward-looking]]
**3** (*NZ*) denoting a football team representing a province
 *n*
**4** an [[unsophisticated]] person
**5** a person from a province or the provinces
 **[[provincialism]]** *n*
