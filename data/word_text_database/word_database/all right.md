**all right** *adj*
**1** [[acceptable]] or [[satisfactory]]: is everything all right?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satisfactory]], O.K. *or* okay (*informal*), [[average]], [[fair]], [[sufficient]], [[standard]], [[acceptable]], [[good enough]]
**2** unharmed; [[safe]]: I'm going to check if he's all right
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well]], O.K. *or* okay (*informal*), [[whole]] (*archaic*), [[sound]], [[fit]], [[safe]], [[healthy]], unharmed
 *interj*
**3** an expression of approval or agreement
 *adv*
**4** satisfactorily
**5** safely
**6** without doubt: it was her all right
