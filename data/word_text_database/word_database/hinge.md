**hinge** *n*
**1** a device for holding together two parts, such as a door and its frame, so that one can swing freely
 *vb* **hinging**, **[[hinged]]**
**2** to join or open (something) by means of a hinge
**3** **hinge on** to depend on: billions of dollars of western aid [[hinged]] on the outcome of the talks
 **[[hinged]]** *adj*
