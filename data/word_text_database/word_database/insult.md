**insult** *vb*
**1** to treat or speak to rudely: they insulted us and even threatened to kill us
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offend]], [[abuse]], [[wound]], [[slight]], [[put down]], [[snub]], [[malign]], [[affront]]
 *n*
**2** an offensive remark or action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jibe]], [[slight]], [[put-down]], [[abuse]], [[snub]], [[barb]], [[affront]], [[abusive remark]]
**3** a person or thing producing the effect of an insult: their explanation is an insult to our intelligence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offence]], [[slight]], [[snub]], [[slur]], [[affront]], [[slap in the face]] (*informal*), [[kick in the teeth]] (*informal*), [[insolence]]
