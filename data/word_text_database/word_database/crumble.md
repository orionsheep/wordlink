**crumble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to break into crumbs or fragments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crush]], [[fragment]], [[pulverize]], [[pound]], [[grind]], [[powder]], [[granulate]]
**2** to [[fall apart]] or [[decay]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disintegrate]], [[collapse]], [[deteriorate]], [[decay]], [[fall apart]], [[degenerate]], [[tumble down]], go to pieces
 *n*
**3** a baked pudding consisting of stewed fruit with a [[crumbly]] topping: rhubarb crumble
 **[[crumbly]]** *adj* 
 **[[crumbliness]]** *n*
