**confess** *vb*
**1** to [[admit]] (a fault or crime)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[admit]], [[acknowledge]], [[disclose]], [[confide]], [[own up]], [[come clean]] (*informal*), [[divulge]]
**2** to [[admit]] to be true, esp. reluctantly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[declare]], [[allow]], [[reveal]], [[confirm]], [[concede]], [[assert]], [[affirm]], [[profess]]
**3** (*Christianity*) to [[declare]] (one's sins) to God or to a priest, so as to obtain forgiveness
