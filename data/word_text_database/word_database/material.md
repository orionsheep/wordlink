**material** *n*
**1** the [[substance]] of which a thing is made
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substance]], [[matter]], [[stuff]]
**2** [[cloth]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cloth]], [[fabric]], [[textile]]
**3** ideas or [[notes]] that a finished work may be based on: the material of the story resembles an incident in his own life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[information]], [[details]], [[facts]], [[notes]], [[evidence]], [[particulars]], [[data]], info (*informal*)
 *adj*
**4** concerned with or composed of [[physical]] [[matter]] or [[substance]]; not relating to spiritual or abstract things: the material universe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physical]], [[solid]], [[substantial]] (*formal*), [[concrete]], [[bodily]], [[tangible]], [[palpable]], [[corporeal]]
**5** of or affecting economic or [[physical]] wellbeing: material prosperity
**6** [[relevant]] or pertinent: material [[evidence]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relevant]], [[important]], [[significant]], [[essential]], [[vital]], [[serious]], [[meaningful]], [[applicable]]
