**obscene** *adj*
**1** [[offensive]] to accepted standards of decency or modesty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indecent]], [[dirty]], [[offensive]], [[filthy]], [[improper]], [[immoral]], [[pornographic]], [[lewd]]
**2** (*law*) tending to deprave or corrupt: an obscene publication
**3** [[disgusting]]: a great dark obscene pool of blood
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offensive]], [[shocking]], [[evil]], [[disgusting]], [[outrageous]], [[revolting]], [[sickening]], [[vile]]
 **[[obscenity]]** *n*
