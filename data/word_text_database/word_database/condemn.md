**condemn** *vb*
**1** to express strong disapproval of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[denounce]], [[damn]], [[criticize]], [[disapprove]], [[censure]], [[reprove]], [[upbraid]], [[blame]]
**2** to pronounce [[sentence]] on in a court of law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sentence]], [[convict]], [[damn]], [[doom]], [[pass sentence on]]
**3** to force into a particular state: a system that condemns most of our youngsters to failure
**4** to judge or declare (something) unfit for use
**5** to indicate the guilt of: everything the man had said condemned him, morally if not technically
 **[[condemnation]]** *n* 
 **[[condemnatory]]** *adj*
