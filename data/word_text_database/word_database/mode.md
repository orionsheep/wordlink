**mode** *n*
**1** a [[manner]] or [[way]] of doing, acting, or existing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[method]], [[way]], [[system]], [[form]], [[process]], [[style]], [[technique]], [[manner]]
**2** a particular [[fashion]] or [[style]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fashion]], [[style]], [[trend]], [[rage]], [[vogue]], [[look]], [[craze]]
**3** (*music*) any of the various scales of notes within one octave
**4** (*maths*) the most frequently occurring of a range of values
