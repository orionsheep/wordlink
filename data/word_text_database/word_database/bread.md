**bread** *n*
**1** a [[food]] made from a dough of flour or meal mixed with water or milk, usually raised with yeast and then baked
**2** necessary [[food]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[food]], [[fare]], [[kai]] (*NZ* *informal*), [[nourishment]], [[sustenance]]
**3** (*slang*) [[money]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[money]], [[cash]], dough (*slang*)
 *vb*
**4** to cover ([[food]]) with breadcrumbs before cooking
