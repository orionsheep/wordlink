**drop** *vb* **dropping**, **dropped**
**1** to [[fall]] or allow (something) to [[fall]] vertically
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plunge]], [[fall]], [[tumble]], [[descend]], [[plummet]]
**2** to [[decrease]] in amount, strength, or value
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fall]], [[decline]], [[diminish]]
**3** to [[fall]] to the ground, as from exhaustion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sink]], [[fall]], [[descend]]
**4** to [[sink]] to a lower position, as on a scale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decline]], [[fall]], [[sink]]
**5** to mention casually: she dropped a hint
**6** to set down (passengers or goods): can you drop me at the hotel?
**7** (*informal*) to send: drop me a letter
**8** to [[discontinue]]: can we drop the subject?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quit]], [[give up]], [[axe]] (*informal*), [[kick]] (*informal*), [[relinquish]], [[discontinue]]
**9** (*informal*) to be no longer friendly with: I dropped him when I discovered his political views
**10** to leave out in speaking: she has a tendency to drop her h's
**11** (of animals) to give birth to (offspring)
**12** (*sport*) to omit (a player) from a team
**13** to lose (a game or point)
**14** **drop back** to progress more slowly than other people going in the same direction
**15** **drop in *or* by** (*informal*) to pay someone a casual visit
 *n*
**16** a small quantity of liquid forming a round shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[droplet]], [[bead]], [[globule]], [[bubble]], [[pearl]], [[drip]]
**17** a small quantity of liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dash]], [[shot]] (*informal*), [[spot]], [[trace]], [[sip]], [[tot]], [[trickle]], [[mouthful]]
**18** a small round sweet: a lemon drop
**19** a [[decrease]] in amount, strength, or value
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decrease]], [[fall]], [[cut]], [[lowering]], [[decline]], [[reduction]], [[slump]], [[fall-off]]
**20** the vertical distance that anything may [[fall]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fall]], [[plunge]], [[descent]]
**21** the act of unloading troops or supplies by parachute
 See also **drop off**, **dropout**
