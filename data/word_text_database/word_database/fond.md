**fond** *adj*
**1** **[[fond of]]** having a liking for
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[fond of]]**: attached to, in love with, keen on, attracted to, having a soft spot for, enamoured of
**2** [[loving]] and [[affectionate]]: his fond parents
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loving]], [[caring]], [[warm]], [[devoted]], [[tender]], [[adoring]], [[affectionate]], [[indulgent]]
**3** (of hopes or wishes) cherished but unlikely to be realized
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unrealistic]], [[empty]], [[naive]], [[vain]], [[foolish]], [[deluded]], [[overoptimistic]], [[delusive]]
 **[[fondly]]** *adv* 
 **[[fondness]]** *n*
