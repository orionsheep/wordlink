**taboo** * or ***tabu** *n*, *pl* **-boos*** or ***-bus**
**1** a [[restriction]] or [[prohibition]] resulting from social or other conventions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prohibition]], [[ban]], [[restriction]], [[anathema]], [[interdict]], [[proscription]], [[tapu]] (*NZ*)
**2** a ritual [[prohibition]], esp. of something that is considered holy or unclean
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prohibition]], [[ban]], [[restriction]], [[anathema]], [[interdict]], [[proscription]], [[tapu]] (*NZ*)
 *adj*
**3** [[forbidden]] or disapproved of: a taboo subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forbidden]], [[banned]], [[prohibited]], [[unacceptable]], [[outlawed]], [[anathema]], [[proscribed]], [[unmentionable]]
