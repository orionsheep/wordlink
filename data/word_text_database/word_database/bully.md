**bully** *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**1** a person who repeatedly hurts, persecutes, or intimidates other people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persecutor]], [[tough]], [[oppressor]], [[tormentor]], [[bully boy]], [[browbeater]], [[coercer]], ruffian
 *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
**2** to hurt, [[intimidate]], or [[persecute]] (another person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persecute]], [[intimidate]], [[torment]], [[oppress]], [[pick on]], [[victimize]], [[terrorize]], push around (*slang*)
 *interj*
**3** **bully for you** (*informal*) well done! bravo!: now usually used sarcastically
