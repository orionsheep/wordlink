**anthem** *n*
**1** a song of loyalty or devotion: a national anthem
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[song of praise]], [[carol]], [[chant]], [[hymn]], [[psalm]], [[paean]], [[chorale]], [[canticle]]
**2** a piece of music for a choir, usually set to words from the Bible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[song of praise]], [[carol]], [[chant]], [[hymn]], [[psalm]], [[paean]], [[chorale]], [[canticle]]
