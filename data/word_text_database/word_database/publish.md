**publish** *vb*
**1** to [[produce]] and [[issue]] (printed matter) for sale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put out]], [[issue]], [[produce]], [[print]]
**2** to have one's written work issued for publication
**3** to [[announce]] formally or in public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[announce]], [[reveal]], [[spread]], [[advertise]], [[broadcast]], [[disclose]], [[proclaim]], [[circulate]]
 **[[publishing]]** *n*
