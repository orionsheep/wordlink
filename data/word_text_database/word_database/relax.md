**relax** *vb*
**1** to make or become less tense, looser, or less rigid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make less tense]], [[rest]]
**2** to [[ease]] up from effort or attention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calm down]], [[calm]], [[unwind]]
**3** to make (rules or discipline) less strict
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moderate]], [[ease]], [[relieve]], [[weaken]], [[slacken]]
**4** to become more friendly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: be *or* feel at [[ease]], [[chill out]] (*slang*), [[take it easy]], [[lighten up]] (*slang*), [[outspan]] (*S African*)
**5** to [[lessen]] the intensity of: he [[relaxed]] his vigilance in the lulls between attacks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lessen]], [[reduce]], [[ease]], [[relieve]], [[weaken]], [[loosen]], [[let up]], [[slacken]]
 **[[relaxed]]** *adj* 
 **[[relaxing]]** *adj*
