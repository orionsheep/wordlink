**wander** *vb*
**1** to [[walk]] about in a place without any definite purpose or destination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[roam]], [[walk]], [[drift]], [[stroll]], [[range]], [[stray]], [[ramble]], [[prowl]]
**2** (often foll. by *off*) to leave a place where one is supposed to stay: kids wander off
**3** (of the mind) to lose concentration
 *n*
**4** the act or an instance of [[wandering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excursion]], [[walk]], [[stroll]], [[cruise]], [[ramble]], [[meander]], [[promenade]], [[mosey]] (*informal*) 
 **[[wanderer]]** *n* 
 **[[wandering]]** *adj*, *n*
