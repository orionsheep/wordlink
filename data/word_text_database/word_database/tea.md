**tea** *n*
**1**
**A** a drink made by infusing the dried chopped leaves of an Asian shrub in boiling water: would you like a cup of tea?
 **B** the dried chopped leaves of an Asian shrub used to make this drink: could you get some tea at the grocer's?
 **C** the Asian shrub on which these leaves grow
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*) the main evening meal
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a light meal eaten in mid-afternoon, usually consisting of tea and cakes, sometimes with sandwiches
**4** a drink like tea made from other plants: mint tea
