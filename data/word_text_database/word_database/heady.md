**heady** *adj* **headier**, **headiest**
**1** (of an experience or period of time) extremely [[exciting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exciting]], [[thrilling]], [[stimulating]], [[exhilarating]], [[intoxicating]]
**2** (of alcoholic drink, atmosphere, etc.) strongly affecting the physical senses: a powerful, heady scent of cologne
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intoxicating]], [[strong]], [[potent]], [[inebriating]]
**3** rash and impetuous
