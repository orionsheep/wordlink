◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believable]], [[possible]], [[likely]], [[reasonable]], [[credible]], [[probable]], [[persuasive]], [[conceivable]]
**2** apparently trustworthy or [[believable]]: he is an extraordinarily plausible liar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glib]], [[smooth]], [[specious]], [[smooth-talking]], [[smooth-tongued]]
 **[[plausibility]]** *n* 
 **[[plausibly]]** *adv*
