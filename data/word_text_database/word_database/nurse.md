**nurse** *n*
**1** a person trained to [[look after]] sick people, usually in a hospital
**2** short for **nursemaid**
 *vb* **[[nursing]]**, **nursed**
**3** to [[look after]] (a sick person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look after]], [[treat]], [[tend]], [[care for]], [[take care of]], [[minister to]]
**4** to [[breast-feed]] (a baby)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breast-feed]], [[feed]], [[nurture]], [[nourish]], [[suckle]], [[wet-nurse]]
**5** (of a baby) to [[feed]] at its mother's breast
**6** to try to cure (an ailment)
**7** to [[harbour]] or foster (a feeling)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harbour]], [[have]], [[maintain]], [[preserve]], [[entertain]], [[cherish]]
**8** to clasp fondly: she nursed her drink
 **[[nursing]]** *n*, *adj*
