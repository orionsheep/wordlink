◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bright]], [[shining]], [[intense]], [[sparkling]], [[glittering]], [[dazzling]], [[vivid]], [[radiant]]
**2** (of a colour) [[vivid]]
**3** [[splendid]]; [[magnificent]]: a brilliant show
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[splendid]], [[famous]], [[celebrated]], [[outstanding]], [[superb]], [[magnificent]], [[glorious]], [[notable]]
**4** of [[outstanding]] intelligence or intellect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intelligent]], [[sharp]], [[intellectual]], [[clever]], [[profound]], [[penetrating]], [[inventive]], perspicacious (*formal*)
 *n*
**5** a diamond cut with many facets to increase its sparkle
