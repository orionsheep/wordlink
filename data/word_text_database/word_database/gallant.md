**gallant** *adj*
**1** persistent and courageous in the face of overwhelming odds: a gallant fight
**2** (of a man) making a show of polite attentiveness to women
**3** having a reputation for bravery: Police Medal for gallant and meritorious services
 *n*
**4** (*history*) a young man who tried to impress women with his fashionable clothes or daring acts
 **[[gallantly]]** *adv*
