**technique** *n*
**1** a [[method]] or [[skill]] used for a particular task: modern management techniques
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[method]], [[way]], [[system]], [[approach]], [[means]], [[style]], [[manner]], [[procedure]]
**2** [[proficiency]] in a practical or mechanical [[skill]]: he lacks the technique to be a good player
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skill]], [[performance]], [[craft]], [[touch]], [[execution]], [[artistry]], [[craftsmanship]], [[proficiency]]
