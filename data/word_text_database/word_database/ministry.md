**ministry** *n*, *pl* **-tries**
**1** the profession or duties of a minister of religion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the priesthood]], [[the church]], [[the cloth]], [[holy orders]]
**2** ministers considered as a group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administration]], [[council]]
**3**
**A** a government [[department]] headed by a minister
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[department]], [[office]], [[bureau]], government [[department]]
 **B** the buildings of such a [[department]]
