**dawn** *n*
**1** [[daybreak]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[daybreak]], [[morning]], [[sunrise]], [[daylight]], [[aurora]] (*poetic*), [[crack of dawn]], [[sunup]], [[cockcrow]]
**2** the [[beginning]] of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beginning]], [[start]], [[birth]], [[rise]], [[origin]], [[emergence]], [[advent]], genesis
 *vb*
**3** to [[begin]] to [[grow light]] after the night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grow light]], [[break]], [[brighten]], [[lighten]]
**4** to [[begin]] to [[develop]] or appear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[begin]], [[start]], [[rise]], [[develop]], [[emerge]], [[unfold]], [[originate]]
**5** **dawn on *or* upon** to become apparent (to someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dawn on someone]]**: hit, strike, occur to, register (*informal*), become apparent, come to mind, come into your head
