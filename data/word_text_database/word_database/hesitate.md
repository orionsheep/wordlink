**hesitate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to be slow and uncertain in acting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waver]], [[delay]], [[pause]], [[wait]], [[doubt]], [[falter]], [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[vacillate]]
**2** to [[be reluctant]] (to do something): I hesitate to use the word 'squandered'
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be reluctant]], [[be unwilling]], [[shrink from]], [[think twice]], [[scruple]], [[demur]], [[hang back]], [[be disinclined]]
**3** to [[pause]] during speech because of uncertainty
 **[[hesitation]]** *n*
