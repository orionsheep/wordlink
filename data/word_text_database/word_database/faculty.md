**faculty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** one of the powers of the mind or body, such as memory, sight, or hearing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[power]], [[reason]], [[sense]], [[intelligence]], [[mental ability]], [[physical ability]]
**2** any [[ability]] or [[power]], either inborn or acquired: his faculties of reasoning were considerable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ability]], [[power]], [[skill]], [[facility]], [[capacity]], [[propensity]], [[aptitude]]
**3**
**A** a [[department]] within a university or college
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[department]], [[school]]
 **B** its [[staff]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: teaching [[staff]], [[staff]], [[teachers]], [[professors]], [[lecturers]]
 **C** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) all the teaching [[staff]] of a university, [[school]], or college
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: teaching [[staff]], [[staff]], [[teachers]], [[professors]], [[lecturers]]
