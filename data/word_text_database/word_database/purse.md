**purse** *n*
**1** a small [[pouch]] for carrying [[money]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pouch]], [[wallet]], [[money-bag]], e-[[wallet]] *or* eWallet
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*) a woman's [[handbag]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[handbag]], [[bag]], [[shoulder bag]], [[pocket book]], [[clutch bag]]
**3** [[wealth]] or [[resources]]: the public purse appeared bottomless
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[funds]], [[means]], [[money]], [[resources]], [[treasury]], [[wealth]], [[exchequer]]
**4** a sum of [[money]] that is offered as a prize
 *vb* **pursing**, **pursed**
**5** to pull (the lips) into a small rounded shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pucker]], [[contract]], [[tighten]], [[pout]], [[press together]]
