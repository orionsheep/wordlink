**fraud** *n*
**1** deliberate [[deception]] or cheating intended to gain an advantage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deception]], [[deceit]], [[treachery]], [[swindling]], [[trickery]], [[duplicity]], [[double-dealing]], [[chicanery]]
**2** an act of such [[deception]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scam]], [[deception]] (*slang*)
**3** (*informal*) a person who acts in a false or deceitful way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impostor]], [[fake]], [[hoaxer]], [[pretender]], [[charlatan]], [[fraudster]], [[swindler]], phoney *or* phony (*informal*)
