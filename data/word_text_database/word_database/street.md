**street** *n*
**1** a public [[road]] that is usually lined with buildings, esp. in a town: Sauchiehall Street
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[road]], [[lane]], [[avenue]], [[terrace]], [[row]], [[roadway]]
**2** the part of the [[road]] between the pavements, used by vehicles
**3** the people living in a particular street
**4** **on the streets** homeless
**5** **right up one's street** (*informal*) just what one knows or likes best
**6** **streets ahead of** (*informal*) superior to or more advanced than
