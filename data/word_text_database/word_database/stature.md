**stature** *n*
**1** the [[height]] and [[size]] of a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[height]], [[build]], [[size]]
**2** the reputation of a person or their achievements: a batsman of international stature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[importance]], [[standing]], [[prestige]], [[rank]], [[prominence]], [[eminence]]
**3** moral or intellectual distinction
