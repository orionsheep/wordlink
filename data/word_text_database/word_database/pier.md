**pier** *n*
**1** a structure with a deck that is built out over water and used as a [[landing place]] or [[promenade]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jetty]], [[wharf]], [[quay]], [[promenade]], [[landing place]]
**2** a [[pillar]] or [[support]] that bears heavy loads
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pillar]], [[support]], [[post]], [[column]], [[pile]], [[upright]], [[buttress]]
**3** the part of a wall between two adjacent openings
