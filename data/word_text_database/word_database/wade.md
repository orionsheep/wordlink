**wade** *vb* **wading**, **waded**
**1** to walk slowly and with difficulty through water or mud
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paddle]], [[splash]], [[splash about]], [[slop]]
**2** **wade in *or* into** to begin doing (something) in an energetic way: wading into the fray
**3** **wade through** to proceed with difficulty through: a stack of literature to wade through
