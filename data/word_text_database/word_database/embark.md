**embark** *vb*
**1** to [[go on board]] a ship or aircraft
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go aboard]], [[climb aboard]], [[board ship]], [[step aboard]], [[go on board]], [[take ship]]
**2** **embark on** to begin (a new project or venture)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[embark on something]]**: begin, start, launch, enter, take up, set out, set about, plunge into
 **[[embarkation]]** *n*
