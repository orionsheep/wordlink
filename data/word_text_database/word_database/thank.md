**thank** *vb*
**1** to convey feelings of gratitude to: he thanked the nursing staff for saving his life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[say thank you to]], [[show your appreciation to]]
**2** to hold responsible: he has his father to thank for his familiarity with the film world
**3** **thank you** a polite response or expression of gratitude
**4** **thank goodness** *or ***thank heavens** *or ***thank God** an exclamation of relief
