**implicit** *adj*
**1** expressed indirectly: an implicit agreement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[implied]], [[understood]], [[suggested]], [[hinted at]], [[taken for granted]], [[unspoken]], [[inferred]], [[tacit]]
**2** [[absolute]] and unquestioning: implicit trust
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[absolute]], [[full]], [[complete]], [[firm]], [[fixed]], [[constant]], [[utter]], [[outright]]
**3** contained in, although not stated openly: this view of the mind was implicit in all his work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inherent]], [[underlying]], [[intrinsic]], [[latent]], [[ingrained]], [[inbuilt]]
 **[[implicitly]]** *adv*
