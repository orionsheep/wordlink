**fume** *vb* **fuming**, **fumed**
**1** to be overcome with anger or fury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rage]], [[seethe]], [[see red]] (*informal*), [[storm]], [[rant]], [[smoulder]], [[get hot under the collar]] (*informal*)
**2** to give off (fumes) or (of fumes) to be given off, esp. during a chemical reaction
**3** to treat with fumes
 *n*
**4** (*often pl*) pungent or toxic [[vapour]], [[gas]], or [[smoke]]: [[exhaust]] fumes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smoke]], [[gas]], [[exhaust]], [[pollution]], [[vapour]], [[smog]]
