**volunteer** *n*
**1** a person who offers voluntarily to do something
**2** a person who freely undertakes military service
 *vb*
**3** to [[offer]] (oneself or one's services) by choice and without being forced
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offer]], [[step forward]]
**4** to enlist voluntarily for military service
**5** to give (information) willingly
**6** to [[offer]] the services of (another person)
