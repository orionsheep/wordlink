**rival** *n*
**1** a person or group that competes with another for the same object or in the same field
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opponent]], [[competitor]], [[contender]], [[contestant]], [[adversary]]
**2** a person or thing that is considered the [[equal]] of another: she is without rival in the field of physics
 *adj*
**3** in the position of a rival
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[competing]], [[conflicting]], opposing
 *vb* **-valling**, **-valled** (*US*) **-valing**, **-valed**
**4** to be the [[equal]] or near [[equal]] of: his inarticulateness was rivalled only by that of his brother
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compete with]], [[match]], [[equal]], [[compare with]], [[come up to]], [[be a match for]]
**5** to try to [[equal]] or surpass
