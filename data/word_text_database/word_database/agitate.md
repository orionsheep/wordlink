**agitate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to [[excite]], [[disturb]], or [[trouble]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upset]], [[worry]], [[trouble]], [[excite]], [[distract]], [[unnerve]], [[disconcert]], [[fluster]]
**2** to [[shake]] or [[stir]] (a liquid)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stir]], [[beat]], [[shake]], [[disturb]], [[toss]], [[rouse]]
**3** to attempt to [[stir]] up public opinion for or against something
 **[[agitated]]** *adj* 
 **[[agitatedly]]** *adv* 
 **[[agitation]]** *n* 
 **[[agitator]]** *n*
