**embryo** (**em**-bree-oh) *n*, *pl* **-bryos**
**1** an unborn animal or human being in the early stages of development, in humans up to approximately the end of the second month of pregnancy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fetus]], [[unborn child]], [[fertilized egg]]
**2** something in an early stage of development: the embryo of a serious comic novel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[germ]], [[beginning]], [[source]], [[root]], [[seed]], [[nucleus]], [[rudiment]]
