**pretend** *vb*
**1** to claim or give the appearance of (something untrue): he pretended to be asleep
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feign]], [[affect]], [[assume]], [[allege]], [[fake]], [[simulate]], [[profess]], [[sham]]
**2** to [[make believe]]: the children pretended to be pop stars
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make believe]], [[suppose]], [[imagine]], [[act]], [[make up]]
**3** (foll. by *to*) to present a claim, esp. a doubtful one: to pretend to the throne
