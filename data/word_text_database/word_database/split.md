**split** *vb* **splitting**, **split**
**1** to [[break]] or cause (something) to [[break]] into [[separate]] pieces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[crack]], [[burst]], [[open]], [[give way]], [[come apart]], [[come undone]]
**2** to [[separate]] (a piece) or (of a piece) to be separated from (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut]], [[break]], [[crack]], [[snap]], [[chop]]
**3** (of a group) to [[separate]] into smaller groups, through disagreement: the council is split over [[rent]] increases
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[divide]], [[separate]], [[disunite]], [[disband]], [[cleave]]
**4** (often foll. by *up*) to [[divide]] (something) among two or more people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share out]], [[divide]], [[distribute]], [[halve]], [[allocate]], [[partition]], [[allot]], [[apportion]]
**5** (*slang*) to leave a place
**6** **split on** (*slang*) to betray; inform on: he didn't tell tales or split on him
**7** **split one's sides** to laugh a great deal
 *n*
**8** a [[gap]] or [[rift]] caused by splitting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crack]], [[tear]], [[rip]], [[gap]], [[rent]], [[breach]], [[slit]], [[fissure]]
**9** a [[division]] in a group or the smaller group resulting from such a [[division]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[division]], [[breach]], [[rift]], [[rupture]], [[discord]], [[schism]], [[estrangement]], [[dissension]]
**10** a dessert of sliced fruit and ice cream, covered with whipped cream and nuts: banana split
 *adj*
**11** [[divided]] (esp. in opinion, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[divided]]
**12** having a split or splits: split ends
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[broken]], [[cracked]], [[fractured]], [[ruptured]], [[cleft]]
