**howl** *n*
**1** the long, loud wailing noise made by a wolf or dog
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[baying]], [[cry]], [[bay]], [[bark]], [[barking]], [[yelping]]
**2** a similar [[cry]] of pain or sorrow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cry]], [[scream]], [[roar]], [[bay]], [[wail]], [[shriek]], [[clamour]], [[bawl]]
**3** a loud burst of laughter
 *vb*
**4** to express (something) in a howl or utter such cries
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bay]], [[cry]]
**5** (of the wind, etc.) to make a wailing noise
