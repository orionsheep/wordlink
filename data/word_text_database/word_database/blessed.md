**blessed** *adj*
**1** made [[holy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holy]], [[sacred]], [[divine]], [[adored]], [[revered]], [[hallowed]], [[sanctified]], [[beatified]]
**2** (*RC Church*) (of a person) [[beatified]] by the pope
**3** bringing great happiness or good fortune: he was blessed with good looks
**4** (*euphemistic*) damned: I'm blessed if I know
