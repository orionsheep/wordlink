**quilt** *n*
**1** a cover for a bed, consisting of a soft filling sewn between two layers of material, usually with crisscross seams
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bedspread]], [[duvet]], [[coverlet]], [[eiderdown]], [[counterpane]], [[doona]] (*Austral*), [[continental quilt]]
**2** a [[continental quilt]]; [[duvet]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bedspread]], [[duvet]], [[coverlet]], [[eiderdown]], [[counterpane]], [[doona]] (*Austral*), [[continental quilt]]
 *vb*
**3** to stitch together two layers of (fabric) with padding between them
 **[[quilted]]** *adj*
