**melody** *n*, *pl* **-dies**
**1** (*[[music]]*) a succession of notes forming a distinctive sequence; [[tune]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tune]], [[song]], [[theme]], [[air]], [[music]], [[strain]]
**2** sounds that are pleasant because of their tone or arrangement, esp. words of poetry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tunefulness]], [[harmony]], [[musicality]], [[euphony]], [[melodiousness]]
