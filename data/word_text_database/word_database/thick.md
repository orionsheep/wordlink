**thick** *adj*
**1** having a relatively great distance between opposite surfaces: thick slices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bulky]], [[broad]], [[big]], [[large]], [[fat]], [[solid]], [[substantial]], [[hefty]]
**2** having a specified distance between opposite surfaces: fifty metres thick
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wide]], [[across]], [[deep]], [[broad]], in extent *or* diameter
**3** having a [[dense]] consistency: thick fog
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opaque]], [[heavy]], [[dense]], [[impenetrable]]
**4** consisting of a lot of things grouped closely together: thick forest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowded]], [[full]], [[covered]], [[bursting]], [[bristling]], [[brimming]]
**5** (of clothes) made of [[heavy]] cloth or wool: a thick jumper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[heavy]], [[heavyweight]], [[dense]], [[chunky]], [[bulky]], [[woolly]]
**6** (*informal*) [[stupid]], slow, or insensitive
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stupid]], [[dense]], [[dopey]] (*informal*), [[obtuse]], [[brainless]], [[dumb-ass]] (*informal*)
**7** (of an accent) very noticeable: each word was pronounced in a thick Dutch accent
**8** Also: **thick as thieves** (*informal*) very [[friendly]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[friendly]], [[close]], [[intimate]], [[familiar]], [[pally]] (*informal*), [[devoted]], [[inseparable]]
**9** **a bit thick** (*Brit* *informal*) unfair or unreasonable: £2 an hour, that's a bit thick!
**10** **thick with**
**A** [[covered]] with a lot of: glass panels thick with dust
 **B** (of a voice) throaty and hard to make out: his voice was thick with emotion
 *adv*
**11** in order to produce something thick: the machine sliced the potatoes too thick
**12** **lay it on thick** (*informal*)
**A** to exaggerate a story
 **B** to flatter someone excessively
**13** **thick and fast** quickly and in [[large]] numbers: theories were flying thick and fast
 *n*
**14** **the thick** the most intense or active part: in the thick of the fighting
**15** **through thick and thin** in good times and bad
 **[[thickly]]** *adv*
