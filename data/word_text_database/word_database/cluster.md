**cluster** *n*
**1** a number of things growing, fastened, or occurring close together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gathering]], [[group]], [[collection]], [[bunch]] (*informal*), [[knot]], [[clump]], [[assemblage]], [[aggregate]]
**2** a number of people or things grouped together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gathering]], [[group]], [[collection]], [[bunch]] (*informal*), [[knot]], [[clump]], [[assemblage]], [[aggregate]]
 *vb*
**3** to [[gather]] or be gathered in clusters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gather]], [[group]], [[collect]], [[bunch]], [[assemble]], [[flock]], [[huddle]]
