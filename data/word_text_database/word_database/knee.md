**knee** *n*
**1** the joint of the leg between the thigh and the lower leg
**2** the area around this joint
**3** the upper surface of a sitting person's thigh: a little girl being cuddled on her father's knee
**4** the part of a garment that covers the knee
**5** **bring someone to his or her knees** to force someone into submission
 *vb* **kneeing**, **kneed**
**6** to strike, nudge, or push with the knee
