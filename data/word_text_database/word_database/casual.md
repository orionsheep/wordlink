**casual** *adj*
**1** being or seeming [[careless]] or [[nonchalant]]: she was casual about security
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[careless]], [[relaxed]], [[unconcerned]], [[blasé]], [[offhand]], [[nonchalant]], [[lackadaisical]]
**2** occasional or irregular: casual workers
**3** shallow or superficial: casual relationships
**4** for [[informal]] wear: a casual jacket
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[informal]], [[leisure]], [[sporty]], [[non-dressy]]
**5** happening by [[chance]] or without planning: a casual comment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[unexpected]], [[random]], [[accidental]], incidental
 *n*
**6** an occasional worker
 **[[casually]]** *adv*
