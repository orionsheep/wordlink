**unite** *vb* **uniting**, **united**
**1** to make or become an integrated whole: conception occurs when a sperm unites with the egg
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[join]], [[link]], [[combine]], [[couple]], [[blend]], [[merge]], [[unify]], [[fuse]]
**2** to form an association or alliance: the opposition parties united to fight against privatization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cooperate]], [[ally]], [[join forces]], [[band]], [[pool]], [[collaborate]]
**3** to possess (a combination of qualities) at the same time: he manages to unite charm and ruthlessness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[join]], [[link]], [[combine]], [[couple]], [[blend]], [[merge]], [[unify]], [[fuse]]
