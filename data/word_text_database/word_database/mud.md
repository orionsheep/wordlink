**mud** *n*
**1** soft wet earth, as found on the ground after rain or at the bottom of ponds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dirt]], [[clay]], [[ooze]], [[silt]], [[sludge]], [[mire]], [[slime]]
**2** **(someone's) name is mud** (*informal*) (someone) is disgraced
**3** **throw mud at** (*informal*) to slander or vilify
 *adj*
**4** made from mud or dried mud: a mud hut
