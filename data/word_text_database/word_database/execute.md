**execute** *vb* **-cuting**, **-cuted**
**1** to put (a condemned person) to death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put to death]], [[kill]], [[shoot]], [[hang]], [[behead]], [[decapitate]], [[guillotine]], [[electrocute]]
**2** to [[carry out]] or [[accomplish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perform]], [[carry out]], [[accomplish]]
**3** to produce or create (a work of art)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry out]], [[effect]], [[implement]], [[accomplish]], [[discharge]], [[administer]], [[prosecute]], [[enact]]
**4** (*law*) to render (a deed) effective, for example by signing it
**5** to [[carry out]] the terms of (a contract, will, etc.)
 **[[executer]]** *n*
