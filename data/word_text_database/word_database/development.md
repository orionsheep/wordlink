**development** *n*
**1** the process of growing or developing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[growth]], [[increase]], [[advance]], [[progress]], [[spread]], [[expansion]], [[evolution]], [[enlargement]]
**2** the product of developing
**3** an [[event]] or [[incident]] that changes a situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[event]], [[happening]], [[result]], [[incident]], [[improvement]], [[evolution]], [[unfolding]], [[occurrence]]
**4** an area of land that has been developed
 **[[developmental]]** *adj*
