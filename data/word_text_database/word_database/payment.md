**payment** *n*
**1** the act of [[paying]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settlement]], [[paying]], [[discharge]], [[remittance]]
**2** a sum of money paid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remittance]], [[advance]], [[deposit]], [[premium]], [[instalment]], [[e-payment]]
**3** something given in return; punishment or [[reward]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wages]], [[fee]], [[reward]], [[hire]], [[remuneration]]
