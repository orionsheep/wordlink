◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interest]], [[fascinate]], [[attract]], [[rivet]], [[titillate]]
**2** to [[plot]] secretly or dishonestly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[scheme]], [[manoeuvre]], [[conspire]], [[connive]], machinate
 *n*
**3** secret plotting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[scheme]], [[conspiracy]], [[manoeuvre]], [[collusion]], [[stratagem]], [[chicanery]], [[wile]]
**4** a secret love [[affair]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affair]], [[romance]], [[intimacy]], [[liaison]], [[amour]]
