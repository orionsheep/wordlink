**cancer** *n*
**1** a serious disease resulting from a malignant [[growth]] or [[tumour]], caused by abnormal and uncontrolled cell division
**2** a malignant [[growth]] or [[tumour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[growth]], [[tumour]], [[malignancy]]
**3** an [[evil]] influence that spreads dangerously: their country would remain a cancer of instability
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evil]], [[corruption]], [[sickness]], [[pestilence]]
 **[[cancerous]]** *adj*
