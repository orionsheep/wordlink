**recorder** *n*
**1** a person who keeps records
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chronicler]], [[archivist]], [[historian]], [[clerk]], [[scribe]], [[diarist]]
**2** a device for recording sound, data, or pictures
**3** (*music*) a wind instrument, blown through the end, with finger-holes and a reedlike tone
**4** (in England and Wales) a barrister or solicitor appointed to sit as a part-time judge in the crown court
