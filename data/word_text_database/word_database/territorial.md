**territorial** *adj*
**1** of or relating to a territory or territories
**2** of or concerned with the ownership and control of an area of land or water: a territorial dispute
**3** (of an animal or bird) establishing and defending an area which it will not let other animals or birds into: the baboon is a territorial species
**4** of or relating to a territorial army
 **[[territorially]]** *adv* 
 **[[territoriality]]** *n*
