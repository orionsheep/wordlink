**growth** *n*
**1** the process of growing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[development]], [[expansion]], [[proliferation]], [[enlargement]], [[multiplication]]
**2** an [[increase]] in size, number, or significance: the growth of drug trafficking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[progress]], [[success]], [[improvement]], [[expansion]], [[advance]], [[prosperity]]
**3** something grown or growing: a thick growth of ivy
**4** any abnormal tissue, such as a [[tumour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tumour]], [[cancer]], [[swelling]], [[lump]], [[carcinoma]] (*pathol*), sarcoma (*med*)
 *adj*
**5** of or relating to growth: growth hormone
