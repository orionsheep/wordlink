**race**^2 *n*
**1** a group of [[people]] of common ancestry with distinguishing physical features, such as skin colour or build
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[people]], [[nation]], [[blood]], [[stock]], [[type]], [[folk]], [[tribe]]
**2** **the human race** human beings collectively
**3** a group of animals or plants having common characteristics that distinguish them from other members of the same species
