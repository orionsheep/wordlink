**invention** *n*
**1** something that is invented
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creation]], [[machine]], [[device]], [[design]], [[instrument]], [[discovery]], [[innovation]], [[gadget]]
**2** the act of inventing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[development]], [[design]], [[production]], [[setting up]], [[foundation]], [[construction]], [[creation]], [[discovery]]
**3** creative power; inventive skill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creativity]], [[imagination]], [[initiative]], [[enterprise]], [[genius]], [[ingenuity]], [[originality]], [[inventiveness]]
**4** (*euphemistic*) a [[lie]]: his story is a malicious invention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fiction]], [[fantasy]], [[lie]], [[yarn]] (*informal*), [[fabrication]], [[falsehood]], [[untruth]]
