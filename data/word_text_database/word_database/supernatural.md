**supernatural** *adj*
**1** of or relating to things that cannot be explained by science, such as clairvoyance, ghosts, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paranormal]], [[unearthly]], [[uncanny]], [[ghostly]], [[psychic]], [[mystic]], [[miraculous]], occult
 *n*
**2** **the supernatural** forces, occurrences, and beings that cannot be explained by science
