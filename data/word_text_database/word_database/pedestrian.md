**pedestrian** *n*
**1** a person who travels on foot
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[walker]], foot-traveller
 *adj*
**2** [[dull]] or [[commonplace]]: a pedestrian performance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[ordinary]], [[boring]], [[commonplace]], [[mundane]], [[mediocre]], [[banal]], [[prosaic]], [[half-pie]] (*NZ* *informal*)
