**blame** *vb* **blaming**, **blamed**
**1** to consider (someone) responsible for: I blame her for the failure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold responsible]], [[accuse]], [[denounce]], [[indict]], [[impeach]], [[incriminate]], [[impute]]
**2** (foll. by *on*) to put [[responsibility]] for (something) on (someone): she blames the failure on me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attribute to]], [[credit to]], [[assign to]], [[put down to]], [[impute to]]
**3** **be to blame** to be at fault
 *n*
**4** [[responsibility]] for something that is wrong: they must take the blame for the failure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsibility]], [[liability]], [[accountability]], [[onus]], [[culpability]], [[answerability]]
**5** an expression of condemnation: analysts lay the blame on party activists
 **[[blamable]]** * or ***[[blameable]]** *adj* 
 **[[blameless]]** *adj*
