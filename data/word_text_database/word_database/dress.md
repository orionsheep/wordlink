**dress** *n*
**1** a one-piece garment consisting of a skirt and bodice and sometimes sleeves
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frock]], [[gown]], [[robe]]
**2** complete style of [[clothing]]: contemporary dress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clothing]], [[clothes]], [[costume]], [[garments]], [[apparel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[attire]], [[garb]], togs
 *adj*
**3** suitable for a formal occasion: he was wearing a dress shirt
 *vb*
**4** to put [[clothes]] on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put on clothes]], [[don clothes]], slip on *or* into something
**5** to put on formal [[clothes]]
**6** to apply protective covering to (a wound)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bandage]], [[treat]], [[plaster]], [[bind up]]
**7** to cover (a salad) with dressing
**8** to prepare (meat, poultry, or fish) for selling or cooking by cleaning or gutting
**9** to put a finish on (the surface of stone, metal, or other building material)
