**fund** *n*
**1** a [[reserve]] of money set aside for a certain purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reserve]], [[stock]], [[supply]], [[store]], [[collection]], [[pool]]
**2** a [[supply]] or [[store]] of something
 *vb*
**3** to provide money to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[finance]], [[back]], [[support]], [[pay for]], [[subsidize]], [[provide money for]], [[put up the money for]]
**4** (*[[finance]]*) to convert (short-term debt) into long-term debt bearing fixed interest
 **[[funder]]** *n*
