**lunge** *n*
**1** a sudden forward motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thrust]], [[charge]], [[pounce]], [[spring]], [[swing]], [[jab]]
**2** (*fencing*) a [[thrust]] made by advancing the front foot and straightening the back leg
 *vb* **lunging**, **lunged**
**3** to move with a lunge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pounce]], [[charge]], [[dive]], [[leap]], [[plunge]], [[thrust]]
**4** (*fencing*) to make a lunge
