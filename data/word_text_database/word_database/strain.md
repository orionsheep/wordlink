**strain**^2 *n*
**1** a group of animals or plants within a species or variety, distinguished by one or more minor characteristics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breed]], [[family]], [[race]], [[blood]], [[descent]], [[extraction]], [[ancestry]], [[lineage]]
**2** a [[trace]] or [[streak]]: a strain of ruthlessness in their play
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[suggestion]], [[tendency]], [[streak]]
