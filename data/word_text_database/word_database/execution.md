**execution** *n*
**1** the act of executing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carrying out]], [[performance]], [[operation]], [[administration]], [[prosecution]], [[enforcement]], [[implementation]], [[accomplishment]]
**2** the [[carrying out]] or undergoing of a sentence of death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[killing]], [[hanging]], [[the death penalty]], [[the rope]], [[capital punishment]], [[beheading]], [[the electric chair]], [[the guillotine]]
**3** the manner in which something is performed; technique
