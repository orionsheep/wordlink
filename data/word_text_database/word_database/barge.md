**barge** *n*
**1** a flat-bottomed boat, used for transporting freight, esp. on canals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[canal boat]], [[lighter]], [[narrow boat]], [[flatboat]]
**2** a boat, often decorated, used in pageants, etc.
 *vb* **barging**, **barged** (*informal*)
**3** (foll. by *into*) to bump into
**4** to push one's way violently
**5** (foll. by *into* or *in*) to interrupt rudely: he barged into our conversation
