**arc** *n*
**1** something curved in shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curve]], [[bend]], [[bow]], [[arch]], [[crescent]], [[half-moon]]
**2** (*maths*) a section of a circle or other [[curve]]
**3** (*electronics*) a stream of very bright light that forms when an electric current flows across a small gap between two electrodes
 *vb*
**4** to form an arc
