**jerk** *vb*
**1** to move with an irregular or spasmodic motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jolt]], [[bang]], [[bump]], [[lurch]]
**2** to pull or push (something) abruptly or spasmodically
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jolt]], [[bang]], [[bump]], [[lurch]]
 *n*
**3** an abrupt or spasmodic [[movement]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lurch]], [[movement]], [[thrust]], [[twitch]], [[jolt]]
**4** an irregular jolting motion: the irritating jerk that heralded a gear change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lurch]], [[movement]], [[thrust]], [[twitch]], [[jolt]]
**5** (*slang*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) a stupid or ignorant person
