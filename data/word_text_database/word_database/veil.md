**veil** *n*
**1** a piece of thin cloth, usually as part of a hat or headdress, used to [[cover]] a woman's face
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mask]], [[cover]], [[shroud]], [[film]], [[curtain]], [[cloak]]
**2** something that conceals the truth: a veil of secrecy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[screen]], [[mask]], [[disguise]], [[blind]]
**3** **take the veil** to become a nun
 *vb*
**4** to [[cover]] or [[conceal]] with or as if with a veil
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[screen]], [[hide]], [[mask]], [[shield]], [[disguise]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]]
