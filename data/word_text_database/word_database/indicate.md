**indicate** *vb* **-cating**, **-cated**
**1** to be or give a sign or symptom of: to concede 18 goals in 7 games indicates a serious malaise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[suggest]], [[reveal]], [[display]], [[demonstrate]], [[point to]], [[imply]], [[manifest]], [[flag up]]
**2** to [[point out]] or [[show]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point to]], [[point out]], [[specify]], [[gesture towards]], [[designate]]
**3** to state briefly
**4** to switch on the indicators in a motor vehicle to [[show]] that one is changing direction
**5** (of measuring instruments) to [[show]] a reading of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[register]], [[show]], [[record]], [[read]], [[express]], [[display]], [[demonstrate]]
**6** (*usually passive*) to recommend or require: surgery seems to be indicated for this patient
 **[[indication]]** *n*
