**morning** *n*
**1** the first part of the day, ending at noon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[before noon]], [[forenoon]], [[morn]] (*poetic*), a.m.
**2** [[daybreak]]; [[dawn]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dawn]], [[sunrise]], [[first light]], [[daybreak]], [[break of day]]
**3** **the morning after** (*informal*) the aftereffects of excess, esp. a hangover
 *adj*
**4** of or in the morning: morning coffee
