**shovel** *n*
**1** a tool for lifting or moving loose material, consisting of a broad blade attached to a large handle
**2** a machine or part of a machine resembling a shovel in function
 *vb* **-velling**, **-velled** (*US*) **-veling**, **-veled**
**3** to lift or [[move]] (loose material) with a shovel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[move]], [[scoop]], [[dredge]], [[load]], [[heap]]
**4** to put away large quantities of (something) quickly: shovelling food into their mouths
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stuff]], [[ladle]]
