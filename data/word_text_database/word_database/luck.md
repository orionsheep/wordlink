**luck** *n*
**1** events that are subject to [[chance]]; [[fortune]], good or bad
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fortune]], [[lot]], [[stars]], [[chance]], [[accident]], [[fate]], [[destiny]], [[twist of fate]]
**2** [[success]] or [[good fortune]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good fortune]], [[success]], [[advantage]], [[prosperity]], [[blessing]], [[windfall]], [[godsend]], [[serendipity]]
**3** **down on one's luck** lacking [[good fortune]] to the extent of suffering hardship
**4** **no such luck** (*informal*) unfortunately not
**5** **try one's luck** to attempt something that is uncertain
