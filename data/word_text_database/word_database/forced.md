**forced** *adj*
**1** done because of force: forced labour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compulsory]], [[enforced]], [[mandatory]], [[obligatory]], [[involuntary]], [[conscripted]]
**2** [[false]] or [[unnatural]]: forced jollity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[false]], [[affected]], [[strained]], [[wooden]], [[stiff]], [[artificial]], [[contrived]], [[unnatural]]
**3** due to an emergency: a forced landing
