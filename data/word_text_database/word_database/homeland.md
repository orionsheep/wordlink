**homeland** *n*
**1** the country from which the ancestors of a person or group came: defending their homeland
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[native land]], [[birthplace]], [[motherland]], [[fatherland]], [[country of origin]], [[mother country]], [[Godzone]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**2** (*S African*) (formerly) an area reserved for occupation by a Black African people
