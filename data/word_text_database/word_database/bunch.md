**bunch** *n*
**1** a number of things growing, fastened, or grouped together: a bunch of grapes; a bunch of keys
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bouquet]], [[sheaf]]
**2** a collection; [[group]]: a bunch of queries
**3** a [[group]] or company: a bunch of cowards
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[band]], [[crowd]], [[party]], [[team]], [[gathering]], [[gang]], flock
 *vb*
**4** (sometimes foll. by *together* or *up*) to [[group]] or be grouped into a bunch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[bunch together or up]]**: [[group]], mass, collect, assemble, cluster, huddle
