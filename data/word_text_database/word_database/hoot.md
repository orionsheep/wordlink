**hoot** *n*
**1** the sound of a car horn
**2** the cry of an owl
**3** a high-pitched noise showing disapproval
**4** (*informal*) an amusing person or thing
 *vb*
**5** (*Brit*) to blow (a car horn)
**6** to make a hoot
**7** to jeer or yell contemptuously at someone
**8** to drive (speakers or performers on stage) off by hooting
