**smother** *vb*
**1** to [[extinguish]] (a fire) by covering so as to cut it off from the air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extinguish]], [[put out]], [[stifle]], [[snuff]]
**2** to [[suffocate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suffocate]], [[choke]], [[strangle]], [[stifle]]
**3** to surround or overwhelm (with): she smothered him with her idea of affection
**4** to [[suppress]] or [[stifle]]: he smothered an ironic chuckle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suppress]], [[stifle]], [[repress]], [[hide]], [[conceal]], [[muffle]]
**5** to cover over thickly: ice cream smothered with sauce
