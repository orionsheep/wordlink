**project** *n*
**1** a proposal or [[plan]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scheme]], [[plan]], [[job]], [[idea]], [[campaign]], [[operation]], [[activity]], [[venture]]
**2** a detailed study of a particular subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assignment]], [[task]], [[homework]], piece of research
 *vb*
**3** to make a prediction based on known data and observations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forecast]], [[expect]], [[estimate]], [[predict]], [[reckon]], [[calculate]], [[gauge]], [[extrapolate]]
**4** to cause (an image) to appear on a surface
**5** to communicate (an impression): he wants to project an image of a deep-thinking articulate gentleman
**6** to [[jut]] out
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick out]], [[extend]], [[stand out]], [[bulge]], [[protrude]], [[overhang]], [[jut]]
**7** to cause (one's voice) to be heard clearly at a distance
**8** to transport in the imagination: it's hard to project oneself into his situation
