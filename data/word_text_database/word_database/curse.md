**curse** *vb* **cursing**, **cursed**
**1** to [[swear]] or [[swear]] at (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swear]], [[cuss]] (*informal*), [[blaspheme]], [[take the Lord's name in vain]]
**2** to call on supernatural powers to bring harm to (someone or something)
 *n*
**3** a profane or obscene expression, usually of anger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oath]], [[obscenity]], [[blasphemy]], [[expletive]], [[profanity]], [[imprecation]], [[swearword]]
**4** an appeal to a supernatural power for harm to come to a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[malediction]], [[jinx]], [[anathema]], [[hoodoo]] (*informal*), [[excommunication]]
**5** harm resulting from a curse
**6** something that causes great [[trouble]] or harm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affliction]], [[plague]] (*informal*), [[scourge]], [[trouble]], [[torment]], [[hardship]], [[bane]]
**7** **the curse** (*informal*) menstruation or a menstrual period
