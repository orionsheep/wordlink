**oath** *n*, *pl* **oaths**
**1** a solemn [[promise]], esp. to tell the truth in a court of law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise]], [[bond]], [[pledge]], [[vow]], [[word]], [[affirmation]], [[avowal]]
**2** an offensive or blasphemous expression; a swearword
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swear word]], [[curse]], [[obscenity]], [[blasphemy]], [[expletive]], [[four-letter word]], [[profanity]]
**3** **on *or* under oath** having made a solemn [[promise]] to tell the truth, esp. in a court of law
