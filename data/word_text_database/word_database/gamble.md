**gamble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to [[play]] games of [[chance]] to win money or prizes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bet]], [[play]], [[game]], [[speculate]], [[punt]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[wager]], [[have a flutter]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[risk]] or [[bet]] (something) on the outcome of an event or sport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[risk]], [[chance]], [[hazard]], [[wager]]
**3** **gamble away** to lose by [[gambling]]
**4** **gamble on** to act with the expectation of: she has gambled on proving everyone wrong
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take a chance]], [[speculate]], stick your neck out (*informal*)
 *n*
**5** a risky act or [[venture]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[risk]], [[chance]], [[venture]], [[lottery]], [[speculation]], [[uncertainty]], [[leap in the dark]]
**6** a [[bet]] or [[wager]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bet]], [[flutter]] (*informal*), [[punt]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[wager]]
 **[[gambler]]** *n*
