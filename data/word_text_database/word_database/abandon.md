**abandon** *vb*
**1** to [[desert]] or [[leave]]: he had already abandoned his first wife
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leave]], [[strand]], [[ditch]] (*slang*), [[forsake]], [[run out on]], [[desert]], [[dump]]
**2** to [[give up]] completely: did you abandon all attempts at contact with the boy?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[give up]], [[halt]], [[pack in]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[discontinue]], [[leave off]]
**3** to give oneself over completely to an emotion
 *n*
**4** **with abandon** uninhibitedly and without restraint
 **[[abandonment]]** *n*
