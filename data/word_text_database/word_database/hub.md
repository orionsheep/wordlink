**hub** *n*
**1** the central portion of a wheel, through which the axle passes
**2** the central, most important, or active part of a place or organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[centre]], [[heart]], [[focus]], [[core]], [[middle]], [[focal point]], [[nerve centre]]
**3** an airport, railway station, etc. from which many services operate and connecting journeys can be made
**4** a device for connecting computers in a network
