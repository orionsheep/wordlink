**shatter** *vb*
**1** to [[break]] suddenly into many small pieces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smash]], [[break]], [[burst]], [[crack]], [[crush]], [[pulverize]]
**2** to damage badly or [[destroy]]: to shatter American confidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destroy]], [[ruin]], [[wreck]], [[demolish]], [[torpedo]]
**3** to upset (someone) greatly: the whole experience shattered me
 **[[shattering]]** *adj*
