**chalk** *n*
**1** a soft white rock consisting of calcium carbonate
**2** a piece of chalk, either white or coloured, used for writing and drawing on blackboards
**3** **as different as chalk and cheese** (*informal*) totally different
**4** **not by a long chalk** (*informal*) by no means: you haven't finished by a long chalk
 *vb*
**5** to draw or mark with chalk
 **[[chalky]]** *adj*
