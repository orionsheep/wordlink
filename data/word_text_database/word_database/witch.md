**witch** *n*
**1** (in former times) a woman believed to possess evil magic powers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enchantress]], [[magician]], [[hag]], [[crone]], [[sorceress]], [[Wiccan]]
**2** a person who practises magic or sorcery, esp. black magic
**3** (*derogatory*) an ugly or wicked old woman
