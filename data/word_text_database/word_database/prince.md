**prince** *n*
**1** a male member of a royal family, esp. the son of the king or queen
**2** the male [[ruler]] of a small country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ruler]], [[lord]], [[monarch]], [[sovereign]], [[crown prince]], [[liege]], [[prince regent]], [[crowned head]]
**3** an outstanding member of a specified group: Dryden, that prince of poets
