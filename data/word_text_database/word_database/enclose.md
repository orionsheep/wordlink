**enclose** *vb* **-closing**, **-closed**
**1** to [[surround]] completely: the house enclosed a courtyard
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surround]], [[circle]], [[bound]], [[fence]], [[confine]], [[close in]], [[wall in]], [[encircle]]
**2** to [[include]] along with something else: he enclosed a letter with the parcel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[send with]], [[include]], [[put in]], [[insert]]
