**fidelity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** [[faithfulness]] to one's spouse or lover
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loyalty]], [[devotion]], [[allegiance]], [[constancy]], [[faithfulness]], [[dependability]], [[trustworthiness]], [[staunchness]]
**2** [[loyalty]] to a person, belief, or cause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loyalty]], [[devotion]], [[allegiance]], [[constancy]], [[faithfulness]], [[dependability]], [[trustworthiness]], [[staunchness]]
**3** [[accuracy]] in reporting detail: an account of the invasion written with objectivity and fidelity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accuracy]], [[precision]], [[correspondence]], [[closeness]], [[faithfulness]], [[exactness]], [[scrupulousness]]
**4** (*electronics*) the degree to which an amplifier or radio accurately reproduces the input signal
