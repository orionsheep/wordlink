**brawl** *n*
**1** a loud disagreement or [[fight]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[clash]], [[fray]], [[skirmish]], [[scuffle]], [[punch-up]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[fracas]], [[altercation]], biffo (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb*
**2** to quarrel or [[fight]] noisily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[scrap]] (*informal*), [[wrestle]], [[tussle]], [[scuffle]]
