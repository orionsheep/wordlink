**misuse** *n*
**1** incorrect, improper, or careless use: misuse of drugs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waste]], [[squandering]]
**2** cruel or inhumane treatment
 *vb* **-using**, **-used**
**3** to use wrongly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abuse]], [[misapply]], prostitute (*old-fashioned*, *derogatory*)
**4** to treat badly or harshly
