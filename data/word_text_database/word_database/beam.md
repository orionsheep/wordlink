**beam** *n*
**1** a broad [[smile]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smile]], [[grin]]
**2** a [[ray]] of light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ray]], [[flash]], [[stream]], [[glow]], [[streak]], [[shaft]], [[gleam]], [[glint]]
**3** a narrow flow of electromagnetic radiation or particles: an electron beam
**4** a long thick piece of wood, metal, etc. used in building
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rafter]], [[support]], [[timber]], [[spar]], [[plank]], [[girder]], [[joist]]
**5** the central [[shaft]] of a plough to which all the main parts are attached
**6** the breadth of a ship at its widest part
**7** **off (the) beam** (*informal*) mistaken or irrelevant
 *vb*
**8** to [[smile]] broadly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smile]], [[grin]]
**9** to [[send out]] or [[radiate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[radiate]], [[flash]], [[shine]], [[glow]], [[glitter]], [[glare]], [[gleam]]
**10** to divert or aim (a radio signal, light, etc.) in a certain direction: the concert was beamed live from Geneva
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transmit]], [[show]], [[air]], [[broadcast]], [[cable]], [[send out]], [[relay]], [[televise]], [[stream]]
