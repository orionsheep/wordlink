**tutor** *n*
**1** a [[teacher]], usually one instructing individual pupils
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teacher]], [[coach]], [[instructor]], [[educator]], [[guide]], [[guardian]], [[lecturer]], [[guru]]
**2** (at a college or university) a member of staff responsible for the teaching and supervision of a certain number of students
 *vb*
**3** to act as a tutor to (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teach]], [[educate]], [[school]], [[train]], [[coach]], [[guide]], [[drill]], [[instruct]]
 **[[tutorship]]** *n*
