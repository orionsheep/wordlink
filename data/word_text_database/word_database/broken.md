**broken** *vb*
**1** the past participle of **break**
 *adj*
**2** [[fractured]], [[smashed]], or splintered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smashed]], [[burst]], [[shattered]], [[fragmented]], [[fractured]], [[severed]], [[ruptured]], [[separated]]
**3** [[interrupted]]; disturbed: broken sleep
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interrupted]], [[incomplete]], [[erratic]], [[intermittent]], [[fragmentary]], [[spasmodic]], [[discontinuous]]
**4** not functioning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defective]], [[not working]], [[imperfect]], [[out of order]], [[on the blink]] (*slang*), [[kaput]] (*informal*)
**5** (of a promise or contract) violated; infringed
**6** (of the speech of a foreigner) imperfectly spoken: broken English
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imperfect]], [[halting]], [[hesitating]], [[stammering]], [[disjointed]]
**7** Also: **broken-in** made tame by training
**8** exhausted or weakened, as through ill-health or misfortune
