**version** *n*
**1** a [[form]] of something, such as a piece of writing, with some differences from other forms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[form]], [[variety]], [[variant]], [[sort]], [[class]], [[design]], [[style]], [[model]]
**2** an [[account]] of something from a certain point of [[view]]: so far there's been no official version of the incident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[account]], [[report]], [[description]], [[record]], [[reading]], [[story]], [[view]], [[understanding]]
**3** an [[adaptation]], for example of a book or play into a film
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adaptation]], [[edition]], [[interpretation]], [[form]], [[copy]], [[rendering]], [[reproduction]], [[portrayal]]
