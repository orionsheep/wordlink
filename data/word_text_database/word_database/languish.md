**languish** *vb* (*literary*)
**1** to [[suffer]] deprivation, hardship, or neglect: she won't languish in jail for it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decline]], [[fade away]], [[wither away]], [[flag]], [[weaken]], [[wilt]]
**2** to lose or diminish in strength or energy: the design languished into oblivion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waste away]], [[suffer]], [[rot]], [[be abandoned]], [[be neglected]]
**3** (often foll. by *for*) to be listless with [[desire]]; [[pine]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pine]], [[long]], [[desire]], [[hunger]], [[yearn]], [[hanker]]
 **[[languishing]]** *adj*
