**vision** *n*
**1** the ability to see
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sight]], [[seeing]], [[eyesight]], [[view]], [[perception]]
**2** a vivid mental [[image]] produced by the [[imagination]]: I kept having visions of her getting lost
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[image]], [[idea]], [[dream]], [[plans]], [[hopes]], [[prospect]], [[ideal]], [[concept]]
**3** a [[hallucination]] caused by divine [[inspiration]], madness, or drugs: visions of angels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hallucination]], [[illusion]], [[apparition]], [[revelation]], [[delusion]], [[mirage]], [[chimera]]
**4** great [[perception]] of future developments: what he had instead of charisma was vision
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[foresight]], [[imagination]], [[perception]], [[insight]], [[awareness]], [[inspiration]], [[innovation]], [[creativity]]
**5** the [[image]] on a television screen
**6** a person or thing of extraordinary beauty
