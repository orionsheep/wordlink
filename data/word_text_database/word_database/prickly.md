**prickly** *adj* **-lier**, **-liest**
**1** having prickles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spiny]], [[barbed]], [[thorny]], [[bristly]]
**2** [[tingling]] or [[stinging]]: he had a prickly feeling down his back
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[itchy]], [[sharp]], [[smarting]], [[stinging]], [[crawling]], [[tingling]], [[scratchy]]
**3** touchy or irritable: you know how prickly they can be about the issue
