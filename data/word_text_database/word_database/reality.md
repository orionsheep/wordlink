**reality** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state of things as they are or appear to be, rather than as one might wish them to be
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fact]], [[truth]], [[realism]], [[validity]], [[verity]], [[actuality]]
**2** something that is real
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[truth]], [[fact]], [[actuality]]
**3** the state of being real
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fact]], [[truth]], [[realism]], [[validity]], [[verity]], [[actuality]]
**4** **in reality** in [[fact]]
