**fertile** *adj*
**1** capable of producing offspring, crops, or vegetation
**2** (*biol*) capable of growth and development: fertile seeds
**3** highly [[productive]]: a fertile imagination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[productive]], [[rich]], [[lush]], [[prolific]], [[abundant]], [[plentiful]], [[fruitful]], [[teeming]]
**4** (*physics*) (of a substance) able to be transformed into fissile or fissionable material
 **[[fertility]]** *n*
