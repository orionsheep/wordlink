**fun** *n*
**1** [[pleasant]], [[enjoyable]], and light-hearted activity or [[amusement]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amusement]], [[sport]], [[pleasure]], [[entertainment]], [[recreation]], [[enjoyment]], [[merriment]], [[jollity]]
**2** **for *or* in fun** for [[amusement]] or as a joke
**3** **make fun of** *or ***poke fun at** to ridicule or tease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make fun of something or someone]]**: mock, tease, ridicule, poke fun at, laugh at, mimic, parody, send up (*Brit* *informal*)
 *adj*
**4** (of a person) [[amusing]] and likeable
**5** (of a place or activity) [[amusing]] and [[enjoyable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enjoyable]], [[entertaining]], [[pleasant]], [[amusing]], [[lively]], [[diverting]], [[witty]], [[convivial]]
