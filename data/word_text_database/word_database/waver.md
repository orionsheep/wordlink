**waver** *vb*
**1** to [[hesitate]] between possibilities; be indecisive
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hesitate]], [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[vacillate]], [[falter]], [[fluctuate]], [[seesaw]], [[hum and haw]]
**2** to swing from one thing to another: she wavered between annoyance and civility
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hesitate]], [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[vacillate]], [[falter]], [[fluctuate]], [[seesaw]], [[hum and haw]]
**3** (of a voice or stare) to become unsteady
**4** to move back and forth or one way and another: the barrel of the gun began to waver
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flicker]], [[shake]], [[tremble]], [[wobble]], [[quiver]], [[totter]]
 **[[wavering]]** *adj*
