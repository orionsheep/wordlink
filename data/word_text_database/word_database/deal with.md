**deal with** *vb*
**1** to take action on: he was not competent to deal with the legal aspects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[deal with something or someone]]**: handle, manage, treat, cope with, take care of, see to, attend to, get to grips with
**2** to be concerned with: I do not wish to deal with specifics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[deal with something]]**: be concerned with, involve, concern, touch, regard, apply to, bear on, pertain to
**3** to do business with
