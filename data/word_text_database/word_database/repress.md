**repress** *vb*
**1** to keep (feelings) under [[control]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[suppress]], [[hold back]], [[bottle up]], [[check]], [[curb]], [[restrain]], [[inhibit]]
**2** to restrict the freedom of: he continued to repress his people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[subdue]], [[abuse]], [[wrong]], [[persecute]], [[quell]], [[subjugate]], [[maltreat]]
**3** (*psychol*) to banish (unpleasant thoughts) from one's conscious mind
 **[[repression]]** *n* 
 **[[repressive]]** *adj*
