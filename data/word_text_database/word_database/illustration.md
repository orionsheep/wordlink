**illustration** *n*
**1** a [[picture]] or diagram used to explain or decorate a text
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[picture]], [[drawing]], [[painting]], [[image]], [[print]], [[plate]], [[figure]], [[portrait]]
**2** an [[example]]: an illustration of the brutality of the regime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[example]], [[case]], [[instance]], [[sample]], [[specimen]], [[exemplar]]
**3** the art of illustrating
