**construction** *n*
**1** the act of constructing or manner in which a thing is constructed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[building]], [[creation]], [[composition]]
**2** something that has been constructed
**3** the business or work of [[building]] houses or other structures
**4** (*formal*) an [[interpretation]]: the financial markets will put the worst possible construction on any piece of news which might affect them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interpretation]], [[reading]], [[explanation]], [[rendering]], [[inference]]
**5** (*grammar*) the way in which words are arranged in a sentence, clause, or phrase
 **[[constructional]]** *adj*
