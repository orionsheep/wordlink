**real** *adj*
**1** existing or occurring in the physical world
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[true]], [[genuine]], [[sincere]], [[factual]], [[dinkum]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[unfeigned]]
**2** [[actual]]: the real agenda
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[actual]], [[true]]
**3** important or serious: the real challenge
**4** rightly so called: a real friend
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proper]], [[true]], [[valid]]
**5** [[genuine]]: the council has no real authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[actual]], [[true]]
**6** (of food or drink) made in a traditional way to ensure the best flavour
**7** relating to immovable property such as land or buildings: real estate
**8** (*econ*) (of prices or incomes) considered in terms of purchasing power rather than nominal currency value
**9** **the real thing** the [[genuine]] article, not a substitute or imitation
