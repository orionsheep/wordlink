**defend** *vb*
**1** to [[protect]] from harm or danger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]], [[cover]], [[guard]], [[screen]], [[preserve]], [[look after]], [[shelter]], [[shield]]
**2** to [[support]] in the face of criticism: I spoke up to defend her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[champion]], [[justify]], [[endorse]], [[uphold]], [[vindicate]], [[stand up for]], [[speak up for]]
**3** to represent (a defendant) in court
**4** to [[protect]] (a title or championship) against a challenge
 **[[defender]]** *n*
