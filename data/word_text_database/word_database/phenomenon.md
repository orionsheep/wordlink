**phenomenon** *n*, *pl* **-ena*** or ***-enons**
**1** anything that can be perceived as an [[occurrence]] or [[fact]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occurrence]], [[happening]], [[fact]], [[event]], [[incident]], [[circumstance]], [[episode]]
**2** any remarkable [[occurrence]] or person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wonder]], [[sensation]], [[exception]], [[miracle]], [[marvel]], [[prodigy]], [[rarity]]
