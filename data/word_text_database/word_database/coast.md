**coast** *n*
**1** the place where the land meets the sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shore]], [[border]], [[beach]], [[seaside]], [[coastline]], [[seaboard]]
**2** **the coast is clear** (*informal*) the obstacles or dangers are gone
 *vb*
**3** to move by momentum or force of gravity, without the use of power
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cruise]], [[sail]], [[drift]], [[taxi]], [[glide]], [[freewheel]]
**4** to proceed without great effort: they coasted to a 31–9 win in the pairs
 **[[coastal]]** *adj*
