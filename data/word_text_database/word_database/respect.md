**respect** *n*
**1** [[consideration]]: respect for my feelings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consideration]], [[kindness]], [[deference]], [[tact]], [[thoughtfulness]], [[considerateness]]
**2** an attitude of [[deference]] or [[esteem]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regard]], [[honour]], [[recognition]], [[esteem]], [[admiration]], [[estimation]]
**3** the state of being honoured or esteemed
**4** a [[detail]] or characteristic: in virtually all respects boys develop more slowly than girls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[particular]], [[way]], [[point]], [[matter]], [[sense]], [[detail]], [[feature]], [[aspect]]
**5** **in respect of** *or ***with respect to** in reference or relation to
**6** **respects** polite greetings: he paid his respects to her and left
 *vb*
**7** to have an attitude of [[esteem]] towards: she is the person I most respect and wish to emulate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think highly of]], [[value]], [[honour]], [[admire]], [[esteem]], [[look up to]], [[defer to]], have a good *or* high opinion of
**8** to pay proper attention or [[consideration]] to: he called on rebel groups to respect a cease-fire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abide by]], [[follow]], [[observe]], [[comply with]], [[obey]], [[heed]], [[keep to]], [[adhere to]]
 **[[respecter]]** *n*
