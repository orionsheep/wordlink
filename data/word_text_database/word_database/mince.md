**mince** *vb* **mincing**, **[[minced]]**
**1** to chop, [[grind]], or [[cut]] into very small pieces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cut]], [[grind]], [[crumble]], [[dice]], [[hash]], [[chop up]]
**2** to walk or speak in an affected dainty manner
**3** **not mince one's words** to be direct and to the point rather than making an effort to avoid upsetting people
 *n*
**4** (*chiefly* *Brit** & **NZ*) [[minced]] meat
 **[[minced]]** *adj* 
 **[[mincer]]** *n*
