**fever** *n*
**1** an abnormally high body temperature, accompanied by a fast pulse rate, shivering, and nausea. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**febrile**
**2** any disease characterized by a high temperature
**3** intense nervous [[excitement]]: she waited in a fever of anxiety
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excitement]], [[frenzy]], [[ferment]], [[agitation]], [[fervour]], [[restlessness]], [[delirium]]
