**brain** *n*
**1** the soft mass of nervous tissue within the skull of vertebrates that controls and coordinates the nervous system
**2** (*often pl*, *informal*) intellectual ability: he's got brains
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intelligence]], [[understanding]], [[sense]], [[intellect]]
**3** (*informal*) an intelligent person
**4** **on the brain** (*informal*) constantly in mind: I had that song on the brain
**5** **the brains** (*informal*) a person who plans and organizes something: the brains behind the bid
 *vb*
**6** (*slang*) to hit (someone) hard on the head
