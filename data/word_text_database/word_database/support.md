**support** *vb*
**1** to [[carry]] the weight of (a thing or person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bear]], [[carry]], [[sustain]], [[prop]] (up), [[reinforce]], [[hold]], [[brace]], [[buttress]]
**2** to provide the necessities of life for (a family or person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[provide for]], [[maintain]], [[look after]], [[keep]], [[fund]], [[finance]], [[sustain]]
**3** to give practical or emotional [[help]] to (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[help]], [[back]], [[champion]], [[second]], [[aid]], [[defend]], [[assist]], [[side with]]
**4** to give approval to (a cause, idea, or political party)
**5** to take an active interest in and be loyal to (a particular football or other sports team)
**6** to establish the truthfulness or accuracy of (a theory or statement) by providing new facts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bear out]], [[confirm]], [[verify]], [[substantiate]], [[corroborate]]
**7** to speak in a debate in favour of (a motion)
**8** (in a concert) to perform earlier than (the main attraction)
**9** (*films*, *theatre*) to play a less important role to (the leading actor)
 *n*
**10** the act of supporting or the condition of being supported
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[furtherance]], [[backing]], [[promotion]], [[assistance]], [[encouragement]]
**11** a thing that bears the weight of an object from below
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prop]], [[post]], [[foundation]], [[brace]], [[pillar]]
**12** a person who gives someone practical or emotional [[help]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[prop]], [[mainstay]], [[tower of strength]], [[second]], [[backer]]
**13** the means of providing the necessities of life for a family or person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upkeep]], [[maintenance]], [[keep]], [[subsistence]], [[sustenance]]
**14** a band or entertainer not topping the bill
 **[[supportive]]** *adj*
