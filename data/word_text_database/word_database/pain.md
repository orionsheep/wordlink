**pain** *n*
**1** physical [[hurt]] or [[discomfort]] caused by injury or illness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suffering]], [[discomfort]], [[hurt]], [[irritation]], [[tenderness]], [[soreness]]
**2** emotional [[suffering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sorrow]], [[suffering]], [[torture]], [[distress]], [[despair]], [[misery]], [[agony]], [[sadness]]
**3** Also called: **pain in the neck** (*informal*) a person or thing that is annoying or irritating
**4** **on pain of** subject to the penalty of: orders which their soldiers were bound to follow on pain of death
 *vb*
**5** to cause (a person) physical or mental [[suffering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distress]], [[hurt]], [[torture]], [[grieve]], [[torment]], [[sadden]], [[agonize]], [[cut to the quick]]
**6** (*informal*) to annoy; irritate
 **[[painless]]** *adj*
