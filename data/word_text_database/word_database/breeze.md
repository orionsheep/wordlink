**breeze** *n*
**1** a gentle or [[light wind]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[light wind]], [[air]], [[draught]], [[gust]], [[waft]], [[zephyr]], [[breath of wind]], [[current of air]]
**2** (*informal*) an easy task
 *vb* **breezing**, **breezed**
**3** to move quickly or casually: he breezed into the room
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sweep]], [[move briskly]], [[pass]], [[sail]], [[hurry]], [[glide]], [[flit]]
