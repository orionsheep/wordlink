**follower** *n*
**1** a person who accepts the teachings of another: a follower of Nietzsche
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[fan]], [[disciple]], [[devotee]], [[apostle]], [[pupil]], [[adherent]], [[groupie]] (*slang*)
**2** a [[supporter]], such as of a sport or team
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[fan]], [[disciple]], [[devotee]], [[apostle]], [[pupil]], [[adherent]], [[groupie]] (*slang*)
