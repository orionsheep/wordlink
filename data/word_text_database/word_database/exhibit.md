**exhibit** *vb*
**1** to [[display]] (a work of art) to the public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[display]], [[show]], [[set out]], [[parade]], [[unveil]], [[put on view]]
**2** to [[show]] (a quality or feeling): they exhibited extraordinary courage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[reveal]], [[display]], [[demonstrate]], [[express]], [[indicate]], manifest
 *n*
**3** an object exhibited to the public
**4** (*law*) a document or object produced in court as evidence
 **[[exhibitor]]** *n*
