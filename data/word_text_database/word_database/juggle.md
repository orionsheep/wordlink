**juggle** *vb* **-gling**, **-gled**
**1** to throw and catch several objects continuously so that most are in the air at the same time
**2** to keep (several activities) in progress at the same time: people who are adept at juggling priorities
**3** to [[manipulate]] (facts or figures) to suit one's purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manipulate]], [[change]], [[alter]], [[modify]], [[manoeuvre]]
 **[[juggler]]** *n*
