◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sadness]], [[depression]], [[misery]], [[gloom]], [[sorrow]], [[unhappiness]], [[despondency]], [[dejection]]
**2** a [[sad]], thoughtful state of mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sadness]], [[depression]], [[misery]], [[gloom]], [[sorrow]], [[unhappiness]], [[despondency]], [[dejection]]
 *adj*
**3** characterized by, causing, or expressing [[sadness]]
