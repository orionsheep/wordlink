**platform** *n*
**1** a raised floor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stage]], [[stand]], [[podium]], [[rostrum]], [[dais]], [[pulpit]], [[soapbox]]
**2** a raised area at a railway station where passengers get on or off the trains
**3** the declared aims of a political party
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[policy]], [[programme]], [[principle]], [[objective]](s), [[manifesto]], [[party line]]
**4** the thick raised sole of some shoes
**5** a type of computer hardware or operating system
