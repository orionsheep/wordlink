**bring up** *vb*
**1** to care for and train (a child); rear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[bring someone up]]**: rear, raise, support, train, develop, teach, breed, foster
**2** to raise (a subject) for discussion; mention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[bring something up]]**: mention, raise, introduce, point out, refer to, allude to, broach
**3** to vomit (food)
