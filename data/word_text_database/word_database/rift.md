**rift** *n*
**1** a [[break]] in friendly relations between people or groups of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breach]], [[division]], [[split]], [[separation]], [[falling out]] (*informal*), [[disagreement]], [[quarrel]]
**2** a [[gap]] or space made by splitting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[split]], [[opening]], [[crack]], [[gap]], [[break]], [[fault]], [[flaw]], [[cleft]]
