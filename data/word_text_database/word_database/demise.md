**demise** *n*
**1** the eventual [[failure]] of something originally successful
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[failure]], [[end]], [[fall]], [[defeat]], [[collapse]], [[ruin]], [[breakdown]], [[overthrow]]
**2** (*euphemistic*, *formal*) [[death]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[death]], [[end]], [[dying]], [[passing]], [[departure]], [[decease]]
**3** (*property law*) a transfer of an estate by lease
 *vb* **-mising**, **-mised**
**4** (*property law*) to transfer for a limited period; lease
