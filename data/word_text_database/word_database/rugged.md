**rugged** (**rug**-gid) *adj*
**1** [[rocky]] or steep: the rugged mountains of Sicily's interior
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rocky]], [[broken]], [[rough]], [[craggy]], [[difficult]], [[ragged]], [[irregular]], [[uneven]]
**2** with an [[uneven]] or jagged surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rocky]], [[broken]], [[rough]], [[craggy]], [[difficult]], [[ragged]], [[irregular]], [[uneven]]
**3** (of the face) [[strong-featured]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strong-featured]], [[rough-hewn]], [[weather-beaten]]
**4** [[rough]], [[sturdy]], or determined in character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tough]], [[strong]], [[robust]], [[muscular]], [[sturdy]], [[burly]], [[husky]] (*informal*), [[brawny]]
**5** (of equipment or machines) designed to withstand [[rough]] treatment or use in [[rough]] conditions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-built]], [[strong]], [[tough]], [[robust]], [[sturdy]]
