**mature** *adj*
**1** fully developed physically or mentally; [[grown-up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grown-up]], [[adult]], [[of age]], [[fully fledged]], [[full-grown]]
**2** (of plans or theories) fully considered and thought-out
**3** sensible and balanced in personality and emotional behaviour
**4** due or payable: a mature insurance policy
**5** (of fruit, wine, or cheese) [[ripe]] or fully aged
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[matured]], [[seasoned]], [[ripe]], mellow
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**6** to make or become mature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[develop]], [[grow up]], [[bloom]], [[blossom]], [[come of age]], [[age]]
**7** (of bills or bonds) to become due for payment or repayment
 **[[maturity]]** *n*
