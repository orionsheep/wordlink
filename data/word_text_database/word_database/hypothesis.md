**hypothesis** (hie-**poth**-iss-iss) *n*, *pl* **-ses** (-seez) a suggested explanation for a group of facts, accepted either as a basis for further verification or as likely to be true
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theory]], [[premise]], [[proposition]], [[assumption]], [[thesis]], [[postulate]], supposition
 **[[hypothesize]]** * or ***-ise** *vb*
