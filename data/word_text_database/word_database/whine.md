**whine** *n*
**1** a long high-pitched plaintive [[cry]] or [[moan]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cry]], [[moan]], [[sob]], [[wail]], [[whimper]]
**2** a peevish [[complaint]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complaint]], [[moan]] (*informal*), [[grumble]], [[grouse]], [[gripe]] (*informal*), [[whinge]] (*informal*), [[grouch]] (*informal*)
 *vb* **[[whining]]**, **[[whined]]**
**3** to [[whinge]] or [[complain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complain]], [[grumble]], [[gripe]] (*informal*), [[whinge]] (*informal*), [[moan]], [[grouse]], grizzle (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[grouch]] (*informal*)
**4** to issue a long high-pitched [[moan]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cry]], [[sob]], [[wail]], [[whimper]], [[sniffle]], [[snivel]], [[moan]]
 **[[whiner]]** *n* 
 **[[whining]]** *adj*, *n*
