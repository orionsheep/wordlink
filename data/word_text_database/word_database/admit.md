**admit** *vb* **-mitting**, **-mitted**
**1** to [[confess]] or [[acknowledge]] (a crime or mistake)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confess]], [[confide]], [[own up]], [[come clean]] (*informal*)
**2** to concede (the truth of something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[allow]], [[agree]], [[accept]], [[reveal]], [[grant]], [[declare]], [[acknowledge]], [[recognize]]
**3** to [[allow]] (someone) to enter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[let in]], [[allow]], [[receive]], [[accept]], [[introduce]], [[take in]], [[initiate]], [[give access to]]
**4** to take (someone) in to a hospital for treatment: he was admitted for tests
**5** **admit to** to [[allow]] someone to participate in something
**6** **admit of** to [[allow]] for: these rules admit of no violation
