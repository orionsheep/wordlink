**silver** *n*
**1** a precious greyish-white metallic element: used in jewellery, tableware, and coins. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**Ag**
**2** a coin or coins made of silver
**3** any household articles made of silver
 *adj*
**4** greyish-white: silver hair
**5** (of anniversaries) the 25th in a series: Silver Jubilee; silver wedding
 *vb*
**6** to coat with silver or a silvery substance: a company that silvers their own mirrors
**7** to cause (something) to become silvery in colour: the sun silvered the tarmac
