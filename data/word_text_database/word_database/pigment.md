**pigment** *n*
**1** any substance which gives [[colour]] to [[paint]] or [[dye]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[colour]], [[colouring]], [[paint]], [[stain]], [[dye]], [[tint]], [[tincture]]
**2** a substance which occurs in plant or animal tissue and produces a characteristic [[colour]]
 **[[pigmentary]]** *adj*
