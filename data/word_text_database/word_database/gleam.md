**gleam** *n*
**1** a small [[beam]] or [[glow]] of light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glimmer]], [[flash]], [[beam]], [[glow]], [[sparkle]]
**2** a brief or dim indication: a gleam of anticipation in his eye
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[suggestion]], [[hint]], [[flicker]], [[glimmer]], inkling
 *vb*
**3** to [[shine]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shine]], [[flash]], [[glow]], [[sparkle]], [[glitter]], [[shimmer]], [[glint]], [[glimmer]]
 **[[gleaming]]** *adj*
