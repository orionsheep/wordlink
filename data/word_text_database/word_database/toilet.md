**toilet** *n*
**1**
**A** a bowl fitted with a water-flushing device and connected to a drain, for receiving and disposing of urine and faeces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lavatory]], [[bathroom]], [[loo]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[privy]] (*obsolete*), [[cloakroom]] (*Brit*), [[urinal]], [[latrine]], [[washroom]], dunny (*Austral** & **NZ* *old-fashioned*, *informal*), [[bogger]] (*Austral* *slang*), brasco (*Austral* *slang*)
 **B** a room with such a fitment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bathroom]], gents *or* ladies (*Brit* *informal*), [[privy]], [[latrine]], [[water closet]], [[ladies' room]], W.C.
**2** (*old-fashioned*) the act of dressing and preparing oneself
