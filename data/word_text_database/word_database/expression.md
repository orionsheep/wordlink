**expression** *n*
**1** the transforming of ideas into words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[statement]], [[declaration]], [[announcement]], [[communication]], [[utterance]], [[articulation]]
**2** a [[showing]] of emotion without words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indication]], [[demonstration]], [[exhibition]], [[display]], [[showing]], [[show]], [[sign]], [[symbol]]
**3** [[communication]] of emotion through music, painting, etc.
**4** a [[look]] on the [[face]] that indicates mood or emotion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look]], [[countenance]] (*literary*), [[face]], [[air]], [[appearance]], [[aspect]]
**5** a particular [[phrase]] used conventionally to express something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[phrase]], [[saying]], [[word]], [[term]], [[remark]], [[maxim]], [[idiom]], [[adage]]
**6** (*maths*) a variable, function, or some combination of these
 **[[expressionless]]** *adj*
