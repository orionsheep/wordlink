**male** *adj*
**1** of the sex that can fertilize female reproductive cells
**2** of or characteristic of a man
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[masculine]], [[manly]], [[macho]], [[virile]]
**3** for or composed of men or boys: a male choir
**4** (of flowers) bearing stamens but lacking a pistil
**5** (*electronics*, *engineering*) having a projecting part or parts that fit into a hollow counterpart: a male plug
 *n*
**6** a male person, animal, or plant
 **[[maleness]]** *n*
