**lip** *n*
**1** (*anatomy*) either of the two fleshy folds surrounding the mouth
**2** any structure resembling a lip, such as the [[rim]] of a jug
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[rim]], [[brim]], [[margin]], [[brink]]
**3** (*slang*) impudent talk or [[backchat]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impudence]], [[insolence]], [[impertinence]], [[cheek]] (*informal*), [[effrontery]], [[backchat]] (*informal*), [[brass neck]] (*informal*)
**4** **bite one's lip** to avoid showing feelings of anger or distress
**5** **keep a stiff upper lip** to maintain one's composure during a time of trouble
**6** **lick *or* smack one's lips** to anticipate or recall something with glee or relish
