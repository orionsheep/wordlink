**entangle** *vb* **-gling**, **-gled**
**1** to catch very firmly in something, such as a net or wire: a fishing line had entangled his legs
**2** to involve in a complicated series of problems or difficulties: he entangles himself in contradictions
**3** to involve in a troublesome relationship: Harvey soon got entangled with a certain Miss Gilmour
 **[[entanglement]]** *n*
