**revolve** *vb* **-volving**, **-volved**
**1** to move or cause to move around a centre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rotate]], [[turn]], [[wheel]], [[spin]], [[twist]], [[whirl]]
**2** **revolve around** to be centred or focused upon: the campaign revolves around one man
**3** to occur periodically or in cycles
**4** to consider or be considered
 **[[revolvable]]** *adj*
