**chat** *n*
**1** an informal [[conversation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[talk]], [[tête-à-tête]], [[conversation]], [[gossip]], [[heart-to-heart]], [[natter]], [[blather]], [[blether]] (*Scot*), [[korero]] (*NZ*)
**2** the activity of exchanging short messages using a computer or mobile phone
 *vb* **chatting**, **chatted**
**3** to have an informal [[conversation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[talk]], [[gossip]], [[jaw]] (*slang*), [[natter]], [[blather]], [[blether]] (*Scot*)
**4** to exchange short messages using a computer or mobile phone
