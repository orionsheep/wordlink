**fidget** *vb* **-dgeting**, **-dgeted**
**1** to move about restlessly
**2** **fidget with** to make restless or uneasy movements with (something): he broke off, fidgeting with the papers, unable to meet their gaze
 *n*
**3** a person who fidgets
**4** **the fidgets** a state of restlessness: these youngsters are very highly strung and tend to get the fidgets
 **[[fidgety]]** *adj*
