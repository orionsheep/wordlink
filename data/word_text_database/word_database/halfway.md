**halfway** *adv*
**1** at or to half the distance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[midway]], to *or* in the [[middle]]
**2** at or towards the [[middle]] of a period of time or of an event or process
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[midway]], to *or* in the [[middle]]
**3** rather: halfway decent
**4** **meet someone halfway** to compromise with someone
 *adj*
**5** at the same distance from two points: the halfway line
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[midway]], [[middle]], [[mid]], [[central]], [[intermediate]], [[equidistant]]
