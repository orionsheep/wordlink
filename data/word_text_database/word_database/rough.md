**rough** *adj*
**1** not smooth; [[uneven]] or [[irregular]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneven]], [[broken]], [[rocky]], [[irregular]], [[jagged]], [[bumpy]], [[stony]], [[craggy]]
**2** not using enough care or gentleness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ungracious]], [[blunt]], [[rude]], [[coarse]], [[brusque]], [[uncouth]], [[impolite]], [[uncivil]]
**3** [[difficult]] or [[unpleasant]]: tomorrow will be a rough day
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unpleasant]], [[hard]], [[difficult]], [[tough]], [[uncomfortable]]
**4** [[approximate]]: a rough guess
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[approximate]], [[estimated]]
**5** violent or [[stormy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stormy]], [[wild]], [[turbulent]], [[choppy]], [[squally]]
**6** troubled by violence or crime: he lived in a rough area
**7** [[incomplete]] or [[basic]]: a rough [[draft]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[basic]], [[crude]], [[unfinished]], [[incomplete]], [[imperfect]], [[rudimentary]], [[sketchy]], [[unrefined]]
**8** lacking refinement: a rough shelter
**9** (of ground) covered with scrub or rubble
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneven]], [[broken]], [[rocky]], [[irregular]], [[jagged]], [[bumpy]], [[stony]], [[craggy]]
**10** [[harsh]] or grating to the ear
**11** [[harsh]] or sharp: the rough interrogation of my father
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harsh]], [[tough]], [[nasty]], [[cruel]], [[unfeeling]]
**12** unfair: rough luck
**13** (*informal*) ill: if you're feeling rough, you won't want to eat 
**14** shaggy or hairy: the rough wool of her sweater
**15** (of work, etc.) requiring physical rather than mental effort: wear gloves for any rough work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boisterous]], [[hard]], [[tough]], arduous
 *vb*
**16** to make rough
**17** **rough it** (*informal*) to live without the usual comforts of life
 *n*
**18** rough ground
**19** a sketch or preliminary piece of artwork
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[draft]], [[mock-up]], [[preliminary sketch]]
**20** (*informal*) a violent person
**21** **in rough** in an [[unfinished]] or [[crude]] state
**22** **the rough** (*golf*) the part of the course beside the fairways where the grass is untrimmed
**23** the [[unpleasant]] side of something: you have to take the rough with the smooth
 *adv*
**24** [[roughly]]
**25** **sleep rough** to spend the night in the open without shelter
 See also **rough out**
