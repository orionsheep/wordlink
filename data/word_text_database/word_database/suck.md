**suck** *vb*
**1** to [[draw]] (a liquid) into the mouth through pursed lips
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drink]], [[sip]], [[draw]]
**2** to [[take]] (something) into the mouth and moisten, dissolve, or roll it around with the tongue: suck a mint
**3** to [[extract]] liquid from (a solid food): he sat sucking orange segments
**4** to [[draw]] in (fluid) as if by sucking: the mussel sucks in water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take]], [[draw]], [[pull]], [[extract]]
**5** to [[drink]] milk from (a mother's breast); suckle
**6** (often foll. by *down* or *in, etc.*) to [[draw]] (a thing or person somewhere) with a powerful force
**7** (*slang*) to be contemptible or disgusting
