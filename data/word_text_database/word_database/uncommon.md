**uncommon** *adj*
**1** not happening or encountered often
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rare]], [[unusual]], [[odd]], [[novel]], [[strange]], [[peculiar]], [[scarce]], [[queer]] (*old-fashioned*)
**2** in excess of what is normal: an uncommon amount of powder
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extraordinary]], [[remarkable]], [[special]], [[outstanding]], [[distinctive]], [[exceptional]], [[notable]]
