**tax** *n*
**1** a compulsory payment to a government to raise revenue, levied on income, property, or goods and services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]], [[duty]], [[toll]], [[levy]], [[tariff]], [[excise]], tithe
 *vb*
**2** to [[levy]] a tax on (people, companies, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]] (*formal*), [[rate]], [[assess]]
**3** to make heavy demands on: the task taxed his ingenuity and patience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strain]], [[stretch]], [[try]], [[test]], [[load]], [[burden]], [[exhaust]], [[weaken]]
**4** **tax someone with** to accuse someone of: he was taxed with parochialism and meanness
 **[[taxable]]** *adj* 
 **[[taxing]]** *adj*
