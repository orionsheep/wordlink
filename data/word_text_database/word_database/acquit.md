**acquit** *vb* **-quitting**, **-quitted**
**1** to pronounce someone not guilty: he's been acquitted of negligence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clear]], [[free]], [[release]], [[excuse]], [[discharge]], [[liberate]], [[vindicate]]
**2** to behave in a particular way: she acquitted herself well in the meeting
 **[[acquittal]]** *n*
