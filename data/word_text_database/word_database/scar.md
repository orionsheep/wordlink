**scar** *n*
**1** a [[mark]] left on the skin following the healing of a [[wound]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[injury]], [[wound]], [[blemish]]
**2** a permanent effect on a person's character resulting from emotional distress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trauma]], [[suffering]], [[pain]], [[torture]], [[anguish]]
**3** a [[mark]] on a plant where a leaf was formerly attached
**4** a [[mark]] of [[damage]]
 *vb* **scarring**, **scarred**
**5** to [[mark]] or become marked with a scar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[disfigure]], [[damage]], [[mar]], [[mutilate]], [[blemish]], [[deface]]
**6** to permanently effect or be permanently affected by mental [[trauma]]: the disaster will scar the victims for life
