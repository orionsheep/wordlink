**acquaintance** *n*
**1** a person whom one knows slightly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[associate]], [[contact]], [[ally]], [[colleague]], [[comrade]]
**2** slight knowledge of a person or subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relationship]], [[connection]], [[fellowship]], [[familiarity]]
**3** **make the acquaintance of** to come into social [[contact]] with
**4** the people one knows: an actress of my acquaintance
