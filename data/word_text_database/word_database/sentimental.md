**sentimental** *adj*
**1** feeling or expressing tenderness, romance, or sadness to an exaggerated extent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[romantic]], [[touching]], [[emotional]], [[nostalgic]], [[maudlin]], [[weepy]] (*informal*), [[slushy]] (*informal*), [[schmaltzy]] (*slang*)
**2** appealing to the emotions, esp. to [[romantic]] feelings: she kept the ring for sentimental reasons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[romantic]], [[touching]], [[emotional]], [[nostalgic]], [[maudlin]], [[weepy]] (*informal*), [[slushy]] (*informal*), [[schmaltzy]] (*slang*)
