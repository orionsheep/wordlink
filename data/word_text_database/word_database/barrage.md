**barrage** (**bar**-rahzh) *n*
**1** a continuous delivery of questions, complaints, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[torrent]], [[mass]], [[burst]], [[stream]], [[hail]], [[spate]], [[onslaught]], [[deluge]]
**2** (*military*) the continuous firing of artillery over a wide area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bombardment]], [[attack]], [[bombing]], [[assault]], [[shelling]], [[battery]], [[volley]], [[blitz]]
**3** a construction built across a river to control the water level
