**blossom** *n*
**1** the [[flower]] or flowers of a plant, esp. producing edible fruit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flower]], [[bloom]], [[bud]], [[efflorescence]], [[floret]]
**2** the period of flowering
 *vb*
**3** (of plants) to [[flower]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flower]], [[bloom]], [[bud]]
**4** to come to a promising stage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[succeed]], [[progress]], [[thrive]], [[flourish]], [[prosper]]
