**[[pull up]]** *vb*
**1** (of a motor vehicle) to stop
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pull up]]**: stop, halt, brake
**2** to remove by the roots
**3** to rebuke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pull someone up]]**: reprimand, rebuke, admonish, read the riot act to, tell someone off (*informal*), reprove, bawl someone out (*informal*), tear someone off a strip (*Brit* *informal*)
