**exile** *n*
**1** a prolonged, usually enforced absence from one's country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[banishment]], [[expulsion]], [[deportation]], [[eviction]], [[expatriation]]
**2** a person banished or living away from his or her country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expatriate]], [[refugee]], [[outcast]], [[émigré]], deportee
 *vb* **-iling**, **-iled**
**3** to [[expel]] (someone) from his or her country; [[banish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[banish]], [[expel]], [[throw out]], [[deport]], [[drive out]], [[eject]], [[expatriate]], [[cast out]]
