**depot** (**dep**-oh) *n*
**1** a place where goods and vehicles are kept when not in use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arsenal]], [[warehouse]], [[storehouse]], [[repository]], [[depository]]
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **NZ*) a bus or railway [[station]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bus station]], [[station]], [[garage]], [[terminus]]
