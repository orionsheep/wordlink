**detachment** *n*
**1** the state of not being personally involved in something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indifference]], [[fairness]], [[neutrality]], [[objectivity]], [[impartiality]], [[coolness]], [[remoteness]], [[nonchalance]]
**2** (*military*) a small group of soldiers separated from the main group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unit]], [[party]], [[force]], [[body]], [[squad]], [[patrol]], [[task force]]
