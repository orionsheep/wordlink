**divert** *vb*
**1** to change the course or direction of (traffic)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[redirect]], [[switch]], [[avert]], [[deflect]], [[deviate]], [[turn aside]]
**2** to [[distract]] the attention of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distract]], [[sidetrack]], [[lead astray]], draw *or* lead away from
**3** to [[entertain]] or [[amuse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entertain]], [[delight]], [[amuse]], [[please]], [[charm]], [[gratify]], [[beguile]], [[regale]]
