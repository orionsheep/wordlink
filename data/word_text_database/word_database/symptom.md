**symptom** *n*
**1** (*med*) a [[sign]] indicating the presence of an illness or disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sign]], [[mark]], [[indication]], [[warning]]
**2** anything that is taken as an [[indication]] that something is wrong: a growing symptom of grave social injustice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manifestation]], [[sign]], [[indication]], [[mark]], [[evidence]], [[expression]], [[proof]], [[token]]
 **[[symptomatic]]** *adj*
