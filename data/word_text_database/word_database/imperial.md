**imperial** *adj*
**1** of an empire, emperor, or empress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[royal]], [[regal]], [[kingly]], [[queenly]], [[princely]], [[sovereign]], [[majestic]], [[monarchial]]
**2** [[majestic]]; commanding
**3** exercising supreme authority; imperious
**4** (of weights or measures) conforming to the standards of a system formerly official in Great Britain
 *n*
**5** a wine bottle holding the equivalent of eight normal bottles
