**instant** *n*
**1** a very brief [[time]]; [[moment]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moment]], [[second]], [[flash]], [[split second]], [[jiffy]] (*informal*), [[trice]], [[twinkling of an eye]] (*informal*)
**2** a particular [[moment]]: at the same instant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[time]], [[point]], [[hour]], [[moment]], [[stage]], [[occasion]], [[phase]], juncture
 *adj*
**3** [[immediate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immediate]], [[prompt]], [[instantaneous]], [[direct]], [[quick]], [[on-the-spot]], [[split-second]]
**4** (of foods) able to be prepared very quickly and easily: instant coffee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ready-made]], [[fast]], [[convenience]], [[ready-mixed]], [[ready-cooked]], [[precooked]]
**5** urgent or pressing
**6** of the present month: a letter of the 7th instant
