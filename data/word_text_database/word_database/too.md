**too** *adv*
**1** [[as well]] or [[also]]: I'll miss you, too
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[also]], [[as well]], [[further]], [[in addition]], [[moreover]], [[besides]], [[likewise]], [[to boot]]
**2** in or to an excessive degree: it's too noisy in here
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excessively]], [[very]], [[extremely]], [[overly]], [[unduly]], [[unreasonably]], [[inordinately]], [[immoderately]]
**3** [[extremely]]: you're too kind
**4** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral* *informal*) used to emphasize contradiction of a negative statement: You didn't! – I did too!
