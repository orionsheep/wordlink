**scholarship** *n*
**1** academic achievement; [[learning]] gained by serious study
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[learning]], [[education]], [[knowledge]], [[erudition]], [[book-learning]]
**2** financial aid provided for a scholar because of academic merit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grant]], [[award]], [[payment]], [[endowment]], [[fellowship]], [[bursary]]
