**absorption** *n*
**1** the process of absorbing something or the state of being absorbed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immersion]], [[involvement]], [[concentration]], [[fascination]], [[preoccupation]], [[intentness]]
**2** (*physiol*) the process by which nutrients enter the tissues of an animal or a plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soaking up]], [[consumption]], [[digestion]], [[sucking up]]
 **[[absorptive]]** *adj*
