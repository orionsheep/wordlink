**paw** *n*
**1** a four-legged mammal's foot with claws and pads
**2** (*informal*) a hand
 *vb*
**3** to scrape or hit with the paws
**4** (*informal*) to touch or caress (someone) in a rough or overfamiliar manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manhandle]], [[grab]], [[maul]], [[molest]], [[handle roughly]]
