**priest** * or * (*fem*) **priestess** *n*
**1** (in the Christian Church) a person ordained to administer the sacraments and preach
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: clergyman *or* woman, [[minister]], [[father]], [[divine]], [[vicar]], [[pastor]], [[cleric]], [[curate]]
**2** a [[minister]] of any religion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: clergyman *or* woman, [[minister]], [[father]], [[divine]], [[vicar]], [[pastor]], [[cleric]], [[curate]]
**3** an official who performs religious ceremonies
