**identity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state of being a specified person or thing: the identity of his murderers was not immediately established
**2** the individual characteristics by which a person or thing is recognized
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[individuality]], [[self]], [[character]], [[personality]], [[existence]], [[originality]], [[separateness]]
**3** the state of being the same
**4** (*maths*) Also called: **identity element** a member of a set that when combined with any other member of the set, leaves it unchanged: the identity for multiplication of numbers is 1
