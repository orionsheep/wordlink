**compensate** *vb* **-sating**, **-sated**
**1** to make amends to (someone), esp. for loss or injury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recompense]], [[repay]], [[refund]], [[reimburse]], [[remunerate]] (*formal*), [[make good]]
**2** to [[cancel out]] the effects of (something): the car's nifty handling fails to compensate for its many flaws
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[balance]], [[cancel]] (out), [[offset]], [[make up for]], [[redress]], [[counteract]], [[counterbalance]]
**3** to serve as compensation for (injury or loss)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make amends for]], [[make up for]], [[atone for]], [[pay for]], [[do penance for]], [[cancel out]], [[make reparation for]]
 **[[compensatory]]** *adj*
