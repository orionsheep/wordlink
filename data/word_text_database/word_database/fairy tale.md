**fairy tale** * or ***fairy story** *n*
**1** a story about fairies or magical events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[folk tale]], [[romance]], [[traditional story]]
**2** a highly improbable account: his report was little more than a fairy tale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lie]], [[fiction]], [[invention]], [[fabrication]], [[untruth]], [[urban myth]], [[tall story]], urban legend
 *adj* **fairy-tale**
**3** of or like a fairy tale: a fairy-tale wedding
**4** highly improbable: a fairy-tale account of his achievements
