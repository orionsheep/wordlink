**addict** *n*
**1** a person who is unable to stop taking narcotic drugs
**2** (*informal*) a person who is devoted to something: he's a telly addict
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fan]], [[lover]], [[nut]] (*slang*), [[follower]], [[enthusiast]], [[admirer]], [[buff]] (*informal*), [[junkie]] (*informal*) 
 **[[addictive]]** *adj*
