**extension** *n*
**1** a room or rooms added to an existing building
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annexe]], [[addition]], [[supplement]], [[appendix]], [[appendage]]
**2** a [[development]] that includes or affects more people or things than before: an extension of democracy within the EU
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[development]], [[expansion]], [[widening]], [[increase]], [[broadening]], [[enlargement]], [[diversification]]
**3** an additional telephone connected to the same line as another
**4** an extra period of time in which something continues to exist or be valid: an extension of the contract for another 2 years
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lengthening]], [[extra time]], [[continuation]], additional period of time
 *adj*
**5** denoting something that can be extended or that extends another object: an extension ladder
**6** of or relating to the provision of teaching and other facilities by a school or college to people who cannot attend full-time courses
