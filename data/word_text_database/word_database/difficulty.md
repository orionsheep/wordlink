**difficulty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state or quality of being difficult
**2** a task or [[problem]] that is hard to deal with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[trouble]], [[obstacle]], [[hurdle]], [[dilemma]], [[complication]], [[snag]], [[uphill]] (*S African*)
**3** a troublesome or embarrassing situation: in financial difficulties
**4** an objection or [[obstacle]]: you're just making difficulties
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[trouble]], [[obstacle]], [[hurdle]], [[dilemma]], [[complication]], [[snag]], [[uphill]] (*S African*)
**5** lack of ease; [[awkwardness]]: she could run only with difficulty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hardship]], [[strain]], [[awkwardness]], [[strenuousness]], [[arduousness]], [[laboriousness]]
