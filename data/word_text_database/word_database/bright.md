**bright** *adj*
**1** emitting or reflecting much light; [[shining]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shining]], [[glowing]], [[dazzling]], [[gleaming]], [[shimmering]], [[radiant]], [[luminous]], [[lustrous]]
**2** (of colours) intense or [[vivid]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vivid]], [[rich]], [[brilliant]], [[glowing]], [[colourful]]
**3** full of promise: a bright future
**4** lively or cheerful
**5** quick-witted or [[clever]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clever]], [[smart]], ingenious
 *adv*
**6** [[brightly]]: the light burned bright in his office
 **[[brightly]]** *adv* 
 **[[brightness]]** *n*
