**tide** *n*
**1** the alternate rise and fall of sea level caused by the gravitational pull of the sun and moon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[current]], [[flow]], [[stream]], [[ebb]], [[undertow]], [[tideway]]
**2** the [[current]] caused by these changes in level: I got caught by the tide and almost drowned
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[current]], [[flow]], [[stream]], [[ebb]], [[undertow]], [[tideway]]
**3** a widespread [[tendency]] or [[movement]]: the rising tide of nationalism
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[course]], [[direction]], [[trend]], [[movement]], [[tendency]], [[drift]]
**4** (*literary*, *or* *old-fashioned*) a season or time: Yuletide
