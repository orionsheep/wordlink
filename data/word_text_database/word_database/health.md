**health** *n*
**1** the general [[condition]] of body and mind: better health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condition]], [[state]], [[shape]], [[constitution]], [[fettle]]
**2** the [[state]] of being bodily and mentally vigorous and free from disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wellbeing]], [[strength]], [[fitness]], [[vigour]], [[good condition]], [[soundness]], [[robustness]], [[healthiness]]
**3** the [[condition]] of an organization, society, etc.: the economic health of the republics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[state]], [[condition]], [[shape]]
