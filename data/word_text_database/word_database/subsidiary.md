**subsidiary** *n*, *pl* **-aries**
**1** Also called: **subsidiary company** a company which is at least half owned by another company
**2** a person or thing that is of [[lesser]] importance
 *adj*
**3** of [[lesser]] importance; [[subordinate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secondary]], [[lesser]], [[subordinate]], [[minor]], [[supplementary]], [[auxiliary]], [[ancillary]]
