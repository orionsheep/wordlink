**discretion** (diss-**kresh**-on) *n*
**1** the quality of behaving so as to avoid social embarrassment or distress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tact]], [[consideration]], [[caution]], [[diplomacy]], [[prudence]], [[wariness]], [[carefulness]], [[judiciousness]]
**2** freedom or authority to make judgments and to act as one sees fit: at his discretion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[choice]], [[will]], [[pleasure]], [[preference]], [[inclination]], [[volition]]
 **[[discretionary]]** *adj*
