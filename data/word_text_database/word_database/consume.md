**consume** *vb* **-suming**, **-sumed**
**1** to [[eat]] or drink
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eat]], [[swallow]], [[devour]], [[put away]], [[gobble]] (up), [[eat up]]
**2** to [[use up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[use up]], [[spend]], [[waste]], [[absorb]], [[exhaust]], [[squander]], [[dissipate]], [[expend]]
**3** to [[destroy]]: the ship blew up and was consumed by flames
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destroy]], [[devastate]], [[demolish]] (*facetious*), [[ravage]], [[annihilate]], [[lay waste]]
**4** to [[obsess]]: he was consumed with jealousy over the ending of their affair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obsess]], [[dominate]], [[absorb]], [[preoccupy]], [[eat up]], [[monopolize]], [[engross]]
