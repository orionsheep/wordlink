◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[last]], [[latest]], [[closing]], [[finishing]], [[concluding]], [[ultimate]], [[terminal]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[irrevocable]], [[absolute]], [[definitive]], [[decided]], [[settled]], [[definite]], [[conclusive]], irrefutable
 *n*
**3** a deciding contest between the winners of previous rounds in a competition
