**revolt** *n*
**1** a [[rebellion]] or [[uprising]] against authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uprising]], [[rising]], [[revolution]], [[rebellion]], [[mutiny]], [[insurrection]], [[insurgency]]
**2** **in revolt** in the state of rebelling
 *vb*
**3** to [[rise up]] in [[rebellion]] against authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rebel]], [[rise up]], [[resist]], [[mutiny]]
**4** to cause to feel [[disgust]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disgust]], [[sicken]], [[repel]], [[repulse]], [[nauseate]], [[gross out]] (*slang*), [[turn your stomach]], [[make your flesh creep]]
