**humble** *adj*
**1** conscious of one's failings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[modest]], [[meek]], [[unassuming]], [[unpretentious]], [[self-effacing]], [[unostentatious]]
**2** [[modest]] and [[unpretentious]]: humble domestic objects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lowly]], [[poor]], [[mean]], [[simple]], [[ordinary]], [[modest]], [[obscure]], [[undistinguished]]
**3** [[ordinary]] or not very important: humble beginnings
 *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**4** to cause to become humble; [[humiliate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humiliate]], [[disgrace]], [[crush]], [[subdue]], [[chasten]], put (someone) in their place, [[take down a peg]] (*informal*) 
 **[[humbly]]** *adv*
