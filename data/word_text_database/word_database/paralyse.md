**paralyse** * or * (*US*) **-lyze** *vb* **-lysing**, **-lysed*** or ***-lyzing**, **-lyzed**
**1** (*pathol*) to affect with paralysis
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disable]], [[cripple]], [[lame]], [[incapacitate]]
**2** to make immobile: he was paralysed by fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[freeze]], [[stun]], [[numb]], [[petrify]], [[halt]], [[immobilize]]
