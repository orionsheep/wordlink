**magnitude** *n*
**1** relative [[importance]]: an evil of the first magnitude
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[importance]], [[consequence]], [[significance]], [[moment]], [[note]], [[weight]], [[greatness]]
**2** relative [[size]] or [[extent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immensity]], [[size]], [[extent]], [[enormity]] (*informal*), [[volume]], [[vastness]]
**3** (*astron*) the apparent brightness of a celestial body expressed on a numerical scale on which bright stars have a low value
