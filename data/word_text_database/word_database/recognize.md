**recognize** * or ***-nise** *vb* **-nizing**, **-nized*** or ***-nising**, **-nised**
**1** to [[identify]] (a person or thing) as someone or something already known
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[identify]], [[know]], [[place]], [[remember]], [[spot]], [[notice]], [[recall]], [[recollect]]
**2** to [[accept]] or be aware of (a fact or problem): to recognize change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acknowledge]], [[allow]], [[accept]], [[admit]], [[grant]], [[concede]]
**3** to [[acknowledge]] formally the status or legality of (something or someone): an organization recognized by the UN
**4** to show approval or appreciation of (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appreciate]], [[respect]], [[notice]]
**5** to make formal acknowledgment of (a claim or duty): I must ask for her to be recognized as a hostile witness
 **[[recognizable]]** * or ***-nisable** *adj*
