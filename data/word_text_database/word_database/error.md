**error** *n*
**1** a [[mistake]], inaccuracy, or misjudgment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mistake]], [[slip]], [[blunder]], [[oversight]], [[howler]] (*informal*), [[bloomer]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[miscalculation]], [[solecism]] (*formal*), barry *or* Barry Crocker (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** the act or state of being wrong or making a misjudgment: the plane was shot down in error
**3** the amount by which the actual value of a quantity might differ from an estimate: a 3% margin of error
