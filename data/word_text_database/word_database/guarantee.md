**guarantee** *n*
**1** a formal assurance in writing that a product or service will meet certain standards or specifications
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[warranty]], [[contract]], [[bond]]
**2** something that makes a specified condition or outcome certain: there was no guarantee that there would not be another military coup
 *vb* **-teeing**, **-teed**
**3** to [[promise]] or make certain: to guarantee absolute loyalty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[promise]], [[pledge]], [[undertake]]
**4** (of a company) to provide a guarantee in writing for (a product or service)
**5** to take responsibility for the debts or obligations of (another person)
