**beacon** *n*
**1** a [[signal]] fire or light on a hill or tower, used formerly as a warning of invasion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[signal]], [[sign]], [[beam]], [[flare]], [[bonfire]]
**2** a [[lighthouse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lighthouse]], [[watchtower]]
**3** a radio or other [[signal]] marking a flight course in air navigation
