**stormy** *adj* **stormier**, **stormiest**
**1** (of weather) violent with dark skies, heavy rain or snow, and strong winds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wild]], [[rough]], [[raging]], [[turbulent]], [[windy]], [[blustery]], [[inclement]], [[squally]]
**2** involving violent emotions: a stormy affair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[angry]], [[heated]], [[fierce]], [[passionate]], [[fiery]], [[impassioned]]
