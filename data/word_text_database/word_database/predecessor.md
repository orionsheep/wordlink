**predecessor** *n*
**1** a person who precedes another in an office or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[previous job holder]], [[precursor]], [[forerunner]], [[antecedent]]
**2** an [[ancestor]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ancestor]], [[forebear]], [[antecedent]], [[forefather]], tupuna *or* tipuna (*NZ*)
**3** something that precedes something else: the library will be more extravagant than its predecessors
