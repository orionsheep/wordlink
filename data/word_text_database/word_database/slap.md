**slap** *n*
**1** a sharp [[blow]] or [[smack]] with something flat, such as the open hand
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smack]], [[blow]], [[cuff]], [[swipe]] (*informal*), [[spank]]
**2** the sound made by or as if by such a [[blow]]
**3** **slap and tickle** (*Brit* *old-fashioned*, *informal*) sexual play
**4** **a slap in the face** an unexpected rejection or insult
**5** **a slap on the back** congratulations
 *vb* **slapping**, **slapped**
**6** to strike sharply with something flat, such as the open hand
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smack]], [[beat]], [[clap]], [[cuff]], [[swipe]] (*informal*), [[spank]], [[clobber]] (*slang*), [[wallop]] (*informal*)
**7** to bring (something) down forcefully: he slapped down a fiver
**8** (usually foll. by *against*) to strike (something) with a slapping sound
**9** (*informal*) to cover with quickly or carelessly: she slapped on some make-up
**10** **slap on the back** to congratulate
 *adv* (*informal*)
**11** exactly: slap in the middle
**12** **slap into** forcibly or abruptly into: he ran slap into the guard
