**neuter** *adj*
**1** (*grammar*) denoting a gender of nouns which are neither male nor female
**2** (of animals and plants) sexually underdeveloped
 *n*
**3** (*grammar*)
**A** the neuter gender
 **B** a neuter noun
**4** a sexually underdeveloped female insect, such as a worker bee
**5** a castrated animal
 *vb*
**6** to castrate (an animal)
