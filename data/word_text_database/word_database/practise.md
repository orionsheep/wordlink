**practise** * or * (*US*) **practice** *vb* **-tising**, **-tised*** or ***-ticing**, **-ticed**
**1** to [[do]] repeatedly in order to gain skill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rehearse]], [[study]], [[prepare]], [[perfect]], [[repeat]], [[go through]], [[go over]], [[refine]]
**2** to take part in or [[follow]] (a religion, etc.): none of them practise Islam
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry out]], [[follow]], [[apply]], [[perform]], [[observe]], [[engage in]]
**3** to [[work at]] (a profession): he originally intended to practise medicine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[work at]], [[pursue]], [[carry on]]
**4** to [[do]] regularly: they practise meditation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[do]], [[train]], [[exercise]], [[drill]]
