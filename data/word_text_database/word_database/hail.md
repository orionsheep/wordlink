**hail**^2 *vb*
**1** to call out to; [[greet]]: a voice from behind hailed him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[salute]], [[greet]], [[address]], [[welcome]], [[say hello to]], [[halloo]]
**2** to praise, [[acclaim]], or [[acknowledge]]: his crew had been hailed as heroes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acclaim]], [[honour]], [[acknowledge]], [[cheer]], [[applaud]]
**3** to stop (a taxi) by shouting or gesturing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flag down]], [[summon]], [[signal to]], [[wave down]]
**4** **hail from** to come originally from: she hails from Nova Scotia
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[hail from somewhere]]**: come from, be born in, originate in, be a native of, have your roots in
**5** **within hailing distance** within hearing range
 *interj*
**6** (*poetic*) an exclamation of greeting
