**fable** *n*
**1** a short [[story]], often one with animals as characters, that illustrates a moral
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legend]], [[myth]], [[parable]], [[allegory]], [[story]], [[tale]]
**2** an unlikely [[story]] which is usually untrue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fiction]], [[fantasy]], [[myth]], [[invention]], [[yarn]] (*informal*), [[fabrication]], [[urban myth]], [[tall story]] (*informal*)
**3** a [[story]] about mythical characters or events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legend]], [[myth]], [[parable]], [[allegory]], [[story]], [[tale]]
