**knowledge** *n*
**1** the facts or experiences known by a person or group of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[learning]], [[education]], [[intelligence]], [[instruction]], [[wisdom]], [[scholarship]], [[enlightenment]], [[erudition]]
**2** the state of knowing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[understanding]], [[sense]], [[judgment]], [[perception]], [[awareness]], [[insight]], [[grasp]], [[appreciation]]
**3** specific information about a subject
**4** **to my knowledge** as I understand it
