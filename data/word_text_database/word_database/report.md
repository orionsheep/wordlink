**report** *vb*
**1** to give an [[account]] (of)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inform of]], [[communicate]], [[recount]]
**2** to give an [[account]] of the results of an investigation (into): the commission is to report on global warming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communicate]], [[tell]], [[state]], [[detail]], [[describe]], [[relate]], [[broadcast]], [[post]], [[tweet]], [[pass on]]
**3** to make a formal report on (a subject)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communicate]], [[tell]], [[state]], [[detail]], [[describe]], [[relate]], [[broadcast]], [[post]], [[tweet]], [[pass on]]
**4** to make a formal complaint about
**5** to present (oneself) at an appointed place or for a specific purpose: report to the manager's office
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[present yourself]], [[come]], [[appear]], [[arrive]], [[turn up]]
**6** **report to** to be responsible to and under the authority of
**7** to act as a reporter
 *n*
**8** an [[account]] prepared after investigation and published or [[broadcast]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[article]], [[story]], [[piece]], [[write-up]]
**9** an [[account]] of the discussions of a committee or other group of people: I have the report of the mining union
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[account]], [[record]], [[statement]], [[communication]], [[description]], [[narrative]]
**10** a [[story]] for which there is no absolute proof: according to report, he is not dead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rumour]], [[talk]], [[buzz]], [[gossip]], [[goss]] (*informal*), [[hearsay]]
**11** a [[statement]] on the progress of a schoolchild
**12** a loud [[bang]] made by a gun or [[explosion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bang]], [[sound]], [[crack]], [[noise]], [[blast]], [[boom]], [[explosion]], [[discharge]]
**13** comment on a person's character or actions: he is of good report here
 **[[reportedly]]** *adv*
