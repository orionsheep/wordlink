**distant** *adj*
**1** [[far-off]]; [[remote]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[far-off]], [[far]], [[remote]], [[abroad]], [[out-of-the-way]], [[far-flung]], [[faraway]], [[outlying]]
**2** [[far]] apart
**3** separated by a specified distance: five kilometres distant
**4** apart in relationship: a distant cousin
**5** going to a [[faraway]] place
**6** [[remote]] in manner; [[aloof]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reserved]], [[withdrawn]], [[cool]], [[remote]], [[detached]], [[aloof]], [[unfriendly]], [[reticent]]
**7** abstracted: a distant look entered her eyes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faraway]], [[blank]], [[vague]], [[distracted]], [[vacant]], [[preoccupied]], [[oblivious]], [[absent-minded]]
 **[[distantly]]** *adv*
