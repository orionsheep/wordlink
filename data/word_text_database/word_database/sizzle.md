**sizzle** *vb* **-zling**, **-zled**
**1** to make a hissing sound like the sound of frying fat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hiss]], [[spit]], [[crackle]], [[fry]], [[frizzle]]
**2** (*informal*) to be very hot: the city was [[sizzling]] in a hot summer spell
**3** (*informal*) to be very angry
 *n*
**4** a hissing sound
 **[[sizzling]]** *adj*
