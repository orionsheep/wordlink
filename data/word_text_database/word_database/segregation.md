**segregation** *n*
**1** the practice or policy of creating separate facilities within the same society for the use of a racial or minority group
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separation]], [[discrimination]], [[apartheid]], [[isolation]]
**2** the act of segregating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separation]], [[discrimination]], [[apartheid]], [[isolation]]
 **[[segregational]]** *adj* 
 **[[segregationist]]** *n*
