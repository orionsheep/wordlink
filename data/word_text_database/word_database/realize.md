**realize** * or ***-lise** *vb* **-lizing**, **-lized*** or ***-lising**, **-lised**
**1** to be aware of or [[grasp]] the significance of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[become aware of]], [[understand]], [[take in]], [[grasp]], [[comprehend]], [[get the message]]
**2** to [[achieve]] (a plan or ambition)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fulfil]], [[achieve]], [[accomplish]], [[make real]]
**3** to convert (property or goods) into cash
**4** (of goods or property) to sell for (a certain sum): this table realized a large sum at auction
**5** to produce (a [[complete]] work of art) from an idea or draft
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[achieve]], [[do]], [[effect]], [[complete]], [[perform]], [[fulfil]], [[accomplish]], carry out *or* through
 **[[realizable]]** * or ***-lisable** *adj* 
 **[[realization]]** * or ***-lisation** *n*
