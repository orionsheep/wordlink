**[[play up]]** *vb*
**1** to highlight: the temptation is to [[play up]] the sensational aspects of the story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[play something up]]**: emphasize, highlight, underline, stress, accentuate
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral* *informal*) to behave in an unruly way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[play up]]**: be awkward, misbehave, give trouble, be disobedient, be stroppy (*Brit* *slang*)
**3** to give (one) trouble or not be working properly: my back's playing me up again; the photocopier's started to [[play up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[play up]]**: hurt, be painful, bother you, trouble you, be sore, pain you
**4** **[[play up]] to** to try to please by flattery
