**instalment** * or * (*US*) **installment** *n*
**1** one of the portions into which a debt is divided for [[payment]] at regular intervals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[payment]], [[repayment]], [[part payment]]
**2** a [[portion]] of something that is issued, broadcast, or published in parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[section]], [[chapter]], [[episode]], [[portion]], [[division]]
