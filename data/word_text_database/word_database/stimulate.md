**stimulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to [[encourage]] to start or progress further: a cut in interest rates should help stimulate economic recovery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[encourage]], [[inspire]], [[prompt]], [[fire]], [[spur]], [[provoke]], [[arouse]], [[rouse]]
**2** to fill (a person) with ideas or enthusiasm: books satisfy a part of the intellect that needs to be stimulated
**3** (*physiol*) to excite (a nerve or organ) with a stimulus
 **[[stimulation]]** *n*
