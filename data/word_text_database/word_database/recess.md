**recess** *n*
**1** a space, such as an [[alcove]], set back in a wall
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alcove]], [[corner]], [[bay]], [[hollow]], [[niche]], [[nook]]
**2** a [[holiday]] between sessions of work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[rest]], [[holiday]], [[interval]], [[vacation]], [[respite]], [[intermission]], [[schoolie]] (*Austral*)
**3** **recesses** secret hidden places: the recesses of her brain
**4** (*US** & **Canad*) a [[break]] between classes at a school
