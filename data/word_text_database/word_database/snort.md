**snort** *vb*
**1** to exhale air noisily through the nostrils
**2** to express contempt or annoyance by snorting
**3** to say with a snort
**4** (*slang*) to inhale (a powdered drug) through the nostrils
 *n*
**5** a loud exhalation of air through the nostrils to express contempt or annoyance: Clare gave a snort of disgust
