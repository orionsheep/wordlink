**fury** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** violent [[anger]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anger]], [[passion]], [[rage]], [[madness]] (*informal*), [[frenzy]], [[wrath]], [[impetuosity]]
**2** uncontrolled [[violence]]: the fury of the sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[violence]], [[force]], [[intensity]], [[severity]], [[ferocity]], [[savagery]], [[vehemence]], [[fierceness]]
**3** an outburst of violent [[anger]]
**4** a person with a violent temper
**5** **like fury** (*old-fashioned*) with great energy, strength, or power
