◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[animal]], [[creature]], [[brute]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brute]], [[monster]], [[savage]], [[barbarian]], [[fiend]], [[swine]], [[ogre]], [[sadist]]
**3** [[savage]] nature or characteristics: the beast in people
