**prolific** *adj*
**1** producing a constant [[creative]] output: a prolific author
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[productive]], [[creative]], [[fertile]], [[inventive]], [[copious]]
**2** producing fruit or offspring in abundance
**3** (often foll. by *in* or *of*) rich or [[fruitful]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fruitful]], [[fertile]], [[abundant]], [[luxuriant]], [[profuse]], [[fecund]]
 **[[prolifically]]** *adv*
