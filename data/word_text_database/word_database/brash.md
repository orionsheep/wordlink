**brash** *adj*
**1** tastelessly or offensively loud, or showy: brash modernization
**2** [[impudent]] or [[bold]]: I thought it was very brash of her to ask me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bold]], [[rude]], [[cocky]], [[pushy]] (*informal*), [[brazen]], [[impertinent]], [[insolent]], [[impudent]]
 **[[brashness]]** *n*
