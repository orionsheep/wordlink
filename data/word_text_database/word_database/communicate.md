**communicate** *vb* **-cating**, **-cated**
**1** to exchange (thoughts) or make known (information or feelings) by speech, writing, or other means
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contact]], [[talk]], [[speak]], [[make contact]], [[get in contact]], email *or* e-mail, [[text]], [[message]]
**2** (usually foll. by *to*) to [[transmit]] (to): the reaction of the rapturous audience communicated itself to the performers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pass on]], [[transfer]], [[spread]], [[transmit]]
**3** to have a sympathetic mutual understanding
**4** (*Christianity*) to receive Communion
 **[[communicator]]** *n* 
 **[[communicative]]** *adj*
