**embarrass** *vb*
**1** to make (someone) feel shy, ashamed, or guilty about something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shame]], [[distress]], [[show up]] (*informal*), [[humiliate]], [[disconcert]], [[fluster]], [[mortify]], [[discomfit]]
**2** to cause political problems for (a government or party)
**3** to cause to have financial difficulties
 **[[embarrassed]]** *adj* 
 **[[embarrassing]]** *adj* 
 **[[embarrassingly]]** *adv* 
 **[[embarrassment]]** *n*
