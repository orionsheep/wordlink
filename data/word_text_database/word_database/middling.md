**middling** *adj*
**1** neither very good nor very bad
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mediocre]], [[all right]], [[indifferent]], [[so-so]] (*informal*), [[unremarkable]], [[tolerable]] (*informal*), [[run-of-the-mill]], [[passable]], [[half-pie]] (*NZ* *informal*)
**2** [[moderate]] in size
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moderate]], [[medium]], [[average]], [[fair]], [[ordinary]], [[modest]], [[adequate]]
**3** **[[fair]] to middling** neither good nor bad, esp. in health
 *adv*
**4** (*informal*) moderately: middling well
