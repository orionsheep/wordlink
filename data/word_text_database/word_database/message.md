**message** *n*
**1** a [[communication]] from one person or group to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communication]], [[note]], [[bulletin]], [[word]], [[letter]], [[dispatch]], [[memorandum]], [[communiqué]], email *or* e-mail, text *or* text message, [[SMS]], [[IMS]], [[tweet]], mention *or* @mention
**2** an implicit [[meaning]] or [[moral]], as in a work of art
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[meaning]], [[idea]], [[moral]], [[theme]], [[import]], [[purport]]
**3** a religious or political belief that someone attempts to communicate to others: paintings with a fierce feminist message
**4** **get the message** (*informal*) to understand
