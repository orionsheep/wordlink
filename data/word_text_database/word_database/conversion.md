**conversion** *n*
**1** a change or [[adaptation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adaptation]], [[reconstruction]], [[modification]], [[alteration]], [[remodelling]], [[reorganization]]
**2** (*maths*) a calculation in which a weight, volume, or distance is worked out in a different system of measurement: the conversion from Fahrenheit to Celsius
**3** a change to another belief or religion
**4** (*rugby*) a score made after a try by kicking the ball over the crossbar from a place kick
