**cruise** *n*
**1** a [[sail]] taken for pleasure, stopping at various places
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sail]], [[voyage]], [[boat trip]], sea trip
 *vb* **cruising**, **[[cruised]]**
**2** to [[sail]] about from place to place for pleasure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sail]], [[coast]], [[voyage]]
**3** (of a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) to travel at a moderate and efficient speed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[travel along]], [[coast]], [[drift]], [[keep a steady pace]]
**4** to proceed steadily or easily: they [[cruised]] into the final of the qualifying competition
