**receipt** *n*
**1** a written acknowledgment that money or goods have been received
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sales slip]], [[proof of purchase]], [[counterfoil]]
**2** the act of [[receiving]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[receiving]], [[delivery]], [[reception]], [[acceptance]]
**3** **receipts** money taken in over a particular period by a shop or business
