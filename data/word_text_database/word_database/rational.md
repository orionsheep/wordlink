**rational** *adj*
**1** [[reasonable]] or [[sensible]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sensible]], [[sound]], [[wise]], [[reasonable]], [[intelligent]], [[realistic]], [[logical]], [[sane]], [[grounded]]
**2** using reason or logic in thinking out a problem
**3** capable of reasoning: man is a rational being
**4** [[sane]]: rational behaviour
**5** (*maths*) able to be expressed as a ratio of two integers: a rational number
 **[[rationality]]** *n* 
 **[[rationally]]** *adv*
