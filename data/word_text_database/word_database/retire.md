**retire** *vb* **-tiring**, **-tired**
**1** to give up or to cause (a person) to [[give up work]], esp. on reaching pensionable age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop working]], [[give up work]]
**2** to [[go away]] into seclusion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[withdraw]], [[leave]], [[exit]], [[go away]], [[depart]]
**3** to [[go to bed]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go to bed]], [[turn in]] (*informal*), [[hit the sack]] (*slang*), [[hit the hay]] (*slang*)
**4** to [[withdraw]] from a sporting contest, esp. because of injury
**5** to pull back (troops) from battle or (of troops) to fall back
 **[[retired]]** *adj* 
 **[[retirement]]** *n*
