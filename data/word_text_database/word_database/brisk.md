**brisk** *adj*
**1** [[lively]] and [[quick]]; [[vigorous]]: brisk trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quick]], [[lively]], [[energetic]], [[active]], [[vigorous]], [[bustling]], [[sprightly]], [[spry]]
**2** invigorating or sharp: brisk weather
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[short]], [[brief]], [[blunt]], [[abrupt]], [[terse]], [[gruff]], [[brusque]], [[monosyllabic]]
**3** practical and businesslike: his manner was brisk
 **[[briskly]]** *adv*
