**metaphor** *n* a [[figure of speech]] in which a word or phrase is applied to an object or action that it does not literally apply to in order to imply a resemblance, for example *he is a lion in battle*
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[figure of speech]], [[image]], [[symbol]], [[analogy]], [[conceit]] (*literary*), [[allegory]], [[trope]], [[figurative expression]]
 **[[metaphorical]]** *adj* 
 **[[metaphorically]]** *adv*
