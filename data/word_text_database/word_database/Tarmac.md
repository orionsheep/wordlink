**Tarmac** *n*
**1** (*trademark*) a paving material made of crushed stone bound with a mixture of tar and bitumen, used for a road or airport runway
**2** **the tarmac** the area of an airport where planes wait and take off or land: we had to wait for an hour on the tarmac
 *vb* **tarmac****-macking**, **-macked**
**3** to apply Tarmac to (a surface)
