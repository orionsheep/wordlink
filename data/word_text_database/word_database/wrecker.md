**wrecker** *n*
**1** a person who destroys or badly damages something: a marriage wrecker
**2** (formerly) a person who lured ships onto rocks in order to plunder them
**3** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad** & **NZ*) a person whose job is to demolish buildings or dismantle cars
**4** (*US** & **Canad*) a breakdown van
