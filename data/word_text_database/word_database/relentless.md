**relentless** *adj*
**1** never stopping or reducing in severity: relentless deterioration in standards
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unremitting]], [[persistent]], [[unrelenting]], [[incessant]], [[nonstop]], [[unrelieved]]
**2** (of a person) determined and [[pitiless]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merciless]], [[fierce]], [[cruel]], [[ruthless]], [[unrelenting]], [[implacable]], [[remorseless]], [[pitiless]]
