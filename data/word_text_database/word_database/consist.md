**consist** *vb*
**1** **consist of** to be made up of: a match consists of seven games
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[consist of something]]**: be made up of, include, contain, incorporate, amount to, comprise, be composed of
**2** **consist in** to have as its main or only part: their religion consists only in going to church
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[consist in something]]**: lie in, involve, reside in, be expressed by, subsist in, be found *or* contained in
