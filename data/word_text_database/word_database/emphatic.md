**emphatic** *adj*
**1** expressed, spoken, or done forcefully: an emphatic denial of the allegations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[significant]], [[pronounced]], [[decisive]], [[resounding]], [[conclusive]]
**2** [[forceful]] and [[positive]]: he was emphatic about his desire for peace talks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forceful]], [[positive]], [[definite]], [[vigorous]], [[unmistakable]], [[insistent]], [[unequivocal]], [[vehement]]
 **[[emphatically]]** *adv*
