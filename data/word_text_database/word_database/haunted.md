**haunted** *adj*
**1** (of a place) frequented or visited by ghosts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[possessed]], [[ghostly]], [[cursed]], [[eerie]], [[spooky]] (*informal*), [[jinxed]]
**2** (of a person) [[obsessed]] or [[worried]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[preoccupied]], [[worried]], [[troubled]], [[plagued]], [[obsessed]], [[tormented]]
