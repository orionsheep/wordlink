**pulp** *n*
**1** a soft wet substance made from matter which has been crushed or beaten: [[mash]] the strawberries to a pulp
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[paste]], [[mash]], [[mush]]
**2** the soft fleshy part of a fruit or vegetable: halve the tomatoes then scoop the seeds and pulp into a bowl
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flesh]], [[meat]], [[soft part]]
**3** printed or recorded material with little depth or designed to shock: a music player churned out disco pulp
 *vb*
**4** to reduce (a material) to pulp: he began to pulp the orange in his fingers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crush]], [[squash]], [[mash]], pulverize
 **[[pulpy]]** *adj*
