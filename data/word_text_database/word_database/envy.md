**envy** *n*, *pl* **-vies**
**1** a feeling of discontent aroused by someone else's possessions, achievements, or qualities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covetousness]], [[resentment]], [[jealousy]], [[bitterness]], [[resentfulness]], [[enviousness]] (*informal*)
**2** something that causes envy: their standards are the envy of the world
 *vb* **-vies**, **-vying**, **-vied**
**3** to wish that one had the possessions, achievements, or qualities of (someone else)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be jealous]] (of), [[resent]], [[begrudge]], [[be envious]] (of)
 **[[envyingly]]** *adv*
