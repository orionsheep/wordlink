**rude** *adj*
**1** [[insulting]] or [[impolite]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impolite]], [[insulting]], [[cheeky]], [[abusive]], [[disrespectful]], [[impertinent]], [[insolent]], [[impudent]]
**2** [[vulgar]] or obscene: rude words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vulgar]]
**3** unexpected and [[unpleasant]]: we received a rude awakening
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unpleasant]], [[sharp]], [[sudden]], [[harsh]], [[startling]], [[abrupt]]
**4** roughly or crudely made: the rude hovels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[roughly-made]], [[simple]], [[rough]], [[raw]], [[crude]], [[primitive]], [[makeshift]], [[artless]]
**5** robust or sturdy: the very picture of rude health
**6** lacking refinement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncivilized]], [[rough]], [[coarse]], [[brutish]], [[boorish]], [[uncouth]], [[loutish]], [[graceless]]
 **[[rudely]]** *adv* 
 **[[rudeness]]** *n*
