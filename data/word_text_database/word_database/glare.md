**glare** *vb* **glaring**, **glared**
**1** to stare angrily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scowl]], [[frown]], [[glower]], [[look daggers]], lour *or* lower
**2** (of light or colour) to be too bright
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dazzle]], [[blaze]], [[flare]], [[flame]]
 *n*
**3** an angry stare
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scowl]], [[frown]], [[glower]], [[dirty look]], [[black look]], lour *or* lower
**4** a dazzling light or [[brilliance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dazzle]], [[glow]], [[blaze]], [[flame]], [[brilliance]]
**5** **in the glare of publicity** receiving a lot of attention from the media or the public
