**convenience** *n*
**1** the quality of being suitable or convenient
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suitability]], [[fitness]], [[appropriateness]]
**2** **at your convenience** at a time suitable to you
**3** an object that is useful: a house with every modern convenience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appliance]], [[facility]], [[comfort]], [[amenity]], [[labour-saving device]], [[help]]
**4** (*euphemistic*, *chiefly* *Brit*) a public toilet
