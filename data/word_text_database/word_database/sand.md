**sand** *n*
**1** a powdery substance consisting of very small rock or mineral grains, found on the seashore and in deserts
**2** **sands** a large sandy area, esp. on the seashore or in a desert
 *vb*
**3** to smooth or polish the surface of (something) with sandpaper or a sander
**4** to fill with sand: the channel sanded up
