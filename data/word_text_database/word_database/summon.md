**summon** *vb*
**1** to order (someone) to come
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[send for]], [[call]], [[bid]], [[invite]]
**2** to [[send for]] (someone) to appear in court
**3** to [[call]] upon (someone) to do something: the authorities had summoned the relatives to be available
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[send for]], [[call]], [[bid]], [[invite]]
**4** to convene (a meeting)
**5** (often foll. by *up*) to [[call]] into action (one's strength, courage, etc.); [[muster]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gather]], [[muster]], [[draw on]]
