**incendiary** (in-**send**-ya-ree) *adj*
**1** (of bombs, etc.) designed to cause fires
**2** tending to create strife or violence
**3** relating to the illegal burning of property or goods
 *n*, *pl* **-aries**
**4** a bomb that is designed to start fires
**5** a person who illegally sets fire to property or goods
 **[[incendiarism]]** *n*
