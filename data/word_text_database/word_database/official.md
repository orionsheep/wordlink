**2** approved by or derived from authority: there has been no official announcement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[authorized]], [[formal]], [[sanctioned]], [[licensed]], [[proper]], [[legitimate]], [[authentic]], [[certified]]
**3** [[formal]] or [[ceremonial]]: he was speaking at an official dinner in Warsaw
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[formal]], [[bureaucratic]], [[ceremonial]], [[solemn]], ritualistic
 *n*
