**offspring** *n*
**1** the immediate descendant or descendants of a person or animal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[child]], [[baby]], [[kid]] (*informal*), [[youngster]], [[infant]], [[successor]], [[babe]], [[toddler]], [[littlie]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[ankle-biter]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[tacker]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** a product, outcome, or result: the women's liberation movement was the offspring of the 1960s
