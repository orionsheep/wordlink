**sickly** *adj* **-lier**, **-liest**
**1** weak and unhealthy
**2** (of a person) looking pale and unwell: sickly pallor
**3** unpleasant to smell, taste, or look at
**4** showing excessive emotion in a weak and rather pathetic way: a sickly tune
 *adv*
**5** suggesting sickness: sickly pale
 **[[sickliness]]** *n*
