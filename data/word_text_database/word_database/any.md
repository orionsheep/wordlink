**any** *determiner*
**1** one, some, or several, no matter how much or what kind: the jar opener fits over the top of any bottle or jar; have you left me any?
**2** even the smallest amount or even one: we can't answer any questions; don't give her any
**3** whatever or whichever: police may board any bus or train
**4** an indefinite or unlimited amount or number: he would sign cheques for any amount of money
 *adv*
**5** to even the smallest extent: the outcome wouldn't have been any different
