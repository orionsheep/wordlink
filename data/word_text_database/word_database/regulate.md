**regulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to [[control]] by means of rules: a code of practice to regulate advertising by schools
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[run]], [[rule]], [[manage]], [[direct]], [[guide]], [[handle]], [[govern]]
**2** to [[adjust]] slightly: he had to take drugs to regulate his heartbeat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moderate]], [[control]], [[modulate]], [[fit]], [[tune]], [[adjust]]
 **[[regulatory]]** *adj*
