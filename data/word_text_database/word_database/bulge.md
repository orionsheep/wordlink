**bulge** *n*
**1** a [[swelling]] or an outward curve on a normally flat surface
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lump]], [[swelling]], [[bump]], [[projection]], [[hump]], [[protuberance]], [[protrusion]]
**2** a sudden [[increase]] in number, esp. of population
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[rise]], [[boost]], [[surge]], intensification
 *vb* **[[bulging]]**, **[[bulged]]**
**3** to swell outwards
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[swell out]], [[project]], [[expand]], [[stick out]], [[protrude]], [[puff out]], [[distend]]
 **[[bulging]]** *adj*
