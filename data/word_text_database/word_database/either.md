**either** *adj*, *pron*
**1** one or the other (of two): we were offered either fish or beef
**2** both one and the other: we sat at either end of a long sofa
 *conj*
**3** used preceding two or more possibilities joined by *or*: it must be stored either in the fridge or in a cool place
 *adv*
**4** likewise: I don't eat meat and my husband doesn't either
**5** used to qualify or modify a previous statement: he wasn't exactly ugly, but he wasn't handsome either
