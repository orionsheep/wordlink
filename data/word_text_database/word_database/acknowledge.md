**acknowledge** *vb* **-edging**, **-edged**
**1** to [[recognize]] or [[admit]] the truth of a statement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[admit]], [[own up to]], [[allow]], [[accept]], [[reveal]], [[grant]], [[declare]], [[recognize]]
**2** to show recognition of a person by a greeting or glance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[greet]], [[address]], [[notice]], [[recognize]], [[salute]], [[accost]]
**3** to make known that a message or letter has been received
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reply to]], [[answer]], [[notice]], [[recognize]], [[respond to]], [[react to]], [[retort to]]
**4** to express gratitude for (a favour or compliment)
