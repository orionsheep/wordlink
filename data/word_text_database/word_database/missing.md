**missing** *adj*
**1** not in its proper or usual place and unable to be found
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lost]], [[misplaced]], [[not present]], [[astray]], [[unaccounted for]], [[mislaid]]
**2** not able to be traced and not known to be dead: seven men were reported missing after the raid
**3** not included in something although it perhaps should have been: two things are missing from the report
