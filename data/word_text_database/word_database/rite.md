**rite** *n*
**1** a formal act which forms part of a religious [[ceremony]]: the rite of burial
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[custom]], [[ritual]], [[practice]], [[procedure]], [[observance]]
**2** a [[custom]] that is carried out within a particular group: the barbaric rites of public execution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[custom]], [[ritual]], [[practice]], [[procedure]], [[observance]]
**3** a particular body of such acts, esp. of a particular Christian Church: the traditional Anglican rite
