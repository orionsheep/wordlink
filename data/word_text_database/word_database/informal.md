**informal** *adj*
**1** [[relaxed]] and friendly: an informal interview
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[natural]], [[relaxed]], [[casual]], [[familiar]], [[unofficial]], [[laid-back]], [[easy-going]], [[colloquial]]
**2** appropriate to [[everyday]] life or use rather than formal occasions: informal clothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[casual]], [[comfortable]], [[leisure]], [[everyday]], [[simple]]
**3** (of speech or writing) appropriate to ordinary conversation rather than to formal written language
 **[[informality]]** *n* 
 **[[informally]]** *adv*
