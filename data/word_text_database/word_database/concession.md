**concession** *n*
**1** any [[grant]] of rights, land, or property by a government, local authority, or company
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[privilege]], [[right]], [[permit]], [[licence]], [[entitlement]], [[indulgence]], [[prerogative]]
**2** a [[reduction]] in price for a certain category of person: fare concessions for senior citizens
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reduction]], [[saving]], [[grant]], [[discount]], [[allowance]]
**3** the act of [[yielding]] or [[conceding]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surrender]], [[yielding]], [[conceding]], [[renunciation]], [[relinquishment]]
**4** something conceded
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compromise]], [[agreement]], [[settlement]], [[accommodation]], [[adjustment]], [[trade-off]], [[give-and-take]]
**5** (*Canad*) a land subdivision in a township survey
 **[[concessionary]]** *adj*
