**intellect** *n*
**1** the ability to understand, think, and [[reason]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intelligence]], [[mind]], [[reason]], [[understanding]], [[sense]], [[brains]] (*informal*), [[judgment]]
**2** a particular person's [[mind]] or [[intelligence]], esp. a brilliant one: his intellect is wasted on that job
**3** (*informal*) a person who has a brilliant [[mind]]
