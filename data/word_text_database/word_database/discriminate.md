**discriminate** *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**1** to make a distinction against or in favour of a particular person or group
**2** to recognize or understand a difference: to discriminate between right and wrong
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[differentiate]], [[distinguish]], [[separate]], [[tell the difference]], [[draw a distinction]]
 **[[discriminating]]** *adj*
