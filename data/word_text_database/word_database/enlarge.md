**enlarge** *vb* **-larging**, **-larged**
**1** to make or grow larger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expand]], [[increase]], [[extend]], [[add to]], [[build up]], [[widen]], [[intensify]], [[broaden]]
**2** **enlarge on** to speak or write about in greater detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[enlarge on something]]**: [[expand]] on, develop, [[add to]], fill out, elaborate on, flesh out, expatiate on, give further details about
 **[[enlargement]]** *n*
