**premiere** *n*
**1** the first public performance of a film, play, or opera
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[first night]], [[opening]], debut
 *vb* **-ering**, **-ered**
**2** to give, or (of a film, play, or opera) be, a premiere: the play was premiered last year in Johannesburg; the movie premieres tomorrow
