**veteran** *n*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[old hand]], [[past master]], [[warhorse]] (*informal*), [[old stager]]
**2** a soldier who has seen a lot of active service
**3** a person who has served in the military forces
 *adj*
**4** [[long-serving]]: the veteran American politician
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[long-serving]], [[seasoned]], [[experienced]], [[old]], [[established]], [[qualified]], [[mature]], [[practised]]
