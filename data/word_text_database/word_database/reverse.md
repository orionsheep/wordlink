**reverse** *vb* **-versing**, **-versed**
**1** to turn or set in an [[opposite]] direction, order, or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[turn round]], [[turn over]], [[turn upside down]], [[upend]]
**2** to [[change]] into something different or [[contrary]]: the cabinet intends to reverse the trend of recent polls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transpose]], [[change]], [[move]], [[exchange]], [[transfer]], [[switch]], [[shift]], [[alter]]
**3** to [[move backwards]] or in an [[opposite]] direction: as he started to reverse the car, the bomb exploded
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go backwards]], [[retreat]], [[back up]], [[turn back]], [[move backwards]], [[back]]
**4** to run (machinery) in the [[opposite]] direction to normal
**5** to turn inside out
**6** (*law*) to [[revoke]] or set aside (a judgment or decree)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[cancel]], [[overturn]], [[overthrow]], [[undo]], [[repeal]], [[quash]], [[revoke]]
**7** **reverse the charges** (*Brit*) to make a telephone call at the recipient's expense
 *n*
**8** the [[opposite]] or [[contrary]] of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opposite]], [[contrary]], [[converse]], [[inverse]]
**9** the [[back]] or [[rear]] side of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[back]], [[rear]], [[other side]], [[wrong side]], [[underside]]
**10** a [[change]] to an [[opposite]] position, state, or direction
**11** a [[change]] for the worse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misfortune]], [[blow]], [[failure]], [[disappointment]], [[setback]], [[hardship]], [[reversal]], [[adversity]]
**12** the gear by which a motor vehicle can be made to [[go backwards]]
**13** the side of a coin bearing a secondary design
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[back]], [[rear]], [[other side]], [[wrong side]], [[underside]]
**14** **in reverse** in an [[opposite]] or backward direction
**15** **the reverse of** not at all: the result was the reverse of his expectations
 *adj*
**16** [[opposite]] or [[contrary]] in direction, position, etc.
**17** denoting the gear by which a motor vehicle can be made to [[go backwards]]
