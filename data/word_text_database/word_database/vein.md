**vein** *n*
**1** any of the tubes that carry blood to the heart
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blood vessel]]
**2** a thin line in a leaf or in an insect's wing
**3** a clearly defined [[layer]] of ore or mineral in rock
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seam]], [[layer]], [[stratum]], [[course]], [[current]], [[bed]], [[deposit]], [[streak]]
**4** an irregular [[streak]] of colour in marble, wood, or cheese
**5** a distinctive trait or quality in speech or writing: critics have exposed a strong vein of moralism in the poem
**6** a temporary [[mood]]: we're in a very humorous vein tonight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mood]], [[style]], [[note]], [[tone]], [[mode]], [[temper]], [[tenor]]
 **[[veined]]** *adj*
