**linger** *vb*
**1** to [[delay]] or prolong departure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stay]], [[remain]], [[stop]], [[wait]], [[delay]], [[hang around]], [[idle]], [[dally]]
**2** to survive in a weakened condition for some time before death
**3** to spend a long time doing or considering something
 **[[lingering]]** *adj*
