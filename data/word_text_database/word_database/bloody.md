◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bloodstained]], [[raw]], [[bleeding]], [[blood-soaked]], [[blood-spattered]]
**2** marked by much killing and bloodshed: a bloody war
**3** [[cruel]] or murderous: a bloody tyrant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cruel]], [[fierce]], [[savage]], [[brutal]], [[vicious]], [[ferocious]], [[cut-throat]], warlike
 *adj*, *[[adv]]*
**4** (*slang*) extreme or extremely: a bloody fool; a bloody good idea
 *vb* **bloodies**, **bloodying**, **bloodied**
**5** to stain with blood
