**flame** *n*
**1** a hot luminous body of burning gas coming in flickering streams from burning material
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fire]], [[light]], [[spark]], [[glow]], [[blaze]], [[brightness]], [[inferno]]
**2** **flames** the state of burning: half the building was in flames
**3** intense passion: the flame of love
 *vb* **flaming**, **flamed**
**4** to [[burn]] brightly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[burn]], [[flash]], [[shine]], [[glow]], [[blaze]], [[flare]], [[glare]]
**5** to become red or fiery: colour flamed in Sally's cheeks
**6** to become angry or excited
