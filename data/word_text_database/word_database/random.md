**random** *adj*
**1** lacking any definite plan or prearranged order: a random sample
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[casual]]
**2** (*informal*) unknown or unspecified: some random guy
 *n*
**3** (*informal*) an unknown or unspecified person
**4** **[[at random]]** not following any prearranged order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at random]]**: haphazardly, [[randomly]], arbitrarily, by chance, willy-nilly, unsystematically
 **[[randomly]]** *adv* 
 **[[randomness]]** *n*
