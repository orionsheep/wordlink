**performance** *n*
**1** the act or process of performing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[presentation]], [[playing]], [[acting]] (out), [[staging]], [[production]], [[exhibition]], [[rendering]], [[portrayal]]
**2** an artistic or dramatic [[production]]: the [[concert]] includes the first performance of a new trumpet concerto
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[appearance]], [[concert]], [[gig]] (*informal*), [[recital]]
**3** manner or quality of functioning: the car's overall performance is excellent
**4** (*informal*) conduct or behaviour, esp. when distasteful: what did you mean by that performance at the restaurant?
