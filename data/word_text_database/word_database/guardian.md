**guardian** *n*
**1** one who looks after, protects, or defends someone or something: the nation's moral guardians
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[keeper]], [[champion]], [[defender]], [[guard]], [[warden]], [[curator]], [[protector]], [[custodian]]
**2** someone legally appointed to manage the affairs of another person, such as a child or other vulnerable person
 **[[guardianship]]** *n*
