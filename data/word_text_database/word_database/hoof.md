**hoof** *n*, *pl* **hooves*** or ***hoofs**
**1** the horny covering of the end of the foot in the horse, deer, and certain other mammals
**2** **on the hoof**
**A** (of livestock) alive
 **B** in an impromptu way: thinking on the hoof
 *vb*
**3** **hoof it** (*slang*) to walk
 **[[hoofed]]** *adj*
