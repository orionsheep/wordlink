**verge** *n*
**1** a grass [[border]] along a road
**2** **on the verge of** having almost reached (a point or condition)
**3** an [[edge]] or rim
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[border]], [[edge]], [[margin]], [[limit]], [[boundary]], [[threshold]], brim
 *vb* **verging**, **[[verged]]**
**4** **verge on** to be near to: she was verging on hysteria
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[verge on something]]**: come near to, approach, [[border]] on, resemble, incline to, be similar to, touch on, be more or less
