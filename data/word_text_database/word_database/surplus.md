**surplus** *n*
**1** a quantity or amount left over in [[excess]] of what is required
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excess]], [[surfeit]]
**2** (*accounting*) an [[excess]] of income over spending
 *adj*
**3** being in [[excess]]; [[extra]]: surplus to requirements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extra]], [[spare]], [[excess]], [[remaining]], [[odd]], [[superfluous]]
