**desert**^2 *vb*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abandon]], [[leave]], [[quit]] (*informal*), [[forsake]]
**2** (*chiefly* *military*) to [[leave]] (a post or duty) with no intention of returning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abscond]]
 **[[deserted]]** *adj* 
 **[[deserter]]** *n* 
 **[[desertion]]** *n*
