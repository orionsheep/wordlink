**competition** *n*
**1** the act of competing; [[rivalry]]: competition for places was keen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rivalry]], [[opposition]], [[struggle]], [[strife]]
**2** an [[event]] in which people compete
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contest]], [[event]], [[championship]], [[tournament]], [[head-to-head]]
**3** the [[opposition]] offered by competitors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opposition]], [[field]], [[rivals]], [[challengers]]
**4** people against whom one competes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opposition]], [[field]], [[rivals]], [[challengers]]
