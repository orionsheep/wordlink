**agent** *n*
**1** a person who arranges business for other people, esp. for actors or singers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representative]], [[rep]] (*informal*), [[negotiator]], [[envoy]], [[surrogate]], [[go-between]]
**2** a spy
**3** a substance which causes change in other substances: an emulsifying agent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[force]], [[means]], [[power]], [[cause]], [[instrument]]
**4** someone or something which causes an effect: the agent of change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[author]], [[worker]], [[vehicle]], [[instrument]], [[operator]], [[performer]], [[catalyst]], [[doer]]
