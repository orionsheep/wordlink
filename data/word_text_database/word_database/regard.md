**regard** *vb*
**1** to [[look]] upon or [[think of]] in a specified way: angina can therefore be regarded as heart cramp
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consider]], [[see]], [[rate]], [[view]], [[judge]], [[think of]], [[esteem]] (*formal*), [[deem]]
**2** to [[look]] closely or attentively at (something or someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look at]], [[view]], [[eye]], [[watch]], [[observe]], [[clock]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[check out]] (*informal*), [[gaze at]]
**3** to take notice of: he has never regarded the conventions
**4** **[[as regards]]** on the subject of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[as regards]]**: concerning, regarding, relating to, pertaining to
 *n*
**5** [[respect]] or affection: you haven't a high regard for her opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[respect]], [[esteem]], [[thought]], [[concern]], [[care]], [[consideration]]
**6** attention: he eats what he wants with no regard to health
**7** a [[gaze]] or [[look]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look]], [[gaze]], [[scrutiny]], [[stare]], [[glance]]
**8** reference or connection: with regard to my complaint
**9** **regards** an expression of goodwill: give her my regards
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good wishes]], [[respects]], [[greetings]], [[compliments]], [[best wishes]]
