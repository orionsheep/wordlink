**yarn** *n*
**1** a continuous twisted strand of natural or synthetic fibres, used for knitting or making cloth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thread]], [[fibre]], [[cotton]], [[wool]]
**2** (*informal*) a long involved [[story]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[story]], [[tale]], [[anecdote]], [[account]], [[narrative]], [[fable]], [[reminiscence]], [[urban myth]]
**3** **spin a yarn** (*informal*) to tell such a [[story]]
