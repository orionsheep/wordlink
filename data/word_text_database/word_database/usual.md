**usual** *adj*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[normal]], [[customary]], [[regular]], [[general]], [[common]], [[standard]], [[ordinary]], typical
 *n*
**2** [[ordinary]] or commonplace events: the dirt was nothing out of the usual
**3** **as usual** as happens normally
**4** **the usual** (*informal*) the habitual or usual drink
 **[[usually]]** *adv*
