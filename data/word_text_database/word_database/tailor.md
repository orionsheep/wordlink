**tailor** *n*
**1** a person who makes, repairs, or alters outer garments, esp. menswear. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**sartorial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outfitter]] (*old-fashioned*), [[couturier]], [[dressmaker]], [[seamstress]], [[clothier]], costumier
 *vb*
**2** to cut or [[style]] (a garment) to satisfy specific requirements
**3** to [[adapt]] (something) so as to make it suitable: activities are [[tailored]] to participants' capabilities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adapt]], [[adjust]], [[modify]], [[style]], [[fashion]], [[shape]], [[alter]], [[mould]]
 **[[tailored]]** *adj*
