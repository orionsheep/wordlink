**sphere** *n*
**1** (*geom*) a round solid figure in which every point on the surface is equally distant from the centre
**2** an object having this shape, such as a planet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ball]], [[globe]], [[orb]], [[globule]], [[circle]]
**3** a particular [[field]] of activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[field]], [[department]] (*informal*), [[function]], [[territory]], [[capacity]], [[province]], [[patch]], [[scope]]
**4** people of the same rank or with shared interests: a humbler social sphere
