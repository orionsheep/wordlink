**sweetheart** *n*
**1** an affectionate name to call someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dearest]], [[beloved]], [[sweet]], [[angel]] (*informal*), [[treasure]], [[honey]], [[dear]], [[sweetie]] (*informal*)
**2** (*old-fashioned*) one's [[boyfriend or girlfriend]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[love]], boyfriend *or* girlfriend, [[beloved]], [[lover]], [[darling]]
**3** (*informal*) a lovable or generous person
