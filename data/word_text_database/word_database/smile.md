**smile** *n*
**1** a facial expression in which the corners of the mouth are turned up, showing amusement or friendliness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grin]], [[beam]], [[smirk]]
 *vb* **smiling**, **[[smiled]]**
**2** to give a smile
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grin]], [[beam]], [[smirk]], [[twinkle]], [[grin from ear to ear]]
**3** **smile at**
**A** to look at with a kindly expression
 **B** to look with amusement at
**4** **smile on *or* upon** to regard favourably: fortune [[smiled]] on us today
**5** to express by a smile: he [[smiled]] a comrade's greeting
