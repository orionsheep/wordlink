**bribe** *vb* **bribing**, **bribed**
**1** to promise, offer, or give something, often illegally, to (a person) to receive services or gain influence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[buy off]], [[reward]], [[pay off]] (*informal*), [[corrupt]], [[suborn]], grease the palm *or* hand of (*slang*)
 *n*
**2** a [[reward]], such as money or favour, given or offered for this purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inducement]], [[pay-off]] (*informal*), [[sweetener]] (*slang*), [[kickback]], [[backhander]] (*slang*), [[enticement]], [[allurement]]
 **[[bribery]]** *n*
