**windy** *adj* **windier**, **windiest**
**1** denoting a time or conditions in which there is a strong wind: a windy day
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breezy]], [[wild]], [[stormy]], [[windswept]], [[blustery]], [[gusty]], [[squally]], [[blowy]]
**2** exposed to the wind: the windy graveyard
**3** long-winded or pompous: his speeches are long and windy
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *slang*) frightened
