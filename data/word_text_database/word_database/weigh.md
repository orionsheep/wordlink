**weigh** *vb*
**1** to have weight as specified: the tree weighs nearly three tons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[have a weight of]], [[tip the scales at]] (*informal*)
**2** to measure the weight of
**3** to [[consider]] carefully: the President now has to weigh his options
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consider]], [[examine]], [[contemplate]], [[evaluate]], [[ponder]], [[think over]], [[reflect upon]], [[meditate upon]]
**4** to be influential: the authorities did not enter my mind or weigh with me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[matter]], [[carry weight]], [[count]]
**5** **weigh anchor** to raise a ship's anchor
**6** **weigh out** to measure out by weight
