**give up** *vb*
**1** to stop (doing something): I did give up smoking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[give something up]]**: abandon, stop, quit, cease, renounce, leave off, desist
**2** to resign from (a job or position)
**3** to admit defeat or failure
**4** to abandon (hope)
**5** **give oneself up**
**A** to surrender to the police or other authorities
 **B** to devote oneself completely: she gave herself up to her work
