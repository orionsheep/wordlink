**portrait** *n*
**1** a [[painting]], drawing, or [[photograph]] of a person, often only of the face
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[picture]], [[painting]], [[image]], [[photograph]], [[representation]], [[likeness]]
**2** a [[description]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[description]], [[profile]], [[portrayal]], [[depiction]], [[characterization]], thumbnail sketch
 *adj*
**3** (of a page or screen) having greater height than width
 **[[portraitist]]** *n*
