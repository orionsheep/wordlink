**there** *adv*
**1** in, at, or to that place or position: he won't be there
**2** in that respect: you're right there
**3** **there and then** immediately and without delay: he walked out there and then
 *adj*
**4** **not all there** (*informal*) mentally unbalanced or silly
 *pron*
**5** that place: to return from there
**6** used as a grammatical subject when the true subject follows the verb, esp. the verb 'to be': there are no children in the house
**7** **so there!** an exclamation, used esp. by children, that usually follows a declaration of refusal or defiance: you can't come, so there!
**8** **there you are *or* go**
**A** an expression used when handing a person something
 **B** an exclamation of satisfaction or vindication
 *interj*
**9** an expression of sympathy, for example when consoling a child: there, there, pet!
