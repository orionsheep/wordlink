**conjure** *vb* **-juring**, **-jured**
**1** to [[make]] (something) appear, as if by magic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[produce]], [[generate]], [[bring about]], [[give rise to]], [[make]], [[create]], [[effect]], [[produce as if by magic]]
**2** to perform tricks that appear to be magic
**3** to summon (a spirit or demon) by magic
**4** (*formal*, *or* *literary*) to appeal earnestly to: I conjure you by all which you profess: answer me!
 **[[conjuring]]** *n*
