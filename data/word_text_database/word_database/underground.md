**underground** *adv*
**1** below ground level: moles digging underground
**2** secretly: several political parties had to operate underground for many years
 *adj*
**3** beneath the ground: an underground bunker
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[subterranean]], [[basement]], [[lower-level]], [[sunken]], [[covered]], [[buried]], [[subterrestrial]]
**4** [[secret]]; clandestine: an underground organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secret]], [[covert]], [[hidden]], [[guerrilla]], [[revolutionary]], [[confidential]], [[dissident]], [[closet]]
**5** (of art, film, music, etc.) avant-garde, experimental, or subversive
 *n*
**6** a movement dedicated to overthrowing a government or occupation forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the underground]]**: the Resistance, partisans, freedom fighters
**7** (often preceded by *the*) an electric passenger railway operated in underground tunnels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the underground]]**: the tube (*Brit*), the subway, the metro
