**secular** *adj*
**1** relating to [[worldly]] as opposed to sacred things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worldly]], [[lay]], [[earthly]], [[civil]], [[temporal]], [[nonspiritual]]
**2** not connected with religion or the church
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worldly]], [[lay]], [[earthly]], [[civil]], [[temporal]], [[nonspiritual]]
**3** (of clerics) not bound by religious vows to a monastic or other order
