**sheep** *n*, *pl* **sheep**
**1** a cud-chewing mammal with a thick woolly coat, kept for its wool or meat. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**ovine**
**2** a timid person
**3** **like sheep** (of a group of people) allowing a single person to dictate their actions or beliefs
**4** **separate the sheep from the goats** to pick out the members of a group who are superior in some respects
 **[[sheeplike]]** *adj*
