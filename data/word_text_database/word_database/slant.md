**slant** *vb*
**1** to [[lean]] at an [[angle]]; [[slope]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slope]], [[incline]], [[tilt]], [[list]], [[bend]], [[lean]], [[heel]], [[cant]]
**2** to write or present (information) in a biased way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bias]], [[colour]], [[twist]], [[angle]], distort
 *n*
**3** a sloping line or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slope]], [[incline]], [[tilt]], [[gradient]], [[camber]]
**4** a [[point of view]], esp. a biased one: a right-wing slant on the story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bias]], [[emphasis]], [[prejudice]], [[angle]], [[point of view]], [[one-sidedness]]
**5** **on a *or* the slant** sloping
 *adj*
**6** oblique; sloping
 **[[slanting]]** *adj* 
 **[[slantwise]]** *adv*
