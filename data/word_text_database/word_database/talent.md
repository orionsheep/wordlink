**talent** *n*
**1** a natural [[ability]] to do something well: the boy has a real talent for writing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ability]], [[gift]], [[aptitude]], [[capacity]], [[genius]], [[flair]], [[knack]]
**2** a person or people with such [[ability]]: he is the major talent in Italian fashion
**3** (*informal*) people regarded as attractive or as potential sexual partners: there's always lots of talent in that pub
**4** any of various ancient units of weight and money
 **[[talented]]** *adj*
