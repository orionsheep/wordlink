**mutual** (**mew**-chew-al) *adj*
**1** experienced or expressed by each of two or more people about the other; [[reciprocal]]: mutual respect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shared]], [[common]], [[joint]], [[returned]], [[reciprocal]], [[interchangeable]], [[requited]]
**2** [[common]] to or [[shared]] by two or more people: a mutual friend
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shared]], [[common]], [[joint]], [[returned]], [[reciprocal]], [[interchangeable]], [[requited]]
**3** denoting an organization, such as an insurance company, in which the policyholders or investors share the profits and expenses and there are no shareholders
 **[[mutuality]]** *n* 
 **[[mutually]]** *adv*
