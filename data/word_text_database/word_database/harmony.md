**harmony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a state of peaceful [[agreement]] and [[cooperation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accord]], [[peace]], [[agreement]], [[friendship]], [[sympathy]], [[cooperation]], [[rapport]], [[compatibility]]
**2** (*music*) a pleasant combination of two or more notes sounded at the same time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tune]], [[melody]], [[unison]], [[tunefulness]], [[euphony]]
**3** the way parts combine well together or into a whole
