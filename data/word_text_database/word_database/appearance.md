**appearance** *n*
**1** a sudden or unexpected [[arrival]] of someone or something at a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrival]], [[presence]], [[introduction]], [[emergence]]
**2** the [[introduction]] or invention of something: the appearance of smartphones
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrival]], [[presence]], [[introduction]], [[emergence]]
**3** an act or instance of appearing: it will be her fiftieth appearance for her country
**4** the way a person or thing [[looks]]: I spotted a man of extraordinary appearance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look]], [[form]], [[figure]], [[looks]], [[manner]], [[expression]], [[demeanour]], [[mien]] (*literary*)
**5** **keep up appearances** to maintain the public impression of wellbeing or normality
**6** **put in an appearance** to attend an event briefly
**7** **to all appearances** apparently: to all appearances they seemed enthralled by what she was saying
