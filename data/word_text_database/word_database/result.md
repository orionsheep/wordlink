**result** *n*
**1** the [[outcome]] or consequence of an action, policy, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outcome]], [[end]]
**2** the final score of a sporting contest
**3** a number or value obtained by solving a mathematical problem
**4** a favourable result, esp. a victory or success: the best chance of a result is at Cheltenham
**5** **results** the marks or grades obtained in an examination
 *vb*
**6** **result from** to be the [[outcome]] or consequence of: poverty resulting from high unemployment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arise]], [[follow]], [[issue]], [[happen]], [[appear]], [[develop]], [[spring]], [[derive]]
**7** **result in** to [[end]] in (a specified way): negotiations which resulted in the Treaty of Paris
