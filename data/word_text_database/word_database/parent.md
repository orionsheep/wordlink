**parent** *n*
**1** a [[father or mother]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: father *or* mother, [[sire]], [[progenitor]], [[procreator]], [[old]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[oldie]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[patriarch]]
**2** a person acting as a [[father or mother]]; guardian
**3** a plant or animal that has produced one or more plants or animals
 **[[parental]]** *adj*
