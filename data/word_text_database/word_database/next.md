**next** *adj*
**1** immediately [[following]]: the next generation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[following]], [[later]], [[succeeding]], [[subsequent]]
**2** immediately [[adjoining]]: in the next room
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adjacent]], [[closest]], [[nearest]], [[neighbouring]], [[adjoining]]
**3** [[closest]] to in degree: the next-best thing
 *adv*
**4** at a time immediately to follow: the patient to be examined next
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[afterwards]], [[then]], [[later]], [[following]], [[subsequently]], [[thereafter]]
**5** **next to**
**A** [[adjacent]] to: the house next to ours
 **B** [[following]] in degree: next to my wife, I love you most
 **C** almost: the evidence is next to totally useless
