**preference** *n*
**1** a [[liking]] for one thing above the rest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liking]], [[wish]], [[taste]], [[desire]], [[leaning]], [[bent]], [[bias]], [[inclination]]
**2** a person or thing preferred
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[first choice]], [[choice]], [[favourite]], [[pick]], [[option]], [[selection]]
