**rave** *vb* **raving**, **raved**
**1** to talk in a wild or incoherent manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rant]], [[rage]], [[roar]], [[go mad]] (*informal*), [[babble]], [[be delirious]]
**2** (*informal*) to write or speak (about) with great enthusiasm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enthuse]], [[praise]], [[gush]], [[be mad about]] (*informal*), be wild about (*informal*)
 *n*
**3** (*informal*) an enthusiastically favourable review
**4** (*slang*) a professionally organized large-scale party with electronic dance music
**5** a name given to various types of dance music, such as techno, that feature a fast electronic rhythm
