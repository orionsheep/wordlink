**difficult** *adj*
**1** not easy to do, understand, or solve
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hard]], [[tough]], [[taxing]], [[demanding]], [[challenging]], [[exacting]], [[formidable]], [[uphill]]
**2** not easily pleased or satisfied: a difficult patient
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[troublesome]], [[demanding]], [[perverse]], [[fussy]], [[fastidious]], [[hard to please]], [[refractory]], [[unaccommodating]]
**3** full of hardships or trials: he had recently had a difficult time with his job as a self-employed builder
