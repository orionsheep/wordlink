**rapid** *adj*
**1** (of an action) taking or lasting a short time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sudden]], [[prompt]], [[speedy]], [[express]], [[swift]]
**2** acting or moving quickly: a rapid advance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quick]], [[fast]], [[hurried]], [[swift]], [[brisk]], [[hasty]]
 **[[rapidly]]** *adv* 
 **[[rapidity]]** *n*
