**seizure** *n*
**1** (*pathol*) a sudden violent [[attack]] of an illness, such as an epileptic [[convulsion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[fit]], [[spasm]], [[convulsion]], [[paroxysm]]
**2** the act of seizing: a seizure of drug traffickers' assets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[taking]], [[grabbing]], [[annexation]], [[confiscation]], [[commandeering]]
