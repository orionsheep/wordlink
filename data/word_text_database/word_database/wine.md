**wine** *n*
**1**
**A** an alcoholic drink produced by the fermenting of grapes with water and sugar
 **B** an alcoholic drink produced in this way from other fruits or flowers: dandelion wine
 *adj*
**2** dark purplish-red
 *vb* **wining**, **wined**
**3** **wine and dine** to entertain (someone) with wine and fine food
