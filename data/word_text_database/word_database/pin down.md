**pin down** *vb*
**1** to force (someone) to make a decision or carry out a promise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pin someone down]]**: force, pressure, compel, put pressure on, pressurize, nail someone down, make someone commit themselves
**2** to define clearly: the courts have found it difficult to pin down what exactly obscenity is
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pin something down]]**: determine, identify, locate, name, specify, pinpoint
