**confuse** *vb* **-fusing**, **-fused**
**1** to fail to distinguish between (one thing and another)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mix up with]], [[take for]], [[muddle with]]
**2** to [[perplex]] or disconcert
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bewilder]], [[puzzle]], [[baffle]], [[perplex]], [[mystify]], [[fluster]], [[faze]], [[flummox]]
**3** to make unclear: he confused his talk with irrelevant detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obscure]], [[cloud]], [[make more difficult]]
**4** to throw into disorder
 **[[confusing]]** *adj* 
 **[[confusingly]]** *adv*
