**colony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a group of people who settle in a new country but remain under the rule of their homeland
**2** the [[territory]] occupied by such a [[settlement]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settlement]], [[territory]], [[province]], [[possession]], [[dependency]], [[outpost]], [[dominion]], [[satellite state]]
**3** a group of people with the same nationality or interests, forming a community in a particular place: an artists' colony
**4** (*zool*) a group of the same type of animal or plant living or growing together
**5** (*bacteriol*) a group of microorganisms when grown on a culture medium
