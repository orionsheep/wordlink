**traction** *n*
**1** pulling, esp. by engine power: the increased use of electric traction
**2** (*med*) the application of a steady pull on an injured limb using a system of weights and pulleys or splints: he was in traction for weeks following the accident
**3** the grip that the wheels of a vehicle have on the ground: four-wheel drive gives much better traction in wet or icy conditions
**4** the quality of being influential or popular: her ideas are gaining traction with the public
