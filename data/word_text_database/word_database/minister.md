**minister** *n*
**1** (esp. in Presbyterian and some Nonconformist Churches) a member of the clergy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: clergyman *or* woman, [[priest]], [[vicar]], [[parson]], [[preacher]], [[pastor]], [[cleric]], [[rector]]
**2** a head of a government department
**3** a diplomat with a lower rank than an ambassador
 *vb*
**4** **[[minister to]]** to attend to the needs of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[minister to]]**: attend to, serve, tend to, take care of, cater to, pander to, administer to
 **[[ministerial]]** *adj*
