**strangle** *vb* **-gling**, **-gled**
**1** to kill (someone) by pressing his or her windpipe; [[throttle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throttle]], [[choke]], [[asphyxiate]], [[strangulate]]
**2** to prevent the growth or development of: another attempt at strangling national identity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suppress]], [[inhibit]], [[subdue]], [[stifle]], [[repress]], [[overpower]], [[quash]], [[quell]]
**3** to [[stifle]] (a voice, cry, or laugh) by swallowing suddenly: the words were strangled by sobs
 **[[strangler]]** *n*
