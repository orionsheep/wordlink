**beauty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the combination of all the qualities of a person or thing that delight the senses and mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attractiveness]], [[charm]], [[grace]], [[glamour]], [[elegance]], [[loveliness]], [[handsomeness]], [[comeliness]] (*old-fashioned*)
**2** a very attractive woman
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good-looker]], [[lovely]] (*slang*), [[belle]], [[stunner]] (*informal*), [[beaut]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
**3** (*informal*) an outstanding example of its kind
**4** (*informal*) an advantageous feature: the beauty of this job is the short hours
