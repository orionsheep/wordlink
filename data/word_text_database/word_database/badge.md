**badge** *n*
**1** a distinguishing [[emblem]] or [[mark]] worn to show membership or achievement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[image]], [[brand]], [[stamp]], [[identification]], [[crest]], [[emblem]], [[insignia]]
**2** any revealing feature or [[mark]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[sign]], [[token]]
