**parting** *n*
**1** a departure or leave-taking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farewell]], [[goodbye]]
**2** (*Brit** & **NZ*) the line of scalp showing when sections of hair are combed in opposite directions
**3** the act of dividing (something): the parting of the Red Sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[division]], [[breaking]], [[split]], [[separation]], [[rift]], [[rupture]]
