**bracket** *n*
**1** a pair of characters, [ ], (), or { }, used to enclose a section of writing or printing
**2** a group or category falling within certain defined limits: the lower income bracket
**3** an L-shaped or other support fixed to a wall to hold a shelf, etc.
 *vb* **-eting**, **-eted**
**4** to put (written or printed matter) in brackets
**5** to group or class together
