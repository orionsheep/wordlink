**arithmetic** *n*
**1** the branch of mathematics concerned with numerical calculations, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
**2** calculations involving numerical operations
**3** knowledge of or skill in arithmetic: even simple arithmetic was beyond him
 *adj* *also* **arithmetical**
**4** of or using arithmetic
 **[[arithmetically]]** *adv* 
 **[[arithmetician]]** *n*
