**adrift** *adj*, *adv*
**1** [[drifting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drifting]], [[afloat]], [[unmoored]], [[unanchored]]
**2** without a clear purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aimless]], [[goalless]], [[directionless]], [[purposeless]]
**3** (*informal*) [[off course]], [[wrong]]: it was obvious that something had gone adrift
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrong]], [[astray]], [[off course]], [[amiss]], [[off target]], [[wide of the mark]]
