**faction** *n*
**1** a small [[group]] of people within a larger body, but differing from it in certain aims and ideas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[set]], [[party]], [[gang]], [[bloc]], [[contingent]], [[clique]], [[coterie]], [[public-interest group]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**2** strife within a [[group]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dissension]], [[division]], [[conflict]], [[rebellion]], [[disagreement]], [[variance]], [[discord]], [[infighting]]
 **[[factional]]** *adj*
