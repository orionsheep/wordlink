**journal** *n*
**1** a [[newspaper]] or magazine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[newspaper]], [[paper]], [[daily]], [[weekly]], [[monthly]]
**2** a [[daily]] [[record]] of events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diary]], [[record]], [[history]], [[log]], [[notebook]], [[chronicle]], [[annals]], [[yearbook]], [[blog]] (*informal*)
