**scholar** *n*
**1** a person who studies an [[academic]] subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intellectual]], [[academic]], [[savant]], [[acca]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** a [[student]] who has a scholarship
**3** a [[pupil]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[student]], [[pupil]], [[learner]], schoolboy *or* schoolgirl
 **[[scholarly]]** *adj*
