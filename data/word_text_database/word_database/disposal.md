**disposal** *n*
**1** the act or means of getting rid of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[throwing away]], [[dumping]] (*informal*), [[scrapping]], [[removal]], [[discarding]], [[jettisoning]], [[ejection]], [[riddance]]
**2** **at one's disposal** available for use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at your disposal]]**: available, ready, to hand, accessible, handy, at hand, on tap (*informal*), expendable
