**vanish** *vb*
**1** to [[disappear]] suddenly: the choppers vanished from radar screens at dawn yesterday
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disappear]], [[dissolve]], [[evaporate]], [[fade away]], [[melt away]], [[evanesce]] (*formal*)
**2** to [[cease to exist]]: the old landmarks had vanished
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[die out]], [[disappear]], [[pass away]], [[end]], [[fade]], [[dwindle]], [[cease to exist]], [[become extinct]]
