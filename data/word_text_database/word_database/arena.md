**arena** *n*
**1** a seated [[enclosure]] where sports events take place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ring]], [[ground]], [[field]], [[theatre]], [[bowl]], [[pitch]], [[stadium]], [[enclosure]]
**2** the [[area]] of an ancient Roman amphitheatre where gladiators fought
**3** a sphere of intense activity: the political arena
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scene]] (*informal*), [[world]], [[area]], [[stage]], [[field]], [[sector]], [[territory]], [[province]]
