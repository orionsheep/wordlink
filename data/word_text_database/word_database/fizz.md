**fizz** *vb*
**1** to make a hissing or bubbling sound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sputter]], [[buzz]], [[sparkle]], [[hiss]], [[crackle]]
**2** (of a drink) to produce bubbles of carbon dioxide
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bubble]], [[froth]], [[fizzle]], [[effervesce]], produce bubbles
 *n*
**3** a hissing or bubbling sound
**4** releasing of small bubbles of gas by a liquid
**5** any effervescent drink
 **[[fizzy]]** *adj* 
 **[[fizziness]]** *n*
