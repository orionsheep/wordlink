**quicken** *vb*
**1** to make or become faster; [[accelerate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speed up]], [[hurry]], [[accelerate]], [[hasten]], [[gee up]] (*informal*)
**2** to impart to or receive vigour or enthusiasm: science quickens the imagination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stimulate]], [[inspire]], [[arouse]], [[excite]], [[revive]], [[incite]], [[energize]], [[invigorate]]
**3**
**A** (of a fetus) to begin to show signs of life
 **B** (of a pregnant woman) to reach the stage of pregnancy at which movements of the fetus can be felt
