**imitation** *n*
**1** a copy of an original or genuine article
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[replica]], [[fake]], [[reproduction]], [[sham]], [[forgery]], [[counterfeiting]], [[likeness]], [[duplication]]
**2** an instance of imitating someone: her Coward imitations were not the best thing she did
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impression]], [[impersonation]]
**3** behaviour modelled on the behaviour of someone else: to learn by imitation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copying]], [[resemblance]], mimicry
 *adj*
**4** made to resemble something which is usually superior or more expensive: imitation leather
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[artificial]], [[mock]], [[reproduction]], [[dummy]], [[synthetic]], [[man-made]], [[simulated]], [[sham]]
