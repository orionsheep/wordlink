**queue** *n*
**1** a [[line]] of people or vehicles waiting for something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[line]], [[row]], [[file]], [[train]], [[series]], [[chain]], [[string]], column
 *vb* **queuing*** or ***queueing**, **[[queued]]**
**2** (often foll. by *up*) to form or remain in a [[line]] while waiting
