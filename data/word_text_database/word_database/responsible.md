**responsible** *adj*
**1** **responsible for** having control or authority over
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[in charge]], [[in control]], [[in authority]]
**2** being the agent or cause (of some action): only a small number of students were responsible for the disturbances
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[to blame]], [[guilty]], [[at fault]], [[culpable]]
**3** **responsible to** being [[accountable]] for one's actions and decisions to: management should be made more responsible to shareholders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accountable]], [[liable]], [[answerable]]
**4** [[rational]] and [[accountable]] for one's own actions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sensible]], [[reliable]], [[rational]], [[dependable]], [[trustworthy]], [[level-headed]]
**5** (of a position or duty) involving decision and accountability
 **[[responsibly]]** *adv*
