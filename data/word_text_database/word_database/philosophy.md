**philosophy** *n*, *pl* **-phies**
**1** the academic study of [[knowledge]], [[thought]], and the meaning of life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thought]], [[knowledge]], [[thinking]], [[reasoning]], [[wisdom]], [[logic]], [[metaphysics]]
**2** the particular doctrines of a specific individual or school relating to these issues: the philosophy of John Locke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thought]], [[knowledge]], [[thinking]], [[reasoning]], [[wisdom]], [[logic]], [[metaphysics]]
**3** any system of [[beliefs]] or [[values]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outlook]], [[values]], [[principles]], [[convictions]], [[thinking]], [[beliefs]], [[doctrine]], [[ideology]]
**4** a personal [[outlook]] or viewpoint
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outlook]], [[values]], [[principles]], [[convictions]], [[thinking]], [[beliefs]], [[doctrine]], [[ideology]]
