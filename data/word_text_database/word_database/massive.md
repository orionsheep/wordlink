**massive** *adj*
**1** (of objects) large, bulky, heavy, and usually solid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[huge]], [[big]], [[enormous]], [[immense]], [[hefty]], [[gigantic]], [[monumental]] (*informal*), [[mammoth]], [[supersize]]
**2** impressive or imposing
**3** intensive or considerable: a massive overdose
 **[[massively]]** *adv*
