**eclectic** *adj*
**1** composed of elements selected from a wide range of styles, ideas, or sources: the contenders for this year's Turner Prize are an eclectic bunch
**2** selecting elements from a wide range of styles, ideas, or sources: an eclectic approach that takes the best from all schools of psychology
 *n*
**3** a person who takes an eclectic approach
 **[[eclecticism]]** *n*
