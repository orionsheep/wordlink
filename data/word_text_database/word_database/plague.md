**plague** *n*
**1** any widespread and usually highly contagious [[disease]] with a high fatality rate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disease]], [[infection]], [[epidemic]], [[pestilence]]
**2** an infectious [[disease]] of rodents transmitted to humans by the bite of the rat flea; bubonic plague
**3** something that afflicts or harasses: a plague of locusts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[infestation]], [[invasion]], [[epidemic]], [[influx]], [[host]], [[swarm]], [[multitude]]
**4** (*informal*) a nuisance
 *vb* **plaguing**, **plagued**
**5** to afflict or [[harass]]: a playing career plagued by injury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[torment]], [[trouble]], [[torture]]
**6** (*informal*) to [[annoy]] or [[pester]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pester]], [[trouble]], [[bother]], [[annoy]], [[tease]], [[harry]], [[harass]], [[hassle]]
