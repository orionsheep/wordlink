**global** *adj*
**1** of or applying to the whole earth: global environmental problems
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worldwide]], [[world]], [[international]], [[universal]]
**2** of or applying to the whole of something: a global [[total]] for local-authority revenue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comprehensive]], [[general]], [[total]], [[unlimited]], [[exhaustive]], [[all-inclusive]]
 **[[globally]]** *adv*
