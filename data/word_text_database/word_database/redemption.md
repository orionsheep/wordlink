**redemption** *n*
**1** the act of redeeming
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compensation]], [[amends]], [[reparation]], [[atonement]]
**2** the state of being redeemed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[salvation]], [[release]], [[rescue]], [[liberation]], [[emancipation]], [[deliverance]]
**3** (*Christianity*) [[deliverance]] from sin through the incarnation and death of Christ
 **[[redemptive]]** *adj*
