**vital** *adj*
**1** [[essential]] or highly [[important]]: marriage isn't such a vital part of his life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[essential]], [[important]], [[necessary]], [[key]], [[basic]], [[significant]], [[critical]], [[crucial]] (*informal*)
**2** [[energetic]] or [[lively]]: the epitome of vital youthful manhood
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lively]], [[vigorous]], [[energetic]], [[spirited]], [[dynamic]], [[animated]], [[vibrant]], [[vivacious]]
**3** [[necessary]] to maintain life: the vital organs
 *n*
**4** **vitals** the bodily organs, such as the brain and heart, that are [[necessary]] to maintain life
 **[[vitally]]** *adv*
