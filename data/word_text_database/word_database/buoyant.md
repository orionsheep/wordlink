**buoyant** *adj*
**1** able to float in or rise to the surface of a liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[floating]], [[light]]
**2** (of a liquid or gas) able to keep a body afloat
**3** thriving: a buoyant economy
**4** [[cheerful]] or resilient
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cheerful]], [[happy]], [[upbeat]] (*informal*), [[carefree]], [[jaunty]], [[chirpy]] (*informal*), [[light-hearted]]
 **[[buoyancy]]** *n*
