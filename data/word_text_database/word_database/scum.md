**scum** *n*
**1** a layer of impure or waste matter that forms on the surface of a liquid: the build-up of soap scum
**2** a person or people regarded as worthless or criminal
 *vb* **scumming**, **scummed**
**3** to remove scum from
**4** (*rare*) to form a layer of or become covered with scum
 **[[scummy]]** *adj*
