**dispense** *vb* **-pensing**, **-pensed**
**1** to [[distribute]] in portions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distribute]], [[assign]], [[allocate]], [[allot]], [[dole out]], [[share out]], [[apportion]], [[deal out]]
**2** to [[prepare]] and [[distribute]] (medicine)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prepare]], [[measure]], [[supply]], [[mix]]
**3** to [[administer]] (the law, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administer]], [[operate]], [[carry out]], [[implement]], [[enforce]], [[execute]], [[apply]], [[discharge]]
**4** **dispense with** to do away with or manage without
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dispense with something or someone]]**: do away with, give up, cancel, abolish, brush aside, forgo, relinquish
 **[[dispenser]]** *n*
