**intermediate** *adj*
**1** occurring between two points or extremes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[middle]], [[mid]], [[halfway]], [[in-between]] (*informal*), [[midway]], [[intervening]], [[transitional]], [[median]]
**2** (of a class, course, etc.) suitable for learners with some level of skill or competence
 *n*
**3** something intermediate
**4** (*chem*) a substance formed between the first and final stages of a chemical process
 **[[intermediation]]** *n*
