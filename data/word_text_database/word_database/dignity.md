**dignity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** serious, calm, and controlled behaviour or manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decorum]], [[gravity]], [[majesty]], [[grandeur]], [[respectability]], [[nobility]], [[solemnity]], [[courtliness]]
**2** the quality of being worthy of honour
**3** sense of [[self-importance]]: he considered the job beneath his dignity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[self-importance]], [[pride]], [[self-esteem]], [[self-respect]]
