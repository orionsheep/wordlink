**leaflet** *n*
**1** a sheet of printed matter distributed, usually free, for advertising or information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[booklet]], [[notice]], [[brochure]], [[circular]], [[flyer]], [[tract]], [[pamphlet]], [[handout]]
**2** any small leaf
**3** one of the divisions of a compound leaf
 *vb* **-leting**, **-leted**
**4** to distribute leaflets (to)
