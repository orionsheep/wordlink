**virtue** *n*
**1** moral [[goodness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[goodness]], [[integrity]], [[worth]], [[morality]], [[righteousness]], [[probity]] (*formal*), [[rectitude]], [[incorruptibility]]
**2** a positive moral quality: the virtue of humility
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merit]], [[strength]], [[asset]], [[plus]] (*informal*), [[attribute]], [[good point]], [[strong point]]
**3** an [[advantage]] or [[benefit]]: the added virtue of being harmless
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advantage]], [[benefit]], [[merit]], [[credit]], [[usefulness]], [[efficacy]]
**4** chastity, esp. in women
**5** **by virtue of** by reason of; because of: they escaped execution by virtue of their high rank
