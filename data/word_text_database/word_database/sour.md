**sour** *adj*
**1** having a [[sharp]] biting taste like the taste of lemon juice or vinegar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sharp]], [[acid]], [[tart]], [[bitter]], [[pungent]], [[acetic]]
**2** made [[acid]] or bad, such as when milk ferments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rancid]], [[turned]], [[gone off]], [[curdled]], [[gone bad]], [[off]]
**3** (of a person's mood) bad-tempered and unfriendly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bitter]], [[tart]], [[acrimonious]], [[embittered]], [[disagreeable]], [[ill-tempered]], [[waspish]], [[ungenerous]]
**4** **go *or* turn sour** to become less enjoyable or happy: the dream has [[turned]] sour
 *vb*
**5** to make or become less enjoyable or friendly: relations soured shortly after the war
 **[[sourly]]** *adv*
