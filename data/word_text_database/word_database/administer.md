**administer** *vb*
**1** to [[manage]] (an organization or estate)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manage]], [[run]], [[control]], [[direct]], [[handle]], [[conduct]], [[command]], [[govern]]
**2** to organize and put into practice: anyone can learn to administer the test procedure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[execute]], [[give]], [[provide]], [[apply]], [[perform]], [[carry out]], [[impose]], [[implement]]
**3** to [[give]] medicine to someone
**4** to supervise the taking of (an oath)
