**revise** *vb* **-vising**, **-vised**
**1** to [[change]] or [[alter]]: he grudgingly revised his opinion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[review]]
**2** to prepare a new edition of (a previously printed work)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edit]], [[correct]], [[alter]], [[update]], [[amend]], [[rework]], [[redo]], [[emend]]
**3** to read (something) several times in order to learn it in preparation for an examination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[study]], [[go over]], [[run through]], [[cram]] (*informal*), [[swot up on]] (*Brit* *informal*)
