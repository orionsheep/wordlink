**queer** *adj*
**1** not normal or usual; [[odd]] or [[strange]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strange]], [[odd]], [[funny]], [[unusual]], [[extraordinary]], [[curious]], [[weird]], [[peculiar]]
**2** dubious; shady
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strange]], [[odd]], [[funny]], [[unusual]], [[extraordinary]], [[curious]], [[weird]], [[peculiar]]
**3** (*Brit*) [[faint]], [[giddy]], or [[queasy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faint]], [[dizzy]], [[giddy]], [[queasy]], [[light-headed]]
**4** (*informal*, *sometimes* *offensive*) not heterosexual
**5** (*informal*) eccentric or slightly mad
 *n*
**6** (*informal*, *sometimes* *offensive*) a person who is not heterosexual
 *vb*
**7** **queer someone's pitch** (*informal*) to spoil or thwart someone's chances of something
