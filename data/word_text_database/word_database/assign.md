**assign** *vb*
**1** to select (someone) for a [[post]] or task
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[select for]], [[post]], [[commission]], [[elect]], [[appoint]], [[delegate]], [[nominate]], [[name]]
**2** to [[give]] a task or duty (to someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give]], [[set]], [[grant]], [[allocate]], [[give out]], [[consign]], [[allot]], [[apportion]]
**3** to [[attribute]] to a specified cause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attribute]], [[credit]], [[put down]], [[set down]], [[ascribe]], [[accredit]]
**4** to [[set]] apart (a place or time) for a particular function or event: to assign a day for the meeting
**5** (*law*) to transfer (one's right, interest, or title to property) to someone else
