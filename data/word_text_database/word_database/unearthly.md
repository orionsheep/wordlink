**unearthly** *adj*
**1** [[strange]], unnatural, or [[eerie]]: unearthly beauty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[eerie]], [[strange]], [[supernatural]], [[ghostly]], [[weird]], [[phantom]], [[uncanny]], [[spooky]] (*informal*)
**2** ridiculous or unreasonable: the unearthly hour of seven in the morning
 **[[unearthliness]]** *n*
