**gravity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** (*physics*)
**A** the force that attracts bodies towards the centre of the earth, a moon, or any planet
 **B** same as **gravitation**
**2** [[seriousness]] or [[importance]]: the gravity of the situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seriousness]], [[importance]], [[significance]], [[urgency]], [[severity]], [[acuteness]], [[weightiness]], [[momentousness]]
**3** [[seriousness]] or [[solemnity]] of appearance or behaviour: his priestly gravity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solemnity]], [[seriousness]], [[gravitas]]
