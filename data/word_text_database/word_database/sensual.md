**sensual** *adj*
**1** giving pleasure to the body and senses rather than the mind: soft sensual music
**2** having a strong liking for [[physical]], esp. [[sexual]], pleasures
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sexual]], [[erotic]], [[raunchy]] (*informal*), [[lewd]], [[lascivious]], [[lustful]], [[lecherous]]
**3** of the body and senses rather than the mind or soul
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physical]], [[bodily]], [[voluptuous]], [[animal]], [[luxurious]], [[fleshly]], [[carnal]]
 **[[sensualist]]** *n*
