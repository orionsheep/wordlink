**bust**^2 (*informal*) *vb* **busting**, **busted*** or ***bust**
**1** to [[burst]] or [[break]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[smash]], [[split]], [[burst]], [[shatter]], [[fracture]], [[rupture]]
**2** (of the police) to [[raid]] or search (a place) or [[arrest]] (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrest]], [[catch]], [[raid]]
**3** (*US** & **Canad*) to demote in military rank
 *adj*
**4** broken
**5** **[[go bust]]** to become bankrupt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[go bust]]**: go bankrupt, fail, be ruined, become insolvent
