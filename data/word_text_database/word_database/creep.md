**creep** *vb* **creeping**, **crept**
**1** to move quietly and cautiously
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sneak]], [[steal]], [[tiptoe]], [[slink]], [[skulk]], [[approach unnoticed]]
**2** to crawl with the body near to or touching the ground
**3** to have the sensation of something crawling over the skin, from fear or disgust: she makes my flesh creep
**4** (of plants) to grow along the ground or over rocks
 *n*
**5** a creeping movement
**6** (*slang*) an obnoxious or servile person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bootlicker]] (*informal*), [[sneak]], [[sycophant]], [[crawler]] (*slang*), [[toady]]
