**clause** *n*
**1** a [[section]] of a legal document such as a will or contract
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[section]], [[condition]], [[article]], [[chapter]], [[passage]], [[part]], [[paragraph]]
**2** (*grammar*) a group of words, consisting of a subject and a predicate including a finite verb, that does not necessarily constitute a sentence
 **[[clausal]]** *adj*
