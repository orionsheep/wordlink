**obligation** *n*
**1** a moral or legal [[duty]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[duty]], [[compulsion]]
**2** the binding power of such a [[duty]]: I feel under some obligation to help you with your education
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsibility]], [[duty]], [[liability]], [[accountability]], [[answerability]]
**3** a sense of being in debt because of a service or favour: I don't want him marrying me out of obligation
