**dynasty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a series of rulers or influential people from the same family
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empire]], [[house]], [[rule]], [[regime]], [[sovereignty]]
**2** a period of time during which a country is ruled by the same family
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empire]], [[house]], [[rule]], [[regime]], [[sovereignty]]
 **[[dynastic]]** *adj*
