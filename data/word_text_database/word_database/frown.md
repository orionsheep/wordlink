**frown** *vb*
**1** to wrinkle one's brows in worry, anger, or concentration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scowl]], [[glare]], [[glower]], [[make a face]], [[look daggers]], [[knit your brows]], lour *or* lower
**2** **frown on** to disapprove of: unnecessary waste is frowned on
 *n*
**3** the act of frowning
**4** a look of disapproval or displeasure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scowl]], [[glare]], [[glower]], [[dirty look]]
