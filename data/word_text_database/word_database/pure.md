**pure** *adj*
**1** not mixed with any other materials or elements: pure wool
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmixed]], [[real]], [[simple]], [[natural]], [[straight]], [[genuine]], [[neat]], [[authentic]]
**2** free from tainting or polluting matter: pure water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clean]], [[wholesome]], [[sanitary]], [[spotless]], [[sterilized]], [[squeaky-clean]], [[untainted]], [[uncontaminated]]
**3** [[innocent]]: pure love
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[innocent]], [[modest]], [[good]], [[moral]], [[impeccable]], [[righteous]], [[virtuous]], [[squeaky-clean]]
**4** [[complete]]: Pamela's presence on that particular flight was pure chance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complete]], [[total]], [[perfect]], [[absolute]], [[sheer]], [[patent]], [[utter]], [[outright]]
**5** (of a subject) studied in its theoretical aspects rather than for its practical applications: pure mathematics
**6** of [[unmixed]] descent: a pure Dalmatian
 **[[purely]]** *adv* 
 **[[pureness]]** *n*
