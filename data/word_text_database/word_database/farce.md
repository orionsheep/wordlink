**farce** *n*
**1** a humorous play involving characters in unlikely and ridiculous situations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comedy]], [[satire]], [[slapstick]], [[burlesque]], [[buffoonery]]
**2** the style of [[comedy]] of this kind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comedy]], [[satire]], [[slapstick]], [[burlesque]], [[buffoonery]]
**3** a ludicrous situation: the game degenerated into farce
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mockery]], [[joke]], [[nonsense]], [[parody]], [[shambles]], [[sham]], [[travesty]]
 **[[farcical]]** *adj* 
 **[[farcically]]** *adv*
