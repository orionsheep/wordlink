**legion** *n*
**1** any large military [[force]]: the French Foreign Legion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[army]], [[company]], [[force]], [[division]], [[troop]], [[brigade]]
**2** (*often pl*) any very large [[number]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[multitude]], [[host]], [[mass]], [[drove]], [[number]], [[horde]], [[myriad]], [[throng]]
**3** an infantry unit in the ancient Roman [[army]] of three to six thousand men
**4** an association of veterans
 **[[legionary]]** *adj*, *n*
