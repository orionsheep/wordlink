**drug** *n*
**1** any substance used in the treatment, prevention, or diagnosis of disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[medication]], [[medicine]], [[remedy]], [[physic]], [[medicament]]
**2** a chemical substance, such as a [[narcotic]], taken for the effects it produces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[narcotic]] (*slang*), [[stimulant]], opiate
 *vb* **drugging**, **[[drugged]]**
**3** to administer a drug to (a person or animal) in order to induce sleepiness or unconsciousness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[knock out]], [[dope]] (*slang*), [[numb]], [[deaden]], [[stupefy]], [[anaesthetize]]
**4** to mix a drug with (food or drink): who [[drugged]] my wine?
