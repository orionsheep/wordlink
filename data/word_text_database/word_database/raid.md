**raid** *n*
**1** a sudden surprise [[attack]]: a bombing raid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[invasion]], [[foray]], [[sortie]], [[incursion]], [[sally]], [[inroad]]
**2** a surprise visit by police searching for people or goods: a drugs raid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bust]] (*informal*), swoop
 *vb*
**3** to make a raid on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[invade]], [[assault]]
**4** to sneak into (a place) in order to steal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steal from]], [[plunder]], [[pillage]], [[sack]]
 **[[raider]]** *n*
