**torpedo** *n*, *pl* **-does**
**1** a cylindrical self-propelled weapon carrying explosives that is launched from aircraft, ships, or submarines and follows an underwater path to hit its target
 *vb* **-doing**, **-doed**
**2** to attack or hit (a ship) with one or a number of torpedoes
**3** to destroy or wreck: the Prime Minister warned his party against torpedoing the bill
