**improvise** *vb* **-vising**, **-vised**
**1** to do or make quickly from whatever is available, without previous planning
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[devise]], [[contrive]], [[concoct]], [[throw together]]
**2** to make up (a piece of music, speech, etc.) as one goes along
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ad-lib]], [[invent]], [[busk]], [[wing it]] (*informal*), [[play it by ear]] (*informal*), [[extemporize]], [[speak off the cuff]] (*informal*) 
 **[[improvisation]]** *n*
