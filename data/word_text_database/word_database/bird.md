**bird** *n*
**1** a two-legged creature with feathers and wings, which lays eggs and can usually fly. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**avian**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feathered friend]], [[fowl]], [[songbird]]
**2** (*Brit* *slang*, *sometimes* *offensive*) a girl or young woman
**3** (*informal*) a person: he's a rare bird
**4** **a bird in the hand** something definite or certain
**5** **birds of a feather** people with the same ideas or interests
**6** **kill two birds with one stone** to accomplish two things with one action
