**restrain** *vb*
**1** to hold (someone) back from some action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold back]], [[control]], [[check]], [[contain]], [[restrict]], [[curb]], [[hamper]], [[hinder]]
**2** to limit or [[restrict]]: restrain any tendency to impulse-buy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[inhibit]]
**3** to deprive (someone) of liberty
