**age** *n*
**1** the length of [[time]] that a person or thing has existed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[years]], [[days]], [[generation]], [[lifetime]], [[length of existence]]
**2** a [[period]] or state of human life
**3** the latter part of human life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[old age]], [[experience]], [[maturity]], [[seniority]], [[majority]], [[senility]], [[decline]], [[advancing years]]
**4** a [[period]] of history marked by some feature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[time]], [[day]](s), [[period]], [[generation]], [[era]], [[epoch]]
**5** **ages** (*informal*) a long [[time]]
**6** **come of age** to become legally responsible for one's actions (usually at 18 [[years]])
 *vb* **ageing*** or ***aging**, **aged**
**7** to become old: skin type changes as one ages
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grow old]], [[decline]], [[weather]], [[fade]], [[deteriorate]], [[wither]]
**8** to appear or cause to appear older: the [[years]] had not aged her in any way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mature]], [[season]], [[condition]], [[soften]], [[mellow]], [[ripen]]
