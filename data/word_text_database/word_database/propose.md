**propose** *vb* **-posing**, **-posed**
**1** to [[put forward]] (a [[plan]]) for consideration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put forward]], [[present]], [[suggest]], [[advance]], [[submit]]
**2** to [[nominate]] (someone) for a position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nominate]], [[name]], [[present]], [[recommend]]
**3** to [[intend]] (to do something): I don't propose to waste any more time discussing it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intend]], [[mean]], [[plan]], [[aim]], [[design]], [[scheme]], [[have in mind]]
**4** to ask people to drink (a toast)
**5** (often foll. by *to*) to make an offer of marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offer marriage]], [[pop the question]] (*informal*), [[ask for someone's hand]] (in marriage)
