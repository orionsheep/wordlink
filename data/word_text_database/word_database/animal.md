**animal** *n*
**1** (*zool*) any living being that is capable of voluntary movement and possesses specialized sense organs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creature]], [[beast]], [[brute]]
**2** any living being other than a human being
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creature]], [[beast]], [[brute]]
**3** any living being with four legs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creature]], [[beast]], [[brute]]
**4** a cruel or coarse person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brute]], [[devil]], [[monster]], [[savage]], [[beast]], [[villain]], [[barbarian]]
**5** (*facetious*) a person or thing: there's no such animal
 *adj*
**6** of or from animals
**7** of or relating to [[physical]] needs or desires
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[physical]], [[gross]], [[bodily]], [[sensual]], [[carnal]], [[brutish]], [[bestial]], [[animalistic]]
