**fabric** *n*
**1** any [[cloth]] made from yarn or fibres by weaving or knitting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cloth]], [[material]], [[stuff]], [[textile]], [[web]]
**2** the [[structure]] that holds a system together: the fabric of society
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[framework]], [[structure]], [[make-up]], [[organization]], [[frame]], [[foundations]], [[construction]], [[constitution]]
**3** the walls, floor, and roof of a building
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[foundations]], [[construction]], [[framework]]
