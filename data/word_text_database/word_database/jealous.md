**jealous** *adj*
**1** [[suspicious]] or fearful of being displaced by a rival
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suspicious]], [[protective]], [[wary]], [[doubtful]], [[sceptical]], [[vigilant]], [[watchful]], [[possessive]]
**2** [[envious]]: I was jealous of the girls who had boyfriends
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[envious]], [[grudging]], [[resentful]], [[green]], [[green with envy]], [[desirous]], [[covetous]]
**3** resulting from jealousy: my jealous tears
 **[[jealously]]** *adv*
