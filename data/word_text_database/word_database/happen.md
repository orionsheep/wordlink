**happen** *vb*
**1** to [[take place]]; [[occur]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occur]], [[take place]], [[come about]], [[result]], [[develop]], [[transpire]] (*informal*), [[come to pass]]
**2** to [[chance]] (to be or do something): I happen to know him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[turn out]] (*informal*)
**3** to be the case, esp. by [[chance]]: it happens that I know him
**4** **happen to** (of some unforeseen event, such as death) to be the experience or fate of: if anything happens to me you will know
