**unfortunate** *adj*
**1** caused or accompanied by bad luck: an unfortunate coincidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disastrous]], [[calamitous]], [[adverse]], [[ill-fated]]
**2** having bad luck: my unfortunate daughter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unlucky]], [[unhappy]], [[doomed]], [[cursed]], [[unsuccessful]], [[hapless]], [[wretched]]
**3** [[regrettable]] or [[unsuitable]]: an unfortunate choice of phrase
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regrettable]], [[deplorable]], [[lamentable]], [[unsuitable]], unbecoming
 *n*
**4** an [[unlucky]] person
 **[[unfortunately]]** *adv*
