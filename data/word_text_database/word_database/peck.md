**peck** *vb*
**1** to [[strike]] or [[pick]] up with the beak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pick]], [[hit]], [[strike]], [[tap]], [[poke]], [[jab]], [[prick]]
**2** (*informal*) to [[kiss]] (a person) quickly and lightly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kiss]], [[plant a kiss]], [[give someone a smacker]], give someone a peck *or* [[kiss]]
**3** **peck at** to eat slowly and reluctantly: pecking away at your lunch
 *n*
**4** a quick light blow from a bird's beak
**5** a mark made by such a blow
**6** (*informal*) a quick light [[kiss]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kiss]], [[smacker]], [[osculation]] (*rare*)
