**monument** *n*
**1** something, such as a statue or building, erected in [[commemoration]] of a person or event
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[memorial]], [[cairn]], [[marker]], [[shrine]], [[tombstone]], [[mausoleum]], [[commemoration]], [[headstone]]
**2** an ancient building which is regarded as an important part of a country's history
**3** an exceptional example of the results of something: the whole town is a monument to bad sixties' architecture
