**camouflage** (**kam**-moo-flahzh) *n*
**1** the use of natural surroundings or artificial aids to [[conceal]] or [[disguise]] something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: protective colouring
 *vb* **-flaging**, **-flaged**
**2** to [[conceal]] by camouflage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disguise]], [[cover]], [[screen]], [[hide]], [[mask]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]], [[veil]]
