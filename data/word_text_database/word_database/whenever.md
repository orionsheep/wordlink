**whenever** *conj*
**1** at every or any time that: the filly was trained to stop whenever a jockey used a whip
 *adv*
**2** no matter when: I am eager to come whenever you suggest
**3** (*informal*) at an unknown or unspecified time: the 16th, 17th, or whenever
**4** an intensive form of *when*, used in questions: if we can't exercise restraint now, whenever can we?
