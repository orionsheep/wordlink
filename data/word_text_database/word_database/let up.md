**[[let up]]** *vb*
**1** to diminish or stop
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[let up]]**: stop, diminish, decrease, subside, relax, ease (up), moderate, lessen
**2** (foll. by *on*) (*informal*) to be less harsh (towards someone)
 *n* **let-up**
**3** (*informal*) a lessening: there has been no let-up in the war
