**stress** *n*
**1** mental, emotional, or physical [[strain]] or [[tension]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strain]], [[pressure]], [[worry]], [[tension]], [[burden]], [[anxiety]], [[trauma]]
**2** special [[emphasis]] or [[significance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[emphasis]], [[significance]], [[force]], [[weight]]
**3** [[emphasis]] placed upon a syllable by pronouncing it more loudly than those that surround it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accent]], [[beat]], [[emphasis]], [[accentuation]]
**4** (*physics*) [[force]] producing a change in shape or volume
 *vb*
**5** to [[give emphasis to]] (a point or subject): she stressed how difficult it had been
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[emphasize]], [[underline]], [[dwell on]]
**6** to pronounce (a word or syllable) more loudly than those surrounding it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[place the emphasis on]], [[emphasize]], [[give emphasis to]], [[lay emphasis upon]]
 **[[stressful]]** *adj*
