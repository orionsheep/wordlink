**spend** *vb* **[[spending]]**, **spent**
**1** to [[pay out]] (money)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pay out]], [[fork out]] (*slang*), [[expend]], [[disburse]]
**2** to [[pass]] (time) in a specific way or place: I spent a year in Budapest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pass]], [[fill]], [[occupy]], [[while away]]
**3** to concentrate (effort) on an activity: a lot of energy was spent organizing the holiday
**4** to [[use up]] completely: the hurricane spent its force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[use up]], [[waste]], [[squander]], [[empty]], [[drain]], [[exhaust]], [[consume]], [[run through]]
 **[[spending]]** *n*
