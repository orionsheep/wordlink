**switch** *n*
**1** a device for opening or closing an electric circuit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[button]], [[lever]], [[on/off device]]
**2** a sudden quick [[change]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[shift]], [[reversal]]
**3** an [[exchange]] or [[swap]]
**4** a flexible rod or twig, used for punishment
**5** (*US** & **Canad*) a pair of movable rails for diverting moving trains from one track to another
 *vb*
**6** to [[change]] quickly and suddenly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[shift]], [[divert]], [[deviate]]
**7** to [[exchange]] (places) or [[swap]] (something for something else)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exchange]], [[swap]], [[substitute]]
**8** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to transfer (rolling stock) from one railway track to another
 See also **switch on**
