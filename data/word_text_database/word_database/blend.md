**blend** *vb*
**1** to [[mix]] or [[mingle]] (components)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mix]], [[join]], [[combine]], [[compound]], [[merge]], [[unite]], [[mingle]], [[amalgamate]]
**2** to [[mix]] (different varieties of tea, whisky, etc.)
**3** to look good together; harmonize
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go well]], [[match]], [[fit]], [[suit]], [[go with]], [[correspond]], [[complement]], [[coordinate]]
**4** (esp. of colours) to shade gradually into each other
 *n*
**5** a [[mixture]] produced by blending
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mixture]], [[mix]], [[combination]], [[compound]], [[brew]], [[union]], [[synthesis]], [[alloy]]
