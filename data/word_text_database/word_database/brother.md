**brother** *n*
**1** a man or boy with the same parents as another person. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**fraternal**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[male sibling]]
**2** a man belonging to the same group, trade union, etc. as another or others; fellow member
**3** comrade; friend
**4** (*Christianity*) a member of a male [[religious]] order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[monk]], [[cleric]], [[friar]], [[religious]]
