**woe** *n*
**1** (*literary*) intense [[grief]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misery]], [[distress]], [[grief]], [[agony]], [[gloom]], [[sadness]], [[sorrow]], [[anguish]]
**2** **woes** misfortunes or problems: economic woes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[problem]], [[grief]], [[misery]], [[sorrow]]
**3** **woe betide someone** someone will or would experience misfortune: woe betide anyone who got in his way
