**recognition** *n*
**1** the act of recognizing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[identification]], [[recollection]], [[discovery]], [[remembrance]]
**2** [[acceptance]] or acknowledgment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acceptance]], [[admission]], [[allowance]], [[confession]]
**3** formal acknowledgment of a government or of the independence of a country
**4** **in recognition of** as a token of thanks for
