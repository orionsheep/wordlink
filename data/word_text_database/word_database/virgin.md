**virgin** *n*
**1** a person who has never had sexual intercourse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: maiden (*archaic*, *literary*), [[girl]] (*archaic*)
**2** a person who is inexperienced in a specified field: a ski virgin
 *adj*
**3** not having had sexual intercourse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pure]], [[chaste]], [[immaculate]], [[virginal]], [[vestal]], [[uncorrupted]], [[undefiled]]
**4** fresh and unused: he found a scrap of virgin paper in a sea of memoranda
**5** not yet cultivated, explored, or exploited by people: virgin territory
