**inhibit** *vb*
**1** to [[restrain]] or [[hinder]] (an impulse or desire)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hinder]], [[check]], [[frustrate]], [[curb]], [[restrain]], [[constrain]], [[obstruct]], [[impede]]
**2** to prohibit or [[prevent]]: an attempt to inhibit nuclear proliferation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prevent]], [[stop]], [[frustrate]]
**3** (*chem*) to [[stop]], [[prevent]], or decrease the rate of (a chemical reaction)
 **[[inhibited]]** *adj* 
 **[[inhibitor]]** *n*
