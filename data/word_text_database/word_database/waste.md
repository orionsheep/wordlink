**waste** *vb* **wasting**, **wasted**
**1** to use up thoughtlessly, carelessly, or unsuccessfully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squander]], [[throw away]], [[blow]] (*slang*), [[lavish]], [[misuse]], [[dissipate]], [[fritter away]]
**2** to fail to take advantage of: let's not waste an opportunity to see the children
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squander]], [[throw away]], [[blow]] (*slang*), [[lavish]], [[misuse]], [[dissipate]], [[fritter away]]
**3** **be wasted on** to be too good for; not be appreciated by: fine brandy is wasted on you
**4** **[[waste away]]** to lose one's strength or health: wasting away from unrequited love
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[waste away]]**: decline, dwindle, wither, fade, crumble, decay, wane, wear out
 *n*
**5** the act of wasting something or the state of being wasted: a waste of time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squandering]], [[misuse]], [[extravagance]], [[frittering away]], [[dissipation]], [[wastefulness]], [[prodigality]]
**6** something that is left over because it is in excess of requirements
**7** [[rubbish]]: toxic waste
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rubbish]], [[refuse]], [[debris]], [[scrap]], [[litter]], [[garbage]] (*chiefly* *US*), [[trash]] (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*), [[leftovers]]
**8** (*physiol*) matter discharged from the body as faeces or urine
**9** **wastes** a region that is [[wild]] or [[uncultivated]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desert]], [[wilderness]], wasteland
 *adj*
**10** rejected as being [[useless]], [[unwanted]], or [[worthless]]: waste products
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unwanted]], [[useless]], [[worthless]], [[unused]], [[leftover]], [[superfluous]], [[unusable]], [[supernumerary]]
**11** not cultivated or productive: waste ground
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncultivated]], [[wild]], [[bare]], [[barren]], [[empty]], [[desolate]], [[unproductive]], [[uninhabited]]
**12** (*physiol*) discharged from the body as faeces or urine: waste matter
**13** **lay waste** *or ***lay waste to** to devastate or destroy: the Bikini atoll, laid waste by nuclear tests
