**prohibition** *n*
**1** the act of forbidding
**2** a legal [[ban]] on the sale or drinking of alcohol
**3** an order or decree that forbids
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[boycott]], [[embargo]], [[bar]], [[veto]], [[prevention]], [[exclusion]], [[injunction]], restraining order (*US*, *law*) 
 **[[prohibitionist]]** *n*
