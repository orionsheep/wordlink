**lesson** *n*
**1**
**A** a single [[period]] of [[instruction]] in a subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[class]], [[schooling]], [[period]], [[teaching]], [[coaching]], [[session]], [[instruction]], [[lecture]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[example]], [[warning]], [[message]], [[moral]], [[deterrent]]
