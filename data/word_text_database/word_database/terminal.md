**terminal** *adj*
**1** (of an illness) ending in death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fatal]], [[deadly]], [[lethal]], [[killing]], [[mortal]], [[incurable]], [[inoperable]], [[untreatable]]
**2** situated at an end, [[terminus]], or boundary: the terminal joints of the fingers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[final]], [[last]], [[closing]], [[finishing]], [[concluding]], [[ultimate]], [[terminating]]
**3** (*informal*) extreme or severe: terminal boredom
 *n*
**4** a place where vehicles, passengers, or goods begin or end a journey: the ferry terminal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terminus]], [[station]], [[depot]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[end of the line]]
**5** a point at which current enters or leaves an electrical device
**6** (*computers*) a device, usually a keyboard and a visual display unit, having input/output links with a computer
 **[[terminally]]** *adv*
