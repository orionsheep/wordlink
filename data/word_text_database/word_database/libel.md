**libel** *n*
**1** (*law*) the publication of something false which damages a person's reputation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defamation]], [[misrepresentation]], [[denigration]], [[smear]], [[calumny]], [[aspersion]]
**2** any damaging or unflattering representation or statement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defamation]], [[misrepresentation]], [[denigration]], [[smear]], [[calumny]], [[aspersion]]
 *vb* **-belling**, **-belled** (*US*) **-beling**, **-beled**
**3** (*law*) to make or publish a false damaging statement or representation about (a person)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[defame]], [[smear]], [[slur]], [[blacken]], [[malign]], [[denigrate]], [[revile]], [[vilify]]
 **[[libellous]]** * or * (*US*) **[[libelous]]** *adj*
