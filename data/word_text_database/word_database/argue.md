**argue** *vb* **-guing**, **-gued**
**1** to try to prove by presenting reasons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[claim]], [[reason]], [[challenge]], [[insist]], [[maintain]], [[allege]], [[assert]], [[uphold]]
**2** to [[debate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discuss]], [[debate]], [[dispute]]
**3** to [[quarrel]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quarrel]], [[fight]], [[row]], [[clash]], [[dispute]], [[disagree]], [[squabble]], [[bicker]]
**4** to persuade: we argued her out of going
**5** to suggest: her looks argue despair
 **[[arguable]]** *adj* 
 **[[arguably]]** *adv*
