**clinch** *vb*
**1** to settle (an argument or agreement) decisively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secure]], [[close]], [[confirm]], [[conclude]], [[seal]], [[sew up]] (*informal*), [[close out]], [[set the seal on]]
**2** to [[secure]] (a nail) by bending the protruding point over
**3** to engage in a clinch, as in boxing or wrestling
 *n*
**4** the act of clinching
**5** (*boxing*, *wrestling*) a movement in which one or both competitors hold on to the other to avoid punches or regain wind
**6** (*slang*) a lovers' embrace
