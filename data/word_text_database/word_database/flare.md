**flare** *vb* **flaring**, **[[flared]]**
**1** to burn with an unsteady or sudden bright [[flame]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blaze]], [[flame]], [[glare]], [[flicker]], [[burn up]]
**2** (of temper, violence, or trouble) to break out suddenly
**3** to [[spread]] outwards from a narrow to a wider shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[widen]], [[spread]], [[broaden]], [[spread out]], [[dilate]], splay
 *n*
**4** an unsteady [[flame]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flame]], [[burst]], [[flash]], [[blaze]], [[glare]], [[flicker]]
**5** a sudden [[burst]] of [[flame]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flame]], [[burst]], [[flash]], [[blaze]], [[glare]], [[flicker]]
**6**
**A** a [[blaze]] of light used to illuminate, signal distress, alert, etc.
 **B** the device producing such a [[blaze]]
**7** **flares** trousers with legs that flare out at the bottom
 **[[flared]]** *adj*
