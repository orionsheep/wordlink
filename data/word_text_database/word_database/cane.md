**cane** *n*
**1** the long flexible stems of the bamboo or any similar plant
**2** strips of such stems, woven to make wickerwork
**3** a bamboo stem tied to a garden plant to support it
**4** a flexible rod used to beat someone
**5** a slender walking stick
 *vb* **caning**, **caned**
**6** to beat with a cane
