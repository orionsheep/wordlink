**divide** *vb* **-viding**, **-vided**
**1** to [[separate]] into parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[split]], [[segregate]], [[bisect]]
**2** to [[share]] or be shared out in parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[share]], [[distribute]], [[allocate]], [[dispense]], [[allot]], [[mete]], [[deal out]]
**3** to disagree or [[cause to disagree]]: experts are divided over the plan
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[split]], [[break up]], [[come between]], [[estrange]], [[cause to disagree]]
**4** to keep apart or be a boundary between
**5** to categorize or classify
**6** to calculate how many times one number can be contained in another
 *n*
**7** a division or [[split]]
**8** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) an area of high ground separating drainage basins
