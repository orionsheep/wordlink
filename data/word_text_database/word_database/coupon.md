**coupon** *n*
**1** a piece of paper entitling the holder to a discount or free gift
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slip]], [[ticket]], [[certificate]], [[token]], [[voucher]], [[card]]
**2** a detachable [[slip]] that can be used as a commercial order form
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slip]], [[ticket]], [[certificate]], [[token]], [[voucher]], [[card]]
**3** (*Brit*) a detachable entry form for a competition
