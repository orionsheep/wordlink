**referee** *n*
**1** the [[umpire]] in various sports, such as football and boxing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[umpire]], [[umpie]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[judge]], [[ref]] (*informal*), [[arbiter]], [[arbitrator]], [[adjudicator]]
**2** a person who is willing to provide a reference for someone for a job
**3** a person referred to for a decision or opinion in a dispute
 *vb* **-reeing**, **-reed**
**4** to act as a referee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[umpire]], [[judge]], [[mediate]], [[adjudicate]], [[arbitrate]]
