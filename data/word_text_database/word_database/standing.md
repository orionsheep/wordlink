**standing** *adj*
**1** [[permanent]], [[fixed]], or [[lasting]]: it was a standing joke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[permanent]], [[lasting]], [[fixed]], [[regular]]
**2** used to stand in or on: standing room only
**3** (*athletics*) (of a jump or the start of a race) begun from a standing [[position]]
 *n*
**4** social or financial [[status]] or [[reputation]]: her international standing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[status]], [[position]], [[footing]], [[rank]], [[reputation]], [[eminence]], [[repute]]
**5** [[duration]]: a friendship of at least ten years' standing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[duration]], [[existence]], [[continuance]]
