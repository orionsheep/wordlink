**disappointment** *n*
**1** the feeling of being disappointed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regret]], [[discontent]], [[dissatisfaction]], [[disillusionment]], [[chagrin]], [[disenchantment]], [[dejection]], [[despondency]]
**2** a person or thing that disappoints
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[letdown]], [[blow]], [[setback]], [[misfortune]], [[calamity]], [[choker]] (*informal*)
