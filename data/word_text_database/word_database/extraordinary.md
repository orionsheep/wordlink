**extraordinary** *adj*
**1** very [[unusual]] or surprising: the award of a Victoria Cross for an act of extraordinary bravery under hostile fire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unusual]], [[strange]], [[remarkable]], [[uncommon]]
**2** having some special or extreme quality: an extraordinary first novel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remarkable]], [[outstanding]], [[amazing]], [[fantastic]] (*informal*), [[astonishing]], [[exceptional]], [[phenomenal]], [[extremely good]]
**3** (of a meeting, ambassador, etc.) specially called or appointed to deal with one particular topic
 **[[extraordinarily]]** *adv*
