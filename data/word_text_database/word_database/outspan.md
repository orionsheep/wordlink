**outspan** (*S African*) *n*
**1** an area on a farm kept available for travellers to rest and refresh their animals
 *vb* **-spanning**, **-spanned**
**2** to unharness or unyoke (animals)
**3** to [[relax]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relax]], [[chill out]] (*slang*), [[take it easy]], [[loosen up]], [[put your feet up]]
