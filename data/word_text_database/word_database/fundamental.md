◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[central]], [[key]], [[basic]], [[essential]], [[primary]], [[principal]], [[cardinal]]
**2** [[basic]]: a fundamental error
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[basic]], [[essential]], [[underlying]], [[profound]], [[elementary]], rudimentary
 *n*
**3** **fundamentals** the most important and [[basic]] parts of a subject or activity
