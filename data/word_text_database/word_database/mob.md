**mob** *n*
**1** a riotous or disorderly [[crowd]] of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowd]], [[pack]], [[mass]], [[host]], [[drove]], [[flock]], [[swarm]], [[horde]]
**2** (*informal*) any [[group]] of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gang]], [[group]], [[set]], [[lot]], [[crew]] (*informal*)
**3** the masses
**4** (*slang*) a [[gang]] of criminals
 *vb* **mobbing**, **mobbed**
**5** to attack in a [[group]] resembling a mob
**6** to [[surround]] in a [[crowd]] to acclaim or attack: she was mobbed by her fans when she left the theatre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surround]], [[besiege]], [[jostle]], [[fall on]], [[set upon]], [[crowd around]], [[swarm around]]
