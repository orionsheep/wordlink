**dumb** *adj*
**1** (*offensive*) lacking the power to speak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unable to speak]], [[mute]] (*old-fashioned*, *offensive*)
**2** lacking the power of human speech: the event was denounced as cruelty to dumb animals
**3** temporarily [[unable to speak]]: I was struck dumb when I heard the news
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[silent]], [[mute]], [[speechless]], [[tongue-tied]], [[wordless]], [[voiceless]], [[soundless]], [[mum]]
**4** done or performed without speech: I looked at her in dumb puzzlement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[silent]], [[mute]], [[speechless]], [[tongue-tied]], [[wordless]], [[voiceless]], [[soundless]], [[mum]]
**5** (*informal*) [[stupid]] or slow to understand
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stupid]], [[thick]], [[dull]], [[foolish]], [[dense]], [[unintelligent]], [[asinine]], dim-witted (*informal*)
 See also **dumb down**
 **[[dumbly]]** *adv*
