**volley** *n*
**1** the simultaneous firing of several weapons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[barrage]], [[blast]], [[burst]], [[shower]], [[hail]], [[bombardment]], [[salvo]], [[fusillade]]
**2** the bullets fired
**3** a [[burst]] of questions or critical comments
**4** (*sport*) a stroke or kick at a moving ball before it hits the ground
 *vb*
**5** to fire (weapons) in a volley
**6** (*sport*) to hit or kick (a moving ball) before it hits the ground
