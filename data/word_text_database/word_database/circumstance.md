**circumstance** *n*
**1** an occurrence or [[condition]] that accompanies or influences a person or event
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condition]], [[situation]], [[contingency]], [[state of affairs]], [[lie of the land]]
**2** unplanned events and situations which cannot be controlled: a victim of circumstance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chance]], [[the times]], [[accident]], [[fortune]], [[luck]], [[fate]], [[destiny]], [[providence]]
**3** **pomp and circumstance** formal display or ceremony
**4** **under *or* in no circumstances** in no case; never
**5** **under the circumstances** because of conditions
