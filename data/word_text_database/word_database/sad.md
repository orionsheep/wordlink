**sad** *adj* **sadder**, **saddest**
**1** feeling sorrow; [[unhappy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unhappy]], [[down]], [[low]], [[blue]], [[depressed]], [[melancholy]], [[mournful]], [[dejected]]
**2** causing, suggesting, or expressing sorrow: a sad story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tragic]], [[moving]], [[upsetting]], [[depressing]], [[dismal]], [[pathetic]], [[poignant]], [[harrowing]]
**3** deplorably [[bad]]: the garden was in a sad state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deplorable]], [[bad]], [[sorry]], [[terrible]], [[unfortunate]], [[regrettable]], [[lamentable]], [[wretched]]
**4** [[regrettable]]: it's rather sad he can't be with us
**5** (*Brit* *informal*) ridiculously [[pathetic]]: a sad, boring little wimp
 *n*
**6** **pack a sad** (*NZ* *slang*) to strongly express [[sadness]] or displeasure
 **[[sadly]]** *adv* 
 **[[sadness]]** *n*
