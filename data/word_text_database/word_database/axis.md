**axis** (**ax**-iss) *n*, *pl* **axes** (**ax**-eez)
**1** a real or imaginary line about which a body can rotate or about which an object or geometrical construction is symmetrical
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pivot]], [[shaft]], [[axle]], [[spindle]], [[centre line]]
**2** one of two or three reference lines used in coordinate geometry to locate a point in a plane or in space
