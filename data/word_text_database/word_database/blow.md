**blow**^2 *n*
**1** a powerful or heavy [[stroke]] with the fist, a weapon, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[knock]], [[stroke]], [[punch]], [[bang]], [[sock]] (*slang*), [[smack]], [[thump]], [[clout]] (*informal*)
**2** a sudden [[setback]]: the scheme was dealt a blow by the introduction of martial law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[setback]], [[shock]], [[disaster]], [[reverse]], [[disappointment]], [[catastrophe]], [[misfortune]], [[bombshell]]
**3** an attacking action: a blow for freedom
**4** **come to blows**
**A** to fight
 **B** to result in a fight
