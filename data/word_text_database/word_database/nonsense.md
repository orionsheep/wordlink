**nonsense** *n*
**1** something that has or makes no sense
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rubbish]], [[hot air]] (*informal*), [[twaddle]], [[drivel]], [[tripe]] (*informal*), [[gibberish]], [[claptrap]] (*old-fashioned*), [[double Dutch]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[bizzo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[bull's wool]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
**2** unintelligible language
**3** foolish behaviour: she'll stand no nonsense
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idiocy]], [[stupidity]]
 **[[nonsensical]]** *adj*
