**savage** *adj*
**1** [[wild]] and [[untamed]]: savage tigers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wild]], [[fierce]], [[ferocious]], [[unbroken]], [[feral]], [[untamed]], [[undomesticated]]
**2** [[fierce]] and [[cruel]]: savage cries
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cruel]], [[brutal]], [[vicious]], [[fierce]], [[harsh]], [[ruthless]], [[ferocious]], [[sadistic]]
**3** (of peoples) [[uncivilized]] or [[primitive]]: savage tribes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[primitive]], [[undeveloped]], [[uncultivated]], [[uncivilized]]
**4** rude, crude, and violent: savage behaviour on the terraces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cruel]], [[brutal]], [[vicious]], [[fierce]], [[harsh]], [[ruthless]], [[ferocious]], [[sadistic]]
**5** (of terrain) [[wild]] and [[uncultivated]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncultivated]], [[rugged]], [[unspoilt]], [[uninhabited]], [[rough]], [[uncivilized]]
 *n*
**6** a member of an [[uncivilized]] or [[primitive]] society
**7** a [[fierce]] or [[vicious]] person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lout]], [[yob]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[barbarian]], [[yahoo]], [[hoon]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[boor]], [[cougan]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[scozza]] (*Austral* *slang*), bogan (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb* **-vaging**, **-vaged**
**8** to [[attack]] ferociously and wound: savaged by a [[wild]] dog
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[maul]], [[tear]], [[claw]], [[attack]], [[mangle]], [[lacerate]], [[mangulate]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**9** to criticize extremely severely: savaged by the press for incompetence
 **[[savagely]]** *adv*
