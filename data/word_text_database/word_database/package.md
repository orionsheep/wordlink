**package** *n*
**1** a small [[parcel]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[parcel]], [[box]], [[container]], [[packet]], [[carton]]
**2** Also: **package deal** a deal in which separate items are presented together as a [[unit]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collection]], [[lot]], [[unit]], [[combination]], [[compilation]]
**3** (*US** & **Canad*) same as **[[packet]]**
 *vb* **-aging**, **-aged**
**4** to put (something) into a package
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pack]], [[box]], [[parcel]] (up)
 **[[packaging]]** *n*
