**disciple** (diss-**sipe**-pl) *n*
**1** a [[follower]] of the doctrines of a teacher
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[follower]], [[student]], [[supporter]], [[pupil]], [[devotee]], [[apostle]], [[adherent]]
**2** (*Christianity*) one of the personal followers of Christ during his earthly life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[apostle]]
