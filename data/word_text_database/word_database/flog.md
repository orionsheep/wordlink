**flog** *vb* **[[flogging]]**, **flogged**
**1** to [[beat]] harshly, esp. with a [[whip]] or stick
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[whip]], [[lash]], [[thrash]], [[whack]], [[scourge]], [[hit hard]], [[trounce]]
**2** (sometimes foll. by *off*) (*informal*) to sell
**3** (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) to steal
**4** **flog a dead horse** (*chiefly* *Brit*) to waste one's energy
 **[[flogging]]** *n*
