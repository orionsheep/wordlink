**environment** (en-**vire**-on-ment) *n*
**1** the [[surroundings]] in which a person, animal, or plant lives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surroundings]], [[setting]], [[conditions]], [[situation]], [[medium]], [[circumstances]], [[background]], [[atmosphere]]
**2** (*ecology*) **the environment** the natural world of land, sea, air, plants, and animals: nuclear waste must be prevented from leaking into the environment
 **[[environmental]]** *adj*
