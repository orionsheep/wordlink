**demon** *n*
**1** an [[evil spirit]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evil spirit]], [[devil]], [[fiend]], [[goblin]], [[ghoul]], [[malignant spirit]], [[atua]] (*NZ*), [[wairua]] (*NZ*)
**2** a person, obsession, etc. thought of as evil or persistently tormenting
**3** a person extremely skilful in or devoted to a given activity: a demon at pool
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wizard]], [[master]], [[ace]] (*informal*), [[fiend]] (*informal*) 
 **[[demonic]]** *adj*
