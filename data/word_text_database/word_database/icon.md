**icon** * or ***ikon** *n*
**1** a picture of Christ, the Virgin Mary, or a saint, venerated in the Orthodox Church
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representation]], [[image]], [[likeness]], [[avatar]], [[favicon]] (*computers*)
**2** a picture on a computer screen representing a computer function that can be activated by moving the cursor over it
**3** a person or thing regarded as a symbol of a belief or cultural movement: a feminist icon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idol]], [[hero]], [[superstar]]
