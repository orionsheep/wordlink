**disorder** *n*
**1** a state of [[untidiness]] and disorganization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untidiness]], [[mess]], [[confusion]], [[chaos]], [[havoc]] (*informal*), [[muddle]], [[clutter]], [[shambles]], [[disarray]]
**2** public violence or rioting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disturbance]], [[riot]], [[turmoil]], [[unrest]], [[uproar]], [[commotion]], [[unruliness]], [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**3** an [[illness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[illness]], [[disease]], [[complaint]], [[condition]], [[sickness]], [[ailment]], [[affliction]], [[malady]]
 **[[disordered]]** *adj*
