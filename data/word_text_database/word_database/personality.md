**personality** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** (*psychol*) the distinctive characteristics which make an individual unique
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nature]], [[character]], [[make-up]], [[identity]], [[temperament]], [[disposition]], [[individuality]]
**2** the distinctive [[character]] of a person which makes him or her socially attractive: some people find him lacking in personality and a bit colourless
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[character]], [[charm]], [[attraction]], [[charisma]], [[magnetism]]
**3** a well-known person in a certain field; [[celebrity]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[celebrity]], [[star]], [[notable]], [[household name]], [[famous name]], [[personage]], [[megastar]] (*informal*)
**4** a remarkable person: she is a personality to be reckoned with
**5** (*often pl*) an offensive personal remark: the argument never degenerated into personalities
