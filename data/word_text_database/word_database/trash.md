**trash** *n*
**1** foolish ideas or talk; [[nonsense]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonsense]], [[rubbish]], [[rot]], [[drivel]], [[twaddle]], [[tripe]] (*informal*), [[moonshine]], [[hogwash]] (*informal*), kak (*S African* *vulgar*, *slang*), [[bizzo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[bull's wool]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **NZ** & **S African*) unwanted objects; [[rubbish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[litter]], [[refuse]], [[waste]], [[rubbish]], [[junk]] (*informal*), [[garbage]] (*chiefly* *US*), [[dross]]
**3** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad** & **NZ*) a worthless person or group of people
 *vb*
**4** (*slang*) to attack or destroy maliciously: we've never trashed a hotel room
 **[[trashy]]** *adj*
