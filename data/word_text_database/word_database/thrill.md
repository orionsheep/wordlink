**thrill** *n*
**1** a sudden sensation of excitement and [[pleasure]]: he felt a thrill of excitement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pleasure]], [[kick]] (*informal*), [[buzz]] (*slang*), [[high]], [[stimulation]], [[tingle]], [[titillation]]
**2** a situation producing such a sensation: all the thrills of rafting the meandering Dordogne
**3** a sudden trembling sensation caused by fear or emotional shock
 *vb*
**4** to feel or cause to feel a thrill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excite]], [[stimulate]], [[arouse]], [[move]], [[stir]], [[electrify]], [[titillate]], [[give someone a kick]]
**5** to vibrate or quiver
 **[[thrilling]]** *adj*
