**cloak** *n*
**1** a loose sleeveless outer garment, fastened at the throat and falling straight from the shoulders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cape]], [[coat]], [[wrap]], [[mantle]] (*archaic*)
**2** something that covers or conceals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covering]], [[layer]], [[blanket]], [[shroud]]
 *vb*
**3** to [[hide]] or [[disguise]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hide]], [[cover]], [[screen]], [[mask]], [[disguise]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]], [[veil]]
**4** to [[cover]] with or as if with a cloak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[coat]], [[wrap]], [[blanket]], [[shroud]], [[envelop]]
