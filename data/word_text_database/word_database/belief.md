**belief** *n*
**1** [[trust]] or [[confidence]]: belief in the free market
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trust]], [[confidence]], [[conviction]]
**2** [[opinion]]; [[conviction]]: it's my firm belief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opinion]], [[feeling]], [[idea]], [[impression]], [[assessment]], [[notion]], [[judgment]], [[point of view]]
**3** a principle, etc. accepted as true, often without proof
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[faith]], [[principles]], [[doctrine]], [[ideology]], [[creed]], [[dogma]], [[tenet]], [[credo]]
**4** religious [[faith]]
