**sacrifice** *n*
**1** a [[surrender]] of something of value in order to gain something more desirable or prevent some evil
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surrender]], [[loss]], [[giving up]], [[rejection]], [[abdication]], [[renunciation]], [[repudiation]], [[forswearing]]
**2** a ritual killing of a person or animal as an [[offering]] to a god
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offering]], [[oblation]]
**3** a symbolic [[offering]] of something to a god
**4** the person or animal killed or offered
 *vb* **-ficing**, **-ficed**
**5** to make a sacrifice (of)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offer]], [[offer up]], [[immolate]] (*literary*)
**6** (*chess*) to permit or force one's opponent to capture (a piece) as a tactical move
 **[[sacrificial]]** *adj*
