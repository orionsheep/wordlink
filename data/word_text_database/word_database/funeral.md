**funeral** *n*
**1** a ceremony at which a dead person is buried or cremated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[burial]], [[committal]], [[laying to rest]], [[cremation]], [[interment]], [[obsequies]], [[entombment]]
**2** **it's your funeral** (*informal*) a mistake has been made and you alone will be responsible for its consequences
 *adj*
**3** of or for a funeral
 **[[funerary]]** *adj*
