**publication** *n*
**1** the [[publishing]] of a printed work
**2** any printed work offered for sale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pamphlet]], [[newspaper]], [[magazine]], [[issue]], [[title]], [[leaflet]], [[brochure]], [[periodical]], [[blog]] (*informal*)
**3** the act of making information known to the public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[publishing]], [[announcement]], [[broadcasting]], [[reporting]], [[declaration]], [[disclosure]], [[proclamation]], [[notification]]
