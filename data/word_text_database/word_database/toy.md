**toy** *n*
**1** an object designed for children to play with, such as a [[doll]] or model car
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plaything]], [[game]], [[doll]]
**2** an object that adults use for entertainment rather than for a serious purpose: I do use my computer: it's not just a toy
 *adj*
**3** being an imitation or model of something for children to play with: a toy aeroplane
**4** (of a dog) of a variety much smaller than is normal for that breed: a toy poodle
