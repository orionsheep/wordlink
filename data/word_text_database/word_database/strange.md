**strange** *adj*
**1** [[odd]] or unexpected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[odd]], [[curious]], [[weird]], [[wonderful]], [[extraordinary]], [[bizarre]], [[peculiar]], [[abnormal]], [[daggy]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**2** not known, seen, or experienced before; [[unfamiliar]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unfamiliar]], [[new]], [[unknown]], [[foreign]], [[novel]], [[alien]], [[exotic]], [[untried]]
**3** **strange to** inexperienced in or unaccustomed to: they are in some degree strange to it
 **[[strangely]]** *adv* 
 **[[strangeness]]** *n*
