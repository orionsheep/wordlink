**bland** *adj*
**1** [[dull]] and uninteresting: the bland predictability of the film
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dull]], [[boring]], [[plain]], [[flat]], [[dreary]], [[run-of-the-mill]], [[uninspiring]], [[humdrum]]
**2** (of food, drink, etc.) [[flavourless]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tasteless]], [[insipid]], [[flavourless]], [[thin]]
**3** smooth in manner: he looked at his visitor with a bland smile
 **[[blandly]]** *adv*
