**perceive** *vb* **-ceiving**, **-ceived**
**1** to become aware of (something) through the senses
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[see]], [[notice]], [[note]], [[identify]], [[discover]], [[spot]], [[observe]], [[recognize]]
**2** to [[understand]] or [[grasp]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[understand]], [[gather]], [[see]], [[learn]], [[realize]], [[grasp]], [[comprehend]], [[suss]] (out) (*slang*) 
 **[[perceivable]]** *adj*
