**wardrobe** *n*
**1** a tall [[cupboard]], with a rail or hooks on which to hang [[clothes]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clothes cupboard]], [[cupboard]], [[closet]] (*US*), [[cabinet]]
**2** the total collection of articles of clothing belonging to one person: your autumn wardrobe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clothes]], [[apparel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[attire]]
**3** the collection of costumes belonging to a theatre or theatrical company
