**example** *n*
**1** a [[specimen]] that is typical of its group; [[sample]]: a fine example of Georgian architecture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instance]], [[specimen]], [[case]], [[sample]], [[illustration]], [[particular case]], [[particular instance]], [[typical case]]
**2** a particular event, object, or person that demonstrates a point or supports an argument, theory, etc.: Germany is a good example of how federalism works in practice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[illustration]], [[model]], [[ideal]], [[standard]], [[prototype]], [[paradigm]], [[archetype]], [[paragon]]
**3** a person, action, or thing that is worthy of imitation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[illustration]], [[model]], [[ideal]], [[standard]], [[prototype]], [[paradigm]], [[archetype]], [[paragon]]
**4** a punishment or the person punished regarded as a [[warning]] to others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[warning]], [[lesson]], [[caution]], [[deterrent]]
**5** **for example** as an [[illustration]]
