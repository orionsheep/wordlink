**ramble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to [[walk]] for relaxation, sometimes with no particular direction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[walk]], [[range]], [[wander]], [[stroll]], [[stray]], [[roam]], [[rove]], [[saunter]], [[go walkabout]] (*Austral*)
**2** to speak or write in a confused style
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[babble]], [[rabbit]] (on) (*Brit* *informal*), waffle (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[witter on]] (*informal*)
**3** to grow or develop in a random fashion
 *n*
**4** a [[walk]], esp. in the countryside
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[walk]], [[tour]], [[stroll]], [[hike]], [[roaming]], [[roving]], [[saunter]]
