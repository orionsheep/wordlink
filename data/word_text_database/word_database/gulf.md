**gulf** *n*
**1** a large deep [[bay]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bay]], [[bight]], [[sea inlet]]
**2** something that divides or separates people, such as a lack of understanding: gradually the gulf between father and son has lessened
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chasm]], [[opening]], [[split]], [[gap]], [[separation]], [[void]], [[rift]], [[abyss]]
