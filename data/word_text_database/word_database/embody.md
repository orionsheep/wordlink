**embody** *vb* **-bodies**, **-bodying**, **-bodied**
**1** to be an example of or express (an idea or other abstract concept)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[personify]], [[represent]], [[stand for]], [[manifest]], [[exemplify]], [[symbolize]], [[typify]], [[actualize]]
**2** to [[include]] as part of a whole: the proposal has been embodied in a draft resolution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incorporate]], [[include]], [[contain]], [[combine]], [[collect]], [[take in]], [[encompass]]
 **[[embodiment]]** *n*
