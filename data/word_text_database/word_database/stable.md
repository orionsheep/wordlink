**stable**^2 *adj*
**1** steady in position or balance; [[firm]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solid]], [[firm]], [[fixed]], [[substantial]], [[durable]], [[well-made]], [[well-built]], [[immovable]]
**2** [[lasting]] and not likely to experience any sudden changes: a stable environment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secure]], [[lasting]], [[strong]], [[sound]], [[fast]], [[sure]], [[established]], [[permanent]]
**3** having a calm personality; not prone to changes in mood
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-balanced]], [[balanced]], [[sensible]], [[reasonable]], [[rational]]
**4** (*physics*) (of an elementary particle) not subject to decay
**5** (*chem*) (of a chemical compound) not easily decomposed
