**post**^3 *n*
**1** a [[position]] to which a person is appointed; [[job]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[place]], [[office]], [[position]], [[situation]], [[employment]], [[appointment]], [[assignment]]
**2** a [[position]] to which a soldier or guard is assigned for duty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[position]], [[place]], [[base]], [[beat]], [[station]]
**3** a permanent military establishment
**4** (*Brit*) either of two military bugle calls (**first post** and **last post**) giving notice of the time to retire for the night
 *vb*
**5** (*Brit** & **Austral*) to send (someone) to a new [[place]] to work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[station]], [[assign]], [[put]], [[place]], [[position]], [[situate]], [[put on duty]]
**6** to [[assign]] to or [[station]] at a particular [[place]] or [[position]]: guards were posted at the doors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[station]], [[assign]], [[put]], [[place]], [[position]], [[situate]], [[put on duty]]
