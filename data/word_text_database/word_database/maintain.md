**maintain** *vb*
**1** to [[continue]] or keep in existence: we must maintain good relations with them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continue]], [[retain]], [[preserve]], [[sustain]], [[carry on]], [[keep up]], [[prolong]], [[perpetuate]]
**2** to keep in proper or good condition: an expensive car to maintain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look after]], [[care for]], [[take care of]], [[conserve]], [[keep in good condition]]
**3** to [[sustain]] or [[keep up]] a particular level or speed: he set off at a high speed, but couldn't maintain it all the way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continue]], [[retain]], [[preserve]], [[sustain]], [[carry on]], [[keep up]], [[prolong]], [[perpetuate]]
**4** to enable a person to have the money, food and other things he or she needs to live: the money maintained us for a month
**5** to [[assert]]: he had always maintained that he never wanted children
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assert]], [[state]], [[claim]], [[insist]], [[declare]], [[contend]], [[profess]], [[avow]]
**6** to defend against contradiction: he maintained his innocence
