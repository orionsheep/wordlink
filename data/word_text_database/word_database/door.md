**door** *n*
**1** a hinged or sliding panel for closing the [[entrance]] to a building, room, or cupboard
**2** a [[doorway]] or [[entrance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opening]], [[entry]], [[entrance]], [[exit]], [[doorway]]
**3** a means of access or escape: the door to happiness
**4** **lay something at someone's door** to blame someone for something
**5** **out of doors** in the open air
