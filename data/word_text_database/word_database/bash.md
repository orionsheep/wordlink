**bash** (*informal*) *vb*
**1** to [[strike]] violently or crushingly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hit]], [[beat]], [[strike]], [[knock]], [[smash]], [[belt]] (*informal*), [[slap]], [[sock]] (*slang*)
**2** (foll. by *into*) to crash into
 *n*
**3** a heavy blow
**4** **have a bash** (*informal*) to make an attempt
