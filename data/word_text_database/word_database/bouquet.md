**bouquet** *n*
**1** a [[bunch of flowers]], esp. a large carefully arranged one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bunch of flowers]], [[spray]], [[garland]], [[wreath]], [[posy]], [[buttonhole]], [[corsage]], [[nosegay]]
**2** the [[aroma]] of wine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aroma]], [[smell]], [[scent]], [[perfume]], [[fragrance]], [[savour]], [[odour]], [[redolence]]
