**click** *n*
**1** a short light often metallic sound
 *vb*
**2** to make a clicking sound: cameras clicked and whirred
**3** Also: **click on** (*computers*) to press and release (a button on a mouse) or select (a particular function) by pressing and releasing a button on a mouse
**4** (*informal*) to become suddenly clear: it wasn't until I saw the photograph that everything clicked into place
**5** (*slang*) (of two people) to get on well together: I met him at a dinner party and we clicked straight away
**6** (*slang*) to be a great success: the film cost so much that if it hadn't clicked at the box office we'd have been totally wiped out
