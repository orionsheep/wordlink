**obstruct** *vb*
**1** to [[block]] a way with an obstacle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[block]], [[close]], [[bar]], [[plug]], [[barricade]], [[stop up]], [[bung up]] (*informal*)
**2** to make progress or activity difficult: this government will never obstruct the course of justice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold up]], [[stop]], [[check]], [[block]], [[restrict]], [[slow down]], [[hamper]], [[hinder]]
**3** to [[block]] a clear view of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obscure]], [[screen]], [[cover]]
