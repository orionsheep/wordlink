**dispose** *vb* **-posing**, **-posed**
**1** **dispose of**
**A** to throw away
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dispose of something]]**: get rid of, destroy, dump (*informal*), scrap, discard, unload, jettison, throw out *or* away
 **B** to give, sell, or transfer to another
 **C** to deal with or settle: I disposed of that problem right away
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dispose of something]]**: deal with, manage, treat, handle, settle, cope with, take care of, see to
 **D** to kill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dispose of someone]]**: kill, murder, destroy, execute, slaughter, assassinate, slay, liquidate
**2** to [[arrange]] or [[place]] in a particular way: around them are disposed the moulded masks of witch doctors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrange]], [[put]], [[place]], [[group]], [[order]], [[distribute]], [[array]]
