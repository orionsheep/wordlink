**necessity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a set of circumstances that inevitably requires a certain result: the necessity to maintain safety standards
**2** something needed: the daily necessities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[essential]], [[need]], [[requirement]], [[fundamental]], [[requisite]], [[prerequisite]], [[sine qua non]] (*Latin*), [[desideratum]], [[must-have]]
**3** great poverty
**4** **of necessity** inevitably
