**emergency** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** an unforeseen or sudden occurrence, esp. of [[danger]] demanding [[immediate]] action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crisis]], [[danger]], [[difficulty]], [[accident]], [[disaster]], [[necessity]], [[plight]], [[scrape]] (*informal*)
**2** **state of emergency** a time of [[crisis]], declared by a government, during which normal laws and civil rights can be suspended
 *adj*
**3** for use in an emergency: the emergency exit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[urgent]], [[crisis]], [[immediate]]
**4** made necessary because of an emergency: emergency surgery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[urgent]], [[crisis]], [[immediate]]
