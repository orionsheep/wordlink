**rehearse** *vb* **-hearsing**, **-hearsed**
**1** to [[practise]] (a play, concert, etc.) for public performance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[practise]], [[prepare]], [[run through]], [[go over]], [[train]], [[repeat]], [[drill]], [[recite]]
**2** to [[repeat]] aloud: he rehearsed his familiar views on the press
**3** to [[train]] (a person) for public performance
 **[[rehearsal]]** *n* 
 **[[rehearser]]** *n*
