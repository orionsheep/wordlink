**claw** *n*
**1** a curved pointed [[nail]] on the foot of birds, some reptiles, and certain mammals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nail]], [[talon]]
**2** a similar part in some invertebrates, such as a crab's pincer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: pincer
 *vb*
**3** to [[scrape]], [[tear]], or [[dig]] with claws or nails: she clawed his face with her fingernails
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scratch]], [[tear]], [[dig]], [[rip]], [[scrape]], [[maul]], [[mangulate]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[lacerate]]
**4** to achieve (something) only after overcoming great difficulties: he clawed his way to power and wealth; settlers attempting to claw a living from the desert
