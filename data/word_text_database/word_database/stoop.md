**stoop** *vb*
**1** to [[bend]] (the body) forward and downward
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bend]], [[lean]], [[bow]], [[duck]], [[crouch]]
**2** to stand or walk with head and shoulders habitually bent forward
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hunch]]
**3** **stoop to** to lower one's normal standards of behaviour; degrade oneself: no real journalist would stoop to faking
 *n*
**4** the act, position, or habit of [[stooping]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slouch]], [[bad posture]], [[round-shoulderedness]]
 **[[stooping]]** *adj*
