**revelation** *n*
**1** the act of making known a truth which was previously secret
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhibition]], [[publication]], [[exposure]], [[unveiling]], [[uncovering]], [[unearthing]], [[proclamation]]
**2** a fact newly made known
**3** a person or experience that proves to be different from expectations: New York State could prove a revelation to first-time visitors
**4** (*Christianity*) God's disclosure of his own nature and his purpose for mankind
