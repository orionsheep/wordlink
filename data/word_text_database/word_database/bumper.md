**bumper**^2 *n*
**1** a glass or tankard, filled to the brim, esp. as a toast
**2** an unusually large or fine example of something
 *adj*
**3** unusually large, fine, or [[abundant]]: a bumper crop
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exceptional]], [[excellent]], [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[massive]], [[jumbo]] (*informal*), [[abundant]], [[whopping]] (*informal*), [[bountiful]]
