**stain** *vb*
**1** to [[discolour]] (something) with marks that are not easily removed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[soil]], [[discolour]], [[dirty]], [[tinge]], [[spot]], [[blot]], [[blemish]]
**2** to [[dye]] (something) with a lasting pigment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dye]], [[colour]], [[tint]]
 *n*
**3** a [[mark]] or [[discoloration]] that is not easily removed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[spot]], [[blot]], [[blemish]], [[discoloration]], [[smirch]]
**4** an incident in someone's life that has damaged his or her reputation: a stain on his character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stigma]], [[shame]], [[disgrace]], [[slur]], [[dishonour]]
**5** a liquid used to penetrate the surface of a material, such as wood, and [[colour]] it without covering up the surface or grain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dye]], [[colour]], [[tint]]
