**crush** *vb*
**1** to [[press]] or [[squeeze]] so as to injure, [[break]], or put out of shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squash]], [[break]], [[squeeze]], [[compress]], [[press]], [[pulverize]]
**2** to [[break]] or grind into small pieces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squash]], [[break]], [[squeeze]], [[compress]], [[press]], [[pulverize]]
**3** to control or [[subdue]] by force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overcome]], [[overwhelm]], [[put down]], [[subdue]], [[overpower]], [[quash]], [[quell]], [[stamp out]]
**4** to extract (liquid) by pressing: crush the juice from a lemon
**5** to defeat or [[humiliate]] utterly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[demoralize]], [[depress]], [[devastate]], [[discourage]], [[humble]], [[put down]] (*slang*), [[humiliate]], [[squash]]
**6** to [[crowd]] together
 *n*
**7** a dense [[crowd]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowd]], [[mob]], [[horde]], [[throng]], [[pack]], [[mass]], [[jam]], [[huddle]]
**8** the act of crushing
**9** (*informal*) an infatuation: I had a teenage crush on my French teacher
**10** a drink made by crushing fruit: orange crush
