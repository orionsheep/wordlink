**horizon** *n*
**1** the apparent line that divides the earth and the sky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skyline]], [[view]], [[vista]]
**2** **horizons** the limits of a person's interests and activities: seeking to broaden his horizons at college
**3** **on the horizon** almost certainly going to happen or be done in the future: a new type of computer is on the horizon
