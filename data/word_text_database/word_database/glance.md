**glance** *n*
**1** a quick [[look]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peek]], [[look]], [[glimpse]], [[peep]], dekko (*slang*, *old-fashioned*)
 *vb* **[[glancing]]**, **[[glanced]]**
**2** to [[look]] quickly at something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peek]], [[look]], [[view]], [[glimpse]], [[peep]]
**3** to be deflected off an object at an oblique angle: the ball [[glanced]] off a spectator
 **[[glancing]]** *adj*
