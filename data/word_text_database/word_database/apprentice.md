**apprentice** *n*
**1** someone who works for a skilled person for a fixed period in order to learn his or her trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trainee]], [[student]], [[pupil]], [[novice]], [[beginner]], [[learner]], probationer
 *vb* **-ticing**, **-ticed**
**2** to take or place as an apprentice
 **[[apprenticeship]]** *n*
