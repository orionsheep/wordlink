**graduate** *n*
**1** a person who holds a university or college degree
**2** (*US** & **Canad*) a student who has completed a course of studies at a high school and received a diploma
**3** same as **postgraduate**
 *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**4** to receive a degree or diploma
**5** to change by degrees: the winds graduate from tropical storms to cyclones
**6** to mark (a measuring flask or instrument) with units of measurement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark off]], [[grade]], [[proportion]], [[regulate]], [[gauge]], [[calibrate]], [[measure out]]
