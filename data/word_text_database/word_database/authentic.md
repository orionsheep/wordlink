**authentic** *adj*
**1** of [[undisputed]] origin or authorship; [[genuine]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[real]], [[pure]], [[genuine]], [[valid]], [[undisputed]], [[lawful]], [[bona fide]], [[dinkum]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[true-to-life]], [[live]]
**2** reliable or [[accurate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accurate]], [[legitimate]], [[authoritative]]
**3** (*music*) using period instruments, scores, and playing techniques
 **[[authentically]]** *adv* 
 **[[authenticity]]** *n*
