**forget** *vb* **-getting**, **-got**, **-gotten**
**1** to [[fail to remember]] (someone or something once known)
**2** to [[neglect]], either by mistake or on purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[neglect]], [[overlook]], [[omit]], [[not remember]], [[be remiss]], [[fail to remember]]
**3** to [[leave behind]] by mistake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leave behind]], [[lose]], [[lose sight of]], [[mislay]]
**4** **forget oneself** to act in an uncharacteristically unrestrained or unacceptable manner: behave yourself or I might forget myself and slap your wrists
 **[[forgettable]]** *adj*
