**defence** * or * (*US*) **defense** *n*
**1** resistance against attack
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[cover]], [[security]], [[guard]], [[shelter]], [[safeguard]], [[immunity]]
**2** something that provides such resistance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[cover]], [[security]], [[guard]], [[shelter]], [[safeguard]], [[immunity]]
**3** an [[argument]] or piece of writing in support of something that has been criticized or questioned
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[argument]], [[explanation]], [[excuse]], [[plea]], [[justification]], [[vindication]], [[rationalization]]
**4** a country's military resources
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[armaments]], [[weapons]]
**5** (*law*) a defendant's [[denial]] of the truth of a charge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plea]] (*law*), [[testimony]], [[denial]], [[alibi]], [[rebuttal]]
**6** (*law*) the defendant and his or her legal advisers collectively
**7** (*sport*) the players in a team whose function is to prevent the opposing team from scoring
**8** **defences** fortifications
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shield]], [[barricade]], [[fortification]], [[buttress]], [[rampart]], [[bulwark]], [[fortified pa]] (*NZ*) 
 **[[defenceless]]** * or * (*US*) **[[defenseless]]** *adj*
