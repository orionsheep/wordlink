**rot** *vb* **rotting**, **rotted**
**1** to [[decay]] or cause to [[decay]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decay]], [[spoil]], [[deteriorate]], [[perish]], [[decompose]], [[moulder]], [[go bad]], [[putrefy]] (*formal*)
**2** to [[deteriorate]] slowly, mentally and physically: I thought he was either dead or rotting in a jail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deteriorate]], [[decline]], waste away
 *n*
**3** the process of rotting or the state of being rotten
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decay]], [[decomposition]], [[corruption]], [[mould]], [[blight]], [[canker]], [[putrefaction]]
**4** something decomposed
**5** short for **dry rot**
**6** a plant or animal disease which causes [[decay]] of the tissues
**7** [[nonsense]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonsense]], [[rubbish]], [[drivel]], [[twaddle]], [[garbage]] (*informal*), [[trash]], [[tripe]] (*informal*), [[claptrap]] (*informal*), [[bizzo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[bull's wool]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
