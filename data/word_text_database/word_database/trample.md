**trample** *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**1** Also: **trample on** to [[tread]] on and [[crush]]: three children were trampled to death when the crowd panicked and ran
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stamp]], [[crush]], [[squash]], [[tread]], [[flatten]], [[run over]], [[walk over]]
**2** **trample on** to treat (a person or his or her rights or feelings) with disregard or contempt
