**parade** *n*
**1** an ordered [[march]] or [[procession]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[procession]], [[march]], [[pageant]], [[cavalcade]]
**2** a public [[promenade]] or street of shops
**3** a blatant but sometimes insincere [[display]]: a man who made a parade of liking his own company best
 *vb* **-rading**, **-raded**
**4** to [[exhibit]] or [[flaunt]]: he neither paraded nor disguised his devout faith
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flaunt]], [[display]], [[exhibit]], [[show off]] (*informal*)
**5** to walk or [[march]], esp. in a [[procession]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[march]], [[process]], [[promenade]]
