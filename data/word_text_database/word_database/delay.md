**delay** *vb*
**1** to put (something) off to a later time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put off]], [[suspend]], [[postpone]], [[shelve]], [[defer]], [[hold over]]
**2** to slow up or cause to be late
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold up]], [[detain]], [[hold back]], [[hinder]], [[obstruct]], [[impede]], [[bog down]], [[set back]]
**3**
**A** to hesitate in doing something
 **B** to deliberately take longer than necessary to do something
 *n*
**4** the act of delaying
**5** a period of inactivity or waiting before something happens or continues
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold-up]], [[wait]], [[setback]], [[interruption]], [[stoppage]], [[impediment]], [[hindrance]]
