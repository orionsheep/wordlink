**inland** *adj*
**1** of or in the [[interior]] of a country or region, away from a sea or border
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interior]], [[internal]], [[upcountry]]
**2** (*chiefly* *Brit*) operating within a country or region; domestic: inland trade
 *n*
**3** the [[interior]] of a country or region
 *adv*
**4** towards or into the [[interior]] of a country or region
