◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nagging]], [[worrying]], [[persistent]], [[uncomfortable]]
**2** [[secret]]: a sneaking admiration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secret]], [[private]], [[hidden]], [[unexpressed]], [[unvoiced]], [[undivulged]]
**3** acting in a cowardly and furtive way
