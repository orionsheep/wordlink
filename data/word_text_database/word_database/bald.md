**bald** *adj*
**1** having no hair or fur, esp. of a person having no hair on the scalp
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hairless]], [[depilated]], [[baldheaded]]
**2** lacking natural covering
**3** [[plain]] or [[blunt]]: the bald facts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plain]], [[direct]], [[frank]], [[straightforward]], [[blunt]], [[rude]], [[forthright]], [[unadorned]]
**4** (of a tyre) having a worn tread
 **[[baldly]]** *adv* 
 **[[baldness]]** *n*
