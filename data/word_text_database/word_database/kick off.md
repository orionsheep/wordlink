**kick off** *vb*
**1** to start play in a game of football by kicking the ball from the centre of the field
**2** (*informal*) to commence (a discussion, event, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[kick something off]]**: begin, start, open, commence, initiate, get on the road
 *n* **kick-off**
**3**
**A** the kick that officially starts a game of football
 **B** the time when the first kick is due to take place
**4** (*informal*) the time when an event is due to begin
