**reign** *n*
**1** the period during which a monarch is the official ruler of a country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rule]], [[power]], [[control]], [[command]], [[monarchy]], [[dominion]]
**2** a period during which a person or thing is dominant: a reign of terror
 *vb*
**3** to [[rule]] (a country)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rule]], [[govern]], [[be in power]], [[influence]], [[command]]
**4** to [[be supreme]]: a sense of confusion reigns in the capital
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be supreme]], [[prevail]], [[predominate]], [[hold sway]]
