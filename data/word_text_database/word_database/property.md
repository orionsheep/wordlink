**property** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** something owned
**2** (*law*) the right to possess, use, and dispose of anything
**3** [[possessions]] collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[possessions]], [[goods]], [[effects]], [[holdings]], [[capital]], [[riches]], [[estate]], [[assets]]
**4** [[land]] or buildings owned by someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[land]], [[holding]], [[estate]], [[real estate]], [[freehold]]
**5** a [[quality]] or [[attribute]]: the oils have healing properties
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quality]], [[feature]], [[characteristic]], [[attribute]], [[trait]], [[hallmark]]
**6** same as **prop**
