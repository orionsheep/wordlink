**pastoral** *adj*
**1** of or depicting [[country]] life or scenery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rustic]], [[country]], [[rural]], [[bucolic]]
**2** (of land) used for pasture
**3** of or relating to a member of the Christian clergy or his or her duties
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ecclesiastical]], [[priestly]], [[ministerial]], [[clerical]]
**4** of or relating to shepherds or their work
 *n*
**5** a literary work, picture, or piece of music portraying [[country]] life
**6** (*Christianity*) a letter from a bishop to the clergy or people of his diocese
