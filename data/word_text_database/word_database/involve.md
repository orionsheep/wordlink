**involve** *vb* **-volving**, **-volved**
**1** to include as a necessary part
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[entail]], [[mean]], [[require]], [[occasion]] (*formal*), [[imply]], [[give rise to]], [[necessitate]]
**2** to have an effect on: around fifty riders were involved and some were hurt
**3** to implicate: several people were involved in the crime
**4** to make complicated: the situation was further involved by her disappearance
 **[[involvement]]** *n*
