**sham** *n*
**1** anything that is not genuine or is not what it appears to be
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fraud]] (*informal*), [[imitation]], [[hoax]], [[pretence]], [[forgery]], [[counterfeit]], [[humbug]], [[impostor]]
**2** a person who pretends to be something other than he or she is
 *adj*
**3** not real or genuine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[false]], [[artificial]], [[bogus]], [[pretended]], [[mock]], [[imitation]], [[simulated]], [[counterfeit]]
 *vb* **shamming**, **[[shammed]]**
**4** to fake or feign (something); pretend: he made a point of shamming nervousness
