**muddy** *adj* **-dier**, **-diest**
**1** covered or filled with mud
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[boggy]], [[swampy]], [[marshy]], [[quaggy]]
**2** not clear or bright: muddy colours
**3** cloudy: a muddy liquid
**4** (esp. of thoughts) confused or vague
 *vb* **-dies**, **-dying**, **-died**
**5** to make muddy
**6** to make a situation or issue less clear: the allegations of sexual misconduct only serve to muddy the issue
 **[[muddily]]** *adv*
