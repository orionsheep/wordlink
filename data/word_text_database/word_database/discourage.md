**discourage** *vb* **-raging**, **-raged**

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dishearten]], [[depress]], [[intimidate]], [[overawe]], [[demoralize]], [[put a damper on]], [[dispirit]], [[deject]]
**2** to oppose by expressing disapproval
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put off]], [[deter]], [[prevent]], [[dissuade]], [[talk out of]]
 **[[discouragement]]** *n* 
 **[[discouraging]]** *adj*
