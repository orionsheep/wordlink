**superficial** *adj*
**1** not careful or thorough: a superficial analysis
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hasty]], [[cursory]], [[perfunctory]], [[hurried]], [[casual]], [[sketchy]], [[desultory]], [[slapdash]]
**2** only outwardly apparent rather than genuine or actual: those are merely superficial differences
**3** (of a person) lacking deep emotions or serious interests; [[shallow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shallow]], [[frivolous]], [[empty-headed]], [[silly]], [[trivial]]
**4** of, near, or forming the [[surface]]: the gash was superficial
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slight]], [[surface]], [[external]], [[on the surface]], [[exterior]]
 **[[superficiality]]** *n* 
 **[[superficially]]** *adv*
