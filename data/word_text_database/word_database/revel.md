**revel** *vb* **-velling**, **-velled** (*US*) **-veling**, **-veled**
**1** **revel in** to take pleasure or wallow in: he would revel in his victory
**2** to take part in noisy festivities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[celebrate]], [[carouse]], [[live it up]] (*informal*), make merry
 *n*
**3** **revels** noisy [[merrymaking]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merrymaking]], [[party]], [[celebration]], [[spree]] (*old-fashioned*), [[festivity]], [[carousal]]
