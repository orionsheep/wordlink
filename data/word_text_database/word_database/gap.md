**gap** *n*
**1** a [[break]] or [[opening]] in something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opening]], [[space]], [[hole]], [[break]], [[crack]], [[slot]], [[aperture]], [[cleft]]
**2** an [[interruption]] or [[interval]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interval]], [[pause]], [[interruption]], [[respite]], [[lull]], [[interlude]], [[breathing space]], [[hiatus]]
**3** a [[difference]] in ideas or viewpoint: the generation gap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difference]], [[gulf]], [[contrast]], [[disagreement]], [[discrepancy]], [[inconsistency]], [[disparity]], divergence
 **[[gappy]]** *adj*
