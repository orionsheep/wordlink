**pants** *pl n*
**1** (*Brit*) an undergarment with two leg holes, covering the body from the waist or hips to the thighs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[underpants]], [[briefs]], [[drawers]], [[knickers]], [[panties]], [[boxer shorts]], [[broekies]] (*S African*), [[underdaks]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*) [[trousers]] or shorts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trousers]], [[slacks]]
**3** **bore *or* scare the pants off someone** (*informal*) to bore or scare someone very much
