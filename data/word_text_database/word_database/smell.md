**smell** *vb* **smelling**, **smelt*** or ***smelled**
**1** to perceive the [[scent]] of (a substance) with the nose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sniff]], [[scent]]
**2** to have a specified kind of smell: it smells fruity; your supper smells good
**3** (often foll. by *of*) to emit an [[odour]] (of): the place smells of milk and babies
**4** to give off an unpleasant [[odour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stink]], [[reek]], [[pong]] (*Brit* *informal*)
**5** (often foll. by *out*) to detect through instinct: I smell trouble
**6** to use the sense of smell; [[sniff]]
**7** **smell of** to indicate or suggest: anything that smells of devaluation
 *n*
**8** the sense by which scents or odours are perceived. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**olfactory**
**9** an [[odour]] or [[scent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[odour]], [[scent]], [[fragrance]], [[perfume]], [[bouquet]], [[aroma]]
**10** the act of smelling
