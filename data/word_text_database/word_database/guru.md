**guru** *n*
**1** a Hindu or Sikh religious [[teacher]] or [[leader]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[teacher]], [[mentor]], [[sage]], [[master]], [[tutor]]
**2** a [[leader]] or adviser of a person or group of people: inside a team of advertising gurus are at work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[authority]], [[expert]], [[leader]], [[master]], [[pundit]], [[Svengali]], [[fundi]] (*S African*)
