**when** *adv*
**1** at what time?: when are they leaving?
 *conj*
**2** at the time at which: he was twenty when the war started
**3** although: he drives when he could walk
**4** considering the fact that: how did you pass the exam when you hadn't studied for it?
 *pron*
**5** at which time: she's at the age when girls get interested in boys
