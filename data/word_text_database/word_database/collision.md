**collision** *n*
**1** a violent [[crash]] between moving objects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crash]], [[impact]], [[accident]], [[smash]], [[bump]], [[pile-up]] (*informal*), [[prang]] (*informal*)
**2** the [[conflict]] of opposed ideas or wishes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conflict]], [[opposition]], [[clash]], [[encounter]], [[disagreement]], [[incompatibility]]
