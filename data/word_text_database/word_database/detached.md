**detached** *adj*
**1** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **S African*) [[separate]] or standing apart: a detached farmhouse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[disconnected]], [[discrete]], [[unconnected]], [[undivided]]
**2** showing no emotional involvement: she continued to watch him in her grave and detached manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[objective]], [[neutral]], [[impartial]], [[reserved]], [[impersonal]], [[disinterested]], [[unbiased]], [[dispassionate]]
