**monkey** *n*
**1** any long-tailed [[primate]] that is not a lemur or tarsier
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[simian]], [[ape]], [[primate]]
**2** (loosely) any [[primate]] that is not a human
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[simian]], [[ape]], [[primate]]
**3** a naughty or mischievous child
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rascal]], [[horror]] (*informal*), [[devil]], [[rogue]], [[imp]], [[tyke]], [[scallywag]], [[scamp]], [[nointer]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**4** (*slang*) £500 or $500
**5** **give a monkey's** (*Brit* *slang*) to care about or regard as important: who gives a monkey's what he thinks?
 *vb*
**6** **monkey around *or* about with** to meddle or tinker with
