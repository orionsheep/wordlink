**rebound** *vb*
**1** to spring back from a sudden impact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bounce]], [[ricochet]], [[recoil]]
**2** (of a plan or action) to [[misfire]] so as to hurt the person responsible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misfire]], [[backfire]], [[recoil]], boomerang
 *n*
**3** the act of rebounding
**4** **on the rebound** (*informal*) while recovering from rejection: she married him on the rebound
