**ordinary** *adj*
**1** [[usual]] or [[normal]]: it was an ordinary working day for them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usual]], [[standard]], [[normal]], [[common]], [[regular]], [[typical]], [[conventional]], [[routine]]
**2** not special or different in any way: her campaign speech dwells on the problems faced by ordinary Americans
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commonplace]], [[plain]], [[modest]], [[humble]], [[mundane]], [[banal]], [[unremarkable]], [[run-of-the-mill]]
**3** dull or unexciting: the restaurant charged very high prices for very ordinary cooking
 *n*, *pl* **-naries**
**4** (*RC Church*) the parts of the Mass that do not vary from day to day
**5** **out of the ordinary** unusual
