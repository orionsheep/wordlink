**intrusion** *n*
**1** the act of intruding; an unwelcome visit, etc.: an intrusion into her private life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interruption]], [[interference]], [[infringement]], [[trespass]], [[encroachment]]
**2** (*geol*)
**A** the forcing of molten rock into spaces in the overlying strata
 **B** molten rock formed in this way
 **[[intrusive]]** *adj*
