**paralysis** *n*
**1** (*pathol*) inability to move all or part of the body due to damage to the nervous system
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immobility]], [[palsy]]
**2** a state of inactivity: the economic chaos and political paralysis into which the country has sunk
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[standstill]], [[breakdown]], [[stoppage]], [[halt]]
