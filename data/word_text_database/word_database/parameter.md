**parameter** (par-**am**-it-er) *n*
**1** (*maths*) an arbitrary constant that determines the specific form of a mathematical expression, such as *a* and *b* in *y = ax^2 + b*
**2** (*informal*) any limiting factor: exchange rates are allowed to fluctuate only within designated parameters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[limit]], [[restriction]], [[framework]], [[limitation]], [[specification]]
