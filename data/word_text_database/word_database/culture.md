**culture** *n*
**1** the ideas, [[customs]], and art of a particular [[society]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lifestyle]], [[habit]], [[way of life]], [[mores]]
**2** a particular [[civilization]] at a particular period
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[civilization]], [[society]], [[customs]], [[way of life]]
**3** a developed understanding of the arts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refinement]], [[education]], [[enlightenment]], [[sophistication]], [[good taste]], [[urbanity]]
**4** the cultivation and rearing of plants or animals
**5** a growth of bacteria for study
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**6** to grow (bacteria) in a special medium
 **[[cultural]]** *adj*
