**network** *n*
**1** a [[system]] of intersecting lines, roads, veins, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[web]], [[system]], [[arrangement]], [[grid]], [[lattice]]
**2** an interconnecting group or [[system]]: a network of sympathizers and safe-houses
**3** (*radio*, *television*) a group of broadcasting stations that all transmit the same programme at the same time
**4** (*electronics*, *computers*) a [[system]] of interconnected components or circuits
 *vb*
**5** (*radio*, *television*) to broadcast (a programme) over a network
**6** to form business contacts through informal social meetings
