**drown** *vb*
**1** to die or kill by immersion in liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[go down]], [[go under]]
**2** to [[drench]] thoroughly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drench]], [[flood]], [[soak]], [[steep]], [[swamp]], [[saturate]], [[engulf]], [[submerge]]
**3** to make (a sound) impossible to hear by making a loud noise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overwhelm]], [[overcome]], [[wipe out]], [[overpower]], [[obliterate]], [[swallow up]]
