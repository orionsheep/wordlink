**blow out** *vb*
**1** (of a flame) to extinguish or be extinguished
**2** (of a tyre) to puncture suddenly
**3** (of an oil or gas well) to lose oil or gas in an uncontrolled manner
 *n* **blowout**
**4** a sudden burst in a tyre
**5** the uncontrolled escape of oil or gas from a well
**6** (*slang*) a large filling meal
**7** (*Austral*) a sudden large increase
