**couch** *n*
**1** a piece of upholstered furniture for seating more than one person
**2** a bed on which patients of a doctor or a psychoanalyst lie during examination or treatment
 *vb*
**3** to express in a particular style of language: a proclamation couched in splendidly archaic phraseology
**4** (*archaic*) (of an animal) to crouch, as when preparing to leap
