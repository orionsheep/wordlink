**indirect** *adj*
**1** done or caused by someone or something else: indirect benefits
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[related]], [[secondary]], [[subsidiary]], [[incidental]], [[unintended]]
**2** not going in a direct course or line: he took the indirect route home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[circuitous]], [[roundabout]], [[curving]], [[wandering]], [[rambling]], [[deviant]] (*old-fashioned*), [[meandering]], [[tortuous]]
**3** not coming straight to the point: an indirect question
 **[[indirectly]]** *adv*
