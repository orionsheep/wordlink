**rule** *n*
**1** a statement of what is allowed, for example in a game or procedure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regulation]], [[law]], [[direction]], [[guideline]], [[decree]]
**2** a customary form or procedure: he has his own rule: be firm, be clear, but never be rude
**3** **the rule** the common order of things: humanitarian gestures were more the exception than the rule
**4** the exercise of governmental [[authority]] or [[control]]: under the rule of the dictator
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[government]], [[power]], [[control]], [[authority]], [[command]], [[regime]], [[reign]], [[jurisdiction]], [[mana]] (*NZ*)
**5** the period of time in which a monarch or [[government]] has [[power]]: four decades of Communist rule
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[government]], [[power]], [[control]], [[authority]], [[command]], [[regime]], [[reign]], [[jurisdiction]], [[mana]] (*NZ*)
**6** a device with a straight edge for guiding or measuring: a slide rule
**7** (*printing*) a long thin line or dash
**8** (*Christianity*) a systematic body of laws and customs followed by members of a religious order
**9** (*[[law]]*) an order by a court or [[judge]]
**10** **[[as a rule]]** usually
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[as a rule]]**: usually, generally, mainly, normally, on the whole, ordinarily
 *vb* **ruling**, **ruled**
**11** to [[govern]] (people or a political unit)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[govern]], [[control]], [[direct]], [[have power over]], [[command over]], [[have charge of]]
**12** to be pre-eminent or superior
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be prevalent]], [[prevail]], [[predominate]], [[be customary]], [[preponderate]]
**13** to [[be customary]] or prevalent: chaos ruled as the scene turned into one of total confusion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be prevalent]], [[prevail]], [[predominate]], [[be customary]], [[preponderate]]
**14** to [[decide]] authoritatively: the judges ruled that men could be prosecuted for rape offences against their wives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decree]], [[decide]], [[judge]], [[settle]], [[pronounce]]
**15** to mark with straight parallel lines or one straight line
**16** to restrain or [[control]]
