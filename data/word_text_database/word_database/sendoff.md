**sendoff** *n*
**1** (*informal*) a show of good wishes to a person about to set off on a journey or start a new career
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[farewell]], [[departure]], [[leave-taking]], valediction
 *vb* **send off**
**2** to dispatch (something, such as a letter)
**3** (*sport*) (of a referee) to dismiss (a player) from the field of play for some offence
