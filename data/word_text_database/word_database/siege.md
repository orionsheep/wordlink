**siege** *n*
**1** a military operation carried out to capture a place by surrounding and blockading it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blockade]], [[encirclement]], [[besiegement]]
**2** a similar operation carried out by police, for example to force people out of a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blockade]], [[encirclement]], [[besiegement]]
**3** **lay siege to** to subject (a place) to a siege
