**congratulate** *vb* **-lating**, **-lated**
**1** to express one's pleasure to (a person) at his or her success or good fortune
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compliment]], [[pat on the back]], [[wish joy to]]
**2** **congratulate oneself** to consider oneself clever or fortunate (as a result of): she congratulated herself on her own business acumen
 **[[congratulatory]]** *adj*
