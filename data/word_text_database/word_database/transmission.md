**transmission** *n*
**1** the sending or passing of something, such as a message or disease from one place or person to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transfer]], [[spread]], [[spreading]], [[passing on]], [[circulation]], [[dispatch]], [[relaying]], [[mediation]]
**2** something that is transmitted, esp. a radio or television [[broadcast]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[programme]], [[broadcast]], [[show]], [[production]], [[telecast]], [[podcast]]
**3** a system of shafts and gears that transmits power from the engine to the driving wheels of a motor vehicle
