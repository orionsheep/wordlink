◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protest]], [[opposition]], [[complaint]], [[doubt]], [[dissent]], [[outcry]], [[protestation]], [[scruple]]
**2** a reason for opposing something: the planning officer had raised no objection to the proposals
