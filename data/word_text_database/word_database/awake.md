**awake** *adj*
**1** [[not sleeping]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[not sleeping]], [[sleepless]], [[wide-awake]], [[aware]], [[conscious]], [[aroused]], [[awakened]], [[restless]]
**2** [[alert]] or [[aware]]: awake to the danger
 *vb* **awaking**, **awoke*** or ***awaked**, **awoken*** or ***awaked**
**3** to emerge or [[rouse]] from sleep
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wake up]], [[come to]], [[wake]], [[stir]], [[awaken]], [[rouse]]
**4** to become or cause to become [[alert]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alert]], [[stimulate]], [[provoke]], [[revive]], [[arouse]], [[stir up]], [[kindle]]
