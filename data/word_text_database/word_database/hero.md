**hero** *n*, *pl* **-roes**
**1** the principal male character in a novel, play, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protagonist]], [[leading man]]
**2** a man of exceptional courage, nobility, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[star]], [[champion]], [[victor]], [[superstar]], [[conqueror]]
**3** a man who is idealized for having superior qualities in any field
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idol]], [[favourite]], [[pin-up]] (*slang*), [[fave]] (*informal*)
