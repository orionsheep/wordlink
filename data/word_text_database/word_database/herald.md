**herald** *n*
**1** a person who announces important news
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[messenger]], [[courier]], [[proclaimer]], [[announcer]], [[crier]], [[town crier]]
**2** (*often* *literary*) a [[forerunner]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forerunner]], [[sign]], [[signal]], [[indication]], [[token]], [[omen]], [[precursor]], harbinger (*literary*)
 *vb*
**3** to announce or [[signal]] the approach of: his arrival was heralded by excited barking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indicate]], [[promise]], [[usher in]], [[presage]], [[portend]], [[foretoken]]
 **[[heraldic]]** *adj*
