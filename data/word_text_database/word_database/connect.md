**connect** *vb*
**1** to [[link]] or be linked: high blood pressure is closely connected to heart disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[link]], [[join]], [[couple]], [[attach]], [[fasten]], [[affix]], [[unite]]
**2** to put into telephone communication with
**3** (of two public vehicles) to have the arrival of one timed to occur just before the departure of the other, for the convenience of passengers
**4** to [[associate]] in the mind: he had always connected sex with violence and attacks rather than loving and concern
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[associate]], [[join]], [[link]], [[identify]], [[lump together]]
**5** to relate by birth or marriage: she was distantly connected with the Wedgwood family
 **[[connective]]** *adj*
