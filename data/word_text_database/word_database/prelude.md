**prelude** (**prel**-yewd) *n*
**1**
**A** an introductory movement in music
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overture]], [[opening]], [[introduction]], introductory movement
 **B** a short piece of music for piano or organ
**2** an event introducing or preceding the main event
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[introduction]], [[beginning]], start
 *vb* **-luding**, **-luded**
**3** to act as a prelude to (something)
**4** to introduce by a prelude
