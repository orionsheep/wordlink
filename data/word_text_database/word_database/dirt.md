**dirt** *n*
**1** any unclean substance, such as [[mud]]; [[filth]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[filth]], [[muck]], [[grime]], [[dust]], [[mud]], [[impurity]], kak (*S African* *vulgar*, *slang*)
**2** loose [[earth]]; [[soil]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soil]], [[ground]], [[earth]], [[clay]], [[turf]], [[loam]]
**3** packed [[earth]], cinders, etc., used to make a racetrack
**4** obscene speech or writing
**5** (*informal*) harmful gossip
