**spiritual** *adj*
**1** relating to a person's beliefs as opposed to his or her physical or material needs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonmaterial]], [[immaterial]], [[incorporeal]]
**2** relating to [[religious]] beliefs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sacred]], [[religious]], [[holy]], [[divine]], [[devotional]]
**3** **one's spiritual home** the place where one feels one belongs
 *n*
**4** Also called: **Negro spiritual** a type of [[religious]] folk song originally sung by African slaves in the American South
 **[[spirituality]]** *n* 
 **[[spiritually]]** *adv*
