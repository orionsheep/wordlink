**because** *conj*
**1** on account of the fact that: because it's so cold we'll go home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[since]], [[as]], [[in that]]
**2** **[[because of]]** on account of: I lost my job [[because of]] her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[because of]]**: [[as]] a result of, on account of, by reason of, thanks to, owing to
