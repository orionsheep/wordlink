**liability** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** someone or something that is a problem or embarrassment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disadvantage]], [[burden]], [[drawback]], [[inconvenience]], [[handicap]], [[nuisance]], [[hindrance]], [[millstone]]
**2** the state of being legally responsible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsibility]], [[accountability]], [[culpability]], [[answerability]]
**3** (*often pl*) sums of money owed by an organization
