**motif** (moh-**teef**) *n*
**1** a distinctive [[idea]], esp. a [[theme]] elaborated on in a piece of music or literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theme]], [[idea]], [[subject]], [[concept]], [[leitmotif]], [[trope]]
**2** a recurring [[shape]] in a [[design]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[shape]], [[decoration]], [[ornament]]
**3** a single [[decoration]], such as a symbol or name on a piece of clothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[shape]], [[decoration]], [[ornament]]
