**benefit** *n*
**1** something that improves or promotes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good]], [[help]], [[profit]], [[favour]]
**2** [[advantage]] or sake: I'm doing this for your benefit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advantage]], [[aid]], [[favour]], [[assistance]]
**3** a payment made by an institution or government to a person who is ill, unemployed, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good]], [[help]], [[profit]], [[favour]]
**4** a theatrical performance or sports event to raise money for a charity
 *vb* **-fiting**, **-fited** (*US*) **-fitting**, **-fitted**
**5** to do or receive [[good]]; [[profit]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[profit from]], [[make the most of]], [[gain from]], [[do well out of]], [[reap benefits from]], [[turn to your advantage]]
