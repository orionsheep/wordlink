**presume** *vb* **-suming**, **-sumed**
**1** to take (something) for granted: I presume he's dead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believe]], [[think]], [[suppose]], [[assume]], [[guess]] (*informal*), [[take for granted]], [[infer]], [[conjecture]]
**2** to [[dare]] (to): I would not presume to lecture you on medical matters, Dr Jacobs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dare]], [[venture]], [[go so far as]], [[take the liberty]], [[make so bold as]]
**3** (foll. by *on* or *upon*) to rely or depend: don't presume on his agreement
**4** (foll. by *on* or *upon*) to take advantage (of): I'm afraid I presumed on Aunt Ginny's generosity
 **[[presumedly]]** *adv* 
 **[[presuming]]** *adj*
