**stance** *n*
**1** an [[attitude]] towards a particular matter: a tough stance in the trade talks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attitude]], [[stand]], [[position]], [[viewpoint]], [[standpoint]]
**2** the manner and [[position]] in which a person stands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[posture]], [[carriage]], [[bearing]], [[deportment]]
**3** (*sport*) the [[position]] taken when about to play the ball
