**inflate** *vb* **-flating**, **-flated**
**1** to [[expand]] or cause to [[expand]] by filling with gas or air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow up]], [[pump up]], [[swell]], [[dilate]], [[distend]], [[bloat]], puff up *or* out
**2** to give an impression of greater importance than is justified: something to inflate their self-esteem
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[expand]], [[enlarge]]
**3** to cause or undergo economic inflation
