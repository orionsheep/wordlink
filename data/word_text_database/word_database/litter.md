**litter** *n*
**1** small items of [[rubbish]] carelessly dropped in public places
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rubbish]], [[refuse]], [[waste]], [[junk]], [[debris]], [[garbage]] (*chiefly* *US*), [[trash]] (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*), [[muck]]
**2** a disordered or untidy collection of objects
**3** a group of animals produced at one birth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brood]], [[young]], [[offspring]], [[progeny]]
**4** straw or hay used as bedding for animals
**5** dry material used to line a receptacle in which a domestic cat can urinate and defecate
**6** (esp. formerly) a bed or seat held between parallel poles and used for carrying people
 *vb*
**7** to [[strew]] with litter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clutter]], [[mess up]], [[clutter up]], [[be scattered about]], [[disorder]], [[disarrange]], [[derange]], [[muss]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**8** to [[scatter]] or be scattered in an untidy fashion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scatter]], [[spread]], [[shower]], [[strew]]
**9** (of animals) to give birth to [[offspring]]
**10** to provide (an animal) with straw or hay for bedding
