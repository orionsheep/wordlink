**fatigue** (fat-**eeg**) *n*
**1** extreme physical or mental [[tiredness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tiredness]], [[lethargy]], [[weariness]], [[heaviness]], [[languor]], [[listlessness]]
**2** the weakening of a material caused by repeated stress or movement
**3** the duties of a soldier that are not military
**4** **fatigues** a soldier's clothing for nonmilitary or battlefield duties
 *vb* **-tiguing**, **-tigued**
**5** to make or become [[weary]] or exhausted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tire]], [[exhaust]], [[weaken]], [[weary]], [[drain]], [[wear out]], [[take it out of]] (*informal*), [[tire out]]
