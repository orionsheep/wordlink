**duff** *adj*
**1** (*chiefly* *Brit* *informal*) broken or [[useless]]: my car had a duff clutch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bad]], [[poor]], [[useless]], [[inferior]], [[unsatisfactory]], [[defective]], [[imperfect]], [[substandard]], bodger *or* bodgie (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb*
**2** (*golf*, *informal*) to bungle (a shot)
**3** **duff up** (*Brit* *slang*) to beat (someone) severely
