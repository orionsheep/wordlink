**quarrel** *n*
**1** an angry [[disagreement]]; [[argument]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagreement]], [[fight]], [[row]], [[argument]], [[dispute]], [[controversy]], [[breach]], [[contention]], [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** a cause of [[dispute]]; grievance
 *vb* **-relling**, **-relled** (*US*) **-reling**, **-reled** (often foll. by *with*)
**3** to engage in a [[disagreement]] or [[dispute]]; [[argue]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagree]], [[fight]], [[argue]], [[row]], [[clash]], [[dispute]], [[differ]], [[fall out]] (*informal*)
**4** to find fault; complain
