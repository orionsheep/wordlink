**arrive** *vb* **-riving**, **-rived**
**1** to reach a place or destination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[come]], [[appear]], [[turn up]], [[show up]] (*informal*), [[draw near]]
**2** **arrive at** to [[come]] to (a conclusion, idea, or decision)
**3** to [[occur]]: the crisis he predicted then has now arrived
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[occur]], [[happen]], [[take place]]
**4** (*informal*) to be born
**5** (*informal*) to attain success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[succeed]], [[make it]] (*informal*), [[triumph]], [[do well]], [[thrive]], [[flourish]], [[be successful]], [[make good]]
