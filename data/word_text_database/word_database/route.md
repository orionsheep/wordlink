**route** *n*
**1** the choice of roads taken to get to a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[course]], [[road]], [[direction]], [[path]], [[journey]], [[itinerary]]
**2** a fixed [[path]] followed by buses, trains, etc. between two places
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[circuit]]
**3** a chosen [[way]] or method: the route to prosperity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[course]], [[road]], [[direction]], [[path]], [[journey]], [[itinerary]]
 *vb* **routeing**, **[[routed]]**
**4** to send by a particular route
