**overpower** *vb*
**1** to conquer or [[subdue]] (someone) by superior force
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overcome]], [[master]], [[overwhelm]], [[overthrow]], [[subdue]], [[quell]], [[subjugate]], [[prevail over]]
**2** to have such a strong effect on (someone) as to make him or her helpless or ineffective: I was so appalled, so overpowered by my guilt and my shame that I was unable to speak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overwhelm]], [[overcome]], [[bowl over]] (*informal*), [[stagger]]
 **[[overpowering]]** *adj*
