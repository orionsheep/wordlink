**war** *n*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conflict]], [[drive]], [[attack]], [[fighting]], [[fight]], [[operation]], [[battle]], [[movement]]
**2** a particular armed [[conflict]]: the American war in Vietnam
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[campaign]], [[drive]], [[attack]], [[operation]], [[movement]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[mission]], [[offensive]], [[cyberwar]]
**3** any [[conflict]] or contest: a trade war
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[campaign]], [[drive]], [[attack]], [[operation]], [[movement]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[mission]], [[offensive]], [[cyberwar]]
**4** **have been in the wars** (*informal*) to look as if one has been in a [[fight]]
 *adj*
**5** relating to war or a war: the war effort; a war correspondent
 *vb* **[[warring]]**, **warred**
**6** to conduct a war
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[battle]], [[clash]], [[wage war]], [[campaign]], [[combat]], [[do battle]], [[take up arms]]
