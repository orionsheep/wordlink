**adore** *vb* **[[adoring]]**, **adored**
**1** to [[love]] intensely or deeply
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[love]], [[honour]], [[admire]], [[worship]], [[esteem]], [[cherish]], [[revere]], [[dote on]]
**2** (*informal*) to like very much: I adore being in the country
**3** to [[worship]] a god with religious rites
 **[[adoration]]** *n* 
 **[[adoring]]** *adj* 
 **[[adoringly]]** *adv*
