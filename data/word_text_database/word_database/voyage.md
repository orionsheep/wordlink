**voyage** *n*
**1** a long [[journey]] by sea or in space
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[journey]], [[trip]], [[passage]], [[expedition]], [[crossing]], [[sail]], [[cruise]], excursion
 *vb* **-aging**, **-aged**
**2** to go on a voyage: in this story he voyages to Ireland
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[travel]], [[journey]], [[tour]], [[cruise]], [[steam]], [[take a trip]], [[go on an expedition]]
 **[[voyager]]** *n*
