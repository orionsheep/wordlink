**exterior** *n*
**1** a part or [[surface]] that is on the [[outside]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outside]], [[face]], [[surface]], [[covering]], [[skin]], [[shell]], [[coating]], [[façade]]
**2** the [[outward]] appearance of a person: Amir's grumpy exterior concealed a warm heart
**3** a film scene shot [[outside]]
 *adj*
**4** of, situated on, or suitable for the [[outside]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outer]], [[outside]], [[external]], [[surface]], [[outward]], [[outermost]]
**5** coming or acting from [[outside]] or abroad
