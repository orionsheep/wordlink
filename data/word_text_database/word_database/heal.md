**heal** *vb*
**1** (of a wound) to repair by natural processes, such as by scar formation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mend]], [[get better]], [[get well]], [[regenerate]], [[show improvement]]
**2** to [[restore]] (someone) to health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cure]], [[restore]], [[mend]], [[make better]], [[remedy]], [[make good]], [[make well]]
**3** to repair (a rift in a personal relationship or an emotional wound)
 **[[healer]]** *n* 
 **[[healing]]** *n*, *adj*
