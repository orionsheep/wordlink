**vicious** *adj*
**1** [[cruel]] or [[violent]]: vicious attacks
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[savage]], [[brutal]], [[violent]], [[cruel]], [[ferocious]], [[barbarous]]
**2** forceful or [[ferocious]]: she gave the chair a vicious jerk
**3** intended to cause hurt or distress: vicious letters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[malicious]], [[vindictive]], [[spiteful]], [[mean]], [[cruel]], [[venomous]]
**4** (of an animal) fierce or hostile
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[savage]], [[brutal]], [[violent]], [[cruel]], [[ferocious]], [[barbarous]]
 **[[viciously]]** *adv* 
 **[[viciousness]]** *n*
