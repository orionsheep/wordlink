**rogue** *n*
**1** a dishonest or unprincipled person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scoundrel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[crook]] (*informal*), [[villain]], [[fraud]] (*informal*), [[blackguard]], [[skelm]] (*S African*), [[rorter]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[wrong 'un]] (*slang*)
**2** a mischievous person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scamp]], [[rascal]], [[scally]] (*Northwest England* *dialect*), [[nointer]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**3** a crop plant which is inferior, diseased, or of a different variety
**4** an inferior or defective specimen
 *adj*
**5** (of a wild animal) having a savage temper and living apart from the herd: a rogue elephant
**6** inferior or defective: rogue heroin
 **[[roguish]]** *adj*
