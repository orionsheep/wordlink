**entail** *vb*
**1** to [[bring about]] or impose inevitably: he wasn't ready for all that marriage entails
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[involve]], [[require]], [[produce]], [[demand]], [[call for]], [[occasion]] (*formal*), [[need]], [[bring about]]
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ*, *property law*) to restrict the ability to inherit (a piece of property) to designated heirs
