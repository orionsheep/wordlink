**idol** (**eye**-dl) *n*
**1** an object of excessive devotion or admiration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hero]], [[pin-up]], [[favourite]], [[pet]], [[darling]], [[beloved]] (*slang*), [[fave]] (*informal*)
**2** an image of a [[god]] used as an object of worship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[graven image]], [[god]], [[deity]]
