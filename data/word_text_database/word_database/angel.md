**angel** *n*
**1** a spiritual being believed to be an attendant or messenger of God
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[divine messenger]], [[cherub]], [[archangel]], [[seraph]]
**2** a conventional representation of an angel as a human being with wings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[divine messenger]], [[cherub]], [[archangel]], [[seraph]]
**3** (*informal*) a person who is kind, pure, or beautiful
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dear]], [[beauty]], [[saint]], [[treasure]], [[darling]], [[jewel]], [[gem]], [[paragon]]
**4** (*informal*) an investor in a theatrical production
