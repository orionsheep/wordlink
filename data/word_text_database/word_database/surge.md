**surge** *n*
**1** a sudden powerful increase: a surge in spending
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rush]], [[flood]]
**2** a strong rolling movement of the sea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[wave]], [[rush]], [[roller]], [[gush]], [[outpouring]]
**3** a heavy rolling motion or sound: a great surge of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wave]], [[rush]], [[storm]], [[torrent]], eruption
 *vb* **surging**, **[[surged]]**
**4** to move forward strongly and suddenly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rush]], [[pour]], [[rise]], [[gush]]
**5** to increase quickly and strongly
**6** (of the sea) to [[rise]] or [[roll]] with a heavy swelling motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[roll]], [[rush]], [[heave]]
