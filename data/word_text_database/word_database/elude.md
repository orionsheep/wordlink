**elude** *vb* **eluding**, **eluded**
**1** to [[avoid]] or [[escape]] from (someone or something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evade]], [[escape]], [[lose]], [[avoid]], [[flee]], [[duck]] (*informal*), [[dodge]], [[get away from]]
**2** to fail to be understood or remembered by: the mysteries of commerce elude me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[escape]], [[baffle]], [[frustrate]], [[puzzle]], [[stump]], [[be beyond]] (someone)
