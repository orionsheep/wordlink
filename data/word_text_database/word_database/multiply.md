**multiply** *vb* **-plies**, **-plying**, **-plied**
**1** to [[increase]] or cause to [[increase]] in number, quantity, or degree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[extend]], [[expand]], [[spread]], [[build up]], [[proliferate]]
**2** to combine numbers or quantities by multiplication
**3** to [[increase]] in number by reproduction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reproduce]], [[breed]], [[propagate]]
