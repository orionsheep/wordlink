**censor** *n*
**1** a person authorized to examine films, letters, or publications, in order to ban or [[cut]] anything considered obscene or objectionable
 *vb*
**2** to ban or [[cut]] portions of (a film, letter, or publication)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expurgate]], [[cut]], [[blue-pencil]], [[bowdlerize]]
