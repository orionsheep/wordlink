**explode** *vb* **-ploding**, **-ploded**
**1** to [[burst]] with great violence; [[blow up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow up]], [[erupt]], [[burst]], [[go off]], [[shatter]]
**2** (of a gas) to undergo a sudden violent expansion as a result of a fast chemical or nuclear reaction
**3** to react suddenly or violently with emotion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lose your temper]], [[rage]], [[erupt]], [[become angry]], [[hit the roof]] (*informal*), [[go crook]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
**4** (esp. of a population) to [[increase]] rapidly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[grow]], [[develop]], [[extend]], [[advance]], [[shoot up]], [[soar]], [[boost]]
**5** to show (a theory, etc.) to be baseless
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disprove]], [[discredit]], [[refute]], [[demolish]], [[repudiate]], [[put paid to]], [[invalidate]], [[debunk]]
