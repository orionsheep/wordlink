**luxury** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** [[indulgence]] in rich and sumptuous living
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opulence]], [[splendour]], [[richness]], [[extravagance]], [[affluence]], [[hedonism]], [[a bed of roses]], [[the life of Riley]]
**2** something considered an [[indulgence]] rather than a necessity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extravagance]], [[treat]], [[extra]], [[indulgence]], frill
 *adj*
**3** relating to, indicating, or supplying luxury: a luxury hotel
