**evolution** *n*
**1** (*biol*) a gradual change in the characteristics of a population of animals or plants over successive generations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rise]], [[development]], [[adaptation]], [[natural selection]], [[Darwinism]], [[survival of the fittest]]
**2** a gradual [[development]], esp. to a more complex form
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[development]], [[growth]], [[advance]], [[progress]], [[working out]], [[expansion]], [[extension]], [[unfolding]]
 **[[evolutionary]]** *adj*
