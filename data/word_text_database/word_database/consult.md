**consult** *vb*
**1** to [[ask]] advice from or discuss matters with (someone): he never consults his wife about what he's about to do
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ask]], [[refer to]], [[turn to]], [[take counsel]], pick (someone's) brains, [[question]]
**2** to [[refer to]] for information: she consulted the map again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refer to]], [[check in]], [[look in]]
