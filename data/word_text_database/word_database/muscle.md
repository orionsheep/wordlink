**muscle** *n*
**1** a tissue in the body composed of bundles of elongated cells which produce movement in an organ or part by contracting or relaxing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tendon]], [[sinew]]
**2** an organ composed of muscle tissue: the heart is essentially just another muscle
**3** [[strength]] or force: we do not have the political muscle to force through these reforms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strength]], [[might]], [[power]], [[weight]], [[stamina]], brawn
 *vb* **-cling**, **-cled**
**4** **[[muscle in]]** to force one's way into a situation; intrude: I don't like the way he's trying to [[muscle in]] here
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[muscle in]]**: impose yourself, encroach, butt in, force your way in
