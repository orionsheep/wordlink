**third** *adj*
**1** of or being number three in a series
**2** rated, graded, or ranked below the second level
**3** denoting the third from lowest forward gear in a motor vehicle
 *n*
**4** one of three equal parts of something
**5** the fraction equal to one divided by three (^1⁄3)
**6** the third from lowest forward gear in a motor vehicle
**7** (*Brit*) an honours degree of the third and usually the lowest class
**8** (*music*) the interval between one note and the note four semitones (**major third**) or three semitones (**minor third**) higher or lower than it
 *adv*
**9** Also: **thirdly** in the third place
