◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idle]], [[inactive]], [[indolent]], [[slack]], [[negligent]], [[inert]], [[workshy]], [[slothful]] (*formal*)
**2** done in a relaxed manner with little effort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lethargic]], [[languorous]], [[slow-moving]], [[languid]], [[sleepy]], [[sluggish]], [[drowsy]], [[somnolent]]
**3** moving in a [[sluggish]] manner: the lazy drift of the bubbles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lethargic]], [[languorous]], [[slow-moving]], [[languid]], [[sleepy]], [[sluggish]], [[drowsy]], [[somnolent]]
