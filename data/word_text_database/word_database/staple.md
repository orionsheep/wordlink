**staple**^2 *adj*
**1** of prime importance; [[principal]]: the staple diet of a country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[principal]], [[chief]], [[main]], [[key]], [[basic]], [[fundamental]], predominant
 *n*
**2** something that forms a [[main]] part of the product, consumption, or trade of a region
**3** a [[main]] constituent of anything: the personal reflections which make up the staple of the book
