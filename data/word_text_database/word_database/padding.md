**padding** *n*
**1** any soft material used to pad something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[filling]], [[stuffing]], [[packing]], [[wadding]]
**2** unnecessary information put into a speech or written work to make it longer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: waffle (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[hot air]] (*informal*), [[verbiage]], [[wordiness]], [[verbosity]]
