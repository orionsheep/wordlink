**and** *conj*
**1** [[in addition to]]: plants and birds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[also]], [[including]], [[along with]], [[together with]], [[in addition to]], [[as well as]]
**2** as a consequence: she fell downstairs and broke her neck
**3** afterwards: she excused herself and left
**4** used for emphasis or to indicate repetition or continuity: they called again and again
**5** used to express a contrast between instances of something: there are jobs and jobs
**6** (*informal*) used in place of *to* in infinitives after verbs such as *try, go,* and *come*: come and see us again
