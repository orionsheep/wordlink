**sick** *adj*
**1** vomiting or likely to vomit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nauseous]], [[ill]], [[queasy]], [[nauseated]]
**2** physically or mentally [[unwell]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unwell]], [[ill]], [[poorly]] (*informal*), [[diseased]], [[crook]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[ailing]], [[under the weather]] (*informal*), [[indisposed]]
**3** of or for [[ill]] people: sick pay
**4** deeply affected with mental or spiritual distress: sick at heart
**5** mentally disturbed
**6** (*informal*) making fun of death, illness, or misfortune: a sick joke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[morbid]], [[sadistic]], [[black]], [[macabre]], [[ghoulish]]
**7** **sick of** *or ***sick and [[tired]] of** (*informal*) disgusted by or [[weary]] of: I'm sick of this town
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tired]], [[bored]], [[fed up]], [[weary]], jaded
 *n*, *[[vb]]*
**8** (*informal*) same as **vomit**
