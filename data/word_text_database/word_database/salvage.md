**salvage** *n*
**1** the [[rescue]] of a ship or its cargo from loss at sea
**2** the saving of any goods or property from destruction or waste
**3** the goods or property so saved
**4** compensation paid for the salvage of a ship or its cargo
 *vb* **-vaging**, **-vaged**
**5** to [[save]] (goods or property) from shipwreck, destruction, or waste
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[save]], [[recover]], [[rescue]], [[get back]], [[retrieve]], [[redeem]]
**6** to gain (something beneficial) from a failure: it's too late to salvage anything from the whole dismal display
 **[[salvageable]]** *adj*
