**slaughter** *n*
**1** the indiscriminate or brutal [[killing]] of large numbers of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slaying]], [[killing]], [[murder]], [[massacre]], [[bloodshed]], [[carnage]], [[butchery]]
**2** the savage [[killing]] of a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slaying]], [[killing]], [[murder]], [[massacre]], [[bloodshed]], [[carnage]], [[butchery]]
**3** the [[killing]] of animals for food
 *vb*
**4** to [[kill]] indiscriminately or in large numbers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[butcher]], [[kill]], slay (*archaic*, *literary*), [[massacre]]
**5** to [[kill]] brutally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kill]] (*informal*), [[murder]], [[massacre]], [[destroy]], [[execute]], [[assassinate]]
**6** to [[kill]] (animals) for food
**7** (*informal*) (in sport) to defeat easily
