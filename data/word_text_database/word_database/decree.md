**decree** *n*
**1** a [[law]] made by someone in authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[law]], [[order]], [[ruling]], [[act]], [[command]], [[statute]], [[proclamation]], [[edict]]
**2** a [[judgment]] of a court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[judgment]], [[finding]], [[ruling]], [[decision]], [[verdict]], arbitration
 *vb* **decreeing**, **[[decreed]]**
**3** to [[order]] by decree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[order]], [[rule]], [[command]], [[demand]], [[proclaim]], [[prescribe]], [[pronounce]], [[ordain]] (*formal*)
