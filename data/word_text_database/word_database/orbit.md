**orbit** *n*
**1** the curved [[path]] followed by something, such as a heavenly body or spacecraft, in its motion around another body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[path]], [[course]], [[cycle]], [[circle]], [[revolution]], [[rotation]], [[trajectory]], [[sweep]]
**2** a [[range]] or sphere of action or [[influence]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sphere of influence]], [[reach]], [[range]], [[influence]], [[province]], [[scope]], [[domain]], [[compass]]
**3** (*anatomy*) the eye socket
 *vb* **-biting**, **-bited**
**4** to move around (a heavenly body) in an orbit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[circle]], [[ring]], [[go round]], [[revolve around]], [[encircle]], [[circumscribe]], [[circumnavigate]]
**5** to send (a satellite or spacecraft) into orbit
 **[[orbital]]** *adj*
