**uncomfortable** *adj*
**1** not physically relaxed: he was forced to sit in an uncomfortable cross-legged position
**2** not comfortable to be in or use: an uncomfortable chair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[painful]], [[awkward]], [[rough]]
**3** causing discomfort or unease: the uncomfortable truth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uneasy]], [[troubled]], [[disturbed]], [[embarrassed]], [[awkward]], [[discomfited]]
 **[[uncomfortably]]** *adv*
