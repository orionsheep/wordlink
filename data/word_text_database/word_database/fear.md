**fear** *n*
**1** a feeling of distress or [[alarm]] caused by danger or pain that is about to happen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dread]], [[horror]], [[panic]], [[terror]], [[fright]], [[alarm]], [[trepidation]] (*formal*), [[fearfulness]]
**2** something that causes fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bugbear]], [[bête noire]], [[horror]], [[nightmare]], [[anxiety]], [[terror]], [[dread]], [[spectre]]
**3** possibility or likelihood: there is no fear of her agreeing to that
**4** **no fear** (*informal*) certainly not
 *vb*
**5** to [[be afraid of]] (someone or something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be afraid of]], [[dread]], [[shudder at]], [[be fearful of]], [[tremble at]], [[be terrified by]], [[take fright at]], [[shake in your shoes about]]
**6** (*formal*) to be sorry: I fear the children were not very good yesterday
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regret]], [[feel]], [[suspect]], [[have a feeling]], [[have a hunch]], [[have a sneaking suspicion]], [[have a funny feeling]]
**7** **fear for** to [[feel]] [[anxiety]] about
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[fear for something or someone]]**: worry about, be anxious about, [[feel]] concern for
 **[[fearless]]** *adj* 
 **[[fearlessly]]** *adv*
