**vulnerable** *adj*
**1** able to be physically or emotionally hurt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[susceptible]], [[helpless]], [[unprotected]], [[defenceless]], [[exposed]], [[weak]], [[sensitive]], [[tender]]
**2** easily influenced or tempted
**3** (*military*) [[exposed]] to attack
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exposed]], [[open]], [[unprotected]], [[defenceless]], [[accessible]], [[wide open]], [[assailable]]
**4** financially [[weak]] and likely to fail: this company could be vulnerable in a prolonged economic slump
**5** (*bridge*) (of a side that has won one game towards rubber) subject to increased bonuses or penalties
 **[[vulnerability]]** *n*
