**reckon** *vb*
**1** (*informal*) to be of the opinion: she reckoned she could find them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[believe]], [[suppose]], [[imagine]], [[assume]], [[guess]] (*informal*)
**2** to [[consider]]: he reckoned himself a failure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consider]], [[rate]], [[account]], [[judge]], [[regard]], [[count]], [[esteem]] (*formal*), [[deem]]
**3** to [[calculate]] or [[compute]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[count]], [[figure]], [[total]], [[calculate]], [[compute]], [[add up]], [[tally]], [[number]]
**4** to expect
**5** **reckon with** to take into [[account]]: there are a few problems to reckon with
**6** **reckon without** to fail to take into [[account]]
**7** **reckon on *or* upon** to rely on or expect: they can't reckon on your automatic support
