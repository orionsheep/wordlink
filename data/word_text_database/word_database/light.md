**light**^2 *adj*
**1** not heavy; weighing relatively little
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insubstantial]], [[thin]], [[slight]], [[portable]], [[buoyant]], [[airy]], [[flimsy]], [[underweight]]
**2** relatively low in density, strength, amount, degree, etc.: light oil; light alloy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weak]], [[soft]], [[gentle]], [[moderate]], [[slight]], [[mild]], [[faint]], [[indistinct]]
**3** lacking sufficient weight
**4** not bulky or clumsy: light bedclothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insubstantial]], [[thin]], [[slight]], [[portable]], [[buoyant]], [[airy]], [[flimsy]], [[underweight]]
**5** not serious or difficult to understand; [[entertaining]]: light music
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[light-hearted]], [[funny]], [[entertaining]], [[amusing]], [[witty]], [[humorous]], [[frivolous]], [[unserious]]
**6** [[graceful]] or [[agile]]: light movements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nimble]], [[graceful]], [[deft]], [[agile]], [[sprightly]], [[lithe]], [[limber]], [[lissom]](e)
**7** without strong emphasis or serious meaning: he gazed about with a light inattentive smile
**8** easily digested: a light lunch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[digestible]], [[modest]], [[frugal]]
**9** relatively low in alcohol: a light wine
**10** without burdens, difficulties, or problems: a light heart lives longest
**11** dizzy or unclear: a light head
**12** (of bread or cake) spongy or well risen
**13**
**A** (of transport) designed to carry light loads
 **B** (of a vessel, aircraft, or other transport) not loaded
**14** carrying light arms or equipment: light infantry
**15** (of an industry) producing small consumer goods using light machinery
**16** **make light of** to treat as insignificant or unimportant
 *adv*
**17** with little equipment or luggage: travelling light
 *vb* **lighting**, **lighted*** or ***lit**
**18** (esp. of birds) to settle or land after flight
**19** (foll. by *on* or *upon*) to discover by chance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[light on something]]**: come across, find, discover, encounter, stumble on, hit upon, happen upon
