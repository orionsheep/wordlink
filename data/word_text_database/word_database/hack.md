**hack**^2 *n*
**1** a [[writer]] or [[journalist]] who produces work fast and on a regular basis
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reporter]], [[writer]], [[correspondent]], [[journalist]], [[scribbler]], [[contributor]], [[literary hack]]
**2** a horse kept for riding, often one for hire
**3** (*Brit*) a country ride on horseback
 *vb*
**4** (*Brit*) to ride (a horse) cross-country for pleasure
 *adj*
**5** unoriginal or of a low standard: clumsily contrived hack verse
