**king** *n*
**1** a male [[ruler]] of a country who has inherited the throne from his parents
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ruler]], [[monarch]], [[sovereign]], [[leader]], [[lord]], [[Crown]], [[emperor]], [[head of state]]
**2** a [[ruler]] or chief: the king of the fairies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ruler]], [[monarch]], [[sovereign]], [[leader]], [[lord]], [[Crown]], [[emperor]], [[head of state]]
**3** a person, animal, or thing considered as the best or most important of its kind: the king of rock and roll
**4** a playing card with a picture of a king on it
**5** a chess piece, able to move one square in any direction: the object of the game is to checkmate one's opponent's king
**6** (*draughts*) a piece which has moved entirely across the board and been crowned and which may therefore move backwards as well as forwards
 **[[kingship]]** *n*
