**club** *n*
**1** a [[group]] or [[association]] of people with common aims or interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[association]], [[company]], [[group]], [[union]], [[society]], [[lodge]], [[guild]], [[fraternity]] (*US** & **Canad*)
**2** the building used by such a [[group]]
**3** a stout [[stick]] used as a weapon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick]], [[bat]], [[bludgeon]], [[truncheon]] (*Brit*), [[cosh]] (*Brit*), [[cudgel]]
**4** a [[stick]] or [[bat]] used to [[strike]] the ball in various sports, esp. golf
**5** an establishment where people go to drink and dance, esp. late in the evening
**6** a building in which members go to meet, dine, read, etc.
**7** (*chiefly* *Brit*) an organization, esp. in a shop, set up as a means of saving
**8** a playing card marked with one or more black trefoil symbols
 *vb* **clubbing**, **clubbed**
**9** to [[beat]] with a club
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[strike]], [[hammer]] (*informal*), [[batter]], [[bash]], [[bludgeon]], [[pummel]], [[cosh]] (*Brit*)
**10** **club together** to combine resources or efforts for a common purpose
