**pronounce** *vb* **-nouncing**, **-nounced**
**1** to [[speak]] (a [[sound]] or sounds), esp. clearly or in a certain way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[say]], [[speak]], [[sound]], [[articulate]], [[enunciate]]
**2** to [[announce]] or [[declare]] officially: a specialist pronounced her fully fit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[declare]], [[announce]], [[deliver]], [[proclaim]], [[decree]], [[affirm]]
**3** to [[declare]] as one's judgment: he pronounced the wine drinkable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[declare]], [[announce]], [[deliver]], [[proclaim]], [[decree]], [[affirm]]
 **[[pronounceable]]** *adj*
