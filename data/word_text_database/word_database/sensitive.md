**sensitive** *adj*
**1** [[easily hurt]]; [[tender]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delicate]], [[tender]]
**2** [[responsive]] to feelings and moods
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thoughtful]], [[kindly]], [[concerned]], [[patient]], [[attentive]], [[tactful]], [[unselfish]]
**3** [[responsive]] to external stimuli or impressions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[susceptible]], [[responsive]], [[easily affected]]
**4** [[easily offended]] or shocked
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[touchy]], [[oversensitive]], [[easily upset]], [[easily offended]], [[easily hurt]]
**5** (of a subject or issue) liable to arouse controversy or strong feelings
**6** (of an instrument) capable of registering small differences or changes in amounts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[precise]], [[fine]], [[acute]], [[keen]], [[responsive]]
**7** (of photographic materials) responding readily to the action of light: a sensitive emulsion
**8** (*chiefly* *US*) connected with matters affecting national security
 **[[sensitively]]** *adv* 
 **[[sensitivity]]** *n*
