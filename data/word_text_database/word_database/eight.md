**eight** *n*
**1** the cardinal number that is the sum of one and seven
**2** a numeral, 8 or VIII, representing this number
**3** something representing or consisting of eight units
**4** (*rowing*)
**A** a light narrow boat rowed by eight people
 **B** the crew of such a boat
 *adj*
**5** amounting to eight: eight apples
 **[[eighth]]** *adj*, *n*
