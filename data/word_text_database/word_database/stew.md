**stew** *n*
**1** a dish of meat, fish, or other food, cooked slowly in a closed pot
**2** **in a stew** (*informal*) in a troubled or worried state
 *vb*
**3** to cook by long slow simmering in a closed pot
**4** (*informal*) (of a person) to be too hot
**5** to cause (tea) to become bitter or (of tea) to become bitter through infusing for too long
**6** **stew in one's own juice** to suffer, without help, the results of one's actions
