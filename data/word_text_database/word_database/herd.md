**herd** *n*
**1** a large group of mammals, esp. cattle, living and feeding together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flock]], [[crowd]], [[collection]], [[mass]], [[drove]], [[mob]], [[swarm]], [[horde]]
**2** (*often* *derogatory*) a large group of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flock]], [[crowd]], [[collection]], [[mass]], [[drove]], [[mob]], [[swarm]], [[horde]]
 *vb*
**3** to collect or be collected into, or as if into, a herd
