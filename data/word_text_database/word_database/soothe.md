**soothe** *vb* **[[soothing]]**, **soothed**
**1** to make (a worried or angry person) [[calm]] and relaxed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calm]], [[still]], [[quiet]], [[hush]], [[appease]], [[lull]], [[pacify]], [[mollify]]
**2** (of an ointment or cream) to [[relieve]] (pain)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relieve]], [[ease]], [[alleviate]], [[assuage]]
 **[[soothing]]** *adj*
