**trauma** (**traw**-ma) *n*
**1** (*psychol*) an emotional [[shock]] that may have long-lasting effects
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shock]], [[suffering]], [[pain]], [[torture]], [[ordeal]], [[anguish]]
**2** (*pathol*) any bodily [[injury]] or [[wound]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[injury]], [[damage]], [[hurt]], [[wound]], [[agony]]
 **[[traumatic]]** *adj* 
 **[[traumatically]]** *adv* 
 **[[traumatize]]** * or ***-ise** *vb*
