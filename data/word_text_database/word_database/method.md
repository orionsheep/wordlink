**method** *n*
**1** a [[way]] of doing something, esp. a systematic or regular one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manner]], [[process]], [[approach]], [[technique]], [[way]], [[system]], [[style]], [[procedure]]
**2** [[orderliness]] of thought or action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[orderliness]], [[planning]], [[order]], [[system]], [[purpose]], [[pattern]], [[organization]], [[regularity]]
**3** the techniques of a particular field or subject
