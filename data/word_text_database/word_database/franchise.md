**franchise** *n*
**1** the right to vote, esp. for a member of parliament
**2** any exemption, privilege, or right granted by a public authority
**3**
**A** (*commerce*) authorization granted by a manufacturing or entertainment enterprise to market its products
 **B** an organization that receives such an authorization
**4** a series of films, video games, etc that has a strong identity and lends itself to merchandising
 *vb* **-chising**, **-chised**
**5** (*commerce*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) to grant (a person, firm, etc.) a franchise
