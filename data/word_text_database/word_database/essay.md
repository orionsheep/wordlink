**essay** *n*
**1** a short literary [[composition]] on a single subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[composition]], [[study]], [[paper]], [[article]], [[piece]], [[assignment]], [[discourse]], [[tract]]
**2** a short [[piece]] of writing on a subject done as an exercise by a student
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[composition]], [[study]], [[paper]], [[article]], [[piece]], [[assignment]], [[discourse]], [[tract]]
**3** an [[attempt]]
 *vb*
**4** (*formal*) to [[attempt]]: he essayed a faint smile
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attempt]], [[try]], [[undertake]], [[endeavour]]
