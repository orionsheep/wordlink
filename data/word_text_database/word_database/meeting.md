**meeting** *n*
**1** an act of coming together: a meeting was fixed for the following day
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[encounter]], [[introduction]], [[confrontation]], [[engagement]], [[rendezvous]], [[tryst]], [[assignation]]
**2** an [[assembly]] or [[gathering]] of people: the meeting voted in favour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conference]], [[gathering]], [[assembly]], [[congress]], [[session]], [[convention]], [[get-together]] (*informal*), [[reunion]], [[hui]] (*NZ*)
**3** a sporting competition, as of athletes, or of horse racing
