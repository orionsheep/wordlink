**summary** *n*, *pl* **-maries**
**1** a brief account giving the main points of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[synopsis]], [[résumé]], [[précis]], [[review]], [[outline]], [[rundown]], abridgment
 *adj*
**2** performed quickly, without formality or attention to details: a summary judgment
 **[[summarily]]** *adv*
