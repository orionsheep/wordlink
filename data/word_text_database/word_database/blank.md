**blank** *adj*
**1** (of a writing surface) not written on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmarked]], [[white]], [[clear]], [[clean]], [[empty]], [[plain]], [[bare]], [[void]]
**2** (of a form, etc.) with spaces left for details to be filled in
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmarked]], [[white]], [[clear]], [[clean]], [[empty]], [[plain]], [[bare]], [[void]]
**3** without ornament or break: a blank wall
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmarked]], [[white]], [[clear]], [[clean]], [[empty]], [[plain]], [[bare]], [[void]]
**4** [[empty]] or [[void]]: a blank [[space]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unmarked]], [[white]], [[clear]], [[clean]], [[empty]], [[plain]], [[bare]], [[void]]
**5** showing no interest or expression: a blank look
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expressionless]], [[empty]], [[vague]], [[vacant]], [[deadpan]], [[impassive]], [[poker-faced]] (*informal*)
**6** lacking ideas or inspiration: his mind went blank
 *n*
**7** an [[empty space]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empty space]], [[space]], [[gap]]
**8** an [[empty space]] for writing in
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[empty space]], [[space]], [[gap]]
**9** the condition of not understanding: my mind went a complete blank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[void]], [[vacuum]], [[vacancy]], [[emptiness]], [[nothingness]]
**10** a mark, often a dash, in place of a word
**11** **draw a blank** to get no results from something
 *vb*
**12** (foll. by *out*) to cross out, blot, or obscure
**13** **blank something out** to refuse to think about something; [[clear]] something from one's mind
**14** (*slang*) to ignore: the crowd blanked her for the first four numbers
 **[[blankly]]** *adv*
