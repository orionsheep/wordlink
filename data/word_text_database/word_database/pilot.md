**pilot** *n*
**1** a person who is qualified to [[fly]] an aircraft or spacecraft
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[airman]], [[flyer]] (*old-fashioned*), [[aviator]], [[aeronaut]]
**2** a person employed to [[steer]] a ship into or out of a port
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[helmsman]], [[navigator]], steersman *or* woman *or* person
**3** a person who acts as a guide
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[helmsman]], [[navigator]], steersman *or* woman *or* person
 *adj*
**4** serving as a [[test]] or [[trial]]: a pilot scheme
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trial]], [[test]], [[model]], [[sample]], [[experimental]]
**5** serving as a guide: a pilot beacon
 *vb* **-loting**, **-loted**
**6** to act as pilot of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fly]], [[operate]], [[be at the controls of]]
**7** to guide or lead (a project or people): the legislation was piloted through its committee stage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[direct]], [[conduct]], [[steer]]
