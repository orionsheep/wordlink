**silhouette** *n*
**1** the [[outline]] of a dark [[shape]] seen against a light background
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[form]], [[shape]], [[profile]]
**2** an [[outline]] drawing, often a [[profile]] portrait, filled in with black
 *vb* **-etting**, **-etted**
**3** to show (something) in silhouette
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[etch]]
