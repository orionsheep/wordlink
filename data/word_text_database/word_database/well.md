**well**^2 *n*
**1** a [[hole]] or [[shaft]] bored into the earth to tap a supply of water, oil, or gas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hole]], [[bore]], [[pit]], [[shaft]]
**2** an open [[shaft]] through the floors of a building, used for a staircase
 *vb*
**3** to [[flow]] upwards or outwards: tears welled up into my eyes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[spring]], [[pour]], [[jet]], [[surge]], [[gush]], [[spurt]], [[spout]]
