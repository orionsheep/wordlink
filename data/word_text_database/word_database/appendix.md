**appendix** (ap-**pen**-dix) *n*, *pl* **-dices** (-diss-seez) * or ***-dixes**
**1** separate additional material at the end of a book
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supplement]], [[postscript]], [[adjunct]], [[appendage]], [[addendum]], [[addition]]
**2** (*anatomy*) a short thin tube, closed at one end and attached to the large intestine at the other end
