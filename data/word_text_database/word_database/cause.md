**cause** *n*
**1** something that produces a particular effect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[origin]], [[source]], [[spring]], [[agent]], [[maker]], [[producer]], [[root]], [[beginning]]
**2** [[grounds]] for action; justification: there is cause for concern
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reason]], [[call]], [[need]], [[grounds]], [[basis]], [[incentive]], [[motive]], [[motivation]]
**3** an [[aim]] or [[principle]] which an individual or group is interested in and supports: the Socialist cause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aim]], [[movement]], [[principle]], [[ideal]], enterprise
 *vb* **causing**, **[[caused]]**
**4** to be the cause of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[produce]], [[create]], [[lead to]], [[result in]], [[generate]], [[induce]], [[bring about]], [[give rise to]]
 **[[causeless]]** *adj*
