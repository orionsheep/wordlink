**insert** *vb*
**1** to [[place]] or fit (something) inside something else
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put]], [[place]], [[position]], [[slip]], [[slide]], [[slot]], [[thrust]], [[stick in]]
**2** to introduce (a clause or comment) into text or a speech
 *n*
**3** something inserted, esp. an advertisement in between the pages of a magazine
