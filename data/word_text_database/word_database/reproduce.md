**reproduce** *vb* **-ducing**, **-duced**
**1** to make a [[copy]] or representation of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[print]], [[copy]]
**2** (*biol*) to produce offspring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breed]], [[procreate]] (*formal*), [[multiply]], [[spawn]], [[propagate]]
**3** to re-create
 **[[reproducible]]** *adj*
