**funny** *adj* **-nier**, **-niest**
**1** causing amusement or laughter; [[humorous]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humorous]], [[amusing]], [[comical]], [[entertaining]], [[comic]], [[witty]], [[hilarious]], [[riotous]]
**2** [[peculiar]] or [[odd]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peculiar]], [[odd]], [[strange]], [[unusual]], [[bizarre]], [[curious]], [[weird]], [[mysterious]]
**3** (*informal*) faint or [[ill]]: this smell is making me feel a bit funny
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ill]], [[poorly]] (*informal*), [[sick]], [[odd]], [[crook]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[ailing]], [[unhealthy]], [[unwell]], [[off-colour]] (*informal*)
**4** **funny business** (*informal*) suspicious or dubious behaviour
 **[[funnily]]** *adv* 
 **[[funniness]]** *n*
