**diversion** *n*
**1** (*chiefly* *Brit*) an official [[detour]] used by traffic when a main route is closed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[detour]], [[roundabout way]], [[indirect course]]
**2** something that distracts someone's attention or concentration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distraction]], [[deviation]], [[digression]]
**3** the act of diverting from a specified course
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deviation]], [[departure]], [[straying]], [[divergence]], [[digression]]
**4** a pleasant or amusing [[pastime]] or activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pastime]], [[game]], [[sport]], [[entertainment]], [[hobby]], [[relaxation]], [[recreation]], [[distraction]]
 **[[diversionary]]** *adj*
