**catalogue** * or * (*US*) **catalog** *n*
**1** a book containing details of items for sale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[record]], [[schedule]], [[index]], [[register]], [[directory]], [[inventory]], [[gazetteer]]
**2** a [[list]] of all the books of a library
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[record]], [[schedule]], [[index]], [[register]], [[directory]], [[inventory]], [[gazetteer]]
**3** a [[list]] of events, qualities, or things considered as a group: a catalogue of killings
 *vb* **-loguing**, **-logued*** or ***-loging**, **-loged**
**4** to enter (an item) in a catalogue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[file]], [[index]], [[register]], [[classify]], [[inventory]], [[tabulate]], [[alphabetize]]
**5** to [[list]] a series of (events, qualities, or things): the report catalogues two decades of human-rights violations
 **[[cataloguer]]** *n*
