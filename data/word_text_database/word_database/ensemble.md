**ensemble** (on-**som**-bl) *n*
**1** all the parts of something considered as a [[whole]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collection]], [[set]], [[body]], [[whole]], [[total]], [[sum]], [[combination]], [[entity]]
**2** the complete [[outfit]] of clothes a person is wearing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outfit]], [[suit]], [[get-up]] (*informal*), [[costume]]
**3** a [[group]] of musicians or actors performing together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[company]], [[band]], [[troupe]], [[cast]], [[orchestra]], [[chorus]]
**4** (*music*) a passage in which all or most of the performers are playing or singing at once
