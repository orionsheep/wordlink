**fantastic** *adj*
**1** (*informal*) [[excellent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wonderful]], [[great]] (*informal*), [[excellent]], [[very good]], [[smashing]] (*informal*), [[superb]], [[tremendous]] (*informal*), [[magnificent]], [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**2** (*informal*) very large in degree or amount: a fantastic amount of money
**3** [[strange]] or exotic in appearance: fantastic costumes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strange]], [[bizarre]], [[grotesque]], [[fanciful]], [[outlandish]]
**4** difficult to believe or [[unlikely]] to happen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[implausible]], [[unlikely]], [[incredible]], [[absurd]], [[preposterous]], [[cock-and-bull]] (*informal*) 
 **[[fantastically]]** *adv*
