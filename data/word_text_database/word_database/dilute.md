**dilute** *vb* **-luting**, **-luted**
**1** to make (a liquid) less concentrated by adding water or another liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[water down]], [[thin]] (out), [[weaken]], [[adulterate]], [[make thinner]], [[cut]] (*informal*)
**2** to make (someone's power, idea, or role) weaker or less effective: socialists used their majority in parliament to dilute legislation crucial to developing a market economy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reduce]], [[weaken]], [[diminish]], [[temper]], [[decrease]], [[lessen]], [[diffuse]], mitigate
 *adj*
**3** (*chem*) (of a solution) having a low concentration
 **[[dilution]]** *n*
