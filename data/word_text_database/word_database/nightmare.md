**nightmare** *n*
**1** a terrifying or deeply distressing dream
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bad dream]], [[hallucination]]
**2** a terrifying or unpleasant experience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ordeal]], [[trial]], [[hell]] (*informal*), [[horror]], [[torture]], [[torment]], [[tribulation]], [[purgatory]]
**3** a thing that is feared: wheels and loose straps are a baggage handler's nightmare
 **[[nightmarish]]** *adj*
