**sickness** *n*
**1** a particular [[illness]] or [[disease]]: sleeping sickness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[illness]], [[disorder]], [[ailment]], [[disease]], [[complaint]], [[bug]] (*informal*), [[affliction]], [[malady]]
**2** the state of being ill or unhealthy: absent from work due to sickness
**3** a feeling of [[queasiness]] in the stomach followed by vomiting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nausea]], [[queasiness]]
