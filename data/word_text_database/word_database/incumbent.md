**incumbent** (*formal*) *n*
**1** a person who holds a particular office or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[holder]], [[keeper]], bearer
 *adj*
**2** morally [[binding]] as a duty: it is incumbent on cricketers to respect the umpire's impartiality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obligatory]], [[required]], [[necessary]], [[essential]], [[binding]], [[compulsory]], [[mandatory]], [[imperative]]
