**average** *n*
**1** the [[typical]] or [[normal]] amount or quality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[standard]], [[normal]], [[usual]], [[par]], [[mode]], [[mean]], [[medium]], [[norm]]
**2** the result obtained by adding the numbers or quantities in a set and dividing the total by the number of members in the set
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[standard]], [[normal]], [[usual]], [[par]], [[mode]], [[mean]], [[medium]], [[norm]]
**3** **[[on average]]** usually or typically
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[on average]]**: usually, generally, normally, typically, for the most part, as a rule
 *adj*
**4** [[usual]] or [[typical]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usual]], [[standard]], [[general]], [[normal]], [[regular]], [[ordinary]], [[typical]], [[commonplace]]
**5** calculated as an average
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mean]], [[middle]], [[medium]], [[intermediate]], [[median]]
**6** mediocre or inferior
 *vb* **-aging**, **-aged**
**7** to calculate or estimate the average of
**8** to amount to or [[be on average]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make on average]], [[be on average]], [[even out to]], [[do on average]], [[balance out to]]
