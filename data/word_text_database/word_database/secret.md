**secret** *adj*
**1** kept hidden or separate from the knowledge of all or all but a few others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[undisclosed]], [[unknown]], [[confidential]], [[underground]], [[undercover]], [[unrevealed]]
**2** [[secretive]]: she had become a secret drinker
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secretive]], [[reserved]], [[close]]
**3** operating without the knowledge of outsiders: secret organizations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[undercover]], furtive
 *n*
**4** something kept or to be kept hidden
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[private affair]]
**5** something [[unrevealed]]; a mystery: the secrets of nature
**6** an underlying explanation or reason: the secret of great-looking hair
**7** **[[in secret]]** without the knowledge of others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in secret]]**: [[secretly]], surreptitiously, slyly
 **[[secretly]]** *adv*
