**closet** *n*
**1** (*US** & **Austral*) a small cupboard
**2** a small private room
**3** short for **water closet**
**4** **in the closet** (*informal*) not openly acknowledging the fact that one is homosexual
 *adj*
**5** private or secret: a closet fan of horror films
 *vb* **-eting**, **-eted**
**6** to shut away in private, esp. in order to talk: he was closeted with the President
