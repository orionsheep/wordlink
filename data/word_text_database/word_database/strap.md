**strap** *n*
**1** a strip of strong flexible material used for carrying, lifting, fastening, or holding things in place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tie]], [[thong]], [[belt]]
**2** a loop of leather or rubber, hanging from the roof in a bus or train for standing passengers to hold on to
**3** **the strap** a beating with a strap as a punishment
 *vb* **strapping**, **strapped**
**4** to [[tie]] or bind (something) with a strap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tie]], [[thong]], [[belt]]
