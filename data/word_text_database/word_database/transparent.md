**transparent** *adj*
**1** able to be seen through; [[clear]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clear]], [[sheer]], [[see-through]], [[lucid]], [[translucent]], [[crystalline]], [[limpid]], [[diaphanous]]
**2** easy to understand or recognize; [[obvious]]: transparent honesty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obvious]], [[plain]], [[patent]], [[evident]], [[explicit]], [[manifest]], [[recognizable]], [[unambiguous]]
 **[[transparently]]** *adv*
