**obstacle** *n*
**1** a situation or event that prevents something being done: there are obstacles which could slow the development of a vaccine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hindrance]], [[bar]], [[difficulty]], [[barrier]], [[handicap]], [[hurdle]], [[hitch]], [[drawback]], [[uphill]] (*S African*)
**2** a person or thing that hinders movement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obstruction]], [[block]], [[barrier]], [[hurdle]], [[snag]], [[impediment]], [[blockage]], [[hindrance]]
