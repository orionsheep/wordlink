**than** *conj*, *prep*
**1** used to introduce the second element of a comparison, the first element of which expresses difference: I'm less optimistic than I was at first
**2** used to state a number, quantity, or value in approximate terms by contrasting it with another number, quantity, or value: temperatures lower than 25 degrees
**3** used after the adverbs *rather* and *sooner* to introduce a rejected alternative: fruit is examined by hand, rather than by machine
