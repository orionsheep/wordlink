**sprawl** *vb*
**1** to sit or lie with one's arms and legs spread out
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loll]], [[slump]], [[lounge]], [[flop]], [[slouch]]
**2** to spread out untidily over a large area: the pulp mill sprawled over the narrow flats
 *n*
**3** the part of a city or town that has not been planned and spreads out untidily over a large area: the huge Los Angeles sprawl
 **[[sprawling]]** *adj*
