**success** *n*
**1** the achievement of something attempted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[victory]], [[triumph]]
**2** the attainment of wealth, [[fame]], or position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prosperity]], [[fortune]], [[luck]], [[fame]]
**3** a person or thing that is successful
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hit]] (*informal*), [[winner]], [[smash]] (*informal*), [[triumph]], [[sensation]]
