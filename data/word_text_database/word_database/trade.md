**trade** *n*
**1** the buying and selling of goods and services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commerce]], [[business]], [[transactions]], [[dealing]], [[exchange]], [[traffic]], [[truck]], [[barter]]
**2** a person's [[job]], esp. a [[craft]] requiring skill: he's a plumber by trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[employment]], [[business]], [[craft]], [[profession]], [[occupation]], [[line of work]], [[métier]]
**3** the people and practices of an industry, [[craft]], or [[business]]
**4** amount of custom or commercial dealings: a brisk trade in second-hand weapons
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commerce]], [[business]], [[transactions]], [[dealing]], [[exchange]], [[traffic]], [[truck]], [[barter]]
**5** a specified market or [[business]]: the wool trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[employment]], [[business]], [[craft]], [[profession]], [[occupation]], [[line of work]], [[métier]]
**6** **trades** the trade winds
 *vb* **[[trading]]**, **traded**
