**submerge** *vb* **-merging**, **-merged**
**1** to put or go below the surface of water or another liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immerse]], [[plunge]], [[duck]]
**2** to involve totally: she submerged herself in her work
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overwhelm]], [[swamp]], [[engulf]], [[deluge]]
 **[[submersion]]** *n*
