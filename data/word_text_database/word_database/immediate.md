**immediate** *adj*
**1** taking place without delay: an immediate cut in interest rates
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instant]], [[prompt]], [[instantaneous]], [[quick]], [[on-the-spot]], [[split-second]]
**2** [[next]] or [[nearest]] in space, time, or relationship: our immediate neighbour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nearest]], [[next]], [[direct]], [[close]], [[near]]
**3** present; current: they had no immediate plans to [[close]] it
 **[[immediacy]]** *n* 
 **[[immediately]]** *adv*
