**substance** *n*
**1** the basic matter of which a thing consists
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[material]], [[body]], [[stuff]], [[fabric]]
**2** a specific type of matter with definite or fairly definite chemical composition: a fatty substance
**3** the essential [[meaning]] of a speech, thought, or written article
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meaning]], [[main point]], [[gist]], [[import]], [[significance]], [[essence]]
**4** important or meaningful quality: the only evidence of substance against him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[importance]], [[significance]], [[concreteness]]
**5** [[material]] possessions or [[wealth]]: a woman of substance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wealth]], [[means]], [[property]], [[assets]], [[resources]], [[estate]]
**6** **in substance** with regard to the most important points
