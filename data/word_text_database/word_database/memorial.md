**memorial** *n*
**1** something, such as a statue, built or displayed to preserve the memory of someone or something: a war memorial
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[monument]], [[shrine]], [[plaque]], cenotaph
 *adj*
**2** in memory of someone or something: a memorial service
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commemorative]], [[remembrance]], [[monumental]]
