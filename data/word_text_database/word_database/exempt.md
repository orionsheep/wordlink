**exempt** *adj*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immune]], [[free]], [[excepted]], [[excused]], [[released]], [[spared]], not liable to
 *vb*
**2** to [[release]] (someone) from an obligation, tax, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grant immunity]], [[free]], [[excuse]], [[release]], [[spare]], [[relieve]], [[discharge]], [[let off]]
 **[[exemption]]** *n*
