**partner** *n*
**1** either member of a couple in a relationship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spouse]], [[consort]], significant other (*informal*, *chiefly* *US*), [[mate]], husband *or* wife, [[plus-one]] (*informal*)
**2** a member of a business partnership
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[associate]], [[colleague]], [[collaborator]]
**3** one of a pair of dancers or of players on the same side in a game: her bridge partner
**4** an [[ally]] or [[companion]]: the country's main European trading partner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[companion]], [[ally]], [[colleague]], [[associate]], [[mate]], comrade
 *vb*
**5** to be the partner of (someone)
