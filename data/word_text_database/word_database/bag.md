**bag** *n*
**1** a flexible [[container]] with an opening at one end
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sack]], [[container]], [[sac]], [[receptacle]]
**2** the contents of such a [[container]]
**3** a piece of luggage
**4** a handbag
**5** a loose fold of skin under the eyes
**6** any [[sac]] in the body of an animal
**7** (*derogatory*, *slang*) an ugly or bad-tempered woman: an old bag
**8** the amount of game taken by a hunter
**9** **in the bag** (*slang*) assured of succeeding
 *vb* **bagging**, **bagged**
**10** to put into a bag
**11** to bulge or cause to bulge
**12** to [[capture]] or [[kill]], as in hunting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[catch]], [[kill]], [[shoot]], [[capture]], [[acquire]], [[trap]]
**13** (*informal*) to succeed in securing: he bagged the best chair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get]], [[land]] (*informal*), [[score]] (*slang*), [[capture]], [[acquire]], [[procure]]
