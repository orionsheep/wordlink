**treble** *adj*
**1** three times as much or as many
**2** of or denoting a soprano voice or part or a high-pitched instrument
**3** of the highest range of musical notes: these loudspeakers give excellent treble reproduction
 *n*
**4** a soprano voice or part or a high-pitched instrument
 *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**5** to make or become three times as much or as many: sales have trebled in three years
