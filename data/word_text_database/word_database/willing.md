**willing** *adj*
**1** favourably disposed or [[inclined]]: I'm willing to hear what you have to say
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclined]], [[prepared]], [[consenting]], [[agreeable]], [[compliant]], [[amenable]]
**2** keen and obliging: willing volunteers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ready]], [[game]] (*informal*) 
 **[[willingly]]** *adv* 
 **[[willingness]]** *n*
