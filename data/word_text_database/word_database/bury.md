**bury** *vb* **buries**, **burying**, **buried**
**1** to place (a corpse) in a grave
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inter]], [[lay to rest]], [[entomb]], [[consign to the grave]], [[inhume]]
**2** to place (something) in the earth and [[cover]] it with soil
**3** to [[cover]] (something) from sight; [[hide]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hide]], [[cover]], [[conceal]], [[stash]] (*informal*), [[secrete]], [[stow away]]
**4** to occupy (oneself) with deep concentration: he buried himself in his work
**5** to dismiss (a feeling) from the mind: they decided to bury any hard feelings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forget]]
