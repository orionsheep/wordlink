**distinction** *n*
**1** the act of distinguishing or differentiating
**2** a distinguishing [[feature]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feature]], [[quality]], [[characteristic]], [[mark]], [[individuality]], [[peculiarity]], [[distinctiveness]], [[particularity]]
**3** the state of being different or distinguishable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difference]], [[contrast]], [[variation]], [[differential]], [[discrepancy]], [[disparity]], [[dissimilarity]]
**4** special [[honour]], recognition, or [[fame]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[merit]], [[honour]], [[integrity]], [[excellence]], [[rectitude]]
**5** [[excellence]] of character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellence]], [[importance]], [[fame]], [[merit]], [[prominence]], [[greatness]], [[eminence]], [[repute]]
**6** a symbol of [[honour]] or rank
