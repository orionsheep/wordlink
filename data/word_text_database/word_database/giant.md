**giant** *n*
**1** a mythical figure of superhuman size and strength
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ogre]], [[monster]], [[titan]], [[colossus]]
**2** a person or thing of exceptional size, ability, or importance: industrial giants
 *adj*
**3** remarkably large
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[huge]], [[vast]], [[enormous]], [[tremendous]], [[immense]], [[titanic]], [[gigantic]], [[monumental]] (*informal*), [[supersize]]
**4** (of an atom or ion or its structure) having large numbers of particles present in a crystal lattice, with each particle exerting a strong force of attraction on those near to it
