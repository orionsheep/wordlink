**tendency** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** an [[inclination]] to act in a particular way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclination]], [[leaning]], [[liability]], [[disposition]], [[propensity]], [[susceptibility]], [[proclivity]] (*formal*), [[proneness]]
**2** the general course or drift of something
**3** a faction, esp. within a political party
