**brake** *n*
**1** a device for slowing or stopping a vehicle
**2** something that slows down or stops progress: he put a brake on my enthusiasm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[check]], [[curb]], [[restraint]], [[constraint]], rein
 *vb* **braking**, **[[braked]]**
**3** to [[slow]] down or cause to [[slow]] down, by or as if by using a brake
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slow]], [[decelerate]], [[reduce speed]]
