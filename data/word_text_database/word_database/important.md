**1** of great significance, value, or consequence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[significant]], [[critical]], [[substantial]], [[urgent]], [[serious]], [[far-reaching]], [[momentous]], [[seminal]]
**2** of social significance: the second most important person in the government 
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[powerful]], [[prominent]], [[commanding]], [[dominant]], [[influential]], [[eminent]], [[high-ranking]], [[authoritative]], [[skookum]] (*Canad*)
**3** of great concern: it was important to me to know

 **[[importantly]]** *adv*
