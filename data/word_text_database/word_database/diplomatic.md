**diplomatic** *adj*
**1** of or relating to diplomacy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consular]], [[official]], [[foreign-office]], [[ambassadorial]], [[foreign-politic]]
**2** skilled in negotiating between nations
**3** [[tactful]] in dealing with people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tactful]], [[politic]], [[sensitive]], [[subtle]], [[delicate]], [[polite]], [[discreet]], [[prudent]]
 **[[diplomatically]]** *adv*
