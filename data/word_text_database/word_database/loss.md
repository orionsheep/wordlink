**loss** *n*
**1** the act or an instance of [[losing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[losing]], [[waste]], [[squandering]], [[forfeiture]]
**2** the person, thing, or amount lost: the only loss was a sleeping bag
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deficit]], [[debt]], [[deficiency]], [[debit]], [[depletion]]
**3** the disadvantage or deprivation resulting from [[losing]]: a loss of sovereignty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[cost]], [[injury]], [[hurt]], [[harm]]
**4** **[[at a loss]]**
**A** uncertain what to do; bewildered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at a loss]]**: confused, puzzled, baffled, bewildered, helpless, stumped, perplexed, mystified
 **B** with income less than outlay: they cannot afford to run branches [[at a loss]]
