**cartoon** *n*
**1** a humorous or satirical [[drawing]] in a newspaper or magazine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drawing]], [[parody]], [[satire]], [[caricature]], [[comic strip]], [[takeoff]] (*informal*), [[lampoon]], [[sketch]]
**2** a film produced by photographing a series of gradually changing drawings, which give the illusion of movement when the series is projected rapidly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[animation]], [[animated film]], [[animated cartoon]]
 **[[cartoonist]]** *n*
