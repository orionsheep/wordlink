**parish** *n*
**1** an area that has its own [[church]] and a priest or pastor. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**parochial**
**2** the people who live in a parish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[community]], [[flock]], [[church]], [[congregation]]
**3** (in England and, formerly, Wales) the smallest unit of local government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[district]], [[community]]
