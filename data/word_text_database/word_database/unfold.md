**unfold** *vb*
**1** to [[open]] or [[spread out]] from a folded state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[open]], [[spread out]], [[undo]], [[expand]], [[unfurl]], [[unwrap]], [[unroll]]
**2** to [[reveal]] or be revealed: a terrible truth unfolds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reveal]], [[tell]], [[present]], [[show]], [[disclose]], [[uncover]], [[divulge]], [[make known]]
**3** to develop or be developed: the novel unfolds through their recollections
