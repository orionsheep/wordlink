**unravel** *vb* **-velling**, **-velled** (*US*) **-veling**, **-veled**
**1** to [[separate]] something knitted or woven into individual strands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[undo]], [[separate]], [[disentangle]], [[free]], [[unwind]], [[untangle]]
**2** to become separated into individual strands
**3** to [[explain]] or [[solve]]: we unravelled the secrets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solve]], [[explain]], [[work out]], [[resolve]], [[figure out]] (*informal*)
