**ass** *n*
**1** a mammal resembling the horse but with longer ears
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[donkey]], [[moke]] (*slang*)
**2** a foolish person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fool]], [[idiot]], twit (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[oaf]], [[jackass]], [[blockhead]], dorba *or* dorb (*Austral* *slang*)
