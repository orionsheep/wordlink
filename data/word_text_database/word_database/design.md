**design** *vb*
**1** to work out the structure or [[form]] of (something), by making a [[sketch]] or plans
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[draw]], [[draft]], [[trace]], [[outline]], [[devise]], [[sketch]], [[formulate]]
**2** to [[plan]] and make (something) artistically
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[create]], [[plan]], [[fashion]], [[propose]], [[invent]], [[conceive]], [[originate]], [[fabricate]]
**3** to [[intend]] (something) for a specific [[purpose]]: the move is designed to reduce travelling costs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intend]], [[mean]], [[plan]], [[aim]], [[purpose]]
 *n*
**4** a [[sketch]], [[plan]], or preliminary [[drawing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plan]], [[drawing]], [[model]], [[scheme]], [[draft]], [[outline]], [[sketch]], [[blueprint]]
**5** the [[arrangement]] or features of an artistic or decorative work: he built it to his own design
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pattern]], [[form]], [[style]], [[shape]], [[organization]], [[arrangement]], [[construction]]
**6** a finished artistic or decorative creation
**7** the art of designing
**8** an [[intention]]; [[purpose]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intention]], [[end]], [[aim]], [[goal]], [[target]], [[purpose]], [[object]], [[objective]]
**9** **have designs on** to plot to gain possession of
