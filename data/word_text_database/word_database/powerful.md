◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[influential]], [[dominant]], [[controlling]], [[commanding]], [[prevailing]], [[authoritative]], [[skookum]] (*Canad*)
**2** having great physical strength
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strong]], [[strapping]], [[mighty]], [[vigorous]], [[potent]], [[energetic]], [[sturdy]]
**3** extremely effective: a powerful drug
