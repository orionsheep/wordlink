**telescope** *n*
**1** an optical instrument for making distant objects appear closer by use of a combination of lenses
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glass]], [[scope]] (*informal*), spyglass
 *vb* **-scoping**, **-scoped**
**2** to [[shorten]] (something) while still keeping the important parts: a hundred years of change has been telescoped into five years
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shorten]], [[contract]], [[compress]], [[shrink]], [[condense]], [[abbreviate]], [[abridge]]
 **[[telescopic]]** *adj*
