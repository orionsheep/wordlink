**ceremony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a formal act or [[ritual]] performed for a special occasion: a wedding ceremony
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ritual]], [[service]], [[rite]], [[observance]], [[commemoration]], [[solemnities]]
**2** formally polite behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[formality]], [[ceremonial]], [[propriety]], [[decorum]]
**3** **stand on ceremony** to insist on or act with excessive [[formality]]
