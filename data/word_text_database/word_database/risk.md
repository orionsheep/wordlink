**risk** *n*
**1** the [[possibility]] of bringing about misfortune or loss
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[danger]], [[chance]], [[possibility]], [[hazard]]
**2** a person or thing considered as a potential [[hazard]]: in parts of the world transfusions carry the risk of infection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gamble]], [[chance]], [[speculation]], [[leap in the dark]]
**3** **at risk** in a dangerous situation
**4** **take *or* run a risk** to act without regard to the [[danger]] involved
 *vb*
**5** to act in spite of the [[possibility]] of (injury or loss): if they clamp down they risk a revolution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stand a chance of]]
**6** to expose to [[danger]] or loss
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dare]], [[endanger]], [[jeopardize]], [[imperil]], [[venture]], [[gamble]], [[hazard]]
 **[[risky]]** *adj*
