**tribute** *n*
**1** something given, done, or said as a mark of respect or admiration
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accolade]], [[testimonial]], [[eulogy]], [[recognition]], [[compliment]], [[commendation]], [[panegyric]]
**2** a payment by one ruler or state to another, usually as an acknowledgment of submission
**3** something that shows the merits of a particular quality of a person or thing: the car's low fuel consumption is a tribute to the quality of its engine
