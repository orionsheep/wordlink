**restraint** *n*
**1** something that restrains
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[limitation]], [[limit]], [[check]], [[ban]], [[embargo]], [[curb]], [[rein]], [[interdict]], restraining order (*US*, *law*)
**2** the ability to [[control]] one's impulses or passions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[self-control]], [[self-discipline]], [[self-restraint]], [[self-possession]]
**3** a restraining or being restrained
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[constraint]], [[limitation]], [[inhibition]], [[control]], [[restriction]]
