**rupture** *n*
**1** the act of breaking or the state of being broken
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[tear]], [[split]], [[crack]], [[rent]], [[burst]], [[breach]], [[fissure]]
**2** a [[breach]] of peaceful or friendly relations
**3** (*pathol*) a hernia
 *vb* **-turing**, **-tured**
**4** to [[break]] or [[burst]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[separate]], [[tear]], [[split]], [[crack]], [[burst]], [[sever]]
**5** to cause a [[breach]] in relations or friendship
**6** to affect or be affected with a hernia
