**infant** *n*
**1** a very young [[child]]; [[baby]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[baby]], [[child]], [[babe]], [[toddler]], [[tot]], [[bairn]] (*Scot** & **N English*), [[littlie]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[ankle-biter]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[tacker]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** (*law*) same as **minor**
**3** (*Brit*) a young schoolchild
 *adj*
**4** of, relating to, or designed for young children: infant school
**5** in an early stage of development: an infant democracy
