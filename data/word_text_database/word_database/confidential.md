**confidential** *adj*
**1** spoken or given in confidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secret]], [[private]], [[intimate]], [[classified]], [[privy]] (*archaic*), [[off the record]], [[hush-hush]] (*informal*), closed *or* closed source (*computers*), [[protected]] (*computers*)
**2** entrusted with another's [[secret]] affairs: a confidential secretary
**3** suggestive of intimacy: a halting, confidential manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secretive]], [[low]], [[soft]], [[hushed]]
 **[[confidentiality]]** *n* 
 **[[confidentially]]** *adv*
