**degree** *n*
**1** a [[stage]] in a scale of relative [[amount]] or intensity: this task involved a greater degree of responsibility
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amount]], [[stage]], [[grade]]
**2** an academic award given by a university or college on successful completion of a course
**3** (*grammar*) any of the forms of an adjective used to indicate relative [[amount]] or intensity
**4** a unit of temperature. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**°**
**5** a measure of angle equal to one three-hundred-and-sixtieth of the circumference of a circle. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**°**
**6** a unit of latitude or longitude. <small>SYMBOL: </small>**°**
**7** **by degrees** little by little; gradually
