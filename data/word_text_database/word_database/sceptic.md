**sceptic** * or * (*US*) **skeptic** (**skep**-tik) *n*
**1** a person who habitually doubts generally accepted beliefs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[doubter]], [[cynic]], [[disbeliever]]
**2** a person who doubts the truth of a religion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agnostic]], [[doubter]], [[unbeliever]], [[doubting Thomas]]
 **[[sceptical]]** * or * (*US*) **[[skeptical]]** *adj* 
 **[[sceptically]]** * or * (*US*) **[[skeptically]]** *adv* 
 **[[scepticism]]** * or * (*US*) **[[skepticism]]** *n*
