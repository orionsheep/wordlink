**consequence** *n*
**1** a logical [[result]] or [[effect]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[result]], [[effect]], [[outcome]], [[repercussion]], [[issue]], [[sequel]], [[end result]], [[upshot]]
**2** [[significance]] or [[importance]]: we said little of consequence to each other; my opinion is of little consequence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[importance]], [[concern]], [[moment]], [[value]], [[account]], [[weight]], [[import]] (*formal*), [[significance]]
**3** **in consequence** as a [[result]]
**4** **take the consequences** to accept whatever results from one's actions
