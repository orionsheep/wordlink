**friction** *n*
**1** a [[resistance]] encountered when one body moves relative to another body with which it is in contact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resistance]], [[rubbing]], [[scraping]], [[grating]], [[rasping]], [[chafing]], [[abrasion]]
**2** the act of [[rubbing]] one object against another
**3** [[disagreement]] or [[conflict]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conflict]], [[hostility]], [[resentment]], [[disagreement]], [[animosity]], [[discord]], [[bad blood]], [[dissension]]
 **[[frictional]]** *adj*
