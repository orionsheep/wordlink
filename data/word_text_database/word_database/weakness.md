**weakness** *n*
**1** the state of being weak
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frailty]], [[fatigue]], [[exhaustion]], [[fragility]], [[infirmity]], [[feebleness]], [[decrepitude]]
**2** a [[failing]] in a person's character: his weakness is his impetuosity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[failing]], [[fault]], [[defect]], [[deficiency]], [[flaw]], [[shortcoming]], [[blemish]], [[imperfection]]
**3** a self-indulgent [[liking]]: a weakness for gin
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[liking]], [[appetite]], [[penchant]], [[soft spot]], [[passion]], [[inclination]], [[fondness]], [[partiality]]
