**enjoy** *vb*
**1** to receive pleasure from
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: take pleasure in *or* from, [[like]], [[love]], [[appreciate]], [[relish]], [[delight in]], [[be pleased with]], [[be fond of]]
**2** to [[have]] or [[experience]] (something, esp. something good): many older people enjoy excellent health
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[have]], [[use]], [[own]], [[experience]], [[possess]], [[have the benefit of]], [[reap the benefits of]], be blessed *or* favoured with
**3** **enjoy oneself** to [[have]] a good time
 **[[enjoyable]]** *adj* 
 **[[enjoyably]]** *adv* 
 **[[enjoyment]]** *n*
