**severe** *adj*
**1** [[strict]] or [[harsh]] in the treatment of others: a severe parent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strict]], [[hard]], [[harsh]], [[cruel]], [[rigid]], [[drastic]], [[oppressive]], [[austere]]
**2** [[serious]] in appearance or manner: a severe look; a severe hairdo
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grim]], [[serious]], [[grave]], [[forbidding]], [[stern]], [[unsmiling]], [[tight-lipped]]
**3** very [[intense]] or unpleasant: severe chest pains; the punishments are severe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[serious]], [[critical]], [[terrible]], [[desperate]], [[extreme]], [[awful]], [[drastic]], [[catastrophic]]
**4** causing discomfort by its harshness: severe frost
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acute]], [[intense]], [[violent]], [[piercing]], [[harrowing]], [[unbearable]], [[agonizing]], [[insufferable]]
**5** [[hard]] to perform or accomplish: a severe challenge
 **[[severely]]** *adv* 
 **[[severity]]** *n*
