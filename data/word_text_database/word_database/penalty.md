**penalty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a legal [[punishment]] for a crime or offence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[punishment]], [[price]], [[fine]], [[handicap]], [[forfeit]]
**2** loss or suffering as a result of one's own action: we are now paying the penalty for neglecting to keep our equipment up to date
**3** (*sport*, *games*, *etc.*) a [[handicap]] awarded against a player or team for illegal play, such as a free shot at goal by the opposing team
