**encounter** *vb*
**1** to [[meet]] (someone) unexpectedly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meet]], [[confront]], [[come across]], [[bump into]] (*informal*), [[run across]], [[come upon]], [[chance upon]], [[meet by chance]]
**2** to be faced with: he had rarely encountered such suffering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[experience]], [[meet]], [[face]], [[suffer]], [[have]], [[go through]], [[sustain]], [[endure]]
**3** to [[meet]] (an opponent or enemy) in a competition or [[battle]]
 *n*
**4** a casual or unexpected [[meeting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting]], [[brush]], [[confrontation]], [[rendezvous]], [[chance meeting]]
**5** a game or [[battle]]: a fierce encounter between the army and armed rebels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[battle]], [[conflict]], [[clash]], [[contest]], [[run-in]] (*informal*), [[confrontation]], [[head-to-head]]
