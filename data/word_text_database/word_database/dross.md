**dross** *n*
**1** the scum formed on the surfaces of molten metals
**2** anything of inferior quality: we can't publish this dross
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nonsense]], [[garbage]] (*informal*), [[twaddle]], [[rot]], [[trash]], [[hot air]] (*informal*), [[tripe]] (*informal*), [[claptrap]] (*informal*), [[bizzo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[bull's wool]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *slang*)
