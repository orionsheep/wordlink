**dairy** *n*, *pl* **dairies**
**1** a company or shop that sells milk and milk products
**2** a place where milk and cream are stored or made into butter and cheese
**3** food containing milk or milk products: I can't eat dairy
**4** (*NZ*) a small shop selling groceries and milk often outside normal trading hours
 *adj*
**5** of milk or milk products: dairy produce
