**same** *adj* (usually preceded by *the*)
**1** being the very one: she is wearing the same hat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[the very same]], [[one and the same]], [[selfsame]]
**2** being the one previously referred to: it causes problems for the same reason
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aforementioned]], [[aforesaid]]
**3** [[alike]] in kind or quantity: the same age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[identical]], [[similar]], [[alike]], [[equal]], [[twin]], [[corresponding]], [[duplicate]]
**4** [[unchanged]] in character or nature: his attitude is the same as ever
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unchanged]], [[consistent]], [[constant]], [[unaltered]], [[invariable]], [[unvarying]], [[changeless]]
**5** **all the same** *or ***just the same** nevertheless; even so
**6** **be all the same** to be a matter of indifference: it was all the same to me
 *adv*
**7** in the same way; similarly: I felt much the same
 *n*
**8** **the same** something that is like something else in kind or quantity: this is basically much more of the same
 **[[sameness]]** *n*
