**speak** *vb* **speaking**, **spoke**, **spoken**
**1** to [[say]] words; [[talk]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[talk]], [[say something]]
**2** to communicate or [[express]] (something) in words
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[articulate]], [[say]], [[pronounce]], [[utter]], [[tell]], [[state]], [[talk]], [[express]]
**3** to give a speech or [[lecture]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lecture]], [[address an audience]]
**4** to know how to [[talk]] in (a specified language): I don't speak French
**5** **on speaking terms** on good terms; friendly
**6** **so to speak** as it were
**7** **speak one's mind** to [[express]] one's opinions honestly and plainly
**8** **to speak of** of a significant nature: no licensing laws to speak of
