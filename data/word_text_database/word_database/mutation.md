**mutation** (mew-**tay**-shun) *n*
**1** a [[change]] or [[alteration]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[variation]], [[evolution]], [[transformation]], [[modification]], [[alteration]], [[metamorphosis]], [[transfiguration]]
**2** a [[change]] in the chromosomes or genes of a cell which may affect the structure and development of the resultant offspring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[change]], [[variation]], [[evolution]], [[transformation]], [[modification]], [[alteration]], [[metamorphosis]], [[transfiguration]]
**3** a physical characteristic in an organism resulting from this type of chromosomal [[change]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anomaly]], [[variation]], [[deviant]] (*old-fashioned*), [[freak of nature]]
