**conventional** *adj*
**1** following the [[accepted]] customs and lacking originality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[traditional]], [[accepted]], [[orthodox]], [[customary]]
**2** established by [[accepted]] usage or general agreement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ordinary]], [[standard]], [[normal]], [[regular]], [[usual]]
**3** (of weapons or warfare) not nuclear
 **[[conventionally]]** *adv*
