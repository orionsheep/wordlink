**groan** *n*
**1** a long deep [[cry]] of pain, grief, or disapproval
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moan]], [[cry]], [[sigh]], [[whine]]
**2** (*informal*) a [[grumble]] or [[complaint]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complaint]], [[protest]], [[objection]], [[grumble]], [[grouse]], [[gripe]] (*informal*)
 *vb*
**3** to give a long deep [[cry]] of pain, grief, or disapproval
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moan]], [[cry]], [[sigh]]
**4** (*informal*) to [[complain]] or [[grumble]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complain]], [[object]], [[moan]] (*informal*), [[grumble]], [[gripe]] (*informal*), [[carp]], [[lament]], [[whine]]
**5** **groan under** to be weighed down by: chemists' shelves groan under the weight of slimming aids
 **[[groaning]]** *adj*, *n*
