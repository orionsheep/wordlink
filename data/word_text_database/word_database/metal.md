**metal** *n*
**1**
**A** (*chem*) a chemical element, such as iron or copper, that reflects light and can be shaped, forms positive ions, and is a good conductor of heat and electricity
 **B** an alloy, such as brass or steel, containing one or more of these elements
**2** (*informal*) short for **heavy metal**
**3** **metals** the rails of a railway
 *adj*
**4** made of metal
