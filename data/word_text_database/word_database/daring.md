**daring** *adj*
**1** willing to do things that may be dangerous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brave]], [[bold]], [[adventurous]], [[reckless]], [[fearless]], [[audacious]], [[intrepid]], daredevil
 *n*
**2** the [[courage]] to do things that may be dangerous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bravery]], [[nerve]] (*informal*), [[courage]], [[spirit]], [[bottle]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[pluck]], [[audacity]], [[boldness]]
 **[[daringly]]** *adv*
