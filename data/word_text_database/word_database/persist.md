**persist** *vb*
**1** to [[continue]] without interruption: if the symptoms persist, see your doctor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continue]], [[last]], [[remain]], [[carry on]], [[keep up]], [[linger]]
**2** (often foll. by *in* or *with*) to [[continue]] obstinately despite opposition: she persisted in using these controversial methods
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persevere]], [[continue]], [[go on]], [[carry on]], [[keep on]], [[keep going]], [[press on]], [[not give up]], [[crack on]] (*informal*)
