**piece** *n*
**1** a separate [[bit]] or [[part]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bit]], [[slice]], [[part]], [[block]], [[quantity]], [[segment]], [[portion]], [[fragment]]
**2** an instance or occurrence: a piece of luck
**3** an example or specimen of a style or type: each piece of furniture is crafted from native red pine by traditional methods
**4** a literary, musical, or artistic [[composition]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[composition]], [[work]], [[production]], [[opus]]
**5** a coin: a fifty-pence piece
**6** a firearm or cannon
**7** a small object used in playing various games: a chess piece
**8** **go to pieces** (of a person) to lose control of oneself; have a breakdown
 *vb* **piecing**, **pieced**
**9** (often foll. by *together*) to fit or assemble [[bit]] by [[bit]]
**10** (often foll. by *up*) to patch or make up (a garment) by adding pieces
