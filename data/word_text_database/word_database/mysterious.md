**mysterious** *adj*
**1** of unknown cause or nature: a mysterious illness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strange]], [[puzzling]], [[secret]], [[weird]], [[perplexing]], [[uncanny]], [[mystifying]], [[arcane]]
**2** creating a feeling of strangeness, curiosity, or wonder: a fascinating and mysterious place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secretive]], [[enigmatic]], [[evasive]], [[discreet]], [[covert]], [[reticent]], [[furtive]], [[inscrutable]]
 **[[mysteriously]]** *adv*
