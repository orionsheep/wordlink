**stomach** *n*
**1** an organ inside the body in which food is stored until it has been partially digested
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[belly]], [[gut]] (*informal*), [[abdomen]], [[tummy]] (*informal*), [[puku]] (*NZ*)
**2** the front of the body around the waist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tummy]] (*informal*), [[pot]], [[spare tyre]] (*informal*)
**3** [[desire]] or [[appetite]]: he still has the stomach for a fight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inclination]], [[taste]], [[desire]], [[appetite]], relish
 *vb*
**4** to put up with: liberals could not stomach the rest of the package
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bear]], [[take]], [[tolerate]], [[endure]], [[swallow]], [[abide]]
