**archive** (**ark**-ive) *n*
**1** a place where [[records]] or [[documents]] are kept
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[record office]], [[museum]], [[registry]], [[repository]]
**2** **archives** a collection of [[records]] or [[documents]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[records]], [[papers]], [[accounts]], [[rolls]], [[documents]], [[files]], [[deeds]], [[chronicles]]
**3** (*computers*) data moved to a disk or directory for long-term storage
 *vb*
**4** to store in an archive
 **[[archival]]** *adj*
