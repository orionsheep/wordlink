**common** *adj*
**1** frequently encountered: a fairly common plant; this disease is most common in kittens and young cats
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usual]], [[standard]], [[regular]], [[ordinary]], [[familiar]], [[conventional]], [[routine]], [[frequent]]
**2** [[widespread]] among people in [[general]]: common practice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[popular]], [[general]], [[accepted]], [[standard]], [[routine]], [[widespread]], [[universal]], [[prevailing]]
**3** belonging to two or more people: we share common interests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shared]], [[collective]]
**4** belonging to the whole [[community]]: common property
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collective]], [[public]], [[community]], [[social]], [[communal]]
**5** low-class, [[vulgar]], or [[coarse]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vulgar]], [[inferior]], [[coarse]], [[plebeian]]
**6** (*maths*) belonging to two or more: the lowest common denominator
**7** not belonging to the upper classes: the common people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ordinary]], [[average]], [[typical]], [[dinki-di]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**8** **common or garden** (*informal*) [[ordinary]]
 *n*
**9** a piece of open land belonging to all the members of a [[community]]
**10** **in common** [[shared]], in joint use
 **[[commonly]]** *adv*
