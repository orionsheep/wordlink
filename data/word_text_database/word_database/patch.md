**patch** *n*
**1** a [[piece of material]] used to [[cover]] a hole in a garment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reinforcement]], [[piece of fabric]], [[piece of cloth]], [[piece of material]], [[piece sewn on]]
**2** a small contrasting section: there was a bald patch on the top of his head
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spot]] (*Brit*), [[bit]], [[scrap]], [[shred]], [[small piece]]
**3** a small [[plot]] of [[land]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[area]], [[ground]], [[land]], [[tract]]
**4** (*med*) a protective covering for an injured eye
**5** a [[scrap]] or remnant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spot]] (*Brit*), [[bit]], [[scrap]], [[shred]], [[small piece]]
**6** the [[area]] under someone's supervision, such as a police officer or social worker
**7** **a bad patch** a difficult time
**8** **not a patch on** not nearly as good as
 *vb*
**9** to [[mend]] (a garment) with a patch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sew]] (up), [[mend]], [[repair]], [[reinforce]], [[stitch]] (up)
**10** **patch together** to produce (something) by piecing parts together hurriedly or carelessly
**11** **patch up**
**A** to [[mend]] (something) hurriedly or carelessly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mend]], [[cover]], [[reinforce]]
 **B** to make up (a quarrel)
