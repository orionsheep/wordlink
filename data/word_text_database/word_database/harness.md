**harness** *n*
**1** an arrangement of straps for attaching a horse to a cart or plough
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equipment]], [[tackle]], [[gear]], [[tack]]
**2** something resembling this, for attaching something to a person's body: a parachute harness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equipment]], [[tackle]], [[gear]], [[tack]]
**3** **in harness** at one's routine work
 *vb*
**4** to put a harness on (a horse or other animal)
**5** to [[control]] something in order to make use of it: learning to harness the power of your own mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exploit]], [[control]], [[channel]], [[employ]], [[utilize]], [[mobilize]]
