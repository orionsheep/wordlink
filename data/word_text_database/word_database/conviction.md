**conviction** *n*
**1** a firmly held [[belief]] or [[opinion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[belief]], [[view]], [[opinion]], [[principle]], [[faith]], [[persuasion]], [[creed]], [[tenet]], [[kaupapa]] (*NZ*)
**2** an instance of being found guilty of a crime: he had several convictions for petty theft
**3** a convincing or being convinced
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[certainty]], [[confidence]], [[assurance]], [[firmness]], [[certitude]]
**4** **carry conviction** to be convincing
