**forecast** *vb* **-casting**, **-cast*** or ***-casted**
**1** to [[predict]] or calculate (weather, events, etc.), in advance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[predict]], [[anticipate]], [[foresee]], [[foretell]], [[divine]], [[prophesy]], [[augur]], forewarn
 *n*
**2** a statement predicting the weather
**3** a [[prediction]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prediction]], [[prognosis]], [[guess]], [[prophecy]], [[conjecture]], [[forewarning]]
 **[[forecaster]]** *n*
