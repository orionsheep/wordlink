**accompaniment** *n*
**1** something that accompanies something else
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supplement]], [[extra]], [[addition]], [[companion]], [[accessory]], [[complement]], [[decoration]], [[adjunct]]
**2** (*music*) a supporting part for an instrument, a band, or an orchestra
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[backing music]], [[backing]], [[support]], [[obbligato]]
