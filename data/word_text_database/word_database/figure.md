**figure** *n*
**1** a written [[symbol]] for a [[number]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[digit]], [[character]], [[symbol]], [[number]], [[numeral]]
**2** an amount expressed in numbers
**3** **figures** calculations with numbers
**4** visible [[shape]] or form; outline
**5** a slim bodily [[shape]]: it's not good for your figure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shape]], [[build]], [[body]], [[frame]], [[proportions]], [[physique]]
**6** a well-known [[person]]: a public figure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[personage]], [[person]], [[individual]], [[character]] (*informal*), [[personality]], [[celebrity]], [[big name]], [[dignitary]]
**7** a [[representation]] in painting or sculpture, esp. of the human [[body]]
**8** an [[illustration]] or [[diagram]] in a text
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diagram]], [[drawing]], [[picture]], [[illustration]], [[representation]], [[sketch]]
**9** a decorative [[pattern]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[shape]], [[pattern]]
**10** a fixed set of movements in dancing or skating
**11** (*geom*) any combination of points, lines, curves, or planes
**12** (*music*) a characteristic short [[pattern]] of notes
**13** **figure of fun** a [[person]] who is often laughed at by other people
 *vb* **-uring**, **-ured**
**14** to [[calculate]] (sums or amounts)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calculate]], [[work out]], [[compute]], [[tot up]], [[total]], [[count]], [[reckon]], [[tally]]
**15** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) to consider

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feature]], [[act]], [[appear]], [[contribute to]], [[play a part]], [[be featured]]
