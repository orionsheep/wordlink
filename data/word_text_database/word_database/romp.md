**romp** *vb*
**1** to play or run about wildly or joyfully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frolic]], [[sport]], [[have fun]], [[caper]], [[cavort]], [[frisk]], [[gambol]]
**2** **romp home *or* in** to win a race or other competition easily
**3** **romp through** to do (something) quickly and easily
 *n*
**4** a noisy or boisterous game or prank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frolic]], [[lark]] (*informal*), [[caper]]
