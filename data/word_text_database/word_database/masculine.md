**masculine** *adj*
**1** possessing qualities or characteristics considered typical of or appropriate to a man
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[male]], [[manly]], [[mannish]], [[boyish]], [[manlike]], [[virile]]
**2** of men
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[male]], [[manly]], [[mannish]], [[boyish]], [[manlike]], [[virile]]
**3** (*grammar*) denoting a gender of nouns that includes some [[male]] animate things
**4** (*prosody*) denoting a rhyme between pairs of single final stressed syllables
 **[[masculinity]]** *n*
