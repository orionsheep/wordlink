**poke** *vb* **poking**, **poked**
**1** to [[jab]] or [[prod]] with an elbow, finger, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jab]], [[push]], [[stick]], [[dig]], [[stab]], [[thrust]], [[shove]], [[nudge]]
**2** to make a hole by poking
**3** (sometimes foll. by *at*) to [[thrust]] (at): she poked at the food with her fork
**4** (usually foll. by *in* or *through* etc) to [[thrust]] forward or out: yellow hair poked from beneath his cap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protrude]], [[stick]], [[thrust]], [[jut]]
**5** to stir (a fire) by poking
**6** (often foll. by *about* or *around*) to search or pry
**7** **poke one's nose into** to meddle in
 *n*
**8** a [[jab]] or [[prod]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jab]], [[dig]], [[thrust]], [[nudge]], [[prod]]
