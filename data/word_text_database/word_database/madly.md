**madly** *adv*
**1** in an crazy or foolish manner: I yelled and waved madly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insanely]], [[frantically]], [[hysterically]], [[crazily]], [[deliriously]], [[distractedly]], [[frenziedly]]
**2** with great speed and energy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[energetically]], [[wildly]], [[furiously]], [[excitedly]], [[recklessly]], [[speedily]], [[like mad]] (*informal*)
**3** (*informal*) extremely or excessively: she was madly in love with him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[passionately]], [[wildly]], [[desperately]], [[intensely]], [[to distraction]], [[devotedly]]
