**indent** *vb*
**1** to start (a line of text) or position (a block of text) further from the margin than the main part of the text
**2** to order (goods) using a special order form
**3** to notch (an edge or border)
**4** to write out (a document) in duplicate
**5** to bind (an apprentice) by indenture
 *n*
**6** (*chiefly* *Brit*) an official order for goods, esp. foreign merchandise
