**cock** *n*
**1** a male bird, esp. of domestic fowl
**2** a stopcock
**3** (*vulgar*, *slang*) a penis
**4** the hammer of a gun
**5** (*Brit* *informal*) friend: used as a term of address
 *vb*
**6** to draw back the hammer of (a gun) so that it is ready to fire
**7** to lift and turn (part of the body) in a particular direction
