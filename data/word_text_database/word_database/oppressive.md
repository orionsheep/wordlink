**oppressive** *adj*
**1** [[cruel]], [[harsh]], or [[tyrannical]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tyrannical]], [[severe]], [[harsh]], [[cruel]], [[brutal]], [[authoritarian]], [[unjust]], [[repressive]]
**2** uncomfortable or depressing: a small flat can become rather oppressive
**3** (of weather) hot and [[humid]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stifling]], [[close]], [[sticky]], [[stuffy]], [[humid]], [[sultry]], [[airless]], [[muggy]]
 **[[oppressiveness]]** *n*
