**pearl** *n*
**1** a hard smooth greyish-white rounded object found inside the shell of a clam or oyster and much valued as a gem
**2** a person or thing that is like a pearl in beauty or value
 *adj*
**3** of, made of, or set with pearl or mother-of-pearl
 *vb*
**4** to set with or as if with pearls
**5** to shape into or assume a pearl-like form or colour
**6** to dive for pearls
