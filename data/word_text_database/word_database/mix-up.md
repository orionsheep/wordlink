**mix-up** *n*
**1** a confused condition or situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confusion]], [[mistake]], [[misunderstanding]], [[mess]], [[tangle]], muddle
 *vb* **mix up**
**2** to make into a mixture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[mix something up]]**: blend, beat, mix, stir, fold
**3** to confuse: he mixes Ryan up with Lee
**4** **mixed up in** involved in (an activity or group, esp. one that is illegal): she's mixed up in a drugs racket
