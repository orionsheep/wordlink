**shaky** *adj* **shakier**, **shakiest**
**1** [[weak]] and [[unsteady]], esp. due to illness or shock
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unstable]], [[weak]], [[precarious]], [[rickety]]
**2** [[uncertain]] or doubtful: their prospects are shaky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncertain]], [[suspect]], [[dubious]], [[questionable]], [[iffy]] (*informal*)
**3** tending to shake or tremble
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsteady]], [[faint]], [[trembling]], [[faltering]], [[quivery]]
 **[[shakily]]** *adv*
