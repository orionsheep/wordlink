**song** *n*
**1** a piece of music with words, composed for the voice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ballad]], [[air]], [[tune]], [[carol]], [[chant]], [[chorus]], [[anthem]], [[number]], [[waiata]] (*NZ*)
**2** the tuneful call made by certain birds or insects
**3** the act or process of singing: he broke into song
**4** **for a song** at a bargain price
**5** **make a song and dance** (*informal*) to make an unnecessary fuss
