◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[despondency]], [[depression]], [[misery]], [[gloom]], [[desperation]], [[anguish]], [[hopelessness]], dejection
 *vb*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lose hope]], [[give up]], [[lose heart]]
