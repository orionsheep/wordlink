**paint** *n*
**1** a coloured substance, spread on a surface with a brush or a roller, that forms a hard coating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[colouring]], [[colour]], [[stain]], [[dye]], [[tint]], [[pigment]], [[emulsion]]
**2** a dry film of paint on a surface
**3** (*informal*) face make-up
 *vb*
**4** to [[apply paint to]] paper or canvas to make a [[picture]] of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depict]], [[draw]], [[portray]], [[picture]], [[represent]], [[sketch]]
**5** to [[coat]] (a surface) with paint
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[colour]], [[cover]], [[coat]], [[stain]], [[whitewash]], [[daub]], [[distemper]], [[apply paint to]]
**6** to describe vividly in words: the survey paints a dismal [[picture]] of growing hunger and disease
**7** to apply make-up to (the face)
**8** to apply (liquid) to (a surface): paint the varnish on and leave it to dry for at least four hours
**9** **paint the town red** (*informal*) to celebrate in a lively way
