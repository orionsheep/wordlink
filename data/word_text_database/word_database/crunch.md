**crunch** *vb*
**1** to bite or chew with a noisy crushing sound
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chomp]], [[champ]], [[munch]], [[chew noisily]], [[grind]]
**2** to make a crisp or brittle sound
 *n*
**3** a crunching sound
**4** **[[the crunch]]** (*informal*) the critical moment or situation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the crunch]]**: critical point, test, crisis, emergency, crux, moment of truth
 **[[crunchy]]** *adj* 
 **[[crunchiness]]** *n*
