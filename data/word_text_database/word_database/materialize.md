**materialize** * or ***-lise** *vb* **-lizing**, **-lized*** or ***-lising**, **-lised**
**1** to become fact; actually happen: the promised pay rise never materialized
**2** to appear after being invisible: trees materialized out of the gloom
**3** to take shape: after hours of talks, a plan began to materialize
 **[[materialization]]** * or ***-lisation** *n*
