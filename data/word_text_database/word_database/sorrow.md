**sorrow** *n*
**1** deep [[sadness]] or [[regret]], associated with death or sympathy for another's misfortune
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grief]], [[sadness]], [[woe]], [[regret]], [[distress]], [[misery]], [[mourning]], [[anguish]]
**2** a particular cause of this
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hardship]], [[trial]], [[tribulation]], [[affliction]], [[trouble]], [[woe]], misfortune
 *vb*
**3** (*literary*) to feel deep [[sadness]] (about death or another's misfortunes); [[mourn]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grieve]], [[mourn]], [[lament]], [[be sad]], [[bemoan]], [[agonize]], [[bewail]]
 **[[sorrowful]]** *adj* 
 **[[sorrowfully]]** *adv*
