**agency** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** an [[organization]] providing a specific service: an advertising agency
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[business]], [[company]], [[office]], [[firm]], [[department]], [[organization]], [[enterprise]], [[establishment]]
**2** the [[business]] or functions of an agent
**3** action or power by which something happens: the intervention of a human agency in the sequence of events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[medium]], [[means]], [[activity]], [[vehicle]], [[instrument]], [[mechanism]]
