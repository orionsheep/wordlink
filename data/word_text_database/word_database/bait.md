**bait** *n*
**1** something edible fixed to a hook or in a trap to attract fish or animals
**2** an enticement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lure]], [[attraction]], [[incentive]], [[carrot]] (*informal*), [[temptation]], [[snare]], [[inducement]], decoy
 *vb*
**3** to put a piece of food on or in (a hook or trap)
**4** to persecute or [[tease]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tease]], [[annoy]], [[irritate]], [[bother]], [[mock]], [[wind up]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[hound]], [[torment]]
**5** to set dogs upon (a bear or badger)
