**flirt** *vb*
**1** to behave as if sexually attracted to someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chat up]], [[lead on]] (*informal*), [[make advances at]], [[make eyes at]], [[philander]], [[make sheep's eyes at]]
**2** (foll. by *with*) to [[consider]] lightly; [[toy with]]: he had often flirted with the idea of emigrating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[toy with]], [[consider]], [[entertain]], [[play with]], [[dabble in]], [[trifle with]], [[give a thought to]], expose yourself to
 *n*
**3** a person who flirts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tease]], [[philanderer]], [[coquette]], [[heart-breaker]]
 **[[flirtation]]** *n* 
 **[[flirtatious]]** *adj*
