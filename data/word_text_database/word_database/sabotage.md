**sabotage** *n*
**1** the deliberate [[destruction]] or [[damage]] of equipment, for example by enemy agents or dissatisfied employees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[destruction]], [[wrecking]]
**2** deliberate obstruction of or [[damage]] to a cause or effort
 *vb* **-taging**, **-taged**
**3** to [[destroy]] or [[disrupt]] by sabotage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[destroy]], [[wreck]], [[disable]], [[disrupt]], [[subvert]], [[incapacitate]], [[vandalize]]
