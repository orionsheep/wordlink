**stupid** *adj*
**1** lacking in common sense or intelligence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unintelligent]], [[thick]], [[dim]] (*informal*), [[dense]]
**2** trivial, [[silly]], or childish: we got into a stupid quarrel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[silly]], [[foolish]], [[daft]] (*informal*), [[rash]], [[pointless]], [[senseless]], [[idiotic]], [[fatuous]]
**3** unable to think clearly; [[dazed]]: stupid with tiredness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[senseless]], [[dazed]], [[groggy]], [[insensate]], [[semiconscious]]
 **[[stupidity]]** *n* 
 **[[stupidly]]** *adv*
