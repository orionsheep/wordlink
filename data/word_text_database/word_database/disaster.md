**disaster** *n*
**1** an accident that causes great distress or destruction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[catastrophe]], [[trouble]], [[tragedy]], [[ruin]], [[misfortune]], [[adversity]], [[calamity]], [[cataclysm]]
**2** something, such as a project, that fails or has been ruined
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[failure]], [[mess]], [[flop]] (*informal*), [[catastrophe]], [[debacle]], [[cock-up]] (*Brit* *slang*), [[washout]] (*informal*) 
 **[[disastrous]]** *adj* 
 **[[disastrously]]** *adv*
