**infinite** (**in**-fin-it) *adj*
**1** having no limits or boundaries in time, space, extent, or size
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[limitless]], [[endless]], [[unlimited]], [[eternal]], [[never-ending]], [[boundless]], [[everlasting]], [[inexhaustible]]
**2** extremely or immeasurably great or numerous: infinite wealth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vast]], [[enormous]], [[immense]], [[countless]], [[measureless]]
**3** (*maths*) having an [[unlimited]] or uncountable number of digits, factors, or terms
 **[[infinitely]]** *adv*
