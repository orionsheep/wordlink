**blot** *n*
**1** a stain or spot, esp. of ink
**2** something that spoils
**3** a stain on one's character
 *vb* **blotting**, **blotted**
**4** to stain or spot
**5** to cause a blemish in or on: he blotted his copybook by missing a penalty
**6** to soak up (excess ink, etc.) by using blotting paper
**7** **blot out**
**A** to darken or hide completely: the mist blotted out the sea
 **B** to block from one's mind: to blot out the memories
