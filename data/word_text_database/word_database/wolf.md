**wolf** *n*, *pl* **wolves**
**1** a predatory doglike wild animal which hunts in packs
**2** (*old-fashioned*, *informal*) a man who habitually tries to seduce women
**3** **cry wolf** to give false alarms repeatedly: if you cry wolf too often, people will take no notice
 *vb*
**4** **wolf down** to eat quickly or greedily: they will wolf down kidneys but refuse tongue
