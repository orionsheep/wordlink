**engineer** *n*
**1** a person trained in any branch of engineering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[designer]], [[producer]], [[architect]], [[developer]], [[deviser]], [[creator]], [[planner]], [[inventor]]
**2** a person who repairs and maintains mechanical or electrical devices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worker]], [[specialist]], [[operator]], [[practitioner]], [[operative]], [[driver]], [[conductor]], [[technician]]
**3** a soldier trained in engineering and construction work
**4** an officer responsible for a ship's engines
**5** (*US** & **Canad*) a train [[driver]]
 *vb*
**6** to cause or [[plan]] (an event or situation) in a clever or devious manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring about]], [[plan]], [[effect]], [[set up]] (*informal*), [[scheme]], [[arrange]], [[plot]], [[mastermind]]
**7** to [[design]] or [[construct]] as a professional engineer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[plan]], [[create]], [[construct]], [[devise]]
