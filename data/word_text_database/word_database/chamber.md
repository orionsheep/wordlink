**chamber** *n*
**1** a meeting [[hall]], usually one used for a legislative or judicial [[assembly]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hall]], [[room]]
**2** a [[room]] equipped for a particular purpose: a decompression chamber
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[room]], [[bedroom]], [[enclosure]], [[cubicle]]
**3** a legislative or judicial [[assembly]]: the Senate, the upper chamber of Canada's parliament
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[council]], [[assembly]], [[legislature]], [[legislative body]]
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *or* *poetic*) a [[room]] in a house, esp. a [[bedroom]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[room]], [[bedroom]], [[enclosure]], [[cubicle]]
**5** a [[compartment]] or cavity: the heart chambers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compartment]]
**6** a [[compartment]] for a cartridge or shell in a gun
