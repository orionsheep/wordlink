**staff** *n*, *pl* (*for senses 1 and 2*) **staffs**, (*for senses 3 and 4*) **staffs*** or ***staves**
**1** the people employed in a company, school, or organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[workers]], [[employees]], [[personnel]], [[workforce]], [[team]]
**2** (*military*) the officers appointed to assist a commander
**3** a [[stick]] with some special use, such as a walking [[stick]] or an emblem of authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stick]], [[pole]], [[rod]], [[crook]], [[cane]], [[stave]], [[wand]], [[sceptre]]
**4** (*music*) a set of five horizontal lines on which music is written and which, along with a clef, indicates pitch
 *vb*
**5** to provide (a company, school, or organization) with a staff
