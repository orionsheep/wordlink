**freight** (**frate**) *n*
**1**
**A** commercial transport of [[goods]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transportation]], [[traffic]], [[delivery]], [[carriage]], [[shipment]], [[haulage]], [[conveyance]], transport
 **B** the [[cargo]] transported
 **C** the cost of this
**2** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a ship's [[cargo]] or part of it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cargo]], [[goods]], [[load]], [[delivery]], [[burden]], [[shipment]], [[merchandise]], consignment
 *vb*
**3** to transport ([[goods]]) by freight
**4** to [[load]] with [[goods]] for transport
