**question** *n*
**1** a form of words addressed to a person in order to obtain an answer; interrogative sentence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inquiry]], [[enquiry]], [[query]], [[investigation]], [[examination]], [[interrogation]]
**2** a [[point]] at [[issue]]: they were silent on the question of social justice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[issue]], [[point]], [[matter]], [[subject]], [[problem]], [[debate]], [[proposal]], [[theme]]
**3** a [[difficulty]] or uncertainty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difficulty]], [[problem]], [[doubt]], [[argument]], [[dispute]], [[controversy]], [[query]], [[contention]]
**4**
**A** an act of asking
 **B** an [[investigation]] into some [[problem]]
**5** a motion presented for [[debate]]
**6** **beyond (all) question** beyond (any) [[doubt]]
**7** **call something into question**
**A** to make something the [[subject]] of disagreement
 **B** to cast [[doubt]] upon the validity or truth of something
**8** **in question** under discussion: the area in question was not contaminated
**9** **[[out of the question]]** beyond consideration; impossible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[out of the question]]**: impossible, unthinkable, inconceivable, not on (*informal*), hopeless, unimaginable, unworkable, unattainable
 *vb*
**10** to put a question or questions to (a person); [[interrogate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interrogate]], [[cross-examine]], [[interview]], [[examine]], [[probe]], [[quiz]], [[ask questions]]
**11** to make (something) the [[subject]] of [[dispute]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[challenge]], [[doubt]], [[suspect]], [[oppose]], [[query]], [[mistrust]], [[disbelieve]]
**12** to express uncertainty; [[doubt]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[challenge]], [[doubt]], [[suspect]], [[oppose]], [[query]], [[mistrust]], [[disbelieve]]
