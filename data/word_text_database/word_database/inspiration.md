**inspiration** *n*
**1** stimulation of the mind or feelings to activity or [[creativity]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imagination]], [[creativity]], [[ingenuity]], [[insight]], [[originality]], [[inventiveness]], [[cleverness]]
**2** a person or thing that causes this state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[influence]], [[spur]], [[stimulus]], [[muse]]
**3** an inspired idea or action
 **[[inspirational]]** *adj*
