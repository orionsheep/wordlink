**awkward** *adj*
**1** [[clumsy]] or [[ungainly]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clumsy]], [[lumbering]], [[bumbling]], [[unwieldy]], [[ponderous]], [[ungainly]], [[gauche]], [[gawky]], [[unco]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** embarrassed: he was awkward and nervous around girls
**3** [[difficult]] to deal with: the lawyer was in an awkward situation
**4** [[difficult]] to use or handle: it was small but heavy enough to make it awkward to carry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inconvenient]], [[difficult]], [[troublesome]], [[cumbersome]], [[unwieldy]], [[unmanageable]], [[clunky]] (*informal*)
**5** [[embarrassing]]: there were several moments of awkward silence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[embarrassing]], [[difficult]], [[sensitive]], [[delicate]], [[uncomfortable]], [[humiliating]], [[disconcerting]], [[inconvenient]], [[barro]] (*Austral* *slang*) 
 **[[awkwardly]]** *adv* 
 **[[awkwardness]]** *n*
