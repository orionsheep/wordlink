**shave** *vb* **shaving**, **shaved**, **shaved*** or ***shaven**
**1** to remove (the beard or hair) from (the face, head, or body) by using a razor or shaver
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trim]], [[crop]]
**2** to remove thin slices from (wood or other material) with a sharp cutting tool
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scrape]], [[trim]], [[shear]], [[pare]]
**3** to touch (someone or something) lightly in passing
 *n*
**4** the act or an instance of shaving
**5** the removal of hair from the face or body by a razor
**6** a tool for cutting off thin slices
**7** **close shave** (*informal*) a narrow escape
