**midnight** *n*
**1** the [[middle of the night]]; 12 o'clock at night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[twelve o'clock]], [[middle of the night]], [[dead of night]], the witching hour
 *adj*
**2** happening or apparent at midnight or in the [[middle of the night]]: midnight Mass
**3** **burn the midnight oil** to work or study late into the night
