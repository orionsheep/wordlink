**query** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** a [[question]], esp. one expressing [[doubt]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[question]], [[inquiry]], [[enquiry]], [[problem]]
**2** a [[question]] mark
 *vb* **-ries**, **-rying**, **-ried**
**3** to express uncertainty, [[doubt]], or an objection concerning (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[question]], [[challenge]], [[doubt]], [[suspect]], [[dispute]], [[object to]], [[distrust]], [[mistrust]]
**4** to express as a query; [[ask]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ask]], inquire *or* enquire, [[question]]
