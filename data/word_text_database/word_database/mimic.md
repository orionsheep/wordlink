**mimic** *vb* **-icking**, **-icked**
**1** to [[imitate]] a person or a way of acting or speaking, esp. to entertain or make fun of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imitate]], [[do]] (*informal*), [[take off]] (*informal*), [[ape]], [[parody]], [[caricature]], [[impersonate]]
**2** to take on the appearance of: certain flies mimic wasps
**3** to copy closely or in a servile manner: social climbers in the colonies began to mimic their conquerors
 *n*
**4** a person or an animal, such as a parrot, that is clever at mimicking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imitator]], [[impressionist]], [[copycat]] (*informal*), [[impersonator]], [[caricaturist]]
