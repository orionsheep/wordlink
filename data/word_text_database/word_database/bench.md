**bench** *n*
**1** a long [[seat]] for more than one person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seat]], [[stall]], [[pew]]
**2** **[[the bench]]**
**A** a judge or magistrate sitting in court
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the bench]]**: court, judges, magistrates, tribunal, judiciary, courtroom
 **B** judges or magistrates collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[the bench]]**: court, judges, magistrates, tribunal, judiciary, courtroom
**3** a long and strong [[worktable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worktable]], [[stand]], [[table]], [[counter]], [[trestle table]], [[workbench]]
