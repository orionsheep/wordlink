**throttle** *n*
**1** a device that controls the fuel-and-air mixture entering an engine
 *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**2** to kill or injure (someone) by squeezing his or her throat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strangle]], [[choke]], [[garrotte]], [[strangulate]]
**3** to suppress or censor: the government is trying to throttle dissent
