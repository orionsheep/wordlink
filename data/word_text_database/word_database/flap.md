**flap** *vb* **flapping**, **flapped**
**1** to move backwards and forwards or up and down, like a bird's wings in flight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flutter]], [[wave]], flail
 *n*
**2** the action of or noise made by flapping
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flutter]], [[beating]], [[waving]], [[shaking]], [[swinging]], [[swish]]
**3** a piece of material attached at one edge and usually used to cover an opening, such as on a pocket
**4** a hinged section of an aircraft wing that is raised or lowered to control the aircraft's speed
**5** (*informal*) a [[state]] of [[panic]] or [[agitation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[panic]], [[state]] (*informal*), [[agitation]], [[commotion]], [[sweat]] (*informal*), [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[fluster]], [[tizzy]] (*informal*)
