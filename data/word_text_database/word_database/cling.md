**cling** *vb* **[[clinging]]**, **clung**
**1** (often foll. by *to*) to hold fast or stick closely (to something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clutch]], [[grip]], [[embrace]], [[grasp]], [[hug]], [[hold on to]], [[clasp]]
**2** to be emotionally overdependent on
**3** to continue to do or believe in: he clings to the belief that people are capable of change
 **[[clinging]]** * or ***[[clingy]]** *adj*
