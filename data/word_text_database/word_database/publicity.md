**publicity** *n*
**1** the process or information used to arouse public [[attention]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advertising]], [[press]], [[promotion]], [[hype]], [[boost]], [[plug]] (*informal*)
**2** the [[public interest]] so aroused
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attention]], [[exposure]], [[fame]], [[celebrity]], [[fuss]], [[public interest]], [[limelight]], [[notoriety]]
