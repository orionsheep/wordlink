**plume** *n*
**1** a large ornamental feather
**2** a group of feathers worn as a badge or ornament on a hat
**3** something like a plume: a plume of smoke
 *vb* **pluming**, **plumed**
**4** to adorn with plumes
**5** (of a bird) to preen (its feathers)
**6** **plume oneself** (foll. by *on* or *upon*) to be proud of oneself or one's achievements, esp. unjustifiably: she was pluming herself on her figure
