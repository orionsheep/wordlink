◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flexible]], [[supple]], [[rubbery]], [[pliable]], [[plastic]], [[springy]], [[pliant]], [[tensile]]
**2** capable of being adapted to meet the demands of a particular situation: an elastic interpretation of the law
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adaptable]], [[yielding]], [[variable]], [[flexible]], [[accommodating]], [[tolerant]], [[adjustable]], [[supple]]
**3** made of elastic
 *n*
**4** tape, cord, or fabric containing [[flexible]] rubber
