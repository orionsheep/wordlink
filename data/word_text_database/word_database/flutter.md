**flutter** *vb*
**1** to wave rapidly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beat]], [[flap]], [[tremble]], [[ripple]], [[waver]], [[quiver]], [[vibrate]], [[palpitate]]
**2** (of birds or butterflies) to [[flap]] the wings
**3** to move with an irregular motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flit]]
**4** (*pathol*) (of the heart) to [[beat]] abnormally rapidly
**5** to move about restlessly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flit]]
 *n*
**6** a quick flapping or vibrating motion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tremor]], [[tremble]], [[shiver]], [[shudder]], [[palpitation]]
**7** a [[state]] of nervous [[excitement]] or [[confusion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agitation]], [[state]] (*informal*), [[confusion]], [[excitement]], [[flap]] (*informal*), [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[commotion]], [[fluster]]
**8** excited interest
**9** (*Brit* *informal*) a modest bet
**10** (*pathol*) an abnormally rapid beating of the heart
**11** (*electronics*) a slow variation in pitch in a sound-reproducing system
