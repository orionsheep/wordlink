**recall** *vb*
**1** to bring back to mind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recollect]], [[remember]], [[evoke]], [[call to mind]]
**2** to order to return
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call back]]
**3** to [[annul]] or [[cancel]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annul]], [[withdraw]], [[cancel]], [[repeal]], [[revoke]], [[retract]], countermand
 *n*
**4** the ability to [[remember]] things
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recollection]], [[memory]], [[remembrance]]
**5** an order to return
