**mythology** *n*, *pl* **-gies**
**1** myths collectively, esp. those associated with a particular culture or person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legend]], [[folklore]], [[tradition]], [[lore]]
**2** a body of stories about a person, institution, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legend]], [[folklore]], [[tradition]], [[lore]]
**3** the study of myths
 **[[mythological]]** *adj*
