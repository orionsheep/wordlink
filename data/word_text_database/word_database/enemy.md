**enemy** *n*, *pl* **-mies**
**1** a person who is hostile or opposed to a person, group, or idea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: foe (*formal*, *literary*), [[rival]], [[opponent]], [[the opposition]], [[competitor]], [[the other side]], [[adversary]], [[antagonist]]
**2** a hostile nation or people
**3** something that harms or opposes something: oil is an enemy of the environment <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**inimical**
 *adj*
**4** of or belonging to an enemy: enemy troops
