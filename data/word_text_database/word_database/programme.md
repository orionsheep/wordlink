**programme** * or * (*US*) **program** *n*
**1** a planned series of events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[schedule]], [[plan]], [[agenda]], [[timetable]], [[listing]], [[list]], [[line-up]], [[calendar]]
**2** a [[broadcast]] on radio or television
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[performance]], [[production]], [[broadcast]], [[episode]], [[presentation]], [[transmission]], [[telecast]], [[podcast]]
**3** a printed [[list]] of items or performers in an entertainment
 *vb* **-gramming**, **-grammed** (*US*) **-graming**, **-gramed**
**4** to [[schedule]] (something) as a programme
 **[[programmatic]]** *adj*
