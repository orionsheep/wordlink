**against** *prep*
**1** standing or leaning [[beside]]: she leaned against a tree
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beside]], [[on]], [[up against]], [[in contact with]], [[abutting]]
**2** [[opposed to]] or in disagreement with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[opposed to]], [[anti]] (*informal*), [[hostile to]], [[in opposition to]], [[averse to]], [[opposite to]]
**3** in contrast to: his complexion was a sickly white against the black stubble of his beard
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[in opposition to]], [[resisting]], [[versus]], [[counter to]], [[in the opposite direction of]]
**4** coming [[in contact with]]: rain rattled against the window
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beside]], [[on]], [[up against]], [[in contact with]], [[abutting]]
**5** having an unfavourable effect [[on]]: the system works against you when you don't have money

**7** in exchange for or in return for: the dollar has gained very slightly against the yen
