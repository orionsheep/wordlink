**genius** (**jean**-yuss) *n*, *pl* **-uses**
**1** a person with exceptional [[ability]] in a particular subject or activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[master]], [[expert]], [[mastermind]], [[maestro]], [[virtuoso]], [[whiz]] (*informal*), [[hotshot]] (*informal*), [[brainbox]], [[fundi]] (*S African*)
**2** such [[ability]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brilliance]], [[ability]], [[talent]], [[capacity]], [[gift]], [[bent]], [[excellence]], [[flair]]
**3** a person considered as exerting influence of a certain sort: the evil genius behind the drug-smuggling empire
