**haul** *vb*
**1** to [[drag]] or [[pull]] (something) with effort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drag]], [[draw]], [[pull]], [[heave]]
**2** to transport, such as in a lorry
**3** (*naut*) to alter the course of (a vessel)
 *n*
**4** the act of dragging with effort
**5** a quantity of something obtained: a good haul of fish; a huge haul of stolen goods
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[yield]], [[gain]], [[spoils]], [[catch]], [[harvest]], [[loot]], [[takings]], [[booty]]
**6** **long haul**
**A** a long journey
 **B** a long difficult process
