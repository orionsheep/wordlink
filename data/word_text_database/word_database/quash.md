**quash** *vb*
**1** to officially reject (something, such as a judgment or decision) as invalid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[annul]], [[overturn]], [[reverse]], [[cancel]], [[overthrow]], [[revoke]], [[overrule]], [[rescind]]
**2** to defeat or [[suppress]] forcefully and completely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suppress]], [[crush]], [[put down]], [[beat]], [[overthrow]], [[squash]], [[subdue]], [[repress]]
