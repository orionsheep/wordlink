**killing** *adj*
**1** (*informal*) very [[tiring]]: a killing pace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tiring]], [[taxing]], [[exhausting]], [[punishing]], [[fatiguing]], [[gruelling]], [[sapping]], [[debilitating]]
**2** (*informal*) extremely funny
**3** causing death; fatal
 *n*
**4** the act of causing death; [[slaying]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[murder]], [[massacre]], [[slaughter]], [[dispatch]], [[manslaughter]], [[elimination]], [[slaying]], [[homicide]]
**5** **[[make a killing]]** (*informal*) to have a sudden financial success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[make a killing]]**: profit, gain, clean up (*informal*), be lucky, be successful, make a fortune, strike it rich (*informal*), make a bomb (*slang*)
