**mop** *n*
**1** a tool with a head made of twists of cotton or [[sponge]] and a long handle used for washing or polishing floors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squeegee]], [[sponge]], [[swab]]
**2** a similar tool, except smaller and without a long handle, used to [[wash]] dishes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squeegee]], [[sponge]], [[swab]]
**3** a thick untidy [[mass]] of hair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mane]], [[shock]], [[mass]], [[tangle]], [[mat]], thatch
 *vb* **mopping**, **[[mopped]]**
**4** to [[clean]] or soak up with or as if with a mop: she [[mopped]] her brow with a handkerchief
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clean]], [[wash]], [[wipe]], [[sponge]], [[swab]]
