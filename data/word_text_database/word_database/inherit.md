**inherit** *vb*
**1** to receive money, property, or a title from someone who has died
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be left]], [[come into]], [[be willed]], [[succeed to]], [[fall heir to]]
**2** to receive (a characteristic) from an earlier generation by heredity
**3** to receive (a position or situation) from a predecessor: he inherited a mess
 **[[inheritor]]** *n*
