**length** *n*
**1** the [[extent]] or measurement of something from end to end
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distance]], [[reach]], [[measure]], [[extent]], [[span]], [[longitude]]
**2** a specified [[distance]], esp. between two positions: the length of a cricket pitch
**3** a [[period]] of time, as between specified limits or moments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[duration]], [[term]], [[period]], [[space]], [[stretch]], [[span]], [[expanse]]
**4** the quality, state, or fact of being long rather than short
**5** a [[piece]] of something, usually longer than it is wide: a length of twine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[piece]], [[measure]], [[section]], [[segment]], [[portion]]
**6** (*usually pl*) the amount of trouble taken in doing something: to go to great lengths
**7** (*prosody*, *phonetics*) the [[duration]] of a vowel or syllable
**8** **[[at length]]**
**A** after a long interval or [[period]] of time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at length]]**: at last, finally, eventually, in time, in the end, at long last
 **B** in great detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[at length]]**: for a long time, completely, fully, thoroughly, for hours, in detail, for ages, in depth
