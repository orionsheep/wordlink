**identical** *adj*
**1** that is the same: they got the identical motel room as last year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alike]], [[matching]], [[twin]], [[duplicate]], [[indistinguishable]], [[interchangeable]]
**2** exactly [[alike]] or equal
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[alike]], [[matching]], [[twin]], [[duplicate]], [[indistinguishable]], [[interchangeable]]
**3** (of twins) developed from a single fertilized ovum that has split into two, and thus of the same sex and very much [[alike]]
