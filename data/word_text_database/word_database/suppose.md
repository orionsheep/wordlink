**suppose** *vb* **-posing**, **-posed**
**1** to [[presume]] (something) to be true without certain knowledge: I suppose it will be in the papers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[imagine]], [[expect]], [[assume]], [[guess]] (*informal*), [[presume]], [[conjecture]]
**2** to [[consider]] (something) as a possible suggestion for the sake of discussion: suppose you're arrested on a misdemeanour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imagine]], [[consider]], [[conjecture]], [[postulate]] (*formal*), [[hypothesize]]
**3** (of a theory) to depend on the truth or existence of: this scenario supposes that he would do so
