**pardon** *vb*
**1** to forgive or excuse (a person) for (an offence, mistake, etc.): I hope you'll pardon the wait
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acquit]], [[let off]] (*informal*), [[exonerate]], absolve
 *n*
**2** [[forgiveness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[forgiveness]], [[absolution]]
**3** official release from punishment for a crime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acquittal]], [[amnesty]], exoneration
 *interj*
**4** Also: **[[pardon me]]**, **I beg your pardon**
**A** sorry; excuse me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[pardon me]]**: forgive me, excuse me
 **B** what did you say?
 **[[pardonable]]** *adj*
