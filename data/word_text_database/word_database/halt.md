**halt** *vb*
**1** to come to a [[stop]] or bring (someone or something) to a [[stop]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[break off]], [[stand still]], [[wait]], rest
 *n*
**2** a temporary [[standstill]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[end]], [[close]], [[pause]], [[standstill]], [[stoppage]]
**3** a military command to [[stop]]
**4** (*chiefly* *Brit*) a minor railway station without a building: Deeside Halt
**5** **call a halt to** to put an [[end]] to
