**telephone** *n*
**1** a piece of equipment for transmitting speech, consisting of a microphone and receiver mounted on a [[handset]]: the telephone was ringing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[phone]], [[mobile]], [[mobile]] [[phone]] *or (informal)* moby, cellphone *or* cellular [[phone]] (*US*), [[handset]], [[landline]], [[dog and bone]] (*slang*), [[iPhone]] (*trademark*), [[smartphone]], [[Blackberry]], [[camera phone]], [[picture phone]]
**2** the worldwide system of communications using telephones: reports came in by telephone
 *vb* **-phoning**, **-phoned**
**3** to [[call]] or talk to (a person) by telephone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call]], [[phone]], [[ring]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), dial
 *adj*
**4** of or using a telephone: a telephone [[call]]
 **[[telephonic]]** *adj*
