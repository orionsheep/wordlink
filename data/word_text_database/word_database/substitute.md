**substitute** *vb* **-tuting**, **-tuted**
**1** (often foll. by *for*) to take the place of or put in place of another person or thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[replace]], [[exchange]], [[swap]], [[change]], [[switch]], [[interchange]]
**2** (*chem*) to [[replace]] (an atom or group in a molecule) with (another atom or group)
 *n*
**3** a person or thing that takes the place of another, such as a player who takes the place of a team-mate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[replacement]], [[reserve]], [[surrogate]], [[deputy]], [[sub]], [[proxy]], [[locum]]
 **[[substitution]]** *n*
