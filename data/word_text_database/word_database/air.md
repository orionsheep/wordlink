**air** *n*
**1** the mixture of gases that forms the earth's [[atmosphere]]. It consists chiefly of nitrogen, oxygen, argon, and carbon dioxide
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[atmosphere]], [[sky]], [[heavens]], [[aerosphere]]
**2** the space above and around the earth; [[sky]]. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**aerial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[atmosphere]], [[sky]], [[heavens]], [[aerosphere]]
**3** a distinctive quality, [[appearance]], or [[manner]]: I thought he had an air of elegance and celebrity about him
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manner]], [[appearance]], [[look]], [[aspect]], [[atmosphere]], [[mood]], [[impression]], [[aura]]
**4** a simple [[tune]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tune]], [[song]], [[theme]], [[melody]], [[strain]], [[lay]], [[aria]]
**5** transportation in aircraft: I went off to Italy by air and train
**6** **in the air** in circulation; current: a sense of expectation is in the air
**7** **into thin air** leaving no trace behind
**8** **on the air** in the act of broadcasting on radio or television
**9** **up in the air** uncertain
 *vb*
**10** to make known publicly: these issues will be aired at a ministerial meeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[publicize]], [[reveal]], [[exhibit]], [[voice]], [[express]], [[display]], [[circulate]], [[make public]]
**11** to expose to air to dry or ventilate
**12** (of a television or radio programme) to be broadcast
