**threat** *n*
**1** a declaration of an intention to inflict harm: they carried out their threat to kill the hostages
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[threatening remark]], [[menace]]
**2** a strong possibility of something dangerous or unpleasant happening: the wet weather will bring a threat of flooding
**3** a person or thing that is regarded as dangerous and likely to inflict harm: unemployment is a serious threat to the social order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[danger]], [[risk]], [[hazard]], [[menace]], [[peril]]
