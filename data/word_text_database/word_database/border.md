**border** *n*
**1** the dividing [[line]] between political or geographic regions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frontier]], [[line]], [[limit]], [[bounds]], [[boundary]], [[perimeter]], [[borderline]]
**2** a band or [[margin]] around or along the [[edge]] of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[margin]], [[verge]] (*Brit*), [[rim]]
**3** a design around the [[edge]] of something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[margin]], [[verge]] (*Brit*), [[rim]]
**4** a narrow strip of ground planted with flowers or shrubs: a herbaceous border
 *vb*
**5** to provide with a border
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[bound]], [[decorate]], [[trim]], [[fringe]], [[rim]], [[hem]]
**6**
**A** to be adjacent to; lie along the [[boundary]] of
 **B** to be nearly the same as; [[verge]] on: a story that borders on the unbelievable
