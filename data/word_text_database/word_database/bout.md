**bout** *n*
**1**
**A** a [[period]] of time spent doing something, such as drinking alcohol
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[term]], [[fit]], [[spell]], [[turn]], interval
 **B** a [[period]] of illness: a bad bout of flu
**2** a boxing, wrestling, or fencing [[match]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fight]], [[match]], [[competition]], [[struggle]], [[contest]], [[set-to]] (*informal*), [[encounter]], [[engagement]]
