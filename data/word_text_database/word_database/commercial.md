**commercial** *adj*
**1** of or engaged in commerce: commercial exploitation of sport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mercantile]], [[trading]]
**2** sponsored or paid for by an advertiser: commercial radio
**3** having profit as the main aim: this is a more commercial, accessible album than its predecessor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[materialistic]], [[mercenary]], profit-making
 *n*
**4** a radio or television advertisement
