**echo** *n*, *pl* **-oes**
**1**
**A** the [[reflection]] of sound by a solid object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverberation]], [[ringing]], [[repetition]], [[answer]], [[resonance]], [[resounding]]
 **B** a sound reflected by a solid object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverberation]], [[ringing]], [[repetition]], [[answer]], [[resonance]], [[resounding]]
**2** a [[repetition]] or [[imitation]] of someone else's opinions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]], [[reflection]], [[clone]], [[reproduction]], [[imitation]], [[duplicate]], [[double]], [[reiteration]]
**3** something that brings back memories: an echo of the past
**4** the signal reflected back to a radar transmitter by an object
 *vb* **-oing**, **-oed**
**5** (of a sound) to be reflected off an object in such a way that it can be heard again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverberate]], [[repeat]], [[resound]], [[ring]], [[resonate]]
**6** (of a place) to be filled with a sound and its echoes: the church echoed with singing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reverberate]], [[repeat]], [[resound]], [[ring]], [[resonate]]
**7** (of people) to [[repeat]] or [[imitate]] (what someone else has said): his conclusion echoed that of Jung
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[recall]], [[reflect]], [[copy]], [[mirror]], [[resemble]], [[imitate]], [[ape]]
 **[[echoing]]** *adj*
