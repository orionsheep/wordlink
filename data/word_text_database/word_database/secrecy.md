**secrecy** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** the state of being secret
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confidentiality]], [[privacy]]
**2** the ability or tendency to keep things secret
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mystery]], [[stealth]], [[concealment]], [[furtiveness]], [[secretiveness]], [[clandestineness]], [[covertness]]
