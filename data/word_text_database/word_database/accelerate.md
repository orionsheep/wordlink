**accelerate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to move or cause to move more quickly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[grow]], [[advance]], [[extend]], [[expand]], [[raise]], [[swell]], [[enlarge]]
**2** to cause to happen sooner than expected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expedite]], [[further]], [[speed up]], [[hasten]]
