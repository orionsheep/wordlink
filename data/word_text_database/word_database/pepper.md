**pepper** *n*
**1** a sharp hot condiment obtained from the fruit of an Asian climbing plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seasoning]], [[flavour]], [[spice]]
**2** Also called: **capsicum** a colourful tropical fruit used as a vegetable and a condiment
 *vb*
**3** to season with pepper
**4** to [[sprinkle]] liberally: his speech is heavily peppered with Americanisms
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sprinkle]], [[spot]], [[scatter]], [[dot]], [[fleck]], [[intersperse]], [[speck]], [[spatter]]
**5** to [[pelt]] with small missiles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pelt]], [[hit]], [[shower]], [[blitz]], [[rake]], [[bombard]], [[assail]], [[strafe]]
