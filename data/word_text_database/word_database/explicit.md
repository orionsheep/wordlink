**explicit** *adj*
**1** precisely and clearly expressed, leaving nothing to implication: an explicit commitment to democracy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clear]], [[obvious]], [[specific]], [[direct]], [[precise]], [[straightforward]], [[definite]], [[overt]]
**2** leaving little to the imagination; graphically detailed: the film contains some sexually explicit scenes
**3** (of a person) expressing something in a [[precise]] and [[clear]] way, so as to leave no doubt about what is meant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frank]], [[specific]], [[graphic]], [[unambiguous]], [[unrestricted]], [[unrestrained]], [[uncensored]]
 **[[explicitly]]** *adv*
