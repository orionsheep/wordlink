**edit** *vb* **editing**, **edited**
**1** to prepare (text) for publication by checking and improving its accuracy or clarity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[revise]], [[improve]], [[correct]], [[polish]], [[adapt]], [[rewrite]], [[condense]], [[redraft]]
**2** to be in charge of (a newspaper or magazine)
**3** to prepare (a film, television or radio programme, etc.) by rearranging or selecting material
**4** **edit out** to remove (a section) from a text, film, etc.
