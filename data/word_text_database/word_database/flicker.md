**flicker** *vb*
**1** to give out an unsteady or irregular light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[twinkle]], [[flash]], [[sparkle]], [[flare]], [[shimmer]], [[gutter]], [[glimmer]]
**2** to move quickly to and fro
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flutter]], [[waver]], [[quiver]], vibrate
 *n*
**3** an unsteady or brief light
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glimmer]], [[flash]], [[spark]], [[flare]], [[gleam]]
**4** a brief or faint indication of emotion: a flicker of fear in his voice
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[breath]], [[spark]], [[glimmer]], [[iota]]
