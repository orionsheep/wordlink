**bark**^2 *n*
**1** an outer protective [[layer]] of dead corklike cells on the trunks of trees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covering]], [[casing]], [[cover]], [[skin]], [[layer]], [[crust]], cortex (*anatomy*, *botany*), rind
 *vb*
**2** to scrape or rub off ([[skin]]), as in an injury
**3** to remove the bark from (a tree)
