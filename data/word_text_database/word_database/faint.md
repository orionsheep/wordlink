**faint** *adj*
**1** lacking clarity, brightness, or volume: her voice was very faint
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dim]], [[low]], [[soft]], [[faded]], [[distant]], [[vague]], [[unclear]], [[muted]]
**2** feeling [[dizzy]] or [[weak]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dizzy]], [[giddy]], [[light-headed]], [[weak]], [[exhausted]], [[wobbly]], [[muzzy]], [[woozy]] (*informal*)
**3** lacking conviction or force: a faint attempt to smile
 *vb*
**4** to [[lose consciousness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pass out]], [[black out]], [[lose consciousness]], [[keel over]] (*informal*), [[go out]], [[collapse]], [[swoon]] (*literary*), flake out (*informal*)
 *n*
**5** a sudden loss of consciousness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blackout]], [[collapse]], [[coma]], [[swoon]] (*literary*), [[unconsciousness]]
 **[[faintly]]** *adv*
