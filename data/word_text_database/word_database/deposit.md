**deposit** *vb* **-iting**, **-ited**
**1** to [[put]] down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[put]], [[place]], [[lay]], [[drop]]
**2** to [[entrust]] (money or valuables) for safekeeping
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[store]], [[keep]], [[put]], [[bank]], [[lodge]], [[entrust]], [[consign]]
**3** to [[place]] (money) in a [[bank]] account or other savings account
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[store]], [[keep]], [[put]], [[bank]], [[lodge]], [[entrust]], [[consign]]
**4** to [[lay]] down naturally: the river deposits silt
 *n*
**5** a sum of money placed in a [[bank]] account or other savings account
**6** money given in [[part payment]] for goods or services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[down payment]], [[security]], [[stake]], [[pledge]], [[instalment]], [[retainer]], [[part payment]]
**7** an amount of a substance left on a surface as a result of a chemical or geological process
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accumulation]], [[mass]], [[build-up]], [[layer]]
