**independent** *adj*
**1** [[free]] from the influence or control of others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[separate]], [[unattached]], [[uncontrolled]], [[unconstrained]]
**2** not dependent on anything else for function or validity
**3** not relying on the support, esp. financial support, of others
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[self-sufficient]], [[free]], [[liberated]], [[self-contained]], [[self-reliant]], [[self-supporting]]
**4** capable of acting for oneself or on one's own
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[self-governing]], [[free]], [[autonomous]], [[liberated]], [[sovereign]], [[self-determining]], [[nonaligned]]
**5** of or having a private income large enough to enable one to live without working: independent means
**6** (*maths*) (of a variable) not dependent on another variable
 *n*
**7** an independent person or thing
**8** a politician who does not represent any political party
 **[[independence]]** *n* 
 **[[independently]]** *adv*
