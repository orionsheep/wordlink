**flavour** * or * (*US*) **flavor** *n*
**1** [[taste]] perceived in food or liquid in the mouth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[taste]], [[seasoning]], [[flavouring]], [[savour]], [[relish]], [[smack]], [[aroma]], [[zest]]
**2** a distinctive [[quality]] or atmosphere: Rome has its own particular flavour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[quality]], [[feeling]], [[feel]], [[style]], [[character]], [[tone]], [[essence]], tinge
 *vb*
**3** to give flavour to: salmon flavoured with dill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[season]], [[spice]], [[add flavour to]], [[enrich]], [[infuse]], [[imbue]], [[pep up]], [[leaven]]
 **[[flavourless]]** * or * (*US*) **[[flavorless]]** *adj*
