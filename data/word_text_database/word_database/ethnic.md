**ethnic** * or ***ethnical** *adj*
**1** of or relating to a human group with [[racial]], religious, and linguistic characteristics in common
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cultural]], [[national]], [[traditional]], [[native]], [[folk]], [[racial]], [[genetic]], [[indigenous]]
**2** characteristic of another culture, esp. a peasant one: ethnic foodstuffs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cultural]], [[national]], [[traditional]], [[native]], [[folk]], [[racial]], [[genetic]], [[indigenous]]
 **[[ethnically]]** *adv*
