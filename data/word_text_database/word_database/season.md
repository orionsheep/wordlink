**season** *n*
**1** one of the four divisions of the year (spring, summer, autumn, and winter), each of which has characteristic weather conditions
**2** a [[period]] of the year characterized by particular conditions or activities: the typhoon season; the football season
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[time]], [[term]], [[spell]]
**3** the [[period]] during which any particular species of animal, bird, or fish is legally permitted to be caught or killed: the deer season
**4** any definite or indefinite [[period]]: the busy season
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[time]], [[term]], [[spell]]
**5** any [[period]] during which a show or play is performed at one venue: the show ran for three seasons
**6** **in season**
**A** (of game) permitted to be killed
 **B** (of fresh food) readily available
 **C** (of animals) ready to mate
 *vb*
**7** to add herbs, [[salt]], pepper, or [[spice]] to (food) in order to enhance the [[flavour]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flavour]], [[salt]], [[spice]], [[enliven]], [[pep up]]
**8** (in the preparation of timber) to dry and harden
**9** to make experienced: old men [[seasoned]] by living
 **[[seasoned]]** *adj*
