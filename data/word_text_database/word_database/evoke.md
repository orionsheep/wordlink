**evoke** *vb* **evoking**, **evoked**
**1** to call or [[summon up]] (a memory or feeling) from the past
**2** to provoke or bring about: his sacking evoked a huge public protest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arouse]], [[cause]], [[induce]], [[awaken]], [[give rise to]], [[stir up]], [[rekindle]], [[summon up]]
