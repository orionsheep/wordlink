**apartment** *n*
**1** any [[room]] in a building, usually one of several forming a [[suite]], used as living [[accommodation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rooms]], [[quarters]], [[accommodation]], [[living quarters]]
**2** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) a set of [[rooms]] forming a home within a building usually incorporating other similar homes. Also called (Brit): **[[flat]]**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flat]], [[room]], [[suite]], [[penthouse]], [[duplex]] (*US** & **Canad*), [[crib]], [[bachelor apartment]] (*Canad*)
