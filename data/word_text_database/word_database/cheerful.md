**1** having a [[happy]] disposition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happy]], [[optimistic]], [[enthusiastic]], [[jolly]], [[merry]], [[upbeat]] (*informal*), [[buoyant]], [[cheery]]
**2** pleasantly bright: a cheerful colour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pleasant]]
**3** ungrudging: a cheerful giver
 **[[cheerfully]]** *adv* 
 **[[cheerfulness]]** *n*
