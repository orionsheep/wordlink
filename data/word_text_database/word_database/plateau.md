**plateau** (**plat**-oh) *n*, *pl* **-teaus*** or ***-teaux** (-ohs)
**1** a wide [[level]] area of high land
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upland]], [[table]], [[highland]], [[tableland]]
**2** a relatively long period of stability: the body temperature rises to a plateau that it keeps until shortly before bedtime
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[levelling off]], [[level]], [[stage]], stability
 *vb*
**3** to remain stable for a long period
