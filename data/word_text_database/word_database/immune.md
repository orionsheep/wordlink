**immune** *adj*
**1** protected against a specific disease by inoculation or as the result of natural resistance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[immune to]]**: resistant to, free from, protected from, safe from, not open to, spared from, secure against, invulnerable to
**2** exempt from obligation or penalty
**3** **[[immune to]]** secure against: football is not [[immune to]] economic recession
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[immune to]]**: unaffected by, invulnerable to
