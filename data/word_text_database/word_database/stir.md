**stir** *vb* **stirring**, **stirred**
**1** to [[mix]] up (a liquid) by moving a spoon or stick around in it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mix]], [[beat]], [[agitate]]
**2** to [[move]] slightly
**3** **stir from** to depart from (one's usual or preferred place)
**4** to get up after sleeping
**5** to [[excite]] or [[move]] (someone) emotionally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stimulate]], [[move]], [[excite]], [[spur]], [[provoke]], [[arouse]], [[awaken]], [[rouse]]
**6** to [[move]] (oneself) quickly or vigorously; exert (oneself)
**7** to wake up: to stir someone from sleep
 *n*
**8** a stirring
**9** a strong reaction, usually of [[excitement]]: she created a stir wherever she went
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commotion]], [[excitement]], [[activity]], [[disorder]], [[fuss]], [[disturbance]], [[bustle]], [[flurry]]
