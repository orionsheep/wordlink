**shunt** *vb*
**1** to move (objects or people) to a different position
**2** (*railways*) to transfer (engines or carriages) from track to track
 *n*
**3** the act of shunting
**4** a railway point
**5** (*electronics*) a conductor connected in parallel across a part of a circuit to divert a known fraction of the current
**6** (*informal*) a collision where one vehicle runs into the back of another
