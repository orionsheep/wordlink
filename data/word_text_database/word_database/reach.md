**reach** *vb*
**1** to [[arrive at]] or [[get to]] (a place)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrive at]], [[get to]], [[make]], [[attain]]
**2** to [[make]] a movement (towards), as if to [[grasp]] or [[touch]]: she reached for her bag
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[touch]], [[grasp]], [[extend to]], [[stretch to]], [[contact]]
**3** to succeed in touching: I can't reach that shelf unless I stand on a chair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[touch]], [[grasp]], [[extend to]], [[stretch to]], [[contact]]
**4** to [[make]] [[contact]] or communication with: to reach a wider audience
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contact]], [[get in touch with]], [[get through to]], [[communicate with]], [[get hold of]]
**5** to extend as far as (a point or place): to reach the ceiling
**6** to come to (a certain condition or situation): to reach a compromise
**7** to [[arrive at]] or amount to (an amount or value): temperatures in Greece reached 35° yesterday
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attain]], [[get to]]
**8** (*informal*) to give (something to a person) with the outstretched hand
 *n*
**9** the [[extent]] or [[distance]] of reaching: within easy reach
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grasp]], [[range]], [[distance]], [[stretch]], [[capacity]], [[extent]], [[extension]], [[scope]]
**10** the [[range]] of [[influence]] or [[power]]: it symbolized America's global reach
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jurisdiction]], [[power]], [[influence]]
**11** **reaches** a section of river, land, or sky: the quieter reaches of the upper Thames
 **[[reachable]]** *adj*
