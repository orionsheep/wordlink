**mask** *n*
**1** any covering for the whole or a part of the face worn for amusement, protection, or [[disguise]]
**2** behaviour that hides one's true feelings: his mask of detachment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[façade]], [[disguise]], [[front]] (*informal*), [[cover]], [[screen]], [[veil]], [[guise]], [[camouflage]]
**3** (*surgery*) a sterile gauze covering for the nose and mouth worn to minimize the spread of germs
**4** a device placed over the nose and mouth to facilitate or prevent inhalation of a gas
**5** a moulded likeness of a face or head, such as a death mask
**6** the face or head of an animal such as a fox
 *vb*
**7** to [[cover]] with or put on a mask
**8** to [[hide]] or [[disguise]]: a high brick wall that [[masked]] the [[front]] of the building
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disguise]], [[hide]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]], [[cover]] (up), [[screen]], [[blanket]], [[veil]]
**9** to [[cover]] so as to protect
 **[[masked]]** *adj*
