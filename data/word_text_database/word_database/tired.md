**tired** *adj*
**1** [[weary]] or [[exhausted]]: they were tired after their long journey
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhausted]], [[fatigued]], [[weary]], [[flagging]], [[drained]], [[sleepy]], [[worn out]], [[drowsy]], [[tuckered out]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
**2** [[bored]] with or no longer interested in something: I'm tired of staying in watching TV every night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bored]], [[fed up]], [[weary]], [[sick]] (*informal*), [[hoha]] (*NZ*)
**3** having been used so often as to be no longer interesting: you haven't fallen for that tired [[old]] line, have you?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hackneyed]], [[stale]], [[well-worn]], [[old]], [[corny]] (*slang*), [[threadbare]], [[trite]], [[clichéd]]
 **[[tiredness]]** *n*
