**knock down** *vb*
**1** to strike to the ground with a blow, such as in boxing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[knock someone down]]**: run over, hit, run down, knock over, mow down
**2** (in auctions) to declare an article sold
**3** to demolish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[knock something down]]**: demolish, destroy, flatten, tear down, level, fell, dismantle, bulldoze, kennet (*Austral* *slang*), jeff (*Austral* *slang*)
**4** (*informal*) to reduce (a price)
 *adj* **knockdown**
**5** powerful: a knockdown argument
**6** (*chiefly* *Brit*) (of a price) very cheap
**7** easily dismantled: knockdown furniture
