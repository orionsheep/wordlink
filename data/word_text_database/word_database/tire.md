**tire** *vb* **[[tiring]]**, **tired**
**1** to reduce the energy of, as by exertion: she could still do things that would tire people half her age
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhaust]], [[drain]], [[fatigue]], [[weary]], [[wear out]]
**2** to become wearied or bored: he simply stopped talking when he tired of my questions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flag]], [[become tired]], [[fail]]
 **[[tiring]]** *adj*
