**reserve** *vb* **-serving**, **-served**
**1** to [[keep]] back or [[set aside]] for future use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[keep]], [[hold]], [[save]], [[store]], [[retain]], [[set aside]], [[stockpile]], [[hoard]]
**2** to obtain by arranging beforehand: I phoned to reserve two tickets
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[book]], [[prearrange]], [[engage]]
**3** to [[keep]] for oneself: the association reserves the right to charge a fee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[keep]], [[hold]], [[save]], [[store]], [[retain]], [[set aside]], [[stockpile]], [[hoard]]
**4** to delay announcing (a legal judgment)
 *n*
**5** something kept back or [[set aside]] for future use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[store]], [[fund]], [[savings]], [[stock]], [[supply]], [[reservoir]], [[hoard]], [[cache]]

**7** (*sport*) a [[substitute]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[substitute]], [[extra]], [[spare]], [[fall-back]], [[auxiliary]]
**8** a protected area for wildlife: an elephant reserve
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[park]], [[reservation]], [[preserve]], [[sanctuary]], [[tract]], [[forest park]] (*NZ*)
**9** the hiding of one's feelings and personality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shyness]], [[silence]], [[restraint]], [[constraint]], [[reticence]], [[secretiveness]], [[taciturnity]]
