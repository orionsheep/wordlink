**1** to [[happen]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happen]], [[take place]], [[come about]], [[turn up]] (*informal*), [[crop up]] (*informal*), [[transpire]] (*informal*), [[befall]]
**2** to [[be found]] or [[be present]]; [[exist]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exist]], [[appear]], [[be found]], [[develop]], [[turn up]], [[be present]], [[manifest itself]], [[present itself]]
**3** **occur to** to come into the mind of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[occur to someone]]**: come to mind, strike someone, dawn on someone, spring to mind, cross someone's mind, enter someone's head, suggest itself to someone
