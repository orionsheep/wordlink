**deduction** *n*
**1** the act or process of subtracting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[subtraction]], [[reduction]], [[concession]]
**2** something that is deducted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[discount]], [[reduction]], [[cut]], [[concession]], [[decrease]], [[rebate]], [[diminution]]
**3** (*logic*)
**A** a process of [[reasoning]] by which a conclusion necessarily follows from a set of general premises
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reasoning]], [[thinking]], [[thought]], [[analysis]], logic
 **B** a conclusion reached by this process
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: conclusion, [[finding]], [[verdict]], [[judgment]], [[assumption]], [[inference]]
 **[[deductive]]** *adj*
