**interval** *n*
**1** the [[period]] of time between two events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[spell]], [[space]], [[stretch]], [[pause]], [[span]]
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral*) a short [[period]] between parts of a play, concert, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break]], [[interlude]], [[intermission]], [[rest]], [[gap]], [[pause]], [[respite]], [[lull]]
**3** (*music*) the difference of pitch between two notes
**4** **at intervals**
**A** now and then: add water to the mix at intervals
 **B** with a certain amount of [[space]] between: the poles were placed at intervals of twenty metres
