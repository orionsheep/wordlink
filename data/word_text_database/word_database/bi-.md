**bi-** *combining form*
**1** having two: bifocal
**2** occurring or lasting for two: biennial
**3** on both sides, directions, etc.: bilateral
**4** occurring twice during: biweekly
**5** (*chem*)
**A** denoting a compound containing two identical cyclical hydrocarbon systems: biphenyl
 **B** indicating an acid salt of a dibasic acid: sodium bicarbonate
