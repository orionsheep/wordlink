**trend** *n*
**1** general [[tendency]] or [[direction]]: a trend towards flexible working hours
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tendency]], [[swing]], [[drift]], [[inclination]], [[current]], [[direction]], [[flow]], [[leaning]]
**2** fashionable [[style]]: she set a trend for wearing lingerie as outer garments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fashion]], [[craze]], [[fad]] (*informal*), [[mode]], [[thing]], [[style]], [[rage]], vogue
 *vb*
**3** to take a certain trend
**4** to be widely discussed on a social media site
