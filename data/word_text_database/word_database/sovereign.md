**sovereign** *n*
**1** the [[Royal]] [[ruler]] of a country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[monarch]], [[ruler]], king *or* queen, [[chief]], [[potentate]], emperor *or* empress, prince *or* princess
**2** a former British gold coin worth one pound sterling
 *adj*
**3** independent of outside authority; not governed by another country: a sovereign nation
**4** [[supreme]] in rank or authority: a sovereign queen
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supreme]], [[ruling]], [[absolute]], [[royal]], [[principal]], [[imperial]], kingly *or* queenly
**5** (*old-fashioned*) [[excellent]] or outstanding: a sovereign remedy for epilepsy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excellent]], [[efficient]], [[effectual]]
