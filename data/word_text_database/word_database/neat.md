**neat** *adj*
**1** clean and [[tidy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tidy]], [[trim]], [[orderly]], [[spruce]], [[shipshape]], [[spick-and-span]]
**2** smoothly or competently done: a neat answer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[graceful]], [[elegant]], [[adept]], [[nimble]], [[adroit]], [[efficient]]
**3** (of alcoholic drinks) [[undiluted]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[undiluted]], [[straight]], [[pure]], [[unmixed]]
**4** (*slang*, *chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) admirable; [[excellent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cool]], [[great]] (*informal*), [[excellent]], [[brilliant]], [[superb]], [[fantastic]] (*informal*), [[tremendous]], [[fabulous]] (*informal*), [[booshit]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[exo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[sik]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[rad]] (*informal*), [[phat]] (*slang*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*)
