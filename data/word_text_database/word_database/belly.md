**belly** *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**1** the part of the body of a vertebrate containing the intestines and other organs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stomach]], [[insides]] (*informal*), [[gut]], [[abdomen]], [[tummy]] (*informal*), [[paunch]], [[potbelly]], [[corporation]] (*informal*), [[puku]] (*NZ*)
**2** the [[stomach]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stomach]], [[insides]] (*informal*), [[gut]], [[abdomen]], [[tummy]] (*informal*), [[paunch]], [[potbelly]], [[corporation]] (*informal*), [[puku]] (*NZ*)
**3** the front, lower, or inner part of something
**4** **go belly up** (*informal*) to die, fail, or end
 *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
**5** to swell out; bulge
