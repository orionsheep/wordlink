**appreciate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to [[value]] highly: we appreciate his music but can't afford £400 a seat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enjoy]], [[like]], [[value]], [[respect]], [[prize]], [[admire]], [[treasure]], [[rate highly]]
**2** to [[be aware of]] and [[understand]]: I can fully appreciate how desperate you must feel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be aware of]], [[understand]], [[realize]], [[recognize]], [[perceive]], [[take account of]], [[be sensitive to]], [[sympathize with]]
**3** to feel grateful for: we do appreciate all you do for us
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be grateful for]], [[be obliged for]], [[be thankful for]], [[give thanks for]], [[be indebted for]], [[be in debt for]], [[be appreciative of]]
**4** to [[increase]] in [[value]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[increase]], [[rise]], [[grow]], [[gain]], [[improve]], [[enhance]], [[soar]]
