**shelter** *n*
**1** something that provides [[cover]] or [[protection]] from weather or danger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[screen]]
**2** the [[protection]] given by such a [[cover]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[safety]], [[refuge]], [[cover]]
 *vb*
**3** to [[take cover]] from bad weather
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take shelter]], [[hide]], [[seek refuge]], [[take cover]]
**4** to provide with a place to live or a hiding place: to shelter refugees
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]], [[shield]], [[harbour]], [[safeguard]], [[cover]], [[hide]], [[guard]], [[defend]]
