**approach** *vb*
**1** to [[come close]] or closer to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[move towards]], [[reach]], [[near]], [[come close]], [[come near]], [[draw near]]
**2** to make a [[proposal]] or suggestion to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make a proposal to]], [[speak to]], [[apply to]], [[appeal to]], [[proposition]], [[solicit]], [[sound out]], [[make overtures to]]
**3** to begin to deal with (a matter)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[set about]], [[tackle]], [[undertake]], [[embark on]], [[get down to]], [[launch into]], [[begin work on]], commence on
 *n*
**4** the act of [[coming]] close or closer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advance]], [[coming]], [[nearing]], [[appearance]], [[arrival]], [[drawing near]]
**5** a [[proposal]] or suggestion made to a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proposal]], [[offer]], [[appeal]], [[advance]], [[application]], [[invitation]], [[proposition]], [[overture]]
**6** the [[way]] or [[means]] of reaching a place; [[access]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[access]], [[way]], [[drive]], [[road]], [[passage]], [[entrance]], [[avenue]], [[passageway]]
**7** a [[way]] of dealing with a matter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[means]], [[style]], [[method]], [[technique]], [[manner]]
**8** an approximation
**9** the course followed by an aircraft preparing for landing
 **[[approachable]]** *adj*
