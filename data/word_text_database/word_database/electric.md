**electric** *adj*
**1** produced by, transmitting, or [[powered]] by electricity: an electric fire
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[electric-powered]], [[powered]], [[cordless]], [[battery-operated]], [[electrically-charged]], [[mains-operated]]
**2** very [[tense]] or [[exciting]]: the atmosphere was electric
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charged]], [[exciting]], [[stirring]], [[thrilling]], [[stimulating]], [[dynamic]], [[tense]], [[rousing]], adrenalized
 *n*
**3** **electrics** (*Brit*) an electric circuit or electric appliances
