**mundane** *adj*
**1** [[everyday]], [[ordinary]], and therefore not very interesting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ordinary]], [[routine]], [[commonplace]], [[banal]], [[everyday]], [[day-to-day]], [[prosaic]], [[humdrum]]
**2** relating to the world or [[worldly]] matters
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[earthly]], [[worldly]], [[secular]], [[mortal]], [[terrestrial]], [[temporal]]
