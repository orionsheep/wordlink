**comparative** *adj*
**1** [[relative]]: despite the importance of his discoveries, he died in comparative poverty
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relative]], [[qualified]], [[by comparison]]
**2** involving comparison: comparative religion
**3** (*grammar*) the form of an adjective or adverb that indicates that the quality denoted is possessed to a greater extent. In English the comparative is marked by the suffix *-er* or the word *more*
 *n*
**4** the comparative form of an adjective or adverb
 **[[comparatively]]** *adv*
