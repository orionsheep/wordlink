**discipline** *n*
**1** the practice of imposing strict rules of behaviour on other people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[authority]], [[regulation]], [[supervision]], [[orderliness]], [[strictness]]
**2** the ability to behave and work in a controlled manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[self-control]], [[control]], [[restraint]], [[self-discipline]], [[willpower]], [[self-restraint]], [[orderliness]]
**3** a particular [[area]] of academic study
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[field of study]], [[area]], [[subject]], [[theme]], [[topic]], [[course]], [[curriculum]], speciality
 *vb* **-plining**, **-plined**
**4** to improve or attempt to improve the behaviour of (oneself or someone else) by training or rules
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[train]], [[educate]]
**5** to [[punish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[punish]], [[correct]], [[reprimand]], [[castigate]], [[chastise]], [[chasten]], [[penalize]], [[bring to book]]
