**mainstream** *n*
**1** the people or things representing the most common or generally [[accepted]] ideas and styles in a society, art form, etc.: the mainstream of academic life
**2** the main [[current]] of a river
 *adj*
**3** belonging to the social or cultural mainstream: mainstream American movies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conventional]], [[general]], [[established]], [[received]], [[accepted]], [[current]], [[prevailing]], [[orthodox]], [[lamestream]] (*informal*)
