**gush** *vb*
**1** to [[pour]] out suddenly and profusely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flow]], [[run]], [[rush]], [[flood]], [[pour]], [[stream]], [[cascade]], [[spurt]]
**2** to speak or behave in an overenthusiastic manner: I'm not about to start gushing about raspberry coulis
 *n*
**3** a sudden large [[flow]] of liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stream]], [[flow]], [[rush]], [[flood]], [[jet]], [[cascade]], [[torrent]], [[spurt]]
**4** a sudden surge of strong feeling: she felt a gush of pure affection for her mother
