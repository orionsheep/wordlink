**annual** *adj*
**1** occurring or done [[once a year]]: the union's annual conference
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[once a year]], [[yearly]]
**2** lasting for a year: the annual subscription
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[yearlong]], [[yearly]]
 *n*
**3** a plant that completes its life cycle in one year
**4** a book published once every year
