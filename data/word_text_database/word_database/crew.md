**crew** *n*
**1** the people who serve on or operate a ship, boat, or aircraft
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: (ship's) [[company]], [[hands]], (ship's) [[complement]]
**2** a group of people working together: a film crew
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[team]], [[squad]], [[gang]], [[corps]], [[posse]]
**3** (*informal*) any group of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crowd]], [[set]], [[bunch]] (*informal*), [[band]], [[pack]], [[gang]], [[mob]], horde
 *vb*
**4** to serve as a crew member on a ship or boat
