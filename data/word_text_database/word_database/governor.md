**governor** *n*
**1** the [[chief]] political [[administrator]] of a region, such as a US state or a colony. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**gubernatorial**
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leader]], [[administrator]], [[ruler]], [[head]], [[director]], [[manager]], [[chief]], [[executive]]
**2** (*Brit*) the senior [[administrator]] of a school, prison, or other institution
**3** (*Brit* *informal*) one's employer or father
 **[[governorship]]** *n*
