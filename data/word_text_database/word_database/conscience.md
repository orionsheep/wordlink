**conscience** *n*
**1** the [[sense of right and wrong]] that governs a person's thoughts and actions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[principles]], [[scruples]], [[moral sense]], [[sense of right and wrong]], [[still small voice]]
**2** a feeling of [[guilt]]: he showed no hint of conscience over the suffering he had inflicted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[guilt]], [[shame]], [[regret]], [[remorse]], [[contrition]], [[self-reproach]]
**3** **in (all) conscience** in fairness
**4** **on one's conscience** causing feelings of [[guilt]]
