**heroine** *n*
**1** the principal female character in a novel, play, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protagonist]], [[leading lady]], [[diva]], [[prima donna]]
**2** a woman of exceptional courage, nobility, etc.
**3** a woman who is idealized for having superior qualities in any field
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idol]], [[favourite]], [[pin-up]] (*slang*), [[fave]] (*informal*)
