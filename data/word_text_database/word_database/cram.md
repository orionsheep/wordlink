**cram** *vb* **cramming**, **crammed**
**1** to [[force]] (more people or things) into (a place) than it can hold
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stuff]], [[force]], [[jam]], [[shove]], [[compress]]
**2** to eat or feed to excess
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) to [[study]] hard just before an examination
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[study]], [[revise]], [[swot]] (*informal*), [[bone up]] (*informal*), [[mug up]] (*slang*)
