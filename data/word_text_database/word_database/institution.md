**institution** *n*
**1** a large important organization such as a [[university]] or bank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[establishment]], [[body]], [[centre]], [[school]], [[university]], [[society]], [[association]], [[college]]
**2** an organization providing residential care for people with special needs
**3** an established [[custom]], [[law]], or principle: the institution of marriage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[custom]], [[practice]], [[tradition]], [[law]], [[rule]], [[procedure]], [[convention]], [[ritual]]
**4** (*informal*) a well-established person or feature: the programme has became an institution
**5** an instituting or being instituted
