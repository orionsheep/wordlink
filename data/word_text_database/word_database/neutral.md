**neutral** *adj*
**1** not taking any side in a war or dispute
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unbiased]], [[impartial]], [[disinterested]], [[even-handed]], [[uninvolved]], [[nonpartisan]], [[unprejudiced]], [[nonaligned]]
**2** of or belonging to a neutral party or country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unbiased]], [[impartial]], [[disinterested]], [[even-handed]], [[uninvolved]], [[nonpartisan]], [[unprejudiced]], [[nonaligned]]
**3** not displaying any emotions or opinions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expressionless]], [[dull]]
**4** (of a colour) not definite or striking
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[colourless]]
**5** (*chem*) neither acidic nor alkaline
**6** (*physics*) having zero charge or potential
 *n*
**7** a neutral person or nation
**8** the position of the controls of a gearbox that leaves the gears unconnected to the engine
 **[[neutrality]]** *n*
