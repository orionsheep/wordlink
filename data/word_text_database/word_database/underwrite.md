**underwrite** *vb* **-writing**, **-wrote**, **-written**
**1** to accept financial responsibility for a commercial project or enterprise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[finance]], [[back]], [[fund]], [[guarantee]], [[sponsor]], [[insure]], [[ratify]], [[subsidize]]
**2** to sign and issue an insurance policy, thus accepting liability
**3** to support
 **[[underwriter]]** *n*
