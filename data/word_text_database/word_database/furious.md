**furious** *adj*
**1** extremely [[angry]] or annoyed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[angry]], [[raging]], [[fuming]], [[infuriated]], [[incensed]], [[enraged]], [[inflamed]], [[very angry]], [[tooshie]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** [[violent]] or [[unrestrained]], such as in speed or energy: fast and furious dance routines
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[violent]], [[intense]], [[fierce]], [[savage]], [[turbulent]], [[vehement]], [[unrestrained]]
 **[[furiously]]** *adv*
