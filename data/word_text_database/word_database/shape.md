**shape** *n*
**1** the outward [[form]] of an object, produced by its [[outline]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[form]], [[profile]], [[outline]], [[lines]], [[build]], [[figure]], [[silhouette]], [[configuration]]
**2** the [[figure]] or [[outline]] of the body of a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[form]], [[profile]], [[outline]], [[lines]], [[build]], [[figure]], [[silhouette]], [[configuration]]
**3** organized or definite [[form]]: to preserve the union in its present shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appearance]], [[form]], [[aspect]], [[guise]], [[likeness]], [[semblance]]
**4** the specific [[form]] that anything takes on: a gold locket in the shape of a heart
**5** a [[pattern]] or [[mould]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pattern]], [[model]], [[frame]], [[mould]]
**6** [[condition]] or [[state]] of efficiency: in poor shape
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[condition]], [[state]], [[health]], [[trim]], [[fettle]]
**7** **take shape** to assume a definite [[form]]
 *vb* **shaping**, **shaped**
**8** (often foll. by *into* or *up*) to receive or cause to receive shape or [[form]]: spinach shaped into a ball
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mould]], [[form]], [[make]], [[fashion]], [[model]], [[frame]]
**9** to [[mould]] into a particular [[pattern]] or [[form]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mould]], [[form]], [[make]], [[fashion]], [[model]], [[frame]]
**10** to devise or develop: to shape a system of free trade
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[form]], [[make]], [[produce]], [[create]], [[model]], [[fashion]], [[mould]]
