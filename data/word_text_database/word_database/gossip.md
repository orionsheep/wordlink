**gossip** *n*
**1** [[idle talk]], usually about other people's private lives, esp. of a disapproving or malicious nature: office gossip
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idle talk]], [[scandal]], [[hearsay]], [[tittle-tattle]], [[goss]] (*informal*), [[small talk]], [[chitchat]], [[blether]], [[chinwag]] (*Brit* *informal*)
**2** an informal conversation, esp. about other people's private lives: to have a gossip and a giggle
**3** a person who habitually talks about other people, usually maliciously
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[busybody]], [[chatterbox]] (*informal*), [[chatterer]], [[scandalmonger]], [[gossipmonger]], tattletale (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*)
 *vb*
**4** to talk idly or maliciously, esp. about other people's private lives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chat]], [[chatter]], [[jaw]] (*slang*), [[blether]]
 **[[gossipy]]** *adj*
