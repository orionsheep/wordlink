**[[royal]]** *adj*
**1** of or relating to a king or queen or a member of his or her family: the [[royal]] yacht
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[regal]], kingly *or* queenly, [[princely]], [[imperial]], [[sovereign]]
**2** **[[Royal]]** supported by or in the service of royalty: the [[Royal]] Society of Medicine
**3** very [[grand]]: [[royal]] treatment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[splendid]], [[grand]], [[impressive]], [[magnificent]], [[majestic]], stately
 *n*
**4** (*informal*) a king or queen or a member of his or her family
 **[[royally]]** *adv*
