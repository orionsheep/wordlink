**fabulous** *adj*
**1** (*informal*) extremely good
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wonderful]], [[excellent]], [[brilliant]], [[superb]], [[spectacular]], [[fantastic]] (*informal*), [[marvellous]], [[sensational]] (*informal*)
**2** almost [[unbelievable]]: a city of fabulous wealth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[astounding]], [[amazing]], [[extraordinary]], [[remarkable]], [[incredible]] (*informal*), [[astonishing]], [[unbelievable]], [[breathtaking]]
**3** told of in fables and legends: a fabulous horned creature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[legendary]], [[imaginary]], [[mythical]], [[fictitious]], [[made-up]], [[fantastic]], [[invented]], [[unreal]]
 **[[fabulously]]** *adv*
