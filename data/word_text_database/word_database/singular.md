◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remarkable]], [[outstanding]], [[exceptional]], [[notable]], [[eminent]], [[noteworthy]]
**3** [[unusual]]; [[odd]]: a lovable but very singular old woman
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unusual]], [[odd]], [[strange]], [[extraordinary]], [[curious]], [[peculiar]], [[eccentric]], [[queer]] (*old-fashioned*), daggy (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
