**international** *adj*
**1** of or involving two or more nations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[global]], [[world]], [[worldwide]], [[universal]], [[cosmopolitan]], [[intercontinental]]
**2** controlling or legislating for several nations: an international court
**3** available for use by all nations: international waters
 *n*
**4** (*sport*)
**A** a game or match between the national teams of different countries
 **B** a member of a national team
 **[[internationally]]** *adv*
