**domestic** *adj*
**1** of one's own country or a specific country: the domestic economy was generally better
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[home]], [[internal]], [[native]], [[indigenous]]
**2** of the [[home]] or [[family]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[household]], [[home]], [[family]], [[private]]
**3** enjoying [[home]] or [[family]] life: she was never a very domestic sort of person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[home-loving]], [[homely]], [[stay-at-home]], [[domesticated]]
**4** intended for use in the [[home]]: the kitchen was equipped with all the latest domestic appliances
**5** (of an animal) bred or kept as a [[pet]] or for the supply of food
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[domesticated]], [[trained]], [[tame]], [[pet]], house-[[trained]]
 *n*
**6** a [[household]] [[servant]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[servant]], [[help]], [[maid]], [[daily]], [[char]] (*informal*), [[charwoman]]
 **[[domestically]]** *adv*
