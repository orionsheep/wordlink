**path** *n*, *pl* **paths**
**1** a [[road]] or [[way]], often a narrow trodden [[track]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[road]], [[walk]], [[track]], [[trail]], [[avenue]], [[footpath]], [[berm]] (*NZ*)
**2** a surfaced [[walk]], such as through a garden
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[way]], [[road]], [[walk]], [[track]], [[trail]], [[avenue]], [[footpath]], [[berm]] (*NZ*)
**3** the [[course]] or [[direction]] in which something moves: his car skidded into the path of an oncoming lorry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[route]], [[way]], [[course]], [[direction]]
**4** a [[course]] of conduct: the path of reconciliation and forgiveness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[course]], [[way]], [[road]], [[route]]
