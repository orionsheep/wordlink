**eat** *vb* **eating**, **ate**, **eaten**
**1** to take (food) into the mouth and [[swallow]] it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consume]], [[swallow]], [[chew]], [[scoff]] (*slang*), [[devour]], [[munch]], [[tuck into]] (*informal*), [[put away]]
**2** to [[have a meal]]: sometimes we eat out of doors
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[have a meal]], [[lunch]], [[breakfast]], [[dine]], [[snack]], [[feed]], [[graze]] (*informal*), [[have lunch]]
**3** (*informal*) to make anxious or worried: what's eating you?
**4** **eat away *or* into *or* up** to destroy or use up partly or wholly: inflation ate into the firm's profits
 **[[eater]]** *n*
