**rid** *vb* **ridding**, **rid*** or ***ridded**
**1** **rid of** to [[relieve]] (oneself) or make a place [[free]] of (something undesirable)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[free]], [[clear]], [[deliver]], [[relieve]], [[purge]], [[unburden]], [[make free]], [[disencumber]]
**2** **get rid of** to [[relieve]] or [[free]] oneself of (something undesirable)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[get rid of something or someone]]**: dispose of, throw away *or* out, dump, remove, eliminate, expel, eject
