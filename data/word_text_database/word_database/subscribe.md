**subscribe** *vb* **-scribing**, **-scribed**
**1** (usually foll. by *to*) to pay (money) as a contribution (to a charity, for a magazine, etc.) at regular intervals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contribute to]], [[give to]], [[donate to]]
**2** **subscribe to** to give [[support]] or approval to: I do not subscribe to this view
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[advocate]], [[endorse]]
 **[[subscriber]]** *n*
