**umpire** *n*
**1** an official who ensures that the people taking part in a game follow the rules; [[referee]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[referee]], [[judge]], [[arbiter]], [[arbitrator]], umpie (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb* **-piring**, **-pired**
**2** to act as umpire in a game
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[referee]], [[judge]], [[adjudicate]], [[arbitrate]]
