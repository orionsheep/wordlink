**economic** *adj*
**1** of or relating to an economy or economics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[financial]], [[industrial]], [[commercial]]
**2** (*Brit** & **Austral*) capable of being produced or operated for profit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[profitable]], [[successful]], [[commercial]], [[rewarding]], [[productive]], [[lucrative]], [[worthwhile]], [[viable]]
**3** (*informal*) [[inexpensive]] or [[cheap]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[economical]], [[cheap]], [[reasonable]], [[modest]], [[low-priced]], [[inexpensive]]
