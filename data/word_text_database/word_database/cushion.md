**cushion** *n*
**1** a bag filled with a soft material, used to make a seat more comfortable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pillow]], [[pad]], [[bolster]], [[headrest]], [[beanbag]], [[hassock]]
**2** something that provides comfort or absorbs shock
**3** the resilient felt-covered rim of a billiard table
 *vb*
**4** to [[protect]] from injury or shock
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]]
**5** to lessen the effects of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soften]], [[dampen]], [[muffle]], [[mitigate]], [[deaden]], [[suppress]], [[stifle]]
**6** to provide with cushions
 **[[cushiony]]** *adj*
