**more** *adj*
**1** the comparative of **much**, **many**: more joy than you know; even more are leaving the country
**2** [[additional]] or [[further]]: no more apples
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extra]], [[additional]], [[new]], [[other]], [[added]], [[further]], [[new-found]], [[supplementary]]
**3** **more of** to a greater extent or degree: more of a nuisance
 *adv*
**4** used to form the comparative of some adjectives and adverbs: more quickly
**5** the comparative of **much**: people listen to the radio more now
**6** **more or less**
**A** as an estimate; approximately
 **B** to an unspecified extent or degree: the film was a disaster, more or less
