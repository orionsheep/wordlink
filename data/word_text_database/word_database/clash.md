**clash** *vb*
**1** to come into [[conflict]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conflict]], [[grapple]], [[wrangle]], [[lock horns]], [[cross swords]], [[war]], [[feud]], [[quarrel]]
**2** to be incompatible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disagree]], [[conflict]], [[vary]], [[counter]], [[differ]], [[contradict]], [[diverge]], [[run counter to]]
**3** (of dates or events) to coincide
**4** (of colours or styles) to look ugly or incompatible together: patterned fabrics which combine seemingly clashing shades to great effect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[not go]], [[jar]], [[not match]]
**5** to make a loud harsh sound, esp. by striking together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crash]], [[bang]], [[rattle]], [[jar]], [[clatter]], [[jangle]], [[clang]], clank
 *n*
**6** a [[collision]] or [[conflict]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conflict]], [[fight]], [[brush]], [[confrontation]], [[collision]], [[showdown]] (*informal*), [[boilover]] (*Austral*)
**7** a loud harsh noise
