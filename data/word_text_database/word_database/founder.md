**founder** *vb*
**1** to [[break down]] or [[fail]]: his negotiations have foundered on economic grounds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fail]], [[collapse]], [[break down]], [[fall through]], [[be unsuccessful]], [[come unstuck]], [[miscarry]], [[misfire]]
**2** (of a ship) to [[sink]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sink]], [[go down]], [[be lost]], [[submerge]], [[capsize]], [[go to the bottom]]
**3** to [[sink]] into or become stuck in soft ground
**4** (of a horse) to stumble or go lame
