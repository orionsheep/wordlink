**rear**^2 *vb*
**1** to care for and [[educate]] (children) until maturity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring up]], [[raise]], [[educate]], [[train]], [[foster]], [[nurture]]
**2** to [[breed]] (animals) or grow (plants)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[breed]], [[keep]]
**3** (of a horse) to lift the front legs in the air and stand nearly upright
**4** to place or lift (something) upright
