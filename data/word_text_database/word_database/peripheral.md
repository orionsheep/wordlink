**peripheral** (per-**if**-er-al) *adj*
**1** not relating to the most important part of something; [[incidental]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secondary]], [[minor]], [[marginal]], [[irrelevant]], [[unimportant]], [[incidental]], [[inessential]]
**2** of or relating to a periphery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outermost]], [[outside]], [[external]], [[outer]], exterior
 *n*
**3** (*computers*) any device, such as a printer, that can be connected to a computer
