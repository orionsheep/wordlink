**bet** *n*
**1** the act of staking a sum of money or other [[stake]] on the outcome of an event
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gamble]], [[risk]], [[stake]], [[venture]], [[speculation]], [[flutter]] (*informal*), [[punt]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[wager]]
**2** the [[stake]] risked
**3** a course of action: your best bet is to go by train
**4** (*informal*) an opinion: my bet is that you've been up to no good
 *vb* **betting**, **bet*** or ***betted**
**5** to make or place a bet with (someone)
**6** to [[stake]] (money, etc.) in a bet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gamble]], [[chance]], [[stake]], [[venture]], [[hazard]], [[speculate]], [[wager]], [[risk money]]
**7** (*informal*) to predict (a certain outcome): I bet she doesn't turn up
**8** **you bet** (*informal*) of course
