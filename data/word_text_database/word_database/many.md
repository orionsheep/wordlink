**many** *adj*
**1** a large number of; [[numerous]]: many times; many people think the government is incompetent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[numerous]], [[various]], [[countless]], [[abundant]], [[myriad]], [[innumerable]], [[manifold]] (*formal*), umpteen (*informal*)
 *pron*
**2** a number of people or things, esp. a large one: many are seated already; have as many as you want
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[a lot]], [[lots]] (*informal*), [[plenty]], [[scores]], [[heaps]] (*informal*)
**3** **many a** each of a considerable number of: many a man
 *n*
**4** **the many** the majority of mankind, esp. ordinary people
