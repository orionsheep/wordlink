**shelf** *n*, *pl* **shelves**
**1** a board fixed horizontally against a wall or in a cupboard, for holding things
**2** a projecting layer of ice or rock on land or in the sea
**3** **off the shelf** (of products in shops) sold as standard
**4** **on the shelf** put aside or abandoned; used esp. of unmarried women considered to be past the age of marriage
