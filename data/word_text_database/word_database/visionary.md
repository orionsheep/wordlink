**visionary** *adj*
**1** showing foresight: a visionary leader
**2** [[idealistic]] but [[impractical]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idealistic]], [[romantic]], [[unrealistic]], [[utopian]], [[speculative]], [[impractical]], [[unworkable]], [[quixotic]]
**3** given to having visions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prophetic]], [[mystical]], [[predictive]], [[oracular]], [[sibylline]]
**4** of or like visions
 *n*, *pl* **-naries**
**5** a visionary person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idealist]], [[romantic]], [[dreamer]], [[daydreamer]]
