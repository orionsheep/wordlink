◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appoint]], [[name]], [[commission]], [[select]], [[invest]], [[nominate]], [[anoint]], [[consecrate]]
**2** (*formal*) to decree or [[order]] with authority
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[order]], [[will]], [[rule]], [[demand]], [[require]], [[direct]], [[command]], [[dictate]]
 **[[ordainment]]** *n*
