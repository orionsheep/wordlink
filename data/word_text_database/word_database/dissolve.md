**dissolve** *vb* **-solving**, **-solved**
**1** to become or cause to become liquid; [[melt]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[melt]], [[soften]], [[thaw]], [[liquefy]], [[deliquesce]]
**2** to officially [[break up]] (an organization or institution)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[end]], [[suspend]], [[break up]], [[wind up]], [[terminate]], [[discontinue]], [[dismantle]], [[disband]]
**3** to formally [[end]]: the campaign started as soon as Parliament was dissolved last month
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[end]], [[suspend]], [[break up]], [[wind up]], [[terminate]], [[discontinue]], [[dismantle]], [[disband]]
**4** to collapse emotionally: she dissolved in loud tears
**5** (*films*, *television*) to fade out one scene and replace with another to make two scenes merge imperceptibly
