**bristle** *n*
**1** any short stiff [[hair]], such as on a pig's back
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hair]], [[spine]], [[thorn]], [[whisker]], [[barb]], [[stubble]], [[prickle]]
**2** something resembling these hairs: toothbrush bristle
 *vb* **-tling**, **-tled**
**3** to [[stand up]] or cause to [[stand up]] like bristles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stand up]], [[rise]], [[stand on end]]
**4** to show anger or indignation: she bristled at the suggestion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be angry]], [[rage]], [[seethe]], [[flare up]], [[bridle]], [[see red]]
**5** to be thickly covered or set: the hedges bristled with blossom
 **[[bristly]]** *adj*
