**cooperative** * or ***co-operative** *adj*
**1** willing to cooperate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[helpful]], [[obliging]], [[accommodating]], [[supportive]], [[responsive]], [[onside]] (*informal*)
**2** (of an enterprise or farm) owned and managed collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shared]], [[joint]], [[combined]], [[collective]], collaborative
 *n*
**3** a cooperative organization
