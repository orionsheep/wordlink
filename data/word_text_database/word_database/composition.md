**composition** *n*
**1** the act of [[putting together]] or composing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[production]], [[creation]], [[making]], [[fashioning]], [[formation]], [[putting together]], [[compilation]], [[formulation]]
**2** something composed
**3** the things or parts which make up a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[structure]], [[make-up]], [[organization]], [[arrangement]], [[formation]], [[layout]], [[configuration]]
**4** a [[work]] of music, art, or literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creation]], [[work]], [[piece]], [[production]], [[opus]], [[masterpiece]]
**5** the harmonious [[arrangement]] of the parts of a [[work]] of art
**6** a written [[exercise]]; an [[essay]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[essay]], [[exercise]], [[treatise]], [[literary work]]
**7** (*printing*) the act or technique of setting up type
