**main** *adj*
**1** [[chief]] or [[principal]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chief]], [[leading]], [[head]], [[central]], [[essential]], [[primary]], [[principal]], foremost
 *n*
**2** a [[principal]] [[pipe]] or [[line]] in a system used to distribute water, electricity, or gas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pipeline]], [[channel]], [[pipe]], [[conduit]], [[duct]]
**3** **mains** the main distribution network for water, gas, or electricity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cable]], [[line]], [[electricity supply]], [[mains supply]]
**4** great strength or force: with might and main
**5** (*literary*) the open ocean
**6** **[[in the main]]** on the whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[in the main]]**: on the whole, generally, mainly, mostly, in general, for the most part
