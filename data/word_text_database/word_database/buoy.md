**buoy** *n*
**1** a brightly coloured floating object anchored to the sea bed for marking moorings, navigable channels, or obstructions in the water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[float]], [[guide]], [[signal]], [[marker]], beacon
 *vb*
**2** (foll. by *up*) to prevent from sinking: the life belt buoyed him up
**3** to raise the spirits of; hearten: exports are on the increase, buoyed by a weak dollar
**4** (*naut*) to mark (a channel or obstruction) with a buoy or buoys
