**haunt** *vb*
**1** to visit (a person or place) in the form of a ghost
**2** to remain in the memory or thoughts of: it was a belief which haunted her
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plague]], [[trouble]], [[obsess]], [[torment]], [[possess]], [[stay with]], [[recur]], [[prey on]]
**3** to visit (a place) frequently
 *n*
**4** a place visited frequently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting place]], [[hangout]] (*informal*), [[rendezvous]], [[stamping ground]]
