**spike** *n*
**1** a sharp-pointed metal object: a high fence with iron spikes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[stake]], [[spine]], [[barb]], [[prong]]
**2** anything long and pointed: a hedgehog bristling with spikes
**3** a long metal nail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]], [[stake]], [[spine]], [[barb]], [[prong]]
**4** **spikes** sports shoes with metal spikes on the soles for greater grip
 *vb* **spiking**, **spiked**
**5** to secure or supply (something) with spikes: spiked shoes
**6** to drive a spike or spikes into
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[impale]], [[spit]], [[spear]], [[stick]]
**7** to add alcohol to (a drink)
 **[[spiky]]** *adj*
