**contest** *n*
**1** a [[game]] or [[match]] in which people or teams compete
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[competition]], [[game]], [[match]], [[trial]], [[tournament]]
**2** a [[struggle]] for power or control
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[struggle]], [[fight]], [[battle]], [[conflict]], [[dispute]], [[controversy]], combat
 *vb*
**3** to [[dispute]]: he has said he will not contest the verdict
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[oppose]], [[question]], [[challenge]], [[argue]], [[debate]], [[dispute]], [[object to]], call in *or* into [[question]]
**4** to [[take part in]] (a contest or [[struggle]] for power): all parties which meet the legal requirements will be allowed to contest the election
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compete in]], [[take part in]], [[fight in]], [[go in for]], [[contend for]], [[vie in]]
 **[[contestable]]** *adj*
