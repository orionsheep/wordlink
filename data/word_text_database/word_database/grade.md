**grade** *n*
**1** a place on a scale of quality, [[rank]], or size
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[class]]
**2** a [[mark]] or rating indicating a student's [[level]] of achievement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mark]], [[degree]] (*archaic*)
**3** a [[rank]] or [[level]] of importance in a company or organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[level]], [[rank]], [[group]], [[class]], [[stage]], [[category]], [[echelon]]
**4** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **S African*) a [[class]] or year in a school
**5** **make the grade** (*informal*) to be successful by reaching a required standard
 *vb* **grading**, **graded**
**6** to arrange according to quality or [[rank]]: passes are graded from A down to E
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[classify]], [[rate]], [[order]], [[class]], [[group]], [[sort]], [[range]], [[rank]]
**7** to give a grade to: students are graded by teachers based entirely on their coursework
