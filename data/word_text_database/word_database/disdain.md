**disdain** *n*
**1** a feeling of superiority and dislike; [[contempt]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contempt]], [[scorn]], [[arrogance]], [[derision]], [[haughtiness]], superciliousness
 *vb*
**2** to refuse or [[reject]] with disdain: he disdained domestic conventions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scorn]], [[reject]], [[slight]], [[disregard]], [[spurn]], [[deride]], [[look down on]], [[sneer at]]
 **[[disdainful]]** *adj* 
 **[[disdainfully]]** *adv*
