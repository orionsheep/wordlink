**buffer** *n*
**1** one of a pair of spring-loaded steel pads at the ends of railway vehicles and railway tracks that reduces shock on impact
**2** a person or thing that lessens shock or protects from damaging impact, circumstances, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[safeguard]], [[screen]], [[shield]], [[cushion]], [[intermediary]], [[bulwark]]
**3** (*chem*)
**A** a substance added to a solution to resist changes in its acidity or alkalinity
 **B** Also called: **buffer solution** a solution containing such a substance
**4** (*computers*) a memory device for temporarily storing data
 *vb*
**5** to [[cushion]]; provide a buffer for
**6** (*computers*) to store (data) temporarily in a buffer
