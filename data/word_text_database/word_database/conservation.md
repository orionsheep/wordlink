**conservation** *n*
**1** [[protection]] and careful management of the environment and natural resources
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[preservation]], [[saving]], [[protection]], [[maintenance]], [[safeguarding]], [[upkeep]], [[guardianship]], [[safekeeping]]
**2** [[protection]] from change, loss, or injury
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[economy]], [[saving]], [[thrift]], [[husbandry]]
**3** (*physics*) the principle that the quantity of a specified aspect of a system, such as momentum or charge, remains constant
 **[[conservationist]]** *n*
