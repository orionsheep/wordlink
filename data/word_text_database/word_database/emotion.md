**emotion** *n*
**1** any strong [[feeling]], such as joy or fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[spirit]], [[soul]], [[passion]], [[excitement]], [[sensation]], [[sentiment]], [[fervour]]
**2** the part of a person's character based on feelings rather than thought: the conflict between emotion and logic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instinct]], [[sentiment]], [[sensibility]], [[intuition]], [[tenderness]], [[gut feeling]], [[soft-heartedness]]
