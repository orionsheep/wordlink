**hurt** *vb* **hurting**, **hurt**
**1** to cause physical or mental injury to: is she badly hurt?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[injure]], [[damage]], [[wound]], [[cut]], [[bruise]], [[scrape]], [[impair]]
**2** to cause someone to feel [[pain]]: my head hurt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ache]], [[be sore]], [[be painful]], [[burn]], [[smart]], [[sting]], [[throb]], [[be tender]]
**3** (*informal*) to feel [[pain]]: she was hurting
 *n*
**4** physical or mental [[pain]] or [[suffering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distress]], [[suffering]], [[pain]], [[grief]], [[misery]], [[sorrow]], [[heartache]], wretchedness
 *adj*
**5** [[injured]] or pained: his hurt head; a hurt expression
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[injured]], [[wounded]], [[damaged]], [[harmed]], [[cut]], [[bruised]], [[scarred]]
 **[[hurtful]]** *adj*
