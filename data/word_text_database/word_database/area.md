**area** *n*
**1** a [[section]], [[part]], or region
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[section]], [[sector]], [[portion]]
**2** a [[part]] having a specified function: reception area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[section]], [[sector]], [[portion]]
**3** the size of a two-dimensional surface
**4** a subject [[field]]: the area of literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[realm]], [[part]], [[department]] (*informal*), [[field]], [[province]], [[sphere]], [[domain]]
**5** a sunken area giving access to a basement
**6** any flat, curved, or irregular expanse of a surface
**7** range or scope
