**costume** *n*
**1** a style of dressing, including all the clothes and accessories, typical of a particular country or period
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outfit]], [[dress]], [[clothing]], [[uniform]], [[ensemble]], [[livery]], [[apparel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[attire]]
**2** the clothes worn by an actor or performer: a jester's costume
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outfit]], [[dress]], [[clothing]], [[uniform]], [[ensemble]], [[livery]], [[apparel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[attire]]
 *vb* **-tuming**, **-tumed**
**3** to provide with a costume: she was [[costumed]] by many of the great Hollywood designers
 **[[costumed]]** *adj*
