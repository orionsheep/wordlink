**nothing** *pron*
**1** not anything: I felt nothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nought]], [[zero]], [[nil]], [[not a thing]], [[zilch]] (*slang*)
**2** a matter of no importance: don't worry, it's nothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[a trifle]]
**3** absence of meaning, value, or worth: the industry shrank to almost nothing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[void]], [[emptiness]], [[nothingness]], [[nullity]]
**4** the figure 0
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nought]], [[zero]], [[nil]], [[not a thing]], [[zilch]] (*slang*)
**5** **have *or* be nothing to do with** to have no connection with
**6** **nothing but** not something other than; only
**7** **nothing doing** (*informal*) an expression of dismissal or refusal
**8** **nothing less than** downright: nothing less than complete withdrawal
**9** **think nothing of something** to regard something as easy or natural
 *adv*
**10** not at all: he looked nothing like his brother
 *n*
**11** (*informal*) a person or thing of no importance or significance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nobody]], [[cipher]], [[nonentity]]
