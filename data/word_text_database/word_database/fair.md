**fair**^2 *n*
**1** a travelling entertainment with sideshows, rides, and amusements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carnival]], [[fête]], [[gala]], [[bazaar]]
**2** an [[exhibition]] of goods produced by a particular industry to promote business: the Frankfurt book fair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exhibition]], [[show]], [[festival]], [[mart]]
