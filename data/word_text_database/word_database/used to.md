**used to** *adj*
**1** [[accustomed to]]: I am used to being a medical guinea pig
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accustomed to]], familiar with
 *vb*
**2** used as an auxiliary to express habitual or accustomed actions or states taking place in the past but not continuing to be the case in the present: he used to vanish into his studio for days
