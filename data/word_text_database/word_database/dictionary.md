**1**

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wordbook]], [[vocabulary]], [[glossary]], [[lexicon]]

**2** a reference book listing terms and giving information about a particular subject
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wordbook]], [[vocabulary]], [[glossary]], [[lexicon]]
