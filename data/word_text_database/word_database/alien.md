**alien** *adj*
**1** [[foreign]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[foreign]], [[strange]], [[imported]], [[unknown]], [[exotic]], [[unfamiliar]]
**2** from another world
**3** **alien to** repugnant or opposed to: these methods are alien to the world of politics
 *n*
**4** a person who is a citizen of a country other than the one in which he or she lives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[foreigner]], [[incomer]], [[immigrant]], [[stranger]], [[outsider]], [[newcomer]], [[asylum seeker]]
**5** a being from another world
**6** a person who does not seem to fit in with his or her environment
