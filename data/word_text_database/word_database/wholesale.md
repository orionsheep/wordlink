**wholesale** *n*
**1** the business of selling goods in large quantities and at lower prices to retailers for resale
 *adj*
**2** relating to such business: wholesale prices
**3** [[extensive]] or indiscriminate: the wholesale destruction of forests
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extensive]], [[total]], [[mass]], [[sweeping]], [[broad]], [[comprehensive]], [[wide-ranging]], blanket
 *adv*
**4** by or through the wholesale business: we buy beef wholesale
**5** [[extensively]] or [[indiscriminately]]: buffalo were slaughtered wholesale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extensively]], [[comprehensively]], [[across the board]], [[indiscriminately]]
 **[[wholesaler]]** *n*
