**joker** *n*
**1** a person who jokes a lot
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[comedian]], [[comic]], [[wit]], [[clown]], [[wag]], [[jester]], [[prankster]], [[buffoon]]
**2** (*slang*) a person regarded without respect: waiting for the next jokers to sign up
**3** an extra playing card in a pack, which can replace any other card in some games
**4** (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) a chap
