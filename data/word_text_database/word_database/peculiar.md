**peculiar** *adj*
**1** [[strange]] or [[odd]]: a peculiar idea
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[odd]], [[strange]], [[unusual]], [[bizarre]], [[funny]], [[extraordinary]], [[curious]], [[weird]]
**2** distinct or [[special]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[special]], [[particular]], [[unique]], [[characteristic]]
**3** (foll. by *to*) belonging exclusively (to): a fish peculiar to these waters
