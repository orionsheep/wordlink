**copy** *n*, *pl* **copies**
**1** a thing made to look exactly like another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reproduction]], [[duplicate]], [[replica]], [[imitation]], [[forgery]], [[counterfeit]], [[likeness]], [[facsimile]]
**2** a single specimen of a book, magazine, or record of which there are many others exactly the same: my copy of 'Death on the Nile'
**3** written material for printing
**4** the text of an advertisement
**5** (*journalism*, *informal*) suitable material for an article: disasters are always good copy
 *vb* **copies**, **copying**, **copied**
**6** to make a copy (of)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reproduce]], [[replicate]], [[duplicate]], [[transcribe]], [[counterfeit]]
**7** to act or try to be like another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imitate]], [[act like]], [[emulate]], [[behave like]], [[follow]], [[repeat]], [[mirror]], [[ape]]
