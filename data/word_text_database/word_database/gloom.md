**gloom** *n*
**1** [[depression]] or [[melancholy]]: all doom and gloom
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depression]], [[sorrow]], [[woe]], [[melancholy]], [[unhappiness]], [[despondency]], [[dejection]], [[low spirits]]
**2** partial or total [[darkness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[darkness]], [[dark]], [[shadow]], [[shade]], [[twilight]], [[dusk]] (*poetic*), [[obscurity]], [[blackness]]
