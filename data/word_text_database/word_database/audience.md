**audience** *n*
**1** a group of [[spectators]] or [[listeners]] at a concert or play
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spectators]], [[company]], [[crowd]], [[gathering]], [[gallery]], [[assembly]], [[viewers]], [[listeners]]
**2** the people reached by a book, film, or radio or television programme
**3** a formal [[interview]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[interview]], [[meeting]], [[hearing]], [[exchange]], [[reception]], [[consultation]]
