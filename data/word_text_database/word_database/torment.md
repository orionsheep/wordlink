**torment** *vb*
**1** to cause (someone) great [[pain]] or [[suffering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[torture]], [[distress]], [[rack]], [[crucify]]
**2** to [[tease]] or [[pester]] (a person or animal) in an annoying or cruel way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tease]], [[annoy]], [[bother]], [[irritate]], [[harass]], [[hassle]] (*informal*), [[pester]], vex
 *n*
**3** physical or mental [[pain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suffering]], [[distress]], [[misery]], [[pain]], [[hell]] (*informal*), [[torture]], [[agony]], [[anguish]]
**4** a source of [[pain]] or [[suffering]]
 **[[tormentor]]** *n*
