**alone** *adj*
**1** without anyone or anything else
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solitary]], [[isolated]], [[separate]], [[apart]], [[by yourself]], [[unaccompanied]], on your tod (*slang*)
 *adv*
**2** without anyone or anything else
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solely]], [[only]], [[individually]], [[singly]], [[exclusively]], [[uniquely]]
**3** **leave someone *or* something alone** to refrain from annoying someone or interfering with something
**4** **let alone** not to mention: it looked inconceivable that he could run again, let alone be elected
