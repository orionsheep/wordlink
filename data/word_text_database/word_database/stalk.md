**stalk**^2 *vb*
**1** to [[follow]] (an animal or person) quietly and secretly in order to catch or kill them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pursue]], [[follow]], [[track]], [[hunt]], [[shadow]], [[haunt]]
**2** to [[pursue]] persistently and, sometimes, attack (a person with whom one is obsessed)
**3** to spread over (a place) in a menacing way: danger stalked the streets
**4** to walk in an angry, arrogant, or stiff way
 **[[stalker]]** *n*
