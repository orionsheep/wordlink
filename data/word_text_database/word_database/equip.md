**equip** *vb* **equipping**, **equipped**
**1** to [[provide]] with supplies, components, etc.: the car comes equipped with a catalytic converter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supply]], [[provide]], [[stock]], [[arm]], [[array]], [[furnish]], [[fit out]], [[kit out]]
**2** to [[provide]] with abilities, understanding, etc.: stress is something we are all equipped to cope with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prepare]], [[qualify]], [[educate]], [[get ready]]
