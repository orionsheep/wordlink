**designate** (**dez**-zig-nate) *vb* **-nating**, **-nated**
**1** to give a [[name]] to or describe as: vessels sunk during battle are designated as war graves
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[name]], [[call]], [[term]], [[style]], [[label]], [[entitle]], [[dub]]
**2** to [[select]] (someone) for an office or duty; [[appoint]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[appoint]], [[name]], [[choose]], [[commission]], [[select]], [[elect]], [[delegate]], nominate
 *adj*
**3** appointed, but not yet in office: a Prime Minister designate
