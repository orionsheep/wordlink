**infect** *vb*
**1** to [[contaminate]] (a person or thing) with a germ or virus or its consequent disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contaminate]]
**2** to [[taint]] or [[contaminate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pollute]], [[poison]], [[corrupt]], [[contaminate]], [[taint]], [[defile]]
**3** to [[affect]] with an opinion or feeling as if by contagion: even she was infected by the excitement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affect]], [[move]], [[upset]], [[overcome]], [[stir]], [[disturb]]
