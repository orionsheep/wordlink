**absorb** *vb*
**1** to [[soak up]] a liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soak up]], [[suck up]], [[receive]], [[digest]], [[imbibe]]
**2** to [[engage]] the interest of someone
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[engross]], [[involve]], [[engage]], [[fascinate]], [[rivet]], [[captivate]]
**3** to [[receive]] the force of an impact
**4** (*physics*) to take in radiant energy and retain it
**5** to take in or incorporate: the country absorbed almost one million refugees
 **[[absorbent]]** *adj* 
 **[[absorbency]]** *n* 
 **[[absorbing]]** *adj*
