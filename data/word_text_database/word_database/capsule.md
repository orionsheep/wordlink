**capsule** *n*
**1** a soluble gelatine [[case]] containing a dose of medicine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pill]], [[tablet]], [[lozenge]]
**2** (*botany*) a plant's [[seed case]] that opens when ripe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pod]], [[case]], [[shell]], [[vessel]], [[sheath]], [[receptacle]], [[seed case]]
**3** (*anatomy*) a membrane or sac surrounding an organ or part
 *adj*
**4** very concise: capsule courses
