**memorandum** *n*, *pl* **-dums*** or ***-da**
**1** a [[note]] sent by one person or department to another within a business organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[note]], [[minute]], [[message]], [[communication]], [[reminder]], [[memo]], [[jotting]], email *or* e-mail
**2** a [[note]] of things to be remembered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[note]], [[minute]], [[message]], [[communication]], [[reminder]], [[memo]], [[jotting]], email *or* e-mail
**3** (*law*) a short written summary of the terms of a transaction
