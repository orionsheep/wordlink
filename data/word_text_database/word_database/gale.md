**gale** *n*
**1** a strong wind, specifically one of force 8 on the Beaufort scale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[storm]], [[hurricane]], [[tornado]], [[cyclone]], [[blast]], [[typhoon]], [[tempest]] (*literary*), [[squall]]
**2** **gales** a loud [[outburst]]: gales of laughter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outburst]], [[scream]], [[roar]], [[fit]], [[storm]], [[shout]], [[burst]], [[explosion]]
