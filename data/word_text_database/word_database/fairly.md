**fairly** *adv*
**1** to a moderate degree or extent: in the Philippines labour is fairly cheap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[moderately]], [[rather]], [[quite]], [[somewhat]], [[reasonably]], [[adequately]], [[pretty well]], [[tolerably]]
**2** to a great degree or extent: the folder fairly bulged with documents
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[positively]], [[really]], [[simply]], [[absolutely]]
**3** as deserved: the shares are fairly valued
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equitably]], [[objectively]], [[legitimately]], [[honestly]], [[justly]], [[lawfully]], [[without prejudice]], [[dispassionately]]
