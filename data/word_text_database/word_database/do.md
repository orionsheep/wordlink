**do** *vb* **does**, **doing**, **did**, **done**
**1** to [[perform]] or [[complete]] (a deed or action): we do a fair amount of entertaining
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perform]], [[achieve]], [[carry out]], [[complete]], [[accomplish]], [[execute]], [[pull off]]
**2** to [[be adequate]]: it's not what I wanted but it will have to do
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be adequate]], [[be sufficient]], [[satisfy]], [[suffice]], [[pass muster]], [[cut the mustard]], [[meet requirements]]
**3** to provide: this hotel only does bed and breakfast
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perform]], [[achieve]], [[carry out]], [[complete]], [[accomplish]], [[execute]], [[pull off]]
**4** to make tidy or elegant: he watched her do her hair
**5** to improve: that style does nothing for you
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perform]], [[achieve]], [[carry out]], [[complete]], [[accomplish]], [[execute]], [[pull off]]
**6** to find an answer to (a problem or puzzle)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solve]], [[work out]], [[resolve]], [[figure out]], [[decode]], [[decipher]], [[puzzle out]]
**7** to conduct oneself: do as you want
**8** to cause or produce: herbal teas have active ingredients that can do good
**9** to give or grant: do me a favour
**10** to work at as a course of study or a job
**11** to mimic
**12** to [[achieve]] a particular speed, amount, or rate: this computer system can do 40 different cross checks; this car will do 120 mph
