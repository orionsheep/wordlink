**erupt** *vb*
**1** (of a volcano) to throw out molten lava, ash, and steam in a sudden and violent way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explode]], [[blow up]], [[emit lava]]
**2** to burst forth suddenly and violently: riots erupted across the country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gush]], [[burst out]], [[pour forth]], [[belch forth]], spew forth *or* out
**3** (of a group of people) to suddenly become angry and aggressive: the meeting erupted in fury
**4** (of a blemish) to [[appear]] on the skin
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[break out]], [[appear]], [[flare up]]
 **[[eruptive]]** *adj* 
 **[[eruption]]** *n*
