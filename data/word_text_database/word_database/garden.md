**garden** *n*
**1** an area of land usually next to a house, for growing flowers, fruit, or vegetables. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**horticultural**
**2** Also: **gardens** a cultivated area of land open to the public: Kensington Gardens
**3** **lead someone up the garden path** (*informal*) to mislead or deceive someone
 *vb*
**4** to work in or take care of a garden
