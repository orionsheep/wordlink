**import** *vb*
**1** to [[bring in]] (goods) from another country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bring in]], [[buy in]], [[ship in]], [[introduce]]
**2** (*formal*) to signify; mean: to import doom
 *n*
**3** something imported
**4** (*formal*) importance: his new work is of great import
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[significance]], [[concern]], [[value]], [[weight]], [[consequence]], [[substance]], [[moment]], [[magnitude]]
**5** [[meaning]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meaning]], [[implication]], [[significance]], [[sense]], [[intention]], [[substance]], [[drift]], [[thrust]]
**6** (*informal*) a sportsperson who is not native to the area where they play
 **[[importer]]** *n* 
 **[[importation]]** *n*
