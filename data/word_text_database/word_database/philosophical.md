**philosophical** * or ***philosophic** *adj*
**1** of or relating to philosophy or philosophers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theoretical]], [[abstract]], [[wise]], [[rational]], [[logical]], [[thoughtful]], [[sagacious]]
**2** [[calm]] and [[stoical]] in the face of difficulties or disappointments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stoical]], [[calm]], [[composed]], [[cool]], [[collected]], [[serene]], [[tranquil]], [[unruffled]]
 **[[philosophically]]** *adv*
