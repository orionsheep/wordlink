**tar** *n*
**1** a dark sticky substance obtained by distilling organic matter such as coal, wood, or peat
 *vb* **tarring**, **tarred**
**2** to coat with tar
**3** **tar and feather** to cover (someone) with tar and feathers as a punishment
**4** **tarred with the same brush** having, or regarded as having, the same faults
 **[[tarry]]** *adj*
