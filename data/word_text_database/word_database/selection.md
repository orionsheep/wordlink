**selection** *n*
**1** a selecting or being selected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[choice]], [[choosing]], [[pick]], [[option]], [[preference]]
**2** a thing or number of things that have been selected
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[anthology]], [[collection]], [[medley]], [[choice]]
**3** a range from which something may be selected: a good selection of reasonably priced wines
**4** (*biol*) the process by which certain organisms or individuals are reproduced and survive in [[preference]] to others
