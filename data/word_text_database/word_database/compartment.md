**compartment** *n*
**1** one of the sections into which a railway [[carriage]] is sometimes divided
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[section]], [[carriage]], [[berth]]
**2** a separate [[section]]: filing the information away in some compartment of his mind
**3** a small storage space: the ice-making compartment of the fridge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bay]], [[booth]], [[locker]], [[niche]], [[cubicle]], [[alcove]], [[pigeonhole]], [[cubbyhole]]
