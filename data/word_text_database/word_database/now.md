**now** *adv*
**1** at or for the present time
**2** [[immediately]]: bring it now
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[immediately]], [[promptly]], [[instantly]], [[at once]], [[straight away]]
**3** in these times; [[nowadays]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nowadays]], [[at the moment]]
**4** given the present circumstances: now do you understand why?
**5**
**A** used as a hesitation word: now, I can't really say
 **B** used for emphasis: now listen to this
 **C** used at the end of a command: run along now
**6** **just now**
**A** very recently: he left just now
 **B** very soon: I'm going just now
**7** **now and again *or* then** occasionally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[now and then or again]]**: occasionally, sometimes, from time to time, on and off, intermittently, infrequently, sporadically
**8** **now now!** an exclamation used to tell someone off or to calm someone
 *conj*
**9** Also: **now that** seeing that: now you're here, you can help me
 *n*
**10** the present time: now is the time to go
