**tube** *n*
**1** a long hollow cylindrical object, used for the passage of fluids or as a container
**2** a flexible cylinder of soft metal or plastic closed with a cap, used to hold substances such as toothpaste
**3** (*anatomy*) any hollow cylindrical structure: the Fallopian tubes
**4** **the Tube** (*Brit*, *trademark*) the underground railway system in London
**5** (*slang*, *chiefly* *US*) a television set
 **[[tubeless]]** *adj*
