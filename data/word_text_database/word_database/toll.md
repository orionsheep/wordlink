**toll**^2 *n*
**1** a [[charge]] for the use of certain roads and bridges: the Skye Bridge toll
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[charge]], [[tax]], [[fee]], [[duty]], [[payment]], [[levy]], [[tariff]]
**2** [[loss]] or [[damage]] from a disaster: the annual death toll on the roads is about 4500
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[damage]], [[cost]], [[loss]], [[roll]], [[penalty]], [[sum]], [[number]], [[roster]]
**3** **take a *or* its toll** to have a severe and damaging effect: the continued stress had taken a toll on her health
