**most** *n*
**1** the greatest number or degree: the most I can ever remember being paid
**2** the majority: most of his records are dreadful
**3** **at (the) most** at the maximum: she is fifteen at the most
**4** **make the most of** to use to the best advantage: they made the most of their chances
 *adj*
**5** of or being the majority of a group of things or people or the largest part of something: most people don't share your views
**6** **the most** the superlative of **many**, **much**: he has the most talent
 *adv*
**7** **the most** used to form the superlative of some adjectives and adverbs: the most beautiful places in the world
**8** the superlative of **much**: what do you like most about your job?
**9** very; exceedingly: a most unfortunate accident
