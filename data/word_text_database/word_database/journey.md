**journey** *n*
**1** the process of travelling from one place to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trip]], [[drive]], [[tour]], [[flight]], [[excursion]], [[trek]], [[expedition]], [[voyage]]
**2** the time taken or distance travelled on a journey
 *vb*
**3** to make a journey
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[travel]], [[go]], [[move]], [[tour]], [[progress]], [[proceed]], [[wander]], [[trek]], [[go walkabout]] (*Austral*)
