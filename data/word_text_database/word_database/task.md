**task** *n*
**1** a specific piece of work required to be done
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[duty]], [[assignment]], [[exercise]], [[mission]], [[enterprise]], [[undertaking]], [[chore]]
**2** an unpleasant or difficult [[job]] or [[duty]]
**3** **take to task** to criticize or rebuke
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[take someone to task]]**: criticize, blame, censure, rebuke, reprimand, reproach, scold, tell off (*informal*)
