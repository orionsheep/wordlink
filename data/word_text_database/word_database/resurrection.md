**resurrection** *n*
**1** a [[return]] to life by a dead person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: raising *or* rising from the dead, [[return from the dead]]
**2** [[revival]] or [[renewal]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[revival]], [[restoration]], [[renewal]], [[resurgence]], [[return]], [[renaissance]], [[rebirth]], [[reappearance]]
**3** **the Resurrection** (*Christian theol*)
**A** the rising again of Christ from the tomb three days after his death
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: raising *or* rising from the dead, [[return from the dead]]
 **B** the rising again from the dead of all people at the Last Judgment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: raising *or* rising from the dead, [[return from the dead]]
