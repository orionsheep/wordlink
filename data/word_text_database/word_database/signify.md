**signify** *vb* **-fies**, **-fying**, **-fied**
**1** to [[indicate]] or [[suggest]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indicate]], [[mean]], [[suggest]], [[imply]], [[intimate]], [[be a sign of]], [[denote]], [[connote]], [[flag up]]
**2** to stand as a symbol or sign for: a blue line on the map signified a river
**3** to be important
