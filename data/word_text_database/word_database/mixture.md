**mixture** *n*
**1** something produced by blending or combining other things: top with the cheese and breadcrumb mixture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[composite]], [[compound]]
**2** a combination of different things, such as feelings: he speaks of her with a mixture of loyalty and regret
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blend]], [[mix]], [[variety]], [[fusion]], [[assortment]], [[brew]], [[jumble]], [[medley]]
**3** (*chem*) a substance consisting of two or more substances mixed together without any chemical bonding between them
