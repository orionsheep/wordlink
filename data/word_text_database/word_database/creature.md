**creature** *n*
**1** an [[animal]], bird, or fish
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[living thing]], [[being]], [[animal]], [[beast]], [[brute]]
**2** a [[person]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[person]], [[man]], [[woman]], [[individual]], [[soul]], [[human being]], [[mortal]]
**3** a [[person]] or thing controlled by another
