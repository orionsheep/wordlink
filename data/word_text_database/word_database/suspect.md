**suspect** *vb*
**1** to [[believe]] (someone) to be guilty without having any proof
**2** to think (something) to be false or [[doubtful]]: he suspected her intent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distrust]], [[doubt]], [[mistrust]]
**3** to [[believe]] (something) to be the case; think probable: I suspect he had another reason
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believe]], [[feel]], [[guess]], [[consider]], [[suppose]], speculate
 *n*
**4** a person who is believed guilty of a specified offence
 *adj*
**5** not to be trusted or relied upon: her commitment to the cause has always been suspect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dubious]], [[doubtful]], [[questionable]], [[iffy]] (*informal*), [[shonky]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
