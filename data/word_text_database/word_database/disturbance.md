**disturbance** *n*
**1** an [[interruption]] or [[intrusion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upset]], [[bother]], [[distraction]], [[intrusion]], [[interruption]], [[annoyance]]
**2** an unruly outburst in public
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disorder]], [[fray]], [[brawl]], [[fracas]], [[commotion]], [[rumpus]]
