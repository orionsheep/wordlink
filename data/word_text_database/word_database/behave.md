**behave** *vb* **-having**, **-haved**
**1** to [[act]] or function in a particular way
**2** to conduct oneself in a particular way: the baby behaved very well
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[act]]
**3** to conduct oneself properly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be well-behaved]], [[mind your manners]], [[keep your nose clean]], [[act correctly]], [[conduct yourself properly]]
