**uproar** *n*
**1** a [[commotion]] or disturbance characterized by loud [[noise]] and confusion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commotion]], [[noise]], [[racket]], [[riot]], [[turmoil]], [[mayhem]], [[din]], [[pandemonium]]
**2** angry public criticism or debates: the decision to close the railway led to an uproar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protest]], [[outrage]], [[complaint]], [[objection]], [[fuss]], [[stink]] (*informal*), [[outcry]], [[furore]]
