**harvest** *n*
**1** the [[gathering]] of a ripened [[crop]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harvesting]], [[picking]], [[gathering]], [[collecting]], [[reaping]], [[harvest-time]]
**2** the [[crop]] itself
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crop]], [[yield]], [[year's growth]], [[produce]]
**3** the season for [[gathering]] crops
**4** the product of an effort or action
 *vb*
**5** to [[gather]] (a ripened [[crop]])
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gather]], [[pick]], [[collect]], [[bring in]], [[pluck]], [[reap]]
**6** (*chiefly* *US*) to remove (an organ) from the body for transplantation
