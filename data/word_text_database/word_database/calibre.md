**calibre** * or * (*US*) **caliber** (**kal**-lib-ber) *n*
**1** a person's [[ability]] or [[worth]]: a poet of Wordsworth's calibre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worth]], [[quality]], [[ability]], [[talent]], [[capacity]], [[merit]], [[distinction]], [[stature]]
**2** the [[diameter]] of the [[bore]] of a gun or of a shell or bullet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diameter]], [[bore]], [[gauge]], [[measure]]
