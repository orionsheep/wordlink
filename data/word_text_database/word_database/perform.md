**perform** *vb*
**1** to [[carry out]] (an action): the hospital performs more than a hundred such operations each year
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[do]], [[achieve]], [[carry out]], [[complete]], [[fulfil]], [[accomplish]], [[execute]], [[pull off]]
**2** to [[present]] (a [[play]] or concert): he performed a couple of songs from his new album
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[present]], [[act]] (out), [[stage]], [[play]], [[produce]], [[represent]], [[put on]], [[enact]]
**3** to [[fulfil]]: you have performed the first of two conditions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fulfil]], [[carry out]], [[execute]], [[discharge]]
 **[[performable]]** *adj* 
 **[[performer]]** *n*
