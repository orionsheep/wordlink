**continuation** *n*
**1** the act of [[continuing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[continuing]], [[lasting]], [[carrying on]], [[keeping up]], [[endurance]], [[perpetuation]], [[prolongation]]
**2** a part or thing added, such as a [[sequel]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[addition]], [[extension]], [[supplement]], [[sequel]], [[resumption]], [[postscript]]
**3** a renewal of an interrupted action or process
