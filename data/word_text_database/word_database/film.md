**film** *n*
**1**
**A** a sequence of images projected onto a screen, creating the illusion of movement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[movie]], [[picture]], [[flick]] (*slang*), [[motion picture]], [[MPEG]], MP4
 **B** a form of entertainment in such a sequence of images
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: cinema, the movies <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**cinematic**
**2** a thin flexible strip of cellulose coated with a photographic emulsion, used to make negatives and slides
**3** a thin [[coating]], [[covering]], or [[layer]]: a fine film of dust covered the floor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[layer]], [[covering]], [[cover]], [[skin]], [[coating]], [[dusting]], [[tissue]], [[membrane]]
**4** a thin sheet of any material, as of plastic for packaging
 *vb*
**5**
**A** to [[photograph]] with a [[movie]] or [[video]] camera
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[photograph]], [[record]], [[shoot]], [[video]], [[videotape]], take
 **B** to make a film of (a screenplay or event)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: adapt for the screen, [[make into a film]]
**6** **film over** to [[cover]] or become covered with a thin [[layer]]
 *adj*
**7** of or relating to films or the cinema
