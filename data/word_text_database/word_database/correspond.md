**1** to [[be consistent]] or compatible (with)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be consistent]], [[match]], [[agree]], [[accord]], [[fit]], [[square]], [[tally]], [[conform]]
**2** to be similar (to)
**3** to [[communicate]] (with) by letter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communicate]], [[write]], [[keep in touch]], [[exchange letters]], email *or* e-mail, [[text]]
 **[[corresponding]]** *adj* 
 **[[correspondingly]]** *adv*
