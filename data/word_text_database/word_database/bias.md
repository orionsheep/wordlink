**bias** *n*
**1** mental [[tendency]], esp. [[prejudice]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prejudice]], [[leaning]], [[tendency]], [[inclination]], [[favouritism]], [[partiality]]
**2** a diagonal cut across the weave of a fabric
**3** (*bowls*) a bulge or [[weight]] inside one side of a bowl that causes it to roll in a curve
 *vb* **-asing**, **-ased*** or ***-assing**, **-assed**
**4** to cause to have a bias; [[prejudice]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[influence]], [[colour]], [[weight]], [[prejudice]], [[distort]], [[sway]], [[warp]], [[slant]]
 **[[biased]]** * or ***[[biassed]]** *adj*
