**ritual** *n*
**1** a religious or other [[ceremony]] involving a series of fixed actions performed in a certain order
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[rite]], [[observance]]
**2** these ceremonies collectively: people need ritual
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[rite]], [[observance]]
**3** a regular repeated action or behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremony]], [[rite]], [[observance]]
**4** stereotyped activity or behaviour
 *adj*
**5** of or like rituals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ceremonial]], [[conventional]], [[routine]], [[customary]], [[habitual]]
 **[[ritually]]** *adv*
