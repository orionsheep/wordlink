**coil** *vb*
**1** to [[wind]] or be wound into loops
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wind]], [[twist]], [[curl]], [[loop]], [[spiral]], [[twine]]
**2** to move in a winding course
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curl]], [[wind]], [[twist]], [[snake]], [[loop]], [[twine]], wreathe
 *n*
**3** something wound in a connected series of loops
**4** a single [[loop]] of such a series
**5** a contraceptive device in the shape of a coil, inserted in the womb
**6** an electrical conductor wound into a [[spiral]], to provide inductance
