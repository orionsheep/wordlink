**flower** *n*
**1** the part of a plant that is, usually, brightly coloured, and quickly fades, producing seeds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bloom]], [[blossom]], [[efflorescence]]
**2** a plant grown for its colourful flowers. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**floral**
**3** the [[best]] or [[finest]] part: in the flower of her youth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[elite]], [[best]], [[prime]], [[finest]], [[pick]], [[choice]], [[cream]], [[the crème de la crème]]
**4** **in flower** with flowers [[open]]
 *vb*
**5** to produce flowers; [[bloom]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bloom]], [[open]], [[mature]], [[flourish]], [[unfold]], [[blossom]]
**6** to reach full growth or maturity: liberty only flowers in times of peace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blossom]], [[grow]], [[develop]], [[progress]], [[mature]], [[thrive]], [[flourish]], [[bloom]]
