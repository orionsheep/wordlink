**measurement** *n*
**1** the act or process of measuring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calculation]], [[assessment]], [[evaluation]], [[valuation]], [[computation]], [[calibration]], [[mensuration]]
**2** an amount, extent, or size determined by measuring
**3** a system or unit used for measuring: the kilometre is the standard measurement of distance in most countries
**4** **measurements** the size of a person's waist, chest, hips, etc., used when buying clothes
