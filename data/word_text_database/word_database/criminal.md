◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unlawful]], [[illicit]], [[lawless]], [[wrong]], [[illegal]], [[corrupt]], [[crooked]] (*informal*), [[immoral]]
**3** (*informal*) [[senseless]] or [[disgraceful]]: a criminal waste of money
