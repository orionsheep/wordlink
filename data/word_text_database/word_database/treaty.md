**treaty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a formal written [[agreement]] between two or more states, such as an [[alliance]] or trade arrangement: the Treaty of Rome established the Common Market
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[pact]], [[contract]], [[alliance]], [[convention]], [[compact]], [[covenant]], [[entente]]
**2** an [[agreement]] between two parties concerning the purchase of property
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[pact]], [[contract]], [[alliance]], [[convention]], [[compact]], [[covenant]], [[entente]]
