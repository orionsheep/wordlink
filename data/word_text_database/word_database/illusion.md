**illusion** *n*
**1** a false [[appearance]] or deceptive [[impression]] of reality: her upswept hair gave the illusion of above average height
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[false impression]], [[appearance]], [[impression]], [[deception]], [[fallacy]]
**2** a false or misleading idea or belief: we may suffer from the illusion that we are special
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[delusion]], [[misconception]], [[misapprehension]], [[fancy]], [[fallacy]], [[false impression]], [[false belief]]
