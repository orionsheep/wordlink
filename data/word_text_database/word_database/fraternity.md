**fraternity** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a body of people united in interests, aims, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brotherhood]], [[club]], [[union]], [[society]], [[league]], [[association]]
**2** friendship between groups of people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[companionship]], [[fellowship]], [[brotherhood]], [[kinship]], [[camaraderie]]
**3** (*US** & **Canad*) a [[society]] of male students
