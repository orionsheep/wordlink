**hour** *n*
**1** a period of time equal to 60 minutes; ^1⁄24 of a day
**2** any of the points on the face of a clock or watch that indicate intervals of 60 minutes: in my hurry I mistook the hour
**3** the time of day
**4** the time allowed for, or used for, something: a three-and-a-half-hour test
**5** the distance covered in an hour: an hour from the heart of Tokyo
**6** a special moment: the decisive hour
