**anticipate** *vb* **-pating**, **-pated**
**1** to [[foresee]] and act in advance of: he anticipated some probing questions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expect]], [[predict]], [[prepare for]], [[hope for]], [[envisage]], [[foresee]], [[bank on]], [[foretell]]
**2** to [[look forward to]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[await]], [[look forward to]], [[count the hours until]]
**3** to make use of (something, such as one's salary) before receiving it
**4** to mention (part of a story) before its proper time
 **[[anticipatory]]** *adj*
