**overlook** *vb*
**1** to fail to notice (something)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[miss]], [[forget]], [[neglect]], [[omit]], [[disregard]], [[pass over]]
**2** to [[disregard]] or [[ignore]] (misbehaviour or a fault): I'm prepared to overlook your failure, but don't do it again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ignore]], [[excuse]], [[forgive]], [[pardon]], [[disregard]], [[condone]], [[turn a blind eye to]], [[wink at]]
**3** to give a view of (something) from above: a cliff overlooking the Atlantic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: look over *or* out on, [[have a view of]]
