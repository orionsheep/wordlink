**liable** *adj*
**1** probable or [[likely]]: weak and liable to give way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[likely]], [[tending]], [[inclined]], [[disposed]], [[prone]], [[apt]]
**2** commonly suffering a condition: he's liable to colds in the chest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vulnerable]], [[subject]], [[exposed]], [[prone]], [[susceptible]], [[open]], [[at risk of]]
**3** legally obliged or [[responsible]]; [[answerable]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[responsible]], [[accountable]], [[answerable]], [[obligated]]
