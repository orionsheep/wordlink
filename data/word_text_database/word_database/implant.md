**implant** *vb*
**1** to [[fix]] firmly in the mind: to implant sound moral principles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instil]], [[infuse]], [[inculcate]]
**2** to plant or embed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insert]], [[fix]], [[graft]]
**3** (*surgery*) to [[graft]] or [[insert]] (a tissue or hormone) into the body
 *n*
**4** anything implanted in the body, such as a tissue [[graft]]
 **[[implantation]]** *n*
