**volume** *n*
**1** the magnitude of the three-dimensional space enclosed within or occupied by something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[capacity]], [[size]], [[mass]], [[extent]], [[proportions]], [[dimensions]], [[bulk]], [[measurements]]
**2** an [[amount]] or [[total]]: the volume of trade between the two countries; the volume of military traffic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[amount]], [[quantity]], [[level]], [[body]], [[total]], [[measure]], [[degree]], [[mass]]
**3** [[loudness]] of [[sound]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loudness]], [[sound]], [[amplification]]
**4** the control on a radio, etc. for adjusting the [[loudness]] of [[sound]]
**5** a [[book]]: a slim volume
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[book]], [[work]], [[title]], [[opus]], [[publication]], [[manual]], [[tome]], [[treatise]]
**6** one of several books that make up a series
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[book]], [[work]], [[title]], [[opus]], [[publication]], [[manual]], [[tome]], [[treatise]]
**7** a set of issues of a magazine over a specified period
