**telegraph** *n*
**1** (formerly) a system by which information could be transmitted over a distance, using electrical signals sent along a cable
 *vb*
**2** (formerly) to send (a message) by telegraph
**3** to give advance notice of (something), esp. unintentionally: the twist in the plot was telegraphed long in advance
**4** (*Canad* *informal*) to cast (a vote) illegally by impersonating a registered voter
 **[[telegraphist]]** *n* 
 **[[telegraphic]]** *adj*
