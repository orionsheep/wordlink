**turn down** *vb*
**1** to reduce (the volume, brightness, or temperature of something): turn the heat down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[turn something down]]**: lower, soften, mute, lessen, muffle, quieten
**2** to reject or refuse: the invitation was turned down
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[turn something down]]**: refuse, decline, reject, spurn, rebuff, repudiate
**3** to fold down (sheets, etc.)
