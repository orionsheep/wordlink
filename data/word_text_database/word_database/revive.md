**revive** *vb* **-viving**, **-vived**
**1** to make or become lively or active again
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[refresh]]
**2** to bring or be brought back to life, consciousness, or strength: revived by a drop of whisky
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[come round]], [[recover]]
**3** (*theatre*) to put on a new production of (an old play)
