**seek** *vb* **seeking**, **sought**
**1** to [[try]] to find by searching: to seek employment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[look for]], [[pursue]], [[search for]], [[be after]], [[hunt]]
**2** to [[try]] to obtain: to seek a diplomatic solution
**3** to [[try]] (to do something): we seek to establish a stable relationship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[try]], [[attempt]], [[aim]], [[strive]], [[endeavour]], [[essay]] (*formal*), [[aspire to]]
