**dummy** *n*, *pl* **-mies**
**1** a large [[model]] that looks like a human being, used for displaying clothes in a shop, as a target, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[model]], [[figure]], [[mannequin]], [[form]], [[manikin]]
**2** a [[copy]] of an object, often lacking some essential feature of the original
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imitation]], [[copy]], [[duplicate]], [[sham]], [[counterfeit]], [[replica]]
**3** (*slang*) a stupid person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fool]], [[idiot]], [[dunce]], [[nitwit]] (*informal*), [[blockhead]], [[dumb-ass]] (*slang*), dorba *or* dorb (*Austral* *slang*), [[mampara]] (*S African* *informal*)
**4** (*bridge*)
**A** the hand exposed on the table by the declarer's partner and played by the declarer
 **B** the declarer's partner
**5** a rubber teat for babies to suck
 *adj*
**6** [[imitation]] or substitute: you can train them with dummy bombs and live ammunition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[imitation]], [[false]], [[fake]], [[artificial]], [[mock]], [[bogus]], [[simulated]], [[sham]]
