**coax** *vb*
**1** to [[persuade]] (someone) gently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[persuade]], [[cajole]], [[talk into]], [[wheedle]], [[sweet-talk]] (*informal*), [[prevail upon]], [[entice]], [[allure]]
**2** to obtain (something) by persistent coaxing
**3** to work on (something) carefully and patiently so as to make it function as desired: I watched him coax the last few drops of beer out of his glass
