**rinse** *vb* **rinsing**, **rinsed**
**1** to remove soap or shampoo from (clothes, dishes, or hair) by washing it out with [[clean]] water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wash]], [[clean]], [[dip]], [[splash]], [[cleanse]], [[bathe]]
**2** to [[wash]] lightly, esp. without using soap
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wash]], [[clean]], [[dip]], [[splash]], [[cleanse]], [[bathe]]
**3** to [[cleanse]] (the mouth) by swirling water or mouthwash in it and then spitting the liquid out
**4** to give a light tint to (hair)
 *n*
**5** the act or an instance of rinsing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wash]], [[dip]], [[splash]], [[bath]]
**6** (*hairdressing*) a liquid to tint hair: a blue rinse
