**win** *vb* **winning**, **won**
**1** to [[achieve]] first place in (a competition or race)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be victorious in]], [[succeed in]], [[prevail in]], [[come first in]], [[be the victor in]]
**2** to [[gain]] (a prize or first place) in a competition or race
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gain]], [[get]], [[land]] (*informal*), [[achieve]], [[earn]], [[secure]], [[obtain]], [[acquire]]
**3** to [[gain]] [[victory]] in (a battle, argument, or struggle)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be victorious in]], [[succeed in]], [[prevail in]], [[come first in]], [[be the victor in]]
**4** to [[gain]] (sympathy, approval, or support)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gain]], [[get]], [[land]] (*informal*), [[achieve]], [[earn]], [[secure]], [[obtain]], [[acquire]]
 *n*
**5** (*informal*) a [[success]], [[victory]], or [[triumph]]: three consecutive wins
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[victory]], [[success]], [[triumph]], conquest
 See also **win over**
