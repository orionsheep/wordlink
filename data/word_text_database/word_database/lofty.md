◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[high]], [[raised]], [[towering]], [[soaring]], [[elevated]]
**2** morally admirable: lofty ideals
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noble]], [[grand]], [[distinguished]], [[renowned]], [[elevated]], [[dignified]], [[illustrious]], [[exalted]]
**3** unpleasantly superior: a lofty contempt
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[haughty]], [[proud]], [[arrogant]], [[patronizing]], [[condescending]], [[disdainful]], [[supercilious]]
 **[[loftily]]** *adv* 
 **[[loftiness]]** *n*
