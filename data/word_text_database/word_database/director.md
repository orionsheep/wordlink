**director** *n*
**1** a person or thing that directs or controls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[controller]], [[head]], [[leader]], [[manager]], [[chief]], [[executive]], [[governor]], [[administrator]], [[sherang]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**2** a member of the governing board of a business, trust, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[controller]], [[head]], [[leader]], [[manager]], [[chief]], [[executive]], [[governor]], [[administrator]], [[sherang]] (*Austral** & **NZ*)
**3** the person responsible for the artistic and technical aspects of the making of a film or television programme
 **[[directorial]]** *adj* 
 **[[directorship]]** *n*
