**stun** *vb* **stunning**, **stunned**
**1** to [[shock]] or [[astonish]] (someone) so that he or she is unable to speak or act
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overcome]], [[shock]], [[confuse]], [[astonish]], [[stagger]], [[bewilder]], [[astound]], [[overpower]]
**2** (of a heavy blow or fall) to make (a person or an animal) unconscious
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[daze]], [[knock out]], [[stupefy]], [[numb]], [[benumb]]
