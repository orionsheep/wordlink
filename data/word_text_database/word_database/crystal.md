**crystal** *n*
**1** a solid with a regular internal structure and symmetrical arrangement of faces
**2** a single grain of a crystalline substance
**3** a very clear and brilliant glass
**4** something made of crystal
**5** crystal glass articles collectively
**6** (*electronics*) a crystalline element used in certain electronic devices, such as a detector or oscillator
 *adj*
**7** bright and clear: the crystal waters of the pool
