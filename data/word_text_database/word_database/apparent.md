**apparent** *adj*
**1** readily seen or understood; [[obvious]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obvious]], [[marked]], [[visible]], [[evident]], [[distinct]], [[manifest]], [[noticeable]], [[unmistakable]]
**2** [[seeming]] as opposed to real: he frowned in apparent bewilderment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seeming]], [[outward]], [[superficial]], [[ostensible]]
 **[[apparently]]** *adv*
