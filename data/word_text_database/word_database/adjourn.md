**adjourn** *vb*
**1** to close a court at the end of a session
**2** to [[postpone]] or be postponed temporarily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[postpone]], [[delay]], [[suspend]], [[interrupt]], [[put off]], [[defer]], [[discontinue]]
**3** (*informal*) to go elsewhere: can we adjourn to the dining room?
 **[[adjournment]]** *n*
