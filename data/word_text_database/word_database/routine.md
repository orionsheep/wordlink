**routine** *n*
**1** a [[usual]] or regular [[method]] of [[procedure]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[procedure]], [[programme]], [[order]], [[practice]], [[method]], [[pattern]], [[custom]]
**2** the boring repetition of tasks: mindless routine
**3** a set sequence of dance steps
**4** (*computers*) a program or part of a program performing a specific function: an input routine
 *adj*
**5** relating to or characteristic of routine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usual]], [[standard]], [[normal]], [[customary]], [[ordinary]], [[typical]], [[everyday]], [[habitual]]
