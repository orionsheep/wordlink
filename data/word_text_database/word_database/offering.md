**offering** *n*
**1** something that is offered
**2** a [[contribution]] to the funds of a religious organization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[contribution]], [[gift]], [[donation]], [[present]], [[subscription]], [[hand-out]]
**3** a [[sacrifice]] to a god
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sacrifice]], [[tribute]], [[libation]], [[burnt offering]]
