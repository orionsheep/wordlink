**vessel** *n*
**1** a [[ship]] or large [[boat]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ship]], [[boat]], [[craft]]
**2** an object used as a [[container]] for liquid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[container]], [[receptacle]], [[can]], [[bowl]], [[tank]], [[pot]], [[drum]], [[barrel]]
**3** (*biol*) a tubular structure in animals and plants that carries body fluids, such as blood and sap
