**blight** *n*
**1** a person or thing that spoils or prevents growth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[curse]], [[suffering]], [[evil]], [[corruption]], [[pollution]], [[plague]] (*informal*), [[hardship]], [[woe]]
**2** any plant [[disease]] characterized by withering and shrivelling without rotting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disease]], [[pest]], [[fungus]], [[mildew]], [[infestation]], [[pestilence]], [[canker]]
**3** a [[fungus]] or insect that causes blight in plants
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disease]], [[pest]], [[fungus]], [[mildew]], [[infestation]], [[pestilence]], [[canker]]
**4** an ugly urban district
 *vb*
**5** to cause to suffer a blight
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frustrate]], [[destroy]], [[ruin]], [[crush]], [[mar]], [[dash]], [[wreck]], [[spoil]], crool *or* cruel (*Austral* *slang*)
**6** to [[frustrate]] or disappoint: blighted love
**7** to [[destroy]]: the event blighted her life
