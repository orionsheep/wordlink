**companion** *n*
**1** a person who associates with or accompanies someone: a travelling companion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[friend]], [[partner]], [[ally]], [[colleague]], [[associate]], [[mate]] (*informal*), [[comrade]], [[accomplice]], [[plus-one]] (*informal*), cobber (*Austral** & **NZ* *old-fashioned*, *informal*)
**2** (esp. formerly) a woman paid to live or travel with another woman
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assistant]], [[aide]], [[escort]], [[attendant]]
**3** a guidebook or handbook
**4** one of a pair
 **[[companionship]]** *n*
