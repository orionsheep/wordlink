**boundary** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** something that indicates the farthest limit, such as of an area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frontier]], [[edge]], [[border]], [[barrier]], [[margin]], [[brink]]
**2** (*cricket*)
**A** the marked limit of the playing area
 **B** a stroke that hits the ball beyond this limit, scoring four or six runs
