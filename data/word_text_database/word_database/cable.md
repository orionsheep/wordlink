**cable** *n*
**1** a strong thick rope of twisted hemp or wire
**2** a bundle of wires covered with plastic or rubber that conducts electricity
**3** a telegram sent abroad by submarine cable or telephone line
**4** Also called: **cable stitch** a knitted design which resembles a twisted rope
**5** short for **cable television**
 *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**6** to send (someone) a message by cable
