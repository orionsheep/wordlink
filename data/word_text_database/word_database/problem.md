**problem** *n*
**1** something or someone that is difficult to deal with
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difficulty]], [[trouble]], [[dispute]], [[plight]], [[obstacle]], [[dilemma]], [[headache]] (*informal*), [[complication]]
**2** a [[puzzle]] or [[question]] set for solving
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[puzzle]], [[question]], [[riddle]], [[enigma]], [[conundrum]], [[poser]]
**3** (*maths*) a statement requiring a solution usually by means of several operations
 *adj*
**4** of a literary work that deals with difficult moral questions: a problem play
**5** difficult to deal with or creating difficulties for others: a problem child
