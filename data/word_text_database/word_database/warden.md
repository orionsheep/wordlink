**warden** *n*
**1** a person who is in charge of a building, such as a youth hostel, and its occupants
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steward]], [[guardian]], [[administrator]], [[superintendent]] (*US*), [[caretaker]], [[curator]], [[custodian]]
**2** a public [[official]] who is responsible for the enforcement of certain regulations: a game warden
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ranger]], [[keeper]], [[guardian]], [[protector]], [[custodian]], [[official]]
**3** the [[chief]] officer in charge of a prison
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[governor]], [[head]], [[leader]], [[director]], [[manager]], [[chief]], [[executive]], [[commander]]
