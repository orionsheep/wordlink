**flash** *n*
**1** a sudden short [[blaze]] of intense light or flame
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blaze]], [[burst]], [[spark]], [[beam]], [[streak]], [[flare]], [[dazzle]], [[glare]]
**2** a sudden occurrence of a particular emotion or experience: a flash of anger
**3** a very brief time: in a flash he was inside and locked the door behind him
**4** a short unscheduled news announcement
**5** (*Brit** & **Austral*) an emblem on a uniform or vehicle to identify its military formation
**6** (*photog*) short for **flashlight**
**7** **flash in the pan** a project, person, etc., that enjoys only short-lived success
 *adj*
**8** (*informal*) [[ostentatious]] or vulgar
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ostentatious]], [[smart]], [[trendy]], [[showy]], [[bling]] (*slang*)
**9** brief and rapid: a flash fire
 *vb*
**10** to [[burst]] or cause to [[burst]] suddenly into flame
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blaze]], [[shine]], [[beam]], [[sparkle]], [[flare]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[light up]]
**11** to [[shine]] with a bright light suddenly or repeatedly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blaze]], [[shine]], [[beam]], [[sparkle]], [[flare]], [[glare]], [[gleam]], [[light up]]
**12** to move very fast
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speed]], [[race]], [[shoot]], [[fly]], [[tear]], [[dash]], [[whistle]], [[streak]]
**13** to come rapidly (into the mind or vision)
**14**
**A** to signal very fast: a warning was flashed onto a screen in the cockpit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show quickly]], [[display]], [[expose]], [[exhibit]], [[flourish]], [[show off]], [[flaunt]]
 **B** to signal by use of a light, [[such as car headlights]]
**15** (*informal*) to [[display]] in a boastful and extravagant way: flashing banknotes around
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show quickly]], [[display]], [[expose]], [[exhibit]], [[flourish]], [[show off]], [[flaunt]]
**16** (*informal*) to show briefly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show quickly]], [[display]], [[expose]], [[exhibit]], [[flourish]], [[show off]], [[flaunt]]
**17** (*Brit* *slang*) to [[expose]] oneself indecently
