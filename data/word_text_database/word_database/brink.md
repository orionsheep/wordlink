**brink** *n*
**1** the [[edge]] or [[border]] of a steep place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[limit]], [[border]], [[lip]], [[margin]], [[boundary]], [[skirt]], [[frontier]]
**2** the land at the [[edge]] of a body of water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[edge]], [[limit]], [[border]], [[lip]], [[margin]], [[boundary]], [[skirt]], [[frontier]]
**3** **on the brink of** very near; on the point of: on the brink of disaster
