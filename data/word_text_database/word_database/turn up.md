**[[turn up]]** *vb*
**1** to arrive or appear: few people turned up
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[turn up]]**: arrive, come, appear, show up (*informal*), attend, put in an appearance, show your face
**2** to find or discover or be found or discovered: a medical checkup has only turned up a sinus infection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[turn something up]]**: find, reveal, discover, expose, disclose, unearth, dig up
**3** to increase the flow, volume, etc., of: he turned up the radio
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[turn something up]]**: increase, raise, boost, enhance, intensify, amplify
 *n* **turn-up**
**4** (*Brit*) the turned-up fold at the bottom of some trouser legs
**5** **a turn-up for the books** (*informal*) an unexpected happening
