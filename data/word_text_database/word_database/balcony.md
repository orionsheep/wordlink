**balcony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a platform projecting from a building with a balustrade along its outer edge, often with access from a door
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[terrace]], [[veranda]]
**2** an upper tier of seats in a theatre or cinema
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upper circle]], [[gods]], [[gallery]]
