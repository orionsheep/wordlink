**apparatus** *n*
**1** a collection of [[equipment]] used for a particular purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[equipment]], [[tackle]], [[gear]], [[device]], [[tools]], [[mechanism]], [[machinery]], [[appliance]]
**2** any complicated [[device]], [[system]], or [[organization]]: the whole apparatus of law enforcement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[organization]], [[system]], [[network]], [[structure]], [[bureaucracy]], [[hierarchy]], [[setup]] (*informal*), [[chain of command]]
