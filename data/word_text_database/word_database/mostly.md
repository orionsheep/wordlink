**mostly** *adv*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mainly]], [[largely]], [[chiefly]], [[principally]], [[primarily]], [[on the whole]], [[predominantly]]
**2** on many or most occasions; [[usually]]: rattlesnakes mostly hunt at night
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[generally]], [[usually]], [[on the whole]], [[as a rule]]
