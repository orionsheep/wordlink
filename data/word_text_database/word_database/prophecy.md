**prophecy** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** a [[prediction]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prediction]], [[forecast]], [[prognostication]], [[augury]]
**2**
**A** a message revealing God's will
 **B** the act of uttering such a message
**3** the function or activity of a prophet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[second sight]], [[divination]], [[augury]], [[telling the future]], [[soothsaying]]
