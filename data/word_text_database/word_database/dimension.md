**dimension** *n*
**1** an [[aspect]] or factor: the attack brought a whole new dimension to the bombing campaign
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[aspect]], [[side]], [[feature]], [[angle]], [[facet]]
**2** **dimensions** scope or [[extent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extent]], [[size]]
**3** (*often pl*) a measurement of the [[size]] of something in a particular direction
 **[[dimensional]]** *adj*
