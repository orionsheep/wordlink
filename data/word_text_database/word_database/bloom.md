**bloom** *n*
**1** a [[blossom]] on a flowering plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flower]], [[bud]], [[blossom]]
**2** the state or period when flowers [[open]]
**3** a healthy or [[flourishing]] condition; [[prime]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prime]], [[flower]], [[beauty]], [[height]], [[peak]], [[flourishing]], [[heyday]], [[zenith]]
**4** a youthful or healthy [[glow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[glow]], [[freshness]], [[lustre]], [[radiance]]
**5** a fine whitish coating on the surface of fruits or leaves
 *vb*
**6** (of flowers) to [[open]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flower]], [[blossom]], [[open]], [[bud]]
**7** to bear flowers
**8** to [[flourish]] or grow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[succeed]], [[flourish]], [[thrive]], [[prosper]], [[fare well]]
**9** to be in a healthy, glowing condition
