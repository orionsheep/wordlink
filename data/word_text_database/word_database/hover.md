**hover** *vb*
**1** (of a bird, insect, or helicopter) to remain suspended in one place in the air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[float]], [[fly]], [[hang]], [[drift]], [[flutter]]
**2** to [[linger]] uncertainly in a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[linger]], [[loiter]], [[hang]] about *or* around (*informal*)
**3** to be in an unsettled or uncertain situation or frame of mind: hovering between two options
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[waver]], [[fluctuate]], [[dither]] (*chiefly* *Brit*), [[oscillate]], [[vacillate]]
