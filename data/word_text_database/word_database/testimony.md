**testimony** *n*, *pl* **-nies**
**1** a declaration of truth or fact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evidence]], [[statement]], [[submission]], [[affidavit]], [[deposition]]
**2** (*law*) [[evidence]] given by a witness, esp. in court under oath
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evidence]], [[statement]], [[submission]], [[affidavit]], [[deposition]]
**3** [[evidence]] proving or supporting something: that they are still talking is a testimony to their 20-year friendship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proof]], [[evidence]], [[demonstration]], [[indication]], [[support]], [[manifestation]], [[verification]], [[corroboration]]
