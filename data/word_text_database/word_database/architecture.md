**architecture** *n*
**1** the [[style]] in which a [[building]] is designed and built: Gothic architecture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[construction]], [[design]], [[style]]
**2** the science of designing and constructing buildings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[design]], [[planning]], [[building]], [[construction]]
**3** the [[structure]] or [[design]] of anything: computer architecture
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[structure]], [[design]], [[shape]], [[make-up]], [[construction]], [[framework]], [[layout]], [[anatomy]]
 **[[architectural]]** *adj*
