**proposition** *n*
**1** a [[proposal]] or offer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[proposal]], [[plan]], [[suggestion]], [[scheme]], [[bid]], [[recommendation]]
**2** (*logic*) a statement that affirms or denies something and is capable of being true or false
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[theory]], [[idea]], [[argument]], [[concept]], [[thesis]], [[hypothesis]], [[theorem]], [[premise]]
**3** (*maths*) a statement or [[theorem]], usually containing its proof
**4** (*informal*) a person or matter to be dealt with: even among experienced climbers the mountain is considered a tough proposition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[task]], [[problem]], [[activity]], [[job]], [[affair]], [[venture]], [[undertaking]]
**5** (*informal*) an invitation to engage in sexual intercourse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advance]], [[pass]] (*informal*), [[proposal]], [[overture]], [[improper suggestion]], come-on (*informal*)
 *vb*
**6** (*informal*) to invite (someone) to engage in sexual intercourse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make a pass at]], [[solicit]], [[accost]], [[make an improper suggestion to]]
