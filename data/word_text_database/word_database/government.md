**government** *n*
**1** the [[executive]] policy-making body of a country or state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administration]], [[executive]], [[ministry]], [[regime]], [[powers-that-be]], e-government *or* egovernment
**2** the state and its [[administration]]: the assembled heads of state and government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[administration]], [[executive]], [[ministry]], [[regime]], [[powers-that-be]], e-government *or* egovernment
**3** the system by which a country or state is ruled: the old hard-line government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rule]], [[authority]], [[administration]], [[sovereignty]], [[governance]], [[statecraft]]
 **[[governmental]]** *adj*
