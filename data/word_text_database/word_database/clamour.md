**clamour** * or * (*US*) **clamor** *n*
**1** a loud protest
**2** a loud and persistent [[noise]] or [[outcry]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[noise]], [[shouting]], [[racket]], [[outcry]], [[din]], [[uproar]], [[commotion]], hubbub
 *vb*
**3** **clamour for** to demand noisily
**4** to make a loud [[noise]] or [[outcry]]
 **[[clamorous]]** *adj*
