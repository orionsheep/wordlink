**terror** *n*
**1** very great [[fear]], [[panic]], or [[dread]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fear]], [[alarm]], [[dread]], [[fright]], [[panic]], [[anxiety]]
**2** a person or thing that inspires great [[dread]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nightmare]], [[monster]], [[bogeyman]], [[devil]], [[fiend]], [[bugbear]]
**3** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) a troublesome person, esp. a child
