**purchase** *vb* **-chasing**, **-chased**
**1** to [[obtain]] (goods) by payment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[buy]], [[pay for]], [[obtain]], [[get]], [[score]] (*slang*), [[gain]], [[pick up]], [[acquire]]
**2** to [[obtain]] by effort or sacrifice: he had purchased his freedom at the expense of his principles
 *n*
**3** something that is bought
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acquisition]], [[buy]], [[investment]], [[property]], [[gain]], [[asset]], [[possession]]
**4** the act of buying
**5** the mechanical advantage achieved by a lever
**6** a firm [[leverage]] or [[grip]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grip]], [[hold]], [[support]], [[leverage]], [[foothold]]
 **[[purchaser]]** *n*
