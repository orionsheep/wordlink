**sex** *n*
**1** the state of being male or female with reference to reproductive function
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gender]]
**2** either of the two categories, male or female, into which organisms are divided
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gender]]
**3** sexual intercourse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lovemaking]], [[sexual relations]], [[copulation]], [[fornication]], [[coitus]], [[cybersex]]
**4** feelings or behaviour connected with having sex or the desire to have sex
**5** sexual matters in general
 *adj*
**6** of sexual matters: sex education
**7** based on or resulting from the difference between the sexes: sex discrimination
 *vb*
**8** to find out the sex of (an animal)
