**pirate** *n*
**1** a person who commits piracy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[buccaneer]], [[raider]], [[marauder]], [[corsair]], [[freebooter]]
**2** a vessel used by pirates
**3** a person who illegally sells or publishes someone else's literary or artistic work
**4** a person or group of people who broadcast illegally
 *vb* **-rating**, **-rated**
**5** to sell or [[reproduce]] (artistic work, ideas, etc.) illegally
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[copy]], [[steal]], [[reproduce]], [[bootleg]], [[appropriate]], [[poach]], [[crib]] (*informal*), [[plagiarize]]
 **[[piratical]]** *adj*
