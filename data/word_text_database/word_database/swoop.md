**swoop** *vb*
**1** (usually foll. by *down*) to move quickly through the air in a downward curve: an owl swooped down from its perch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drop]], [[plunge]], [[dive]], [[sweep]], [[descend]], [[pounce]], [[stoop]]
**2** (usually foll. by *on*) to move suddenly and quickly towards (a place) in order to [[attack]], arrest, or question the people inside: nine police cars and vans swooped on the premises
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pounce]], [[attack]], [[charge]], [[rush]], [[descend]]
 *n*
**3** the act of swooping
