**adaptation** *n*
**1** something that is produced by adapting something else: a TV adaptation of a Victorian novel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conversion]], [[change]], [[variation]], [[adjustment]], [[transformation]], [[modification]], [[alteration]]
**2** the act of adapting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[acclimatization]], [[naturalization]], [[familiarization]]
