**[[romantic]]** *adj*
**1** of or dealing with love
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loving]], [[tender]], [[passionate]], [[fond]], [[sentimental]], [[amorous]], [[icky]] (*informal*)
**2** [[idealistic]] but [[impractical]]: a [[romantic]] notion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idealistic]], [[unrealistic]], [[impractical]], [[dreamy]], [[starry-eyed]]
**3** evoking or given to thoughts and feelings of love: [[romantic]] images
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loving]], [[tender]], [[passionate]], [[fond]], [[sentimental]], [[amorous]], [[icky]] (*informal*)
**4** **[[Romantic]]** relating to a movement in European art, music, and literature in the late 18th and early 19th centuries, characterized by an emphasis on feeling and content rather than order and form
 *n*
**5** a person who is [[idealistic]] or [[amorous]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idealist]], [[dreamer]], [[sentimentalist]]
**6** a person who likes or produces artistic works in the style of Romanticism
 **[[romantically]]** *adv*
