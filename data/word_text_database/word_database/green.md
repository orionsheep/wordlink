**[[green]]** *adj*
**1** of a colour between yellow and blue; of the colour of grass
**2** covered with grass, plants, or trees: [[green]] fields
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[verdant]] (*literary*), [[leafy]], [[grassy]]
**3** of or concerned with conservation and improvement of the environment: used in a political context: [[green]] issues
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ecological]], [[conservationist]], [[environment-friendly]], [[ozone-friendly]], [[non-polluting]], [[sustainable]], [[recyclable]], [[green-collar]]
**4** (of fruit) fresh, [[raw]], or unripe
**5** pale and sick-looking
**6** [[inexperienced]] or [[gullible]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inexperienced]], [[new]], [[raw]], [[naive]], [[immature]], [[gullible]], [[untrained]], [[wet behind the ears]] (*informal*)
**7** **[[green]] with envy** very envious
 *n*
**8** a colour between yellow and blue
**9** anything [[green]], such as [[green]] clothing or [[green]] ink: printed in [[green]]
**10** a small area of [[grassy]] land: the village [[green]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lawn]], [[common]], [[turf]], [[sward]]
**11** an area of smooth [[turf]] kept for a special purpose: putting greens
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lawn]], [[common]], [[turf]], [[sward]]
**12** **greens** the leaves and stems of certain plants, eaten as a vegetable: turnip greens
**13** **[[Green]]** a person who supports environmentalist issues
