**pierce** *vb* **piercing**, **pierced**
**1** to make a hole in (something) with a sharp point
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[penetrate]], [[stab]], [[spike]], [[enter]], [[bore]], [[drill]], [[puncture]], [[prick]]
**2** to force (a way) through (something)
**3** (of light) to shine through (darkness)
**4** (of sounds or cries) to sound sharply through (the silence)
**5** to [[penetrate]]: the cold pierced the air
