**probe** *vb* **probing**, **probed**
**1** to [[investigate]], or [[look into]], closely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[examine]], [[go into]], [[investigate]], [[explore]], [[search]], [[look into]], [[analyze]], [[dissect]]
**2** to [[poke]] or [[examine]] (something) with or as if with a probe: he probed carefully with his fingertips
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explore]], [[examine]], [[poke]], [[prod]], feel around
 *n*
**3** (*surgery*) a slender instrument for exploring a wound, etc.
**4** a thorough [[inquiry]], such as one into corrupt practices
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[investigation]], [[study]], [[inquiry]], [[analysis]], [[examination]], [[exploration]], [[scrutiny]], [[scrutinization]]
