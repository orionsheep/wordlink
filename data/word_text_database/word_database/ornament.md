**ornament** *n*
**1** anything that adorns someone or something; [[decoration]]: the room's only ornament was a dim, oily picture of the Holy Family
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decoration]], [[trimming]], [[accessory]], [[festoon]], [[trinket]], [[bauble]], [[knick-knack]]
**2** decorations collectively: he had no watch, nor ornament of any kind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[embellishment]], [[decoration]], [[embroidery]], [[elaboration]], [[adornment]], [[ornamentation]]
**3** a small decorative object: I hit a garden ornament while parking
**4** a person whose character or talent makes them an asset to society or the group to which they belong: an ornament of the firm
**5** (*music*) a note or group of notes which embellishes the melody but is not an integral part of it, for instance a trill
 *vb*
**6** to [[decorate]] or [[adorn]]: the hall had a high ceiling, [[ornamented]] with plaster fruits and flowers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decorate]], [[adorn]], [[array]], [[do up]] (*informal*), [[embellish]], [[festoon]], [[beautify]], [[prettify]]
