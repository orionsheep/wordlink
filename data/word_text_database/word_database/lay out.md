**lay out** *vb*
**1** to arrange or spread out
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[lay something out]]**: arrange, order, [[design]], display, exhibit, put out, spread out
**2** to [[plan]] or [[design]]: the main streets were laid out on a grid system
**3** to prepare (a corpse) for burial or cremation
**4** (*informal*) to spend (money), esp. lavishly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[lay something out]]**: spend, pay, invest, fork out (*slang*), expend, shell out (*informal*), disburse
**5** (*informal*) to knock (someone) unconscious
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[lay someone out]]**: knock out, fell, floor, knock unconscious, knock for six
 *n* **layout**
**6** the [[arrangement]] or [[plan]] of something, such as a building
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrangement]], [[design]], [[outline]], [[format]], [[plan]], [[formation]]
**7** the [[arrangement]] of printed material
