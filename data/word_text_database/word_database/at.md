**at** *prep*
**1** indicating location or position: she had planted a vegetable garden at the back
**2** towards; in the direction of: she was staring at the wall behind him
**3** indicating position in time: we arrived at 12.30
**4** engaged in: the innocent laughter of children at play
**5** during the passing of: she works at night as a nurse's aide
**6** for; in exchange for: crude oil is selling at its lowest price since September
**7** indicating the object of an emotion: I'm angry at you because you were rude to me
