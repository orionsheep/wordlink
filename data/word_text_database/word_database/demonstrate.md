**demonstrate** *vb* **-strating**, **-strated**
**1** to [[show]] or [[prove]] by reasoning or evidence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[express]], [[display]], [[indicate]], [[exhibit]], [[manifest]], [[flag up]]
**2** to [[display]] and [[explain]] the workings of (a machine, product, etc.)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[describe]], [[show]], [[explain]], [[teach]], [[illustrate]]
**3** to reveal the existence of: the adult literacy campaign demonstrated the scale of educational deprivation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[prove]], [[show]], [[indicate]], [[make clear]], [[manifest]], [[testify to]], [[flag up]]
**4** to [[show]] support or opposition by public parades or rallies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[march]], [[protest]], [[rally]], [[object]], [[parade]], [[picket]], [[remonstrate]] (*formal*), [[express disapproval]], [[hikoi]] (*NZ*)
