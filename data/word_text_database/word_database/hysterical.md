**hysterical** *adj*
**1** in a state of uncontrolled panic, anger, or excitement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frenzied]], [[frantic]], [[raving]], [[distracted]], [[distraught]], [[crazed]], [[overwrought]]
**2** (*informal*) wildly funny
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hilarious]], [[uproarious]], [[side-splitting]], [[comical]]
 **[[hysterically]]** *adv*
