**dividend** *n*
**1** a [[portion]] of a company's profits paid to its shareholders
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bonus]], [[share]], [[cut]] (*informal*), [[gain]], [[extra]], [[plus]], [[portion]], [[divvy]] (*informal*)
**2** an [[extra]] benefit: Saudi progressives saw a dividend to the crisis
**3** (*maths*) a number to be divided by another number
