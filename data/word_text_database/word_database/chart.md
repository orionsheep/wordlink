**chart** *n*
**1** a [[graph]], [[table]], or sheet of information in the form of a [[diagram]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[table]], [[diagram]], [[blueprint]], [[graph]], [[plan]], [[map]]
**2** a [[map]] of the sea or the stars
**3** **the charts** (*informal*) the weekly lists of the bestselling pop records or the most popular videos
 *vb*
**4** to [[plot]] the course of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[monitor]], [[follow]], [[record]], [[note]], [[document]], [[register]], [[trace]], [[outline]]
**5** to make a chart of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[plot]], [[map out]], [[delineate]], [[sketch]], [[draft]], [[tabulate]]
**6** to appear in the pop charts
