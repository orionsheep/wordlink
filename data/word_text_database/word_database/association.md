**association** *n*
**1** a [[group]] of people with a common interest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[group]], [[club]], [[society]], [[league]], [[band]], [[set]], [[pack]], [[collection]], [[social network]] (*computers*)
**2** the act of associating or the state of being associated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[connection]], [[union]], [[joining]], [[pairing]], [[combination]], [[mixture]], [[blend]], [[juxtaposition]]
**3** friendship: their association still had to remain a secret
**4** a mental [[connection]] of ideas or feelings: the place contained associations for her
