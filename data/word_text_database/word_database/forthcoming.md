**forthcoming** *adj*
**1** about to appear or happen: the forthcoming elections
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[approaching]], [[coming]], [[expected]], [[future]], [[imminent]], [[prospective]], [[impending]], [[upcoming]]
**2** given or made [[available]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[available]], [[ready]], [[accessible]], [[at hand]], [[in evidence]], [[obtainable]], [[on tap]] (*informal*)
**3** (of a person) willing to give information
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[communicative]], [[open]], [[free]], [[informative]], [[expansive]], [[sociable]], [[chatty]], [[talkative]]
