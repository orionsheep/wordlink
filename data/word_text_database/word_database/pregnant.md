**pregnant** *adj*
**1** carrying a fetus or fetuses within the womb
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expectant]], [[expecting]], [[with child]] (*archaic*), [[in the club]] (*Brit* *slang*), up the duff (*Brit* *slang*, *rare*), big *or* heavy [[with child]] (*archaic*)
**2** full of meaning or significance: a pregnant pause
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meaningful]], [[pointed]], [[charged]], [[significant]], [[telling]], [[loaded]], [[expressive]], [[eloquent]]
 **[[pregnancy]]** *n*
