**extra** *adj*
**1** more than is usual, expected, or needed; additional
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[surplus]], [[excess]], [[spare]], [[redundant]], [[unused]], [[leftover]], superfluous
 *n*
**2** a person or thing that is additional
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[addition]], [[bonus]], [[supplement]], [[accessory]]
**3** something for which an additional charge is made
**4** (*films*) a person temporarily engaged, usually for crowd scenes
**5** (*cricket*) a run not scored from the bat
**6** an additional edition of a newspaper
 *adv*
**7** [[unusually]]; [[exceptionally]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exceptionally]], [[very]], [[specially]], [[especially]], [[particularly]], [[extremely]], [[remarkably]], [[unusually]]
