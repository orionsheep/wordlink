**contrive** *vb* **-triving**, **-trived**
**1** to make happen: he had already contrived the murder of King Alexander
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[devise]], [[plan]], [[fabricate]], [[create]], [[design]], [[scheme]], [[manufacture]], [[plot]]
**2** to [[devise]] or construct ingeniously: he contrived a plausible reason to fly back to London; he contrived a hook from a bent nail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[devise]], [[plan]], [[fabricate]], [[create]], [[design]], [[scheme]], [[manufacture]], [[plot]]
