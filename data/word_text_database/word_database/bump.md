**bump** *vb*
**1** to [[knock]] or strike (someone or something) with a [[jolt]]
**2** to travel or proceed in jerks and jolts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jerk]], [[shake]], [[bounce]], [[rattle]], [[jog]], [[lurch]], [[jolt]]
**3** to hurt by knocking
 *n*
**4** an [[impact]]; [[knock]]; [[jolt]]; [[collision]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[knock]], [[blow]], [[impact]], [[collision]], [[thump]]
**5** a dull thud from an [[impact]] or [[collision]]
**6** a [[lump]] on the body caused by a [[blow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lump]], [[swelling]], [[bulge]], [[hump]], [[nodule]], [[protuberance]], [[contusion]] (*formal*)
**7** a raised uneven part, such as on a road surface
 See also **bump off**
 **[[bumpy]]** *adj*
