**wind**^2 (rhymes with **pined**) *vb* **[[winding]]**, **wound**
**1** to [[twist]] (something flexible) round some object: a sweatband was wound round his head
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wrap]], [[twist]], [[reel]], [[curl]], [[loop]], [[coil]]
**2** to tighten the spring of (a clock or watch) by turning a key or knob
**3** to follow a twisting course: a narrow path wound through the shrubbery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meander]], [[turn]], [[bend]], [[twist]], [[curve]], [[snake]], [[ramble]], [[twist]] and [[turn]]
 See also **wind up**
