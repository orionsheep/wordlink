**exquisite** *adj*
**1** extremely [[beautiful]] or [[attractive]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beautiful]], [[elegant]], [[graceful]], [[pleasing]], [[attractive]], [[lovely]], [[charming]], [[comely]] (*old-fashioned*)
**2** showing unusual delicacy and craftsmanship
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fine]], [[beautiful]], [[lovely]], [[elegant]], [[precious]], [[delicate]], [[dainty]]
**3** sensitive or discriminating: exquisite manners
**4** intensely felt: exquisite joy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intense]], [[acute]], [[severe]], [[sharp]], [[keen]], [[extreme]]
 **[[exquisitely]]** *adv*
