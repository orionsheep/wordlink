**popular** *adj*
**1** widely [[liked]] or admired
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-liked]], [[liked]], [[in]], [[accepted]], [[favourite]], [[approved]], [[in favour]], [[fashionable]], [[trending]]
**2** (often foll. by *with*) [[liked]] by a particular person or group: the bay is popular with windsurfers and water-skiers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-liked]], [[liked]], [[in]], [[accepted]], [[favourite]], [[approved]], [[in favour]], [[fashionable]], [[trending]]
**3** [[common]] among the [[general]] public: the groundswell of popular feeling
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[common]], [[general]], [[prevailing]], [[current]], [[conventional]], [[universal]], [[prevalent]]
**4** designed to appeal to a mass audience: an attack on him [[in]] the popular press
