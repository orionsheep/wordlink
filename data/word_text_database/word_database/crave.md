**crave** *vb* **[[craving]]**, **craved**
**1** to [[desire]] intensely: a vulnerable, unhappy girl who craved affection
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[long for]], [[yearn for]], [[hanker after]], [[want]], [[desire]], [[hope for]], [[covet]], [[lust after]]
**2** (*formal*) to [[beg]] or [[plead for]]: may I crave your lordship's indulgence?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[beg]], [[ask for]], [[seek]], [[petition]], [[pray for]], [[plead for]], [[solicit]], [[implore]]
 **[[craving]]** *n*
