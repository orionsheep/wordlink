**daughter** *n*
**1** a female child
**2** a girl or woman who comes from a certain place or is connected with a certain thing: a daughter of the church
 *adj*
**3** (*biol*) denoting a cell, chromosome, etc. produced by the division of one of its own kind
**4** (*physics*) (of a nuclide) formed from another nuclide by radioactive decay
 **[[daughterly]]** *adj*
