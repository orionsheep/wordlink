**triumphant** *adj*
**1** feeling or displaying triumph: her smile was triumphant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[victorious]], [[winning]], [[successful]], [[conquering]]
**2** celebrating a victory or success: the general's triumphant tour round the city
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[celebratory]], [[jubilant]], [[proud]], [[elated]], [[exultant]], [[cock-a-hoop]] (*old-fashioned*) 
 **[[triumphantly]]** *adv*
