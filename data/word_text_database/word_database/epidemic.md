**epidemic** *n*
**1** a widespread occurrence of a disease
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outbreak]], [[plague]], [[growth]], [[spread]], [[scourge]], [[contagion]]
**2** a rapid development or [[spread]] of something: the epidemic of childhood obesity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spate]], [[plague]], [[outbreak]], [[wave]], [[rash]], [[eruption]], upsurge
 *adj*
**3** (esp. of a disease) affecting many people in an area: stress has now reached epidemic proportions
