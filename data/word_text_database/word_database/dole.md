**dole** *n*
**1** **the dole** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) money received from the state while unemployed
**2** **on the dole** (*Brit** & **Austral** & **NZ* *informal*) receiving benefit while unemployed
 *vb* **doling**, **doled**
**3** **dole out** to distribute in small quantities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[dole something out]]**: give out, distribute, assign, allocate, hand out, dispense, allot, apportion
