**sticky** *adj* **stickier**, **stickiest**
**1** covered with a substance that sticks to other things: sticky little fingers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gooey]], [[tacky]] (*informal*), [[viscous]], [[glutinous]], [[gummy]], [[icky]] (*informal*), [[gluey]], [[clinging]]
**2** intended to stick to a surface: sticky labels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[adhesive]], [[gummed]], [[adherent]], [[grippy]]
**3** (*informal*) [[difficult]] or painful: a sticky meeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[difficult]], [[awkward]], [[tricky]], [[embarrassing]], [[nasty]], [[delicate]], [[unpleasant]], [[barro]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**4** (of weather) unpleasantly warm and [[humid]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[humid]], [[close]], [[sultry]], [[oppressive]], [[sweltering]], [[clammy]], [[muggy]]
 **[[stickiness]]** *n*
