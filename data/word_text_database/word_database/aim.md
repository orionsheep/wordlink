**aim** *vb*
**1** to [[point]] (a weapon or missile) or direct (a blow or remark) at a particular person or object
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[point]]
**2** to propose or intend: they aim to provide full and equal rights to all groups
 *n*
**3** the action of directing something at an object
**4** [[intention]] or [[purpose]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intention]], [[point]], [[plan]], [[goal]], [[design]], [[target]], [[purpose]], [[desire]]
**5** **take aim** to [[point]] a weapon or missile at a person or object
