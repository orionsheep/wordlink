**immaculate** *adj*
**1** completely [[clean]] or tidy: an immaculate pinstripe suit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clean]], [[spotless]], [[neat]], [[spruce]], [[squeaky-clean]], [[spick-and-span]]
**2** completely [[flawless]]: his equestrian pedigree is immaculate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perfect]], [[flawless]], [[impeccable]], [[faultless]], [[unblemished]], [[untarnished]], [[unexceptionable]]
 **[[immaculately]]** *adv*
