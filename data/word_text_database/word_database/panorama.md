**panorama** *n*
**1** a wide unbroken [[view]] in all directions: the beautiful panorama of the Cornish coast
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[view]], [[prospect]], [[vista]]
**2** a wide or comprehensive [[survey]] of a subject: the panorama of American life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[survey]], [[perspective]], [[overview]], [[overall picture]]
**3** a picture of a scene unrolled before spectators a part at a time so as to appear continuous
 **[[panoramic]]** *adj*
