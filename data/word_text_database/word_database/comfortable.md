**comfortable** *adj*
**1** giving comfort; [[relaxing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pleasant]], [[homely]], [[relaxing]], [[cosy]], [[agreeable]], [[restful]]
**2** free from trouble or pain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[at ease]], [[happy]], [[at home]], [[contented]], [[relaxed]], [[serene]]
**3** (*informal*) [[well-off]] financially
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-off]], [[prosperous]], [[affluent]], [[well-to-do]], [[comfortably-off]], [[in clover]] (*informal*)
**4** not afraid or embarrassed: he was not comfortable expressing sympathy
 **[[comfortably]]** *adv*
