**private** *adj*
**1** not for general or public use: a private bathroom
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exclusive]], [[individual]], [[privately owned]], [[own]], [[special]], [[reserved]]
**2** [[confidential]] or [[secret]]: a private conversation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secret]], [[confidential]], [[covert]], [[unofficial]], [[clandestine]], [[off the record]], [[hush-hush]] (*informal*)
**3** involving someone's domestic and [[personal]] life rather than his or her work or business: what I do in my private life is none of your business
**4** owned or paid for by individuals rather than by the government: private enterprise
**5** not publicly known: they had private reasons for the decision
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[personal]], [[individual]], [[secret]], [[intimate]], [[undisclosed]], [[unspoken]], [[innermost]], [[unvoiced]]
**6** having no public office, rank, or position: the Red Cross received donations from private citizens
**7** (of a place) quiet and [[secluded]]: the garden is completely private
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[secluded]], [[secret]], [[separate]], [[isolated]], [[sequestered]]
**8** (of a person) quiet and [[retiring]]: she was private – her life was her [[own]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[solitary]], [[reserved]], [[retiring]], [[withdrawn]], [[discreet]], [[secretive]], [[self-contained]], reclusive
 *n*
**9** a soldier of the lowest rank in the army
**10** **in private** in [[secret]]
 **[[privately]]** *adv*
