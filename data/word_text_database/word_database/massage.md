**massage** (**mass**-ahzh) *n*
**1** the kneading or rubbing of parts of the body to reduce pain or stiffness or help relaxation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rub-down]], manipulation
 *vb* **-saging**, **-saged**
**2** to give a massage to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rub down]], [[manipulate]], [[knead]]
**3** to [[manipulate]] statistics or evidence to produce a desired result
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manipulate]], [[alter]], [[distort]], [[doctor]], [[cook]] (*informal*), [[fix]] (*informal*), [[rig]], [[fiddle]] (*informal*)
