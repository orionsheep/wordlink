**chill** *n*
**1** a feverish cold
**2** a moderate [[coldness]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coldness]], [[bite]], [[nip]], [[sharpness]], [[coolness]], [[rawness]], [[crispness]], [[frigidity]]
**3** a feeling of [[coldness]] resulting from a cold or damp environment or from sudden fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shiver]], frisson
 *vb*
**4** to make (something) [[cool]] or cold: chilled white wine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cool]], [[refrigerate]], [[freeze]]
**5** to cause (someone) to feel cold or frightened
**6** (*informal*) to relax or calm oneself
 *adj*
**7** unpleasantly cold: chill winds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chilly]], [[biting]], [[sharp]], [[freezing]], [[raw]], [[bleak]], [[wintry]]
 **[[chilling]]** *adj* 
 **[[chillingly]]** *adv*
