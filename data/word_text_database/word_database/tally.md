**tally** *vb* **-lies**, **-lying**, **-lied**
**1** to [[agree]] with or be consistent with something else: this description didn't seem to me to tally with what we saw
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agree]], [[match]], [[accord]], [[fit]], [[square]], [[coincide]], [[correspond]], [[conform]]
**2** to keep [[score]]
 *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**3** any [[record]] of debit, credit, the [[score]] in a game, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[record]], [[score]], [[total]], [[count]], [[reckoning]], [[running total]]
**4** an identifying label or mark
**5** a stick used (esp. formerly) as a [[record]] of the amount of a debt according to the notches cut in it
