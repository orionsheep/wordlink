**pause** *vb* **pausing**, **paused**
**1** to [[stop]] doing (something) for a short time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop briefly]], [[delay]], [[break]], [[wait]], [[rest]], [[halt]], [[cease]], [[interrupt]]
**2** to hesitate: she answered him without pausing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop briefly]], [[delay]], [[break]], [[wait]], [[rest]], [[halt]], [[cease]], [[interrupt]]
 *n*
**3** a temporary [[stop]] or [[rest]] in speech or action
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[break]], [[interval]], [[rest]], [[gap]], [[halt]], [[respite]], [[lull]]
**4** (*music*) a continuation of a note or [[rest]] beyond its normal length
**5** **give someone pause** to cause someone to hesitate: it gave him pause for reflection
