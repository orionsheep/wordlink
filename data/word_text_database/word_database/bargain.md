**bargain** *n*
**1** an [[agreement]] establishing what each party will give, receive, or perform in a transaction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[agreement]], [[deal]] (*informal*), [[promise]], [[contract]], [[arrangement]], [[settlement]], [[pledge]], [[pact]]
**2** something acquired or received in such an [[agreement]]
**3** something bought or offered at a low price
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good buy]], [[discount purchase]], [[good deal]], [[steal]] (*informal*), [[snip]] (*informal*), [[giveaway]], [[cheap purchase]]
**4** **drive a hard bargain** to forcefully pursue one's own profit in a transaction
**5** **into the bargain** besides
 *vb*
**6** to [[negotiate]] the terms of an [[agreement]] or transaction
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[negotiate]], [[deal]], [[contract]], [[mediate]], [[covenant]], [[stipulate]], [[transact]], [[cut a deal]]
