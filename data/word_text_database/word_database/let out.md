**let out** *vb*
**1** to emit: he let out a scream
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[let something out]]**: emit, make, produce, give vent to
**2** to allow to leave; release
**3** to make (property) available for people to rent
**4** to make (a garment) wider by reducing the seams
**5** to reveal (a secret)
 *n* **let-out**
**6** a chance to escape
