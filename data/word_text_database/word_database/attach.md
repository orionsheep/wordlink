**attach** *vb*
**1** to [[join]], fasten, or [[connect]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affix]], [[stick]], [[secure]], [[add]], [[join]], [[couple]], [[link]], [[tie]]
**2** to [[attribute]] or [[ascribe]]: he attaches particular importance to the proposed sale
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ascribe]], [[connect]], [[attribute]], [[assign]], [[associate]]
**3** **attach oneself *or* be attached to** to become associated with or [[join]]
