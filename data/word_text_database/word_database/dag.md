**dag** (*Austral** & **NZ*) *n*
**1** the dried dung on a sheep's rear
**2** (*informal*) an amusing person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[joker]], [[comic]], [[wag]], [[wit]], [[comedian]], [[clown]], [[humorist]], prankster
 *pl n*
**3** **rattle one's dags** (*informal*) hurry up
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[rattle your dags]]**: hurry up, get a move on, step on it (*informal*), get your skates on (*informal*), make haste
 *vb*
**4** to remove the dags from a sheep
