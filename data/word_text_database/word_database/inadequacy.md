**inadequacy** *n*, *pl* **-cies**
**1** lack or [[shortage]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shortage]], [[poverty]], [[dearth]], [[paucity]] (*formal*), [[insufficiency]], [[meagreness]], [[scantiness]]
**2** the state of being or feeling inferior
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incompetence]], [[inability]], [[deficiency]], [[incapacity]], [[ineffectiveness]]
**3** a [[weakness]] or [[failing]]: their own failures or inadequacies
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shortcoming]], [[failing]], [[weakness]], [[defect]], [[imperfection]]
