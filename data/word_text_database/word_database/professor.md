**professor** *n*
**1** the highest rank of teacher in a university
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[don]] (*Brit*), [[fellow]] (*Brit*), [[prof]] (*informal*)
**2** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) any teacher in a university or college
**3** (*rare*) a person who professes his or her opinions or beliefs
 **[[professorial]]** *adj* 
 **[[professorship]]** *n*
