**sanctuary** *n*, *pl* **-aries**
**1** a holy place, such as a consecrated building or shrine
**2** the part of a church nearest the main altar
**3** a place of [[refuge]] or [[protection]] for someone who is being chased or hunted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[shelter]], [[refuge]], [[haven]], [[retreat]], [[asylum]]
**4** [[refuge]] or safety: the sanctuary of your own home
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protection]], [[shelter]], [[refuge]], [[haven]], [[retreat]], [[asylum]]
**5** a place, protected by law, where animals can live and breed without interference
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reserve]], [[park]], [[preserve]], [[reservation]], [[national park]], [[tract]], [[nature reserve]], [[conservation area]]
