◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be paid]], [[make]], [[get]], [[receive]], [[gain]], [[net]], [[collect]], [[bring in]]
**2** to acquire or [[deserve]] through one's behaviour or action: you've earned a good night's sleep
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[deserve]], [[win]], [[gain]], [[attain]], [[justify]], [[merit]], [[warrant]], [[be entitled to]]
**3** to [[make]] (money) as interest or profit: her savings earned 8% interest
