**revolution** *n*
**1** the overthrow of a regime or political system by the governed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[revolt]], [[rising]], [[coup]], [[rebellion]], [[uprising]], [[mutiny]], [[insurgency]]
**2** (in Marxist theory) the transition from one system of production in a society to the next
**3** a far-reaching and drastic change
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[transformation]], [[shift]], [[innovation]], [[upheaval]], [[reformation]], [[sea change]]
**4**
**A** movement in or as if in a [[circle]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rotation]], [[turn]], [[cycle]], [[circle]], [[spin]], [[lap]], [[circuit]], [[orbit]]
 **B** one complete [[turn]] in a [[circle]]: 33 revolutions per minute
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rotation]], [[turn]], [[cycle]], [[circle]], [[spin]], [[lap]], [[circuit]], [[orbit]]
