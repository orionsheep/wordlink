**vomit** *vb* **-iting**, **-ited**
**1** to eject (the contents of the stomach) through the mouth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be sick]], [[throw up]] (*informal*), [[spew]], [[chuck]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[heave]] (*slang*), [[retch]]
**2** to eject or be ejected forcefully
 *n*
**3** the partly digested food and drink ejected in vomiting
