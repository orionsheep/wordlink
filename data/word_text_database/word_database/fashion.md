**fashion** *n*
**1** [[style]] in clothes, hairstyles, behaviour, etc., that is popular at a particular time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[style]], [[look]], [[trend]], [[rage]], [[custom]], [[mode]], [[vogue]], [[craze]]
**2** the [[way]] that something happens or is done: conversing in a very animated fashion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[method]], [[way]], [[style]], [[manner]], [[mode]]
**3** **after a fashion** in some [[way]], but not very well: she apologized, after a fashion, for her haste
 *vb*
**4** to [[form]], [[make]], or [[shape]]: he had fashioned a crude musical instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make]], [[shape]], [[cast]], [[construct]], [[form]], [[create]], [[manufacture]], [[forge]]
