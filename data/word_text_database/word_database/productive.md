**productive** *adj*
**1** producing or having the power to produce
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[creative]], [[inventive]]
**2** yielding favourable results
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[useful]], [[rewarding]], [[valuable]], [[profitable]], [[effective]], [[worthwhile]], [[beneficial]], [[constructive]]
**3** (*econ*) producing goods and services that have exchange value: the country's productive capacity
**4** (foll. by *of*) resulting in: a period highly productive of books and ideas
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fertile]], [[rich]], [[prolific]], [[plentiful]], [[fruitful]], [[fecund]]
 **[[productivity]]** *n*
