**summit** *n*
**1** the highest point or part of a mountain or hill
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peak]], [[top]], [[tip]], [[pinnacle]], [[apex]], [[head]]
**2** the highest possible degree or state; [[peak]] or climax: the summit of success
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[height]], [[pinnacle]], [[peak]], [[zenith]], [[acme]]
**3** a meeting of heads of governments or other high officials
