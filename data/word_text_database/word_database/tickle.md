**tickle** *vb* **-ling**, **-led**
**1** to touch or stroke (someone), so as to produce laughter or a twitching sensation
**2** to itch or tingle
**3** to amuse or please
**4** **tickled pink *or* to death** (*informal*) greatly pleased
**5** **tickle someone's fancy** to appeal to or amuse someone
 *n*
**6** a sensation of light stroking or itching: a tickle in the throat
**7** the act of tickling
**8** (*Canad*) (in the Atlantic Provinces) a narrow strait
