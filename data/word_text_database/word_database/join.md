**join** *vb*
**1** to become a member of (a club or organization)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enrol in]], [[enter]], [[sign up for]], [[enlist in]]
**2** to become part of (a queue or list)
**3** to meet (someone) as a companion: join me for a cup of coffee
**4** to take part in (an activity): join the relief effort
**5** (of two roads or rivers) to meet and come together
**6** to bring into contact: join hands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[connect]], [[unite]], [[couple]], [[link]], [[combine]], [[attach]], [[fasten]], [[add]]
**7** **join forces** to [[combine]] efforts with someone
 *n*
**8** a place where two things are joined together
 See also **join up**
