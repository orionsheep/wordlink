**clasp** *n*
**1** a [[fastening]], such as a [[catch]] or [[hook]], for holding things together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fastening]], [[catch]], [[grip]], [[hook]], [[pin]], [[clip]], [[buckle]], [[brooch]]
**2** a firm [[grasp]] or [[embrace]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grasp]], [[hold]], [[grip]], [[embrace]], hug
 *vb*
**3** to [[grasp]] or [[embrace]] tightly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grasp]], [[hold]], [[press]], [[grip]], [[seize]], [[squeeze]], [[embrace]], [[clutch]]
**4** to fasten together with a clasp
