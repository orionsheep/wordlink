**dart** *n*
**1** a small narrow pointed missile that is thrown or shot, as in the game of darts
**2** a sudden quick movement
**3** a tapered tuck made in dressmaking
 *vb*
**4** to move or throw swiftly and suddenly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dash]], [[run]], [[race]], [[shoot]], [[fly]], [[speed]], [[spring]], [[tear]]
 **[[darting]]** *adj*
