**ballot** *n*
**1** the practice of selecting a representative or course of action by [[voting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vote]], [[election]], [[voting]], [[poll]], [[polling]], [[referendum]], [[show of hands]]
**2** the number of votes cast in an [[election]]
**3** the actual [[vote]] or paper indicating a person's choice
 *vb* **-loting**, **-loted**
**4** to [[vote]] or ask for a [[vote]] from: we balloted the members on this issue
**5** to [[vote]] for or decide on something by ballot
