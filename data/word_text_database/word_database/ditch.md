**ditch** *n*
**1** a narrow [[channel]] dug in the earth for drainage or irrigation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[channel]], [[drain]], [[trench]], [[dyke]], [[furrow]], [[gully]], [[moat]], watercourse
 *vb*
**2** (*slang*) to abandon or [[discard]]: she ditched her boyfriend last month
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get rid of]], [[dump]] (*informal*), [[scrap]], [[discard]], [[dispose of]], [[dispense with]], [[jettison]], throw out *or* overboard
