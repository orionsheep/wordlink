**coach** *n*
**1** a large comfortable single-decker [[bus]] used for sightseeing or long-distance travel
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bus]], [[charabanc]]
**2** a railway carriage
**3** a large four-wheeled enclosed carriage, usually horse-drawn
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bus]], [[charabanc]]
**4** a [[trainer]] or [[instructor]]: the coach of the Mexican national team
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instructor]], [[teacher]], [[trainer]], [[tutor]], [[handler]]
**5** a [[tutor]] who prepares students for examinations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instructor]], [[teacher]], [[trainer]], [[tutor]], [[handler]]
 *vb*
**6** to [[train]] or teach
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instruct]], [[train]], [[prepare]], [[exercise]], [[drill]], [[tutor]]
