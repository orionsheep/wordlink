**muster** *vb*
**1** to summon or [[gather]]: I put as much disbelief in my expression as I could muster
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[summon up]], [[marshal]]
**2** to call or be called together for duty or inspection: the battalion mustered on the bank of the river
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rally]], [[gather]], [[assemble]], [[marshal]], [[mobilize]], call together
 *n*
**3** an [[assembly]] of military personnel for duty or inspection
**4** a [[collection]], [[assembly]], or [[gathering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assembly]], [[meeting]], [[collection]], [[gathering]], [[rally]], [[convention]], [[congregation]], [[roundup]], [[hui]] (*NZ*), [[runanga]] (*NZ*)
**5** **pass muster** to be acceptable
