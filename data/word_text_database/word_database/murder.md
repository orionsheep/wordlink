**murder** *n*
**1** the unlawful intentional [[killing]] of one human being by another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[killing]], [[homicide]], [[massacre]], [[assassination]], [[slaying]], [[bloodshed]], [[carnage]], [[butchery]]
**2** (*informal*) something dangerous, difficult, or unpleasant: shopping on Christmas Eve is murder
**3** **cry blue murder** (*informal*) to make an outcry
**4** **get away with murder** (*informal*) to do as one pleases without ever being punished
 *vb*
**5** to [[kill]] someone intentionally and unlawfully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kill]], [[massacre]], [[slaughter]], [[assassinate]], [[eliminate]] (*slang*), [[butcher]], [[slay]], [[bump off]] (*slang*)
**6** (*informal*) to ruin a piece of music or drama by performing it very badly: he absolutely murdered that song
**7** (*informal*) to beat decisively
 **[[murderer]]** * or * (*fem*) **[[murderess]]** *n* 
 **[[murderous]]** *adj*
