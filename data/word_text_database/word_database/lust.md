**lust** *n*
**1** a strong sexual [[desire]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lechery]], [[sensuality]], [[lewdness]], [[lasciviousness]]
**2** a strong [[desire]] or drive: a lust for power
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[desire]], [[longing]], [[passion]], [[appetite]], [[craving]], [[greed]], thirst
 *vb*
**3** (often foll. by *after* or *for*) to have a passionate [[desire]] (for)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[lust for or after someone or something]]**: [[desire]], want, crave, yearn for, covet, hunger for *or* after
 **[[lustful]]** *adj* 
 **[[lustfully]]** *adv*
