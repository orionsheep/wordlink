**1** a [[return]] to a former position or status
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[return]], [[revival]], [[rebound]], [[resurgence]], [[rally]], [[recovery]], [[triumph]]
**2** a [[response]] or [[retaliation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[response]], [[reply]], [[retort]], [[retaliation]], [[riposte]], rejoinder
 *vb* **come back**
**3** to [[return]], esp. to the memory
**4** to become fashionable again
