**explanation** *n*
**1** the [[reason]] or reasons why a particular event or situation happened: there is no reasonable explanation for her behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reason]], [[answer]], [[account]], [[excuse]], [[motive]], [[justification]], [[vindication]]
**2** a detailed [[account]] or [[description]]: a 90-minute explanation of his love of jazz
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[description]], [[report]], [[definition]], [[teaching]], [[interpretation]], [[illustration]], [[clarification]], [[simplification]]
