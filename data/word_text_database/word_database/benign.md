**benign** (bin-**nine**) *adj*
**1** showing kindliness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[benevolent]], [[kind]], [[kindly]], [[warm]], [[friendly]], [[obliging]], [[sympathetic]], [[compassionate]]
**2** favourable: a stroke of benign fate
**3** (*pathol*) (of a tumour, etc.) able to be controlled
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[harmless]], [[innocent]], [[innocuous]], [[curable]], [[inoffensive]], [[remediable]]
 **[[benignly]]** *adv*
