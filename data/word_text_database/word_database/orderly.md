**orderly** *adj*
**1** [[tidy]] or [[well-organized]]: they evacuated the building in an orderly manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-organized]], [[regular]], [[in order]], [[organized]], [[precise]], [[neat]], [[tidy]], [[systematic]]
**2** [[well-behaved]]; [[law-abiding]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[well-behaved]], [[controlled]], [[disciplined]], [[quiet]], [[restrained]], [[law-abiding]], peaceable
 *n*, *pl* **-lies**
**3** (*med*) a male hospital attendant
**4** (*military*) a soldier whose duty is to carry out orders or perform minor tasks for a more senior officer
 **[[orderliness]]** *n*
