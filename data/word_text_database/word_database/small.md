**small** *adj*
**1** not large in size or amount
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[little]], [[minute]], [[tiny]], [[mini]], [[miniature]], [[minuscule]], [[diminutive]], [[petite]]
**2** of [[little]] importance or on a [[minor]] scale: a small detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unimportant]], [[minor]], [[trivial]], [[insignificant]], [[little]], [[petty]], [[trifling]], [[negligible]]
**3** mean, ungenerous, or [[petty]]: a small mind
**4** [[modest]] or [[humble]]: small beginnings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[modest]], [[humble]], [[unpretentious]]
**5** **feel small** to be humiliated
**6** (of a child or animal) [[young]]; not mature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[young]], [[little]], [[junior]], [[wee]], [[juvenile]], [[youthful]], [[immature]]
**7** [[unimportant]] or [[trivial]]: a small matter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unimportant]], [[minor]], [[trivial]], [[insignificant]], [[little]], [[petty]], [[trifling]], [[negligible]]
**8** (of a letter) written or printed in lower case rather than as a capital
 *adv*
**9** into small pieces: cut it small
 *n*
**10** the small narrow part of the back
