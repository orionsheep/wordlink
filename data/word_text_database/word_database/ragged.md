**ragged** (**rag**-gid) *adj*
**1** dressed in [[shabby]] or [[torn]] clothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tatty]] (*Brit*), [[worn]], [[torn]], [[rundown]], [[shabby]], [[seedy]], [[scruffy]], [[in tatters]]
**2** (of clothes) tattered and [[torn]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tatty]] (*Brit*), [[worn]], [[torn]], [[rundown]], [[shabby]], [[seedy]], [[scruffy]], [[in tatters]]
**3** having a [[rough]] or [[uneven]] surface or edge
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rough]], [[rugged]], [[unfinished]], [[uneven]], [[jagged]], [[serrated]]
**4** neglected or untidy: the ragged stone-built village
