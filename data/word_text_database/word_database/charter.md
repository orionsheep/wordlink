**charter** *n*
**1** a formal [[document]] granting or demanding certain rights or liberties: a children's charter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[document]], [[contract]], [[permit]], [[licence]], [[deed]], [[prerogative]]
**2** the fundamental principles of an organization: the UN Charter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[constitution]], [[laws]], [[rules]], [[code]]
**3** the [[hire]] or [[lease]] of transportation for private use
 *vb*
**4** to [[lease]] or [[hire]] by charter
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hire]], [[commission]], [[employ]], [[rent]], [[lease]]
**5** to grant a charter to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[authorize]], [[permit]], [[sanction]], [[entitle]], [[license]], [[empower]], [[give authority]]
