**climate** *n*

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weather]], [[temperature]]
**2** an area with a particular kind of climate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weather]], [[temperature]]
**3** a prevailing trend: the current economic climate

 **[[climatically]]** *adv*
