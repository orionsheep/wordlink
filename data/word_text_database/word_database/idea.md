**idea** *n*
**1** any product of mental activity; [[thought]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[notion]], [[thought]], [[view]], [[teaching]], [[opinion]], [[belief]], [[conclusion]], [[hypothesis]]
**2** a scheme, [[intention]], or [[plan]]
**3** the [[thought]] of something: the idea excites me
**4** a [[belief]] or [[opinion]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[notion]], [[thought]], [[view]], [[teaching]], [[opinion]], [[belief]], [[conclusion]], [[hypothesis]]
**5** a vague [[notion]]; inkling: they had no idea of the severity of my injuries
**6** a person's conception of something: his idea of integrity is not the same as mine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[understanding]], [[thought]], [[view]], [[opinion]], [[concept]], [[impression]], [[perception]]
**7** [[aim]] or [[purpose]]: the idea is to economize on transport
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intention]], [[aim]], [[purpose]], [[object]], [[plan]], [[objective]]
**8** (*philosophy*) (in Plato) a universal model of which all things in the same class are only imperfect imitations
