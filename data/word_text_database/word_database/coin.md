**coin** *n*
**1** a metal disc used as [[money]]
**2** metal currency collectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[money]], [[change]], [[cash]], [[silver]], [[copper]], [[specie]], kembla (*Austral* *slang*)
 *vb*
**3** to [[invent]] (a new word or phrase)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[invent]], [[create]], [[make up]], [[forge]], [[originate]], [[fabricate]]
**4** to make or stamp (coins)
**5** **coin it in** *or ***coin [[money]]** (*informal*) to make [[money]] rapidly
