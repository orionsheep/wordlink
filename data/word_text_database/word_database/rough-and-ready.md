**rough-and-ready** *adj*
**1** hastily prepared but adequate for the purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[rough and ready]]**: makeshift, crude, provisional, improvised, sketchy, stopgap
**2** (of a person) without formality or refinement in manner or appearance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[rough and ready]]**: unrefined, shabby, untidy, unkempt, unpolished, ill-groomed, daggy (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*)
