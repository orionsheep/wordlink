**radiate** *vb* **-ating**, **-ated**
**1** to [[spread out]] from a central point
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread out]], [[diverge]], [[branch out]]
**2** to [[show]] (an emotion or quality) to a great degree: she radiated competence and composure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[display]], [[demonstrate]], [[exhibit]], [[emanate]], give off *or* out
**3** to emit or be emitted as radiation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shine]], be diffused
 *adj*
**4** having rays or a radial structure
