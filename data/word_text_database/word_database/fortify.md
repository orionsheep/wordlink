**fortify** *vb* **-fies**, **-fying**, **-fied**
**1** to make (a place) defensible, such as by building walls
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[protect]], [[defend]], [[strengthen]], [[reinforce]], [[support]], [[shore up]], [[augment]], [[buttress]]
**2** to [[strengthen]] physically, mentally, or morally: the news fortified their resolve to succeed
**3** to increase the nutritious value of (a food), such as by adding vitamins
