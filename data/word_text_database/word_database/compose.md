**compose** *vb* **-posing**, **-posed**
**1** to [[put together]] or [[make up]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[arrange]], [[make up]], [[construct]], [[put together]], [[order]], [[organize]]
**2** to be the component elements of
**3** to [[create]] (a musical or literary work)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[create]], [[write]], [[produce]], [[invent]], [[devise]], [[contrive]]
**4** **compose oneself** to calm (oneself)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[compose yourself]]**: calm yourself, control yourself, collect yourself, pull yourself together
**5** to [[arrange]] artistically
**6** (*printing*) to set up (type)
