**false** *adj*
**1** not in accordance with the truth or facts: false allegations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incorrect]], [[wrong]], [[mistaken]], [[misleading]], [[faulty]], [[inaccurate]], [[invalid]], [[erroneous]]
**2** not real or genuine but intended to seem so: false teeth
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[artificial]], [[forged]], [[fake]], [[reproduction]], [[replica]], [[imitation]], [[bogus]], [[simulated]]
**3** [[misleading]] or deceptive: their false promises
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[untrue]], [[fraudulent]], [[trumped up]], [[fallacious]], [[untruthful]]
**4** forced or insincere: false cheer
**5** based on [[mistaken]] ideas
 **[[falsely]]** *adv* 
 **[[falseness]]** *n*
