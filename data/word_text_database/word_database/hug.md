**hug** *vb* **hugging**, **hugged**

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[embrace]], [[cuddle]], [[squeeze]], [[clasp]], [[enfold]], [[hold close]], [[take in your arms]]
**2** to keep close to (a shore or the kerb)
 *n*
**3** a tight or fond [[embrace]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[embrace]], [[squeeze]], [[bear hug]], [[clinch]] (*slang*), [[clasp]]
