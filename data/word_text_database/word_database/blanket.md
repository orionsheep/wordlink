**blanket** *n*
**1** a large piece of thick cloth for use as a bed [[covering]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cover]], [[rug]], [[coverlet]]
**2** a concealing [[cover]], as of smoke, leaves, or snow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[covering]], [[sheet]], [[coat]], [[layer]], [[carpet]], [[cloak]], [[mantle]], thickness
 *adj*
**3** applying to or [[covering]] a wide group or variety of people, conditions, situations, etc.: a blanket ban on all supporters travelling to away matches
 *vb* **-keting**, **-keted**
**4** to [[cover]] as if with a blanket
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[coat]], [[cover]], [[hide]], [[mask]], [[conceal]], [[obscure]], [[cloak]]
**5** to [[cover]] a wide area of; give blanket coverage to
