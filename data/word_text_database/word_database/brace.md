**brace** *n*
**1** something that steadies, binds, or holds up another thing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[stay]], [[prop]], [[bolster]], [[bracket]], [[reinforcement]], [[strut]], [[truss]]
**2** a beam or [[prop]], used to stiffen a framework
**3** a hand tool for drilling holes
**4** a pair, esp. of game birds
**5** either of a pair of characters, { }, used for connecting lines of printing or writing
 *vb* **bracing**, **braced**
**6** to [[steady]] or prepare (oneself) before an impact
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[steady]], [[support]], [[secure]], [[stabilize]]
**7** to provide, [[strengthen]], or fit with a brace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[support]], [[strengthen]], [[steady]], [[reinforce]], [[bolster]], [[fortify]], [[buttress]]
