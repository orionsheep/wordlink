**creek** *n*
**1** a narrow [[inlet]] or [[bay]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inlet]], [[bay]], [[cove]], [[bight]], firth *or* frith (*Scot*)
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*) a small [[stream]] or [[tributary]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stream]], [[brook]], [[tributary]], [[bayou]], [[rivulet]], [[watercourse]], [[runnel]]
**3** **up the creek** (*slang*) in a difficult position
