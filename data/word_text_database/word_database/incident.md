**incident** *n*
**1** an occurrence or [[event]], esp. a minor one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[happening]], [[event]], [[affair]], [[business]], [[fact]], [[matter]], [[occasion]], [[episode]]
**2** a relatively insignificant [[event]] that might have serious consequences
**3** a public [[disturbance]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disturbance]], [[scene]], [[clash]], [[disorder]], [[confrontation]], [[brawl]], [[fracas]], commotion
 *adj*
**4** (*physics*) (of a beam of light or particles) arriving at or striking a surface
**5** **incident to** (*formal*) likely to occur in connection with: the dangers are incident to a police officer's job
