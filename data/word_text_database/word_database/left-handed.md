**left-handed** *adj*
**1** better at using the left hand than the right
**2** done with the left hand
**3** designed for use by the left hand
**4** awkward or clumsy
**5** ambiguous or insincere: a left-handed compliment
**6** turning from right to left; anticlockwise
 *adv*
**7** with the left hand: I write left-handed
 **[[left-hander]]** *n*
