**frost** *n*
**1** a white deposit of ice particles
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hoarfrost]], [[freeze]], [[rime]]
**2** an atmospheric temperature of below freezing point, producing this deposit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hoarfrost]], [[freeze]], [[rime]]
 *vb*
**3** to cover with frost
**4** to kill or damage (plants) with frost
