**expand** *vb*
**1** to make or become greater in extent, size, or scope
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get bigger]], [[increase]], [[grow]], [[extend]], [[swell]], [[widen]], [[enlarge]], [[become bigger]]
**2** to [[spread]] out; [[unfold]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[spread]] (out), [[stretch]] (out), [[unfold]], [[unravel]], [[diffuse]], [[unfurl]], [[unroll]]
**3** **expand on** to go into more detail about (a story or subject)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[expand on something]]**: go into detail about, embellish, elaborate on, develop, flesh out, expound on, [[enlarge]] on, expatiate on
**4** to become increasingly relaxed, friendly, and talkative
**5** (*maths*) to express a function or expression as the sum or product of terms
 **[[expandable]]** *adj*
