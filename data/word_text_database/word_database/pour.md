**pour** *vb*
**1** to flow or cause to flow out in a [[stream]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[let flow]], [[spill]], [[splash]], [[dribble]], [[drizzle]], [[slop]] (*informal*), [[slosh]] (*informal*), [[decant]]
**2** to [[rain]] heavily
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rain]], [[pelt]] (down), [[teem]], [[bucket down]] (*informal*)
**3** to be given or obtained in large amounts: suggestions are pouring in
**4** to move together in large numbers: the fans poured onto the pitch
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stream]], [[crowd]], [[flood]], [[swarm]], [[gush]], [[throng]], [[teem]]
