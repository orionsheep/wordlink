**outlaw** *n*
**1** (*history*) a [[criminal]] who has been deprived of legal protection and rights
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bandit]], [[criminal]], [[thief]], [[robber]], [[fugitive]], [[outcast]], [[felon]], highwayman *or* woman
 *vb*
**2** to make (something) illegal: racial discrimination was formally [[outlawed]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ban]], [[bar]], [[veto]], [[forbid]], [[exclude]], [[prohibit]], [[disallow]], [[proscribe]]
**3** (*history*) to make (someone) an outlaw
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[banish]], put a price on (someone's) head
 **[[outlawed]]** *adj*
