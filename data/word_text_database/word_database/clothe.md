**clothe** *vb* **clothing**, **clothed*** or ***clad**
**1** to put clothes on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dress]], [[array]], [[robe]], [[drape]], [[swathe]], [[attire]], [[fit out]], [[garb]]
**2** to provide with clothes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dress]], [[array]], [[robe]], [[drape]], [[swathe]], [[attire]], [[fit out]], [[garb]]
**3** to cover or envelop (something) so as to change its appearance: a small valley clothed in thick woodland
