**multiple** *adj*
**1** having or involving more than one part, individual, or element
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[many]], [[several]], [[various]], [[numerous]], [[sundry]], [[manifold]] (*formal*), multitudinous
 *n*
**2** a number or polynomial which can be divided by another specified one an exact number of times: 6 is a multiple of 2
 **[[multiply]]** *adv*
