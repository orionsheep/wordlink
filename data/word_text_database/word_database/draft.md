**draft** *n*
**1** a preliminary [[outline]] of a letter, book, or speech
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[plan]], [[sketch]], [[version]], [[rough]], [[abstract]]
**2** a written order for payment of money by a bank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[money order]], [[bill]] (of exchange), [[cheque]], [[postal order]]
**3** (*US** & **Austral*) selection for compulsory military service
 *vb*
**4** to [[write]] a preliminary [[outline]] of a letter, book, or speech
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outline]], [[write]], [[plan]], [[produce]], [[create]], [[design]], [[draw]], [[compose]]
**5** to send (personnel) from one place to another to carry out a specific job
**6** (*chiefly* *US*) to select for compulsory military service
 *n*, *vb*
**7** (*US*) same as **draught**
