**screw** *n*
**1** a metal [[pin]] with a spiral ridge along its length, twisted into materials to [[fasten]] them together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nail]], [[pin]], [[tack]], [[rivet]], [[fastener]], [[spike]]
**2** a threaded cylindrical rod that engages with a similarly threaded cylindrical hole
**3** a thread in a cylindrical hole corresponding with the one on the screw with which it is designed to engage
**4** anything resembling a screw in shape
**5** (*slang*) a prison guard
**6** (*vulgar*, *slang*) an act of or partner in sexual intercourse
**7** **have a screw loose** (*informal*)
**A** to be eccentric
 **B** (*offensive*) to be insane
**8** **put the screws on** (*slang*) to use force on or threatening behaviour against
 *vb*
**9** to rotate (a screw or [[bolt]]) so as to drive it into or draw it out of a material
**10** to [[twist]] or [[turn]]: she screwed up the sheet of paper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[turn]], [[twist]], [[tighten]]
**11** to [[attach]] or [[fasten]] with or as if with a screw or screws
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fasten]], [[fix]], [[attach]], [[bolt]], [[clamp]], [[rivet]]
**12** (*informal*) to take advantage of, esp. illegally: screwed by big business
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cheat]], [[do]] (*slang*), rip (someone) off (*slang*), [[skin]] (*slang*), [[trick]], [[con]], [[sting]] (*informal*), [[fleece]]
**13** (*informal*) to distort or contort: his face was screwed up in pain
**14** (often foll. by *out of*) (*informal*) to force out of; extort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[squeeze]], [[wring]], [[extract]], [[wrest]]
**15** (*vulgar*, *slang*) to have sexual intercourse (with)
**16** **have one's head screwed on the right way** (*informal*) to be sensible
 See also **screw up**
