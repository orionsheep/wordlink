**screw up** *vb*
**1** (*informal*) to mishandle or spoil (something): that screws up all my arrangements
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[screw something up]]**: bungle, botch, mess up, spoil, mishandle, make a mess of (*slang*), make a hash of (*informal*), crool *or* cruel (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** to twist out of shape or distort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[screw something up]]**: contort, wrinkle, distort, pucker
**3** **screw up one's courage** to force oneself to be brave
 **[[screwed-up]]** *adj*
