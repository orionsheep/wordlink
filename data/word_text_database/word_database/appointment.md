**appointment** *n*
**1** an [[arrangement]] to meet a person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meeting]], [[interview]], [[date]], [[arrangement]], [[engagement]], [[fixture]], [[rendezvous]], [[assignation]]
**2** the act of placing someone in a [[job]] or [[position]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[selection]], [[naming]], [[election]], [[choice]], [[nomination]], [[assignment]]
**3** the person appointed
**4** the [[job]] or [[position]] to which a person is appointed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[office]], [[position]], [[post]], [[situation]], [[place]], [[employment]], [[assignment]]
**5** **appointments** fixtures or fittings
