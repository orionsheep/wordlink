**reflexive** *adj*
**1** (*grammar*) denoting a pronoun that refers back to the subject of a sentence or clause. Thus, in *that man thinks a great deal of himself*, the pronoun *himself* is reflexive
**2** (*grammar*) denoting a verb used with a reflexive pronoun as its direct object, as in *to dress oneself*
**3** (*physiol*) of or relating to a reflex
 *n*
**4** a reflexive pronoun or verb
