**suffice** (suf-**fice**) *vb* **-ficing**, **-ficed**
**1** to [[be enough]] or satisfactory for a purpose
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[be enough]], [[do]], [[be sufficient]], [[be adequate]], [[serve]], [[meet requirements]], [[tick all the boxes]]
**2** **suffice it to say ...** it is enough to say ...: suffice it to say that the overall result was very good
