**thanks** *pl n*
**1** an expression of [[appreciation]] or [[gratitude]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gratitude]], [[appreciation]], [[credit]], [[recognition]], [[acknowledgment]], [[gratefulness]]
**2** **[[thanks to]]** because of: the birth went very smoothly, [[thanks to]] the help of the GHQ medical officer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[thanks to]]**: because of, through, due to, as a result of, owing to
 *interj*
**3** (*informal*) an exclamation expressing [[gratitude]]
