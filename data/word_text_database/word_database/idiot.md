**idiot** *n*
**1** a foolish or senseless person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fool]], twit (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[chump]], [[halfwit]], [[galah]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), dorba *or* dorb (*Austral* *slang*), [[dill]] (*Austral** & **NZ* *informal*), [[mampara]] (*S African* *informal*)
**2** (*old-fashioned*, *offensive*) a person with severe learning difficulties
 **[[idiotic]]** *adj* 
 **[[idiotically]]** *adv*
