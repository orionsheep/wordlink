**migrate** *vb* **-grating**, **-grated**
**1** to go from one place to settle in another, esp. in a foreign country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[move]], [[travel]], [[journey]], [[wander]], [[trek]], [[voyage]], [[roam]], [[emigrate]]
**2** (of living creatures, esp. birds) to [[journey]] between different habitats at specific times of the year
 **[[migration]]** *n* 
 **[[migratory]]** *adj*
