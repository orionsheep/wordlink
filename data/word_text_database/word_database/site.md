**site** *n*
**1** the piece of ground where something was, is, or is intended to be located: a building site; a car park is to be built on the site of a Roman fort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[area]], [[plot]]
**2** same as **website**
 *vb* **siting**, **sited**
**3** to [[locate]] (something) on a specific site
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[locate]], [[put]], [[place]], [[set]], [[position]], [[establish]], [[install]], [[situate]]
