**component** *n*
**1** a [[constituent]] [[part]] or feature of a whole
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[part]], [[piece]], [[unit]], [[item]], [[element]], [[ingredient]], [[constituent]]
**2** (*maths*) one of a set of two or more vectors whose resultant is a given vector
 *adj*
**3** forming or functioning as a [[part]] or feature: over 60 component parts
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[constituent]], [[inherent]], [[intrinsic]]
