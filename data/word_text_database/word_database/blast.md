**blast** *n*
**1** an [[explosion]], such as that caused by dynamite
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[explosion]], [[crash]], [[burst]], [[discharge]], [[eruption]], [[detonation]]
**2** the charge used in a single [[explosion]]
**3** a sudden strong [[gust]] of wind or air
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gust]], [[rush]], [[storm]], [[breeze]], [[puff]], [[gale]], [[tempest]] (*literary*), [[squall]]
**4** a sudden loud sound, such as that made by a [[trumpet]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blare]], [[blow]], [[scream]], [[trumpet]], [[wail]], [[resound]], [[clamour]], [[toot]]
**5** a violent verbal outburst, esp. critical
**6** (*slang*) a very enjoyable or thrilling experience: the party was a blast
**7** **at full blast** at maximum speed, volume, etc.
 *interj*
**8** (*slang*) an exclamation of annoyance
 *vb*
**9** to [[blow up]] (a rock, tunnel, etc.) with explosives
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[blow up]], [[bomb]], [[destroy]], [[burst]], [[ruin]], [[break up]], [[explode]], [[shatter]]
**10** to make or cause to make a loud harsh noise
**11** to criticize severely
