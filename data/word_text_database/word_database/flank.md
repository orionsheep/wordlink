**flank** *n*
**1** the [[side]] of a man or animal between the ribs and the [[hip]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[side]], [[hip]], [[thigh]], [[loin]]
**2** a cut of beef from the flank
**3** the [[side]] of a naval or military formation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[wing]], [[side]], [[sector]], aspect
 *vb*
**4** to be positioned at the [[side]] of (a person or thing)
