**use** *vb* **using**, **used**
**1** to put into [[service]] or action; [[employ]] for a given [[purpose]]: use a garden fork to mix them together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[employ]], [[utilize]], [[work]], [[apply]], [[operate]], [[exercise]], [[practise]], [[resort to]]
**2** to choose or [[employ]] regularly: what sort of toothpaste do you use?
**3** to [[take advantage of]]; [[exploit]]: I used Jason and he used me
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take advantage of]], [[exploit]], [[manipulate]]
**4** to [[consume]] or expend: a manufacturing plant uses 1000 tonnes of steel a month
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consume]], [[exhaust]], [[spend]], [[run through]], expend
 *n*
**5** the act or fact of using or being used: large-scale use of pesticides
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[usage]], [[employment]], [[operation]], [[application]]
**6** the ability or permission to use
**7** need or opportunity to use: the Colombian government had no use for them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purpose]], [[end]], [[reason]], [[object]]
**8** usefulness or [[advantage]]: there is no use in complaining
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[good]], [[point]], [[help]], [[service]], [[value]], [[benefit]], [[profit]], [[advantage]]

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purpose]], [[end]], [[reason]], [[object]]
