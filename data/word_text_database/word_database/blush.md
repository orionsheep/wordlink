**blush** *vb*
**1** to become suddenly red in the face, esp. from embarrassment or shame
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[turn red]], [[colour]], [[glow]], [[flush]], [[redden]], [[go red]] (as a beetroot), turn scarlet
 *n*
**2** a sudden [[reddening]] of the face, esp. from embarrassment or shame
**3** a rosy [[glow]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reddening]], [[colour]], [[glow]], [[flush]], [[pink tinge]], [[rosiness]], [[ruddiness]], [[rosy tint]]
**4** same as **rosé**
