**sequel** *n*
**1** a film, novel, or play that continues the story of an earlier one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[follow-up]], [[continuation]], [[development]]
**2** anything that happens after or as a [[result]] of something else: there was an amusing sequel to this incident
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[consequence]], [[result]], [[outcome]], [[conclusion]], [[end]], [[upshot]]
