**elaborate** *adj*
**1** very [[complex]] because of having many different parts: elaborate equipment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complicated]], [[detailed]], [[studied]], [[complex]], [[precise]], [[thorough]], [[intricate]], [[painstaking]]
**2** having a very [[complicated]] design: elaborate embroidery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ornate]], [[involved]], [[complex]], [[fancy]], [[complicated]], [[intricate]], [[baroque]], ornamented
 *vb* **-rating**, **-rated**
**3** **elaborate on** to describe in more detail: he did not elaborate on his plans
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expand upon]], [[extend upon]], [[enlarge on]], [[amplify upon]], [[embellish]], [[flesh out]], [[add detail to]]
**4** to [[develop]] (a plan or theory) in detail
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[develop]], [[flesh out]]
 **[[elaborately]]** *adv* 
 **[[elaboration]]** *n*
