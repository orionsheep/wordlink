**respond** *vb*
**1** to state or utter (something) in [[reply]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[answer]], [[return]], [[reply]], [[counter]], [[retort]], [[rejoin]]
**2** to act in [[reply]]: the government must respond accordingly to our recommendations
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[react]], [[retaliate]], [[reciprocate]]
**3** to [[react]] favourably: most headaches will respond to the use of relaxants
