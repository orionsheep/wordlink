**intent** *n*
**1** something that is intended
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[intention]], [[aim]], [[purpose]], [[meaning]], [[end]], [[plan]], [[goal]], [[design]]
**2** (*law*) the will or [[purpose]] to commit a crime: loitering with intent
**3** **to all intents and purposes** in almost every respect; virtually
 *adj*
**4** having one's attention firmly fixed: an intent look
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[absorbed]], [[intense]], [[fascinated]], [[preoccupied]], [[enthralled]], [[attentive]], [[watchful]], [[engrossed]]
**5** **intent on *or* upon** strongly resolved on: intent on winning the election
 **[[intently]]** *adv* 
 **[[intentness]]** *n*
