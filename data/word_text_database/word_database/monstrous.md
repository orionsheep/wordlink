**monstrous** *adj*
**1** [[hideous]] or [[unnatural]] in size or character
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unnatural]], [[horrible]], [[hideous]], [[grotesque]], [[gruesome]], [[frightful]], [[freakish]], [[fiendish]]
**2** atrocious, unjust, or [[shocking]]: the President described the invasion as monstrous
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[outrageous]], [[shocking]], [[foul]], [[intolerable]], [[disgraceful]], [[scandalous]], [[inhuman]], [[diabolical]]
**3** [[huge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[huge]], [[massive]], [[enormous]], [[tremendous]], [[immense]], [[mammoth]], [[colossal]], [[prodigious]]
**4** of or like a monster
**5** (of plants and animals) abnormal in structure
 **[[monstrously]]** *adv*
