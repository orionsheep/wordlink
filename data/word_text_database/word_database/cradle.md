**cradle** *n*
**1** a baby's bed on rockers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crib]], [[cot]], [[Moses basket]], [[bassinet]]
**2** a place where something originates: the cradle of civilization
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[birthplace]], [[beginning]], [[source]], [[spring]], [[origin]], [[fount]], [[fountainhead]], [[wellspring]]
**3** a supporting framework or structure
**4** a platform or trolley in which workmen are suspended on the side of a building or ship
 *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**5** to [[hold]] gently as if in a cradle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hold]], [[support]], [[rock]], [[nurse]], [[nestle]]
