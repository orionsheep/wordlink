**villain** *n*
**1** a wicked or evil person
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[evildoer]], [[criminal]], [[rogue]], [[scoundrel]] (*old-fashioned*), [[wretch]], [[reprobate]], [[miscreant]], [[blackguard]], [[wrong 'un]] (*slang*)
**2** the main wicked character in a novel or play
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[baddy]] (*informal*), [[antihero]]
