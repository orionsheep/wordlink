**abuse** *n*
**1** prolonged [[ill-treatment]] of or violence towards someone: child abuse
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[maltreatment]], [[damage]], [[injury]], [[hurt]], [[harm]], [[manhandling]], [[ill-treatment]]
**2** insulting comments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insults]], [[blame]], [[slights]], [[put-downs]], [[censure]], [[reproach]], [[scolding]], [[defamation]]
**3** improper use: an abuse of power
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[misuse]], [[misapplication]], exploitation
 *vb* **abusing**, **[[abused]]**
**4** to take advantage of dishonestly: these two ministers had [[abused]] their position for financial gain
**5** to [[ill-treat]] violently or sexually
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ill-treat]], [[damage]], [[hurt]], [[injure]], [[harm]], [[molest]], [[maltreat]], knock about *or* around
**6** to speak insultingly or cruelly to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insult]], [[offend]], [[curse]], [[put down]], [[malign]], [[scold]], [[disparage]], [[castigate]]
