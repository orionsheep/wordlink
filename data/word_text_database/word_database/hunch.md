**hunch** *n*
**1** a [[feeling]] or [[suspicion]] not based on facts: she said that she had had a hunch that the coup would not succeed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feeling]], [[idea]], [[impression]], [[suspicion]], [[intuition]], [[premonition]], [[inkling]], [[presentiment]]
**2** same as **hump**
 *vb*
**3** to draw (oneself or one's shoulders) up or together
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crouch]], [[bend]], [[curve]], [[arch]], [[draw in]]
