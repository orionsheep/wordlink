**devotee** (dev-vote-**tee**) *n*
**1** a person fanatically enthusiastic about a subject or activity
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enthusiast]], [[fan]], [[supporter]], [[follower]], [[admirer]], [[buff]] (*informal*), [[fanatic]], [[adherent]], [[fanboy]] (*informal*), [[fangirl]] (*informal*)
**2** a zealous [[follower]] of a religion
