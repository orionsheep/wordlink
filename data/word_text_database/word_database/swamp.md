**swamp** *n*
**1** an area of permanently waterlogged land; [[bog]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bog]], [[marsh]], [[quagmire]], [[slough]], [[fen]], [[mire]], [[morass]], [[pakihi]] (*NZ*), muskeg (*Canad*)
 *vb*
**2** (*naut*) to cause (a boat) to sink or fill with water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[flood]], [[engulf]], [[submerge]], [[inundate]]
**3** to [[overwhelm]] (a person or place) with more than can be dealt with or accommodated
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[overload]], [[overwhelm]], [[inundate]]
 **[[swampy]]** *adj*
