**endorse** *vb* **-dorsing**, **-dorsed**
**1** to give approval or [[support]] to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[approve]], [[back]], [[support]], [[champion]], [[promote]], [[recommend]], [[advocate]], [[uphold]]
**2** to [[sign]] the [[back]] of (a cheque) to specify the payee
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sign]], [[initial]], [[countersign]], [[sign on the back of]]
**3** (*chiefly* *Brit*) to record a conviction on (a driving licence)
 **[[endorsement]]** *n*
