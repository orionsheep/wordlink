**security** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** [[precautions]] taken to ensure against theft, espionage, or other danger
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[precautions]], [[defence]], [[safeguards]], [[protection]], [[safety measures]]
**2** the state of being free from danger, damage, or worry
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[assurance]], [[confidence]], [[conviction]], [[certainty]], [[reliance]], [[sureness]], [[positiveness]]
**3** assured freedom from poverty: the security of a weekly pay cheque
**4** a certificate of ownership, such as a share, stock, or bond
**5** something given or pledged to [[guarantee]] payment of a loan
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pledge]], [[insurance]], [[guarantee]], [[hostage]], [[collateral]], [[pawn]], [[gage]], [[surety]]
