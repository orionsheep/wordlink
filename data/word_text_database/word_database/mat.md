**mat** *n*
**1** a thick flat piece of fabric used as a floor covering, a place to wipe one's shoes, etc.
**2** a small pad of material used to protect a surface from heat or scratches from an object placed upon it
**3** a large piece of thick padded material put on the floor as a surface for wrestling, gymnastics, etc.
 *vb* **matting**, **matted**
**4** to tangle or become tangled into a dense mass
