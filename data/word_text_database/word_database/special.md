**special** *adj*
**1** distinguished from or better than others of its kind: a special occasion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exceptional]], [[important]], [[significant]], [[particular]], [[unique]], [[unusual]], [[extraordinary]], [[memorable]]
**2** designed or reserved for a [[specific]] purpose: special equipment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[specific]], [[particular]], [[distinctive]], [[individual]], [[appropriate]], [[precise]]
**3** not usual; different from normal: a special case
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[exceptional]], [[important]], [[significant]], [[particular]], [[unique]], [[unusual]], [[extraordinary]], [[memorable]]
**4** [[particular]] or primary: a special interest in gifted children
**5** relating to the education of children with disabilities: a special school
 *n*
**6** a product, TV programme, etc., which is only available or shown at a certain time: a two-hour Christmas special live from Hollywood
**7** a meal, usually at a low price, in a bar or restaurant
 **[[specially]]** *adv*
