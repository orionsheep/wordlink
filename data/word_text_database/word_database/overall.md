**overall** *adj*
**1** from one end to the other: the overall length
**2** including everything; [[total]]: the overall cost
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[total]], [[full]], [[whole]], [[general]], [[complete]], [[entire]], [[global]], comprehensive
 *adv*
**3** [[in general]]; [[on the whole]]: overall, I think this is the better car
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[in general]], [[generally]], [[mostly]], [[all things considered]], [[on average]], [[on the whole]], [[predominantly]], in the main
 *n*
**4** (*Brit** & **NZ*) a coat-shaped work garment worn over ordinary clothes as a protection against dirt
**5** **overalls** work trousers with a bib and braces or jacket attached, worn over ordinary clothes as a protection against dirt and wear
