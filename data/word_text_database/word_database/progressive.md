**progressive** *adj*
**1** favouring political or social reform
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enlightened]], [[liberal]], [[modern]], [[advanced]], [[radical]], [[revolutionary]], [[avant-garde]], [[reformist]]
**2** happening gradually: a progressive illness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[growing]], [[continuing]], [[increasing]], [[developing]], [[advancing]], [[ongoing]]
**3** (of a dance, card game, etc.) involving a regular change of partners
 *n*
**4** a person who favours political or social reform
 **[[progressively]]** *adv*
