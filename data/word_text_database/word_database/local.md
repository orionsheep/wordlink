**local** *adj*
**1** of or concerning a particular area
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[community]], [[regional]]
**2** [[restricted]] to a particular place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confined]], [[limited]], [[restricted]]
**3** (*med*) of, affecting, or [[confined]] to a [[limited]] area or part: a local anaesthetic
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[confined]], [[limited]], [[restricted]]
**4** (of a train or bus) stopping at all stations or stops
 *n*
**5** an [[inhabitant]] of a specified locality: we swim, sunbathe, meet the locals, unwind
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[resident]], [[native]], [[inhabitant]]
**6** (*Brit* *informal*) a pub close to one's home
 **[[locally]]** *adv*
