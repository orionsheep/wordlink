**ferret** *n*
**1** a small yellowish-white animal related to the weasel and bred for hunting rats and rabbits
 *vb* **-reting**, **-reted**
**2** to hunt rabbits or rats with ferrets
**3** to search around
**4** **ferret out**
**A** to drive from hiding
 **B** to find by determined investigation: she could ferret out little knowledge of his background
