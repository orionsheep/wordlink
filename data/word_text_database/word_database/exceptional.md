◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unusual]], [[special]], [[odd]], [[strange]], [[extraordinary]], [[unprecedented]], [[peculiar]], [[abnormal]]
**2** having much more than average intelligence, ability, or skill

 **[[exceptionally]]** *adv*
