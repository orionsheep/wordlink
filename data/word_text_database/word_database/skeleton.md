**skeleton** *n*
**1** the hard framework of [[bones]] that supports and protects the organs and muscles of the body
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bones]], [[bare bones]]
**2** the essential framework of any structure: a metal skeleton supporting the roof and floors
**3** (*informal*) an extremely thin person or animal
**4** an outline consisting of bare essentials: the mere skeleton of a script
**5** a small steel-frame sledge for racing down an ice-covered run
**6** the sport of racing small steel-frame sledges down an ice-covered run
**7** **skeleton in the cupboard *or* closet** an embarrassing or scandalous fact from the past that is kept secret
 *adj*
**8** reduced to a minimum: a skeleton staff
 **[[skeletal]]** *adj*
