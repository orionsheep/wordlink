**coarse** *adj*
**1** [[rough]] in texture or structure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rough]], [[crude]], [[unfinished]], [[homespun]], [[impure]], [[unrefined]], [[unprocessed]], [[unpolished]]
**2** [[unrefined]] or [[indecent]]: coarse humour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[vulgar]], [[rude]], [[indecent]], [[improper]], [[earthy]], [[smutty]], [[ribald]], [[indelicate]]
**3** of inferior quality
 **[[coarsely]]** *adv* 
 **[[coarseness]]** *n*
