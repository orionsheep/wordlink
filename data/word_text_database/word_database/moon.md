**moon** *n*
**1** the natural [[satellite]] of the earth. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**lunar**
**2** this [[satellite]] as it is seen during its revolution around the earth, esp. at one of its phases: new moon; full moon
**3** any natural [[satellite]] of a planet
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[satellite]]
**4** a month
**5** **over the moon** (*informal*) extremely happy; ecstatic
 *vb*
**6** **moon about *or* around** to be [[idle]] in a listless or dreamy way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[idle]], [[drift]], [[loaf]], [[languish]], [[waste time]], [[daydream]], [[mope]]
 **[[moonless]]** *adj*
