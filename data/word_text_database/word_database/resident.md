**resident** *n*
**1** a person who lives in a place
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tenant]], [[occupant]], [[lodger]]
**2** a bird or animal that does not migrate
 *adj*
**3** living in a place
**4** living at a place in order to carry out a job: a resident custodian
**5** employed for one's specialized abilities: the Museum's resident expert on seventeenth-century Dutch art
**6** (of birds and animals) not in the habit of migrating
