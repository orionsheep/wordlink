**complain** *vb*
**1** to express resentment or displeasure
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[find fault]], [[moan]] (*informal*), [[grumble]], [[whinge]] (*informal*), [[carp]], [[groan]], [[lament]], [[whine]], [[nit-pick]] (*informal*)
**2** **complain of** to state that one is suffering from (a pain or illness): she complained of breathing trouble and chest pains
**3** to make a formal protest: he complained to the police about his rowdy neighbours
