**minor** *adj*
**1** [[lesser]] or secondary in size, frequency, or importance than others of the same kind: a minor poet
**2** not very serious or significant: minor injuries
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[small]], [[lesser]], [[slight]], [[petty]], [[trivial]], [[insignificant]], [[unimportant]], [[inconsequential]]
**3** (*music*)
**A** (of a scale) having a semitone between the second and third and fifth and sixth notes (**natural minor**)
 **B** of or based on the minor scale: his quintet in C minor; a minor third
 *n*
**4** a person below the age of legal majority
**5** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral*, *education*) a subsidiary subject
**6** (*music*) a minor key, chord, mode, or scale
 *vb*
**7** **minor in** (*US** & **Canad*, *education*) to study as a subsidiary subject: to minor in politics
