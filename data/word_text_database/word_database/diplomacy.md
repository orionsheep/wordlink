**diplomacy** *n*
**1** the conduct of the relations between nations by peaceful means
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[statesmanship]], [[statecraft]], [[international negotiation]]
**2** [[skill]] in the management of international relations
**3** [[tact]] or [[skill]] in dealing with people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tact]], [[skill]], [[sensitivity]], [[craft]], [[discretion]], [[subtlety]], [[delicacy]], [[finesse]]
