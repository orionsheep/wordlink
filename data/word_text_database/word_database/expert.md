◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[specialist]], [[authority]], [[professional]], [[master]], [[genius]], [[guru]], [[pundit]], [[maestro]], [[fundi]] (*S African*), geek (*informal*)
 *adj*
**2** [[skilful]] or knowledgeable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[skilful]], [[experienced]], [[professional]], [[masterly]], [[qualified]], [[talented]], [[outstanding]], [[practised]], leet (*computers*, *slang*)
**3** of, involving, or done by an expert
 **[[expertly]]** *adv*
