**eloquent** *adj*
**1** (of speech or writing) fluent and [[persuasive]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[silver-tongued]], [[moving]], [[powerful]], [[effective]], [[stirring]], [[articulate]], [[persuasive]], [[forceful]]
**2** (of a person) able to speak in a fluent and [[persuasive]] manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[silver-tongued]], [[moving]], [[powerful]], [[effective]], [[stirring]], [[articulate]], [[persuasive]], [[forceful]]
**3** visibly or vividly [[expressive]]: he raised an eloquent eyebrow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[expressive]], [[telling]], [[pointed]], [[significant]], [[vivid]], [[meaningful]], [[indicative]], [[suggestive]]
 **[[eloquently]]** *adv*
