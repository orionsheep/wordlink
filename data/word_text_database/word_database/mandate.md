**mandate** *n*
**1** an official or authoritative [[command]] to carry out a particular task: the UN force's mandate does not allow it to intervene
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[command]], [[order]], [[commission]], [[instruction]], [[decree]], [[directive]], [[edict]]
**2** (*politics*) the political authority given to a government or an elected representative through an electoral victory
**3** Also: **mandated territory** (formerly) a territory administered by one country on behalf of an international body
 *vb* **-dating**, **-dated**
**4** to delegate authority to
**5** to assign territory to a nation under a mandate
