**brood** *n*
**1** a number of young animals, esp. birds, produced at one hatching
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[offspring]], [[issue]], [[clutch]], [[litter]], [[progeny]]
**2** all the [[children]] in a [[family]]: often used jokingly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[children]], [[family]], [[nearest and dearest]], [[flesh and blood]], ainga (*NZ*)
 *vb*
**3** (of a bird) to sit on or hatch eggs
**4** to [[think]] long and unhappily about something: he brooded on his failure to avert the confrontation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[think]], [[obsess]], [[muse]], [[ponder]], [[agonize]], [[mull over]], [[mope]], [[ruminate]]
 **[[brooding]]** *n*, *adj*
