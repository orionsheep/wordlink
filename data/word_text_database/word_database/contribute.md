**contribute** *vb* **-buting**, **-buted** (often foll. by *to*)
**1** to [[give]] (support or money) for a common purpose or fund
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[give]], [[provide]], [[supply]], [[donate]], [[subscribe]], [[chip in]] (*informal*), [[bestow]]
**2** to [[supply]] (ideas or opinions)
**3** **contribute to** to be partly responsible for: his own unconvincing play contributed to his defeat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[contribute to something]]**: be partly responsible for, lead to, be instrumental in, be conducive to, help
**4** to write (an article) for a publication
 **[[contribution]]** *n* 
 **[[contributory]]** *adj*
