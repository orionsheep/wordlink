**physical** *adj*
**1** of the body, as distinguished from the mind or spirit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[corporal]], [[fleshly]], [[bodily]], [[corporeal]]
**2** of [[material]] things or nature: the physical world
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[earthly]], [[fleshly]], [[mortal]], [[incarnate]]
**3** of or concerned with matter and energy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[material]], [[real]], [[substantial]] (*formal*), [[natural]], [[solid]], [[tangible]], [[palpable]]
**4** of or relating to physics
 **[[physically]]** *adv*
