**succession** *n*
**1** a number of people or things following one another in [[order]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[series]], [[run]], [[sequence]], [[course]], [[order]], [[train]], [[chain]], [[cycle]]
**2** the act or right by which one person succeeds another in a position
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[taking over]], [[assumption]], [[inheritance]], [[accession]]
**3** **in succession** one after another: the third time in succession
