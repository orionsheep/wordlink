**end** *n*
**1** one of the two [[extreme]] points of something such as a road
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extremity]], [[limit]], [[edge]], [[border]], [[extent]], [[extreme]], [[margin]], [[boundary]]
**2** the surface at one of the two [[extreme]] points of an [[object]]: a pencil with a rubber at one end
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[tip]], [[point]], [[head]], [[peak]], [[extremity]]
**3** the [[extreme]] [[extent]] or [[limit]] of something: the end of the runway
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extremity]], [[limit]], [[edge]], [[border]], [[extent]], [[extreme]], [[margin]], [[boundary]]
**4** the most distant place or time that can be imagined: the ends of the earth
**5** the act or an instance of stopping doing something or stopping something from continuing: I want to put an end to all the gossip
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[close]], [[ending]], [[finish]], [[expiry]], [[expiration]]
**6** the last part of something: at the end of the story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[conclusion]], [[ending]], [[climax]], [[completion]], [[finale]], [[culmination]], [[denouement]], [[consummation]]
**7** a [[remnant]] or [[fragment]]: cigarette ends
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remnant]], [[butt]], [[stub]], [[scrap]], [[fragment]], [[stump]], [[remainder]], [[leftover]]
**8** [[death]] or [[destruction]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[death]], [[dying]], [[ruin]], [[destruction]], [[passing on]], [[doom]], [[demise]] (*euphemistic*), [[extinction]]
**9** the [[purpose]] of an action: he will only use you to achieve his own ends
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[purpose]], [[point]], [[reason]], [[goal]], [[target]], [[aim]], [[object]], [[mission]]
**10** (*sport*) either of the two defended areas of a playing field
**11** **in the end** finally
**12** **make ends meet** to have just enough money to meet one's needs
**13** **no end** used for emphasis: these moments give me no end of trouble
**14** **on end** (*informal*) without pause or interruption: for months on end
**15** **the end** (*slang*) the worst, esp. beyond the limits of endurance
 *vb*
**16** to bring or come to a [[finish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stop]], [[finish]], [[halt]], [[cease]], [[wind up]], [[terminate]], [[call off]], [[discontinue]]
**17** **end it all** (*informal*) to die by suicide
