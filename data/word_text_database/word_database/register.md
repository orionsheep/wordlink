**register** *n*
**1** an official [[list]] recording names, events, or transactions
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[record]], [[roll]], [[file]], [[diary]], [[catalogue]], [[log]], [[archives]]
**2** the book in which such a [[list]] is written
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[list]], [[record]], [[roll]], [[file]], [[diary]], [[catalogue]], [[log]], [[archives]]
**3** a device that records data, totals sums of money, etc.: a cash register
**4** a style of speaking or writing, such as slang, used in particular circumstances or social situations
**5** (*music*)
**A** the timbre characteristic of a certain manner of voice production
 **B** any of the stops on an organ in respect of its tonal quality: the flute register
 *vb*
**6** to [[enter]] (an event, person's name, ownership, etc.) in a register
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[enrol]], [[enlist]], [[list]], [[note]], [[enter]]
**7** to [[show]] on a scale or other measuring instrument
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[indicate]], [[show]]
**8** to [[show]] in a person's face or bearing: his face registered surprise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[mark]], [[indicate]], [[manifest]]
**9** (*informal*) to have an effect or make an impression: the news did not register at first
