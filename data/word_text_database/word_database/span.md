**span** *n*
**1** the interval or distance between two points, such as the ends of a [[bridge]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[term]], [[duration]], [[spell]]
**2** the complete extent: that span of time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[period]], [[term]], [[duration]], [[spell]]
**3** a unit of length based on the width of a stretched hand, usually taken as nine inches (23 centimetres)
 *vb* **spanning**, **spanned**
**4** to stretch or [[extend across]], over, or around: her career spanned fifty years; to span the Danube
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[extend across]], [[cross]], [[bridge]], [[cover]], [[link]], [[traverse]]
