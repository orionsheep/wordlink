**flurry** *n*, *pl* **-ries**
**1** a short rush of vigorous activity or movement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[commotion]], [[stir]], [[bustle]], [[flutter]], [[excitement]], [[fuss]], [[disturbance]], [[ado]]
**2** a light [[gust]] of wind or rain, or fall of snow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[gust]], [[shower]], [[gale]], [[swirl]], [[squall]], storm
 *vb* **-ries**, **-rying**, **-ried**
**3** to confuse or bewilder
