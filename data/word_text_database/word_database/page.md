**page**^2 *n*
**1** a small boy who attends a bride at a wedding
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[servant]], [[attendant]], [[squire]], [[pageboy]]
**2** a youth employed to run errands for the guests in a hotel or club
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attendant]], [[pageboy]]
**3** (*medieval history*) a boy in training for knighthood
 *vb* **paging**, **paged**
**4** to [[summon]] (a person), by bleeper or loudspeaker, in order to pass on a message
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[call]], [[summon]], [[send for]]
