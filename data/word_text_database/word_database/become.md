**become** *vb* **-coming**, **-came**, **-come**
**1** to [[come to be]]: she became Prime Minister in 2016
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[come to be]], [[develop into]], [[be transformed into]], [[grow into]], [[change into]], [[alter to]], [[mature into]], [[ripen into]]
**2** (foll. by *of*) to happen to: what became of him?
**3** to [[suit]]: that dress becomes you
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suit]], [[fit]], [[enhance]], [[flatter]], [[embellish]], [[set off]]
