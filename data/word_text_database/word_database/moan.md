**moan** *n*
**1** a low prolonged cry of pain or suffering
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[groan]], [[sigh]], [[sob]], [[lament]], [[wail]], [[grunt]], [[whine]]
**2** any similar sound, esp. that made by the wind
**3** (*informal*) a [[grumble]] or [[complaint]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[complaint]], [[protest]], [[grumble]], [[whine]], [[grouse]], [[gripe]] (*informal*), grouch (*informal*)
 *vb*
**4** to make a low cry of, or talk in a way suggesting, pain or suffering: he moaned in pain
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[groan]], [[sigh]], [[sob]], [[whine]], [[lament]]
**5** to make a sound like a moan: the wind moaned through the trees
**6** (*informal*) to [[grumble]] or [[complain]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[grumble]], [[complain]], [[groan]] (*informal*), [[whine]], [[carp]], [[grouse]], [[whinge]] (*informal*), [[bleat]]
 **[[moaner]]** *n*
