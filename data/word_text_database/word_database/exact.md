**exact** *adj*
**1** [[correct]] in every detail; strictly [[accurate]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accurate]], [[correct]], [[true]], [[right]], [[specific]], [[precise]], [[definite]], [[faultless]]
**2** [[precise]], as opposed to approximate
**3** based on measurement and the formulation of laws: forecasting floods is not an exact science
 *vb*
**4** to obtain or [[demand]] as a [[right]], esp. through [[force]] or strength: the rebels called for revenge to be exacted for the killings
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[demand]], [[claim]], [[force]], [[command]], [[extract]], [[compel]], [[extort]]
