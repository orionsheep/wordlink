**lean**^2 *adj*
**1** (esp. of a person) having a [[trim]] body with no surplus flesh
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thin]], [[slim]], [[slender]], [[skinny]], [[angular]], [[trim]], [[spare]], [[gaunt]]
**2** (of meat) having little or no fat
**3** (of a period) sparse, difficult, or causing hardship: we've been through some lean times in recent years
 *n*
**4** the part of meat that contains little or no fat
 **[[leanness]]** *n*
