**tinker** *n*
**1** (esp. formerly) a travelling mender of pots and pans
**2** (*Scot** & **Irish*, *usually* *derogatory*) a traveller
**3** a mischievous child
 *vb*
**4** **tinker with** to try to repair or improve (something) by making lots of minor adjustments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[meddle]], [[play]], [[potter]], [[fiddle]] (*informal*), [[dabble]], [[mess about]]
