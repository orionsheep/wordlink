**patron** *n*
**1** a person who financially supports artists, writers, musicians, or charities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[friend]], [[champion]], [[sponsor]], [[backer]], [[helper]], [[benefactor]], [[philanthropist]]
**2** a regular [[customer]] of a shop, hotel, etc.
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[customer]], [[client]], [[buyer]], [[frequenter]], [[shopper]], [[habitué]]
