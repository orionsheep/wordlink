**feeble** *adj*
**1** lacking in physical or mental strength
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[weak]], [[frail]], [[debilitated]], [[sickly]], [[puny]], [[weedy]] (*informal*), [[infirm]], [[effete]]
**2** not effective or convincing: feeble excuses for Scotland's latest defeat
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inadequate]], [[pathetic]], [[insufficient]], [[lame]]
 **[[feebly]]** *adv*
