**exert** *vb*
**1** to [[use]] influence, authority, etc. forcefully or effectively
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[apply]], [[use]], [[exercise]], [[employ]], [[wield]], [[make use of]], [[utilize]], [[bring to bear]]
**2** **exert oneself** to make a special effort
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[exert yourself]]**: make an effort, work, labour, struggle, strain, strive, endeavour, toil
