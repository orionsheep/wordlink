**2** to [[watch]] (something) carefully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[watch]], [[study]], [[view]], [[look at]], [[check]], [[survey]], [[monitor]], [[keep an eye on]] (*informal*)
**3** to make scientific examinations of

◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[remark]], [[say]], [[comment]], [[state]], [[note]], [[reflect]], [[mention]], [[opine]] (*formal*)
