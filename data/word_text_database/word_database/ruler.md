**ruler** *n*
**1** a person who rules or commands
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[governor]], [[leader]], [[lord]], [[commander]], [[controller]], [[monarch]], [[sovereign]], [[head of state]]
**2** a strip of wood, metal, or plastic, with straight edges, used for measuring and drawing straight lines
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[measure]], [[rule]], [[yardstick]]
