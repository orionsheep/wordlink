**mission** *n*
**1** a specific [[task]] or [[duty]] assigned to a person or group of people
**2** a [[task]] or [[duty]] that a person believes he or she must achieve; [[vocation]]: he felt it was his mission to pass on his knowledge to other people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[task]], [[job]], [[commission]], [[duty]], [[undertaking]], [[quest]], [[assignment]], [[vocation]]
**3** a group of people representing or working for a particular country or organization in a foreign country: the UN peacekeeping mission
**4** a group of people sent by a church to a foreign country to do religious and social work
**5** the place in which a church or government mission is based
**6** the dispatch of aircraft or spacecraft to achieve a particular [[task]]
**7** a charitable centre that offers shelter or aid to poor or needy people
**8** (*S African*) a long and difficult process
