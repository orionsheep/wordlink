**axe** * or * (*US*) **ax** *n*, *pl* **axes**
**1** a hand tool with one side of its head sharpened to a cutting edge, used for felling trees and splitting timber
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hatchet]], [[chopper]], [[tomahawk]], [[cleaver]], [[adze]]
**2** **an axe to grind** a favourite topic one wishes to promote
**3** (*informal*) a severe cut in spending or in the number of staff employed
 *vb* **axing**, **axed**
**4** (*informal*) to dismiss (employees), restrict (expenditure), or [[terminate]] (a project)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abandon]], [[end]], [[eliminate]], [[cancel]], [[scrap]], [[cut back]], [[terminate]], [[dispense with]]
