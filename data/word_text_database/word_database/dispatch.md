**dispatch** * or ***despatch** *vb*
**1** to [[send]] off to a destination or to [[perform]] a task
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[send]], [[consign]]
**2** to [[carry out]] (a duty or task) promptly
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[carry out]], [[perform]], [[fulfil]], [[effect]], [[finish]], [[achieve]], [[settle]], [[dismiss]]
**3** to [[murder]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kill]], [[murder]], [[destroy]], [[execute]], [[slaughter]], [[assassinate]], slay (*archaic*, *literary*), liquidate
 *n*
**4** an official [[communication]] or [[report]], sent in haste
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[message]], [[news]], [[report]], [[story]], [[account]], [[communication]], [[bulletin]], [[communiqué]]
**5** a [[report]] sent to a newspaper by a correspondent
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[message]], [[news]], [[report]], [[story]], [[account]], [[communication]], [[bulletin]], [[communiqué]]
**6** [[murder]]
