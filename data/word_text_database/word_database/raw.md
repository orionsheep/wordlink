**raw** *adj*
**1** (of food) not cooked
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[uncooked]], [[natural]], [[fresh]]
**2** in an [[unfinished]] or [[unrefined]] state: raw sewage
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unrefined]], [[natural]], [[crude]], [[unprocessed]], [[basic]], [[rough]], [[coarse]], [[unfinished]]
**3** not selected or modified: raw data
**4** (of the skin or a wound) painful, with the surface scraped away
**5** untrained or [[inexperienced]]: a raw recruit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inexperienced]], [[new]], [[green]], [[immature]], [[callow]]
**6** (of the weather) harshly [[cold]] and damp
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chilly]], [[biting]], [[cold]], [[freezing]], [[bitter]], [[piercing]], [[parky]] (*Brit* *informal*)
**7** frank or realistic: a raw reality
**8** **raw deal** (*informal*) unfair or dishonest treatment
 *n*
