**warm up** *vb*
**1** to make or become warm or warmer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[warm something or someone up]]**: heat, thaw, heat up
**2** to prepare for a race, sporting contest, or exercise routine by doing gentle exercises immediately beforehand
**3** (of an engine or machine) to be started and left running until the working temperature is reached
**4** to become more lively: wait until things warm up
**5** to reheat (food that has already been cooked)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small> **[[warm something or someone up]]**: heat, thaw, heat up
 *n* **warm-up**
**6** a preparatory exercise routine
