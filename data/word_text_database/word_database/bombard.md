**bombard** *vb*
**1** to [[attack]] with concentrated artillery fire or bombs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bomb]], [[shell]], [[blitz]], [[open fire]], [[strafe]], [[fire upon]]
**2** to [[attack]] persistently
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[assault]], [[besiege]], [[beset]], [[assail]]
**3** to [[attack]] verbally, esp. with questions
**4** (*physics*) to direct high-energy particles or photons against (atoms, nuclei, etc.)
 **[[bombardment]]** *n*
