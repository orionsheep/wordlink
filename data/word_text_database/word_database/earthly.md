**earthly** *adj* **-lier**, **-liest**
**1** of life on earth as opposed to any heavenly or spiritual state
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worldly]], [[material]], [[secular]], [[mortal]], [[temporal]], [[human]]
**2** (*informal*) [[conceivable]] or [[possible]]: what earthly reason would they have for lying?
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[possible]], [[likely]], [[practical]], [[feasible]], [[conceivable]], [[imaginable]]
