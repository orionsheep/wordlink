**hereditary** *adj*
**1** passed on genetically from one generation to another
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[genetic]], [[inborn]], [[inbred]], [[transmissible]], [[inheritable]]
**2** (*law*) passed on to succeeding generations by inheritance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inherited]], [[passed down]], [[traditional]], [[ancestral]]
