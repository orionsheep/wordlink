**employment** *n*
**1** the act of employing or state of being employed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[taking on]], [[commissioning]], [[appointing]], [[hire]], [[hiring]], [[retaining]], [[engaging]], [[appointment]]
**2** a person's [[work]] or [[occupation]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[work]], [[position]], [[trade]], [[post]], [[situation]], [[profession]], [[occupation]]
**3** the availability of jobs for the population of a town, country, etc.: the party's commitment to full employment
