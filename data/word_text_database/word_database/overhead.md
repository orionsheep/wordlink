**overhead** *adj*
**1** situated or operating [[above]] head height: overhead compartments
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[raised]], [[suspended]], [[elevated]], [[aerial]], overhanging
 *adv*
**2** over or [[above]] head height: the missile streaked overhead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[above]], [[in the sky]], [[on high]], [[aloft]], [[up above]]
