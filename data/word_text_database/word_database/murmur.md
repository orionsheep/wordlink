**murmur** *vb*
**1** to speak or say in a quiet indistinct way
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[mumble]], [[whisper]], [[mutter]]
**2** to complain
 *n*
**3** a continuous low indistinct sound, such as that of a distant conversation
**4** an indistinct utterance: a murmur of protest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[whisper]], [[drone]], [[purr]]
**5** a complaint or grumble: he left without a murmur
**6** (*med*) any abnormal soft blowing sound heard usually over the chest: a heart murmur
 **[[murmuring]]** *n*, *adj* 
 **[[murmurous]]** *adj*
