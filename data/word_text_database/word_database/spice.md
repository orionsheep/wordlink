**spice** *n*
**1**
**A** an aromatic substance, such as ginger or cinnamon, used as flavouring
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: seasoning
 **B** such substances collectively
**2** something that makes life or an activity more exciting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[excitement]], [[zest]], [[colour]], [[pep]], [[zing]] (*informal*), piquancy
 *vb* **spicing**, **[[spiced]]**
**3** to flavour (food) with spices
**4** (often foll. by *up*) to add [[excitement]] or interest to (something): they [[spiced]] their letters with pointed demands
