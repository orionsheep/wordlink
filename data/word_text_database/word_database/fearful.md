**fearful** *adj*
**1** [[afraid]] and full of fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scared]], [[afraid]], [[alarmed]], [[frightened]], [[nervous]], [[terrified]], [[petrified]]
**2** frightening or causing fear: the ship hit a fearful storm
**3** (*informal*) very bad: they were making a fearful noise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[frightful]], [[terrible]], [[awful]], [[dreadful]], [[horrific]], [[dire]], [[horrendous]], [[gruesome]]
 **[[fearfully]]** *adv*
