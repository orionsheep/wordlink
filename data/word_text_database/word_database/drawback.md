**drawback** *n*
**1** a [[disadvantage]] or hindrance
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disadvantage]], [[difficulty]], [[handicap]], [[deficiency]], [[flaw]], [[hitch]], [[snag]], downside
 *vb* **draw back**
**2** to move backwards: the girl drew back as though in pain
**3** to turn aside from an undertaking: the prime minister drew back from his original intention
