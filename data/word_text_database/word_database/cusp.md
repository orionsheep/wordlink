**cusp** *n*
**1** a small point on the grinding or chewing surface of a tooth
**2** a point where two curves meet
**3** (*astrol*) any division between houses or signs of the zodiac
**4** (*astron*) either of the points of a crescent moon
**5** **on the cusp** at the point of moving from one state into another: on the cusp of middle age
