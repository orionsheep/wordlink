**trough** (**troff**) *n*
**1** a long open container, esp. one for animals' food or water
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[manger]], [[water trough]]
**2** a narrow channel between two waves or ridges
**3** a low point in a pattern that has regular high and low points: the trough of the slump in pupil numbers was in 1985
**4** (*meteorol*) a long narrow area of low pressure
**5** a narrow channel or gutter
