**hiss** *n*
**1** a sound like that of a prolonged *s*
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fizz]], [[buzz]], [[hissing]], [[fizzing]], [[sibilation]]
**2** such a sound as an expression of dislike or disapproval
 *vb*
**3** to utter a hiss
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[whistle]], [[wheeze]], [[whiz]], [[whirr]], [[sibilate]]
**4** to express with a hiss: she hissed the name
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[whistle]], [[wheeze]], [[whiz]], [[whirr]], [[sibilate]]
**5** to show dislike or disapproval towards (a speaker or performer) by [[hissing]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[jeer]], [[mock]], [[deride]]
