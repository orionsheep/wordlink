**modest** *adj*
**1** having a humble opinion of oneself or one's accomplishments
**2** not extreme or excessive: a modest increase in inflation
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[moderate]], [[small]], [[limited]], [[fair]], [[ordinary]], [[middling]], [[meagre]], [[frugal]]
**3** not ostentatious or pretentious: a modest flat in the suburbs
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[unpretentious]], [[reserved]], [[retiring]], [[shy]], [[coy]], [[reticent]], [[self-effacing]], [[demure]]
**4** [[shy]] or easily embarrassed
◇ <[[small]]>SYNONYMS</[[small]]>: [[unpretentious]], [[reserved]], [[retiring]], [[shy]], [[coy]], [[reticent]], [[self-effacing]], [[demure]]
**5** (*old-fashioned*) (esp. of clothes) not revealing much of the body: a modest dress
 **[[modestly]]** *adv* 
 **[[modesty]]** *n*
