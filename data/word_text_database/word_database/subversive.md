**subversive** *adj*
**1** intended or intending to weaken or overthrow a government or an institution
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[seditious]], [[riotous]], treasonous
 *n*
**2** a person engaged in subversive activities
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dissident]], [[terrorist]], [[saboteur]], [[fifth columnist]]
