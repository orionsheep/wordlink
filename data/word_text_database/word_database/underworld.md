**underworld** *n*
**1** [[criminals]] and their associates
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[criminals]], [[gangsters]], [[organized crime]], [[gangland]] (*informal*)
**2** (*classical myth*) the regions below the earth's surface regarded as the abode of the dead
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[nether world]], [[Hades]], [[nether regions]]
