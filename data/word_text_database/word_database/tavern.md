**tavern** *n*
**1** (*old-fashioned*) a pub
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inn]], [[bar]], pub (*informal*, *chiefly* *Brit*), [[public house]], [[beer parlour]] (*Canad*), [[beverage room]] (*Canad*), [[hostelry]], [[alehouse]] (*archaic*)
**2** (*US** & **Canad** & **Austral** & **NZ*) a place licensed for the sale and consumption of alcoholic drink
