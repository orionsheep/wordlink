**unfit** *adj*
**1** [[unqualified]] for or [[incapable]] of a particular role or task: an unfit mother; he was unfit to drive
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[incapable]], [[inadequate]], [[incompetent]], [[no good]], [[useless]] (*informal*), [[unqualified]]
**2** [[unsuitable]]: this meat is unfit for human consumption
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[unsuitable]], [[inadequate]], [[useless]], [[unsuited]]
**3** in poor physical condition
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[out of shape]], [[feeble]], [[unhealthy]], [[flabby]], [[in poor condition]]
