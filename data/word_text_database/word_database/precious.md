**precious** *adj*
**1** very [[costly]] or [[valuable]]: precious jewellery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[valuable]], [[expensive]], [[fine]], [[prized]], [[dear]], [[costly]], [[invaluable]], [[priceless]]
**2** [[loved]] and [[treasured]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[loved]], [[prized]], [[dear]], [[treasured]], [[darling]], [[beloved]], [[adored]], [[cherished]]
**3** very [[affected]] in speech, manners, or behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[affected]], [[artificial]], [[twee]] (*Brit* *informal*), [[overrefined]], overnice
 *adv*
**4** (*informal*) very: there's precious little to do in this town
