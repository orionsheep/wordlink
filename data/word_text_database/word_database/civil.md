**civil** *adj*
**1** of or occurring within the state or between citizens: civil unrest
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[civic]], [[political]], [[domestic]], [[municipal]]
**2** of or relating to the citizen as an individual: civil rights
**3** not part of the military, legal, or religious structures of a country: civil aviation
**4** [[polite]] or [[courteous]]: he seemed very civil and listened politely
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[polite]], [[obliging]], [[courteous]], [[considerate]], [[affable]], [[well-mannered]]
 **[[civilly]]** *adv*
