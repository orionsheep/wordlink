**fibre** * or * (*US*) **fiber** *n*
**1** a natural or synthetic [[thread]] that may be spun into yarn
**2** a threadlike animal or plant tissue: a simple network of nerve fibres
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thread]], [[strand]], [[filament]], [[tendril]], [[pile]], [[texture]], [[wisp]]
**3** a [[fibrous]] substance that helps the body digest food: fruits, vegetables, grains, lentils, and beans are high in fibre
**4** strength of character: moral fibre
**5** essential substance or nature: my every fibre sang out in sudden relief
 **[[fibrous]]** *adj*
