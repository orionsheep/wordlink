**ramp** *n*
**1** a [[slope]] that joins two surfaces at different levels
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[slope]], [[incline]], [[gradient]], [[rise]]
**2** a place where the level of a road surface changes because of roadworks
**3** a movable stairway by which passengers enter and leave an aircraft
**4** (*Brit*) a small hump on a road to make traffic slow down
