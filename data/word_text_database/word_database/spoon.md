**spoon** *n*
**1** a small shallow bowl attached to a handle, used for eating, stirring, or serving food
**2** **be born with a silver spoon in one's mouth** to be born into a very rich and respected family
 *vb*
**3** to scoop up (food or liquid) with a spoon
**4** (*old-fashioned*, *slang*) to kiss and cuddle
