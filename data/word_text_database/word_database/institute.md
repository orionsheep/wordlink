**institute** *n*
**1** an organization [[set up]] for a specific purpose, esp. research or teaching
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[establishment]], [[body]], [[centre]], [[school]], [[university]], [[society]], [[association]], [[college]]
**2** the building where such an organization is situated
**3** a rule, custom, or precedent
 *vb* **-tuting**, **-tuted**
**4** to [[start]] or [[establish]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[establish]], [[start]], [[found]], [[launch]], [[set up]], [[introduce]], [[fix]], [[organize]]
**5** to install in a position or office
