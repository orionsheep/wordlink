**link** *n*
**1** any of the separate rings that form a chain
**2** an emotional or logical [[relationship]] between people or things; [[association]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[relationship]], [[association]], [[bond]], [[connection]], [[attachment]], [[affinity]]
**3** a connecting [[part]] or episode
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[component]], [[part]], [[piece]], [[element]], [[constituent]]
**4** a type of communications [[connection]]: a rail link; radio link
**5** short for **hyperlink**
 *vb*
**6** (often foll. by *up*) to [[connect]] with or as if with links
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[connect]], [[join]], [[unite]], [[couple]], [[tie]], [[bind]], [[attach]], [[fasten]]
**7** to [[connect]] by [[association]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[associate]], [[relate]], [[identify]], [[connect]], [[bracket]]
