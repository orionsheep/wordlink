**variety** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** the state of being diverse or various
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[diversity]], [[change]], [[variation]], [[difference]], [[diversification]], [[heterogeneity]], [[multifariousness]]
**2** different things of the same [[kind]]: I'm cooking the mince with a variety of vegetables
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[range]], [[selection]], [[assortment]], [[mix]], [[collection]], [[line-up]], [[mixture]], [[array]]
**3** a particular [[type]] of something in the same general category: this variety of pear is extremely juicy
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[type]], [[sort]], [[kind]], [[class]], [[brand]], [[species]], [[breed]], [[strain]]
**4** (*taxonomy*) a race whose distinct characters do not justify classification as a separate [[species]]
**5** a [[type]] of entertainment consisting of short unrelated acts, such as singing, dancing, and comedy
