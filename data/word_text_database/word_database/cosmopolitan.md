**cosmopolitan** *adj*
**1** composed of people or elements from many different countries or cultures
**2** having lived and travelled in many countries
**3** [[sophisticated]] and [[cultured]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sophisticated]], [[cultured]], [[refined]], [[cultivated]], [[urbane]], [[well-travelled]], worldly-wise
 *n*
**4** a cosmopolitan person
 **[[cosmopolitanism]]** *n*
