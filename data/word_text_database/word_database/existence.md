**existence** *n*
**1** the fact or state of [[being]] real, live, or actual
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reality]], [[being]], [[life]], [[subsistence]], [[actuality]]
**2** a [[way of life]], esp. a poor or hungry one
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[life]], [[situation]], [[way of life]], [[lifestyle]]
**3** everything that exists
 **[[existent]]** *adj*
