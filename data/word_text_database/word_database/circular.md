**circular** *adj*
**1** of or in the shape of a circle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[round]], [[ring-shaped]]
**2** travelling in a circle
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[circuitous]], [[cyclical]], [[orbital]]
**3** (of an argument) not valid because a statement is used to prove the conclusion and the conclusion to prove the statement
**4** (of letters or announcements) intended for general distribution
 *n*
**5** a letter or [[advertisement]] sent to a large number of people at the same time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advertisement]], [[notice]], [[ad]] (*informal*), [[announcement]], [[advert]] (*Brit*), [[press release]]
 **[[circularity]]** *n*
