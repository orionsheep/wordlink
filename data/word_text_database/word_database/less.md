**less** *adj*
**1** the comparative of **little**: less fibre
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[smaller]], [[shorter]], [[not so much]]
**2** (*not standard*) fewer
 *adv*
**3** the comparative of **little**: eat less
**4** **less of** to a [[smaller]] extent or degree: it would become less of a problem
**5** **no less** (*sometimes* *humorous*) used to indicate admiration or surprise: sculpted by a famous Frenchman, Rodin no less
 *prep*
**6** minus: a two pounds-a-week rise (less tax)
