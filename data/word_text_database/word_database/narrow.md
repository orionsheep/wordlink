**narrow** *adj*
**1** small in breadth in comparison to length
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[thin]], [[fine]], [[slim]], [[slender]], [[tapering]], [[attenuated]]
**2** [[limited]] in range, extent, or outlook: a narrow circle of academics
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[limited]], [[restricted]], [[confined]], [[tight]], [[close]], [[meagre]], [[constricted]]
**3** with little margin: a narrow advantage
 *vb*
**4** to make or become narrow
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[get narrower]], [[taper]], [[shrink]], [[tighten]], [[constrict]]
**5** **narrow down** to [[restrict]] or [[limit]]: the search can be narrowed down to a single room
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[restrict]], [[limit]], [[reduce]], [[constrict]]
 See also **narrows**
 **[[narrowly]]** *adv* 
 **[[narrowness]]** *n*
