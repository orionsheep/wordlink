**offensive** *adj*
**1** [[unpleasant]] or [[disgusting]] to the senses: there was an offensive smell of beer
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disgusting]], [[gross]], [[foul]], [[unpleasant]], [[revolting]], [[vile]], [[repellent]], [[obnoxious]], [[festy]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[yucko]] (*Austral* *slang*)
**2** causing annoyance or anger; [[insulting]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[insulting]], [[rude]], [[abusive]], [[degrading]], [[contemptuous]], [[disparaging]], [[objectionable]], [[disrespectful]]
**3** for the purpose of [[attack]] rather than defence
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attacking]], [[threatening]], [[aggressive]], [[striking]], [[hostile]], [[invading]], combative
 *n*
**4** an attitude or position of aggression: to go on the offensive
**5** an [[attack]] or [[hostile]] action: troops had launched a major offensive against the rebel forces
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[attack]], [[charge]], [[campaign]], [[strike]], [[push]] (*informal*), [[assault]], [[raid]], [[drive]]
 **[[offensively]]** *adv*
