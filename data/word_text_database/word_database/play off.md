**play off** *vb*
**1** to set (two people) against each other for one's own ends: she delighted in playing one parent off against the other
**2** to take part in a play-off
 *n* **play-off**
**3** (*sport*) an extra contest to decide the winner when there is a tie
**4** (*chiefly* *US** & **Canad*) a contest or series of games to determine a championship
