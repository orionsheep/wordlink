**dictate** *vb* **-tating**, **-tated**
**1** to [[say]] (words) aloud for another person to transcribe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[speak]], [[say]], [[utter]], [[read out]]
**2** to seek to impose one's will on others
 *n*
**3** an authoritative [[command]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[command]], [[order]], [[decree]], [[demand]], [[direction]], [[injunction]], [[fiat]], [[edict]]
**4** a guiding [[principle]]: the dictates of reason
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[principle]], [[law]], [[rule]], [[standard]], [[code]], [[criterion]], [[maxim]]
