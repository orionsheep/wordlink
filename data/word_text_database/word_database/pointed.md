**pointed** *adj*
**1** having a [[sharp]] tip
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sharp]], [[edged]], [[acute]], [[barbed]]
**2** [[cutting]] or incisive: pointed wit
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cutting]], [[telling]], [[biting]], [[sharp]], [[keen]], [[acute]], [[penetrating]], [[pertinent]]
**3** obviously directed at a particular person: a pointed remark
**4** emphasized or obvious: pointed ignorance
 **[[pointedly]]** *adv*
