**scroll** *n*
**1** a roll of parchment or paper, usually inscribed with writing
**2** an ancient book in the form of a roll of parchment, papyrus, or paper
**3** a decorative carving or moulding resembling a scroll
 *vb*
**4** to move (text) on a computer or phone screen in order to view a section that cannot be fitted into a single display
