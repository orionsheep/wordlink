**tint** *n*
**1** a [[shade]] of a [[colour]], esp. a pale one: his eyes had a yellow tint
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[shade]], [[colour]], [[tone]], [[hue]]
**2** a [[colour]] that is softened by the addition of white: a room decorated in pastel tints
**3** a [[dye]] for the hair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dye]], [[wash]], [[rinse]], [[tinge]], tincture
 *vb*
**4** to give a tint to (something, such as hair)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dye]], [[colour]]
