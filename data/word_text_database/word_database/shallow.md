**shallow** *adj*
**1** having little depth
**2** not involving sincere feelings or serious thought
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[superficial]], [[surface]], [[empty]], [[slight]], [[foolish]], [[trivial]], [[meaningless]], [[frivolous]]
**3** (of breathing) consisting of short breaths
 *n*
**4** (*often pl*) a shallow place in a body of water
 **[[shallowness]]** *n*
