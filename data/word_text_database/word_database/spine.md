**spine** *n*
**1** the row of bony segments that surround and protect the [[spinal]] cord
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[backbone]], [[vertebrae]], [[spinal column]], [[vertebral column]]
**2** the back of a book, record sleeve, or video-tape box
**3** a sharp point on the body of an animal or on a plant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[barb]], [[spur]], [[needle]], [[spike]], [[ray]], [[quill]]
 **[[spinal]]** *adj*
