**hurdle** *n*
**1** (*athletics*) one of a number of light barriers over which runners leap in certain events
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fence]], [[barrier]], [[barricade]]
**2** a [[difficulty]] or problem: the main technical hurdle is the environment
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obstacle]], [[difficulty]], [[barrier]], [[handicap]], [[hazard]], [[uphill]] (*S African*), [[obstruction]], [[stumbling block]]
**3** **hurdles** a race involving hurdles
 *vb* **-dling**, **-dled**
**4** to jump over (a hurdle or other [[obstacle]])
 **[[hurdler]]** *n*
