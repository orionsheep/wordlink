**amnesty** *n*, *pl* **-ties**
**1** a [[general pardon]] for offences against a government
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[general pardon]], [[mercy]], [[pardoning]], [[immunity]], [[forgiveness]], [[reprieve]], [[remission]], [[clemency]]
**2** a period during which a law is suspended, to allow people to confess to crime or give up weapons without fear of prosecution
