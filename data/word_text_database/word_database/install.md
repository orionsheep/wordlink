**install** *vb*
**1** to [[put in]] and prepare (equipment) for use
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[set up]], [[put in]], [[place]], [[position]], [[station]], [[establish]], [[lay]], [[fix]]
**2** to [[place]] (a person) formally in a [[position]] or rank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[institute]], [[establish]], [[introduce]], [[invest]], [[ordain]], [[inaugurate]], [[induct]]
**3** to [[settle]] (a person, esp. oneself) in a [[position]] or state: Tony installed himself in an armchair
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settle]], [[position]], [[plant]], [[establish]], [[lodge]], [[ensconce]]
