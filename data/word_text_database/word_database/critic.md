**critic** *n*
**1** a professional [[judge]] of art, music, or literature
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[judge]], [[authority]], [[expert]], [[analyst]], [[commentator]], [[pundit]], [[reviewer]], [[connoisseur]]
**2** a person who finds fault and criticizes
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fault-finder]], [[attacker]], [[detractor]], [[knocker]] (*informal*)
