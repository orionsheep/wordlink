**tug** *vb* **tugging**, **tugged**
**1** to [[pull]] or [[drag]] with a sharp or powerful movement: she tugged at my arm
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[drag]], [[pull]], [[haul]], [[tow]], [[lug]], [[heave]], [[draw]]
**2** to [[tow]] (a ship or boat) by means of a tug
 *n*
**3** a strong [[pull]] or [[jerk]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[pull]], [[jerk]], [[yank]]
**4** Also called: **tugboat** a boat with a powerful engine, used for towing barges, ships, etc.
