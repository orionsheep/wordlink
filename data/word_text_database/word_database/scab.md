**scab** *n*
**1** the dried crusty surface of a healing skin wound or sore
**2** (*derogatory*) a person who refuses to support a trade union's actions, and continues to work during a strike
**3** a contagious disease of sheep, caused by a mite
**4** a fungal disease of plants
 *vb* **scabbing**, **scabbed**
**5** to become covered with a scab
**6** (*derogatory*) to work as a scab
