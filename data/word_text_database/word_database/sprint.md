**sprint** *n*
**1** (*athletics*)
**A** a short [[race]] [[run]] at top speed
 **B** a fast [[run]] at the end of a longer [[race]]
**2** any quick [[run]]
 *vb*
**3** to [[run]] or cycle a short distance at top speed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[run]], [[race]], [[shoot]], [[tear]], [[dash]], [[dart]], [[hare]] (*Brit* *informal*)
