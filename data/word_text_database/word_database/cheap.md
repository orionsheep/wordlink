**cheap** *adj*
**1** costing relatively little; [[inexpensive]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inexpensive]], [[reduced]], [[keen]], [[reasonable]], [[bargain]], [[low-priced]], [[low-cost]], [[cut-price]]
**2** of [[poor]] quality; [[shoddy]]: planks of cheap, splintery pine
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inferior]], [[poor]], [[worthless]], [[second-rate]], [[shoddy]], [[tawdry]], [[tatty]] (*Brit*), [[trashy]], bodger *or* bodgie (*Austral* *slang*)
**3** not valued highly; not worth much: promises are cheap
**4** (*informal*) [[mean]] or [[despicable]]: a cheap jibe
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[despicable]], [[mean]], [[contemptible]], scungy (*Austral** & **NZ*)
 *n*
**5** **on the cheap** (*Brit* *informal*) at a low cost
 *adv*
**6** at a low cost
