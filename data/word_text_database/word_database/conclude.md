**conclude** *vb* **-cluding**, **-cluded**
**1** to [[decide]] by reasoning: a police investigation concluded that no crime had been committed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[decide]], [[judge]], [[assume]], [[gather]], [[work out]], [[infer]], [[deduce]], [[surmise]]
**2** to come or bring to an [[end]]: the festival concludes on December 19th
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[come to an end]], [[end]], [[close]], [[finish]], [[wind up]]
**3** to arrange or settle finally: officials have refused to comment on the failure to conclude an agreement
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accomplish]], [[effect]], [[bring about]], [[carry out]], [[pull off]]
