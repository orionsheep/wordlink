**smart** *adj*
**1** clean and neatly dressed
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[chic]], [[trim]], [[neat]], [[stylish]], [[elegant]], [[spruce]], [[snappy]], [[natty]] (*informal*), [[schmick]] (*Austral* *informal*)
**2** [[intelligent]] and [[shrewd]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clever]], [[bright]], [[intelligent]], [[quick]], [[sharp]], [[keen]], [[acute]], [[shrewd]]
**3** [[quick]] and witty in speech: a smart talker
**4** (of places or events) fashionable; [[chic]]: smart restaurants
**5** [[vigorous]] or [[brisk]]: a smart pace
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brisk]], [[quick]], [[lively]], [[vigorous]]
**6** causing a [[sharp]] stinging pain
**7** (of a weapon) containing an electronic device which enables it to be guided to its target: a smart bomb
 *vb*
**8** to feel or cause a [[sharp]] stinging physical or mental pain: I was still smarting from the insult
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[sting]], [[burn]], hurt
 *n*
**9** a stinging pain or feeling
 *adv*
**10** in a smart manner
 **[[smartly]]** *adv* 
 **[[smartness]]** *n*
