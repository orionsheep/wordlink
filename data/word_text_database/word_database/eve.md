**eve** *n*
**1** the evening or [[day before]] some special event
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[night before]], [[day before]], [[vigil]]
**2** the period immediately before an event: on the eve of the Second World War
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[brink]], [[point]], [[edge]], [[verge]], [[threshold]]
**3** (*poetic*, *or* *old-fashioned*) evening
