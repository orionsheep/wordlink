**imagine** *vb* **-ining**, **-ined**
**1** to form a mental image of
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[envisage]], [[see]], [[picture]], [[plan]], [[think of]], [[conjure up]], [[envision]], [[visualize]]
**2** to [[think]], [[believe]], or [[guess]]: I would imagine they'll be here soon
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[believe]], [[think]], [[suppose]], [[assume]], [[suspect]], [[guess]] (*informal*), [[take it]], [[reckon]] (*informal*) 
 **[[imaginable]]** *adj*
