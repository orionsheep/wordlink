**occupation** *n*
**1** a person's [[job]] or [[profession]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[job]], [[calling]], [[business]], [[line]] (of work), [[trade]], [[career]], [[employment]], [[profession]]
**2** any [[activity]] on which someone's time is spent: a pleasant and rewarding occupation
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hobby]], [[pastime]], [[diversion]], [[relaxation]], [[leisure pursuit]], (leisure) [[activity]]
**3** the control of a country by a foreign military power
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[invasion]], [[seizure]], [[conquest]], [[incursion]], [[subjugation]]
**4** the act of occupying or the state of being occupied: the occupation of Kuwait
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[invasion]], [[seizure]], [[conquest]], [[incursion]], [[subjugation]]
 **[[occupational]]** *adj*
