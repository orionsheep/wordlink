**quiet** *adj*
**1** characterized by an absence of noise
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[soft]], [[low]], [[muted]], [[lowered]], [[whispered]], [[faint]], [[suppressed]], [[stifled]]
**2** [[calm]] or [[tranquil]]: the sea is quiet today
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calm]], [[peaceful]], [[tranquil]], [[mild]], [[serene]], [[placid]], [[restful]], [[chilled]] (*informal*)
**3** untroubled: a quiet life
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[still]], [[calm]], [[peaceful]], [[tranquil]]
**4** not busy: business is quiet this morning
**5** private or secret: I had a quiet word with her
**6** free from anger, impatience, or other extreme emotion
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calm]], [[peaceful]], [[tranquil]], [[mild]], [[serene]], [[placid]], [[restful]], [[chilled]] (*informal*)
**7** not showy: quiet colours; a quiet wedding
**8** modest or [[reserved]]: quiet humour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[reserved]], [[retiring]], [[shy]], [[gentle]], [[mild]], [[sedate]], meek
 *n*
**9** the state of being silent, [[peaceful]], or untroubled
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[peace]], [[rest]], [[tranquillity]], [[ease]], [[silence]], [[solitude]], [[serenity]], [[stillness]]
**10** **on the quiet** without other people knowing
 *vb*
**11** to make or become [[calm]] or silent
 **[[quietly]]** *adv* 
 **[[quietness]]** *n*
