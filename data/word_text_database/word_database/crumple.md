**crumple** *vb* **-pling**, **-pled**
**1** to [[crush]] or become crushed into untidy wrinkles or creases
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[crush]], [[squash]], [[screw up]], [[scrumple]]
**2** to [[collapse]] in an untidy heap: her father lay crumpled on the floor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[collapse]], [[sink]], [[go down]], fall
 *n*
**3** an untidy crease or wrinkle
 **[[crumply]]** *adj*
