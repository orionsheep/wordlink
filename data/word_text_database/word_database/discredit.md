**discredit** *vb* **-diting**, **-dited**
**1** to damage the reputation of (someone)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disgrace]], [[shame]], [[smear]], [[humiliate]], [[taint]], [[disparage]], [[vilify]], [[slander]]
**2** to cause (an idea) to be disbelieved or distrusted
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dispute]], [[question]], [[challenge]], [[deny]], [[reject]], [[discount]], [[distrust]], mistrust
 *n*
**3** something that causes [[disgrace]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disgrace]], [[scandal]], [[shame]], [[disrepute]], [[stigma]], [[ignominy]], [[dishonour]], [[ill-repute]]
 **[[discreditable]]** *adj*
