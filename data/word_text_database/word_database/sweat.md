**sweat** *n*
**1** the salty liquid that comes out of the skin's pores during strenuous activity in excessive heat or when afraid
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perspiration]]
**2** the state or condition of sweating: he worked up a sweat
**3** (*slang*) hard work or effort: climbing to the crest of Ward Hill was a sweat
**4** **in a sweat** (*informal*) in a state of [[worry]]
**5** **no sweat** (*slang*) no problem
 *vb* **sweating**, **sweat*** or ***sweated**
**6** to have sweat come through the skin's pores, as a result of strenuous activity, excessive heat, nervousness, or fear
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[perspire]], [[glow]]
**7** (*informal*) to suffer anxiety or distress
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[worry]], [[fret]], [[agonize]], [[torture yourself]]
**8** **sweat blood** (*informal*)
**A** to work very hard
 **B** to be filled with anxiety
 **[[sweaty]]** *adj*
