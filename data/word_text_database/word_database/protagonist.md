**protagonist** *n*
**1** a [[supporter]] of a cause: a great protagonist of the ideas and principles of mutuality
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[supporter]], [[champion]], [[advocate]], [[exponent]]
**2** the [[leading character]] in a play or story
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[leading character]], [[principal]], [[central character]], hero *or* heroine
