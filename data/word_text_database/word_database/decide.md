**decide** *vb* **-ciding**, **-cided**
**1** to reach a decision: we must decide on suitable action; he decided to stay on
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[make a decision]], [[make up your mind]], reach *or* come to a decision, [[choose]], [[determine]], [[conclude]]
**2** to cause to reach a decision
**3** to [[settle]] (a question): possible profits decided the issue
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settle]], [[determine]], [[resolve]]
**4** to influence the outcome of (a contest) decisively: the goal that decided the match came just before half-time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[settle]], [[determine]], [[resolve]]
