**conform** *vb*
**1** to [[comply]] with accepted standards, rules, or customs
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fit in]], [[follow]], [[adjust]], [[adapt]], [[comply]], [[obey]], [[fall in]], [[toe the line]]
**2** (usually foll. by *with*) to be like or in accordance with: people tend to absorb ideas that conform with their existing beliefs, and reject those that do not
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[fulfil]], [[meet]], [[match]], [[suit]], [[satisfy]], [[agree with]], [[obey]], [[abide by]]
