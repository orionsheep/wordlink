**corruption** *n*
**1** [[dishonesty]] and illegal behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[dishonesty]], [[fraud]], [[bribery]], [[extortion]], [[venality]], [[shady dealings]] (*informal*)
**2** the act of corrupting morally or sexually
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[depravity]], [[vice]], [[evil]], [[perversion]], [[decadence]], [[wickedness]], [[immorality]]
**3** the process of rotting or decaying
**4** an unintentional or unauthorized alteration in a text or data
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distortion]], [[doctoring]], [[falsification]]
**5** an altered form of a word
