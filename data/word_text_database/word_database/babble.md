**babble** *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**1** to talk in a quick, foolish, or muddled way
**2** to make meaningless sounds: children first gurgle and babble at random
**3** to disclose secrets carelessly
**4** (*literary*) (of streams) to make a low murmuring sound
 *n*
**5** muddled or foolish speech
**6** a murmuring sound
