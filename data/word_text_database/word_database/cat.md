**cat** *n*
**1** a small domesticated mammal with thick soft fur and whiskers
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[feline]], [[pussy]] (*informal*), [[moggy]] (*slang*), [[puss]] (*informal*), [[ballarat]] (*Austral* *informal*), [[tabby]]
**2** a wild animal related to the cat, such as the lynx, lion, or tiger. <small>RELATED ADJECTIVE: </small>**[[feline]]**
**3** **let the cat out of the bag** to disclose a secret
**4** **raining cats and dogs** raining very heavily
**5** **set the cat among the pigeons** to stir up trouble
 **[[catlike]]** *adj*
