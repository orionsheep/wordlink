**teach** *vb* **teaching**, **taught**
**1** to tell or [[show]] (someone) how to do something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[show]], [[train]]
**2** to give instruction or lessons in (a subject) to (students)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[instruct]], [[train]], [[coach]], [[inform]], [[educate]], [[drill]], [[tutor]], [[enlighten]]
**3** to cause to learn or understand: life has taught me to seize the day
**4** **teach someone a lesson** to warn or punish someone: a bully has to be taught a lesson
 **[[teachable]]** *adj*
