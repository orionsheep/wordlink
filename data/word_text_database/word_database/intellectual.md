**intellectual** *adj*
**1** of, involving, or appealing to the intellect: intellectual literature
**2** clever or [[intelligent]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[scholarly]], [[learned]], [[academic]], [[lettered]], [[intelligent]], [[cerebral]], [[erudite]], scholastic
 *n*
**3** a person who has a highly developed intellect
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[academic]], [[expert]], [[genius]], [[thinker]], [[master]], [[mastermind]], [[maestro]], [[highbrow]], [[fundi]] (*S African*), [[acca]] (*Austral* *slang*) 
 **[[intellectuality]]** *n* 
 **[[intellectually]]** *adv*
