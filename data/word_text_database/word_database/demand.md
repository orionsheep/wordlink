**demand** *vb*
**1** to [[request]] forcefully
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[request]], [[ask]] (for), [[order]], [[expect]], [[claim]], [[seek]], [[insist on]], [[exact]]
**2** to [[require]] as just, urgent, etc.: the situation demands intervention
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[require]], [[want]], [[need]], [[involve]], [[call for]], [[entail]], [[necessitate]], [[cry out for]]
**3** to [[claim]] as a right
 *n*
**4** a forceful [[request]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[request]], [[order]]
**5** something that requires special effort or sacrifice: demands upon one's time
**6** (*econ*) willingness and ability to purchase goods and services
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[need]], [[want]], [[call]], [[market]], [[claim]], [[requirement]]
**7** **in demand** sought after; popular
**8** **on demand** as soon as requested: the funds will be available on demand
