**perverse** *adj*
**1** deliberately acting in a way different from what is regarded as normal or proper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[abnormal]], [[unhealthy]], [[improper]], [[deviant]] (*old-fashioned*)
**2** [[wayward]] or [[contrary]]; obstinate
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[stubborn]], [[contrary]], [[dogged]], [[troublesome]], [[rebellious]], [[wayward]], [[intractable]], [[wilful]]
 **[[perversely]]** *adv* 
 **[[perversity]]** *n*
