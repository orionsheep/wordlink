**dose** *n*
**1** a specific quantity of a medicine taken at one time
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[measure]], [[amount]], [[allowance]], [[portion]], [[prescription]], [[ration]], [[draught]], [[dosage]]
**2** (*informal*) something unpleasant to experience: a dose of the cold
**3** the total energy of radiation absorbed
**4** (*slang*) a sexually transmitted infection
 *vb* **dosing**, **dosed**
**5** to administer a quantity of medicine to (someone)
 **[[dosage]]** *n*
