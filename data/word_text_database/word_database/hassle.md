**hassle** (*informal*) *n*
**1** a great deal of [[trouble]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trouble]], [[problem]], [[difficulty]], [[bother]], [[grief]] (*informal*), [[uphill]] (*S African*), [[inconvenience]]
**2** a prolonged argument
 *vb* **-sling**, **-sled**
**3** to cause annoyance or [[trouble]] to (someone): stop hassling me!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bother]], [[bug]] (*informal*), [[annoy]], [[hound]], [[harass]], [[badger]], [[pester]]
