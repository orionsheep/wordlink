**trouble** *n*
**1** difficulties or [[problems]]: I'd trouble finding somewhere to park
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bother]], [[problems]], [[concern]], [[worry]], [[stress]], [[difficulty]], [[anxiety]], [[distress]]
**2** a cause of [[distress]], [[disturbance]], or [[pain]]: we must be sensitive to the troubles of other people
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[distress]], [[problem]], [[worry]], [[pain]], [[anxiety]], [[grief]], [[torment]], [[sorrow]]
**3** [[disease]] or a [[problem]] with one's health: ear trouble
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[ailment]], [[disease]], [[failure]], [[complaint]], [[illness]], [[disorder]], [[defect]], [[malfunction]]
**4** a state of [[disorder]], ill-feeling, or [[unrest]]: the police had orders to intervene at the first sign of trouble
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[disorder]], [[fighting]], [[conflict]], [[bother]], [[unrest]], [[disturbance]], [[to-do]] (*informal*), [[furore]], [[biffo]] (*Austral* *slang*), [[boilover]] (*Austral*)
**5** [[effort]] or exertion to do something: they didn't even take the trouble to see the film before banning it
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[effort]], [[work]], [[thought]], [[care]], [[labour]], [[pains]], [[hassle]] (*informal*), [[inconvenience]]
**6** a personal weakness or cause of annoyance: his trouble is that he's constitutionally jealous
**7** **in trouble**
**A** likely to be punished for something one has done: in trouble with the public prosecutor
 **B** (*old-fashioned*) pregnant when not married
**8** **more trouble than it's worth** involving a lot of time or [[effort]] for very little reward: making your own pasta is more trouble than it's worth
 *vb* **-bling**, **-bled**
**9** to cause trouble to
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[bother]], [[worry]], [[upset]], [[disturb]], [[distress]], [[plague]], [[pain]], [[sadden]]
**10** to [[make an effort]] or exert oneself: he dismissed the letters as forgeries without troubling to examine them
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[take pains]], [[take the time]], [[make an effort]], [[exert yourself]]
**11** to cause [[inconvenience]] or discomfort to: sorry to trouble you!
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[inconvenience]], [[disturb]], [[burden]], [[put out]], [[impose upon]], [[incommode]]
 **[[troubled]]** *adj*
