**regulation** *n*
**1** a [[rule]] that governs procedure or behaviour
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rule]], [[order]], [[law]], [[dictate]], [[decree]], [[statute]], [[edict]], [[precept]]
**2** the act of regulating
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[control]], [[government]], [[management]], [[direction]], supervision
 *adj*
**3** in accordance with rules or conventions: dressed in the orchestra's regulation black tie
