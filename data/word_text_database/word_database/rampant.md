**rampant** *adj*
**1** growing or spreading uncontrollably
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[widespread]], [[prevalent]], [[rife]], [[uncontrolled]], [[unchecked]], [[unrestrained]], [[profuse]], [[spreading like wildfire]]
**2** (*heraldry*) (of a beast) [[standing]] on the hind legs, the right foreleg raised above the left: a lion rampant
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[upright]], [[standing]], [[rearing]], [[erect]]
