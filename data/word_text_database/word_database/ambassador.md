**ambassador** *n*
**1** a [[diplomat]] of the highest rank, sent to another country as permanent [[representative]] of his or her own country
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[representative]], [[minister]], [[agent]], [[deputy]], [[diplomat]], [[envoy]], [[consul]], [[attaché]]
**2** a [[representative]] or messenger: he saw himself as an ambassador for the game
 **[[ambassadorial]]** *adj*
