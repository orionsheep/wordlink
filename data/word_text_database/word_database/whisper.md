**whisper** *vb*
**1** to speak or say (something) very softly, using the breath instead of the vocal cords
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[murmur]], [[breathe]]
**2** to make a low soft rustling sound: the leaves [[whispered]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rustle]], [[sigh]], [[hiss]], [[swish]]
 *n*
**3** a low soft voice: her voice sank to a whisper
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[murmur]], [[mutter]], [[mumble]], [[undertone]]
**4** (*informal*) a [[rumour]]: I just picked up a whisper on this killing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rumour]], [[report]], [[gossip]], [[goss]] (*informal*), [[innuendo]], [[insinuation]]
**5** a low soft rustling sound: a whisper of breeze in the shrubbery
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[rustle]], [[sigh]], [[hiss]], [[swish]]
 **[[whispered]]** *adj*
