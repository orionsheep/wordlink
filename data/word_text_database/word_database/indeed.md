**indeed** *adv*
**1** [[certainly]]; [[actually]]: indeed, the sea featured heavily in his poems
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[certainly]], [[yes]], [[definitely]], [[surely]], [[truly]], [[undoubtedly]], [[without doubt]], [[indisputably]]
**2** [[truly]], very: it has become a dangerous place indeed
**3** [[in fact]]; what is more: it is necessary, indeed indispensable
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[really]], [[actually]], [[in fact]], [[certainly]], [[genuinely]], [[in truth]], in actuality
 *interj*
**4** an expression of doubt or surprise
