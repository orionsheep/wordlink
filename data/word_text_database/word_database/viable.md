**viable** *adj*
**1** able to be put into practice: the party has failed to propose a viable alternative
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[workable]], [[practical]], [[feasible]], [[suitable]], [[realistic]], [[operational]], [[applicable]], [[usable]]
**2** (of seeds or eggs) capable of growth
**3** (of a fetus) sufficiently developed to survive outside the uterus
 **[[viability]]** *n*
