**demolish** *vb*
**1** to [[tear down]] or break up (buildings)
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[knock down]], [[level]], [[destroy]], [[dismantle]], [[flatten]], [[tear down]], [[bulldoze]], [[raze]]
**2** to put an end to; [[destroy]]: I demolished her argument in seconds
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[destroy]], [[wreck]], [[overturn]], [[overthrow]], [[undo]]
**3** (*facetious*) to eat up: he demolished the whole cake
 **[[demolisher]]** *n* 
 **[[demolition]]** *n*
