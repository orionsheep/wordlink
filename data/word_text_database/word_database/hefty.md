**hefty** *adj* **heftier**, **heftiest** (*informal*)
**1** large in size, weight, or amount
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[big]], [[strong]], [[massive]], [[strapping]], [[robust]], [[muscular]], [[burly]], [[hulking]]
**2** forceful and vigorous: a hefty slap on the back
**3** involving a large amount of money: a hefty fine
