**unknown** *adj*
**1** not known, understood, or recognized
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[strange]], [[new]], [[undiscovered]], [[uncharted]], [[unexplored]], [[virgin]], [[remote]], [[alien]]
**2** not famous: a young and then unknown actor
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[obscure]], [[humble]], [[unfamiliar]]
**3** **unknown quantity** a person or thing whose action or effect is unknown or unpredictable
 *n*
**4** an unknown person, quantity, or thing
 *adv*
**5** **unknown to someone** without someone being aware: unknown to him, the starboard engine had dropped off
