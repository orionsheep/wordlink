**embrace** *vb* **-bracing**, **-braced**
**1** to [[clasp]] (someone) with one's arms as an expression of affection or a greeting
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hug]], [[hold]], [[cuddle]], [[seize]], [[squeeze]], [[clasp]], [[envelop]], [[canoodle]] (*slang*)
**2** to [[accept]] eagerly: he has embraced the Islamic faith
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[accept]], [[support]], [[welcome]], [[adopt]], [[take up]], [[seize]], [[espouse]], [[take on board]]
**3** to [[include]] or be made up of: a church that embraces two cultures
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[include]], [[involve]], [[cover]], [[contain]], [[take in]], [[incorporate]], [[comprise]], encompass
 *n*
**4** an act of embracing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[hug]], [[hold]], [[cuddle]], [[squeeze]], [[clinch]] (*slang*), [[clasp]]
