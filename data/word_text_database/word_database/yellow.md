**yellow** *n*
**1** the colour of a [[lemon]] or an egg yolk
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lemon]], [[gold]], [[amber]]
**2** anything yellow, such as yellow clothing or yellow paint: painted in yellow
 *adj*
**3** of the colour yellow; of the colour of a [[lemon]] or an egg yolk
**4** (*informal*) cowardly or afraid
**5** having a [[yellowish]] complexion
 *vb*
**6** to make or become yellow or yellower
 **[[yellowish]]** * or ***[[yellowy]]** *adj*
