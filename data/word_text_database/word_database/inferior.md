**inferior** *adj*
**1** [[lower]] in quality, quantity, or usefulness
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lower]], [[minor]], [[secondary]], [[subsidiary]], [[lesser]], [[humble]], [[subordinate]], [[lowly]]
**2** [[lower]] in rank, position, or status
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[lower]], [[minor]], [[secondary]], [[subsidiary]], [[lesser]], [[humble]], [[subordinate]], [[lowly]]
**3** of poor quality
**4** [[lower]] in position
**5** (*printing*) (of a character) printed at the foot of an ordinary character
 *n*
**6** a person inferior to another, esp. in rank
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[underling]] (*derogatory*), [[junior]], [[subordinate]], [[lesser]], [[menial]], [[minion]]
 **[[inferiority]]** *n*
