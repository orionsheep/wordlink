**description** *n*
**1** a statement or [[account]] that describes someone or something
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[account]], [[report]], [[explanation]], [[representation]], [[sketch]], [[narrative]], [[portrayal]], [[depiction]]
**2** the act of describing
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[calling]], [[naming]], [[branding]], [[labelling]], [[dubbing]], [[designation]]
**3** [[sort]], [[kind]], or [[variety]]: antiques of every description
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[kind]], [[sort]], [[type]], [[order]], [[class]], [[variety]], [[brand]], [[category]]
