**offset** *vb* **-setting**, **-set**
**1** to [[cancel out]] or [[compensate for]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[cancel out]], [[balance]], [[set off]], [[make up for]], [[compensate for]], [[counteract]], [[neutralize]], [[counterbalance]]
**2** to print (something) using the offset process
 *n*
**3** a printing method in which the impression is made onto a surface, such as a rubber roller, which transfers it to the paper
**4** (*botany*) a short runner in certain plants that produces roots and shoots at the tip
