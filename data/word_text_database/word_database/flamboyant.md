**flamboyant** *adj*
**1** behaving in a very noticeable, [[extravagant]] way: a flamboyant jazz pianist
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[camp]] (*informal*), [[dashing]], [[theatrical]]
**2** very bright and [[showy]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[showy]], [[elaborate]], [[extravagant]], [[ornate]], [[ostentatious]]
 **[[flamboyance]]** *n*
