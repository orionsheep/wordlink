**compass** *n*
**1** an instrument for finding direction, with a magnetized needle which points to magnetic north
**2** limits or [[range]]: within the compass of a normal-sized book such a comprehensive survey is not possible
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[range]], [[field]], [[area]], [[reach]], [[scope]], [[limit]], [[extent]], [[boundary]]
**3** **compasses** an instrument used for drawing circles or measuring distances, that consists of two arms, joined at one end
