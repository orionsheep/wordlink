**hint** *n*
**1** a [[suggestion]] given in an indirect or subtle manner
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[clue]], [[suggestion]], [[implication]], [[indication]], [[pointer]], [[allusion]], [[innuendo]], [[intimation]]
**2** a helpful piece of [[advice]]
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[advice]], [[help]], [[tip]](s), [[suggestion]](s), [[pointer]](s)
**3** a small amount: a hint of irony
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[trace]], [[touch]], [[suggestion]], [[dash]], [[suspicion]], [[tinge]], undertone
 *vb*
**4** (sometimes foll. by *at*) to [[suggest]] indirectly: a solution has been hinted at by a few politicians
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[suggest]], [[indicate]], [[imply]], [[intimate]], [[insinuate]]
