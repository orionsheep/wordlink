**reward** *n*
**1** something given in [[return]] for a service
**2** a sum of money offered for finding a criminal or missing property
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[payment]], [[return]], [[prize]], [[wages]], [[compensation]], [[bonus]], [[premium]], [[repayment]]
**3** something received in [[return]] for good or evil: sacrifice provided its own reward
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[punishment]], [[retribution]], [[comeuppance]] (*slang*), just deserts
 *vb*
**4** to give something to (someone) for a service rendered
◇ <small>SYNONYMS</small>: [[compensate]], [[pay]], [[repay]], [[recompense]], [[remunerate]] (*formal*)
